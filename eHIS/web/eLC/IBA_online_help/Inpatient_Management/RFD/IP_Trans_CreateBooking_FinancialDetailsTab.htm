Financial Details

  1. Click Financial Details tab to record or update the financial details of the patient.

  2. Click [Modify] to invoke the Authorization Screen. The Modify option is available on selecting Registered Patients.

    1. User ID - Enter the User ID in alphanumeric format of 20 characters maximum length.

    2. PIN - Enter the PIN of the User in alphanumeric format of 20 characters maximum length.

    3. Remarks - Enter the Remarks in the text box.

    4. Click [OK] to modify the financial details or [Cancel] to exit from the Authorization Screen.

  3. Enter the details in mandatory (*) fields. <Related Field Description>

  4. Click [Associate Subscribed Package] hyperlink to associate the package to the patient, if it already subscribed through the Package Subscription function. This hyperlink is not available for selection if the package is not subscribed. Click [Subscribe Package] hyperlink to subscribe a package.  

  5. On entering the details, a screen pops-up indicating the patient payable and the payer payable amount for the service. If the patient is an Outpatient and if the selected Clinic has visit exemptions defined in the Revenue Management module, the exemption details information too gets displayed in the screen.

  6. The Hospital Charge and the Patient and Payer Payable details are displayed during registration if the check box 'Charge for Patient Registration' check box is cleared in 'BL Parameters' function. If the check box 'Charge for Patient Registration' is selected and the charges are defined for encounter registration in 'Patient Registration Billing Dtls' function, the Patient Registration ID charges are computed during encounter registration in Register Attendance, Register Visit, Admit Patient functions in the external modules. If the check box 'Charge for Patient Registration' is cleared and the charges are defined for patient registration in 'Patient Registration Billing Dtls' function, the patient registration ID charges are computed during patient registration in 'Master Patient Index' module.

  7. Click [Close] to exit from the Financial Details screen.
