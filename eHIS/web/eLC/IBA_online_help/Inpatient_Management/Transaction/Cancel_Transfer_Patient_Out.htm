Cancel Transfer Patient Out

From this function you can Cancel transferring out of an Inpatient by the source Nursing Unit from their care to a vacant bed within the destination Nursing Unit.

Cancel Transfer Patient Out function lists all Inpatients transferred out from the source Nursing Unit.

Canceling Transfer Patient Out

From the IP Transactions > Transfer, select the Cancel Transfer Patient Out function.

The Cancel Transfer Patient Out screen gets displayed.

  1. Click [Search] to display the list of all Inpatients transferred out from the source Nursing Unit or enter the search criteria and click [Search] to select a specific Inpatient transferred out from the source Nursing Unit. Click [Clear] to reset the records displayed in the result area.

    Enter the following query criteria to select the required patient record:

    From Details

    1. Nursing Unit – Enter the Nursing Unit from where the patient has to be transferred in alphanumeric format of 15 characters maximum length or select the Nursing Unit using the [?] function.

    2. Specialty – Enter the Specialty from where the patient has to be transferred in alphanumeric format of 15 characters maximum length or select the Specialty using the [?] function.

    3. Practitioner – Enter the Practitioner name in alphanumeric format of 30 characters maximum length or select the Practitioner using the [?] function.

    To Details

    1. Nursing Unit - Enter the Nursing Unit to where the patient has to be transferred in alphanumeric format of 15 characters maximum length or select the Nursing Unit using [?] function.

    2. Specialty - Enter the Specialty to where the patient has to be transferred in alphanumeric format of 15 characters maximum length or select the Specialty using the [?] function.

    3. Practitioner - Enter the Practitioner name in alphanumeric format of 30 characters maximum length or select the Practitioner using the [?] function.

    4. Encounter ID – Enter the Encounter ID in numeric format of 12 digits maximum length.

    5. Admission Date From-To – Enter the Admission Date range in the format of dd/mm/yyyy or select the date using the calendar function.

    6. Patient ID - Enter the Patient ID in alphanumeric format of maximum length as specified in MP Parameter or select the Patient ID using the [?] function.

  2. On searching, the details of transferred patients are displayed based on the entered criteria.

  3. Click ‘+’ hyperlink to view the Current Encounter Details.

  4. Click the ‘Encounter ID’ hyperlink to display the Cancel Transfer Patient Out screen. The screen displays the Admission Details, Nursing Unit Details, Encounter Details and Other Details.

  5. Enter the Cancellation Details in the mandatory (*) fields.

    1. Date/Time of Authorized Transfer - The Authorization Date and Time of transfer is displayed and disabled.

    2. Authorizing Practitioner – Enter the name of the Practitioner authorizing the cancellation of transfer patient out in alphanumeric format of 30 characters maximum length or select the Practitioner from the [?] function. This field is mandatory.

    3. Reason for Cancellation – Select the Reason for Cancellation from the list. This field is mandatory. The listed values are defined in the ‘Reason’ function of Application Masters module.

  6. Click [Confirm Cancel] to cancel transfer patient out or [Abort Cancel] to exit from the Cancel Transfer Patient Out screen without canceling the patient transfer.

 

Bed Class, Bed Number and Room Number are not displayed for ‘Regular’ transfers as bed is allocated on transferring the patient to the destination Nursing Unit.
