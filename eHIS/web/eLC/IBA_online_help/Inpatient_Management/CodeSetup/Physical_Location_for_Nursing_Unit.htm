Physical Location for Nursing Unit

From this function you can Create an association of Nursing Units to floors within the building of the facility and Query for Nursing Units based on their physical locations. You can Delete associated Physical Location for Nursing Unit records.

Since Nursing Units are logical locations in an inpatient setting, it is necessary to map every Nursing Unit created to its actual physical location within the facility. Nursing Units are associated to buildings and floors and based on this association, rooms and beds are attached to that floor of the building.

Creating an association of Physical Locations to Nursing Units

From the IP Setup > IP Code Setup, select the Physical Location for Nursing Unit function.

The Physical Location for Nursing Unit screen gets displayed.

  1. Click [Create] to invoke the Create screen.

  2. Enter the details in the mandatory (*) fields. <Related Field Description>

  3. Click [Apply] to save the association of Nursing Units to a Physical Location or [Reset] to clear the entered data.

  4. Click [Query] to search for associated Physical Location for Nursing Unit records.

  5. Click [Delete] to delete a queried record.
