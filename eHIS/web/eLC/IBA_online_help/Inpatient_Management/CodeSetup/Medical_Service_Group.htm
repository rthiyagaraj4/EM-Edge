Medical Service Group

From this function you can Create a new Medical Service Group applicable to the facility by setting limits for Maximum Daily Booking and Maximum Monthly Booking and define attributes to generate Booking Reference Number automatically. You can Query for a desired Medical Service Group.

Medical Service Group controls bed bookings against a group of Nursing Units. Bookings are controlled by setting daily and monthly limits against each Medical Service Group.

Creating a Medical Service Group

From the IP Setup > IP Code Setup, select the Medical Service Group function.

The Medical Service Group screen gets displayed.

  1. Click [Create] to invoke the Create screen.

  2. Enter the details in the mandatory (*) fields. <Related Field Description>

  3. Click [Apply] to save the Medical Service Group created or [Reset] to clear the entered data.

  4. Click [Query] to search for existing Medical Service Groups.
