Transfer Blood Units

From this function you can Transfer the blood unit details to the blood transfusion facility.

The Transfer Blood Units function is used to transfer the details of the blood unit between the blood donation facility and the blood transfusion facility, and between blood transfusion facilities. The blood bags are checked with the blood unit details. If the temperature of the blood bag is violated, the blood bags are discarded from the facility.

Transferring Blood Units

From Transactions > Stock Operations, double-click the Transfer Blood Units function.

The Transfer Blood Units screen gets displayed.

  1. Select the Ordering Facility from the drop-down list. The blood units are to be transferred to the selected facility.

  2. Enter the Barcode unit – Product Code in the alphanumeric format.

  3. Enter details in the mandatory (*) fields. <Related Field Description>

  4. Click [Issue] to transfer the blood unit details to the Ordering Facility or [Cancel] to cancel the transaction.
