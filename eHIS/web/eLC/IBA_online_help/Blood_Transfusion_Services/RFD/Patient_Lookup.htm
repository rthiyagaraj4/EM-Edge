Enter the details in the following fields to search for a record matching the criteria:

 

  1. Patient ID – Enter the Patient ID in alphanumeric format of 10 characters maximum length. The records matching the patient ID entered get displayed.

  2. National ID – Enter the National ID in numeric format of 15 digits maximum length. The records matching the National ID of the patient recorded during registration get displayed.

  3. Alt National ID1 - Enter the Alternate National ID in numeric format of 15 digits maximum length. The records matching the Alternate National ID of the patient recorded during registration get displayed.

  4. Last Name – Enter the last name of the patient to search for a specific patient record. The records matching the patient’s family name get displayed.

  5. First Name – Enter the first name of the patient to search for a specific patient record. The records matching the patient’s first name get displayed.

  6. Middle Name – Enter the middle name of the patient to search for a specific patient record. The records matching the patient’s second name get displayed.

  7. Prefix - Prefix associated to the patient during registration is displayed.

  8. Gender – Enter either ‘F’ or ‘M’ to select the female and male patient records respectively.

  9. Res Tel Num – Enter the residence telephone number. The patient records matching the residence telephone number get displayed.

  10. Inpatient? – Select the check box to query the Inpatient records in the facility.

  11. Deceased? - Select the check box to query the deceased patient records in the facility.

  12. [Close] – Click [Close] to exit from the search screen.

You can press [F8] or click [Execute Query] to view all patient records. The color codes indicates if the patient is deceased, inactive or suspended.
