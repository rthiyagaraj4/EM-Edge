The stock details of the product in the blood bank is displayed. The Product Code, Blood Group, Rhesus, Unit number, Unit Status, Donation Date, Expiry Date, Date when the Product was added to the stock, Patient ID and Storage Locations are listed. You can sort the output based on the Collection Date, Added Date or Expiry Date. Select a value for 'Display Order'.

 

Click [Antibody/Phenotype] to view Antibody/Phenotype details of the selected Unit number and product code. This button is enabled only if the Antibody or Phenotype details for the selected Unit number and Product code are available.

Click [Close] to close the screen. The color codes indicate the status of the blood units.
