The specimen list is displayed based on the specimens registered through the Laboratory module. The Specimen Number, registration time and date, expected transfusion date, source where the specimen is registered, product associated, volume of the blood unit, requested, allocated, reserved, issued and transfused units are listed along with the patient ID and name of the patient is displayed.

 

Click [Patient's Antibodies] to view antibodies associated to the patient. Click [Patient's Requirements] to view transfusion requirements of the patient. Click [Transfusion History] to view transfusion details. Click [Process Request] to view allocated, issued or crossmatched units. Click [Transfuse] to view transfusion details. Click [Blood Unit Details] to view specimen unit details.
