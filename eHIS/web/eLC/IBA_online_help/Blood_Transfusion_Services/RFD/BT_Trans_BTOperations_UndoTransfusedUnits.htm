Enter the following details:

 

  1. Priority – The Priority of the specimen is displayed. The valid values are ‘Stat’, ‘Urgent’ and ‘Routine’. The priority is recorded for the specimen in the Registration function in Laboratory module.

  2. Patient ID – On selecting the specimen number, the Patient ID and Name of the patient are displayed.

  3. Comments – The comments recorded while requesting the blood unit in Request Blood Units function is displayed.

  4. Sex – The Gender of the patient recorded during patient registration is displayed.

  5. Age – The age of the patient recorded during the patient registration is displayed. If an asterisk (*) is displayed next to the age, it is a calculated age of the patient; not the actual age.

  6. Blood Group – The Blood Group of the patient recorded in ‘Record Patient Blood Group’ function of the Medical Records module is displayed.

  7. Rhesus – The Rhesus factor of the Blood Group recorded in ‘Record Patient Blood Group’ function of the Medical Records module is displayed.

  8. Regd Date/Time – The Registered Date/Time of the Specimen is displayed. The specimens are registered in Register function in Laboratory module.

 

Product/Category

  1. Product – The Product code for the requested blood unit recorded in ‘Request Blood Unit’ function is displayed.

  2. Description – The Description of the product code defined in ‘Product Codes’ function is displayed.

  3. Category – The Category associated with the requested product is displayed. The Category is defined in ‘Product Codes’ function.

  4. Remarks – The Remarks recorded while requesting the blood unit in Request Blood Units function is displayed.

  5. Volume – The volume of the product defined in the ‘Product Codes’ function is displayed.

  6. Volume Unit – The Unit of the volume defined in the ‘Product Codes’ function is displayed.

  7. Units – The number of Units requested for the patient is displayed. The value is recorded while requesting the blood unit in Request Blood Units function.

  8. Date – The date on which the units are required is displayed. The date is recorded while requesting the blood unit in Request Blood Units function.

  9. Expected Transfusion Date – The Expected Date for Transfusion recorded in ‘Request Blood Unit’ function is displayed.

 

Unit Details

  1. Unit Number – The Unit Number of the transfused units of the selected specimen is displayed. The unit number the blood unit is created in the Pre Donation function in Blood Donor module.

  2. Product – The Product code of the transfused unit is displayed. . The product codes are created in the Product Codes function.  

  3. Volume – The Volume of unit specified in the ‘Product Codes’ function of the product is displayed.

  4. Blood Group – The Blood Group of the patient recorded in ‘Record Patient Blood Group’ function of the Medical Records module is displayed.

  5. Rhesus - The Rhesus factor of the blood group recorded in ‘Record Patient Blood Group’ function of the Medical Records module is displayed.

  6. Expiry Date - The Expiry Date of the product is displayed. The expiry date of the product is defined in the Product Codes function.

  7. Status – The Status of the unit is displayed as ‘Transfused’. The valid values are, Transfused/Compatible/Suitable/Incompatible/Issued/Returned and Least Incompatible. You can undo only the records with the transfused status.

  8. Reserve Date – The Date and Time until when the blood unit can be reserved is displayed. After the allocation of blood units in Allocate/Xmatch/Issue - Blood Units function, the blood unit is reserved for the patient. Reserve date is the date until which the blood unit can be reserved for the patient based on the expiry date of the blood unit.
