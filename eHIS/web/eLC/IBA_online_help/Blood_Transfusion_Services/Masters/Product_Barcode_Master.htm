Product Barcode Master

From this function you can Create the Product Barcode Master records and Query for the existing records.

The Product Barcode Master function enables you to create Barcodes for each Blood Group and Rhesus combination. You can use the created barcode(s) in various transactions of Blood Transfusion Services module. The barcodes are printed at various times and they are pasted on the blood bags and samples to identify the contents. The barcodes display the blood product details such as Blood Group, Rhesus, Unit Number, Product Code, Expiry Date, Donor ID, etc. The Barcodes are printed after blood donation, allocation, crossmatching, issuing, pooling, aliquoting, converting the blood products, etc.  

Creating Product Barcodes

From the Masters, double-click the Product Barcode Master function.

The Product Barcode Master screen gets displayed.

  1. Enter the details in the mandatory fields. <Related Field Description>

  2. Click [Commit] or press [F10] to save the created Product Barcode or click [Clear Record] to clear the entered details.

  3. Click [Execute Query] or press [F8] to search for the existing records.
