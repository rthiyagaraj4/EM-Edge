Customer Balance

From this function you can Query the balance amount the Customer owes to the facility.

Customer Balance function is used to view the business performance statistics for a customer together with the outstanding balance amount. The month to date Sales, Year to date Sales and the Cumulative Amount of Sales is also displayed for viewing. In addition to the Customer details, this query provides details like the last invoice and the receipt generated for a relevant Customer. It also provides information about the amount unsettled by the Customer. The Local Name and Address of the Customer can also be viewed.

Querying Customer Balance

From the Queries, double-click Customer Balance function.

The Customer Balance screen gets displayed.

  1. Click [Execute Query] or press [F8] to view all the Customer Balances.

OR

  1. Click [Enter Query] and enter the details in any of the text field. Press [F8] to view the selected Customer balance records.

  2. Click [Local Name & Address] to view the Customer address recorded in the local language.
