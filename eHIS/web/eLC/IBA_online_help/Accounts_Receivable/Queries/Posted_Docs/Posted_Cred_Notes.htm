Posted Credit Notes

From this function you can Query Credit Notes which are posted to the Customer.

Posted Credit Notes those pertaining to the period for which the month end process is not done only are displayed. When the month end process is done, Posted Credit Notes are deleted. In addition to the document details, related bills details, User accessed list, Text Document details are also displayed.

Querying Posted Credit Notes

From the Queries > Posted Documents, double-click Posted Credit Notes function.

The Posted Credit Notes screen gets displayed.

  1. Click [Execute Query] or press [F8] to view the credit notes posted to the Customer from the Accounts Receivable, Patient Billing Manager and the Laboratory module.

OR

  1. Click Calendar function to select the date range. Click [Execute Query] or press [F8] to view the credit notes posted to the Customer from the Accounts Receivable, Patient Billing Manager and the Laboratory module during the specified date range.

  2. Click [Refresh] to clear the date recorded.

  3. Click [Credit Note Details] to view the information regarding the Posted Credit Notes, Patient Bills, User access and Remarks pertaining to a relevant Document Type.
