Associating Patients with a Customer

From this function you can Associate patients to a Customer for various transactions.

This function is used to Approve and Finalize the association of patients to a Customer. Various levels of authorization power are assigned to the user. This function is used to associate various patients whose financial details exist to a particular customer for a specified period. By doing so, we can group the patients who belong to a particular Customer group. The Billing Group can be changed for a particular patient and can be finalized by an authorized user only. You can associate any number of patients to the Customer in a single transaction.

Associating Patients with a Customer

From the Transactions, double-click Associating Patients with a Customer function.

The Associating Patients with a Customer screen gets displayed.

  1. Enter the details in the mandatory (*) fields. <Related Field Description>

  2. Click [Commit Form] or press [F10] to save the record or [Clear Record] to clear the entered or queried data. An audit report gets generated with the list of the patients associated to selected Customer.

  3. Click [Edit List] to generate an audit report listing patient associated to the selected Customer along with the Billing Group details.

  4. Click [Finalize] to update the financial details of the associated patient. An audit report gets generated for the selected patient after the financial details are updated.

  5. Click [Fin Details] to view the financial details of the associated patient.
