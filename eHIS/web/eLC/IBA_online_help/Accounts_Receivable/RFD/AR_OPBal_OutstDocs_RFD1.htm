Enter the following to create Outstanding Documents:

 

  1. Document Type – Enter the Document Type in alphanumeric format of six characters maximum length or click [List of Values] to select the required Document Type. This is a mandatory field.

  2. Document Number – Enter the Document Number in numeric format of eight digits maximum length. You cannot update the recorded Document Number. This is a mandatory field.

  3. Document Date – Enter the date is the format of dd/mm/yyyy or click Calendar function to select the required Document Date. The Document Date recorded is used as the Base date for calculation purpose, if the Base Date is not set.

  4. Customer Code – Enter the required Customer Code in alphanumeric format of eight characters maximum length or click [List of Values] to select the required Customer Code. On entering the Customer Code, the Customer Name is displayed. This is a mandatory field.

  5. Customer Ref – Enter the Customer Reference in alphanumeric format of 15 characters maximum length. For example, Customer Reference can be the Policy Number, Purchase Order Number, etc.

  6. Payment Terms – On selecting the Customer Code, the Payment Term Code associated to the Customer Group of the selected Customer is displayed. You can click [List of Values] to select the required Payment Term code. The Payment Term name is displayed against the Payment Term code. This is a mandatory field.

  7. Base Date – The entered Document Date is displayed, by default. You can enter the date in the format of dd/mm/yyyy or click the Calendar function to set the Base Date.

  8. Due Date – The Due date is calculated and displayed for the Base Date based on the selected Payment Term. You can enter the Due Date in the format of dd/mm/yyyy or click the Calendar function to set the Due Date.

  9. Settlement Date – The Settlement Date is calculated and displayed for the Base Date based on the selected Payment Term. The settlement days are set for the Payment Term in the Payment Term function. You can enter the settlement date in the format of dd/mm/yyyy or click the Calendar function to set the Settlement Date.

  10. Settlement % - The Settlement Percentage is calculated and displayed based on the selected Payment Term. Enter the settlement percentage in decimal format of five digits maximum length. You can enter the Settlement Percentage only when the Settlement Date is specified.

  11. Gross Amount – Enter the Gross Amount in decimal format of 11 digits maximum length. This is a mandatory field.

  12. Amount Disc – Enter the Discount Amount in decimal format of 11 digits maximum length. The Discount Amount should be lesser than the entered Gross Amount.   

  13. Markup Amt – The Markup Amount is calculated and displayed based on the Markup Percentage defined for the selected Payment Term code. The margin of amount is considered above the Payable Amount. You cannot modify the values in this field.

  14. Amount Pay – The Payable Amount is calculated and displayed on deducting the Discount Amount from the Gross Amount. You cannot modify the values in this field.

  15. Consolidated (Y/N) – Select the check box to mark the document as Consolidated. You must enter the Document Details, if the check box is cleared.

  16. Balance Amt – Enter the outstanding amount for the Document Type. On entering the Balance Amount, the Consolidate (Y/N) check box is disabled. If ‘Consolidated’ is selected, you must enter the Patient Bill Details (Consolidated Invoice).  

  17. Post Account – The Post Account value is displayed based on the Posting Accounts Combination defined in Posting Accounts Combination function in General Ledger setup of Patient Billing Manager module. The Chart of Account is associated to the Customer Code.  Click [List of Values] to select and associate valid Posting Accounts for the Document Type.

  18. Post Month/Year – Enter the posting month and year in the format of mm/yyyy.

  19. Description – On entering the valid Post Account, description recorded for the entered Posting Month/Year is displayed.

  20. Narration – Enter the Narration in alphanumeric format of 50 characters maximum length.
