  1. Position – The positions of only those staffs that are associated to the workplace in “Staff for Workplace” function are displayed. The positions are assigned to staffs in the “Practitioner” function of Application Masters module.

  2. Shift – Based on the facility, the shifts are displayed as shown above in the blue background. Only productive and working shifts are displayed. Unproductive shifts are not displayed since there are no requirements for unproductive shifts. Only those shifts that are associated to the workplace in “Shift for Workplace” function are displayed. The total staff requirement against each shift and days of the week are also displayed. The staff requirements for the particular shifts are defined in “Workplace Requirement” function.

  3. Staff – The staffs associated to the workplace in the “Staff for Workplace” function are displayed. Enter the required shift in its mnemonic code of 1 character maximum length for each day of the week or click the SP hyperlink to select the required shift pattern for that particular staff. The Shift Patterns displayed are those that are associated to the workplace in the “Shiftpattern for Workplace” function. Click the C hyperlink against the staff name to clear the shift pattern assigned to that particular staff. Alternately, click the space around the text box to select the required shift from a menu listing all the available shifts as shown.

The mnemonic codes of only those shifts that are displayed in the menu can be selected or entered in the text box. If any other mnemonic code other than those listed in the menu is entered, an alert is displayed with the message ‘Invalid Shift’.

On entering the required shifts in the text boxes, the count of allocation in cells and color against the shifts get updated dynamically for each day of the week as shown above.
