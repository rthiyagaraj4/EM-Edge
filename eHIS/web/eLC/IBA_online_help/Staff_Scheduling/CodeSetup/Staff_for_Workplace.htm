Staff for Workplace

From this function you can Create an association of Staffs to a particular Workplace and Query for the existing Staff for Workplace records.

This function is used to associate staff for workplace at the facility level and identify the effective period. On completion of staff for workplace association, the schedule made for the workplace will depend on the number of staffs associated to that workplace. This setup is validated during the setup of Master Schedule and Generate Work Schedule in the transaction process.

Creating an association of Staffs for Workplace

From the RS Code Setup, select Staff for Workplace function.

The Staff for Workplace screen gets displayed.

  1. Click [Create] to invoke the Create screen.

  2. Select By Workplace tab to associate Staffs to a particular Workplace or select By Staff tab to associate Workplaces to a particular Staff.

  3. Enter the details in the mandatory (*) fields. <Related Field Description>

  4. Click [Apply] to save the association of Staff for Workplace or [Reset] to clear the entered data.

  5. Click [Query] to search for associated Staff for Workplace records.
