Delivering Medical Report

From the MR Transactions > Medical Reports, select the Status tab.

The Status tab of the Medical Report screen gets displayed.

  1. Select the Medical Report Status as ‘Received by Medical Record’ and click [Search] to display all Medical Reports received or enter the search criteria and click [Search] to select the required Medical Report received.

  2. Click the Request ID hyperlink to invoke ‘Deliver Medical Report’ screen.

  3. Enter the details in the mandatory (*) fields. <Related Field Description>

  4. Click [Record] to save the delivery of the Medical Report or [Close] to exit from the ‘Deliver Medical Report’ screen.
