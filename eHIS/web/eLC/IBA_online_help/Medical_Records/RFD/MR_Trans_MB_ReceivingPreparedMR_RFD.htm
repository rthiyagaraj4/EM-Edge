The following details are displayed:

 

Patient Details

Details like the Patient Name, Age, Gender, etc. of the patient that are captured while registering the patient are displayed.

 

Request Details

The Request details as entered in the 'Requester Details' Tab of the Medical Board main screen is displayed and disabled. The fields cannot be updated.

  1. Click [Date Details] to view the Request Date, Application Completion Date, Forwarded Date and Prepare Date. Click [Close] to exit from the Date Details screen.

  2. Click [Charges Details] to view the charge details of the Medical Board as recorded. The charge details are displayed as read-only text. Click [Close] to exit from the Charge Details screen.

  3. Click [Applicant Address] to view the address details of the applicant. Click [Close] to exit from the Contact Details screen.

 

Enter the following details:

  1. Report Type - The Type of Report as selected in the 'Forward Medical Board Request' screen is displayed and disabled.

  2. Department/Specialty - The Department or Specialty as selected in the 'Forward Medical Board Request' screen is displayed and disabled.

  3. Remarks - The Remarks are displayed as recorded which can be updated.

  4. Collect Date - The Collect Date as recorded in the 'Status of Medical Board Request' screen is displayed and disabled.

  5. Requested Practitioner - The Requested Practitioner to prepare the Medical Report is displayed.

  6. Report Prepared Date - The Date and Time when the Medical Report was prepared in the Clinician Access module is displayed as read-only text.

  7. Received Date - Enter the Date or select the Date when the prepared Medical Report was received by the Medical Board from the calendar function. The date of receipt must not be greater than the collect date/time and lesser than the prepared date/time. This field is mandatory.

  8. Days to Complete - The number of days between the Date of request and the received Date of the report is displayed as read-only text.
