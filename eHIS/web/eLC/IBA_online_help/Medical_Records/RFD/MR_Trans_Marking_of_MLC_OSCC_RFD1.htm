1. Alt ID No. – Enter the alternate number in numeric format of maximum length as specified in the MP Parameter.

  2. Other Alt Type - Select the other Alternate type form the list box. The Other Alternate Types listed include ‘Birth Certificate’, ‘PMI’, ‘Passport’ etc.

  3. Previous HN – Enter the Previous HN number in numeric format of eight characters maximum length.

  4. Government No - Enter the Government number in numeric format of 10 characters maximum length.

  5. Driving License No - Enter the Driving License number in numeric format of 10 characters maximum length.

  6. Expatriate No - Enter the Expatriate number in numeric format of 10 characters maximum length.

  7. Patient ID - Enter the Patient ID in the alphanumeric format of 10 characters maximum length or select the patient id by clicking on the [?] function. This field is mandatory.

  8. Encounter Period - Enter the Encounter period in dd/mm/yyyy format or select the Encounter Period from the calendar function.

  9. Encounter Type – Select encounter type from the encounter type list box. The listed values are ‘Day Care’, ‘Emergency’, ‘Inpatient’, ‘Outpatient’ and ‘External’.

  10. Encounter ID – Enter the Encounter ID in alphanumeric format of 12 characters maximum length.

  11. Order By – Select the order in which the records have to be displayed. The listed values are ‘Encounter Date’ and ‘Encounter Type’.
