Patient Movement History

From this function, you can Query the patient movement history based on encounter type.

This function allows you to query the patient movement records. You can query for inpatient, outpatient, day care and emergency patients. The query results include only patient Active encounter details.

The patient movement is recorded in terms of either patient arrival to a care location or departure from a care location.

Querying Patient Movement History

From the MR Queries menu, select the Patient Movement History function.

The Patient Movement History gets displayed.

  1. Enter the search criteria. <Related Field Description>

  2. Click [Search] to display the records matching the search criteria or [Clear] to clear the records displayed in the result area.

  3. Click + sign before the Admission/Visit Date column, the Encounter Details dialog gets displayed with the current Encounter Details and Stay Details of the patient.

  4. Click [Reset] to clear the entered data.
