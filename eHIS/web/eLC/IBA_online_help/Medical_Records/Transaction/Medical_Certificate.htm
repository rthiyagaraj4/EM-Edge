Medical Certificate

From this function you can Record the details of the Medical Certificate issued to patients on discharge.

The Medical Certificate provides information regarding the period of patient stay in the hospital and fitness of the patient to resume duty. The person authorizing the certificate is defaulted to the treating Practitioner, though you can select other practitioners.

Recording Medical Certificate

From the MR Transactions, select the Medical Certificate function.

The Search Patient Screen gets displayed.

  1. Search the required patient and click the Patient ID hyperlink to select the patient and record Medical Certificate details. The Medical Certificate screen gets displayed.

  2. Enter the details in the mandatory (*) fields. <Related Field Description>

  3. Click [OK] to record Medical Certificate details or [Clear] to reset the entered data or [Cancel] to exit from the screen without recording Medical Certificate details.
