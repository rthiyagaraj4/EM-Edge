Manage Confidential Results

From this function you can Maintain confidential MTP reports and patient details of a patient.

The Manage Confidential Results function is used to maintain the MTP (Medical Termination of Pregnancy) related test results in a MTP Patient ID created for maintaining the confidential reports of the patient. The MTP Patient ID is created with a particular series to maintain the confidential MTP test results. The patient names, addresses and any other details are not displayed while registering MTP Patient ID.

The lab or radiology test results confirming the pregnancy or clinical notes, procedure notes relating to MTP are kept confidential.  This functionality is applicable only for female patients.

Managing Confidential Results

From the MR Transactions, select the Manage Confidential Results function.

The Manage Confidential Results screen gets displayed.

  1. Enter the details in the mandatory (*) fields. <Related Field Descriptions>

  2. Click [Search] to view the events of the selected encounter in the ‘Source Encounter’s Clinical Events’ heading. The details displayed are Event, Date/Time and Details.

  3. Click ‘View Details’ hyperlink in the ‘Details’ column to view lab results, radiology results, procedure notes, etc. of the selected Encounter.

  4. Select the check box and click [Apply] to move the MTP Encounter details from the main Patient ID to the selected MTP Patient ID. Once the events of the encounter are transferred to MTP Patient ID, the events will not be available in the Source Encounter.
