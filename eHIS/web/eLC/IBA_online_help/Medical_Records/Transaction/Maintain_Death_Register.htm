Maintain Death Register

From this function you can Record details related to the death of a patient and maintain a register of deceased patients in the logged in facility.

This function lists out all patients discharged with a Discharge Type as Deceased. The death register is recorded in two stages based on the 'Single Stage Death Registration Applicable' check box in MR Parameter. You can print the death certificate on recording the details in first Stage and at the finalization stage. The recorded death records are indicated by the  color code. You cannot modify the details for the finalized death register.

Maintaining Death Register

From the MR Transactions, select the Maintain Death Register function.

The Maintain Death Register Screen gets displayed.

  1. Enter the query criteria and click [Search] to select deceased patient records.  

    1. Patient ID – Enter the Patient ID in the alphanumeric format of 10 characters maximum length or select the Patient ID by clicking [?] function.

    2. Admission Period – Enter the admission date range in dd/mm/yyyy format or select the admission date from the Calendar function.

    3. Discharge Period - Enter the discharge date range in dd/mm/yyyy format or select the admission date from the Calendar function.

    4. Deceased Period - Enter the date range in dd/mm/yyyy format or select the date range of the deceased patients using the Calendar function.

    5. Status – Select the status from the list. The listed values are Recorded, Finalized and All. The value 'All' is displayed, by default.

    6. Recorded indicates the records that are completed in the first stage of registration. The Recorded records are indicated with the  colour code.

    7. Finalized indicates the records that are completed in the final stage of registration.

  2. Click the Patient ID hyperlink to display the Maintain Death Register screen and record diagnosis details.
