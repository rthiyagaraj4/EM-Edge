Medical Report

From this function you can Record a request for medical report, process the request and deliver the report to the requester.

A Medical Report is a clinical document that contains the details of the encounter(s) that the patient has had with the logged in facility. A medical report is also used to claim for reimbursement of the medical expenses borne by the patient. The same document can also be used by insurance agencies to validate the claim made by a patient. The process of requesting and obtaining a medical report must be scrutinized very closely.

 

The various steps to request, prepare and deliver a medical report are as follows:

• Request for Medical Report

• Record the Status of Medical Report request

• Forward Medical Report request from MRD to HOD

• Forward to Practitioner (From HOD to Practitioner)

• Prepare Medical Report by Practitioner

• Receive the prepared Medical Report

• Deliver the completed Medical Report

• View the completed delivered Medical Report

• Revise Medical Report

• Pending Medical Reports for Delivery

• Display all Medical Reports
