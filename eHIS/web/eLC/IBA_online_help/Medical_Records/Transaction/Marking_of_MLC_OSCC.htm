Marking of MLC/OSCC

From this function you can Record an encounter of a patient as either Medico Legal Case (MLC) or One-Stop Crisis Case (OSCC) or both.

When the Patient ID is specified, the system lists out all encounters that the patient had with the facility. You can choose the appropriate encounter to mark it as either MLC or OSCC or both. The date and name of the person recording these flags for the patient encounter is also recorded. If any of the encounters for the patient has been marked as an MLC/OSCC or both, the recorded details cannot be modified.

Marking Of MLC/OSCC

From the MR Transactions, select the Marking of MLC/OSCC function.

The Marking of MLC/OSCC screen gets displayed.

  1. Search and select the patient ID by clicking [?] function and click [Search] to display all encounters of the patient to mark it as MLC or OSCC or enter the query criteria and click [Search] to display and select a specific encounter of a patient.

    1. Citizen ID – Enter the Citizen ID in alphanumeric format of maximum length as specified in the MP Parameter.

    2. Other Alt Type - Select the other Alternate type form the list of valid values. The Other Alternate Types listed include ‘Birth Certificate’, ‘PMI’, ‘Passport’, etc.

    3. Other Alt ID - Enter the Other Alt ID in numeric format of maximum length as specified in the MP Parameter.

    4. Previous HN – Enter the Previous HN number in numeric format of eight characters maximum length.

    5. Government No - Enter the Government number in numeric format of 10 characters maximum length.

    6. Driving License No - Enter the Driving License number in numeric format of 10 characters maximum length.

    7. Expatriate No - Enter the Expatriate number in numeric format of 10 characters maximum length.

    The 'Prompt' specified for the Citizen ID, Other Alt No, Previous HN, Govt No, Driv Lic No and Expatriate No in MP Parameter appear as the field legend.

    1. Patient ID - Enter the Patient ID in the alphanumeric format of 10 characters maximum length, or select the Patient ID by clicking [?] function. This field is mandatory.

    2. Encounter Period - Enter the Encounter period in dd/mm/yyyy format or select the Encounter Period using the Calendar function.

    3. Encounter Type – Select the Encounter Type from the Encounter Type list. The listed values are ‘Day Care’, ‘Emergency’, ‘Inpatient’, ‘Outpatient’ and ‘External’.

    4. Encounter ID – Enter the Encounter ID in alphanumeric format of 12 characters maximum length.

    5. Order By – Select the order in which the records have to be displayed. The listed values are ‘Encounter Date’ and ‘Encounter Type’.

  2. Click the Encounter ID hyperlink of the required encounter of the patient to display the Marking of MLC/OSCC screen.

  3. Enter the details in the mandatory (*) fields. <Related Field Description>

  4. Click [Record] to record MLC/OSCC details and main screen is displayed or [Close] to close the ‘Marking of MLC/OSCC’ screen.

  5. Click the same Encounter ID hyperlink of the patient to display the Marking of MLC/OSCC screen to record ‘Close Details’.

    1. Closed By - Enter the name of the person recording MLC or OSCC as closed in alphanumeric format of 30 character maximum length.

    2. Closed Date - Enter the Date or select the Date from the calendar function on which the patient marked as MLC or OSCC is closed.
