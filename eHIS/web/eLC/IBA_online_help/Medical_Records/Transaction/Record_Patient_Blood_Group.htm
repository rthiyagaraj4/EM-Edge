Record Patient Blood Group

From this function you can Record the Blood Group and RH factor for a patient.   

This function is used to record the Blood Group and other related details such as Genetic Markers for a Patient. Blood Group function is used to record the various Blood Groups that are clinically significant to the healthcare delivery management. The Blood Groups applicable to most of the organizations are A, B, AB and O.

 

Process Flow Diagram

Recording Blood Group

From the MR Transactions, select the Record Patient Blood Group function.

The Search Patient Screen gets displayed.

  1. Search the required patient and click the Patient ID hyperlink to select the patient and record Blood Group. The Record Patient Blood Group screen gets displayed.

  2. Enter the details in the mandatory (*) fields. <Related Field Description>

  3. Click [Record] to record the blood group or [Clear] to clear the entered data. Click [Cancel] to cancel recording Blood Group details.
