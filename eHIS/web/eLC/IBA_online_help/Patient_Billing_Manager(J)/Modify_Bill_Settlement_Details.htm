Modify Bill Settlement Details

You can update bill settlement details recorded through 'Add Bill Settlement Details'. The recorded details are displayed.

 

Enter the following details to modify bill settlement details:

  1. Remaining Settlement Amount - The pending settlement amount is displayed.

  2. Amount Settled - The amount settled by the patient is displayed. Enter the amount to update the amount settled. Settlement Type - Select a value from the list. The values defined using the 'Settlement Types' function in Patient Billing Manager module gets listed.

  3. [Get CC Info] - Click [Get CC Info] to validate Credit Card details. This button is enabled only if the Settlement Type is Credit Card.

  4. Cash Given - The Cash Given by the patient is displayed. Enter the amount to update the Cash Given. Cash given should be greater than the amount settled.

  5. Cash Returned - The value is displayed automatically, if the Cash Given is greater than the Amount Settled. You cannot edit the value.

  6. Payer's Name - The Payer's Name is defaulted to the patient's name. You can update the Payer's Name.

  7. Payer Ref Id/Deposit Ref No. - The Payer's Reference ID or the Deposit Reference Number recorded for the respective transaction is displayed. Enter the value in alphanumeric format of 30 characters maximum length to update the existing value.

  8. Click [Accept] to record details or [Cancel] to abort the transaction.
