Health Card Subscription

From this function you can Apply subscription for Health Card.

The Health Card Subscription function is used to apply subscription for Health Card. You can associate the card type with the price class. Subscription of Health Card for other family members can also be performed.

Applying Health Card Subscription

From the Transactions > Health Card, select Health Card Subscription.

The Health Card Subscription screen gets displayed.

  1. Enter the details in the mandatory (*) fields. <Related Field Description>

  2. Click [Apply] to save the changes or [Reset] to clear the details.
