Recovery Room

From this function, you can Create a Recovery Room and Query for existing Recovery Room.

A Recovery Room is a location where patients are taken after surgery to safely regain consciousness from anesthesia and receive appropriate post-operative care.

Creating Recovery Room

From the OT Admin Setup > Operating Room, select the Recovery Room function.

The Recovery Room screen gets displayed.

  1. Click [Create] to invoke the Create screen.

  2. Enter the details in the mandatory (*) fields. <Related Field Description>

  3. Click [Apply] to save the created Recovery Room or click [Reset] to clear the entered data.

  4. Click [Query] to search for an existing Recovery Room records.
