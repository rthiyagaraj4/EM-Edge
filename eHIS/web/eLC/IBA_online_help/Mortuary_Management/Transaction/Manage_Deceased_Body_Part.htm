Manage Deceased/Body Part

From this function, you can Manage the flow of deceased and body parts in the mortuary.

Once a deceased patient is registered in the mortuary, it is essential to monitor and manage the flow of the body in various areas within the mortuary before performing any activity. This function is used to view the list of deceased and body parts within the mortuary in various areas of the mortuary and assign them to respective mortuary areas based on the activities performed. If an autopsy has been requested for the deceased patient at the time of registering the deceased patient, the details are captured in this function. The status of the autopsy to be performed is also monitored. This function helps in managing deceased patients who are at various stages of their interaction with the mortuary.

A deceased body can be associated with any of these predefined statuses.

" Assign Area

" Reassign Area

" Postmortem Request

" Record postmortem

" View Movement History

Managing Deceased/Body Parts

From MO Transactions, select Manage Deceased/Body Part function.

The Manage Deceased/Body Part screen gets displayed.

 

The screen consists of 2 tabs:

Deceased Patients

Body Parts

Deceased patients or body parts registered in the mortuary for the current date are listed. The 'Deceased Patients' Tab is displayed, by default.

 

This function refreshes the patient queue list as per the time limit as set in the field 'Refresh Interval' of the MO Parameters.

 

When the orders placed for post mortem are cancelled or discontinued in CA, you can release the body to Release Area without a postmortem. This can be done by clearing the ‘Postmortem Required’ check box under Registration Details tab in Change Deceased Details screen. On clearing the check box, the ‘Record Postmortem’ link will not be available in the Manage Deceased/Body Part screen. Thereby, the body can be released to the Release Area without post-mortem.
