Enter the following to remove the restrictions:

 

  1. Exclude – This field is disabled from selection. You cannot perform any operation.

  2. Access Rights – This field is disabled from selection. You cannot perform any operation.  

  3. Type – The type is displayed, either as ‘Function’, ‘Submenu’ or as a ‘Report’.

  4. Name – The Name of the function is displayed.

  5. Access Level – The default Access Levels in individual check box for ‘Insert’, ‘Update’, ‘Delete’, ‘Print’ and ‘Query’ are displayed. The status of the check box is displayed and no operations are allowed.

  6. I – The status of the check box is displayed; no operations are allowed.

  7. U - The status of the check box is displayed; no operations are allowed.

  8. D - The status of the check box is displayed; no operations are allowed.

  9. P - The status of the check box is displayed; no operations are allowed.

  10. Q - The status of the check box is displayed; no operations are allowed.

  11. Remove – Select the check box to remove restriction imposed on a relevant function.
