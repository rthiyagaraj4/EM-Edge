By Responsibility

  1. Select a Responsibility from the list. This is a mandatory field.  

  2. Click [Search] to display the list of all the facilities or enter the facility name in alphanumeric format of 35 characters maximum length to search for a selected facility. You can also specify an alphabet and search for the desired facility by selecting a criteria from the list. Click [Clear] to clear the data.

  3. The facilities are listed along with two other columns, 'Remarks' and 'Select'.

  4. Click 'Remarks' hyperlink to enter remarks for a Responsibility. Click [OK] to add the remarks for the selected facility.

  5. Select the check box under ‘Select’ to associate the Facility to the Responsibility.
