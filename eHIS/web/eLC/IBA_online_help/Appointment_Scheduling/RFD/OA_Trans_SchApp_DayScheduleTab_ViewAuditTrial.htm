View Audit Trial

The 'View Audit Trial' screen displays modifications with respect to a patient's appointment. The screen displays the Appointment Number, Patient ID and Patient Name.

  1. Previous - The previous appointment Specialty, Location, Resource and Appointment Date/Time is displayed.

  2. Current - The current appointment Specialty, Location, Resource and Appointment Date/Time is displayed.

  3. Action - The Action performed on the appointment is displayed. The action generally performed is either Rescheduling or Transfer of an appointment.

  4. Reason - The Reason for transfer/reschedule of appointment is displayed. The Reason is captured while transferring/rescheduling the appointment.

  5. Modified By - The user who modified the appointment is displayed.

  6. Modified Date/Time -  The Modified Date/Time of the appointment is displayed.
