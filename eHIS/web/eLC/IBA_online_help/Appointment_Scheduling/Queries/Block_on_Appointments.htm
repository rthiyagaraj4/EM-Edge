Block on Appointments

From this function you can Query all the appointments that are blocked for the generated schedule.

Appointments are booked depending on the schedule generated. Some appointments are blocked for reasons such as non-availability of resources. This function lists out all blocks affected against the schedule of a location. This listing of blocks is used to study frequency of the blocks and to analyze the reasons for blocks. This helps healthcare managers to study trends in deviations from planned schedules and take corrective actions to avoid preventable blocks.

Querying Block on Appointments

From the OA Queries, select the Block Appointment function.

The Block Appointment screen gets displayed.

  1. Enter the details in the mandatory (*) fields. <Related Field Description>

  2. Click [Search] to select a specific record and view details or [Clear] to clear the records displayed in the result area.

  3. Click [Reset] to clear the entered data.

The start and end time of the block along with the reason for block is displayed for a Date, Day, Location and Resource combination.
