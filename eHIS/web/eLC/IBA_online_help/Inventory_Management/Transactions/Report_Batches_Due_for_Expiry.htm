Report Batches Due for Expiry

From this function you can Generate a report on the batches due for expiry.

The report on the batches due for expiry is generated based on the specified cut off date. The expiry report is generated for an Item or a Store.

Generating Report on the Batches due for Expiry

From the ST Transactions > Expiry Management, select the Report Batches Due for Expiry function.

The Report Batches Due for Expiry screen gets displayed.

  1. Enter the details in the mandatory fields. <Related Field Description>

  2. Click [Run] to generate the report on the batches due for expiry or [Reset] to clear the entered data.

  3. Select ‘Report Option’ to generate the report.

  4. Click [OK] to generate the report on the batches due for expiry and spool it in the file on the print server or [Cancel] to cancel the action.
