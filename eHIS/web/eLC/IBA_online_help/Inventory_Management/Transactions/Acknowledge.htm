Acknowledge

From this function you can Acknowledge and Confirm the Items after Issue, Issue Return and Stock Transfer.

The Acknowledge function is a mandatory function if  Acknowledgement or Confirmation is required for the applicable transaction and you must capture information before the stock is updated in the Requested Store. The stock details will not get updated until the Stock Item is Acknowledged or Confirmed. If the Item Stock is rejected, the 'From' Store need to Confirm the rejected stock.

Acknowledging/Confirming the Stock Item

From the ST Transactions > Acknowledge, select the Acknowledge function.

The Acknowledge screen gets displayed.

  1. Click [Create] to invoke the Create screen.

  2. Enter the details in the mandatory (*) fields. <Related Field Description>

  3. Click [Search] to display the specific record based on the entered criteria.

  4. On searching, if the Action Type is 'Acknowledge' the document details are displayed for the selected Store. If the Action Type is ' Confirm', the Rejected Stock details are displayed.

    1. Click 'Select' check box to confirm the rejected stock.

    2. Click 'Doc No' hyperlink to view the Acknowledged details.

    3. Click 'Show' hyperlink to view the remarks.

    4. Click [Save] to confirm the stock.

    5. Click [Apply] to confirm the acknowledged details against the required document number.

  5. In the search results, click Doc No hyperlink to view the Item Stock details for the selected Store such as Item, UOM, Batch ID, Expiry Date, Trade Name, Bin Location, Quantity and Remarks.

  6. The scanned item code is displayed in the text box above the Item column and is non-editable. Click next to the text box. the application checks if the scanned barcode matches with any of the items listed under the Item Details section. If it matches, then that item is highlighted and the details of the item are displayed in the respective fields. If it does not match, then the application displays a message  "The scanned barcode and the barcode of the Listed Item(s) does not match. Please scan the correct barcode.". The scanned, scanning pending and current record items are displayed by means of color-coded background as mentioned below:

  1. The Batch details of the Items are displayed. Select the Bin Location from the list.

  2. Under Quantity, enter the Item received details in Received field.

  3. The received quantity should be in numeric format of 10 digits maximum length and should not be greater than the issued quantity. On entering the Received quantity, the rejected quantity is calculated automatically and displayed in the Rejected field. The requested quantity at the item level is displayed in the Req.Qty field.

  4. Click ‘Add’ hyperlink to enter the remarks in alphanumeric format of 200 characters maximum length in the Remarks dialog. Click [Close] to save and exit from the Remarks dialog.

  5. Select the Entry Completed check box to indicate that Acknowledgement is complete. The stock details are updated on acknowledging the issue. If the check box is cleared, the Receiving Store has to confirm the stock.

The check box is cleared, by default. Click [Copy Values] to copy the issued quantity details in the Received filed.

  1. Click [Save] to save the item details.

If there are some more item(s) pending for barcode scanning, the application displays a message “Barcode scanning is not complete. There are item(s) pending for barcode scanning. Do you wish to proceed further?”. However you can proceed further without scanning the rest of the items by clicking ‘Yes’.Click [Apply] to acknowledge the item details.
