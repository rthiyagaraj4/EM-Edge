Practitioner Task List

The components of the home page are categorized under four groups.

Orders

This group contains the list of all the pending activities pertaining to orders that have been placed across locations that the logged in practitioner has access. The activities that the practitioner can perform are:

  1. Pending Appointment – A count of the number of orders that are pending for appointment is displayed. The hyperlink is used to invoke the Pending Appointment screen where orders pending for appointment are displayed. Click the required Order Catalog hyperlink to view details of the order. Select the Book Appointment check box against the required orders to Book an Appointment and the Book Appointment screen is displayed. This group lists out the classification for the patient's pending schedule. The listed values are My Patient, Ordered Patients, My Related Patient and All.  Click the hyperlinks to search the pending appointments.

    • The number of patient’s count with the pending schedules is displayed. You can click ‘All’ hyperlink from home page summary list to invoke the list all patients and select the required patient and schedule the appointment.

    • The number of patient’s count with pending schedules and the patient related to the logged in practitioner is displayed. You can click ‘My Related Patients’ hyperlink from the home page summary list to invoke my related patients screen and select the required patients and schedule the appointment.

    • The number of patient’s count with the pending orders to the Ordering Practitioner and the patients for whom the logged in practitioner is the Ordering practitioner is displayed. You can click ‘Ordered Patients’ hyperlink from the home page summary list to invoke Ordered Patients screen and select the required orders.

    • The number of patient’s count with the pending appointments to the attending practitioner and the patients for whom the logged in practitioner is the attending practitioner is displayed. You can click ‘My Patients’ hyperlink from the home page summary list to invoke My Patients screen and select the required patient and schedule the appointment.

  2. Pending Authorization - A count of the number of orders which have to be authorized by the practitioner is displayed. The hyperlink is used to invoke the Authorize Orders screen where orders are authorized. Select the Order Catalog hyperlink to view, cancel or amend the order. Select the Authorize check box against the required Orders and click [Authorize] to authorize orders.

  3. Pending Consent – A count of the number of orders for which the patient consent has to be obtained is displayed. The hyperlink provided is used to invoke the ‘Record Consent’ function where the consent of the patient is recorded. Select the Consent check box against the required order to display the Consent details screen. View and modify details if required. Click to select the consent. Click [Record Consent] to record the consent. Click Order Catalogs hyperlink to View or Refuse the Order.

  4. Pending Co-sign – A count of the number of orders for which co-sign is pending is displayed. The hyperlink is used to invoke the Cosign Orders screen where the practitioner can co-sign orders. Select the Cosign check box against the required orders and click [Cosign] to cosign the orders.

  5. Due to Expire/Expired – A count of the number of drugs that are due for expiry or expired are displayed. The hyperlink is used to view details of drugs pending expiry.

Results for Review

This group contains the list of all the results that are pending for review by the practitioner. The results for all those orders for which the logged in practitioner was the ordering practitioner or the attending practitioner comes up for review. The results for review are displayed under the following:

  1. All – A count of the number of results across normalcy statuses, pending for review is displayed. The hyperlink is used to invoke ‘Review Result’ function where the practitioner can review the results.

  2. Abnormal - A count of the number of abnormal results pending for review is displayed. The hyperlink is used to invoke ‘Review Result’ function where the practitioner can review the results.

  3. Critical – The count of the number of results which are critical and are pending is displayed. The hyperlink is used to invoke ‘Review Result’ function where the practitioner can review the results.

  4. Normal - A count of the number of normal results pending for review is displayed. The hyperlink is used to invoke ‘Review Result’ function where the practitioner can review the results.

  5. High Risk - A count of the number of results which are highly infectious and pending for review is displayed. The hyperlink is used to invoke ‘Review Result’ function where the practitioner can review the results.

Notes

This group contains the list of all the pending activities pertaining to notes that have been recorded for which the logged in user has privilege. The activities that the practitioner can perform are:

  1. Pending Review – A count of the number of notes that are pending for review is displayed. The hyperlink is used to invoke ‘Review Notes’ function where the practitioner can review the notes that have been forwarded.

  2. Self – A count of the number of notes prepared by the practitioner and that are pending for review is displayed. The hyperlink is used to invoke ‘Review Notes’ function where the practitioner can review the notes. Click View hyperlink to Modify, Forward, Print or Mark the note as error. Select the Reviewed check box against the required notes and click [Reviewed].

  3. Specialty – A count of the number of notes prepared under the Specialty the logged in practitioner has access and those that are pending for review is displayed. The hyperlink is used to invoke ‘Review Notes’ function where the practitioner can review the notes. Click View hyperlink to Modify, Forward, Print or Mark the note as error. Select the Reviewed check box against the required notes and click [Reviewed].

  4. All - A count of all the number of notes under Self and Specialty that are pending for review is displayed. The hyperlink is used to invoke ‘Review Notes’ function where the practitioner can review the notes. Click View hyperlink to Modify, Forward, Print or Mark the note as error. Select the Reviewed check box against the required notes and click [Reviewed].

  5. Pending Signature - A count of the number of notes that are pending for signature is displayed. The hyperlink is used to invoke Sign Notes screen where the practitioner can sign notes that are unauthorized. All the notes recorded for which the practitioner has the authorization privilege are displayed. Click View hyperlink to Delete, Update or Sign the note. Select the Sign check box against the required notes and click [Sign] to sign the notes.

Patients

This group lists out the patient classes and the count of patients that are assigned to the care of the logged in practitioner. The patients who are listed in this group belong to the patient classes to which the logged in practitioner is a primary care provider.

  1. Consultation List – Self /Specialty – The consultant list of the Practitioner and Specialty of the Practitioner is displayed. Click the Self or Specialty hyperlink to view referral patients assigned to the logged in practitioner and Specialty. Select the Patient Name hyperlink to display the Patient Chart and perform transactions.

  2. Day Care Patients - A count of the number of Daycare patients admitted under the practitioners care is displayed. The hyperlink is used to invoke ‘Patient List’ function where the practitioner can view a detailed patient listing.

  3. Emergency Patients – A count of the number of emergency patients registered under the practitioners care is displayed. The hyperlink is used to invoke ‘Patient List’ function where the practitioner can view a detailed patient listing.

  4. Inpatients - A count of the number of Inpatients admitted under the practitioners care is displayed. The hyperlink is used to invoke ‘Patient List’ function where the practitioner can view a detailed patient listing.

  5. Outpatients – A count of the number of outpatients registered under the practitioners care is displayed. The hyperlink is used to invoke ‘Patient List’ function where the practitioner can view a detailed patient listing.
