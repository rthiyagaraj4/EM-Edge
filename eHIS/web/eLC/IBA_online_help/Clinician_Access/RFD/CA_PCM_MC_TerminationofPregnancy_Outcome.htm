Outcome

  1. Click Outcome from the menu to record the outcome of the Termination of Pregnancy event.

  2. The mothercare pending tasks to be recorded for the event Termination of Pregnancy is displayed if any. Click [Record] to continue recording the outcome or click [Cancel] to perform the pending tasks for the event. On clicking [Record], the screen to record outcome gets displayed.

  3. The Outcome list box displays Birth Control and Close as the outcome for the event Termination of Pregnancy.

  4. Select Birth Control as outcome and a message is displayed indicating the outcome will close the current event and create a new event of recording Birth Control details for the patient. Click [OK] to proceed. Click [Cancel] to cancel closing the event.

  5. Select Close as Outcome and message is displayed indicating the current cycle of recording Termination of Pregnancy event for the patient will be closed and new cycle has to be created for recording details of any other events. Click [OK] to close recording Termination of Pregnancy event and the current cycle ends. Click [Cancel] to cancel closing the event and cycle.
