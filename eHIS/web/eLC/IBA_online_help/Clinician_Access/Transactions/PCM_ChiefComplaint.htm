Chief Complaint

From this function you can Record a Chief Complaint for a patient.

The Chief Complaints for recording are defined in the Define Chief Complaint function of the CA Code Setup.

 

Process Flow Diagram

Recording Chief Complaint

From the CA Transactions > Clinician Access > Desktop Menu > select required patient > Patient Chart Menu > Documentation, select Chief Complaint.

The Record Chief Complaint screen gets displayed.

  1. Any Chief Complaint already recorded for the patient is displayed on the top part of the screen.

    Current Chief Complaint

    The Description of the Chief Complaint, Onset Date, Diagnoses associated to the Chief Complaint and Chief Complaint details are displayed.

  2. Enter the details in the mandatory (*) fields. <Related Field Description>

  3. Click [Record] to save the Chief Complaint recorded or [Clear] to clear the entered data. Click [Cancel] to exit from the screen.

  4. The details recorded are displayed on the top part of the screen. Click Diagnosis hyperlink to view the Diagnoses associated to the Chief Complaint. Click Details icon to view the Explanatory Text and Remarks of the Chief Complaint recorded for the patient.
