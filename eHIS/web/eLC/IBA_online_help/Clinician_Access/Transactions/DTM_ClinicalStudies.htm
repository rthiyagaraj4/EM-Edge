Clinical Studies

From this function you can View, Record and Update clinical studies based on the access rights given.

Clinical Studies function is used by the practitioner to update the clinical studies. You can select the research category and risk factor to sort the details. Practitioner can View, Record and Update the clinical studies according to the rights given.

Creating Clinical Studies

From the CA Desktop menu > Patient Chart menu, select Clinical Studies function.

The Clinical Studies screen gets displayed.

  1. Select the Research Category. The categories created in the Research Patient Category are listed.

  2. Select the Research Category Risk Factor. The risk factors created in the Research Patient Category Factors are listed.

  3. Enter the Remarks in the text box provided.

  4. Select the Start Date and End Date using the calendar function.

  5. Click [Record] to save the entered details. The saved details get displayed at the top of the page.

  6. Click [Clear] to clear the entered data or [Cancel] to exit.
