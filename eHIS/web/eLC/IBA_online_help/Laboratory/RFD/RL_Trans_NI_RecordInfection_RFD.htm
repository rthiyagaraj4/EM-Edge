Enter the following details:

  1. Patient ID – Enter the Patient ID in alphanumeric format of length as defined in the MP Parameter function of ‘Master Patient Index’ module or right click on ‘Right Click for User Options’ and select the Patient ID using the ‘Standard Lookup Function’. This is a mandatory field.

  2. Serial No. – The Serial number of the selected patient is displayed,  by default.

  3. Race – The Race of the patient is displayed.

  4. Sex – The Gender of the patient is displayed.

  5. Date of Birth – The Date of Birth of the patient is displayed.

  6. Date of Admission – The Admission Date of the patient is displayed.

  7. Date of Discharge – If the patient is discharged, the discharge date is displayed.

  8. Ward – The Ward at which the patient is admitted is displayed.

  9. Specialty – The Specialty where the Patient is treated is displayed.

  10. Diagnosis – The Diagnosis details of the Patient is displayed.

  11. Onset Date – The onset date for the infection is recorded.

  12. Remarks - Enter the remarks related to the infection/patient.
