Antibiotic/Organism

From this function you can Define antibiotic groups, antibiotic codes, organisms and biochemical tests for organisms. You can Query to view the existing records.

You can create Antibiotic Codes by defining KB (Kirby Bauer test) and MIC (Minimal Inhibitory Concentration) details. You can define Antibiotic Groups and associate antibiotic codes to the group. You can define Organism by associating it to an antibiotic group and define KB, MIC and Bio Chemical Tests for the organisms.

Defining Antibiotics or Organisms

From the Masters, double-click the Antibiotic/Organism function.

The Antibiotic/Organism screen gets displayed with the Antibiotics tab enabled.

  1. Enter the data in the mandatory (*) fields in the following tabs.

Antibiotic Codes

Antibiotic Groups

Organism

  1. Click [Save] or press [F10] to save the created records or click [Clear Record] to clear the entered data.

  2. Click [Execute Query] or press [F8] to search for the existing records.
