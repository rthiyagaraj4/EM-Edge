Quality Codes

From this function you can Define quality codes for a quality control material and Query for the existing records.

The Quality Codes are used to record values, necessary to evaluate material items used in the laboratory. A quality code is specific to a department. You can associate rules and additional codes to a quality code. The Quality Codes specific to a department are defined by associating one or more predefined Westgard Rules. These codes are linked to one or more tests to ensure that the results produced are reliable and accurate.

Defining Quality Codes

From the Masters > Quality, double-click the Quality Codes function.

The Quality Codes screen gets displayed.

  1. The default Department Code is displayed, by default. Select required department from the list if you have access to more than one department. This is a mandatory field.

  2. Enter the data in the mandatory (*) fields. <Related Field Description>

  3. Click [L] to link quality codes to the created quality code or [Close] to close the ‘Link Quality Codes’ screen. <Related Field Description>

  4. Click [Rules] to invoke Rules setup for the Quality Codes. <Related Field Description>

  5. Select desired rule number and click [OK] to return to the Quality Codes screen or [Close] to close the Rules screen.

  6. Click [Commit] or press [F10] to save the created Quality Codes records or click [Clear Record] to clear the entered data.

  7. Click [Execute Query] or press [F8] to search for existing Quality Codes records within the department.
