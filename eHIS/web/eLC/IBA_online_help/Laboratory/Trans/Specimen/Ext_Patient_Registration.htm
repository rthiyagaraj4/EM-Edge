External Patient Registration

From this function you can Register an external patient from the laboratory department and Query for the registered patients.

The details captured while registering an external patient registration are Demographic Details, Other Identification numbers and contact details of the patient. This function allows registering patients directly from the Laboratory module and a Patient ID is generated.

Registering an External Patient

From Laboratory Transactions > Specimen menu double-click the External Patient Registration function.

The External Patient Registration screen gets displayed.

  1. Enter the details in the mandatory fields. <Related Field Description>

  2. Enter details in the mandatory (*) fields of the following tab pages.

Demographics Tab

Other IDs Tab

Contacts Tab      

  1. Click [Save] or press [F10] to register an external patient, click [Clear Record] to clear the entered details.
