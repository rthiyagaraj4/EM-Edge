Perform Quality Unit Lab Reg/Resulting

From this function you can Register the specimens sent for quality screening and Record result for the specimens sent to laboratory.

The Perform Quality Unit Lab Reg/Resulting function is used to register the specimen sent for quality screening and record the result for the specimens waiting for result entry. The register specimen option invokes the Blood Donor Outstanding Orders function. The Enter Result option invokes the Result Entry by Specimen function, where you can enter the result. The Second Level Review option invokes the Second Level Review function, where you can perform the second level review.

The color codes associated to the unit number denotes the status of the unit.

– Specimen not registered

- Results outstanding

– First level Review

– Second Level Review [Abnormal Results]

– Second level Review

 - Results Completed

Performing Quality Unit Lab Reg/Resulting

From the Transactions > Stock Operation, double-click the Perform Quality Unit Lab Reg/Resulting function.

The Perform Quality Unit Lab Reg/Resulting screen gets displayed.

  1. Click the Calendar function to select the Donation Date range. System displays the Donation Dates in the range of a month, by default.

  2. Click the Calendar function to select the Date range of blood units sent for Quality Screening. The system displays the Donation Dates in the range of a month, by default.

  3. Click [List of Values] to select the Unit Number range from the list of registered units.

  4. Click [Display] to view the blood unit details or [Clear] to clear the entered details.

  5. Click on the required blood unit to view specimen list. <Related Field Description>

  6. Click [Register Specimen] to register Specimen. The Blood Donor Outstanding Orders screen is displayed. The screen displays all the specimens waiting for registration. When you register specimen a message, ‘Selected Units Registered’ is displayed. On registering the specimen, the specimen number is displayed against the specimen and the label [Register Specimen] is changed to [Enter Results].

  7. Click [Enter Results] to record the screening results. The Result Entry by Specimen screen is displayed. On recording the results, [Enter Results] is changed to [Results Completed].

  8. Click [Second Level Review] to send the blood unit for second level review. The Second Level Review screen is displayed.
