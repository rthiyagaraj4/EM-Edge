Change Holding Area

Change Holding Area function is used to Change the recorded holding area details.

  1. The Anesthesia Type, Anesthetist and Theatre details are displayed.

  2. Click ‘Pre Operative Diagnosis’ hyperlink to view the Pre Operative Diagnosis remarks. Click [Close] to exit from the Pre Operative Diagnosis screen.

  3. Click ‘Holding Area Notes’ to view the holding area notes.

  4. Click ‘Record Chart’ to record the chart in the Charts function.

Operations/Procedure

The Operation Code, Description, Side Applicable and Remarks are displayed. Click the Remarks hyperlink to view the remarks recorded for the Operation/Procedure.

Holding Area Details

  1. Checked-in Date/Time – The checked-in time and date of the patient in the holding area is displayed.

  2. Checked-our Date/Time – The checked-our time and date of the patient in holding area is displayed.

  3. Holding Bay – The name of the holding bay is displayed.

  4. Chargeable Units – The number of chargeable units are displayed.

Change Holding Bay

  1. Checked-in Date/Time – Select the checked-in date and time of the patient in Holding Bay using the Calendar function. The current date and time is displayed, by default. This is a mandatory field.

  2. Holding Bay – Select the name of the holding bay using [?] function. The listed values are created in Holding Bay function. This is a mandatory field.

  3. Checked-our Date/Time – Select the checked-out date and time of the patient in Holding Bay using the calendar function. This is a mandatory field.

  4. Chargeable Units – Enter the chargeable units of the holding bay. This is a mandatory field.

  5. Click [Apply] to record the changes or [Reset] to clear the entered details.
