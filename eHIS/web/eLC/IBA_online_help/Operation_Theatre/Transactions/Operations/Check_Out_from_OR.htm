Check Out from OR

Check Out from OR function is used to record checking out of the patient to the Operating Room.

  1. The Anesthesia Type, Anesthetist and Theater are displayed.

  2. Click ‘Pre-operative clinical comments’ hyperlink to view the Pre-operative clinical comments.

  3. Click ‘Verify Check-Out Check list’ to view the list of check list.

Operations/Procedure

The Operation Code, Description, Side Applicable, Status and Remarks are displayed. Click the Pre-operative comments hyperlink to view the remarks recorded for the Operation/Procedure.

Check-Out from OR

  1. Check Out – Select the check box to check out the patient from the present ward or recovery room and transfer to another ward, recovery or mortuary depending the values selected in ‘Transfer To’ list.

  2. Transfer To – Select the values from the list. The listed values are Ward, Recovery Room, Mortuary, and Home. Ward is selected, by default. This field gets mandatory on selecting check out check box.

  3. Ward/Recovery Room – Enter the Ward or Recovery room name or select it using [?] function. The name of this field gets changed depending on the values selected in Transfer To field. This field gets mandatory on selecting check out check box.

  4. Check-in to OR Date/Time – The check in time of the patient is displayed and disabled.

  5. Status – Select the status from the list. The listed values are ‘Intubated’ and ‘Extubated’.

  6. Check-Out from OR Date/Time – Select the check out from the calendar function and enter the check out time in hh:mm format. Current date and time is displayed, by default.

Specimen Details Tab

  1. Specimen No/Dtl – Enter the specimen number in numerical format of 27 characters maximum length. This is a mandatory field.

  2. Department – Enter the department description in alphanumeric format of 15 characters of maximum length or select it using [?] function. This is a mandatory field.

  3. Quantity – Enter the number of quantities in numerical format of three digit maximum length. This is a mandatory field.

  4. Click [Select] to select the entered details.

  5. Click [Cancel] to clear the entered details.

  6. Specimen No/Dtl – The Specimen number is displayed and hyperlink. Click the specimen hyperlink to display the specimen details.

  7. Department – The name of the department is displayed.

  8. Quantity – The number of quantities is displayed.
