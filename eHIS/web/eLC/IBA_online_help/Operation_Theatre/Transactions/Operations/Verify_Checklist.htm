Verify Checklist

Verify Checklist function is used to verify the checklist for performing operation.

The Order ID, Surgeon, Specialty, Theatre, Booking Number, Date and Time of the surgery are displayed.

The following Checklist details are displayed:

  1. Check list Item - The Pre-Operative Checklist associated to the Specialty in Specialties function is displayed.

  2. Roles – The Roles displayed are associated to the Specialty under Pre-operative Checklist in the Specialties function. The Role selected for the OT Slate is enabled. Select the required checklist items.

  3. Remarks – Click the Remarks hyperlink to view the Previous Remarks and enter new remarks. Click [OK] to save the remarks or [Cancel] to exit from the screen.

  4. Click [Apply] to save verification of the checklist.

  5. Completed? – Select the check box for the Role and a screen is displayed to enter the PIN. Enter the Pin and click [OK] to confirm verification of the checklist. Click [Cancel] to exit from the screen.

  6. Click [Apply] to save completion of checklist verification.
