Previous Reschedule

Previous Reschedule function displays the details of the previous schedule.

The screen displays the following details:

  1. Patient ID, Gender, Date of Birth, No. of Times Rescheduled.

  2. Details like Surgeon, Booking Date, Start Time, End Time, Room, Rescheduled By, Action Date and Reason are also displayed.

  3. Click the Remarks hyperlink to view the Remarks recorded for reschedule.
