Surgery History

Surgery History is used to view the patient surgery details. This screen provides search conditions to search for the required data and facilitates viewing the notes and all the other operation details.

 

The following details are displayed:

  1. Facility – Patient logged in facility is selected and it is disabled by default.

  2. Operation – Select the Operation using [?] function. The list displays the operation codes defined through the Operations/Procedures function in this module.

  3. Date [From - To] - Enter the date range in the format of dd/mm/yyyy or select the date range for which the surgery history has to be displayed using Calendar function.

  4. Operation Date – Date of the Operation is displayed.

  5. Operation – Name of the Operation is displayed.

  6. Surgeon – Name of the Surgeon is displayed.

  7. Status – The Surgery status is displayed is displayed in unique colour code.

  8. Details – Click ‘Details’ hyperlink to view the details of the Patient Surgery.

  9. Notes – Click ‘Notes’ hyperlink to view patient Holding Area Notes, Surgeon Notes and Recovery Notes.

    Notes entered by the surgeon while the patient is in the recovery room are displayed.

    Notes entered by the surgeon while recording surgeon notes is displayed in this tab.

    Notes entered by the surgeon while the patient is in the Holding Area are displayed.

Click ‘Operation Date’ hyperlink to view ‘ Patient Demographic’, ‘ Patient History’, ‘ View Booking Details’ and ‘ Anaesthesia History’.
