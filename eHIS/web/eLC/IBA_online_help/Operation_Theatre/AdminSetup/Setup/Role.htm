Role

From this function, you can Create a Role by associating a Role Type and Query for existing roles.

A role is indicative of the activities and responsibilities of the person performing an operation. You can specify if the role is applicable to the OT Slate function.

Creating a Role

From the OT Admin Setup > Set up, select the Role function.

The Role screen gets displayed.

  1. Click [Create] to invoke the Create screen.

  2. Enter the details in the mandatory (*) fields. <Related Field Description>

  3. Click [Apply] to save a created Role or [Reset] to clear the entered data.

  4. Click [Query] to search for existing Roles.
