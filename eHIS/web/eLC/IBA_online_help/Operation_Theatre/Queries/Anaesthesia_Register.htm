Anesthesia History

From this function you can View the Anesthesia History of the patient.

Anesthesia History query function gives the complete Anesthesia details. The Anesthesia details are invoked from the Clinician Access module. The details are under three tabs, namely, the Pre Anesthesia Notes, Intra Anesthesia Notes and the Post Anesthesia notes.

Querying Anesthesia History

From OT Queries, select Anesthesia History function.

The Anesthesia History screen gets displayed.

  1. Enter the details in the mandatory (*) fields or enter the search criteria.

  2. Click [Search] to view the Anesthesia History details or [Clear] to clear the records entered in the result area.

  3. Click ‘Patient ID’ to view ‘Patient History’, ‘Surgery History’ and ‘Patient Demographic’ details of the patient.

  4. Click ‘Details’ hyperlink to view the Anesthesia history details of the patients.

  5. Click ‘Notes’ to view the Anesthesia notes recorded performing the operations.
