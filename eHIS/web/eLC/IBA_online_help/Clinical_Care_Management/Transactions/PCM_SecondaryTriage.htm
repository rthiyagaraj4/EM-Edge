Secondary Triage

From this function you can Record and View Secondary Triage details of a Patient.

This function is used to carry out a secondary triage which is more detailed than a primary triage. Using this function, details of patient condition, trauma details if any immunization status, Assessment details and Allergic details can be recorded. In addition, details of medico legal status and also pregnancy details in women are recorded using this function and if the patient is a child then the pediatric details are entered. The final outcome of a secondary triage is to assign a patient priority based on the priority zone of the patient.

Recording Secondary Triage

From the CA Transactions > Clinician Access > Desktop Menu > select required Emergency patient > Patient Chart Menu > ED Assessments, select Secondary Triage function.

The Record Secondary Triage screen gets displayed.
