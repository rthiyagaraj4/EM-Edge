Trauma Assessment

From this function you can Record and View Trauma Assessment details of an Emergency patient.  

Trauma Assessment function is used to record the trauma condition of a patient when brought into an emergency location. Trauma Assessment is recorded to identify and treat all life-threatening injuries.

Recording Trauma Assessment

From the CA Transactions > Clinician Access > Desktop Menu > select required Emergency patient > Patient Chart Menu > ED Assessments, select Trauma Assessment function.

The Record Trauma Assessment screen gets displayed.
