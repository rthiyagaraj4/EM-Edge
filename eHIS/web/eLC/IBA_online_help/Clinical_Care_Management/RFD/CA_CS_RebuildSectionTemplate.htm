Enter the details to Rebuild Section Templates:

 

  1. Section Scope - Select the radio button against All, Notes, Section or Specialty to select the required Section Scope that needs to be rebuilt.

  2. All radio button is selected by default to rebuild the Section Templates. Select the From and To Sections from the [?] functions. Click the Notes radio button to rebuild a Note. Click the Section radio button to rebuild a Section and click the Specialty radio button to rebuild sections and notes of specialty modules Oral Health and Maternity.
