Routine Visit

  1. Select Ectopic Pregnancy from the Routine Visit (Ectopic) task.

  2. Enter the Last Menstrual Period (LMP) as per the patient in dd/mm/yyyy format or select the LMP date using the Calendar function. The LMP Date recorded as per the patient is defaulted in LMP field and the Gestation Age and EDD is calculated based on the LMP value. You cannot modify the LMP as per patient once it is recorded using [Record]. The modification is done in LMP field to rectify the LMP date of the patient after examination.

  3. If the LMP as per patient is not recorded, you can enter the corrected EDD. The system re-calculates the LMP and the EDD is displayed based on the Specialty Parameter setup and the Minimum and Maximum Gestation Age Range defined in MPI Parameter.

  4. Enter the routine visit details (Ectopic Pregnancy, Record Symptoms, Record Signs, Abdominal Examination, Treatment Summary). On entering the required details, click [Record] to save details of Routine Visit recorded for the event Ectopic Pregnancy. The date and time of recording the Routine Visit details is displayed on the left side menu of the screen under Routine Visit task. Click the Date and Time hyperlink and the details recorded for Routine for the event Ectopic Pregnancy gets displayed on the right side of the screen.

  5. Click Ectopic Pregnancy from the Routine Visit (Ectopic) task to record details on a subsequent routine visit.
