Triage Details

The Triage Details Tab displays 2 tabs

Condition/Case

Others

  1. Enter the details in mandatory (*) fields of the tab pages. <Related Field Description>

  2. Click [Allergy Details] to view and record new allergies for the patient.

  3. Click [Chief Complaint] to record the chief complaint of the patient.

  4. Click [Previous Encounters] to view the past encounters recorded for the patient.

  5. Click [Visit within 24 hrs] to view details of the patient’s last visit within 24hrs. This is disabled if patient has not made any visits to the facility within 24 hours from current date and time.

  6. Click [Record] to save Secondary Triage details or click [Reset] to reset the entered details.

  7. The date and time of recording Secondary Triage details are displayed on the left hand side of the screen in the Recorded Details frame. Click the Date and Time hyperlink to view the Secondary Triage details recorded for the Emergency Patient.
