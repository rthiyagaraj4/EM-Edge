Birth Control

Birth control, also known as contraception, is designed to prevent pregnancy. Birth control methods may work in a number of different ways. These include

- Blocking sperm from getting to the eggs - condoms and diaphragms work this way.

- Keeping the woman's ovaries from releasing eggs that could be fertilized - birth control pills work this way.

- Preventing fertilized egg from attaching to the lining of the uterus - intrauterine devices (IUDs) work this way.

- Sterilization, which permanently prevents a woman from getting pregnant or a man from being able to get a woman pregnant.

The Birth Control event is applicable to males as well as females.

 

  1. Select Birth Control Event.

  2. The tasks of Birth Control are Assessment for Mother and Outcome displayed on the left side of the screen. Clinical Notes and Clinical Charts can be recorded for the mother patient.

  3. Click Mother Document from the menu to record clinical notes that reflect the clinical condition of the mother patient for the event Birth Control. Note Types created with Note Group of Applicable Task as 'Mothercare Consultation' is used for recording.

  4. Click Mother Chart from the menu to record clinical charts for the mother patient.

  5. Click Outcome from the menu to record the outcome of the Birth Control event. The Outcome list box displays Close, Ectopic Pregnancy and Pregnant as outcomes for the event Birth Control.

  6. Select Close as Outcome and message is displayed indicating the current cycle of recording events for the patient will be closed. Click [OK] to close recording events and the current cycle ends.

  7. Select Outcome as Pregnant and message is displayed indicating that the current Birth Control event will be closed and new event will be created. Click [OK] to create the new event selected or click [Cancel] to close creating the new event.

  8. Select Outcome as Ectopic Pregnancy and message is displayed indicating that the current Birth Control event will be closed and new event will be created. Click [OK].

  9. The tasks to be recorded for the Ectopic Pregnancy event are displayed on the left side of the screen. The right side of the screen displays the event, outcome recorded, status of the event and period range of the event. Events that are active for the patient are displayed in bold.

  10. Click New Eventicon to record additional events for any of the current event being recorded. The events available for recording are Reproductive Cancers and STD Prevention.

  11. Click icon to view the Maternity Home Page. The screen displays two tabs, namely, Home and History. History tab displays details of all events recorded for the patient.
