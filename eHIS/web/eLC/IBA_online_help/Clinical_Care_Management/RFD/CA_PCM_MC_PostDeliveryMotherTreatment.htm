Post Delivery Mother Treatment

After childbirth, a mother undergoes lots of changes physically as well as emotionally. A new mother may not have a bowel movement for several days. There may also be cases of postpartum depressions (PPD). The other rare cases include postpartum thyroiditis, hypopituitarism, etc. Thus, post delivery (postpartum) mother treatment includes all the care that has to be given to the mother medically.
