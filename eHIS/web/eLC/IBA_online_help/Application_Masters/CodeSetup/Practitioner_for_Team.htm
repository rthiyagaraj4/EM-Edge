Practitioner for Team

From this function you can Create an association of one or more Practitioners to a Medical Team in a Facility and define the Role of the Practitioner in the Team. You can also Query for a required Practitioner for Team record.

Practitioner for Team function facilitates associating practitioners to a Medical Team. Specialty of the practitioner generally matches with the specialty of the medical team. A practitioner is allowed associated to more than one team. A Medical Team consists of one Chief Practitioner and the rest as the members of the team.

Creating an association of Practitioners to a Team

From the Practitioner Setup, select the Practitioner for Team function.

The Practitioner for Team screen gets displayed.

  1. Click [Create] to invoke the Create screen.

  2. Select the required facility from the list. The logged-in facility is displayed, by default. The list box displays facility records as created in the Facility function of the System Manager module and associated to the logged-in user and responsibility.

  3. Select the Medical Team from the list. The Medical Teams defined in the Medical Team function are displayed. On selecting the Medical Team, the Specialty of the Medical Team is displayed as read only text.

  4. Select the Search By field as Practitioner ID or Practitioner Name and enter the required text in the text box provided to search for Practitioners based on ID or Name.

  5. Click [Search] to display Practitioners based on the entered criteria. If the Search By field is not entered, all Practitioners defined in the Practitioner function are displayed. Click the + hyperlink to view Practitioner details. Close the Practitioner Details screen after viewing.

  6. Select the Role against the required Practitioner as Chief or Member.

  7. Select the check box under ‘Select’ against the required Practitioners to associate the practitioners to the selected Medical Team of the facility.

  8. Click [Apply] to save the record or [Reset] to clear the entered data.

  9. Click [Query] to search the existing Practitioners for Team records.
