Reason for Admission

From this function you can Create a Reason for Admission and Query for a required Reason for Admission record.

‘Reason for Admission’ function helps you to set up and maintain various Reasons for Admissions, which represent patient’s complaints and symptoms. They include the nature and duration of symptoms that caused the patient to seek medical attention.

Creating a Reason for Admission

From the AM Other Setup > Other Master Setup, select the Reason for Admission function.

The Reason for Admission screen gets displayed.

  1. Click [Create] to invoke the Create screen.

  2. Enter the details in the mandatory (*) fields. <Related Field Descriptions>

  3. Click [Apply] to save a Reason created or [Reset] to clear the entered data.

  4. Click [Query] to search for an existing Reason for Admission record.
