Service

From this function you can Create a Service and classify it as a Medical Service or/and Ancillary Medical Service. You can Query for a required Service record.

A Service is a medical care provided to patients by a facility that identifies an area of specialization as treatment, therapy or surgery. Each Service defined in this function is allowed to be classified as a Medical Service specific to a Specialty, Clinic or Nursing Unit. An Ancillary Medical Service is classified only for a Medical Service.

Creating a Service

From the Service Setup, select the Service function.

The Service screen gets displayed.

  1. Click [Create] to invoke the Create screen.

  2. Enter the details in the mandatory (*) fields. <Related Field Description>

  3. Click [Apply] to save a Service created or [Reset] to clear the entered data.

  4. Click [Query] to search for an existing Service record.
