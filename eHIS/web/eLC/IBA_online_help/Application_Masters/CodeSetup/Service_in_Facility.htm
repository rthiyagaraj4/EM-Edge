Service in Facility

From this function you can Associate Services to a Facility and an Accounting Department and Query for a required Service in Facility.

A Facility offers several Services. The Services associated to a Facility indicates various healthcare services rendered by the facility to patients. While associating Services to a Facility, the Department that offers the Service is also identified. A service associated to an Accounting Department cannot be associated to another Accounting Department.

Associating Services to a Facility

From the Service Setup, select the Service in Facility function.

The Service in Facility screen gets displayed.

  1. Click [Create] to invoke the Create screen.

  2. Select the required facility from the list. The logged-in facility is displayed, by default. The list box displays facility records as created in the Facility function of the System Manager module and associated to the logged-in user and responsibility.

  3. Select an Accounting Department from the list of valid values. This list box displays Accounting Departments associated to the selected Facility in ‘Facility for Accounting Department’ function.

  4. Select the 'Search By' field as 'Service Code' or 'Service Description' and enter the required text in the text box provided to search for Services based on Code or Description.

  5. Click [Search] to display Services based on the entered criteria. The Services created through the 'Service' function are displayed, if the 'Search By' field is not specified.

  6. Select the check box against the applicable Services to associate the selected Services to the Facility and Accounting Department.

  7. Click [Apply] to save the record or [Reset] to clear the entered data.

  8. Click [Query] to search for an existing record.
