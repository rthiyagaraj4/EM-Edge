Products

From this function you can Create a blood product and associate it to an existing Order Catalog. You can also define a new Order Catalog to associate the blood product. You can Query to view the existing records.

A blood product is any component of the blood collected from a donor for use in a blood transfusion. Transfusion of Whole blood is not common at present. Most of the blood products consist of specific processed components such as red blood cells, blood plasma or platelets. The Products function enables you to create different blood products. The product code and orderable (packed red cells, washed red cells, etc.) comprise of different blood group combinations and Rhesus factors. You can associate them to a defined product category. The product code function enables you to associate the details such as donation, stock level, order, expiry date, volume, confirmation required, specimen and barcode through the different tab frames.

Creating Products

From the Masters, double-click the Products function.

The Products screen gets displayed.

  1. Enter the details in the mandatory (*) fields found in different tabs. <Related Field Description>

  2. Click [Save] or press [F10] to save the created record or click [Clear Record] to clear the entered data.

  3. Click [Execute Query] or press [F8] to search for existing records.
