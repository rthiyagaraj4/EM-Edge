Product Categories

From this function you can Create different blood Product Categories and Query for the existing records.

The Product Categories function enables you to define the categories of blood to which a product belongs. Examples of blood product categories are Whole Blood, Plasma, Cellular components such as Red Blood Cells, Platelets, etc. Select the check boxes to indicate whether the product is a red cell and whether the product requires blood group confirmation.

Creating the Product Categories

From the Masters, double-click the Product Categories function.

The Product Categories screen gets displayed.

  1. Enter the details in the mandatory fields. <Related Field Description>

  2. Click [Save] or press [F10] to save the created record or click [Clear Record] to clear the entered data.

  3. Click [Execute Query] or press [F8] to search for the existing records.
