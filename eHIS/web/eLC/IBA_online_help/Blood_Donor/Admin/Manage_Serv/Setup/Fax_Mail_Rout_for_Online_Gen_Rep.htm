Fax/Mail Routing for Online Generated Reports

From this function you can Define the destination Fax numbers and Mail IDs for each document/output depending on the requirements of the specific document/output.

The Fax/Mail Routing for Online/Generated Reports function is used to define the destination faxes or mails for each document/output. Each report can be routed to multiple faxes and mails, depending on the requirements of the specific document/output.

Fax/Mail Routing for Online Generated Reports

From Administration > Manage Services > Setup, double-click the Fax/Mail Routing for Online Generated Reports function.

The Fax/Mail Routing for Online Generated Reports screen gets displayed.

  1. Click [Save] or press [F10] to save or click [Clear Record] to clear the entered data.

  2. Click [Execute Query] or press [F8] to search for the existing records within the facility.
