The following details are displayed:

 

  1. Unit No – The Unit Number that belongs to the selected product is displayed.

  2. Blood Group – The Blood Group is displayed.

  3. Rhesus – The Rhesus factor of the blood group is displayed.

  4. Date of Expiry – The Date of Expiry of the product is displayed.

  5. Donation Date – The Donation Date of the product is displayed.

  6. Volume – The Volume of the product is displayed.

  7. Collection/Supplier – The Collection/Supplier code and Description is displayed.

  8. Fate – The Fate code attached to the unit that is used in the transaction.

  9. Storage Location – The Storage Location is displayed.

  10. Unit Status – The Unit Status if the unit is used in transaction is displayed.
