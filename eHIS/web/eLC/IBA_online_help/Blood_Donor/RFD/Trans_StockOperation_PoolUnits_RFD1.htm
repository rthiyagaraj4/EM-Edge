Enter the following details to confirm the pooling process:

 

  1. Unit Number – Enter the new Unit Number for the pooled product in alphanumeric format of 30 characters maximum length. If the setup is done in the BD Parameters to create the Unit Numbers automatically, you cannot  enter the Unit Numbers.  

  2. Volume ML – Enter the volume of the blood unit in the volume field. The volume you enter must not exceed the total volume of the units you have entered while adding the units to the stock.  

  3. Blood Group – Click [List of Values] to select the Blood Group.  

  4. Rhesus – The Rhesus factor for the pooled unit based on the rule specified in the Pooling Rhesus Rule function is displayed.

  5. Donation Date – The current system date is displayed as the Donation Date. You can update the donation date using the Calendar function.

  6. Date of Expiry – The calculated Date of Expiry according to the expiry period defined in the Product Codes master is displayed. The system takes the expiry date of the unit that gets expired first as the expiry date of the pooled unit. You cannot make changes to it.
