The following details are displayed:

 

  1. Weight – The Weight of the donor entered during the pre-donation analysis gets displayed.

  2. Temperature – The temperature of the donor entered during the pre-donation analysis is displayed.

  3. Systolic – The Systolic Pressure of the donor entered during the pre-donation analysis is displayed.

  4. Diastolic – The Diastolic Pressure of the donor entered during the pre-donation analysis gets displayed.

  5. Pulse – The Pulse rate of the donor entered during the pre-donation analysis gets displayed.

  6. Hemoglobin – The Hemoglobin level of the donor entered during the pre-donation analysis is displayed.

  7. Question – The questions asked to the donor based on the physical questionnaire setup entered during the pre-donation analysis are displayed.

  8. Response – The response of the donor to the questions entered during the pre-donation analysis is displayed.

  9. Accepted? – The result of the pre-donation analysis is displayed which indicates if the donor is eligible for blood donation.

  10. Test ordered? – The list indicates whether any test was ordered to be conducted on the selected donor during the pre-donation analysis.

 

Physical Checkups

  1. Location – The Location code, where the physical checkup is conducted is displayed.

  2. Performed By – The Name of the practitioner, who conducted the physical checkup, is displayed.

  3. Date/Time – The Date and Time when the physical checkup is conducted is displayed.
