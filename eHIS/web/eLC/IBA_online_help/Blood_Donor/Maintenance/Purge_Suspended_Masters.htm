Purge Suspended Masters

From this function you can Delete the master records that are suspended.

The Purge Suspended Masters function is used to delete master records, which are suspended. If the master codes are already used in certain posted data files, the code cannot be deleted.

Purging Suspended Masters

From the Maintenance, double-click the Purge Suspended Masters function.

The Purge Suspended Masters screen gets displayed.

  1. Click [List of Values] to select the Master Code. The suspended Master Codes get listed.  

  2. Click [Save] or press [F10] to delete the masters or click [Clear Record] to clear the entered data. The confirmation is displayed on successfully completing the transaction.
