View Patient Family Members

From this function you can Query and view the family members of a registered patient and their demographics.

Patients are linked during registration to their family members registered within the enterprise. This function is used to view the family members of a registered patient and their demographics.

Querying for Patient Family Members

From MP Queries, select the View Patient Family Members function.

The Patient Search screen gets displayed.

  1. Search and select the desired patient to view the Patient Family Members details.

  2. The family members are represented in a tree format. This format helps you in identifying the relationship of the members with the selected Patient. The selected patient is highlighted. This information is useful in analyzing hereditary problems of the patient.

  3. The icon of a boy depicts the male member of the family and that of a girl depicts female member. The icons are hyperlinked.

  4. To view the demographics details of the members, click the hyperlinked icon. The 'View Patient Details' function is invoked and you can view the complete demographic details of the selected member.

 

Family link is maintained, if 'Maintain Family Link' is set in MP Parameter Setup.
