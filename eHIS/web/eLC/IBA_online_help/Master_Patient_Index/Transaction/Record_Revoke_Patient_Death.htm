Record/Revoke Patient Death

From this function you can Record patient death for patients not having active encounters within the facility and/or Revoke patient death records.

A unique color indicator displays the ‘Deceased’ status. A patient erroneously recorded as ‘Deceased’ is rectified using this function. To perform any transactions, the patient record has to be revoked as system prohibits transactions on deceased patients.

Recording/Revoking Patient Death

From the MP Transactions, select the Record/Revoke Patient Death function.

The Patient Search screen gets displayed.

  1. Enter at least one search criteria if not all and click [Search] to display patient records according to the entered criteria. <Related Field Description>

  2. Select the desired patient by clicking the Patient ID hyperlink to record Patient Death from the Record/Revoke Patient Death screen that displays.

  3. Enter the details in the mandatory (*) fields. <Related Field Description>

  4. Click [Apply] to record Patient Death.

  5. To revoke Patient Death, select the patient using the search patient function. A unique color code indicates deceased patients. Select the desired patient by clicking the Patient ID hyperlink to revoke Patient Death.

  6. The ‘Deceased’ status appears as a blinking gif on the screen. Enter ‘Remarks on Revoking’ in alphanumeric format of 200 characters maximum length. This is a mandatory field.

  7. Click [Apply] to save the record or [Reset] to clear the entered data.

 

If Mortuary registration is complete, the patient death cannot be revoked for the patient record using this function. If a suspended patient is selected for recording death, the Suspended status appears as a blinking gif on the screen.
