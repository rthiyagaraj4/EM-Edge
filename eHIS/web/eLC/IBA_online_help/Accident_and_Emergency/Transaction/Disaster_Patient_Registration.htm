Disaster Patient Registration

From this function you can Perform bulk registration of patients in situations where the patients’ inflow exceeds the normal average such as during disasters.

Disaster Patient Registration function allows bulk patient registrations during a calamity. You can record the number of patients admitted along with their age and gender. During registration, patients’ are provided with a temporary number, which is updated, with additional details at a later point in time.

Registering Disaster Patients

From the AE Transactions, select the Disaster Patient Registration Function.

Disaster Patient Registration screen gets displayed.

  1. Enter the details in the mandatory (*) fields. < Related Field Description >

  2. Click [Apply] to register disaster patients or [Reset] to clear the entered data.

  3. The Financial Details screen gets displayed on clicking [Apply] only if ‘Create Financial Details’ check box is selected. Click [Modify] and enter User ID, PIN and Remarks to login and update the financial details. Click [Close] to proceed with completing the disaster patient registration process and the system prompts the visit charges applicable for the visit.

  4. Click [Continue] to proceed with disaster patient registration or click [Revise] to modify financial details recorded.

  5. If the internal reports are defined, the Internal Reports screen gets displayed. Click [OK] to generate Internal Reports or click [Cancel] to exit from the screen. The Control shifts back to the ‘Disaster Patient Registration’ screen with Patient ID and Encounter ID generated for disaster patients.
