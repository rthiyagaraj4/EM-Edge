Patient ID

Create a Patient ID for an unregistered patient before Registering Attendance . The Patient ID field is used to register the attendance of a patient with Patient ID.

 

  1. Enter the Patient ID number or click the Patient ID [?] function to search for the patient. The Search Patient screen  gets displayed.

  2. If the required patient is not available while searching, click [Normal Registration] to register the patient normally. The Register Patient screen  gets displayed. Click [Emergency Registration] to register the patient with minimum details under emergency conditions. The Emergency Registration screen gets displayed.

  3. Click [Apply] and control shifts back to the Register Attendance screen.

  4. A Patient ID is generated for the patient and displayed in the Patient ID field which is disabled.

  5. The Patient details are displayed as entered while registering. The name of the patient is displayed as ‘Unknown’ if not entered in case of Emergency Registration.

  6. Enter the mandatory details in the Tab pages. The Register Attendance screen comprises of four tabs, namely, ‘Visit Detail’, ‘Arrival’, ‘Related Contacts’ and ‘Financial Details’. <Related Field Description>
