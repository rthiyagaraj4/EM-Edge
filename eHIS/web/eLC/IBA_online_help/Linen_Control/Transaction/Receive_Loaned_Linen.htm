Received Loaned Linen

From this function you can Receive the Loaned Linen Items back from Nursing Unit, Clinic, Department or from a patient within a Facility and Record the details on the linen items received. You can View the loaned linen item details or Receive/Write-off the loaned linen items.

The Receive Loaned Linen function is used to receive the linen items loaned by a Nursing Unit/Clinic/Department to another Nursing Unit/Clinic/Department or to a patient. You can record the details of the linen items received after for mending from external agencies. You can also track the linen item movement between the laundry and various external agencies. The linen items that are not received after lending it to a patient or other Nursing Units/Clinics/Departments can be written-off. You have to select a Source Type and Source to record the received linen item details. The values selected are defaulted and displayed in the transaction functions.

Receiving Loaned Linen

From the Linen > Linen Transaction, select the Received Loaned Linen function.

The Received Loaned Linen screen gets displayed.

  1. Enter the details in the mandatory (*) fields. <Related Field Description>

  2. Click [Search] to view or mark the linen item details as received or written-off. The Loaned Linen Requests placed are displayed in ascending order based on the Reference Number.

  3. Click Request Number to ‘View’ or ‘Receive/Write-Off’ the Request placed for loaned linen.

  4. Click ‘View’ hyperlink to view the request placed for linen items. You can view Loan Reference Number, Loan Date, Source Type, Source from where the request is placed and the source to which the linen items are loaned, name of the person who has given and received the linen items, remarks recorded and the status of the request. The Linen Item, loaned quantity and received quantity are displayed.

  5. Click ‘Receive/Write-off Detail’ hyperlink to record the quantity received from the source to which the linen item is loaned. Reference Number, Loan Date, Source Type, Source, Loaned To, Source, Given By, Received By, Reason for Loan and Remark are displayed.

    Receive/Write-Off detail

    The Reference Number, Loan Date, Source Type/Source, Loaned To/Source, Given By, Received By, Reason for Loan and Remark of the Reference Number opted to receive or write-off are displayed on the top of the window for view. You cannot modify the data.

    Loan Receipt Details

    1. Receive/Write-off - Select the appropriate value from the system defined list of values. The values listed are Receive or Write-Off. This is a mandatory field.

    2. Received Qty – Enter the quantity received back against the relevant Reference Number in numeric format. The quantity entered can be partially received or fully received quantity.

    3. Received Back By – Enter the name of the person who has received back the loaned linen on behalf of the lending Nursing Unit or Clinic or Department.

    4. Given Back By – Enter the name of the person who has given back the linen taken as a loan from a Nursing Unit or Clinic or Department. The Given Back by name can be a patient also.

  6. The Stock Status and Work Status of the linen items in the laundry are displayed. You can choose to view the status of the linen items in laundry by selecting a source Type and a Source. The current system is displayed. Click [Go] to search for the details in the respective Source and Source Type.
