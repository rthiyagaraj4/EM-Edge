1. Click [Search] to display the completed examinations or enter the search criteria to select a specific examination record.

  2. On searching the Exam details are displayed.

  3. Right click on the Patient ID and select the options Repeat Examination or Recall Patient.

    Enter the following details to Recall the patient to repeat the Examination:

    1. Recalled By - Enter the Recalled Radiologist or Technicians name or click [Lit of Values] to select the Recalled Radiologist or Technicians name. This is a mandatory field.

    2. Reason - Enter the Reason or click [Lit of Values] to select the Reason. On selecting the Reason code, the reason is displayed. This is a mandatory field.

    3. Receiver Type - Select the Receiver Type from the list. The listed values are 'Patients' and 'Others'.

    4. Received By - Enter the received persons name.

    Click [OK] to record the Recall patient details or [Cancel] to exit from the screen.

    Enter the following details to repeat the Examination:

    1. Requested By - Enter the Requested Radiologist or Technicians name or click [Lit of Values] to select the Requested Radiologist or Technicians name.

    2. Reason - Enter the Reason or click [Lit of Values] to select the Reason. On selecting the Reason code, the reason is displayed. This is a mandatory field.

    Click [OK] to record the Repeat Examination details or [Cancel] to exit from the screen.

     

     

  4. Right click on 'Ordered By' and 'Attended By' to view the physicians contact details.
