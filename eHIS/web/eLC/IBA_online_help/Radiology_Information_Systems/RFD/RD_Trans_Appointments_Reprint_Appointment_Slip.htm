Enter the following details:

  1. Ordering Facility – Select the facility from which the examination order was placed. By default, the logged-in facility is displayed.

  2. Performing Facility – Select the facility in which the examination is to be performed. By default, the logged-in facility is displayed.

  3. Patient ID - Enter the Patient ID in the length as specified in the MP Parameter or click ‘Right Click for User Options’ and select the Patient ID using the Standard Patient Lookup function. On selecting the Patient ID, the Name, Gender and the appointment details of the selected patient are displayed.

  4. Click [Search] to display the Name, Gender and the appointment details of the selected patient.

  5. Click [Next] to view the next record of the patient or Click [Prev] to view previous record of  the patient.

If the selected patient does not have multiple records, the ‘Next’ and ‘Prev’ buttons are disabled.

  1. Print – Select the check box to reprint the Appointment Slip.
