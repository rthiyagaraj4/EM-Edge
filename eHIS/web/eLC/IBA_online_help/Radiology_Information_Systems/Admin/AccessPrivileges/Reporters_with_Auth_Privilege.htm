Reporters with Authorisation Privilege

From this function you can Define the reporters with authorisation privileges and Query for the existing reporters with authorisation privileges.

The Reporters with Authorisation Privilege function enables you to define the reporters with authorisation privileges. Only the reporters defined here can authorise a radiology result report.

Defining Reporters with Authorisation Privilege

From the Administration > Access Privileges, double-click Reporters with Authorisation Privilege function.

The Reporters with Authorisation Privilege screen gets displayed.

  1. Enter the details in the mandatory (*) fields. <Related Field Description>

  2. Click [Save], press [F10] to define reporter with authorisation privilege or click [Clear Record] to clear the entered details.

  3. Click [Execute Query] or press [F8] to search for the existing reporters with authorisation privileges.
