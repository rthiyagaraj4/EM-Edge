Reassign Practitioner

The Reassign practitioner option is used to reassign a Practitioner to patients with queue status as ‘Checked-in’ or ‘At Nursing Station’ or ‘Vital Signs Recorded’.

  1. Click the Reassign Practitioner option from the queue status to display the Reassign Practitioner screen.

  2. The details already assigned to the patient record are displayed under ‘Current’ heading.

  3. Select and re-assign the Location, Room, Sub-service and Practitioner to the patient record.

  4. Click [Record] to reassign the Practitioner or [Close] to exit from the screen.

  5. If the Practitioner is reassigned to a patient registered with an appointment, an alert message is displayed – ‘This is an Appointment Patient, delink it’.

  6. Click [OK] to de-link the association. The existing queue record is closed and a new queue record is created as a Walk in patient. Click [Cancel] to abort the operation and the control goes back to Reassign Practitioner screen.

 

Booking group consultation for a patient

You can use this screen to arrange group consultations for a patient. In group consultation, multiple practitioners attend a single patient in the same appointment.

For group consultation, a multi-specialty practitioner must be selected as the primary resource during appointment booking.

 

The following fields in this screen can be used for group consultation:

  • Secondary Resources – The secondary resources of type Practitioner, if selected during appointment booking will be automatically displayed here.

  • Additional Secondary Resources – Click this button and select additional practitioners if you want to arrange group consultation for the patient.

A minimum of two secondary resources of type 'Practitioner' should be available during Re-Assign Practitioner if the specialty is of type multi-specialty. (Group consultation)

If you try to assign less than two Secondary Resources of type Practitioner, the following error message will be displayed: “At least two secondary resources should be selected”.
