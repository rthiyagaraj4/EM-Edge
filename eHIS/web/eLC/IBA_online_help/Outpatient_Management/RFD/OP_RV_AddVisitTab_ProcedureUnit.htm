Add Visit

Add Visit Tab is used to register a visit for patients visiting the Procedure Unit as an Outpatient. You can directly invoke this tab to register visits for Walk-in Patients with or without referral. This tab also registers visits for patients with appointment or invited patients from waiting list.

Privilege to use this function is defined in the operator station attached to user in the Application Masters module.

 

Case 1: Register Visit for walk-in patient with or without referral

  1. Perform a patient search and select the required patient to register the visit. <Related Field Description>

  2. If the patient is not found while searching, register the patient from the Patient Search screen.

  3. In order to register a visit for a referral patient, perform a Referral Search and select the patient.

  4. If the selected referral patient is not registered in the facility, register the patient.

  5. Patient details are displayed in the Add Visit screen.

  6. Enter details in mandatory fields of the Tab pages. <Related Field Description>

 

Case 2: Register Visit for patients with appointment

Patients with appointment are selected from the Appointment List Tab.

  1. Appointment details of the patient are populated into the Add Visit screen.

  2. Enter details in mandatory fields of the Tab pages. <Related Field Description>

Case 3: Register Visit for invited patients from Waiting List

Invited patients from waiting list are selected from the Invitation List Tab

  1. Details of the patient are populated into the Add Visit screen.

  2. Enter details in mandatory fields of the Tab pages. <Related Field Description>

 

  1. Click [Contact Details] to view or modify the contact details of the patient.

  2. Click [Previous Encounter] to view the previous encounter details of the patient.

  3. Click [Apply] to register the visit or click [Reset] to clear the entered data.

  4. Record Patient File Details if file management parameters are set for the facility. <Related Field Description>

  5. Click [OK] to generate file number or click [Cancel] to cancel file generation.

  6. If internal reports are defined then Internal Reports screen appears, click [OK] to generate Internal Reports or click [Cancel] to exit from the screen.

  7. Patient Charge Details screen is displayed showing the charges applicable for the patient. Click [OK] or [Cancel] to proceed.

  8. Encounter ID, Patient File Number and Visit Charge is generated successfully on registering a visit.
