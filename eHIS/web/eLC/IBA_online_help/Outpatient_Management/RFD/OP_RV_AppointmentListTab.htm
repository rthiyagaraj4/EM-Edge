Appointment List

Appointment List tab is applicable if the Appointment Scheduling module is installed. This tab lists all the Outpatients with Appointments and Visit not registered.

  1. Click [Book Appointment] to book an appointment for a patient and generate the Appointment Reference Number.

  2. On generating the Appointment Reference Number, the appointment details of the patient is displayed in the result area. Click [Clear] to reset the records displayed in the result area.

Or

  1. Click [Search] to display all the patient records with Appointment Reference Number or enter the search criteria and click [Search] to search for a specific patient with an appointment. Click [Clear] to reset the records displayed in the result area.

  2. On searching, the appointment details are displayed based on the searched criteria. If a patient is booked under ‘VIP’ category, then icon is displayed near the Patient Name.

  3. Click ‘+’ hyperlink to view the Appointment Details. Click [Contact Details] to display the contact details of the patient, [Secondary Resources] to view the details of secondary resources or [Close] to exit from the Appointment Details screen.

  4. Click ‘Appt No’ hyperlink to display patient details in Add Visit Screen.

  5. If a patient is not registered within the facility while booking an Appointment, on selecting the patient record for Registering a Visit, ‘Enter Patient ID’ screen is displayed to register the patient. Click [Register Patient] to register the patient.

  6. The details of the patient are displayed in Add Visit screen.
