Setup Payers by Billing Group

From this function you can Associate the Payers to the Billing Group and Query for the existing  records.

The function Setup Payers By Billing Group is used to associate Payers to the Billing Group. The selected Billing Group associated to the Payers is relevant for the Facility and the associated Patient Class. The associated Payers only can be associated to the patients during registration or admission on selecting the respective Billing Group.

Setup Payers by Billing Group

From the Masters, double-click on Setup Payers by Billing Group function.

The Setup Payers by Billing Group screen gets displayed.

  1. Enter the details in the mandatory (*) fields. <Related Field Description>

  2. Click [Commit Form] or press [F10] to save a created record or click [Clear Record] to clear the entered or queried data.

  3. Click [Execute Query] or press [F8] to search for an existing record within the facility.
