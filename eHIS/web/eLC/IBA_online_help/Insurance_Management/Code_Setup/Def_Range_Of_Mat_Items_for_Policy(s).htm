Define Range of Material Items for Policy(s)

From this function you can Create an association of multiple policies to a range of material items and Query for any record.                              

The function Define Range of Services for Material Items for Policy(s) is used to associate range of material items to multiple policies in the facility. You also have an option to delete the material items associated to the policies, if they are no longer a part of the policy coverage. You can specify the coverage amount for an Outpatient and Inpatient using the material items associated to the Policy. You are able to view the material items associated to a range of policies.

Define Range of Material Items for Policy(s)

From the Masters, double-click on Define Range of Material Items for Policy(s) function.

The Define Range of Material Items for Policy(s) screen gets displayed.

  1. Select an Action Type from the list. You can either associate or dissociate the material items from a set of policies based on the Action Type selected.

  2. Select a value for ‘ Range of Services to be Copied or Deleted ’ . Select Inclusion/Exclusion to either associate or dissociate additional material items to a set of policies. Select Service Coverage Limits to define the coverage limit for the material items applicable to the patient class in the facility.

  3. Enter the details in the mandatory (*) fields under ‘ Range of Items to be Copied/Deleted for Inclusion/Exclusion ’ . You can enter the details on selecting the value as Inclusion/Exclusion for Range of Material Items to be Copied or Deleted. <Related Field Description>

  4. Enter the details in the mandatory (*) fields under ‘ Range of Items to be Copied/Deleted for Service Coverage Limits ’ . You can enter the details on selecting the value as Service Coverage Limits  for  Range of Services to be Copied or Deleted. <Related Field Description>

  5. Select a facility from the list. The range of items selected for the policies or the service coverage limit defined for the services is valid for the selected facility. This is a mandatory field.

  6. Select a range of policies from the list. <Related Field Description>

  7. Click [Display Records] to display the list of policies between the range selected.

  8. A confirmation message gets displayed on the screen based on the parameters selected to copy the service details or define the coverage limit for the services.

  9. Click [Commit Form] or press [F10] to save a created record or click [Clear Record] to clear the entered or queried data.
