Enter the following details to adjust the amount:

 

  1. Blng Group – The Billing Groups associated to the patient during admission is displayed.

  2. Payer – The Payer associated to the patient during admission is displayed. The description of the respective Payer is displayed in the text box below.

  3. Policy Type – The Policy Type associated to the patient during admission is displayed.

  4. Gross Amt – The Gross Amount to be paid by the Payer for the Service is displayed. If you have already adjusted the amount, the gross amount is displayed after deducting the amount adjusted in the previous transaction.

  5. Disc Amt – The Discount Amount for the respective Payer, Payer Group and Policy Type combination is displayed.

  6. Net Amt – The Net Amount for the respective Payer, Payer Group and Policy Type combination is displayed. If you have already adjusted the amount, the net amount is displayed after deducting the amount adjusted in the previous transaction.

  7. CR/DR – Select a value from the list. The system-defined values are CR (Credit) and DR (Debit). Select CR to credit the amount to patient from Payer or DR to debit the amount from patient to Payer.

  8. Gross Adjustment – Enter the Gross Adjustment amount in numeric format.

The gross amount has to be lesser than or equal to the actual gross amount.

The discount amount has to be lesser than or equal to the actual gross amount.

The total gross adjustment amount has to be zero.

  1. New Gross Amt – The New Gross Amount is displayed based on the Gross Adjustment Amount, Net Amount and the value selected for CR/DR. For Example, if the Gross Adjustment Amount is 100, Net Amt is 50 and the value is selected as CR, the Net Gross Amt is displayed as 150.

  2. Disc Adjustment – Enter the discount amount in numeric format. The discount amount has to be lesser than or equal to the actual gross amount.

  3. New Net Amt – The New Net amount is displayed as the difference of New Gross Amount and Discount Adjustment, that is (New Gross Amt - Disc Adjustment = New Net Amt).

  4. Total – The sum of gross amount, discount amount, net amount, gross adjustment, new gross amount, discount adjustment and new net amount is displayed.
