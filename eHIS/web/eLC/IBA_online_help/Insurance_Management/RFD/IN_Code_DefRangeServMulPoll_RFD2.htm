Enter the following details to define the coverage limit for the services:

 

  1. Service Coverage Limit Criteria - Select a value from the list. The system defined values are ‘Billing Service’, ‘Enterprise Service Group’ and ‘Enterprise Service Group/Line’. Select Billing Services to select and associate a set of Billing Services to the range of Policies in the facility. Select Enterprise Service Group to select and associate a set of Service Groups to the range of Policies in the facility. Select Enterprise Service Group/Line to select and associate a set of Service Groups/Lines to the range of Policies in the facility.

  2. Override Coverage Limits – Select the check box to override the coverage limits for the Billing Services/Enterprise Service Groups/Enterprise Service Group/Lines. The override performs inverse action based on the value selected for ‘Excl/Incl Ind’.

  3. Billing Service [From-To] - Click [List of Values] to view and to select a range of Billing Services/Enterprise Service Groups/Enterprise Service Groups/Lines. The selected range of services or service groups are either included or excluded from service coverage.

  4. Patient Class – Click [List of Values] to select a Patient Class. The selected service or service groups are either excluded or included towards service coverage for the selected Patient Class.

  5. Amt Per Visit – Enter the coverage amount for an Outpatient visit to the facility. The Insurance Company pays the amount specified towards an Outpatient visit for the selected services/service groups associated to the Policy Type.

  6. Amt Per Admission - Enter the coverage amount for an Inpatient admission to the facility. The Insurance Company pays the amount specified towards an Inpatient admission for the selected services/service groups associated to the Policy Type.

  7. Per Inpatient Day – Enter the coverage amount for an Inpatient's stay in the facility per day. The amount set is specific only to the Inpatients admitted to the facility.

  8. No of Days – Enter the number of days for which the coverage amount is applicable towards the Inpatient stay in the facility.
