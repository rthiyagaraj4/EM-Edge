Query Policy Details

From this function you can Query the policy details to view the service coverage and material item coverage details.                                                                 

The Query Policy Details function is used to display the policy details recorded using the function Setup Service Coverage Detail by Policy and Setup Material Item Coverage Detail by Policy. Click [Execute Query] to view the policy definition. You can only view the details of a selected policy and cannot update the contents.

Query Policy Details

From the Queries, double-click on Query Policy Details function.

The Query Policy Details screen gets displayed.

  1. Click [Execute Query] or press [F8]. The policy definition for the selected combination gets displayed. You can only view, but cannot modify the details.

OR

  1. Click [Enter Query] or press [F7] to enter the details and to search for a specific record.
