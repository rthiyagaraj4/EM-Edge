Enter the following details to view the records of the patients in the respective Nursing Unit:

 

  1. Patient Name – The name of the patients admitted in the ward gets displayed.

  2. Patient ID – The ID of the respective patient is displayed. The Patient ID is generated  during patient registration in the Master Patient Index module.

  3. Diet Type – The Diet Types of the patients recorded during ‘Place Diet Order’ get displayed.

  4. Status – Select a Status from the list to change the status of an individual patient record. The system-defined values are Meal Prepared, Meal Served, Meal Received and Meal Rejected. You can acknowledge or document the complaint against the status selected.

  5. Date Time – Click the Calendar function to select the date and time. This indicates the date and time when the complaint is recorded.
