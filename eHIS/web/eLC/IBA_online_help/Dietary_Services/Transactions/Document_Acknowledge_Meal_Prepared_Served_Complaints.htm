Document/Acknowledge Meal Prepared/Served/Complaints

From this function you can Record complaints on the meals prepared and served or Acknowledge the meals prepared or served from the kitchen.   

The Document/Acknowledge Meal Prepared/Served/Complaints function helps to acknowledge completion of food preparation, confirmation on meals served and to document the Complaints received on the meal served.

Documenting Complaints or Acknowledging Meals prepared and served

From the Transactions, select the Document/Acknowledge Meal Prepared/Served/Complaints function.

The Document/Acknowledge Meal Prepared/Served/Complaints screen gets displayed.

  1. Click [Create] to invoke the Create screen.

Meal Type

The Meal Type tab details are displayed, by default.

 

Enter the following details in the Meal Type tab:

  1. Date – Click Calendar function to select a date to acknowledge the meal prepared and served or record complaints on meal prepared, meal received, meal served or meal rejected. The current date is displayed, by default.

  2. Meal Type – Select the Meal Type from the list. The values defined using the ‘Meal Type’ function get listed. This is a mandatory field.

  3. Nursing Unit – Select the Nursing Unit from the list. Nursing Units are defined in Inpatient and Day Care Management module. This is a mandatory field.

Click [Search] to view the records of the patients in the respective Nursing Unit or [Cancel] to cancel the entry.

  1. Change Status of all patients to – Select an appropriate value from the list. List contains four values namely, Meal Prepared, Meal Received, Meal Served and Meal Rejected. On selecting a status from the list, you can acknowledge the change of status for all the patients in the ward. To update the status of the individual patient record, you can manually select the status from the list.

  2. Patient Name – The name of the patients associated with the ward is displayed. This field is not editable.

  3. Patient ID – The ID of the respective patient is displayed. The Patient ID is generated at the time of patient registration in the Patient Identity  Management module. You cannot modify the Patient ID.

  4. Diet Type – The Diet Type associated with the Meal Plan for the relevant Nursing Unit is displayed. This field is not editable.

  5. Status – The values – Meal Prepared, Meal Served, Meal Received and Meal Reject is system defined. Select a Status from the list. User can acknowledge or document the complaint against the Status selected.

  6. Date Time – System date and time is displayed, by default. Click the calendar function to select a date and time. This identifies the date and time when the complaint is acknowledged or documented. The date and time has to be lesser than or equal to the current system date and time.

  7. Complaints – Click the Complaints hyperlink to record a complaint. The screen as shown is displayed on clicking the hyperlink.

    Enter the following details:

    1. Complaints – Select a value from the list. The values are listed from the Complaints function defined in the DS Code Setup. This helps in identifying the reason for criticism for the food items supplied by the facility.

    2. Remarks – The Remarks for the selected complaint is displayed. Additional remarks can be included to provide detailed information about the food items received or consumed.

 

Click [OK] to document or acknowledge the Meals prepared, received, served or rejected with the Complaints (if any). Click [Cancel] to cancel the entry.

Special Food Items

  • Frequency Based

You can document complaint or acknowledge special food items. This is used if special food items are recorded for a patient to be given in regular time interval.

Enter the following details to create Document complaint or Acknowledge for Special Food Items prepared and served:

  1. Date – Click the Calendar function to select the date and document complaint or acknowledge special food items prepared and served on a frequency basis. This is a mandatory field.

  2. Patient ID – Enter the Patient ID with the field length as specified in MP Parameter or click [?] function to select a patient. Patients are registered in Master Patient Index  module.

  3. Nursing Unit – Select the Nursing Unit from the list. The values are listed from the Nursing Unit function defined in the Inpatient Management module. This is a mandatory field.

Click [Search] to view patient records in the respective Nursing Unit. Click [Cancel] to cancel the entry.

  1. Change Status of all patients to – Select an appropriate value from the list. List contains four values namely, Meal Prepared, Meal Received, Meal Served and Meal Rejected. On selecting a status from the list, you can acknowledge the status change for all the patients in a ward. To update the status of an individual patient record, you can manually select the status from the list.

  2. Patient Name – The name of the patients associated to the ward is displayed.

  3. Patient ID – The ID of the patient is displayed. The patient ID is generated at the time of patient registration in the Master Patient Index module.

  4. Food Item – The Food Item associated with the Meal Plan for the respective patient is displayed.

  5. Item Qty – The Item Quantity associated with the Meal Plan for the respective patient is displayed.

  6. Schedule Time – The Schedule Time recorded for food consumption is displayed.

  7. Status - Select a Status from the list. The system-defined values are Meal Prepared, Meal Served, Meal Received and Meal Rejected. You can acknowledge or document the complaint against the Status selected.

  8. Date Time – System date and time is displayed, by default. Click the calendar function to select the date and time. This identifies the date and time when the complaint is acknowledged or documented.

If you record the date and time more than the cut-off time defined in ‘DS Parameter for Facility’ function, an alert message ‘Acknowledge Date Time should not be greater than XXX’ is displayed.

=> If you record the date and time less than the event date and time, an alert  message ‘Acknowledge Date Time should not be less than event date time’ Is displayed.

=> If the cut-off time is not defined in ‘DS Parameter for Facility’ function and if the date and time is not equal to the event date and time, an alert message  ‘Acknowledge Date should be equal to Event Date’ Is displayed.

  1. Complaints – Click the Complaints hyperlink to record a complaint.

  • Non Frequency Based

You can use non-frequency based to record complaint or acknowledge special food items, if there no time schedule for food consumption.

Enter the following to record complaint or acknowledge special food items:

  1. Date – Click the Calendar function to select the date and document complaint or acknowledge special food items prepared and served on a non-frequency basis. This is a mandatory field.

  2. Patient ID – Enter the Patient ID with the field length as specified in MP Parameter or click [?] function to select a patient. Patients are registered in Master Patient Index module.

  3. Nursing Unit – Select the Nursing Unit from the list. The values are listed from the Nursing Unit function defined in the Inpatient & Day Care Management module. This is a mandatory field.

Click [Search] to view patient records in the respective Nursing Unit. Click [Cancel] to cancel the entry.

  1. Change Status of all patients to – Select an appropriate value from the list. List contains four values namely, Meal Prepared, Meal Received, Meal Served and Meal Rejected. On selecting a status from the list, you can acknowledge the status change for all the patients in the ward. To update the status of the individual patient record, select the status from the list.

  2. Patient Name – The name of the patients associated to the ward is displayed.

  3. Patient ID – The ID of the patient is displayed. The patient ID is generated at the time of patient registration in the Master Patient Index module.

  4. Meal Type – The recorded Meal Type of the patient is displayed.  

  5. Food Item – The Food Item associated with the Meal Plan for the relevant patient is displayed. Item Qty – The Item Quantity associated with the Meal Plan for the relevant patient is displayed.

  6. Status – Select a Status from the list. The system-defined values are Meal Prepared, Meal Served, Meal Received and Meal Rejected. You can acknowledge or document the complaint against the Status selected.

  7. Date Time – System date and time is displayed, by default. Click the calendar function to select a date and time. This identifies the date and time when the complaint is acknowledged or documented. The date and time has to be lesser than or equal to the current system date and time.

  8. Complaints – Click the Complaints hyperlink to record a complaint.

    Enter the following details:

    1. Complaints – Select a value from the list. The values are listed from the Complaints function defined in the DS Code Setup. This helps in identifying the reason for criticism for the food items supplied by the facility.

    2. Remarks – The Remarks for the selected complaint is displayed. Additional remarks can be included to provide detailed information about the food items received or consumed.

  1. Click [Apply] to record the details or [Reset] to clear the entered details.

The recorded details are displayed in the patient chart in the Clinician Access module. You can view the patient’s diet in the last 24 hours under the heading ‘Patient Diet in Last 24 Hours’. This information is useful to place an order for the current diet type or modify an existing Diet Order.
