Modify Meal for a Patient

From this function you can Update a meal plan generated for an Inpatient on a particular date.

The Modify Meal for a Patient function is used to review and update the meal plan generated for a particular patient. The dietitian can add or remove the items from the generated meal plan. The Item Types, Items and the Serving Quantity associated to the Meal Type can be modified using this function. You can add   Food Items to the selected Meal Plan of a patient. You can define the value as zero.

Modifying a generated meal plan for a patient

From the Transactions, select the Modify Meal for a Patient function.

The Modify Meal for a Patient screen gets displayed.

  1. Click [Create] to invoke the Create screen.

  2. Enter the details in the mandatory (*) fields. <Related Field Description>

  3. Click [Search] to search for the meal plan generated for the specific patient encounter.

  4. You can associate items to a Meal Plan of a patient. The items associated to the meal plan are displayed on the right hand side of the screen. The Serial Numbers are displayed as a hyperlink.

  5. Click on the Serial Number to modify the respective item. The Serial Number, Item Type, Item and Serving quantity gets displayed in the respective fields.  

  6. You can update serving quantity of the respective item.  

  7. Select the check box ‘Don’t Serve’, if the items associated to the Meal Plan should not be served to the patient. If the ‘Don’t Serve’ check box is selected, you cannot modify the serving quantity of an item. Clear the check box to serve the items to the patient while serving the meal. If the ‘Don’t Serve’ check box is cleared, you can edit the serving quantity of the items.

  8. Click [Select] to update the details. Click [Cancel] to cancel the changes made.

  9. Click [Apply] to record the changes to the Meal Plan of the respective patient or [Reset] to clear the entered details.

You can only update the Serving Quantity of an item. The entry in the Serial Number, Item Type and Item fields are not editable.
