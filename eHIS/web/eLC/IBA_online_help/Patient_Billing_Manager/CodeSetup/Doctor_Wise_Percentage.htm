Doctor Wise Percentage

From this function you can Define the revenue share for the services provided by the Practitioners for each episode. You can Query to view the existing Doctor Wise Percentage details.

Doctor Wise Percentage is used to define revenue share for the services provided by the Practitioners for each Inpatient and Outpatient episode.

Defining Doctor Wise Percentage

From the Masters > Other Masters, double-click Doctor Wise Percentage function.

The Doctor Wise Percentage screen gets displayed.

  1. Enter the details in the mandatory (*) fields. <Related Field Description>

  2. Click [Commit Form] or press [F10] to save or [Clear Record] to clear the entered data.

  3. Click [Execute Query] or press [F8] to search for the existing records within the facility.
