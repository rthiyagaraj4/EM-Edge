Modify Specific Online Legends

From this function you can Define the online report legends for Review Document Number and ISO document number. You can Query to view the online legends defined..

Modify Specific Online Legends function is used to setup the Review Document Number and ISO document number. These details are printed in the footer section of the billing reports.

Defining Online Report Legends

From the Masters > Other Masters, double-click Modify Specific Online Legends function.

The Modify Specific Online Legends screen gets displayed.

  1. Enter the details in the mandatory (*) fields. <Related Field Description>

  2. Click [Commit Form] or press [F10] to save or [Clear Record] to clear the entered data.

  3. Click [Execute Query] or press [F8] to search for the existing records within the facility.
