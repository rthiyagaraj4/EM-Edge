Employer/Insurance Companies

From this function you can Create an Employer and the Insurance Companies pertinent to the Facility. You can Query to view the existing Employers and Insurance Company's records.

Employer/Insurance Companies function is used to define the Employer or an Insurance Company and associate it to a Billing Group pertinent to the Facility. The Employer or the Insurance Company settles a part of the patient bill amount when the patient either associated to the Employer or holding an Insurance Policy from the Insurance Company is admitted to the Facility.

Creating Employer/Insurance Company

From the Masters > Banks/Companies, double-click on Employer/Insurance Co function.

The Employer/Insurance Co screen gets displayed.

  1. Enter the details in the mandatory (*) fields. <Related Field Description>

  2. Click [Commit Form] or press [F10] to save or [Clear Record] to clear the entered data.

  3. Click [Associate Doctor’s] to display the Employer / Insurance Co screen.

    Enter the following details to define and associate the Doctor with Employer:

    1. Employer – Click [List of Values] to select the employer. The Employers defined in the Employer/Insurance Co function are listed.

    2. Doctor – Enter the Doctor ID in the alphanumeric format. This is mandatory field.

    3. Name – Enter the name of the Doctor in character format of the 30 characters maximum length. This is mandatory field.

    4. Valid [From-To] – Select the Valid date range using the calendar function. You can define the validity period of the doctor in the facility. The doctor is employed by the employer and works in the hospital for the selected valid date range. This is mandatory field.

  4. Click [Execute Query] or press [F8] to view the existing records within the facility.

The Associate Doctor’s button is applicable only for Employer and is disabled if the Insurance check box selected.
