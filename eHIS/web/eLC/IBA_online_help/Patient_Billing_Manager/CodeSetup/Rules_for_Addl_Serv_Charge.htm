Rule for Additional Service Charge

From this function you can Define additional charge rules pertinent to the facility and associate the additional charge rules to the Billing Groups and the Billing Services. You can Query to view the existing service charge rules.

Rule for Additional Service Charge function is used to define the rule to charge the service tax on the  services utilized by the patient from the facility. You can define the various service charge rules used in a facility. The service charges defined are associated to either the Billing Services and the Billing Groups valid in the facility. The service can be defined as applicable on the net amount, the gross amount, main rule or the sub rule. You can define a charge rule as the main service charge rule and associate sub service charge rules to it.

Defining Rules for Additional Charges

From the Masters > Billing Rule, double-click on Rules for Additional Service Charge function.

The Rules for Additional Service Charge screen gets displayed.

  1. Enter the details in the mandatory (*) fields found in the following tab pages.

Additional Service Charge Rule

Addl Service Charge By Billing Services

Addl Service Charges By Billing Group

  1. Click [Commit Form] or press [F10] to save or [Clear Record] to clear the entered data.
