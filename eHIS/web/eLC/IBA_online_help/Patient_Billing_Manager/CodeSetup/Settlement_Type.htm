Settlement Types

From this function you can Define the settlement modes for the patient categories within the facility. You can Associate the Settlement Types to the General Ledger Interface Control Account Codes. You can Query to view the Settlement Types defined.

The Settlement Types identify the mode of payments supported by the Facility. Patient or a Customer can choose from the Settlement Types to make a payment towards the service availed. Settlement Types defined are valid for a patient category within the Facility. A Settlement Type is restricted to use by either the Cashier or the Accounts personnel. A direct mode of transfer called the Bank Transfer is also supported using which you can transfer the amount to the facility’s account. Patient/Customer has an option to choose from the below Settlement Types to settle the bill amount:

  1. Cash

  2. Cheque

  3. Credit Card

  4. Autopay

  5. Others

Defining Settlement Types

From the Masters > Accounting Entity Setup, double-click Settlement Types function.

The Settlement Type screen gets displayed.

  1. Enter the details in the mandatory (*) fields found in the following Tab pages:

Settlement Type

Interface to GL

  1. Click [Interface to GL] to associate the Settlement Type to the Control Account Codes in General Ledger.

  2. Click [Commit Form] or press [F10] to save or [Clear Record] to clear the entered data.

  3. Click [Execute Query] or press [F8] to search for the existing records within the facility.
