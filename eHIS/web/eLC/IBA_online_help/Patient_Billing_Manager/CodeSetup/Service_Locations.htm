Service Locations

From this function you can Define the Service Locations pertinent to the Facility. You can Query to view the existing Service Location.

A Service Location identifies the individual locations in the Facility, where the services are rendered. Patients are directed to specific locations for treatment during registration or admission. Patient is diagnosed in the respective Service Location and further treatment is provided based on the severity of sickness. Multiple locations are associated to a facility. Using this function, you can modify the descriptions for a Service Location. Billing personnel at the respective Service Location associates the services to the patient.

Defining Service Locations

From the Masters > Facility Specific Setup, double-click Service Locations function.

The Service Locations screen gets displayed.

  1. Enter the details in the mandatory (*) fields. <Related Field Description>

  2. Click [Commit Form] or press [F10] to save or [Clear Record] to clear the entered data.

  3. Click [Execute Query] or press [F8] to search for the existing records within the facility.
