Cash Counter Logout

From this function you can Logout of the inpatient Cash Counter at the end of the shift. You can Query to view the name of the Cashier logged into the relevant Cash Counter.  

Cash Counter Logout function is used to logout of the Inpatient Cash Counter at the end of the shift. Cashier can only logout of the Cash Counter, but cannot view the Cash Collection details. A subsequent Cashier cannot login to Cash Counter at the beginning of the next shift, if the preceding Cashier does not logout of the Cash Counter at the end of the shift. A Cashier has to logout of the Cash Counter and re-login when a shift has exhausted.

Logout from the Inpatient Cash Counter

From the Transactions > IP Cashier, double-click Cash Counter Logout Function.

The Cash Counter Logout screen gets displayed.

  1. Click [Execute Query] or press [F8] to list the open Cash Counters.

  2. The Cash Counter Code, description for the selected Cash Counter and the name of the logged-in User gets displayed.

  3. The ‘In use, OP/EM, IP and Ext’ check boxes are selected or cleared based on the parameters defined for the selected Cash Counter during creation.

  4. The date and time when the User had logged-in to the Inpatient Cash Counter gets displayed.

  5. Click [Select All] to select and to logout from all the Cash Counters to which the Users have logged in.

  6. Select the check box ‘Select’ to logout from the selected Inpatient Cash Counters.

  7. Click [Clear Record] to clear the entered data.

 

To enable the Cash Counter Logout function, the check box ‘Logout Cash Counter with Closing Bal. Details’ check box has to be cleared under the ‘Cash Counter Processing’ tab in the ‘BL Parameters’ function.
