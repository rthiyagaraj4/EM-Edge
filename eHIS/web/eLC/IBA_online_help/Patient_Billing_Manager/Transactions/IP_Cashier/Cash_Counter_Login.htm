Cash Counter Login

From this function you can Login to an inpatient Cash Counter at the beginning of the shift. You can also View the Logged in User/Counter details using this function.  

Cash Counter Login function is used to login to the Inpatient Cash Counter at the beginning of the shift to perform billing transactions related to Inpatients. The privilege rights for the respective Cashier have to be defined in the Administration menu. The Cash Counter should have access to process receipts, so the Cashier can generate receipts as well at the respective Cash Counter level. The Cashier logged into the Cash Counter has to logout from the Cash Counter at the end of the shift. If the previous Cashier has not logged out, the succeeding Cashier cannot login to the Cash Counter. The Shift Timing for an Inpatient Cash Counter is defined in the BL Parameters function under the Administration menu. The Cash Counters defined for the patient class and the Settlement Types associated to the Cash Counters in the masters get listed. The tab Logged In User/Counter Details displays the details on the Cashiers who have logged into the selected Cash Counters.

Login to Inpatient Cash Counter

From the Transactions > IP Cashier, double-click Cash Counter Login Function.

The Cash Counter Login screen gets displayed.

  1. Click [List of Values] to select a Cash Counter. The Cash Counter codes defined using the function Cash Counter gets listed. This is a mandatory field.

  2. The IP Allowed, OP/Emerg. Allowed and External Allowed check boxes are selected or cleared based on the parameters defined while creating the Cash Counter.

  3. The Shift Start At field is disabled and you cannot enter the date and time. The system date and time is considered as the shift start time, by default.

  4. The name of the logged in User gets displayed in the ‘Shift Started By’ field. You cannot update the name.

  5. Entering the Opening Balance in numeric format. The Opening Balance indicates the amount in the Cash Counter during the beginning of the shift.

  6. Click [Commit Form] or press [F10] to login to the Inpatient Cash Counter or [Clear Record] to clear the data.

  7. Click Logged In User/Counter Details tab to view the Cashier details who have currently logged in to various Cash Counters. The Cash Counter Code, description for the selected Cash Counter, logged-in User name and the login date and time are displayed.

 

The ‘IP/DY Billing enabled’ check box has to be selected under the tab ‘Reports’ under ‘General’ tab in the ‘BL Parameters’ function to define the Cash Counter shift timings.
