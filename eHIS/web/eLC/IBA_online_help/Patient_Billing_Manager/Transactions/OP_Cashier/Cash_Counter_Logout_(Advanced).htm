Cash Counter Logout (Advanced)

From this function you can Logout from the Outpatient Cash Counter at the end of the shift. You can View the Cash Collection report and the Cashier journal for the day.

Cash Counter Logout (Advanced) function is used to logout of the Outpatient Cash Counter at the end of the shift. A subsequent Cashier cannot login to Cash Counter at the beginning of the next shift, if the preceding Cash Counter user does not logout of the Cash Counter at the end of the shift. Cashier is able to view the Settlement Type, the total amount cancelled, received, settled, customer payable amount and the patient payable amount. The opening balance at the beginning of the shift and the closing balance at the end of the shift are also displayed. A Cash delivery form is generated at the end of the shift of every Cash Counter listing the transaction details made by the Cashier during the respective shift. The total amount collected for each mode of settlement is listed separately. The deposit amount collected during the shift is displayed separately along with the receipt number. Two reports namely, Cash Delivery Form Report and Deposit Collection report gets generated while the Cashier logs out from the Cash Counter. A Cash Delivery Form report gets generated when the Cashier logs out from the Cash Counter listing the transactions and the Cash collection details based on each Settlement Type. The report displays the deposit amount collected against the receipt type generated for a Credit Card and a Cash transaction. A Senior Cashier overseeing the Cash Counter transactions and the Cashier handing the transaction report at the end of the shift has to sign off on the report generated, to confirm the Cash Counter transactions at the end of the day.  During the deposit adjustment, the receipts are cancelled and the deposit adjustment transaction is reversed. A Cash delivery report is printed as an online report

Cash Counter Logout (Advanced)

From the Transactions > OP Cashier, double-click Cash Counter Logout (Advanced) Function.

The Cash Counter Logout (Advanced) screen gets displayed.

  1. Click [List of Values] to select an Outpatient Cash Counter.

  2. Enter the details in the mandatory (*) fields. <Related Field Description>

  3. Click [Close Counter] to logout or [Clear Record] to clear the data.

  4. Click [Denomination]. The Denomination screen gets displayed.

    1. Currency Value – System displays the currency value.

    2. Number Of Count - Enter the number of currency.

    3. Actual Amount – System displays the actual amount. The actual amount is derived from the currency value and the number of count.

    4. Total Denomination Amt – System displays the  actual amount total. The total denomination amount should not exceed the cash closing balance. This is a non-editable field.

    5. Shortage Amt – System displays the shortage amount. This is a non-editable field.

    6. Total Closing Bal – System displays the total closing balance. This is a non-editable field.

To enable the Cash Counter Logout (Advanced) function, the check box ‘Logout Cash Counter with Closing Bal. Details’ has to be selected in the ‘BL Parameters’.
