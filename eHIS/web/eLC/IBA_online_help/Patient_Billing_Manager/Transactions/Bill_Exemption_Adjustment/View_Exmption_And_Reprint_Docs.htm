View Exemption and Reprint Documents

From this function you can View or Reprint the exemption or bill details of a patient.  

View Exemption and Reprint Documents function is used to view the exemption details of a relevant patient. The details recorded through the function ‘Bill Exemption’ are displayed. You can print exemption document, exemption approval or exemption cancellation document through this function.

Viewing Exemption Details and Reprinting Documents

From the Transactions > Bill Exemption/Adjustment, double-click View Exemption and Reprint Documents Function.

The View Exemption and Reprint Documents screen gets displayed.

  1. Enter the Patient ID in alphanumeric format of 10 characters maximum length or click [List of Values] to select a Patient ID.. The name of the patient, gender and age are displayed on entering the Patient ID.

  2. Click [List of Values] to select a document range. The documents generated through the 'Bill exemption' function are listed.

  3. Click [List of Values] to select a document date range. The exemption documents generated during the selected period are listed.  

  4. Click [Search] to query for the exemption records matching the search criteria.

  5. Click [Search] to query all the exemption records without specifying the search criteria.

  6. The exemption and bill details are displayed.

    The following details are displayed:

    1. Document No - The document type and number against which the bill amount is exempted is displayed.

    2. Doc. Date - The date when the bill is generated for the services utilized by the patient is displayed.

    3. Episode Type - The Episode type of the patient in the facility is displayed.

    4. Episode ID - The Episode ID of the patient is displayed.

    5. Visit ID - The Visit ID of the patient is displayed.

    6. Billing Grp - The Billing Group assigned to the patient during admission or registration is displayed.

    7. Cust Code - The Customer associated to the patient, if any is displayed.

    8. Adjust/Exempt Amt - The bill amount that is either adjusted or exempted through the functions 'Bill Adjustment' or 'Bill Exemption' are displayed.

    9. Billing Group - The description of the Billing Group associated to the patient is displayed.

    10. Customer - The description of the Customer associated to the patient is displayed.

    The following details are displayed:

    1. Patient No - The patient numbers that match the search criteria are displayed.

    2. Document No - The document type and number that is generated for the respective patient exemption transaction is displayed.

    3. Doc Date - The date when the bill is exempted through the 'Bill Exemption' function is displayed.

    4. Document Ref - The document reference recorded through the 'Bill Exemption' function is displayed.

    5. Date - The date when the bill is exempted through the 'Bill Exemption' function is displayed.

    6. Reason - The reason selected while exempting the bill through 'Bill Exemption' function is displayed. The long description of the selected Reason is displayed.

    7. Status - The status is displayed as 'Valid' or 'Cancelled' based on the whether the bill is exempted or cancelled.

    8. Created By - The name of the authorized user who has exempted the bill is displayed.

    9. Cancelled By - The name of the authorized user who has cancelled the bill exemption is displayed.

  7. Right-click on 'Document No' or 'Doc Date' columns to print Exemption Document, Exemption Approval document or the Exemption Cancellation document to print various documents. Exemption Cancellation is available for selection only if the bill exemption is cancelled.
