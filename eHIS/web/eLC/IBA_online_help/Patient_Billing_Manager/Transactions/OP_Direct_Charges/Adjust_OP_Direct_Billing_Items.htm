Adjust OP Direct Billing Items

From this function you can Modify the service details associated to an Outpatient, Emergency patient or an External patient. You can Update the total quantity of the specified service. The amount is updated based on the quantity of service used.

Adjust OP Direct Billing Items function is used to modify the quantity of the Billing Services associated to an Outpatient, Emergency patient or an External patient. The user can also modify the Unit of Measure associated to the patient details. The charge towards the service is calculated based on the quantity of service associated. The updated service details and the respective charge are considered for the service during bill generation. An authorized User is only permitted to update the service details. The authorization to update the service details is defined using the function ‘Users for Services’ in the Administration menu by selecting the check box ‘Adjust Confirmed Service’. The user can update only the self-entered services or all the services, based on the authorization set.

Adjust OP Direct Billing Items

From the Transactions > OP Direct Charges, double-click Cancel OP Direct Billing Items.

The Cancel OP Direct Billing Items screen gets displayed.

  1. Select an Episode Type from the list. The system defined values are Inpatient and Daycare. Select Inpatient to adjust the services associated to an Inpatient admitted to the facility and Daycare patient to update the services associated to a Daycare patient.

  2. Enter the details in the mandatory (*) fields. <Related Field Description>

OR

  1. Right click on Right Click for User Options to search for the relevant patient record. You can search for a patient record based on the Patients Lookup, Patient/Episode/Visit lookup or Episode/Visit lookup.

  2. Select the check box View Billing to view the billing details.

    The financial details of the patient for the selected episode get displayed. You can also view the unconfirmed services associated to the patient by clicking [Unconfirmed Services]. The Settlement Details, Billing Group details, service charges, gross amount for the services utilized, adjustments or refunds issued, unbilled and outstanding amount get displayed. The name of the User, who has recorded or modified the financial details gets displayed.

  3. The service details get populated in the respective fields on selecting the transaction reference ID to update the service details. <Related Field Description>

  4. Click [Edit] to update the details. The Edit/Enter Charge Details  screen gets displayed with the details populated. Update the Billing Services or a Billing Panel details associated to the patient.

  5. Click [Commit Form] to save the changes to the service details or [Clear Record] to clear the entered data.
