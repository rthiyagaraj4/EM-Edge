Enter Requests – OP

From this function you can Associate billing services or billing panels to an Outpatient, an External patient or an Emergency patient.

Enter Requests – OP function is used to associate billing services to an Outpatient, Emergency patient or an External patient in the facility. A transaction reference ID is generated for each service associated with the patient. An authorized User has to associate the services to the Outpatient. The services defined with the ‘Direct Entry Allowed’ check box as selected are listed. The service price is based on the Multipricing factor, if the service is rate based. If the service is charge based, the charge defined is calculated. The charges for the services associated are calculated automatically and displayed. You cannot update the charges for the rate based services. The total charge is computed and displayed. The Services with the ‘Autoconfirm’ check box selected during definition are confirmed automatically. If the check box is cleared during definition, the services have to be confirmed manually using the function ‘Confirm Inpatient Service Requests’. During visit conclusion, a bill is generated based on the quantity of service used.

Associating a Service

From the Transactions > OP Direct Charges, double-click Enter Requests – OP.

The Enter Requests – OP screen gets displayed.

  1. Select an Episode Type from the list. The system defined values are Inpatient and Daycare. Select Inpatient to associate services to an Inpatient admitted to the facility and Daycare patient to associate services to a Daycare patient admitted to the facility.

  2. Enter the details in the mandatory (*) fields. <Related Field Description>

OR

  1. Right click on Right Click for User Options to search for the relevant patient record. You can search for a patient record based on the Patients Lookup, Patient/Episode /Visit lookup or Episode/Visit lookup.

  2. Select the check box View Billing to view the billing details.

    The financial details of the patient for the selected episode get displayed. You can also view the unconfirmed services associated to the patient by clicking [Unconfirmed Services]. The Settlement Details, Billing Group details, service charges, gross amount for the services utilized, adjustments or refunds issued, unbilled and outstanding amount get displayed. The name of the User, who has recorded or modified the financial details gets displayed.

  3. Click [Future Order] to view any orders placed for future visits. If any orders are placed for future visits using the external modules, you can view the orders and the details by clicking [Future Order].

  4. Associate the Billing Services or a Billing Panel to the patient. The services or the panels are charged based on the price defined for the Billing Service. <Related Field Description>

  5. Click [Commit Form] to associate the service or [Clear Record] to clear the entered data.

  6. A Transaction Reference ID is generated on successfully associating a service or a panel.
