Statement of Patient

From this function you can Print a report on the patient transactions at the Facility for an appropriate month. A statement is generated for a selected patient.

Statement of Patient function is used to generate a report on the billing transactions for a relevant patient in the facility. A report is generated summarizing the transactions for the specified month. You have an option to print the report including or excluding the outstanding bill details.

Statement of a patient

From the Transactions > OP Billing, double-click Statement of Patient Function.

The Statement of Patient screen gets displayed.

  1. Click [List of Values] to select a Patient Id for whom the statement has to be printed for a specific month. The Lookup screen appears. Click [Execute Query] or press [F8] to query all the patient details and to select a relevant patient ID. Double-click on a specific record to select it.

  2. Click Calendar function to select a month and year. The statement for the selected patient on the specific month is printed.

  3. Select the check box to print only the outstanding bills associated to the patient ID.

  4. Select the check box if the bill ageing analysis has to be included in the statement. The Cut-Off Details field gets enabled on selecting the ‘Including Bill Ageing Details’ check box.

  5. The ageing cut-off details defined in the BL Parameters function get displayed. You can update the ageing cut-off details.

  6. Select “Summary” or “Detail” from the list. Based on the value selected, either a detailed or a summarized statement is printed for the selected patient.

  7. Click [Run Process] to print the statement. A confirmation screen pops-up. Click [Yes] to print the statement or [No] to cancel. Click [Clear Record] to clear the entered data.
