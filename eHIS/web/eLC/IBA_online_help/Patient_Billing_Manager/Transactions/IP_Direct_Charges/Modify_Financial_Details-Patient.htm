Modify Financial Details – Patient

From this function you can Update the financial details of an Inpatient or a Daycare patient. You can Query to view the updated financial details of the Inpatients or the Daycare patients admitted to the facility.

Modify Financial Details – Patient function is used to update the financial details of an Inpatient or a Daycare patient. The updated financial details are considered while generating a discharge bill for the patient. An authorized User can modify the financial details for an Inpatient or a Daycare patient. Modification of the financial details results in changes in the price or the discount details too for the services availed. The check box ‘Fin. Dtls Change’ has to be selected in the function ‘User Privileges and Cash Counter Access’ under the Administration menu for the User to login and to modify the details. A message is displayed on whether the patient is deceased or an Inpatient.

Modify Financial Details – Patient

From the Transactions > IP Direct Charges, double-click the Modify Financial Details – Patient function.

The Modify Financial Details – Patient screen gets displayed.

  1. Click [Enter Query] and enter a valid Patient Number. The patient name, age, gender, patient registration date and time get displayed on entering the patient number.

OR

  1. Click [Execute Query] or press [F8] to view all the patient details. Press downward arrow key to navigate to the next record and upward arrow key to navigate to the previous record.

OR

  1. Press [F9] to search and to select a patient ID from the lookup  screen. Click [Execute Query] to view all the patient details or click [Enter Query] and enter search criteria. The records matching the search criteria get displayed. Double-click on the record to select it.

  2. Click [Modify] to login and to update the details. The financial details associated to the patient get displayed. <Related Field Description>

  3. Update the details and click [Commit Form] to save the changes or [Clear Record] to clear the details.
