Patient Registration for Prepaid Program

From this function you can Record patient details who are already registered with the Payer for Prepaid Program.

The Patient Registration for Prepaid Program function is used to record patient details who are registered for the Prepaid Program. Patients' who are registered can only avail the prepaid program. The prepaid program date is validated against the registration date and the protocol details date.

Registering Patient for Prepaid Program

From the Prepaid Management > Transaction, double-click on Patient Registration for Prepaid Program function.

The Patient Registration for Prepaid Program screen gets displayed.

  1. Click [List of Values] to select a Patient ID. This is a mandatory field.

  2. The name, age, gender, national ID and the address of the patient are displayed.

  3. Enter the details in the mandatory (*) fields. <Related Field Description>

  4. Click [Commit Form] or press [F10] to save the records or [Clear Record] to delete the data.

  5. A confirmation message 'Patient Registered Successfully' is displayed.
