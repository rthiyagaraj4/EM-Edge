Service Income – Mth/Yr

From this function you can Query the income incurred by the facility for a specific month and year. You can view the income incurred from an accounting department, from a specific Billing Service, from a department and the income of a Doctor associated to the facility.

The Service Income - Mth/Yr function is used to query upon the income incurred by the facility for the specified month and year. The gross charge, discount amount given and the net charge for the services get listed on selecting the parameters. You have an option to view the income incurred from an originating department or an accounting department. You can also select to view the income incurred from a specific Billing Service or a Doctor for the specified month and year.

Querying Service Income for a month and year

From the Queries > Income Analysis, double-click Service Income – Mth/Yr function.

The Service Income – Mth/Yr screen gets displayed.

  1. Click Calendar function to select the month and year to query the service income. This is a mandatory field.

  2. Select Criteria from the list. The values are system defined – Billing Service, Orig Dept, A/C Dept and Doctor. The details get displayed based on the Criteria selected.

  3. The fields Billing Service, A/C Dept, Orig Dept and Doctor get enabled based on the Criteria selected. Click [List of Values] to select a value for the selected criteria. The description for the selected criteria value gets displayed.

  4. Enter the search criteria and click [Execute Query] or press [F8]. The records matching the entered search criteria get displayed under the respective tab. Click [Clear] to clear the records displayed in the result area.
