Unconfirmed Transaction

From this function you can Query to view the unconfirmed transactions, which are associated to a patient record in the facility.

Unconfirmed Transaction function is used to query on the transactions, which are not confirmed. The unbilled transactions or the unfinalized charge details of a patient get displayed. You have an option to print the unconfirmed transactions for a selected patient. The services where the ‘Autoconfirm’ check box is selected are confirmed automatically on associating the services to the patient. The services where the check box is cleared have to be confirmed manually using the functions ‘Confirm Inpatient Service Requests’ and ‘Confirm Outpatient Service Requests’ under the ‘IP Direct Charges’ and ‘OP Direct Charges’ menu.

Querying to view the unconfirmed transactions

From the Queries > Transactions, double-click Unconfirmed Transaction function.

The Unconfirmed Transaction screen gets displayed.

  1. Enter the details in the fields to query  for a specific patient record. The patient records matching the search criteria get displayed in the result area. Click [Click for Searching Patients] to view the financial and encounter details of a specific patient. Click [Cancel Search Criteria] to clear the entered data.

OR

  1. Click [Click for Searching Patients] to list all the patient records. You can view the financial and encounter details for a specific patient. Click [Cancel Search Criteria] to clear the entered data. The records matching the search criteria get displayed in the result area.

  2. The records get displayed in the result area. Double-click on the relevant patient record. The Unconfirmed Transaction screen gets displayed listing the unconfirmed folio details for the selected patient. The name of the authorised person who has associated the service to the patient gets displayed along with the financial remarks recorded. The total number of unconfirmed transactions, total net amount, total discount amount and the total gross amount gets displayed.

  3. Click [Click for Patient Encounters] to view all the patient encounters recorded in the facility. The encounter details of a patient get listed in the Encounter Details screen. Double-click on the relevant record to view the unconfirmed transactions associated to the patient for the selected encounter. Click [Close] to exit from the Encounter Details screen.
