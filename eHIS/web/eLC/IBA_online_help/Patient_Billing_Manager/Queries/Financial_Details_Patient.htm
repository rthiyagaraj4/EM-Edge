Financial Details – Patient

From this function you can Query to view the financial details of an inpatient in the facility.

The Financial Details – Patient function is used to query on the financial details of a patient. You also have an option to view the unconfirmed services associated to the selected patient record. The charge details for each visit/admission of the patient to the facility are displayed. The charge for inpatient services, outpatient services and the external services, if any, are displayed under the columns Inpatient, Outpatient and External respectively. You can also view the financial details associated to the patient during registration gets displayed.

Querying to view the Financial Details of a patient

From the Queries > Transactions, double-click Financial Details - Patient function.

The Financial Details - Patient screen gets displayed.

  1. Enter the details in the fields to query  for a specific patient record. The patient records matching the search criteria get displayed in the result area. Click [Click for Searching Patients] to view the financial details of a patient. Click [Cancel Search Criteria] to clear the entered data.

OR

  1. Click [Click for Searching Patients] to list all the patient records. You can view the financial details for a specific patient. Click [Cancel Search Criteria] to clear the entered data. The records matching the search criteria get displayed in the result area. Click [Clear Record] to clear each record from the list.

  2. The records get displayed in the result area. Double-click on the relevant patient record to view the financial details of the patient. You can also click [Click for Patient Encounters] to view the financial details.

  3. Click [Unconfirmed Services] to view any unconfirmed services associated to the patient. Click [Close] to exit from the screen.
