List of Inpatients Discharged Without Final Bill

From this function you can Generate a report on the Inpatients who are discharged from the facility  without generating the final bill.

Sometimes it becomes mandatory that the patient have to be discharged from the Facility. The final discharge bill may not be generated during the discharge. The function, List of Inpatients Discharged Without Final Bill is used to list out all the patient records where the inpatients are discharged without the final bill. System generates a report listing the Billing Group, patient details, discharged date, Customer associated and the total amount for the services availed from the facility.

Generating report to list the Inpatients without final discharge bill

From the External Accounting System > Reports, List of Inpatients Discharged Without Final Bill Function.

The List of Inpatients Discharged Without Final Bill screen gets displayed.

  1. Click [List of Values] to select a range of Billing Groups. The report generated lists the patients’ with the selected Billing Group who were discharged without generating the final discharge bill. The description for the selected Billing Groups is displayed.

  2. Click [List of Values] to select a range of Wards. The report generated lists the patients’ who were discharged from the selected wards without generating the final bill. The Wards created in the Inpatient Management module for the respective Facility are displayed.

  3. Click [List of Values] to select a range of the Billing Group Categories to generate a list of the inpatients discharged without the final bill. The Billing Group Categories defined in the Masters get listed.

  4. Click the Calendar function to select the discharge date range. The report generated lists the inpatient details who were discharged without the final bill.

  5. Select a value from the list. The values are system defined – ‘Billing Group Code’ and ‘Ward Code’. The report is sorted based on the Order By value selected.

  6. Click [Run Process] to generate the report based on the entered report criteria or [Clear Record] to clear the entered data.

  7. You can view the report, print the details or save the output in a file in the system.
