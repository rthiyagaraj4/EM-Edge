Notification to Collect Deposit Refund

From this function you can Print notification letters to remind a Patient or a Customer to collect the deposit refund.

Notification to Collect Deposit Refund function is used to view or print the Notification letters to be sent to patients’ requesting them to collect the Deposit refund. In certain cases, a patient may be admitted to the Facility without paying the deposit amount. In such cases, cashier has to generate a report to identify the patient records where the deposit amount is not collected.

Generating Notification Letters for Deposit Collection

From the External Accounting System > Reports, Notification to Collect Deposit Refund Function.

The Notification to Collect Deposit Refund screen gets displayed.

  1. Select an Episode Type from the list. The values are system defined – Inpatient, Outpatient, Daycare, Referral and All.

  2. Click [List of Values] to select a range of Patient ID’s. The report generated includes the refund amount deposited to the inpatients selected during a specific period.

  3. Select a Refund Type from the list. The values are system defined – ‘With Adjusted Deposit’, ‘Without Adjusted Deposit’ and ‘Both’.

  4. Click [Run Process] to generate the report based on the entered report criteria or [Clear Record] to clear the entered data.

  5. You can view the report, print the details or save the output in a file in the system.
