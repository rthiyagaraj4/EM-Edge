Certification of Deposit Account for the Month

From this function you can Print the deposit account details recorded in a month.

Certification of Deposit Account for the Month function is used to print or view the deposit account (balance of cash-in-hand) details for the month in the facility. The report generated contains the Refund Cash Book details. The details are listed only until the last date when the Refund Cash Book is processed.  

Certification of Deposit Account for the Month

From the External Accounting System > Refund Cash Book, double-click Certification of Deposit Account for the Month Function.

The Certification of Deposit Account for the Month  screen gets displayed.

  1. Click the Calendar function to select the date. A report is generated for the date selected. This is a mandatory field.

  2. The Opening Balance Date and the Closed Balance Date is set automatically depending on the date set for ‘As on Date’. The Last Refund Cash Book Period indicates the date when the Refund Cash Book was processed.

  3. Click [Run Process] to print the details. The deposit account details are printed based on the Opening and Closing Balance Date selected.
