View GL Transactions

From this function you can View the billing transactions which are posted to the General Ledger distribution account. The distribution occurs when the billing transactions are posted.

View GL Transactions function is used to view the billing transactions posted to the General Ledger distribution account. It gives an overview of the total amount posted to the General Ledger interface. The debit and the credit amount along with the income account number are displayed.

View GL Transactions

From the Period End, double-click View GL Transactions function.

The View GL Transactions screen gets displayed.

  1. The Module ID is displayed as Patient Billing, by default.

  2. Enter the Posting Month and Year. The transaction details posted from the Patient Billing Manager to the General Ledger module until the selected month and year get listed. This is a mandatory field.

  3. Press [F8] or click [Execute Query] to view the transactions posted from the Patient Billing Manager module to the General Ledger module.

  4. Click [More Details] to view additional details on the transactions posted.
