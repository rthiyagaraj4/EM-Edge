Transfer Bills to DCP

From this function you can Transfer the outstanding bill details of the cash patients to the deferred cash patients’ bill. You can transfer only the posted bills.

Transfer Bills to DCP function is used to transfer the outstanding cash memos to the Deferred Cash Patients bill. The outstanding cash amount only is transferred to the Deferred Cash Patient interface. The outstanding cash bills are posted using the function ‘Post Billing Transactions’.

Transfer Bills to DCP

From the Period End, double-click Transfer Bills to DCP function.

The Transfer Bills to DCP screen gets displayed.

  1. Click the Calendar function to select a range of bill dates. The outstanding bill details for the selected period are transferred to DCP. This is a mandatory field.

  2. Select a Bill Type and the corresponding Bill Number.

  3. Click [Run Process] to generate the report. A screen appears requesting confirmation to transfer the bills. Click [Yes] to transfer the bills and [No] to cancel the process and exit from the screen.

 

You can transfer the posted bills only.
