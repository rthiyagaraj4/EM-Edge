Support Document Master

From this function you can Define various Support Documents and Query for the existing Support Documents.

Support Document Master function enables you to define various Support Documents. A Support Document is associated to an invoice to differentiate between transactions. You can also select if the Document Number is applicable. You can also select a Document Series and record the reference details.

Defining Support Document Master

From the SS Invoice Management > Setup, double-click on Support Document Master function.

The Support Document Master screen gets displayed.

  1. Enter the details in the mandatory (*) fields. <Related Field Description>

  2. Click [Commit Form] or press [F10] to save or click [Clear Record] to clear the entered data.

  3. Click [Execute Query] or press [F8] to search for the existing records within the facility.
