DRG Relative Weight Factor by Payer

From this function you can Define the DRG relative factors based on a Payer. You can Query to view the existing records.

The DRG Relative Weight Factor by Payer function enables you to define the relative weight factor for a DRG code for a specific date range. This is applicable to the associated Payer Group, Payer and the Policy in the Facility.

Defining Relative Weight Factor by Payer

From Invoice Management > Setup, double-click on DRG Relative Weight Factor by Payer function.

The DRG Relative Weight Factor by Payer screen gets displayed.

  1. The logged in Facility is displayed, by default. You cannot define the Relative Weight Factor for the logged facility only. The facilities associated to the logged in user get listed.

  2. Enter the details in the mandatory (*) fields. <Related Field Description>

  3. Click [Save] or press [F10] to save the records or [Clear Record] to clear the entered data.

  4. Click [Execute Query] or press [F8] to view the existing records.
