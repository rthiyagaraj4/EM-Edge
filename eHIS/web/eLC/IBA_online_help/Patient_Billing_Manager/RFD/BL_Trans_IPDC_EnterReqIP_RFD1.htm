Enter the following details to associate a Billing Service to an Inpatient or a Daycare patient:

 

  1. Admission/Tfr. No – Enter the preferred Admission and the transfer serial number generated during registration of the relevant patient. You can also use the lookup option to search for an admission and transfer number associated to a patient encounter. This is a mandatory field.

  2. Transaction Date – The current system date gets displayed as the transaction date. You cannot update the date.

  3. Patient No – Click [List of Values] to select a Patient ID. The patients lookup screen gets displayed. You can select a patient record using the lookup screen. This is a mandatory field. The patient’s name, ward, doctor, bed or room number, gender and age get displayed based on the patient number selected.
