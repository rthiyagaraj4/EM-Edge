Enter the following details to query the invoice or the bill details:

 

  1. Facility – Select the Facility from the list. You can query the invoice or the bill details generated for the outstanding bills of the patients associated to the selected Customer in the facility.

  2. Customer – Click [List of Values] to select the Customer. The invoice or the bill details generated for the outstanding bills of the patients associated to the selected Customer gets listed.

  3. Policy Type - Click [List of Values] to select the Policy Type. The invoice or the bill details generated for the outstanding bills of the patients associated to the selected Customer gets listed.

  4. Patient ID – Click [List of Values] to select the Patient ID. The ID of the patient’s associated to the selected Customer and the Policy Type get listed. The age, gender, patient name and the national ID gets displayed on selecting the Patient ID.

  5. Discharge Date [From – To] – Click Calendar function to select the date range. The search is filtered based on the discharge date selected. The patients discharged during the selected date range are displayed.

 

Episode Type

Select an Episode Type to query for the respective Episode Types with the outstanding bill details or the invoices generated.

  • All – Select ‘All’ to query the bill details and the invoices of all the Episode Types in the selected facility.

  • Outpatient – Select ‘Outpatient’ to query the bill details and the invoices of the Outpatients in the facility.

  • Emergency – Select ‘Emergency’ to query the bill details and the invoices of the Emergency patients in the facility.

  • Daycare – Select ‘Daycare’ to query the bill details and the invoices of the Daycare patients in the facility.

  • Inpatient – Select ‘Inpatient’ to query the bill details and the invoices of the Inpatients in the facility.

  • External – Select ‘External’ to query the bill details and the invoices of the External patients in the facility.

  1. Episode ID – Click [List of Values] to select the Episode ID of the patient.

  2. [Refresh] – Click [Refresh] to query the details. The records matching the search criteria get displayed.

 

Encounter Details for Invoices

The encounter details of the selected patient in the facility get displayed. The Episode ID, Visit ID for an Outpatient/Emergency/External patient gets displayed along with the admission and the discharge date.

  1. [View] – Click [View] to view the additional information captured while generating the invoice. You cannot update the details, but only view the details captured.

 

Invoice/Debit Note/Credit Note Details

The invoices generated for the selected patient gets listed. The transaction type selected while generating the Debit or the Credit Notes using the functions ‘Generate Debit Note/Credit Note – OP/EM’ or ‘Generate Debit Note/Credit Note – IP/DC’ gets displayed. The Document Type, respective document number and the amount against which the invoice or credit or the debit note was generated gets displayed. The invoices which are cancelled are indicated using  color. Click [D] to view the service details for which the amount is overdue and against which the invoice, the credit notes, or the debit notes are generated.

 

Payment Details

The amount settled against the invoice generated gets displayed. The amount settled, the receipt type and the number generated for the respective settlement gets displayed. The date when the receipt was generated for the settlement is also displayed. You can also view the total amount settled.

 

Bill Details

You can view the bills generated for the patient transactions in the facility using the Bill Details tab. The Bill Type and Number, the bill generation date is listed with the total outstanding amount for which the bill is generated. If an invoice is generated against the outstanding bill, the amount for which the invoice is generated also is displayed. You can also view the discount given against an invoice, if any. The invoice document type and the number, amount for which the invoice is generated and the invoice generation date are listed. The service details for a selected record can be viewing by clicking [D].
