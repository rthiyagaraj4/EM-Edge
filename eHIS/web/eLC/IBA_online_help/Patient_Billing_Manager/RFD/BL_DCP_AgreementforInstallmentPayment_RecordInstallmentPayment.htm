Installment Plan

  1. Credit Agreement Doc No - The credit agreement document type and number is displayed. The credit agreement number is generated while recording the credit agreement details for the respective patient.

  2. Inst No – The installment number against which the payment is not settled gets displayed.

  3. Due Date – The date within which the amount had to be settled gets displayed.

  4. Installment Amt – The amount to be settled in each installment gets displayed. The installment amount is defined using the function Enter Credit Agreement ’ for the selected patient record.

  5. Written Off – If the respective record is written-off due to overdue or non-payment, the respective date gets displayed.

  6. Total Paid Amt – If the installment amount is settled, the date when the payment was made gets displayed.

  7. O/S Balance - The outstanding balance amount for the respective date gets displayed.

  8. Payment Date - The date when the installment payment was cleared gets displayed.

  9. Total Amount for Settled - Enter the amount settled in numeric format. You can accept an amount more or lesser than installment payment. The remaining amount after deducting the installment amount can be used to settle the subsequent installment amount.

  10. Receipt Document No – The receipt type and number gets displayed, if the receipt was generated for the installment amount settlement.

  11. Select – Select the check box to settle the payment against subsequent installment records. If the amount entered in ‘Total amount for Settled’ is more than the installment amount, it can be used for subsequent installment payment. If the amount settled is lesser than the installment payment, the pending settlement amount gets displayed in ‘O/S Balance’ column. The payments which are overdue, undue and paid are highlighted in red, green and yellow colors respectively.

  12. Payment Amt – Enter the payment amount. The payment amount has to match with the amount entered in ‘Total Amount for Settled’.

  13. Total Amt selected for Settlement – A sum of the selected Payment Amount gets displayed.

  14. Right-click on the 'Installment Amt' column to settle the amount when the amount is overdue.

  15. Settle Bill Outstanding screen is displayed to record and settle the outstanding amount. The receipt types defined through the 'Receipt Types' function with the 'receipt Nature' as 'IN' are listed.
