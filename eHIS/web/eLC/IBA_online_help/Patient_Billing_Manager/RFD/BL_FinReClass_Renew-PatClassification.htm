Enter the following details to renew the classification details:

 

  1. Patient ID – Click the [List of Values] icon to select a valid Patient ID from the list. The Name, Age and the Sex of the patient is displayed in the relevant text fields.

  2. Classification Type - The Classification Type associated to the patient gets displayed.

  3. Classification Code - The Financial Reclassification Code associated to the patient is displayed.

  4. Income/Asset -The Income or Asset details recorded for the patient are displayed.

  5. Patient/Spouse/Dependants/Family Income - The income details recorded during patient registration is displayed.

  6. Annual Family Income – The Annual Income of the relevant patient family is displayed. The information gets populated only if the details are recorded during patient registration.

  7. Family Assets – The Family Assets Value specified at the time of patient registration is displayed.

  8. Settlement Indicator – The Settlement Indicator specified is displayed.

  9. Billing Group – The Billing Group associated to the relevant patient is displayed.

  10. Customer Group/Customer – The Customer Group/Customer associated to the relevant Patient ID is displayed.

  11. Non Insurance Billing Group - The Non- Insurance Billing Group selected for an Insurance patient is displayed.

  12. Non Insurance Customer Group/Customer - The Non- Insurance Customer Group/Customer selected is displayed.

  13. Classification Date [Start-End] - The re-classification date selected during re-classification is displayed.

  14. New Classification Period - The new re-classification date is displayed based on the assessment period entered in the 'Financial Reclassification Parameters' function.

  15. [Authorize] - Click [Authorize] to proceed with the renewal process.   The Users with the ‘Reclassification Allowed’ check box as selected in the ‘Users for Services’ function under the Administration function are authorized to renew the details.

    1. User ID – Enter the User ID. The Users with the ‘Reclassification Allowed’ check box as selected in the ‘Users for Services’ function under the Administration function are authorized to finalize the details. This is a mandatory field.

    2. PIN No – Enter the PIN number of the respective User. This is a mandatory field.

    3. Remarks – Enter the Remarks in alphanumeric format. This is a mandatory field.
