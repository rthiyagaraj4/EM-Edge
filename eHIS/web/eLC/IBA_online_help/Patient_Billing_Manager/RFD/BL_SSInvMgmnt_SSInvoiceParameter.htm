Enter the following details define SS invoice parameters:

 

Siriraj Social Security

  1. Siriraj Mapped Payer Group Code - Click [List of Values] to select the Payer Group code. The Payer Group Code description gets displayed. This is a mandatory field.

  2. Siriraj Mapped Payer - Click  [List of Values] to select a Payer. The description gets displayed. This is a mandatory field.

  3. Default SS Payer - Click [List of Values] to select a default SS Payer. The description gets displayed. This is a mandatory field.

  4. Default Report Logo - Click [Browse] to select a default report logo. This is a mandatory field.

  5. Support Document Logo - Click [Browse] to select a default document logo. This is a mandatory field.

  6. Print Legends for Receipt - Select the check box to print legends during receipt generation.

 

Invoice

  1. Authorized Signatory 1 - Enter the name of the Invoice Authorized Signatory in alphanumeric format of 40 characters maximum length. This indicates the name of the authorised person who is in charge of the invoices dispatched to Customers. This is a mandatory field.

  2. Signatory Title 1 - Enter the designation of the Invoice Authorised Signatory in alphanumeric format of 40 characters maximum length. This indicates the designation of the authorised person who is in charge of the invoices dispatched to the Customers. This is a mandatory field.

  3. Authorized Signatory 2 - Enter the name of the Invoice Authorized Signatory in alphanumeric format of 40 characters maximum length. This indicates the name of the authorised person who is in charge of the invoices dispatched to Customers.

  4. Signatory Title 2 - Enter the designation of the Invoice Authorised Signatory in alphanumeric format of 40 characters maximum length. This indicates the designation of the authorised person who is in charge of the invoices dispatched to the Customers.
