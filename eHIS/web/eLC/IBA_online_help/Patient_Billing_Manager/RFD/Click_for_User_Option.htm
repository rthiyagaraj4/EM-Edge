You can select any of the links to search for a patient record:

  • Patients Lookup

  • Patients/Episodes/Visits Lookup

  • Episodes/Visits Lookup

 

Patients Lookup

  • You can select ‘Soundex’ or ‘Normal’ from the list to filter the search based on the Soundex.

  • Click [Execute Query] or press [F8] to search for all the patient records.

  • To select a relevant patient record, click [Enter Query] and enter either the patient ID or the patient name. The selected patient record gets displayed.

  • Select the check boxes below Inactive or Deceased to select inactive and deceased patients. The patient details get displayed based on the selection.

  • The patient details are displayed. Deceased, Inactive and Suspended patient records are notified with different colors against the relevant patient record.

  • Double-click on the patient record to select the record and to exit the search screen.

  • Click [Close] in the search screen to exit from the search screen.

  • The Document Number, Document Date, Transaction Type, Patient Name, Patient ID, Due Date, Gender of the patient and age are displayed in years, months and days.

 

Patients/Episodes/Visits Lookup

  • You can select ‘Soundex’ or ‘Normal’ from the list to filter the search based on the Soundex.

  • Click [OP Details] to view the details of all the Outpatients in the facility. The last visit registration number, Episode Number associated to the selected visit registration, visit ID for the respective visit registration and the episode start date get displayed for all the outpatient visits in the facility. Click [Visit Details] to view the visit registration details associated to an Outpatient record. Double click either the selected Visit Detail record in the Visit Lookup screen or the Outpatient record in the Episode Lookup screen to select an Outpatient record.

  • Click [Close] in the search screen to exit from the search screen.

  • Click [Execute Query] or press [F8] to search for all the patient records.

  • Double-click on the patient record to select the record and to exit the search screen.

  • The Document Number, Document Date, Transaction Type, Patient Name, Patient ID, Due Date, Gender of the patient, and age are displayed in years, months and days.
