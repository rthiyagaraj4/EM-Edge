Enter the following details to view and finalize the post-renal transplant treatment details recorded for a patient using the 'Register' tab:

 

  1. Facility - The name of the logged in facility is displayed, by default. You can update the details for the patient in the logged-in facility.

  2. Episode Type - Select the Episode Type from the list.

  3. Admission/Tfr. No - Enter the admission or the transfer number of the selected inpatient in the facility.

  4. Patient ID - Click [List of Values] to select the Patient ID. The details of patients' discharged from the facility are displayed. The name, gender and the age of the patient is displayed on selecting the patient ID are displayed.

  5. Registration Period - Click Calendar function to select the registration period. The list of patients' registered for renal treatment during the selected period gets displayed.

  6. Transplant Period - Click Calendar function to select the transplant period. The list of patients' whose transplant date is in between the date range selected gets displayed.

  7. Discharge Period - Click Calendar function to select the discharge period. The list of patients' whose discharge date is in between the date range selected gets displayed.

  8. [Search] - Click [Search] to search for the patient records matching the search criteria. The patient details are displayed based on the search criteria. You can update the registration date, invoice document type and number for a selected patient.

 

Finalize

  1. Effective To - The effective date until when the treatment is valid for the patient is displayed.

  2. Last Invoice Date - The date when the last invoice was generated for the treatment is displayed.

  3. Donor - The donor ID associated to the patient is displayed.

  4. Recurring Invoice - The check box is selected, if 'Recurring Invoice' if the invoice is selected as recurring invoice while registering the patient for renal treatment.

  5. Agreed Inv Amt - The agreed invoice amount for the selected Payer, Policy and Payer Group combination is displayed.

  6. Finalize - Select the check box to finalize the renal treatment for the selected patient.

  7. Finalized Date - The system date is displayed as the finalized date.

 

Recurring Invoice Amount by Month

The recurring invoice amount for each month is displayed based on the amount recorded for the respective Payer, Policy and Payer Group using the function 'Agreed Amt by Payer/Policy'.
