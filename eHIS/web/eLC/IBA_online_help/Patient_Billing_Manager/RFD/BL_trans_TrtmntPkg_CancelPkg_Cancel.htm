Enter the following details to cancel a package:

 

Package Details

The packages that are subscribed for the patient and are open are listed.

  1. Package Code - Click [List of Values] to select a package that has to be cancelled. The selected package details and package service details are displayed.

  2. Cancel Remarks - Click [List of Values] to select remarks for cancellation. The description of the selected remark is displayed. The reasons that are defined through Reasons for Billing Actions function are listed.

  3. Cancel Unprocessed Orders - Select the check box 'Cancel Unprocessed Orders' to cancel the orders that are placed whose status is not processed during the transfer process.

  4. [Exc] - Click [Exc] to view the services that are excluded from the package. The services that are excluded through the 'Maintain Packages' function for the respective package are listed.

  5. Click [Commit Form] or press [F10] to save the records or click [Clear Record] to clear the data.

  6. A warning message 'Do you want to convert the Package Deposit into Special Agency Deposit?' is displayed if a deposit amount is accepted against the selected package. Click [Yes] to convert or [No] to cancel.

  7. A new screen special agency deposit is displayed on clicking [Yes] . Click the calendar function to select the effective date during which the special agency deposit is available. Click [OK] to convert or [Cancel] to cancel the process. The amount is recalculated on clicking [OK].

  8. The refund screen is displayed on clicking [No] in the pop-up screen mentioned above. You can refund the deposit amount that is collected against the package.
