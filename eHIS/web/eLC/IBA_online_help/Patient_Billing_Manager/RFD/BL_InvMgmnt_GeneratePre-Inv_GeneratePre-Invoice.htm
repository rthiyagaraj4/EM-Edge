Enter the following details to generate a pre-invoice:

  1. Narration - Enter the narration in alphanumeric format. You can record additional details to generate pre-invoice through the field.

 

Encounter Details for Pending Invoice

  1. Patient ID - The patient ID generated during patient registration is displayed.

  2. Admission Date - The date when the patient is admitted to the facility is displayed.

  3. Episode Type - The Episode Type of the patient is displayed.

  4. Episode ID - The Episode ID generated for the respective Inpatient or Daycare patient is displayed.

  5. Visit ID - The Visit ID of the patient is displayed, if any.

  6. Acct Seq -  The account sequence number is displayed. The account sequence number is modified when the policy details are modified.

  7. Payer Group - The Payer Group associated to the patient is displayed.

  8. Payer - The Payer associated to the patient is displayed.

  9. Policy Type Code - The Policy details recorded while capturing the financial details of the patient are displayed.

  10. Discharge Adv Date - The date when the discharge advice is generated for the respective patient is displayed.

  11. Additional Dtls Reqd - The check box is selected, if the setup is defined in the 'Setup invoice Additional Info’ function.

  12. Captured - The check box is selected, if additional details are captured for the selected patient record. The check box is cleared, if additional details are not captured.

  13. [C] - Click [C] to record additional details for the respective patient record. You cannot generate pre-invoice, if the details are not captured.

  14. Select All - Select the check box to generate pre-invoice for all the outstanding bills. Clear the check box, if pre-invoice is not required for the selected bills.

  15. [Generate] - Select the check box to generate pre-invoice. A reference document type and number is generated for the respective record. The reference number is generated based on the invoice records selected in the 'Document Types by Payers' function. A new screen Generated Pre-Invoice Dtls is displayed with the invoice details.

    The following details are displayed:

    1. Doc Type Code - The document type  generated while generating pre-invoice is displayed. The document type is defined through 'Document Types by Payers' function.

    2. Doc Num - The document number generated while generating pre-invoice is displayed.

    3. Doc Date - The date when the pre-invoice is generated is displayed.

    4. Amount - The amount that has to be settled by the payer is displayed.

    5. Payer Group/Payer/Policy Type Code - The Payer Group, Payer and the Policy Type associated to the patient is displayed.

    6. Click [Save] to save the pre-invoice details or click [Cancel] to abort the process. The financial details are rolled-back to the financial details of the patient that is recorded during registration.

    The pre-invoice details have to be saved within two minutes. The financial details are rolled back, if the records are not saved within the time limit specified. The remaining time limit to save the details are displayed in seconds in the screen.

It is mandatory to record diagnosis in order to generate a pre-invoice.

 

Outstanding Bills

The outstanding bill details are displayed.

  1. Doc Type - The document type generated during pre-invoice is displayed.

  2. Doc Number - The document number generated during pre-invoice is displayed.

  3. Date - The date when the pre-invoice is generated is displayed.

  4. Policy Type - The Policy Type selected during pre-invoice generation is displayed.

  5. Pending Amount - The pending amount against which the pre-invoice is generated is displayed.

  6. Patient ID - The ID of the patient is displayed.

  7. Episode Type - The Episode Type of the patient is displayed.

  8. Episode ID - The Episode ID of the patient is displayed.

  9. Visit ID - The Visit ID of the patient is displayed.

  10. Reference Original Invoice - Click [List of Values] to select and associate the original invoice number.

  11. [Service Dtls] - Click [Service Dtls] to view services associated to the patient.
