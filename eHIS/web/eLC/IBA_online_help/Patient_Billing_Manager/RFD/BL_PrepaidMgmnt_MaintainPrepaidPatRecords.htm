Enter the following details:

 

  1. Payer Group - Click [List of Values] to select a Payer Group. The values defined through the 'Payer Groups' function are listed.

  2. Payer - Click [List of Values] to select a Payer. The values defined through the 'Payer' function is listed.

  3. Policy - Click [List of Values] to select a Policy. The values defined through the 'Policy Types' function in Insurance Management module are listed.

  4. Patient ID - Click [List of Values] to select a Patient ID. The protocol details associated to the selected patient ID are listed. The name of the patient, gender, age and national ID are displayed on selecting the patient ID.

  5. Register [From - To] - Click the Calendar function to select the registration date. The patient records that are registered within the date range selected are displayed. The registration date is recorded through the 'Patient Registration For Prepaid Program' function.

  6. Register No. - Click [List of Values] to select the registration number. The registration number that is recorded during patient registration for prepayment is displayed.

  7. Status - Select a Status from the list. The system-defined values are 'All', 'Inactive', 'Active' and 'Cancel'. The patient records matching the selected status are displayed.
