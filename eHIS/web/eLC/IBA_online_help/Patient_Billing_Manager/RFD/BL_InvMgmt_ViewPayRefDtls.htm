Enter the following details to view the payment reference details:

 

  1. Payment Reference Doc – Enter the payment reference document number in alphanumeric format. The document number has to match with the Document Reference number entered while recording the invoice payments of the Customer.

  2. Pmnt Ref Date – Click Calendar function to select the date when the payment details were recorded for the selected document reference number. The payments made on the selected date get listed.

  3. Invoice Dispatch Mode – Select a dispatch mode from the list. The values are system defined – ‘Manual’ and ‘Electronic’. The dispatch mode indicates the means by which the invoice was dispatched to the Customer.

  4. Manual – Select Manual to view the invoices, which were dispatched manually.

  5. Electronic – Select Electronic to view the invoices which were dispatched through electronic means.

  1. Receipt Amount – Enter the Receipt Amount in numeric format. The Receipt Amount indicates the amount dispatched by the Customer to settle the outstanding bills against which the invoice was generated. The records matching the receipt amount entered gets displayed.

  2. Allocated Amount - The Allocated Amount in displayed in numeric format indicating the agreed amount for the Policy and the Payer combination. The Allocated Amount indicates the amount settled by the Customer for the respective invoice. The records matching the allocated amount entered get displayed.

  3. Balance Amount - Enter the Balance Amount in numeric format. The Balance Amount indicates the amount outstanding. The records matching the balance amount entered gets displayed.

  4. Payer Name – Enter the name of the Payer in alphanumeric format. The details for the records matching the name of the Payer entered get displayed.

  5. Receipt Option – Select a Receipt Option from the list. The values are system defined – ‘Invoice-wise’ and ‘Consolidated by Customer’.

  6. Invoice-wise – Select Invoice-wise to list the details based on the Invoices generated for the outstanding documents of the Customer.

  7. Consolidated by Customer - Select Consolidated by Customer to list the invoice details, if the invoice was consolidated by the Customer Group.  

  1. [Edit List] – Click [Edit List] to view, print or save the payment reference details of the records matching the search criteria in the local machine.

 

Invoices for Settlement

The invoices generated for the outstanding bills of the selected Customer get displayed. The Invoice Number, invoice generation date, payable amount, amount settled, and the payer details are listed. You can also view the document reference details. If the invoices are consolidated, detailed information on the invoices that were consolidated can also be viewed. You can also view the prepaid amount received from a payer for an invoice that is generated.

 

Bill Details

The bill details for the services utilized are displayed. You can select the invoices under ‘View All Transactions’. On clicking a particular record, the bill details associated to the selected invoice are listed under ‘Bill Details’. Click [D] to view the Service Details for each bill generated in the facility for the selected Customer.

 

Settlement Details

Using this tab, you can view the payments made by the Customer to the facility. You can also reprint the receipt, which was printed initially by clicking [Reprint Receipt].
