Inquire/Print Credit Agreement

From this function you can Query to view and Print the deferred cash patient details, which are recorded.

The Inquire/Print Credit Agreement function enables you to query the credit agreement statement recorded for a Deferred Cash Patient. The details recorded using the function ‘Enter Credit Agreement’ get displayed for the selected patient. You can also print the agreement using this function. The details include the date when the agreement details are submitted by the patient, the document type and number, the outstanding amount and the amount settled towards the service.

Inquire/Print Credit Agreement

From the Deferred Cash Patients > DCP Queries, double-click on Inquire/Print Credit Agreement function.

The Inquire/Print Credit Agreement screen gets displayed.

  1. Click [Execute Query] or press [F8] to query the details. Click [Enter Query] to enter the Patient ID and view the details of the selected patient. The data recorded using the function ‘Enter Credit Agreement’ for a relevant patient get displayed.  

  2. Click [Print Agreement] to print the details recorded.

 

You can only view the credit agreement details recorded, but cannot edit the details.
