Cancel Credit Agreement

From this function you can Cancel the credit agreement for installment payment. You can Query to view the existing patient records.

Cancel Credit Agreement function is used to cancel a credit agreement that is created for installment payment of a patient. The installment plan, installment payment details, outstanding bill details and remainder letter details are displayed. Overdue, Unpaid, and Paid records are highlighted using , and colors respectively.

 

You cannot cancel the agreement, if the amount is settled.

You cannot cancel the agreement if installment is written-off.

Cancel Credit Agreement

From the Deferred Cash Patients > Agreement for Installment Payment, double-click Cancel Credit Agreement Function.

The Cancel Credit Agreement screen gets displayed.

  1. Enter the search criteria to query for outstanding patient records.

    Enter the following search criteria:

    1. For the Month - Click the Calendar function to select a month and year to query for outstanding patient records.

    2. Patient ID - Select the Patient ID to query for the respective patient record. The name of the patient is displayed.

    3. Credit Agreement Document - Click [List of Values] to select a Credit Agreement Document. Credit Agreement Document number is generated on recording the details through 'Create Agreement for Installment Payment' function.

  2. Click [Search] to query for the patient records.

  3. The credit agreements recorded for the patient are displayed. The patient ID and patient’s name, credit agreement document type and number, date when the agreement is created and the agreement amount are displayed.

  4. The installment plan recorded for the patient, outstanding bill details and the remainder letter details are displayed.

  5. Right-click on the Credit Agreement Document Number to cancel the agreement.

  6. An authorization screen pops-up requesting authorization to cancel. Enter the details to login and cancel.

    Enter the following details:

    1. User ID - Enter the User ID to login and cancel the agreement. This is a mandatory field.

    2. Pin No - Enter the corresponding PIN Number to login and cancel the agreement. This is a mandatory field.

    3. Reason - Click [List of Values] to select the reason. The reasons defined through the function 'Reasons for Billing Action' with the 'Cancel Installment Payment' check box as selected are listed.

  7. Click [OK] to login or [Cancel] to cancel the process. The credit agreement is cancelled on clicking [OK].
