Pending Orders

From this function you can Query the Pending Orders which are kept on hold by the Pharmacist.

Querying the Pending Orders

From the PH Queries, select the Pending Orders function.

The Pending Orders screen gets displayed.

  1. Enter the details in the mandatory (*) fields. <Related Field Description>

  2. Click [Search] to display the Pending Orders based on the entered criteria.

  3. Click ‘Refill’ hyperlink to view the order refill details.

  4. Click [Reset] to clear the specified criteria.

If Refill is applicable for a particular order, Refill hyperlink gets displayed against the Order ID.
