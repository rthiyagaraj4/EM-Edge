Discontinued Drugs

From this function you can Query and view the details of Discontinued Drugs.

On searching, the Drugs that are discontinued in the Discontinue Drug function are displayed. You can view the Date and Time when the Drug was discontinued, the name of the person, who discontinued the Drug and the Reason for discontinuing the Drug.

Querying Discontinued Drugs

From the PH Queries, select the Discontinued Drugs function.

The Discontinued Drugs screen gets displayed.

  1. Click [Search] to display all the Discontinued Drugs or enter the search criteria and click [Search] to display a specific Discontinued Drug.

    Enter the following search criteria:

    1. Drug Name - Enter the Drug Name in alphanumeric format of 40 characters maximum length or select the desired Drug using [?] function.

    2. Drug Class – Select the required Drug Class from the list. The listed values are ‘General’, ‘Narcotic’ and ‘Controlled’.

    3. Date [From-To] – Enter the date range in dd/mm/yyyy format or select the required date range using the Calendar function.

  2. Click ‘+’ hyperlink to view the Drug Details.

  3. Click ‘Details’ hyperlink to view the Discontinued Drug details. Click [Close] to exit from the Discontinued Drug details.

  4. Click [Reset] to clear the specified criteria.
