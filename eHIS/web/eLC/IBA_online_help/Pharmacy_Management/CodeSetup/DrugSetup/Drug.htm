Drug

From this function you can Define and maintain medication drugs and Query for the desired Drug.

Drug function allows defining various attributes of a Drug like specifying Dosage/Dispense Details, Associating Trade Name to the Drug, Specify Inventory Details, Associating Generic Name to the Drug that influence Ordering and Dispensing in a facility.

The attributes defined are Drug Characteristics, Dispensing/Ordering Rules and Intravenous Characteristics. Based on the PH Parameter Setup, if link between Drug and Item is to be established, an Item is linked compulsorily to the Drug. The multiple Items are linked to the Drug based on the Parameter rules. The Generic Name of the Drug has to be specified. If the defined Drug is a Compound Drug, its generic ingredients are to be recorded. Applicable Trade Names are associated to the Drug.

A Drug is compulsorily linked to an Order Catalog and orderable attributes defined. The Frequency Components, Ordering Validation, Instructions and rules for various Patient Classes are specified. The Drugs linked to Order Catalogs are available for ordering.

Defining a Drug

From the PH Code Setup > PH Drug Setup, select the Drug function.

The Drug screen gets displayed.

  1. Click [Create] to invoke the Create screen.

  2. Enter the details in the mandatory (*) fields. <Related Field Description>

  3. Enter the details in the mandatory (*) fields of the tab pages.

Characteristics Tab

Dosage/Disp Details Tab

Trade Name Tab

Inventory Items Tab

Generic Ingredients Tab

Drug Database Interface Tab

Age Groups Tab

Sliding Scale Template Tab

  1. Click [Apply] to save the Drug created or [Reset] to clear the entered data.

  2. On recording the Drug attributes, the system confirms whether the orderable information is required to be set. Click [OK] to add the orderable information or [Cancel] to cancel the process.

  3. On clicking [OK] the Drug is saved and Order Catalog screen gets displayed.

  4. Click [Apply] to record the Orderable Information or [Reset] to cleat the entered data.

  5. Click [Query] to invoke the Drug Search screen.

  6. Select the Drug Name hyperlink to modify the Drug attributes.

  7. Click [Apply] to save the changes or [Reset] to clear the modified details.
