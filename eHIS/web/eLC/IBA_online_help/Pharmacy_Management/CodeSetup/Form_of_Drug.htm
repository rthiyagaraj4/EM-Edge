Form of Drug

From this function you can Define the Form of Drug and Query for desired Form of Drug.

Drugs appear in various forms. Various forms of drugs are defined. For example, a drug may be in the form of Tablet, Capsule, Dry Powder, Syrup or Ointments. Only 10 records can be added at a time. You cannot duplicate the code. The system alerts you on saving the record by highlighting the duplicate code with a unique color coding.

Creating the Form of Drug

From the PH Code Setup > PH Code Setup, select the Form of Drug function.

The Form of Drug screen gets displayed.

  1. Click [Create] to invoke the Create screen.

  2. Enter the details in the mandatory fields. <Related Field Description>

  3. Click [Apply] to save the defined Form of Drug or [Reset] to clear the entered data.

  4. Click [Query] to search for an existing Form of Drug.

 

If ‘Patient Instruction in Local Language’, is selected in ‘PH Parameter for Facility’ function, the defining Form of Drug in Local Language is allowed.
