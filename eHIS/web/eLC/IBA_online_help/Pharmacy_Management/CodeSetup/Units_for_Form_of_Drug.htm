Units for Form of Drug

From this function you can Create an association of Units to Form of Drug and Query for desired association of Units for Form of Drug.

The various forms of drug are associated with applicable Unit of Measurement. A default Unit of Measurement is associated to the form of drug. The Units of Measurement are defined in ‘Application Masters’ module.

Creating an Association of Units to Form of Drug

From the PH Code Setup > PH Code Setup, select the Units for Form of Drug function.

The Units for Form of Drug screen gets displayed.

  1. Click [Create] to invoke the Create screen.

  2. Select the Form of Drug from the list. The Form defined in the Form of Drug function is displayed.

  3. Click the following tab pages to create an association of Unit of Measure to the Form of Drug.

Select Tab

You can select the default and Applicable UOM using this tab. The Select tab is displayed, by default.

  1. + - Click on the hyperlink ‘+’ to view the details of Unit of Measurement. The Unit of Measurement is defined in ‘Application Masters’ module.

 

Applicability

  • Purchase – If the unit of measurement is applicable to Purchase, a ‘Tick’ mark or a ‘Cross’ mark gets displayed.

  • Stock - If the unit of measurement is applicable to Stock, a ‘Tick’ mark or a ‘Cross’ mark gets displayed.

  • Sale - If the unit of measurement is applicable to Sale, a ‘Tick’ mark or a ‘Cross’ mark gets displayed.

  • Strength – If the unit of measurement is applicable to Strength, a ‘Tick’ mark or a ‘Cross’ mark gets displayed.

  • Quantity - If the unit of measurement is applicable to Quantity, a ‘Tick’ mark or a ‘Cross’ mark gets displayed.

 

  1. Applicable UOM – The applicable Units of Measurement are displayed under the column ‘Applicable UOM’. Only those UOMs for which ‘Dosage’ is applicable are displayed (in the function ‘Unit of Measurement’ in Application Masters module).

  2. Default UOM – Select the check box to set the applicable UOM as the default UOM. Only one UOM can be selected as default.

  3. Select – Select the required Units of Measure to be associated to the selected Form of Drug by selecting the check box. The check boxes are cleared, by default.

Association Tab

You can view the selected Units of Measurement under the ‘Association’ tab. You can change the default Unit of Measurement.

  1. Click [Apply] to save the association of Units to Form of Drug or [Reset] to clear the entered data.

  2. Click [Query] to search for an existing association of Units to Form of Drug.
