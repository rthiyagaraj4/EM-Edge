Home Medication Sheet

From this function you can associate the Home Medication Sheet Type with Form of Drugs.

Based on the association done in the master setup, the prescribed home medication drugs are printed in the Home Medication Sheet Summary Report.

Associating Home Medication Sheet Type with Drugs

From the PH Code Setup > PH Code Setup, select the Home Medication Sheet function.

The Home Medication Sheet screen gets displayed.

  1. Click [Create] to invoke the Create screen.

  2. Select any one of the Home Medication Sheet Type. The available types are 'Select’, ‘Include Eye Preparation’, ‘Separate Eye Preparation’ and ‘Only Eye Preparation’. The default selected value will be ‘Select’.

  1. Associate the form of drug against each Home Medication Sheet Type by selecting the form of drug under Select tab. All the selected form of drugs are displayed in the Association tab. By default, all the form of drugs under the Association tab is selected.

You can uncheck the Select check box if it is not required to be associated.

  1. Click Apply and the selected form of drugs will be associated against the selected Home Medication Sheet Type.

You can associate a single form of drug against multiple Home Medication Sheet Types.
