Instructions Tab

The Instructions/Alerts are specified for the Drug in this tab.

 

Enter the following Instructions:

 

  1. Prompt Alert – Select the check box to enter alert to be displayed while ordering the defined drug. Enter the alert in alphanumeric format in the text area provided next to the check box.

  2. Preps – Enter the preparatory instructions in alphanumeric format of 200 characters maximum length. The instructions to be followed before dispensing the drug are specified.

  3. Department Instruction - Enter specific instructions to the department in alphanumeric format.

  4. Patient Instruction - Select either ‘Standard’ or ‘By Age/Sex’ as the value. On selecting an option, click [Instructions]. Based on the selected option, the Standard Instructions or Instruction By Age/Sex screen gets displayed on clicking [Instructions].

    Patient Instruction: By Age/Sex

    This screen gets displayed if patient instruction is Age and Gender dependent.

    Enter the following details to set instructions for the patients:

    1. Sex - Select the gender from the list. The values displayed as Male, Female and Both.

    2. Years/Months/Days - Specify the minimum age and maximum age in years, months and days for the selected gender. Minimum age cannot be greater than the Maximum age.

    3. Instrn - Click on the ‘ Instrn’ hyperlink to enter the instruction.

      • Enter the appropriate instructions in alphanumeric format of 1000 characters maximum length.

      • Click [OK] to confirm the text and the control shifts back to the Instructions by Age/Sex screen.
