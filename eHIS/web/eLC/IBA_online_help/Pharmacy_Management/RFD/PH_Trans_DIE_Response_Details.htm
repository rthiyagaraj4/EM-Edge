Response Details

The Respond details tab consists of three sections, namely, ‘Response/Details’ and ‘Information Source’ and ‘Respond Remarks’.

 

  1. Response/Details - The Response/Details are entered by the Pharmacist/the person who receives the enquiry, in alphanumeric format of 1000 characters maximum length. At least one response has to be entered by the enquirer. This is a mandatory field.

  2. Information Source – The drug information CTR gets displayed in this section as displayed from the other Admin Drug Information Source Master. More than one source can be selected in.

  3. Respond Remarks - The Pharmacist/or any other authorized person, who receives the enquiry has to enter the Respond remarks in alphanumeric format of 1000 characters maximum length.

  4. [Respond] - The Pharmacist/or any other authorized person, who receives the enquiry clicks [Respond] as pointed in the above screen. The details as shown are displayed.

  5. Don’t Respond – The Pharmacist/or any other authorized person, who receives the enquiry can click on the ‘Don’t Respond button’ to refrain from responding to the enquirer.

  6. Response Date/Time – The Pharmacist/or any other authorized person, who receives the enquiry while responding to the enquiry must enter the Respond Date/Time. On entering the date, the ‘Do you want to verify’ check box is enabled.

  7. Replied/Answered by – This logged in User Name is displayed as read-only text.

  8. Response Time – The Response time is calculated on blur of the Response date/Time. It is the difference between enquiry date/time and response date/time.

  9. Do you want to verify – Select the check box to verify the record. If the check box is selected it displays the model window showing the verifier name and time.  Select ‘Do you want to verify’ check box and enter ‘Verify Remarks’. Click [OK] to record ‘Verify Remarks’ or [Cancel] to cancel the entered remarks.
