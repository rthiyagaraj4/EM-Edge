Apheresis Materials

From this function you can Record information about materials used at apheresis. You can Query for the existing records.

The Apheresis Materials function is used to record information about materials used at apheresis. Apheresis is a procedure in which blood is drawn and separated into its components by dialysis; some are retained and the rest are returned to the donor by transfusion.

Recording Apheresis Materials

From the Masters, double-click the Apheresis Materials function.

The Apheresis Materials screen gets displayed.

  1. Enter the details in the mandatory (*) fields. <Related Field Description>

  2. Click [Save] or press [F10] to save the apheresis material details or [Clear Record] to cancel the process.

  3. Click [Execute Query] or press [F8] to search for existing Apheresis Materials.
