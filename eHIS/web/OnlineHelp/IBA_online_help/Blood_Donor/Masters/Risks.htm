Risks

From this function you can Create risk factors, which can be associated to a donor and Query for the existing records.

The Risks function allows you to create the possible risk factors that can be associated to a donor during Pre Donation analysis after examining the donor’s health condition. Some of the risks are chest pain, HIV, fainting, vomiting, etc. If the risks are grave, the donor has to be deferred from donating the blood.

Creating Risks

From the Masters, double-click the Risks function.

The Risks screen gets displayed.

  1. Enter the details in the mandatory fields. <Related Field Description>

  2. Click [Save] or press [F10] to save the created records or click [Clear Record] to clear the entered data.

  3. Click [Execute Query] or press [F8] to search for the existing records.
