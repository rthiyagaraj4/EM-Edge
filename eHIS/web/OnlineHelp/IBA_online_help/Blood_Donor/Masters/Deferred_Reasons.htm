Deferred Reasons

From this function you can Define the reasons to defer a blood donor by specifying the deferral type and period. You can Query for the existing deferral reason records.

The Deferred Reasons function enables you to define the reasons for deferring a donor. A donor can be deferred from donating blood, temporarily or permanently based on the pre-donation analysis. Specify the duration of deferment by selecting the deferral period, if the deferral type is temporary.

Defining Deferred Reasons

From the Masters, double-click the Deferred Reasons function.

The Deferred Reasons screen gets displayed.

  1. Enter the details in the mandatory fields. <Related Field Description>

  2. Click [Save] or press [F10] to save the created records or click [Clear Record] to clear the entered data.

  3. Click [Execute Query] or press [F8] to search for the existing records.
