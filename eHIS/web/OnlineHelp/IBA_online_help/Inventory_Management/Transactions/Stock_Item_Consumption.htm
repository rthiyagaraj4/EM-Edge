Stock Item Consumption

From this function you can Record the consumption of Stock Items within the Store.

The consumption is recorded as a general consumption or as consumption against a specific patient encounter, where the patient is an Inpatient, Outpatient or a Referral patient.

Recording Stock Transfer Details

From the ST Transactions > Issue, select the Stock Item Consumption function.

The Stock Item Consumption screen gets displayed.

  1. Click [Create] to invoke the Create screen.

  2. Modify Document Details screen is displayed by default or click ‘Document’ hyperlink to modify the document details.

  3. On entering the Document Details, click [OK] to record the consumption of Stock Item details or [Reset] to clear the entered data.

  4. Enter the details in the mandatory (*) fields.<Related Field Description>

  5. Enter the Item to record the consumption of Stock Item within the Store in alphanumeric format of 60 characters maximum length or select the Item using [?] function; displays the Item Search screen. Refer <Related Field Descriptions>

The item should be in alphanumeric format of 60 characters maximum length. This is a mandat ory field.

You can click the information icon next to the Item for viewing the ‘Item Remarks’.

  1. Enter the search criteria and click Search; displays the search results.

  2. On selecting the Item, the Unit of Measure and the available stock quantity is displayed.

  3. Enter the Quantity of the Item, which needs to be recorded in numeric format of six digits maximum length. The entered quantity must not be greater than the available quantity of the Item or an error message will be displayed. This is a mandatory field.

  4. Enter the Remarks in alphanumeric format of 200 characters maximum length. The Remarks field is mandatory if the ‘Header Level Remarks Required’ check box is selected in the Transactions Remarks screen

  5. Click [Edit/View] to record the consumption of the first expiring batch in the slot. The Item Batch details are displayed. The Quantity entered is displayed as the Sale Quantity.

  6. Click [Batch Search] to view the Batch details. The selected Item Batch details are displayed. Select the check box to select the Batch and click [Close] to exit from the batch search screen. If batch details are selected using [Edit/View], the selected Batch details are refreshed on selecting the new batch.

  7. On selecting the batch, the batch details are displayed in the Batch Entry area.

  8. Enter the sale quantity in numeric format of five digits maximum length.

  9. Click [Add] to add the Item for transfer or [Cancel] to cancel the selected Item details.

  10. The selected Item details are displayed in the Item Details frame on the left hand side. Click the Information icon near the item name to view either 'Remarks' or 'Specification' or both for the selected item in a popup or as a tool tip. The Information icon will be available only if either 'Remarks' or 'Specification' or both exists for that item. Click ‘Item Details’ hyperlink to modify the Item consumption details. Click [Modify] to modify the entered details or [Cancel] to cancel the modified changes.

  11. You can select the ‘Delete’ check box to delete the selected record. The Delete check box is selected or cleared by default based on the setup done in Kit Template screen.

  12. Click [Preview] to view the Stock Item Consumption preview details.

  13. Click [Apply] to record the Item consumption details or [Reset] to clear the entered data.
