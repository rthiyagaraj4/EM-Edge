Enter the following details to issue Urgent request documents:

  1. Issue UOM – Select the required UOM for issue from the list. On selecting the UOM the corresponding conversion factor is displayed against the Issue UOM.

  2. Issue UOM Qty – Enter the Quantity of Item to be issued in the selected UOM in numeric format of 10 digits maximum length. This is a mandatory field.

  3. Quantity – The quantity based on the entered issue UOM quantity is displayed and disabled. The entered quantity must not be greater than the available quantity of the Item or an error message is displayed. This is a mandatory field.

  4. Remarks - Enter the Item remarks for generating urgent issue document in alphanumeric format of 200 characters maximum length.
