Compute Stock Levels (Procurement)

From this function you can Compute Stock Levels required for Procurement based on the consumption of the Item.

This function allows Computing Stock Levels required for an Item and this is computed based on the period of consumption of the Item that is defined. Once the system computes stock levels in terms of Minimum stock required, Maximum stock required, Re-order level and Reorder quantity, the same can be updated in the Item function which is taken into account for future procurements.

Computing Stock Levels Required for Procurement

From the ST Administration, select the Compute Stock Levels (Procurement) function.

The Compute Stock Levels (Procurement) screen gets displayed.

  1. Enter the details in the mandatory (*) fields. <Related Field Description>

  2. Click [Compute] to display to compute the stock levels for procurement.

  3. Select the check box against the Item and enter updated compute details in Computed Details frame.

    Enter the values in the Computed Details frame:

     

    1. Min Stock – The new minimum stock required is displayed based on the average consumption per day for the entered consumption period or enter the quantity of minimum stock required for the Item in numeric format of 12 digits maximum length.

    2. Max Stock - The new maximum stock required is displayed based on the average consumption per day for the entered consumption period or enter the quantity of minimum stock required for the item in numeric format of 12 digits maximum length.

    3. RO Level – The new Reorder Level for the Item is displayed based on the average consumption per day for the entered consumption period or enter the new reorder level for the Item in numeric format of 12 digits maximum length.

    4. RO Quantity - The new Reorder Quantity for the Item is displayed based on the average consumption per day for the entered consumption period or enter the new reorder quantity for the item in numeric format of 12 digits maximum length.

  4. Click [Apply] to save the computed details or [Reset] to clear the entered data.

  5. On applying, the message ‘Stock Levels will be overwritten in the Item Master’ is displayed. Click [OK] to update the Stock Levels in Item function or [Cancel] to cancel the overwriting.
