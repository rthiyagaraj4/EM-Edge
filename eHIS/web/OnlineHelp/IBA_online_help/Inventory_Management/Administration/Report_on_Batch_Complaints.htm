Report on Batch Complaints

From this function you can Generate report on all complaints recorded against a particular batch.

The report is generated to study batch complaints based on a single Item for a period range. This information is used to study frequency of batch complaints against a particular Item or Supplier and take corrective measures to reduce such Vendor Returns.

Generating Reports on Batch Complaints

From the ST Administration, select the Report on Batch Complaints function.

The Report on Batch Complaints screen gets displayed.

  1. Enter the details in the mandatory fields. <Related Field Description>

  2. Click [Run] to generate report on batch complaints or [Reset] to clear the entered data.

  3. Select ‘Report Option’ and click [OK] to generate report based on the report criteria or [Cancel] to cancel the report generation.
