Enter the following details:

  1. Prescribed By - Select the Prescribed By options 'Strength', 'Ratio' or 'Percentage%'.

  2. Required Final Product Quantity - Enter the required final product quantity in numeric format of three digits maximum length. Select the UOM from the list. The UOM's defined in the Application Masters module are displayed for selection. This is a mandatory field.

  3. Drugs – Select the Drug on clicking [?] function. Maximum of four drugs can be selected for Compounding Rx.

  4. Strength – The Strength of the Drug is displayed. The strength of the drug is recorded in Drug Master function in Pharmacy Management module.

  5. Form – The Form of the drug is displayed. The Form of the drug is recorded in Drug Master Function.

  6. Ratio/Percentage -

  7. Ratio - If the selected 'Prescribed By' value is Ratio, enter the ratio in numeric format of two digits maximum length. On entering the Ratio, the Ratio of the selected ingredients is displayed and the Total Required quantity is displayed.

  8. Percentage - If the selected 'Prescribed By' value is Percentage%, enter the percentage in numeric format of two digits maximum length. On entering the Percentage, the percentage of the selected ingredients is displayed and the Total Required quantity is displayed. Select the 'Base' radio button to define the ingredients as the Base Quantity.  At least one item ingredient should be defined as base Quantity at the time of prescription.

  9. Required Quantity – Enter the required quantity of the drug that is to be mixed.

  10. Desired Quantity - The equivalent quantity UOM is calculated by the system for the selected ingredients irrespective of the prescribed UOM and displayed in the final product quantity UOM.

 

Admin Details

Enter the following details:

  1. Dosage – Enter the Dosage for the Drug, Select the Drug Strength. The strength will be populated according to the Drug selected.

  2. Frequency – Select Frequency for the Compounding Drug.

  3. Admixture – Select the check box to indicate that IV Admixture needs to be prepared for the patient.

  4. Build MAR – By Build MAR is default checked and enabled for inpatients. For remaining patient classes based upon parameter settings it will be checked and enabled.

  5. Duration – Enter the Duration in number of days. Number of days should be in 11 Characters of maximum length.

  6. Start Date – Enter the Start Date and Time for the duration on clicking the Date function.

  7. End Date – End Date and time will be displayed on the basis of start date and Duration specified.
