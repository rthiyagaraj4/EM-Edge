Enter the following details to update the status of the recorded external treatment:

 

  1. Status – Select a Status from the list. Select ‘Incomplete’, if the external treatment on the selected tooth was incomplete. Select ‘Complete’ to mark the external treatment as ‘Complete’. Select ‘Mark Error’, if the external treatment was marked by error. When ‘Marked Error’ is selected, the recorded condition is cancelled and you can record a new condition against the same tooth. On selecting the condition as ‘Mark Error’, a new field ‘Cancel Reason’ is enabled which is mandatory.

  2. Cancel Reason – Click Cancel Reason hyperlink to record the reason for cancelling the treatment. Enter the reason in alphanumeric format. This is a mandatory field. This field is displayed only when the ‘Status’ is selected as ‘Mark Error’.

  3. Outcome – Select the Outcome of the treatment from the list. The system defined values are ‘Success’ and ‘Failure’. Select Success to record the external treatment as complete. Select Failure to mark the external treatment as incomplete.

  4. Remarks – Enter the Remarks in alphanumeric format. This indicates the reason for the external treatment.

  5. Click [Record] to record the status for the selected Condition or [Reset] to reset and record a new condition. On saving the record, the condition is cancelled and it is not listed on the right hand side menu. The images on the tooth for the selected treatment also are deleted.
