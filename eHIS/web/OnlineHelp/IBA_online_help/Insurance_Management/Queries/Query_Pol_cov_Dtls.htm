Query Policy Coverage Details

From this function you can Query the policy details covered for a patient admitted in the hospital.

The Query Policy Coverage Details function is used to view the policy coverage details for a relevant patient admitted in the facility. The details recorded using the function Encounter Specific Policy Definition – Services for a particular patient, gets displayed on clicking [Query Service Policy Details]. You also have an option to view the policy details for a material item by clicking on [Query Material Item Policy Details]. Click [Execute Query] to view the policy coverage details. To view the policy coverage details for a selected patient, click [Enter Query], enter the patient ID and click [Execute Query].

Query Policy Coverage Details

From the Queries, double-click on Query Policy Coverage Details function.

The Query Policy Coverage Details screen gets displayed.

  1. Click [List of Values] to select a Patient Number. The patient lookup screen gets displayed.

  2. Click [Execute Query] or press [F8] to view all the patient details.

OR

  1. Click [Enter Query] and enter the detail in the respective text box. Click [Execute Query] or press [F8] to view the selected patient record.

  2. Double-click on the record to select it. The patient details get displayed in the respective text boxes.

  3. Click [Query Service Policy Details] to view the details recorded using the function Encounter Specific Policy Definition – Services for the selected patient during a specific encounter. You can only view, but cannot modify the details.

  4.  Click [Query Material Item Policy Details] to view the details recorded using the function Encounter Specific Policy Definition – Material Items for the selected patient during a specific encounter. You can only view, but cannot modify the details.
