Copy Policy Definition – Services (Single)

From this function you can Copy the multiple services associated to a Policy Type, Customer and a Customer Group association to another.

The function Copy Policy Definition – Services (Single) is used to copy the services from a Policy Type to another. The Customer Group, Customer and Policy Type association has to be defined using the function Setup Service Coverage Detail by Policy. The services associated to the Policy Type are associated to the  patient when the respective Policy Type is selected during patient billing transactions.

Copy Policy Definition – Services (Single)

From the Masters, double-click on Copy Policy Definition - Services function.

The Copy Policy Definition – Services (Single) screen gets displayed.

  1. Enter the details in the mandatory (*) fields. <Related Field Description>

  2. Click [Commit Form] or press [F10] to save a created record or [Clear Record] to clear the entered or queried data.

 

The Customer, Customer Group and Policy Type association has to be defined to copy the services from a Policy Type to another.
