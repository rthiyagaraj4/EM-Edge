Setup Service Coverage Detail by Policy

From this function you can Define the Service Coverage details for a Policy Type pertinent to the Facility. You can Query to view the existing records.

The function Setup Service Coverage Detail By Policy is used to set up the coverage details for a relevant Policy Type. The Coverage Limit and the Payment Basis are defined and associated to a relevant Patient Class. You have to associate a Policy Type to the Payer Group, Payer and the Facility and record the coverage percentage for the selected Policy Type. The Coverage rule and the coverage percentage is applicable for the services and the material items associated for a selected Payer in a Payer Group and linked to a particular Policy Type. You have an option to include or exclude the material items and define the coverage limit amount for an Outpatient or an Inpatient limit type. Default material item criteria are defined. You can override the inclusion or exclusion of unspecified services by selecting or clearing the relevant check box.

 

You also have an option to include only selected services under the service coverage or exclude certain services from the coverage. Default service policy criteria are defined. You can define whether Credit Authorization is required or whether the Service Policy coverage is covered for an Outpatient/Emergency, Inpatient/DayCare or an external patient class. You can associate the approved days and approved amount to a coverage limit type. Whether mandatory checks for Bill and Invoice Generation are required or not is also defined.  The percentage of amount settled by the payer for the services associated to the Policy Type is also defined. The payer settles the amount specified when the bill is generated for the services utilized by the patient holding the relevant Insurance Policy. The price distribution concept is valid for encounter under the 'Coverage Limit Criteria', if the 'Coverage Limit Includes Other Payers' is selected.

Setup Service Coverage Detail by Policy

From the Masters, double-click on Setup Service Coverage Detail by Policy function.

The Setup Service Coverage Detail by Policy screen gets displayed.

  1. The logged in Facility is displayed, by default. You can select a Facility to define the service coverage detail by policy setup pertinent to the respective Facility. Default values are displayed in some fields on trying to create a new record.

  2. Enter the details in the mandatory (*) fields. <Related Field Description>

  3. Enter the details under the Service Policy Coverage  tab to include or exclude the services from the Policy Type. The Service Policy Coverage is applicable for the Outpatients, Inpatients and the External Patients availing service from the facility holding relevant Policy Type.

    Service Policy Coverage

    1. Default Exclude/Include Criteria - Select a value from the list. The system defined values are ‘All Services Included’ and ‘All Services Excluded’. ‘All Services Included’ is displayed, by default.

    2. All Services Included – Select All Services Included to include only selected services for policy coverage.

    3. All Services Excluded – Select All Services Excluded to exclude selected services from policy coverage.

    1. Service Exclude/Include Criteria - Select a value from the list. The values are system defined – ‘Service Group/Classification’, ‘Enterprise Group/Line’ and ‘Payer Service Group’. ‘Service Group/Classification is displayed, by default. The value selected is either included or excluded from the policy coverage based on the value selected for ‘Default Exclude/Include Criteria’. For example, on selecting Service Group/Classification, the services under the selected Service Group/Classification are either included or excluded.

    2. Service Coverage Limit Criteria - Select a value from the list. The system defined values are ‘By Payer Service Group’ and ‘By Enterprise Service Group/Line’. ‘By Enterprise Service Group/Line’ is displayed, by default.

    3. Override Inclusion/Exclusion for Unspecified Services – Select the check box to override the inclusion or the exclusion criteria for the services. If the check box is selected, you can include or exclude additional services to the policy type for a patient encounter. The override performs inverse action based on the value selected for ‘Service Exclude/Include Criteria’.

    4. Coverage includes other Payer - Select the check box to define an additional payer for the specific Policy, Payer and Payer Group. Clear the check box, if an additional payer is not required for the selected combination.

    5. Last Payer – Select the check box, if the payer is the last payer. Based on whether the coverage amount is selected and the payer is the last payer or not, the policy coverage amount is distributed as follows:

    6. If the check boxes ‘Coverage includes other Payers’ and ‘Last Payer’ are selected, the amount is sent as discount.

    7. If the check box ‘Coverage includes other Payers’ is selected and the check box ‘Last Payer’ is cleared, the remaining amount has to be paid by the patient.

    The Service Coverage is defined for the following patient class:

    Outpatient/Emergency

    Inpatient/Daycare

    External

  4. Enter the details under the Material Item Policy Coverage  tab to include or exclude the material items under the Policy Coverage. You can also define the coverage limit amount for the Inpatient and the Outpatients.

    You can define the Policy Coverage Details for a material item in the facility store using the tab. The material items associated to each patient class can have a separate coverage amount. You can either include/exclude the material items from the policy coverage.

    Enter the following details:

    1. Default item Incl Excl Criteria - Select a value from the list. The system defined values are ‘All Items Included’ and ‘All Items Excluded’. ‘All Items Included’ is displayed, by default.

    2. All Items Included – Select All Items Included to include only selected items for policy coverage.

    3. All Items Excluded – Select All Items Excluded to exclude selected items from policy coverage.

    1. Material Item Exclude/Include Criteria - Select a value from the list. The system defined values are ‘Sale Category’, ‘Enterprise Material Group’ and ‘Payer Material Group’. The values for each of the Material Item selected get populated from the Patient Billing Manager module. The item selected is either included or excluded from the policy coverage based on the value selected for ‘Default item Incl Excl Criteria’.

    2. Material Item Coverage Limit Criteria - Select a value from the list. The system defined values are ‘Payer Material Group’, ‘Sale Category’ and ‘Enterprise Material Group’. The value can be selected from the list based on the ‘Material Item Exclude/Include Criteria’ selected. ‘Payer Material Group’ is displayed, by default.

    3. Override Inclusion/Exclusion for Unspecified Services – Select the check box to override the inclusion or the exclusion criteria for the material items, which are not specified. The override performs inverse action based on the value selected for ‘Material Item Exclude/Include Criteria’.

    4. Outpatient Coverage Limit Type - Select a value from the list. The system defined values are ‘Restricted’ and ‘Un-Restricted’. ‘Un-Restricted’ is displayed, by default.

    5. Restricted – Select Restricted to restrict the coverage limit amount for the Outpatients using the material items and associated to the selected policy type code.

    6. Un-Restricted - Select Un-Restricted and the Coverage Limit is not restricted for the Outpatients while using the material items associated to the selected Policy Type, Payer and the Payer Group.

    1. Inpatient Coverage Limit Type - Select a value from the list. The system defined values are ‘Restricted’ and ‘Un-Restricted’. ‘Un-Restricted’ is displayed, by default.

    2. Restricted – Select Restricted to restrict the coverage limit amount for Inpatients using the material items and associated to the selected policy type code.

    3. Un-Restricted - Select Un-Restricted and the Coverage Limit is not restricted for the Inpatients while using the material items associated to the selected Policy Type, Payer and the Payer Group.

  5. Click Policy Excluded/Included Service Details to associate or dissociate certain services from the Policy Coverage in the respective facility for the selected Customer, Customer Group and Policy Type combination.

    Policy Excluded/Included Service Details

    1. Exclude/Include Criteria - Select a value from the list. The system defined values are ‘Billing Service’, ‘Billing Service Group’ and ‘Billing Service Group/Class’. ‘Billing Service’ is displayed, by default.

    2. Patient Class – Click [List of Values] to select a Patient Class. The selected Billing Service Any Service utilized by the patient from the facility. is either excluded or included for the selected Patient Class. The values are system defined- Inpatient, Outpatient, Daycare, Emergency, External and All. Select ‘All’, if the selected Billing Service is either excluded or included in the coverage details for all the patient class in the facility.

    3. Code – Click [List of Values] to view and select a Billing Service. The selected Billing Service is either included or excluded from the Policy Coverage based on the Exclude/Include Criteria selected.

    4. Description – The description for the selected Billing Service gets displayed.

    5. Special Service – Select the check box to include or exclude any other special services. For example, a high value drug can be a special service.

    6. Excl/Incl Indicator – Select either Exclude or Include Indicator from the list. The special services are associated to the selected Policy based on the value selected from the list.

    7. Override Incl/Excl – Select the check box to override the include/exclude criteria for a selected Billing Service. If the check box is cleared, you cannot override the include/exclude criteria for the selected Billing Service. The override performs inverse action based on the value selected for ‘Excl/Incl Indicator’.

    8. Inclusion Basis – This field is enabled only if the Excl/Incl Indicator is selected as ‘Include’. Select a value from the list. The system defined values are ‘Percentage’, ‘Rate’ and ‘Fixed’.

    9. Percentage - Select Percentage to calculate the coverage amount in terms of percentage for the selected service when the Excl/Incl Indicator is selected as ‘Include’.

    10. Rate - Select Rate to calculate the coverage price as an amount for the selected service when the Excl/Incl Indicator is selected as ‘Include’.

    11. Fixed - Select Fixed to calculate the coverage amount in terms fixed price for the selected service when the Excl/Incl Indicator is selected as ‘Include’.

    1. Include Policy Level Coverage – Select the check box to include coverage details for the selected Billing Service associated to the Policy.

    2. Co-pay/Patient Payable Value – On selecting the Inclusion Basis as ‘Percentage’, the Coverage percentage value is displayed. On selecting the Inclusion Basis as ‘Rate’ or ‘Fixed’, enter the value in numeric format to define the amount, which has to be settled by the co-payers associated to the payer or the amount to be settled by the patient for the Billing Service when the Excl/Incl Indicator is selected as ‘Include’.

    3. Pre-Approval Required – Select the check box, if a pre-approval is required to include or exclude the Billing Services to the selected Policy Type, Payer and Payer Group. The approval is required in the billing transaction functions.

    4. Coverage includes other Payers - Select the check box to define an additional payer for the specific Policy, Payer and Payer Group. Clear the check box, if an additional payer is not required for the selected combination.

    5. Last Payer - Select the check box, if the payer is the last payer. Based on whether the coverage amount is selected and the payer is the last payer or not, the policy coverage amount is distributed as follows:

    6. If the check boxes ‘Coverage includes other Payers’ and ‘Last Payer’ are selected, the amount is sent as discount.

    7. If the check box ‘Coverage includes other Payers’ is selected and the check box ‘Last Payer’ is cleared, the remaining amount has to be paid by the patient.  

  6. Click Coverage Limit Criteria to specify the coverage amount for an Inpatient admission, Inpatient stay in the facility or an Outpatient visit to the facility. You can define the coverage amount for a specific Billing service used by a patient class.

    Coverage Limit Criteria

    Using this tab, you can restrict the policy coverage amount for a Billing Service, Enterprise Service Group or Enterprise Service Group/Line for a specific patient class in the facility. You can specify the coverage amount for an Outpatient visit, an Inpatient admission or for an Inpatient stay for a specific number of days in the facility. The coverage limit is valid for patient encounters, if the ‘Coverage includes Other Payers’ check box is selected.

    Enter the following details to specify the coverage amount:

    1. Exclude/Include Criteria - Select a value from the list. The system defined – “Billing Service”, “Billing Service Group” and “Billing Service Group/Class”.

    2. Patient Class – Click [List of Values] to select a Patient Class. The selected Billing Service is either excluded or included for the selected Patient Class. The system defined values are Inpatient, Outpatient, Daycare, Emergency, External and All. Select ‘All’, if the selected Billing Service is either excluded or included in the coverage details for all the patient class in the facility.

    3. Code – Click [List of Values] to view and select a Billing Service. The selected Billing Service is either included or excluded from the Policy Coverage based on the Exclude/Include Criteria selected.

    4. Description – The description for the selected Billing Service gets displayed.

    5. Amt Per Visit – Enter the coverage amount per Visit. The Insurance Company pays the amount specified towards an Outpatient visit for the selected Policy Type.

    6. Amt Per Admission - Enter the coverage amount per Admission. The Insurance Company pays the amount specified towards an Inpatient admission for the selected Policy Type.

    7. Per Inpatient Day – Enter the coverage amount for Inpatients per day. The amount set is specific only to the Inpatients admitted in the facility for the selected Policy Type.

    8. No Of Days – Enter the number of days for which the coverage amount is applicable towards the inpatient stay in the facility.

    9. Override Coverage Limit – Select the check box, if the coverage amount set can be changed during transaction.

  7. Click Payment Basis by Patient Class to define the payment basis for a patient class in the facility.

    Payment Basis by Patient Class

    You can define the amount per relative weight for diagnosis in the facility and the expected less percentage from the Customer for a specific patient class.

    1. Patient Class – Click [List of Values] to select a Patient Class. The selected Billing Service is either excluded or included for the selected Patient Class. The description for the selected Patient Class is displayed.

    2. Payment Basis – The value is system defined – 'DRG-based Relative Weight/Fee For Service'. DRG is an acronym for Diagnosis Related Group. The amount is set to percentage and is fixed.

    3. Amt per Relative Wt - Enter the amount per relative weight in numeric format. The payment basis is calculated based on the relative weight. Relative weight is the quantity of the service utilized.

    4. Expected Less Payment %age - Enter in numeric format the percentage of amount. This amount cannot be more than 100. This indicates the amount, which is doubtful to be paid for a particular patient class.

    5. Specific Payer Group Reim - Select the check box to define the expected less payment percentage for a specific payer. Clear the check box, if the expected less payment percentage is not applicable.

  8. Click [View Past Prices] to view the prices set previously for the Price Class.

  9. Click [Commit Form] or press [F10] to save or [Clear Record] to clear the entered data.

  10. Click [Execute Query] or press [F8] to search for the existing records within the facility.
