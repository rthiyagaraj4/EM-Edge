The following details are displayed:

 

Adjustment Details

The initial adjustments are highlighted in  color and latest adjustments using color.

  1. Blng Group – The Billing Groups associated to the patient during admission is displayed.

  2. Payer – The Payer associated to the patient during admission is displayed.

 

Old Value

The actual amount before adjustment is displayed.

  1. Gross Amt – The actual Gross Amount to be paid by the Payer for the Service is displayed.

  2. Disc Amt – The Discount Amount for the respective Payer, Payer Group and Policy Type combination is displayed.

  3. Net Amt – The actual Net Amount for the respective Payer, Payer Group and Policy Type combination is displayed. This is displayed as the difference between the Gross Amount and the Discount Amount.

  4. CR/DR –The value is either displayed as CR or DR, based on the value selected while adjusting the payer amount.

The gross amount has to be lesser than or equal to the actual gross amount.

The discount amount has to be lesser than or equal to the actual gross amount.

The total gross adjustment amount has to be zero.

 

New Value

  1. Gross Adjustment – The Gross Amount adjusted is displayed.

  2. New Gross Amt - The New Gross Amount is displayed based on the Gross Adjustment Amount, Net Amount and the value selected for CR/DR. For Example, if the Gross Adjustment Amount is 100, Net Amt is 50 and the value is selected as CR, the Net Gross Amt is displayed as 150. If the Gross Adjustment Amount is 100, Net Amt is 50 and the value is selected as DR, the Net Gross Amt is displayed as 50.

  3. New Disc Amt – The new discount amount is displayed.

  4. New Net Amt – The New Net amount is displayed as the difference of New Gross Amount and Discount Adjustment, that is (New Gross Amt - Disc Adjustment = New Net Amt).

 

Adjustment Summary

  1. Payer – The Payer details are listed based on the adjustments made. The description of the selected Payer is displayed.

  2. Amount – The adjusted amount is displayed for the respective Payer.
