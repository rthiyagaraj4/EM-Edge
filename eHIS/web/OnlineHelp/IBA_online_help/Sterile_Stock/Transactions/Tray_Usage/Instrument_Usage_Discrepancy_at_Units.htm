Instrument Usage/Discrepancy at Units

From this function you can Record the details of instruments fixed to the patients and the discrepancy details of the Tray.

The Instrument Usage/Discrepancy At Units aids the user at the requestor location in entering the details of instruments fixed to the patients and recording instruments discrepancy details of the tray encountered, if any.

Recording the Instrument details and tray discrepancy details

From SS Transactions > Tray Usage, select the Instrument Usage/Discrepancy at Units function.

The Instrument Usage/Discrepancy at Units screen gets displayed.

  1. Click [Create] to invoke the Create screen.

  2. Enter the details in the mandatory (*) fields. <Related Field Description>

  3. Click [Add] to add the Instrument Usage or Discrepancy details or [Cancel] to clear the entered data.

  4. On adding the Instrument Usage or Discrepancy details, the details are displayed in the right hand side of the screen.

  5. Select ‘Del’ check box to exclude the recorded Instrument Usage or Discrepancy details.

  6. Click ‘Instrument Description’ hyperlink to view or modify the Instrument Usage or Discrepancy details.

  7. Click [Modify] to modify the changes or [Cancel] to clear the entered data.

  8. Click [Apply] to record the Instrument Usage or Discrepancy details or [Reset] to clear the entered data.
