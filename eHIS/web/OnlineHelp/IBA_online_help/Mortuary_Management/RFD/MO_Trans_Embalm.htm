Enter the following details:

 

Details

1. Patient is HIV+ - Select this check box if the patient was tested positive for HIV.

2. Patient is HEP+ - Select this check box if the patient was tested positive for HEP.

3. Place Order – Click this button to place the order for embalm service.

 

Embalm Form Details

4. Name of Applicant – Enter the name of the applicant who is requesting the embalm service.

5. Date of Application – Select the date of application from the calendar lookup.

6. Name Prefix – Select the name prefix from the drop-down list. You can select, Mr.or Mrs.

7. First Name – Enter the first name of the applicant.

8. Second Name - Enter the second name of the applicant.

9. Family name - Enter the family name of the applicant.

10. Contact No. of applicant – Enter the contact number of the applicant.

11. Identity Card/ Passport No. of Applicant – Enter the identity card or passport number of the applicant.

 

Address of applicant

12. Address lines 1, 2, 3 , 4 - Enter the address of the applicant in fields Address lines 1, 2, 3, and 4.

13. Town – Enter the town of the applicant.

14. Area – Enter the area of the applicant.

15. Postal Code – Enter the postal code of the applicant.

16. Country – Enter the country of the applicant.

 

Details of Deceased to be Embalmed

Note: These details are automatically displayed as recorded in the Deceased Registration page.

17. Name – Name of the deceased.  

18. Identity Card/ Passport No – The identity card or passport number of the deceased.

19. Date of Birth – The date of birth of the deceased.

20. Age – The age of the deceased.

21. Address lines 1, 2, 3 , 4 - The address of the deceased.

22. Town – The town of the deceased.

23. Area – The area of the deceased.

24. Postal Code – The postal code of the deceased.

25. Country – The country of the deceased.

 

Details to be filled my Mortuary Staff

26. Date of Embalming Procedure – Select the date from the calendar lookup.

27. Amount of Embalming fluid used – Enter the quantity of embalming fluid used to embalm the deceased body, in litres. You can enter maximum of enter 3 digits.

28. Time of Embalming Procedure (From and To) – Enter the start and end time of the embalming procedure.
