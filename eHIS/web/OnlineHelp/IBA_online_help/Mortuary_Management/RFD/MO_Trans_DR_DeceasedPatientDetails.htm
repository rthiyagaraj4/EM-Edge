Deceased Registration

Details of the patient already captured earlier are displayed. You cannot modify any of the details displayed as read only text. Enter details to the fields which are not filled.

  1. Burial Permit No - The burial permit number is displayed and disabled if postmortem is not required for the patient.

  2. Deceased Patient ID - The patient ID of the deceased is displayed and disabled.

  3. Patient details like the name, age, sex, attending practitioner, location, patient Id and Encounter Id are displayed.

  4. Click [Demographics Details] to view patient details.

  5. Click [Previous Encounters] to view all the previous encounters of the patient in the facility.

This screen comprises of four tabs namely

Registration Details

Organ Donation

Claimant

Burial Permit

 

When you transfer a deceased from one facility to another, say facility A to facility B, the following conditions apply:

  1. Details recorded in all tabs except the Claimant tab will be displayed by default in the facility B.

  2. In facility B, you can make changes to the details in the tabs ‘Registration’ and 'Organ Donation’.

  3. If burial permit details have been recorded in facility A, they will be displayed by default in facility B and cannot be edited. Else, you can record the burial permit details in facility B.

  4. You can click the ‘Default Previous Claimant Details’ hyperlink available in facility B and copy the claimant details recorded in facility A to B.

  5. This will be applicable for external patients as well.
