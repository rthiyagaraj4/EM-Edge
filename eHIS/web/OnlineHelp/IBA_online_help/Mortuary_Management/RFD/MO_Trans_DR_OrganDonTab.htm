Organ Donation Tab

This tab is used to record the details of the organs for donation of the deceased. If the deceased patient has a donor card, the information can be captured to donate organs else the consent of the claimant should be entered to donate organs of the deceased. Click the 'Organ Donation' tab to view the screen.

Tissue Procurement Registration

  1. Donor Card Available - Select the check box if a donor card is available for the deceased patient. On selecting the check box, [Donor Card Info] is enabled and [Consent] is disabled.

  2. Click [Donor Card Info] to view the Patient Donor Card Information screen.

    1. Donor Card No - Enter the Donor Card number in alphanumeric format of 100 characters maximum length. This is a mandatory field.

    2. Details - Enter the details of the donor card in alphanumeric format of 500 characters maximum length. This is a mandatory field.

    It is mandatory to enter the donor card information in [Donor Card Info], if the 'Donor Card Available' check box is checked.

    1. Click [Close] to save the record and close the window.

  3. In order to record the consent details for organ donation of the deceased, click [Consent]. This button is enabled only if the 'Donor Card Available' field is unchecked.

List of Organ for Donation

The list of organs that were defined in the "Organs for Donation" function is displayed. Select the required organs by selecting the check box to indicate selection of those organs for donation.
