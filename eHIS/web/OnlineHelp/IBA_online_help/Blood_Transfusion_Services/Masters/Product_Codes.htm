Product Codes

From this function you can Define a blood products and associate it to an order catalogue. You can also Define a new order catalogue to associate the blood product. You can Query for the existing records.

A blood product is any component of the blood collected from a donor for use in a blood transfusion. Transfusion of Whole Blood is not common at present. Most of the blood products consist of specific processed components, such as, red blood cells, blood plasma or platelets. The Products function enables you to create different blood products. The product code and orderable (packed red cells, washed red cells, etc.) comprise of different blood group combinations and Rhesus factors. You can associate them to a defined product category. The product code function enables you to associate the details such as donation, stock level, order, expiry date, volume, confirmation required, specimen and barcode through different tab frames.

Defining Product Codes

From the Masters, double-click Product Codes function.

The Product Codes screen gets displayed.

  1. Enter the details in the mandatory (*) fields found in different tabs. <Related Field Description>

  2. Enter the details in the following tabs:

Blood Type/Stock Levels

Order

Spec Type

Barcode

  1. Click [Commit] or press [F10] to save the Product codes created.

  2. Click [Execute Query] or press [F8] to search for the existing records.
