Enter the following details to update the blood group details of the patient, based on the investigations made:

 

Change To

  1. Blood Group – Click [List of Values] to select and associate a Blood Group to the patient. If any specimens are in the process of transfusion or any transfusion is already performed to the patient, you cannot amend the blood group and rhesus factor of the patient.

  2. Rhesus – Click [List of Values] to select and associate a Rhesus factor.

Rare Blood Group

  1. Rare Blood Group (Y/N)? – Select the check box to indicate that the patient’s blood group is a rare blood group. Clear the check box, if the Blood Group is not a rare Blood Group.

Reason

  1. Reason – Click [List of Values] to select an appropriate reason for amending the Blood Group and Rhesus Factor. The list displays the values defined in the ‘Reason’ function. This is a mandatory field.
