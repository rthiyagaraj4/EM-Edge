The screen displays the patient information recorded while registering the patient through Master Patient Index module.

 

  1. Patient ID – The ID of the patient is displayed.

  2. Nationality – The Nationality of the patient is displayed.

  3. Age - The age of the patient is displayed.

  4. Sex – The gender of the patient is displayed.

  5. Race - The race of the patient is displayed.

  6. Facility - The facility name of the patient is displayed.

  7. Episode Type – The Episode Type of the patient is displayed.

  8. Visit Tfr No – The Visit Transfer Number of the patient is displayed.

  9. Source – The source name is displayed.

  10. Room No/Bed No – The Room Number or the Bed Number is displayed.

  11. Ordering Doctor – The name of the Ordering Doctor is displayed.

  12. Attending Doctor - The name of the Attending Doctor is displayed.

 

Blood Type

  1. Blood Group – The Blood Group of the patient is displayed.

  2. Rhesus Code – The Rhesus Code of the patient is displayed.

  3. RH Pheno Type – The RH Phenomenon description defined in the Result Comment is displayed.

  4. [Antibodies] – Click [Antibodies] to view the Antibody details of the selected patient.

 

Referral Details

  1. Referral Hospital - The name of the Referral Hospital is displayed.

  2. Location – The location of the Referral hospital is displayed.

  3. Referral Patient ID – The referral patient ID is displayed.

  4. [Close] - Click [Close] to close the screen.
