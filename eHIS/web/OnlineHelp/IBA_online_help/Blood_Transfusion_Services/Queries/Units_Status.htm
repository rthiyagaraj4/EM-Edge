Unit Status

From this function you can Query the Status of the blood unit.

The Units Status function enables you to view the status of the blood unit and antigens present in the blood unit.

Querying the Unit Status

From the Queries, double-click Unit Status function.

The Unit Status screen gets displayed.

  1. Click [Execute Query] or press [F8] to search for the Unit Status. You can navigate to the next and the previous record using the downward and the upward arrows on the keyboard.

  2. Click [Enter Query] or press [F7] and click [List of Values] to search for a specific Product, Unit Number, Rhesus or Blood group code. Click [Execute Query] or press [F8] to view the details associated. <Related Field Description>

  3. The Original Unit Number recorded for the respective Product through the 'Blood Units Entry' function is displayed. The process details and the date on which the process details are recorded for the respective Product through the 'Allocate/XMatch/Issue' Blood Units function are displayed.

  4. Click [Unit Attributes] to view the details of the blood Unit Attributes associated to the selected unit.

  5. Click [Unit Antibodies] to view the details of the blood Unit Antibodies associated to the selected unit.

  6. Click [Antigens] to view the details of the Antigens associated to the selected blood unit.

  7. Click [View Multi Allocation] to view the Multi-Allocation details of the selected blood unit.
