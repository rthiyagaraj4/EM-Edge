Assign Treating Practitioner

From this function you can Assign Practitioners who render care to the patient in addition to the Admitting and Attending Practitioner. You can Modify the existing treating Practitioners assigned to the patient.

The roles assigned to these additional practitioners are either consulting practitioners for the current encounter or assisting practitioners. The Practitioner is assigned a specified period for the role. If the end date of the period is not specified, the system automatically ends the role on the day the patient is discharged from the Nursing Unit. This function is used to modify the existing information pertaining to the consultant and Assisting Practitioner for each inpatient.

The logged in user must have rights to assign a treating practitioner. Rights are defined in the Operator Station assigned to the logged in user.

Assigning Treating Practitioners

From the IP Transactions > Admission, select the Assign Treating Practitioner function.

The Assign Treating Practitioner screen gets displayed.

  1. Click [Search] to display all Inpatients or enter the search criteria to select a specific Inpatient to assign a treating practitioner.

    Enter the following search criteria:

    1. Nursing Unit – Enter the Nursing Unit in alphanumeric format of 15 characters maximum length or select the Nursing Unit using [?] function.

    2. Admission Date From-To – Enter the Admission Date range in date format of dd/mm/yyyy or select the date range using the calendar function.

    3. Specialty – Enter the Specialty in alphanumeric format of 15 characters maximum length or select the Specialty using [?] function.

    4. Practitioner – Enter the Practitioner Name in alphanumeric format of 30 characters maximum length or select the Practitioner using [?] function.

    5. Encounter ID – Enter the Encounter ID in numeric format of 12 digits maximum length.

    6. Patient ID - Enter the Patient ID in alphanumeric format of maximum length as specified in MP Parameter or select the Patient ID using [?] function.

  2. On searching, the Admission Details are displayed based on the entered criteria.

  3. Click ‘+’ hyperlink to view the Current Encounter Details.

  4. Click the ‘Encounter ID’ hyperlink to display the Assign Treating Practitioner screen. The screen displays the Admitting and Attending Practitioner details recorded during the patient admission.

  5. Enter details in the mandatory (*) fields. <Related Field Description>

  6. Click [Record] to assign a Practitioner to the selected patient, [Clear] to clear the entered data or [Close] to exit from the Assign Treating Practitioner screen.

  7. On recording, the treating practitioner details are displayed on the top half of the screen. Click Practitioner hyperlink to modify the Remarks.

  8. Click [Record] to save the modified details.

 

Any number of Practitioners can be associated to an Inpatient.
