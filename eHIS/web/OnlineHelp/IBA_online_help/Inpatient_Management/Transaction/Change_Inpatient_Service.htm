Change Inpatient Service

From this function you can Change the Service and Sub Service for an encounter of an Inpatient.

The Service is recorded if an Inpatient is not assigned a Service during Admission or if the Practitioner recommends changing the assigned Service.

Changing Inpatient Service

From the IP Transactions > Admission, select the Change Inpatient Service function.

The Change Inpatient Service screen gets displayed.

  1. Click [Search] to display the list of all Inpatients or enter the search criteria and click [Search] to select a specific Inpatient. Click [Clear] to reset the records displayed in the result area.

    Enter the following query criteria:

    1. Nursing Unit – Enter the Nursing Unit in alphanumeric format of 15 characters maximum length or select the Nursing Unit using the [?] function.

    2. Admission Date From-To – Enter the Admission Date range in format of dd/mm/yyyy or select the date using the calendar function.

    3. Specialty – Enter the Specialty in alphanumeric format of 15 characters maximum length or select the Specialty using the [?] function.

    4. Practitioner – Enter the name of the Practitioner in alphanumeric format of 30 characters maximum length or select the Practitioner using the [?] function.

    5. Encounter ID – Enter the Encounter ID in numeric format of 12 digits maximum length.

    6. Patient ID - Enter the Patient ID in alphanumeric format of maximum length as specified in MP Parameter or select the Patient ID using [?] function.

  2. On searching, Inpatient details are displayed based on the entered criteria.

  3. Click ‘+’ hyperlink to view the Current Encounter Details.

  4. Click the ‘Encounter ID’ hyperlink to display the Change Inpatient Service screen. The screen displays Encounter Details and Nursing Unit Details recorded during patient admission.

  5. Select the Service and the Sub-service associated to the Service from the list. If a Service is and Sub-service is assigned to the patient during admission, the same is displayed by default. The Services associated to the Nursing Unit is available for selection.

  6. Click [Record] to record the assigned Service for an encounter or [Close] to exit from screen.
