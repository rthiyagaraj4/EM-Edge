Record Procedure/Surgery Date

From this function you can Record the Procedure/Surgery Date of an Inpatient and Modify the Procedure/Surgery Date based on the instructions received, pertaining to the Inpatient.

The Procedure/Surgery Date can be recorded during admission. The facility can schedule its resources accordingly, once the Procedure/Surgery Date is known.

Recording Procedure/Surgery Date

From the IP Transactions > Admission, select the Record Procedure/Surgery Date function.

The Record Procedure/Surgery Date screen gets displayed.

  1. Click [Search] to display the list of all admitted patients or enter the search criteria and click [Search] to select a specific admitted patient. Click [Clear] to reset the records displayed in the result area.

    Enter the following query criteria:

    1. Nursing Unit – Enter the Nursing Unit in alphanumeric format of 15 characters maximum length or select the Nursing Unit using the [?] function.

    2. Admission Date From-To – Enter the Admission Date range in date format of dd/mm/yyyy or select the date using the calendar function.

    3. Specialty – Enter the Specialty in alphanumeric format of 15 characters maximum length or select the Specialty using the [?] function.

    4. Practitioner – Enter the name of the Practitioner in alphanumeric format of 30 characters maximum length or select the Practitioner using the [?] function.

    5. Encounter ID – Enter the Encounter ID in numeric format of 12 digits maximum length.

    6. Patient ID - Enter the Patient ID in alphanumeric format of maximum length as specified in MP Parameter or select the Patient ID using the [?] function.

  2. On searching, Admission Details of patients are displayed based on the entered criteria.

  3. Click ‘+’ hyperlink to view the Current Encounter Details.

  4. Click the ‘Encounter ID’ hyperlink to display the Record Procedure/Surgery Date screen. The screen displays Encounter Details, Nursing Unit Details and Billing Details as recorded during the patient admission.

  5. Enter the Procedure/Surgery Date in the format of dd/mm/yyyy hh:mm or select the date using calendar function. This field is mandatory. The entered date and time must be greater than the current system date and must not be less than the admission date and time.

  6. Click [Record] to record the Procedure/Surgery Date or [Close] to exit from the screen.
