Emergency Transfers List

From this function you can Query the emergency transfer list of inpatients.

The Emergency Transfers List function is used to query the list of emergency inpatient transfers within the facility.

Querying for Emergency Transfers List

From IP Queries, select the Emergency Transfers List function.

The Emergency Transfers List screen gets displayed.

  1. Enter the details in the mandatory (*) fields to query for Emergency Transfer List. <Related Field Description>

  2. Click [Search] to view the Emergency Transfer List details or [Reset] to clear the entered details.

  3. On searching, the Emergency Transfer List details are displayed.

  4. Click [Clear] to clear the details on the result screen.
