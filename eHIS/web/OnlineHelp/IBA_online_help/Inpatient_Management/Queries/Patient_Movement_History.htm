Patient Movement History

From this function you can Query on the movement history of Inpatients for a specific encounter.

The function provides various query criteria to filter the listing. On specifying the query criteria, the result screen displayed provides the movement history of inpatients currently in the facility. This helps tracking a patient's movement for a specific admission. The same result provides details on transfers and leaves applicable to a patient's inpatient encounter.

Querying for Patient Movement History

From IP Queries> Admissions/Transfers, select the Patient Movement History function.

The Patient Movement History screen gets displayed.

  1. Enter the details in the mandatory fields to query the Patient Movement History details. <Related Field Description>

  2. Click [Search] to view the Patient Movement History details or [Reset] to clear the entered search criteria.

  3. On searching, the Patient Movement History details are displayed.

  4. On clicking the ‘+’ hyperlink, the system displays the current encounter details. The information displayed is categorized as Encounter Details and Stay Details.
