Enter the following details to record Admission Deposit:

 

  1. Receipt Nature – The Receipt Nature gets displayed as Patient Deposit, by default.

  2. Receipt/Refund – Select a value from list. The value is system defined – Receipt.

  3. Receipt Type – Click [?] to select a Receipt Type. The values defined using the function ‘Receipt Types’ in the Patient Billing Manager module gets displayed. This is a mandatory field.

  4. Deposit Type – Select a Deposit Type from the list. The values are system defined – Transfer Deposit, Admission Deposit, Other Deposit and Not Applicable. This is a mandatory field.

  5. Remarks – Enter the Remarks in alphanumeric format.

  6. Amount – Enter the Amount in numeric format which is accepted as the deposit from the patient. The amount indicates the outstanding deposit amount.

  7. Thru Mail – Select the check box, if the payment has been received through mail.

  8. [Add] - Click [Add] to associate the payment details to the selected patient record. On clicking [Add], the Add Bill Settlement Details screen gets displayed.

    Enter the following details to record the payment:

    1. Amount Settled – The Amount entered in the Admission Deposit screen gets displayed. You can update the amount, but the amount entered has to be lesser than or equal to the amount mentioned in the Admission Deposit screen.

    2. Settlement Type – Select a Settlement Type from the list.  The values defined using the ‘Settlement Type’ function in the Patient Billing Manager module gets listed.

    3. Cash Given – Enter the amount in numeric format. This indicates the amount settled by the patient or the payer.

    4. Cash Returned – The difference of Cash Given and the Amount Settled gets displayed as the Cash Returned. His indicates the amount that is returned to the patient.

    5. Payer’s Name – Enter the Payer’s Name in alphanumeric format of 40 characters maximum length. This indicates the name of the person paying the deposit amount on behalf of the patient.

    6. Payer Ref ID/Deposit Ref.No – Enter the Reference ID of the patient of the reference number of the patient in alphanumeric format of 30 characters maximum length.

    7. [Accept] – Click [Accept] to save the details.

    8. [Cancel] – Click [Cancel] to clear the details.

  9. [Modify] - Select the check box 'Select' and click [Modify] to update the admission details recorded.

  10. [Remove] - Select the check box 'Select' and click [Remove] to dissociate the payment details recorded.

  11. The payment details recorded gets displayed in the Admission Deposit screen. Select the ‘Select’ check box to save the payment details.

  12. [Record] - Click [Record] to record the payment details or [Cancel] to cancel the transaction.

A bill is generated for the transaction indicating the bill document type and the document number.
