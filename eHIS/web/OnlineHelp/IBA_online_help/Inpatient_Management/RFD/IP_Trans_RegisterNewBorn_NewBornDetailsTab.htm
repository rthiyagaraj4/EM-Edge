Enter the Newborn details:

 

  1. Name Details - Enter the name details of the newborn.

  2. Title – Select the Title from the list. The listed values are created in Name Prefix function of Master Patient Index module.

  3. First Name – Enter the First Name in alphanumeric format of maximum length as specified in MP Parameter. The Mothers name is displayed, by default.

  4. Middle Name – Enter the middle Name in alphanumeric format of maximum length as specified in MP Parameter.

  5. Last Name – Enter the last Name in alphanumeric format of maximum length as specified in MP Parameter. The Mothers name is displayed, by default.

  6. Family Name – Enter the Family Name in alphanumeric format of maximum length as specified in MP Parameter

The legends displayed here are based on the MP Parameter setup.

  1. Gender – Select the Gender of the baby from the list. The listed values are ‘Male’, ‘Female’ and ‘Unknown’. This field is mandatory.

  2. Birth Date – System date is displayed as birth date. The date cannot be greater than system date and should be within the period specified in MP Parameter (Allow Registration within [] of days of birth). Use the Calendar function to select the required date. This is a mandatory field.

  3. Birth Time - Enter the Birth time of the baby in numeric format of hh:mm.

  4. Blood Group – Select the Blood Group from the list. The listed values are defined in Blood Group function in Medical Records module.

  5. RH (D) Factor - Select the RH (D) Factor from the list. The listed values are defined in Rhesus function in Medical Records module.

  6. Prefix – Select the prefix from the list. The listed values are created in Name Prefix function of Master Patient Index module.

  7. Relationship – This field is enabled on selecting the gender of the newborn. The default relationship specified in MP Parameter for the gender is displayed. If value is not set for the gender in ‘MP Parameter’ function, select the relationship of the newborn to the inpatient from the list. The listed values are defined in the master codes of Master Patient Index module.

  8. Outcome – Select either ‘Live’ or ‘Still Born’ from the list., ‘Live’ is displayed, by default. If the Outcome is selected as Still birth, you cannot modify it in the Maintain Birth Register function.  

    [Still Birth Details]

    Click [Still Birth Details] to record still birth details. The [Still Birth Details] is displayed only if the Outcome is selected as ‘Still born’. The still birth details can be modified till the new born record is finalized.

    1. Full name of the certified person – The Attending practitioner recorded in the Mother details section in Register New born function is displayed. Click [?] to modify the certified person. The values recorded in the Practitioner function in Application Masters module is displayed.

    2. Age – The Age of the Attending practitioner recorded in the Mother details section in Register New born function is displayed. The age of the practitioner is recorded in the Practitioner function in Application Masters module.

    3. License number – The License number of the Attending practitioner recorded in the Mother details section in Register New born function is displayed. The License number of the practitioner is recorded in the Practitioner setup function in Application Masters module.

    4. Attendant at birth – Select the value from the list. The listed values are ‘Accoucheuse’, ‘Traditional Medicine’, ‘Midwife’, ‘Nurse’ ,’Midwife Assistance’ and ‘Physician’. The value ‘Physician’ is displayed by default.

    Cause of Death  

    1. Maternal condition affecting fetus

    1. a. Main cause of death – Enter the main condition of the mother that caused the death of fetus in free text format. Enter the Duration of Disease in yy/mm/dd/hh format.  

    2. b. Other cause of death – Enter the other condition of the mother that caused the death of fetus in free text format. Enter the Duration of Disease in yy/mm/dd/hh format.  

    3. c. Preexistent cause of death – Enter the Preexistent condition of the mother that caused the death of fetus in free text format. Enter the Duration of Disease in yy/mm/dd/hh format.  

    2. Other Maternal condition affecting fetus

    1. d. Related cause of death – Enter the other condition of the mother that mainly caused the death of fetus in free text format. Enter the Duration of Disease in yy/mm/dd/hh format.

    1. Related cause of death

    1. a. Main cause of death – Enter the main condition that caused the death of fetus in free text format. Enter the Duration of Disease in yy/mm/dd format. This is a mandatory field.

    2. b. Other cause of death – Enter the other condition that caused the death of fetus in free text format. Enter the Duration of Disease in yy/mm/dd format. While recording the Duration of illness for the infant, the year field is not applicable and the Total of month, days and hours must be within the gestation range specified in the MP Parameter function.

    3. c. Preexistent cause of death – Enter the Preexistent condition that caused the death of fetus in free text format. Enter the Duration of Disease in yy/mm/dd format.

    2. Condition causing death in fetus

    1. d. Related cause of death – Enter the other main condition that mainly caused the death of fetus in free text format. Enter the Duration of Disease in yy/mm/dd format.

     

    1. Autopsy required? – Select the check box to order an Autopsy to find out the cause of stillbirth. The status of the selection of the check box is displayed in Still Birth Certificate.

    2. Click [Close] to save and close the Still Birth Details Screen.

    If the Outcome is selected as Still born, you can also print the Stillbirth Certificate from the Internal Report server.

  9. Indicator Mark – This field is enabled only when the Outcome is selected as ‘Still Born’. Select either ‘Fresh’ or ‘Macerated’ from the list.

  10. Born Where – Select the place where the baby was born from the list.

  11. Born At - The location type and location are to be specified. Select the location type from the first list. The listed values are Clinic, Nursing Unit, Day Care Unit and Emergency. On selecting the Location Type, select the required location. The second list, lists locations corresponding to the selected Location Type. This field is applicable only when ‘Born where’ is set as ‘In Facility’.

  12. Location – Select the location from the list. The list of pre-defined location is listed.

  13. Birth Place – Enter the Birth Place in alphanumeric format of 15 characters maximum length or select it using [?] function.

  14. Birth Certificate No – Enter the Birth Certificate Number in alphanumeric format of 15 characters maximum length.

  15. Birth Weight – Enter the Weight of the child in numeric format of six digits maximum length. The entered weight is validated against the weight range specified in MP Parameter. This field is mandatory. The displayed unit of measurement is specified in the MP Parameter setup.

  16. Length of Baby – Enter the length of the child in numeric format of six digits maximum length. The entered length is validated against the length range specified in MP Parameter. This field is mandatory. The displayed unit of measure is specified in the MP Parameter setup.

  17. Head Circumference – Enter the head circumference of the child in numeric format of six digits maximum length. The entered value is validated against the head circumference range specified in MP parameter. This field is mandatory. The displayed unit of measure is specified in the MP Parameter setup.

  18. Chest Circumference – Enter the chest circumference of the child in numeric format of six digits maximum length. The entered value is validated against the chest circumference range specified in MP Parameter. The displayed unit of measure is specified in the MP Parameter setup.

  19. Apgar Score (in 1 min) (in 5 mins) (in 10 mins) – Click  to expand the fields to capture the detailed Apgar Score and  to collapse the fields. On clicking , the Apgar Scores at 1 minute, 5 minutes and 10 minutes get displayed. It is mandatory to capture the Apgar Scores at 1 minute and 5 minutes, if the value in the field ‘Born Where’ is selected as ‘Facility’. The Apgar Score calculated can be modified after finalizing the birth register in the Maintain Birth Register function, if the Change of Data allowed after finalization check is selected in MP Parameter.

Heart Rate

  • Select the heart beat rate at 1 minute, 5 minutes and 10 minutes from the list. The listed values are Absent, Below 100bpm and Above 100bpm. On selecting the values from the list, the Apgar Score is calculated and displayed in the Heart Rate text box. The total score is updated in the Apgar Sign field automatically on selecting the values in the individual Apgar Score fields. You cannot modify the total values. The score for Absent is 0, Below 100bpm is 1 and Above 100bpm is 2.

Breathing

  • Select the Breathing rate at 1 minute, 5 minutes and 10 minutes from the list. The listed values are Absent, Slow, irregular and Good, Crying. On selecting the values from the list, the Apgar Score is calculated and displayed in the Breathing text box. The total score is updated in the Apgar Sign field automatically on selecting the values in the individual Apgar Score fields. You cannot modify the total values. The score for Absent is 0, Slow, irregular is 1 and Good, Crying is 2.

Grimace

  • Select the Breathing rate at 1 minute, 5 minutes and 10 minutes from the list. The listed values are No Response, Facial Grimace and Sneeze, Cough, Pulls away. On selecting the values from the list, the Apgar Score is calculated and displayed in the Grimace text box. The total score is updated in the Apgar Sign field automatically on selecting the values in the individual Apgar Score fields. You cannot modify the total values. The score for No Response is 0, Facial Grimace is 1 and Sneeze, Cough, Pulls away is 2.

Activity

  • Select the Activity rate at 1 minute, 5 minutes and 10 minutes from the list. The listed values are Absent, Arms &legs extended and Active movement with flexed arms& legs. On selecting the values from the list, the Apgar Score is calculated and displayed in the Activity text box. The total score is updated in the Apgar Sign field automatically on selecting the values in the individual Apgar Score fields. You cannot modify the total values. The score for Absent is 0, Arms &legs extended is 1 and Active movement with flexed arms& legs is 2.

Appearance

  • Select the Appearance rate at 1 minute, 5 minutes and 10 minutes from the list. The listed values are Blue-Gray Pale all over, Pink Body and blue extremities and Normal over entire body-completely pink. On selecting the values from the list, the Apgar Score is calculated and displayed in the Appearance text box. The total score is updated in the Apgar Sign field automatically on selecting the values in the individual Apgar Score fields. You cannot modify the total values. The score for Blue-Gray Pale all over is 0, Pink Body and blue extremities is 1 and Normal over entire body-completely pink is 2.

If any of the individual Apgar Scores at 1 minute or 5 minutes is not captured, an error message ‘Apgar score at 1 min and 5 min cannot be blank’ is displayed.

  1. Umbilical Cord – Select umbilical cord status from the list. The listed values are Normal, Other and Stump.

  2. Umbilical Cord Cut By – Enter the name of the person who cut the umbilical cord of the newborn in alphanumeric format of 20 characters. The [?] function gets displayed only if the child is born at the facility as specified in the field ‘Born Where’. On invoking the lookup function, specify the search conditions and search for the required practitioner.

  3. Fetus Complications of Delivery – Select the complication of delivery from the list. The listed values are defined in the function ‘Complications of Delivery’.

  4. Feeding – Select the type of feeding from the list. The values displayed are Breast, Bottle and Other. This field is enabled only if outcome is selected as ‘Live’.

  5. Patency of Anus – Select either Patent or Not Patent as the Patency of Anus. This field is mandatory. This field is enabled only if outcome is selected as ‘Live’ or Born Where is selected as ‘In Facility’.

  6. Patency  of Anus Checked By – This field gets enabled only when you select the Patency of Anus value.  Select the name of the practitioner who checked the patency of anus of new born using [?] function.

  7. Congenital Anomalies – Select the congenital anomalies from the list. The listed values are defined in the function ‘Congenital Anomalies’. This field is enabled only if outcome is selected as ‘Live’.

  8. Stools - Select the nature of stool passed by the newborn from the list. The listed values are Meconium, Yellow Soft and Other. This field is enabled only if outcome is selected as ‘Live’.

 

Vaccination Details

  1. BCG – Select the check box to indicate BCG is administered to the newborn. This field is enabled only if outcome is selected as ‘Live’.

  3. Administered By - Enter the name of the person who administered BCG in alphanumeric format of 30 characters maximum length. This field is enabled on selecting the ‘BCG’ check box.

  4. Designation - Enter the designation of the person who administered the vaccine in alphanumeric format of 30 characters maximum length. This field is enabled on selecting the ‘BCG’ check box.

  5. Admission Date/Time - Enter the BCG administration date and time or select it using the Calendar function. This field is enabled on checking the ‘BCG’ check box.

  6. Vitamin K - Select the check box to indicate Vitamin K is administered to the newborn. This field is enabled only if outcome is selected as ‘Live’.

  7. Vaccination Site - Enter the site of administration in alphanumeric format of 30 characters maximum length. This field is enabled on selecting the ‘Vitamin K’ check box.

  8. Administered By - Enter the name of the person who administered Vitamin K in alphanumeric format of 30 characters maximum length. This field is enabled on checking the ‘Vitamin K’ check box.

  9. Designation - Enter the Designation of the person who administered Vitamin K in alphanumeric format of 30 characters maximum length. This field is enabled on selecting the ‘Vitamin K’ check box.

  10. Admission Date/Time - Enter the Vitamin K administration date and time or select it using the Calendar function. This field is enabled on checking the ‘Vitamin K’ check box.

  11. Hepatitis B - Select the check box to indicate Hepatitis B is administered to the newborn. This field is enabled only if outcome is selected as ‘Live’.

  12. Vaccination Site - Enter the site of administration in alphanumeric format of 30 characters maximum length. This field is enabled on checking the ‘Hepatitis B’ check box.

  13. Administered By - Enter the name of the person who administered Hepatitis B in alphanumeric format of 30 characters maximum length. This field is enabled on checking the ‘Hepatitis B’ check box.

  14. Designation - Enter the designation of the person who administered Hepatitis B in alphanumeric format of 30 characters maximum length. This field is enabled on checking the ‘Hepatitis B’ check box.

  15. Admission Date/Time - Enter the Hepatitis B administration date and time or select it using the Calendar function. This field is enabled on checking the ‘Hepatitis B’ check box.
