This tab is used to record details of any fixtures like a support given to the patient or prosthetic like artificial limb used for the patient. The details of important documents given to the patient and the details of the person accompanying the patient on his discharge is recorded.

 

Other Appliances/Fixtures

This section is used to record a list of all the appliances or items given to the patient like crutches, which has to be returned to the facility.

  1. Item – Enter the Name of the Item/Appliance/Fixture used or given to the patient in alphanumeric format of 80 characters maximum length.

  2. Given By - Enter the name of the Practitioner who gave the appliance to the patient in alphanumeric format of 30 characters maximum length.

  3. Date Time – Enter the Date/Time as to when the appliance was given to the patient in the format of dd/mm/yyyy hh:mm or select the date using the calendar function.

 

Other Documents

This section is used to record details pertaining to a hard copy of any document that has been handed over to the patient.

  1. Referral Letter – Select the check box to indicate that a hard copy of the Referral Letter recorded during the discharge advice has been given to the patient.

  2. Confirm - Select the check box to confirm that a hard copy of the Referral Letter recorded during the discharge advice has been given to the patient. This field is enabled on selecting the ‘Referral Letter’ check box.

  3. Reply Letter - Select the check box to indicate that a hard copy of the Reply Letter has been given to the patient.

  4. Details – Enter the Details regarding the reply letter given to the patient. There are two fields in which the details can be entered in alphanumeric format of 20 characters maximum length.

  5. Clinical Doc/Film - Select the check box to indicate that a hard copy of the Clinical Doc/Film has been given to the patient.

  6. Details – Enter the Details regarding the Clinical Doc/Film given to the patient. There are two fields in which the details can be entered in alphanumeric format of 20 characters maximum length.

  7. Others - Select the check box to indicate that a document other than the ones mentioned above has been given to the patient.

  8. Details – There are three fields available here. The first one is to enter the name of the document given in alphanumeric format of 20 characters maximum length. The remaining two fields are used to enter the details regarding the other document given to the patient. The details can be entered in alphanumeric format of 20 characters maximum length.

 

Leave Ward Accompanied By

This section is used to record the details of the person accompanying the patient after the discharge of the patient.

  1. Relationship – Select the Relationship that the accompanying person shares with the patient, that is, specify how the accompanying person is related to the patient.

  2. Date Time – Enter the Date/Time as to when the person accompanied the patient in the format of dd/mm/yyyy hh:mm or select the date using the calendar function.

  3. Remarks - Enter the Remarks, if any, in alphanumeric format of 100 characters maximum length.

  4. Recorded By – The Name of the logged in person is displayed by default as the person recording the discharge checklist. This field cannot be changed.

  5. On – The date and time as to when the recording of the discharge checklist commenced is displayed. This field cannot be changed.
