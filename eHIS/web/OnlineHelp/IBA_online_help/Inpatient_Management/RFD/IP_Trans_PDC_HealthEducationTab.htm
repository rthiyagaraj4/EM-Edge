This section is used to record details of any education given to the patient or his relatives regarding the kind of care that the patient must be given at home.

  1. Advice on Continuous Care Given To – Two check boxes are used to indicate if the advice on home care was given to the patient or the guardian of the patient. Both the check boxes are selected to indicate that both have been educated regarding the care of the patient.

  2. Item – List of various care that the patient must be aware is displayed. A check box against each Item is provided to mark the area in which the advice was given. There is a free text field with the legend ‘Others’ which can be used to indicate any other advice given apart from the ones listed.

  3. Given By – Enter the name of the Practitioner who advised the patient or the guardian in alphanumeric format of 30 characters maximum length. This field is enabled if the check box against the item is selected.

  4. Date Time – Enter the Date/Time as to when the advice was given in the format dd/mm/yyyy hh:mm or select the date using the calendar function. This field is enabled if the check box against the item is selected.
