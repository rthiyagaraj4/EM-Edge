Generate Consolidated Invoices

From this function you can Generate a Consolidated Invoice for a range of Customers.

The Generate Consolidated Invoices functionality is used to generate consolidated Invoices for a range of Customers based on a range of Bill Type, Bill Number and Bill Date for a specified period. The Consolidated Invoice is generated for the selected Facility. For the Bills raised in the Patient Billing Manager module, one or more Invoice can be generated in the Accounts Receivable module. For example, separate Invoices can be raised for each Service availed or for different patients. One Consolidated Invoice can be raised for a group of services or a group of patients.

Generating a Consolidated Invoice

From the Transactions > Invoices > Billing Invoices, double-click Generate Consolidated Invoices function.

The Generate Consolidated Invoices screen gets displayed.

  1. Enter the details in the mandatory (*) fields. <Related Field Description>

  2. Click [Run Process] to generate a Consolidated Invoice or [Clear Record] to clear the entered data. Click [Yes] to confirm or [No] to cancel the print.

  3. A log file gets generated. Click [OK] to view the report or [Cancel] to exit from the screen.
