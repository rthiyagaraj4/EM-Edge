Enter the following details to create an Opening Status:

 

  1. Customer Code – Enter the Customer Code is alphanumeric format of 8 characters maximum length or click [List of Values] to select the required Customer Code. The Customers defined in the Customer function are displayed in the list. On selecting the Customer Code, Customer Name is displayed in the adjacent text box.  

  2. Outstanding Balance Amount – The outstanding balance amount is updated by the system whenever the transactions are posted for the Customer. You cannot enter or modify the values in this field.

 

Sales Amount

  1. Month to Date – Enter the gross sales generated by the Customer in numeric format of nine digits maximum length. The gross sale value entered is for the current month. You can enter the values in this field during the initial setup only. This is a mandatory field.

  2. Year to Date – Enter the gross sales generated by the Customer in numeric format of nine digits maximum length. The gross sale value entered is for the current financial year. You can enter the values in this field during the initial setup only. This is a mandatory field.

  3. Cum. Amt – Enter the Cumulative gross sales value in numeric format of nine digits maximum length. You can enter the values in this field during the initial setup only. This is a mandatory field.

 

Last Invoice

  1. Invoice Type – Click [List of Values] to select the required Invoice Type. You can enter the values in this field during the initial setup only.

  2. Invoice Num – Enter the Last Invoice Number in numeric format of eight digits maximum length. You can enter the values in this field during the initial setup only.

  3. Invoice Date – Enter the Invoice Date of the last invoice generated for the respective customer. You can enter the values in this field during the initial setup only.

 

Last Receipt

  1. Receipt Type – Click [List of Values] to select the required Receipt Type. You can enter the values in this field during the initial setup only.

  2. Receipt Num – Enter the Last Receipt Number for the Customer in numeric format of eight digits maximum length. You can enter the values in this field during the initial setup only.

  3. Receipt Date – Enter the date when the last receipt was generated for the Customer. You can enter the values in this field during the initial setup only.
