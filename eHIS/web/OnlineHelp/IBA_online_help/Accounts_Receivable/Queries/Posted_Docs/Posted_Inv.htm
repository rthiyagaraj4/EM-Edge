Posted Invoices

From this function you can Query the Invoices, which are posted to the Customer.

Posted Invoices those pertaining to the period for which the month end process is not computed only are displayed. The Posted Invoices are deleted when the month end process is computed. In addition to the document details, related bill details, user accessed list, text document details also gets displayed.

Querying Posted Invoices

From the Queries > Posted Documents, double-click Posted Invoices function.

The Posted Invoice screen gets displayed.

  1. Click [Execute Query] or press [F8] to view the invoices posted to the Customer from the Accounts Receivable, Patient Billing Manager and the Laboratory module.

OR

  1. Click Calendar function to select the date range. Click [Execute Query] or press [F8] to view the invoices posted to the Customer from the Accounts Receivable, Patient Billing Manager and the Laboratory module during the specified date range.

  2. Click [Refresh] to clear the date recorded.

  3. Click [Invoice Details] to view the posted invoice details. The posted invoices details screen gets displayed with the patient bill, details of the User who has posted the invoice and the remarks recorded while posting the invoice.
