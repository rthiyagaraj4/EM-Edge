Deliver Cleaned Linen

From this function you can Record the details on the cleaned linen items delivered to a Nursing Unit/Clinic/Department based on the request placed. You can Deliver the items to the respective Source or View the details.

The Deliver Cleaned Linen function is used to deliver the linen items to the concerned Nursing Units/Clinics/Departments based on the cleaning request. Based on the request placed, the cleaned linen items are delivered after mending. An authorized laundry personnel is able to deliver multiple linen items against a single request. The User can track the status of the linen items in the laundry based on the request status.

Delivering cleaned linen

From the Linen, select the Deliver Cleaned Linen function.

The Deliver Cleaned Linen screen gets displayed.

  1. Click the Calendar function to select a Request Date to indicate when the request is placed. System calculates and displays the time and date seven days prior to the system date. This is a mandatory field. <Related Field Description>

  2. Click [Search] to view and modify the details of Cleaned linen items to be delivered.

  3. The linen items for which the requests are placed get listed. Click Number marked as a hyperlink to Deliver the linen items or View a relevant Request.

    Click ‘View’ hyperlink to view the details of the request placed for cleaned linen. You can view details such as Request Number, Request Date, Source Type, Source, Linen Handed Over By, Linen Collected By and Delivery Date. The Linen Item, requested quantity, delivered quantity, pending quantity, scheduled delivery date, late delivery reason and remark are displayed in tabular format.

    The Request Number, Request Date, Source Type, Source, Linen Handed Over By, Linen Collected By and Delivery Date of the Request are displayed. The details are displayed based on entry made using the ‘Request for Cleaning Linen’ function. You cannot modify the details. Linen Item, Category and Requested Quantity are displayed and cannot be modified.

    Enter the following details to deliver the cleaned linen items:

    1. Curr. Delivered Quantity - Enter the Current Delivered Quantity in numeric format of eight digits maximum length. You can issue the number of linen items requested or deliver the number of items lesser than the requested quantity.

    2. Remark – Click Remark hyperlink to enter the remarks in alphanumeric format of 100 characters maximum length.

    3. [Select]  - Click [Select] to display the details in a tabular format. The details include Linen Item, Category, Requested Quantity, Delivered Quantity, Current Delivered Quantity, Scheduled Delivery Date, Late Delivery Reason and Remarks.

    4. Linen Item - Linen Items and Remarks are displayed as hyperlinks. Click Linen Item hyperlink to display the details of the selected linen item, its requested quantity, issued quantity, current issue quantity and remark against the respective fields.

    5. Remark - Click Remark hyperlink to view the remarks recorded. To edit the remarks, select the Linen Item hyperlink and edit in the respective ‘Remark’ field. Click [OK] to save the details.

    6. [Submit] - Click [Submit] to record the delivery details. A confirmation message ‘Please note your Request Number’ gets displayed. Click [OK] and the control shifts to the Reference Number screen to deliver or view the request.

  4. A confirmation message ‘Please note your Reference Number’ is displayed on recording the linen delivery details.

  5. The Stock Status and Work Status of the linen items in the laundry are displayed. You can choose to view the status of the linen items in laundry by selecting a source Type and a Source. The current system is displayed. Click [Go] to search for the details in the respective Source and Source Type.
