Confirm U/Charge Inpatient Service Request  

From this function you can Validate a service, which is associated to an inpatient after the bill generation. The services defined as ‘Autoconfirm’ selected are authorized automatically. The services, which need manual authorization, are to be confirmed by the user.

Confirm U/Charge Inpatient Service Request function is used to confirm the billing services, which are associated to the patient either after the bill generation or if the service was not included in the generated bill. An authorized User, who has associated the service to the patient has to confirm the service. The Billing Services defined with the ‘Autoconfirm’ check box as selected are confirmed automatically while associating the services to the patient. You cannot update or cancel the services, which are confirmed automatically. The Authorized User can only confirm the services. The check box the ‘Confirm Service Request’ has to be selected for the selected User associated to a User Group. The authorization to confirm the service is defined in the function ‘Users for Services’.

Confirming Undercharge Inpatient Service Request

From the Transactions > IP Under Charges, double-click Confirm U/Charge Inpatient Service Request Function.

The Confirm U/Charge Inpatient Service Request screen gets displayed.

  1. Click [Execute Query] or press [F8] to view all the unconfirmed services associated to the patients’ in the facility.

OR

  1. Select an Episode Type from the list. Click [Enter Query] and enter the Patient ID, Visit ID or the Episode ID to confirm the services associated to a patient. Click [List of Values] to search and to select a Patient ID from the lookup screen. Click [Execute Query] or press [F8] to view unconfirmed services associated to the patient.

  2. The patient ID, respective episode ID, service code and description, UOM, date and time when the service was associated, the transaction reference ID generated for the selected service gets displayed along with the serial number and the patient details.

  3. Select the check box ‘Confirm’ to confirm the service or the panel.

  4. Click [Commit Form] to confirm the service or click [Clear Record] to clear the details.
