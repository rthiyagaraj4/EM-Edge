Post-Discharge Modify Financial Details  

From this function you can Update the financial details of an inpatient or a daycare patient. You can also modify the financial details only after the discharge bill is raised for the patient.

Post-Discharge Modify Financial Details function is used to update the financial details of an Inpatient after raising a discharge bill. The updated financial details are considered to bill any services in the future when the patient avails a service from the facility or if any undercharge items are associated to the patient.

Modifying Financial Details

From the Transactions > IP Under Charges, double-click Post-Discharge Modify Financial Details Function.

The Post-Discharge Modify Financial Details screen gets displayed.

  1. Click [Enter Query] and enter a valid Patient Number. The patient name, age, gender, patient registration date and time get displayed on entering the patient number.

OR

  1. Click [Execute Query] or press [F8] to view all the patient details. Press downward arrow key to navigate to the next record and upward arrow key to navigate to the previous record.

OR

  1. Press [F9] to search and to select a patient ID from the lookup screen. Click [Execute Query] to view all the patient details or click [Enter Query] and enter search criteria. The records matching the search criteria get displayed. Double-click on the record to select it.

  2. Click [Modify] to login and to update the details. The financial details associated to the patient get displayed. <Related Field Description>

  3. Update the details and click [Commit Form] to save the changes or [Clear Record] to clear the details.

 

Cashier has to login to the Cash Counter to modify the financial details of the inpatients for whom the discharge bill is raised.
