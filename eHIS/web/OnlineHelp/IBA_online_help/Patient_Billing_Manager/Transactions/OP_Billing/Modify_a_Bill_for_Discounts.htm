Modify a Bill for Discounts

From this function you can Update a bill generated for an outpatient towards a service.  You can modify the print line discount percentage. System calculates the discount amount and the net amount based on the updated print line discount percentage.   

Modify a Bill for Discounts is an option provided to update the discount amount in a bill generated for a relevant service availed from the Facility. You cannot modify a bill, which is printed. The bill should not be cancelled, superseded or transferred to Accounts Receivable or Deferred Cash Patients Account. You cannot modify the bills, which are posted. The Authorized Users only can login to update the bill. If the option ‘Bill Discount’ is checked in ‘Transaction Processing Privileges’ tab in the function ‘Users for Authorization/Refund/Cash Counters’ under Administration menu, user can login to modify the discounts for a relevant bill. On committing, the system checks that the Gross - Service Disc - Manual Disc - Overall Discount is not negative. The Bill Payable amount and the outstanding amount are also adjusted automatically. If the hospital charges outstanding are fully covered by the discounts, the excess amount is credited into Doctor's account, if Doctor's fee amount is non zero. The excess amount is transferred to Doctor's fee module when the invoice is posted.

Modify a Bill for Discounts

From the Transactions > OP Billing, double-click the Modify a Bill for Discounts Function.

The Modify a Bill for Discounts screen gets displayed.

  1. Select a Patient ID for transaction. The patient name, age and gender of the patient gets displayed.

  2. Select an Episode Type from the list. The values are system defined - “Outpatient”, “Emergency” and “External”. This is a mandatory field.

  3. Click [List of Values] to select a Bill Number. The Bill Lookup appears with the Patient ID, Episode ID, Visit ID, Document Type Code, Document Number, Document Date, bill total outstanding amount, bill paid amount, bill transaction type code, bill nature code and the bill status gets displayed. Double-click on the relevant record to select it. This is a mandatory field. The Bill Nature, billing Group and the Customer associated to the selected patient, if any, gets displayed.

  4. Click [Authorize] to modify the bill. A screen pops-up requesting login information. Enter the User ID, PIN number and click [List of Values] to select a reason code. Click [Clear Record] to clear the entered data. <Related Field Description>

  5. Click [Execute Query] or press [F8]. The bill details for a relevant service are displayed.
