Acknowledge Cashier Trx

From this function you can Acknowledge the cashier collections at a relevant Cash Counter. You can Query to view the unacknowledged transactions in the Cash Counter.  

Acknowledge Cashier Trx function is used to acknowledge the Cash Counter transactions. The transactions in the Cash Counter have to be approved by an Authorized Billing Personnel. A Billing Personnel authorizes the transactions in the Cash Counter. You have an option to view the pending transactions for the selected Cash Counter or all the Cash Counters associated to a Facility. You can also select to view the pending transactions for an Open Counter. You can view the posted and the unposted receipts at the selected Cash Counter.

Acknowledging Cashier Transaction

From the Transactions > Cash Control, double-click Acknowledge Cashier Trx Function.

The Acknowledge Cashier Trx screen gets displayed.

  1. The name of the logged-in User gets displayed in the ‘Acknowledge By’ field. The current date is displayed as the Acknowledge Date, by default.

  2. Click [List of Values] to select a Cash Counter. The Cash Control Receipts generated at the selected Cash Counter gets displayed. This is a mandatory field.

  3. Click the Calendar function to select the date range. The records are filtered and displayed based on the date range selected.

  4. Click [Search] to filter the Cash Counter receipts. The legend changes from [Search] to [Current Date Time] on clicking [Search]. Click [Clear Search] to clear the records displayed.

  5. The details are displayed under the following tabs:

Acknowledge Cashier Collections

You can view the cashier's collection amount or view the cashier's collection transaction using this tab. You can also acknowledge the cashier collections.

The following details get displayed:

  1. Cash Counter - Click [List of Values] to select the Cash Counter. The transactions processed in the selected Cash Counter are listed.

  2. Opening Date Range [From - To] - Click the Calendar function to select the date range. The cashier transactions that are pending to be acknowledged are listed.

  3. [Search] - Click [Search] to search for the details matching the search criteria.

  4. [Clear Search] - Click [Clear Search] to clear the data.

  5. Select - Select the check box to acknowledge the pending cashier transactions. The selected records are displayed under the 'Cancel Acknowledgement' tab.

  6. Delivery Form No/Cashier  - The delivery form number for respective Cashier report is displayed. The delivery form number is generated when the Cashier logs off from the Cash Counter through the 'Cash Counter Logout (Advanced)' function. The name of the Cashier who has generated the report is displayed.

  7. Opening Time/Remarks - The Cash Counter login time is displayed along with any remarks that are recorded.

  8. Closing Time -  The Cash Counter logout time is displayed.

Receipts

  1. Cash - The cash amount against which the receipt is generated is displayed.

  2. Cheque - The cheque amount against which the receipt is generated is displayed.

  3. Credit Card -  The amount processed through Credit Card against which the receipt is generated is displayed.

  4. Others -  The amount settled through any other payment mode for which the receipt is generated is displayed.

  5. Deposit Adj. -   The deposit adjustment amount for which the receipt is generated is displayed.

  6. Total - The total amount for which the receipt is generated is displayed.

Deposits

  1. Cash - The cash deposit amount collected against the receipt is displayed.

  2. Cheque - The Cheque deposit amount collected against the receipt is displayed.

  3. Credit Card - The amount collected through Credit Card as the deposit amount against which the receipt is generated is displayed.

  4. Others - The deposit amount collected through other payment modes for which the receipt is generated is displayed.

  5. Deposit Adj. - The deposit adjustment amount for which the receipt is generated is displayed.

  6. Total - The total deposit amount is displayed.

  4. [Clear Search] - Click [Clear Search] to clear the data.

  7. Opening Time/Cashier Name - The name of the Cashier who has logged into the Cash Counter is displayed along with the login time.

  8. Closing Time -  The Cash Counter logout time is displayed.

  6. Total - The total amount for which the receipt is generated is displayed.

Deposits

Cancel Acknowledgement

Cancel Acknowledgement

You can cancel the cashier acknowledgment generated using the 'Acknowledge Cashier Collections' tab.

  1. Cash Counter - Click [List of Values] to select the Cash Counter. The transactions processed in the selected Cash Counter are listed.

  2. Opening Date Range [From - To] - Click the Calendar function to select the date range. The cashier transactions acknowledged through the 'Acknowledge Cashier's Transactions' tab are displayed.

  3. [Search] - Click [Search] to search for the details matching the search criteria.

  4. [Clear Search] - Click [Clear Search] to clear the data.

  5. Cancel - Select the check box to cancel the acknowledgment. On selecting the check box, the authorization screen pops-up. Enter the User ID and the PIN Number to login. Only authorised users for whom the check box ‘Cashier Acknowledgment’ is selected in the ‘Users for Authorization/Refund/Cash Counters’ in the Administration menu are permitted to cancel the acknowledgment. Click [Accept] to login or [Exit] to cancel the transaction. The acknowledgment is cancelled on clicking [Accept].

  6. Ack Date/Cashier Name - The name of the Cashier who has acknowledged the cashier reports is displayed. The date when the report is acknowledged is also displayed.

  7. Delivery Form No - The delivery form number for respective Cashier report is displayed. The delivery form number is generated when the Cashier logs off from the Cash Counter through the 'Cash Counter Logout (Advanced)' function.

  8. Opening Time/Remarks - The Cash Counter login time is displayed along with any remarks that are recorded.

  9. Closing Time -  The Cash Counter logout time is displayed.

Receipts

  1. Cash - The cash amount against which the receipt is generated is displayed.

  2. Cheque - The cheque amount against which the receipt is generated is displayed.

  3. Credit Card -  The amount processed through Credit Card against which the receipt is generated is displayed.

  4. Others -  The amount settled through any other payment mode for which the receipt is generated is displayed.

  5. Deposit Adj. -   The deposit adjustment amount for which the receipt is generated is displayed.

Deposits

  1. Cash - The cash deposit amount collected against the receipt is displayed.

  2. Cheque - The Cheque deposit amount collected against the receipt is displayed.

  3. Credit Card - The amount collected through Credit Card as the deposit amount against which the receipt is generated is displayed.

  4. Others - The deposit amount collected through other payment modes for which the receipt is generated is displayed.

  5. Deposit Adj. - The deposit adjustment amount for which the receipt is generated is displayed.

  1. Click [Commit Form] to acknowledge the receipts or [Clear Record] to clear the entered data.

 

• The check box ‘Logout Cash Counter with the Closing Bal.Details’ in the BL Parameters has to be selected to enable this function.

• The Cash Control Receipt in Use check box has to be cleared.
