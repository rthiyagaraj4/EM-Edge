Process Interim Bill

From this function you can Generate interim bills for the services utilised by the inpatients in the facility. You can Query to view the processed and the unprocessed bills.

Process Interim Bill function is used to process the interim bills of an inpatient in the facility. The total amount, unbilled amount and the outstanding amount are displayed for the specific patient encounter. You can select to adjust the outstanding amount against the pre-payment or the deposit accepted from the patient during admission. The episode type, admission date, ward code, room number and the bed number are displayed. The records for which the interim bills are processed and not processed are displayed using and colors respectively. A message 'Processing completed, please check and confirm' is displayed when the bill process is complete.  

An Interim Charge Report is generated, which displays the charge incurred for the services utilized during the patient's stay in the facility. The report displays the printed date, encounter ID, hospital details, patient's name, admission and discharge (if the report is generated after patient's discharge) date, Service Groups, total charge, payer and patient payable amount, total amount, discount amount, amount settled, outstanding amount, remaining deposit amount, username and designation of the person who has generated the report and the policy number. The generated report displays the subtotal of each Print Service separately.  

Process Interim Bill

From the Transactions > IP Cashier, double-click Process Interim Bill Function.

The Process Interim Bill screen gets displayed.

  1. Enter the details in the appropriate text  fields. <Related Field Description>

  2. Click [Search] to search for the records matching the search criteria.

  3. Select the check box 'Select' to process interim bills for the selected inpatient records.

  4. Click [Run Process] to process the bills.

  5. A message 'Processing completed, please check and confirm' is displayed when the bill process is complete. Click [OK] to process additional bills.

 

You have to login to the Inpatient Cash Counter to process the interim bills.
