Cash Counter Logout (Advanced)

From this function you can Logout of the Inpatient Cash Counter at the end of the shift. You can also View the Cash Collection report and the Cashier journal for the day.

Cash Counter Logout (Advanced) function is used to logout from the Inpatient Cash Counter at the end of the shift. A subsequent Cashier cannot login to Cash Counter at the beginning of the next shift, if the preceding Cash Counter User does not logout of the Cash Counter at the end of the shift. Cashier is able to view the Settlement Type, the total amount cancelled, received, settled, customer payable amount and the patient payable amount. The opening balance at the beginning of the shift and the closing balance at the end of the shift are also displayed. Cashier has to logout from the Cash Counter and re-login when the shift has exhausted. A report is generated on clicking [Close Counter] displaying the bill collection and the deposit collection details. A Cash delivery form is generated at the end of the shift of every Cash Counter listing the transaction details made by the Cashier during the respective shift. The total amount collected for each mode of settlement is listed separately. The deposit amount collected during the shift is displayed separately along with the receipt number. Two reports namely, Cash Delivery Form Report and Deposit Collection report gets generated while the Cashier logs out from the Cash Counter. A Cash Delivery Form report gets generated when the Cashier logs out from the Cash Counter listing the transactions and the Cash collection details based on each Settlement Type. The report displays the deposit amount collected against the receipt type generated for a Credit Card and a Cash transaction. A Senior Cashier overseeing the Cash Counter transactions and the Cashier handing the transaction report at the end of the shift has to sign off on the report generated, to confirm the Cash Counter transactions at the end of the day. During the deposit adjustment, the receipts are cancelled and the deposit adjustment transaction is reversed. A Cash delivery report is printed as an online report

Logout from the Inpatient Cash Counter

From the Transactions > IP Cashier, double-click Cash Counter Logout (Advanced) Function.

The Cash Counter Logout (Advanced) screen gets displayed.

  1. Click [List of Values] to select a Cash Counter. The Cash Counter description is displayed with the logged-in User name. The login date and time and the closing date and time of the Cash Counter is displayed. The Cash opening balance and closing balance along with the settlement details are displayed. <Related Field Description>

  2. Click [Collection Details Report] to view the Cash Collected by Cashier during the relevant shift. A report on the total amount collected in the respective Cash Counter shift is generated. The report includes a preview of the Cash Delivery Form and details on the Cash Delivery Report. The deposit details report, exemption and discount summary report and outstanding bills report is generated, if there are respective transactions. These reports are printed only for Cashier transactions. You can view, save the output in a file in the local machine or print the report.

  3. Click [View Cashier Journal Report] to view the Cashier journal report for the respective Cashier shift. You can view the summary of the amount collected in the shift along with the receipt time and the document amount and the document type and number.

  4. Click [Denomination]. The Denomination screen gets displayed.

    1. Currency Value – System displays the currency value.

    2. Number Of Count- Enter the number of currency.

    3. Actual Amount – System displays the actual amount. The actual amount is derived from the currency value and the number of count.

    4. Total Denomination Amt – System displays the actual amount total. The total denomination amount should not exceed the cash closing balance. This is a non-editable field.

    5. Shortage Amt – System displays the shortage amount. This is a non-editable field.

    6. Total Closing Bal – System displays the total closing balance. This is a non-editable field.

  5. Click [Close Counter] to logout from the Cash Counter or [Cancel] to cancel the logout process. Click [Clear Record] to clear the data.

Currency counts should be a positive value. Denomination value and Cash Closing Balance should be equal.

When you click Close Counter without entering Denomination value, an error message is displayed.
