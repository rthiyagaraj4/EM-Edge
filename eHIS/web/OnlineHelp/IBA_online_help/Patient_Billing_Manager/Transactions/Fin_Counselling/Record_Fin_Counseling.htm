Record Financial Counselling

From this function you can Record the details to exempt the patient from bill payment or record installment payment for the patient. You can Associate a template while exempting the patient or Print the details after finalizing the exemption or the installment details.   

Record Financial Counselling function is used to record the details while counselling the patient. Based on the details collected from the patient, you can choose to exempt the patient from settling the bill for the services or you can authorize the patient to settle the bill in installments. You have an option to view only the outstanding patient records or present day’s encounters. The pending exemption requests and the installment requests of the logged in user are displayed. You can associate the Counselling details by selecting a Counselling Format.

Recording Financial Counselling Details

From the Transactions > Financial Counseling, double-click Record Financial  Counselling Details Function.

The Record Financial Counselling screen gets displayed.

  1. Click the Calendar function to select the date range. The patient records with the encounter date between the selected date range get listed. This is a mandatory field. <Related Field Description>

  2. Click [Search] to search for the patient records. The legend on the button changes on clicking search. The current day’s date and time gets displayed on clicking [Search].

  3. The number of Exemption Request and Installment Request pending to be approved by the logged in user gets displayed under ‘Pending Tasks’.

  4. The encounter details of the patients during the selected period get listed. <Related Field Description>

  5. Enter the counselling details by selecting a counselling format. <Related Field Description>

  6. The counseling details are displayed based on the Counseling Format selected.

  7. You can associate additional details by selecting the values from the list. The values are displayed based on the details recorded through the 'Counseling Details' function.

  8. Click [Preview] to view or print the report or save the details.

  9. Click [Commit Form] to save the details or [Clear Record] to clear the entered data.

  10. A message ‘Counseling Details is Finalized Successfully’ gets displayed on finalizing the details.

 

The ‘Counseling Finalized’ check box selected under the Encounter details heading on finalizing the process. The check boxes ‘Instalment Payment’ and ‘Exemption Allowed’ are selected based on the check box selected under Counseling Details tab indicating if the patient is exempted from bill payment or authorized to settle the bill amount in installment. The name of the user who is assigned to exempt or authorize the installment payment gets displayed under the ‘Assign To’ column adjacent to the respective check boxes. The Assigned Date is the date when the patient is the date when the details are recorded.
