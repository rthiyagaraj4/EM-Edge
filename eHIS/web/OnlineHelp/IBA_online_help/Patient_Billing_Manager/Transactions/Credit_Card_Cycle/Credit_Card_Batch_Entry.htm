Credit Card Batch Entry  

From this function you can Generate a batch of the credit cards processed at the Cash Counter. The generated credit card batch is sent to the Credit Card Company for processing.    

Credit Card Batch Entry function is used to group the Credit Cards, which are sent to the Credit Card companies for further processing. The Credit Card details are sent in a batch to the Credit Card Companies, where the validity of the Credit Card is checked. The verification of the credit cards in batch are done by the respective Credit Card Companies. After verification, the amount is deposited to the Bank.

This process is applicable only for the manual Credit Card transactions. Online Credit Card transactions are not included in this process. Prior to the Credit Card batch process, Credit Card entries must be entered through Receipt/Refund or Bill-cum-receipt functions. On successful completion of Credit Card batch creation, batch documents will be forwarded to the Credit Card Company. Payment collected from the Credit Card Company against the batch can be entered through “Credit Card Payment Entry” function.

Generating Credit Card Batches

From the Transactions > Credit Card Cycle, double-click the Credit Card Batch Entry function.

The Credit Card Batch Entry screen gets displayed.

  1. Enter the details in the mandatory (*) fields. <Related Field Description>

  2. Click [Execute Query] or press [F8] to query and to select the Credit Card transactions. Select the records to generate a batch.

  3. Click [Commit Form] or press [F10] to save the records.
