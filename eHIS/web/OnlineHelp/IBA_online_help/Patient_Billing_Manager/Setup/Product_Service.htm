Product/Service

From this function you can Define a ProductService in the General Ledger. You can Query to view a Product/Service defined.

A Product/Service is defined to identify the products or the services within the General Ledger module itself. The Product/Service entered is associated with the Posting Account Code. One of the criteria of creating of Product/Service could be of classification of Services or products in Posting Account Code itself.

Defining Product/Service

From the Setup > GL Setup, double click on Product/Service.

The Product/Service screen gets displayed.

  1. Enter the details in the mandatory (*) fields. <Related Field Description>

  2. Click [Commit Form] or press [F10] to save or [Clear Record] to clear the entered data.

  3. Click [Execute Query] or press [F8] to search for the existing records.

 

To define a Product, the check box Product has to be selected in the GL Parameters.
