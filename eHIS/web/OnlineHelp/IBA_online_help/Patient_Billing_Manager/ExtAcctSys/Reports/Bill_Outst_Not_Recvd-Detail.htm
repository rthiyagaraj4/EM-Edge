Bill Outstanding not Received - Detail

From this function you can Print the outstanding bill details where the bill settlement is not made on the payment date.

Bill Outstanding not Received - Detail function is used to view or print the outstanding bill details where the bill settlement is not made on the payment date. System generates a report listing the patient details, outstanding amount and bill details prior to Write-off, disposed amount, action taken, action taken and a summary total displaying the outstanding amount prior to bill Write-off.

Printing Outstanding Bill Details

From the External Accounting System > Reports, Bill Outstanding not Received - Detail Function.

The Bill Outstanding not Received - Detail screen gets displayed.

  1. Click the Calendar function to select the date. The bills, which are not cleared until the date specified are listed. This is a mandatory field.

  2. Enter the duration in years with a maximum of two digits. The bills outstanding for the years specified are generated. This is a mandatory field.

  3. Click [Run Process] to generate the report based on the entered report criteria or [Clear Record] to clear the entered data.

  4. You can view the report, print the details or save the output in a file in the system.
