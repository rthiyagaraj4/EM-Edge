Cancelled Sales Draft

From this function you can Generate a report on the sales draft which are cancelled.

Cancelled Sales Draft function is used to list the sales draft, which are cancelled. System generates a report listing the sales draft details, cancellation and payment date, amount, reason for cancellation and the operator who has cancelled the sales draft.

Listing Cancelled Sales Draft

From the External Accounting System > Reports, Cancelled Sales Draft Function.

The Cancelled Sales Draft screen gets displayed.

  1. Click the Calendar function to select the date range. The sales draft cancelled during the selected date range are listed. This is a mandatory field.

  2. Click [List of Values] to select a range of Cash Counters. The sales draft cancelled in the selected Cash Counters are listed.

  3. Click [List of Values] to select a range of Cash Counter Operators. The report generated includes the details of the cancelled sales drafts during the relevant Operator’s shift.

  4. Click [Run Process] to generate the report based on the entered report criteria or [Clear Record] to clear the entered data.

  5. You can view the report, print the details or save the output in a file in the system.
