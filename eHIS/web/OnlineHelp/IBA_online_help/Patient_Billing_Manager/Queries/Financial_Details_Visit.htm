Financial Details – Visit

From this function you can Query to view the financial details of an inpatient for an episode.  

The Financial Details – Episode function is used to query on the financial details of a patient for a specific episode in the facility. You also have an option to view the unconfirmed services associated to the selected episode. The charge details for the relevant episode of the patient to the facility are displayed. You can also view the financial details associated to the patient for the selected episode gets displayed.

Querying to view the Financial Details of a patient for a specific Visit

From the Queries > Transactions, double-click Financial Details – Visit function.

The Financial Details – Visit screen gets displayed.

  1. Enter the details in the fields to query  for a specific patient record. The patient records matching the search criteria get displayed in the result area. Click [Click for Searching Patients] to view the financial details of a patient for a specific visit. Click [Cancel Search Criteria] to clear the entered data.

OR

  1. Click [Click for Searching Patients] to list all the patient records. You can view the financial details of a patient for a specific visit. Click [Cancel Search Criteria] to clear the entered data. The records matching the search criteria get displayed in the result area. Click [Clear Record] to clear each record from the list.

  2. The records get displayed in the result area. Double-click on the relevant patient record to view the financial details of the patient for a specific visit. You can also click [Click for Patient Encounters] to view the financial details for the selected visit to the facility.

  3. Click [Unconfirmed Services] to view any unconfirmed services associated to the patient. Click [Close] to exit from the screen.
