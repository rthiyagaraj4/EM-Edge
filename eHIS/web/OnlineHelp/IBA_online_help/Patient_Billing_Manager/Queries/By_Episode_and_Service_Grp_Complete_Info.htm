By Episode and Service Group – Complete Info

From this function you can Query to view the complete billing details for a specific patient episode in the facility based on the User department.

The function By Episode and Service Group – Complete Info is used to query on the episode details of a specific patient. The financial details recorded for the specific episode gets displayed along with the practitioner assigned during admission. The charges encountered during the specific episode for the services utilized get displayed. You cannot modify the details, but only view the details recorded. The net and gross amount, discounts, departments associated, print line details are not displayed.  You have an option to view the details based on the services utilized. You can view the details based on the user department.

Querying to view the complete Episode details

From the Queries > Transactions, double-click By Episode and Service Group – Complete Info function.

The By Episode and Service Group – Complete Info screen gets displayed.

  1. Enter the details in the fields to query  for a specific patient record. The patient records matching the search criteria get displayed in the result area. Click [Click for Searching Patients] to view the financial and encounter details of a specific patient. Click [Cancel Search Criteria] to clear the entered data.

OR

  1. Click [Click for Searching Patients] to list all the patient records. You can view the financial and encounter details for a specific patient. Click [Cancel Search Criteria] to clear the entered data. The records matching the search criteria get displayed in the result area.

  2. The records get displayed in the result area. Double-click on the relevant patient record to view the complete charge details recorded during a specific episode. The billing details, discounts and the charges for the specific encounter only gets displayed.

  3. Click [Click for Patient Encounters] to view all the patient encounters recorded in the facility. The encounter details of a patient get listed in the Encounter Details screen. Double-click on the relevant record to view the billing details, discounts and the charges for the specific encounter. Click [Close] to exit from the Encounter Details screen.
