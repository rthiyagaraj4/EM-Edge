Inpatient Bills

From this function you can Query on the inpatient bills details of a patient admitted in the facility.

The Inpatient Bills function is used to query on the bill details of an inpatient admitted to the facility. The details are displayed under four tabs namely, Bill Details, Amount Details, Others, Movement History and Encounter Details. The financial details recorded for the patient, the bills generated, the amount settled against the bills is displayed along with the amount incurred for the services utilized from the facility. You can right click on ‘Right Click for Options’ to view the financial details and the folio transactions. Authorized Users also have an option to reprint the bill details for the selected patient. The check box ‘Reprint Bill’ has to be selected for the User under the ‘Transaction Processing Privileges’ tab in the ‘Users for Authorization/Refund/Cash Counters’ function. A printer has to be defined for the selected report group in the System Manager module to print the bill details.

Click Encounter Details to view the details recorded during a specific encounter. You can view the admission and the discharge date and time of the patient. The billing details are also displayed.

Click Movement History to view the transfer details, if any. The movement details within the facility from one bed type to another are listed.

Click Others to view the billing details recorded during patient admission. The name of the practitioner assigned to the patient during admission is also displayed.

The credit authorization details that are recorded during admission are displayed. The account sequence number, priority assigned, credit authorization reference details and date, effective date and the policy start and expiry date is displayed.

Additional ID Proof details like the Alternate ID, Alternate ID number, Name in ID Proof and the Remarks that are recorded during patient check-out are displayed. This tab is not visible if alternate ID details are not mandatory for payer.

Click Amount Details to view the current bill details, previous bill details and the total amount. The outstanding amount, refunds issued, total amount, discounts issued in the current bill and the previous bill get listed.

Click Bill Details to view the bill generation details, the amount details and the receipts generated. Using this tab, you can also view the refund issued for the selected patient.

Querying to view the Inpatient bills

From the Queries > Discharge Bills, double-click Inpatient Details function.

The Inpatient Details screen gets displayed.

  1. Enter the Bill Type and Number to search for a specific patient record with a relevant Bill Number. Click [Execute Query] or press [F8] to view the details associated to the selected Bill Type and Number.

OR

  1. Click [List of Values] to search for a Bill Number from the Bill Lookup. The Bill Lookup screen appears. Click [Execute Query] or press [F8] and all the inpatient bills get displayed along with the patient details. Double-click a specific record to select it. To search for a specific record in the Bill Lookup screen, click [Enter Query]; enter the details in the respective field. The records matching the query criteria get displayed. Double-click a specific record to select it.

  2. The details get displayed in the respective fields on selecting a Bill Number. Click [Service Details] and the lookup screen appears. You can view the print level and service level bill details. The bill status, outstanding amount and the payable amount are also displayed. Click [More] to toggle between the tabs. Click [Close] to exit from the screen.

  3. Click [Reprint] to print the inpatient details. An authorization screen pops-up requesting the User ID and PIN Number. Enter the details to login and to reprint the details. Click [OK] to login or [Cancel] to cancel.

  4. Right click on ‘Right Click For Options’ to view the financial details and the folio transactions.

    Using this function, you can view the patient details, admission or episode details and the visit details.

    • Click Patient to view the financial details recorded for the patient. The financial details recorded during patient visit/admission and the billing details get displayed. You have an option to view the unconfirmed services by clicking [Unconfirmed Services]. Click [Close] to exit from the screen.

    • Click Admission/Episode to view the financial details recorded during the patient admission. The settlement details for the respective admission or episode get displayed. Click [Unconfirmed Services] to view any unconfirmed services associated to the respective visit. A message is displayed, if there are no unconfirmed services associated to the relevant visit.

    • Click Visit to view the financial details recorded during the specific visit registration. The settlement details for the respective visit get displayed. Click [Unconfirmed Services] to view any unconfirmed services associated to the respective visit. A message is displayed, if there are no unconfirmed services associated to the relevant visit.

  5. Click [Clear Record] to clear the data in the screen. Click [Close] to exit from the screen.
