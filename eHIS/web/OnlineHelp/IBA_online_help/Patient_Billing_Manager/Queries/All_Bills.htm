Query All Bills

From this function you can Query to view all the bills generated for the services utilised by the patient in the facility.

The Query All Bills Function enables you to inquire on all the bills for a patient. By patient, it means for the admission in case of Inpatients/Daycare, by visit in case of outpatients/emergency and by patient number in case of referral patients. The category is differentiated based on the episode type. This function is used to view all the bills raised for the episode details entered in the input parameters. The bills which are adjusted, exempted and written-off are displayed using , and colors respectively. You can right click on each record to either adjust or exempt the bill details.

Querying to view all the bills of the patients in the facility

From the Queries > Bills, double-click Query All Bills function.

The Query All Bills screen gets displayed.

  1. Enter the Patient Number in alphanumeric format of 10 characters maximum length. Alternatively, you can press [F9] to select the patient ID from the lookup screen. The patient details like patient name, gender, age, episode ID and type, visit details and national ID get displayed on selecting the patient ID. The bill details, settlement amount and the details on the outstanding amount for the services get displayed.

  2. Click [Refresh] to view the bill details. The service and the bill details associated to the selected patient get displayed in the respective text fields.

  3. Click [Dtls] to view the transactions which are deferred.

  4. Click [Print] to print the selected records. A printer has to be defined for the selected report in order to print the records.

  5. Click [More] to view the detailed bill information.

  6. Click [Clear Record] to reset the information.
