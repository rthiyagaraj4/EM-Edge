Administer Interfaced Records

From this function you can View the orders placed through external modules. You can also Query to view the unprocessed orders and authorize the pending orders. You can Cancel the orders, which are resulted due to an error. The orders have to be cancelled, so that the patient is not billed for the orders placed.

Administer Interfaced Records function is used to manage the orders placed through external modules. You can either cancel the pending orders placed due to error or process the pending orders. The future orders placed through external modules are also listed. Different colors are used to differentiate between the orders placed, registered, completed and the result generated.

Administering Interfaced Records

From the Administration, double-click Administer Interfaced Records function.

The Administer Interfaced Records screen gets displayed.

  1. Enter the details in the mandatory (*) fields. <Related Field Description>

  2. The details get displayed in the following Tab pages.

Not Charged

The records, where the services are not charged get listed. The Module, Primary Key, Line, Secondary Key, Line Service Item Code, Description, Physician ID, Billing Service Code, Description, Interfaced Date, Gross Amt, Discount, Net Amt, Patient No, Name, Episode ID, Visit ID and  Charging Stage get displayed.

Click [D] adjacent to the record to view the billing details. The colors displayed adjacent to the records indicate the status of the transaction.

Charged

The records, where the charges are processed get listed. The Module name, primary Key, Line, Module, Secondary Key, Line Service Item Code, Description, Physician ID, Billing Service Code, Description, Interfaced Date, Gross Amt, Discount, Net Amt, Patient No, Name, Episode ID, Visit ID and Charging Stage gets displayed.

Click [D] adjacent to the record to view the billing details. The colors displayed adjacent to the records indicate the status of the transaction.

On Error

The transactions, where the services are not charged due to an error get listed. The Module, Primary Key, Line, Module, Secondary Key, Line Service Item Code, Description, Physician ID, Billing Service Code, Description, Interfaced Date, Gross Amt, Discount, Net Amt, Patient No, Name, Episode ID, Visit ID and Charging Stage.

Click [D] adjacent to the record to view the billing details. The colors displayed adjacent to the records indicate the status of the transaction.

  1. Click [Commit Form] or press [F10] to save the records or [Clear Record] to clear the entered data.

  2. Click [Execute Query] or press [F8] to search for existing records within the facility.
