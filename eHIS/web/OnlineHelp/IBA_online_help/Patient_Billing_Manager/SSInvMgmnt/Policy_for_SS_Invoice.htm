Policy for SS Invoice

From this function you can Associate various Payers, Payers Groups and Policy Types to SS Invoice. You can also Associate separate Document Types for each transaction of an Inpatient and Outpatient encounter. You can Query to view the existing details.

The Policy for SS Invoice function enables you to associate Payers and Payer Groups combination along with a Policy Type to an SS Invoice. The default Document Types associated to SS Invoice gets displayed, by default. You have an option to associate document types for each Inpatient and Outpatient encounter.

Associating Policy Types to SS Invoice

From SS Invoice Management > Setup menu, double-click Policy for SS Invoice function.

The Policy for SS Invoice screen gets displayed.

  1. Click [List of Values] to select SS Invoice Type. The values defined using the 'SS Invoice Types' function get listed. Default Document Types associated to the selected SS Invoice Type gets displayed. This is a mandatory field.

  2. Enter the details in the mandatory (*) fields. <Related Field Description>

  3. Click [Commit Form] or press [F10] to save or click [Clear Record] to clear the entered data.

  4. Click [Execute Query] or press [F8] to search for the existing records within the facility.
