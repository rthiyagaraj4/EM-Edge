Support Document Generation for High Cost

From this function you can Generate support documents for high cost items utilized by a patient class in the facility.

The Support Document Generation for High Cost function is used to generate documents listing the invoice details. The services utilized by the patient are listed along with the service charge. You can print the service details or save the record. The total amount is calculated based on the service charge entry for each service.

Generating Support Documents for High Cost Items

From the SS Invoice Management > Transaction, double-click on Support Document Generation for High Cost function.

The Support Document Generation for High Cost screen gets displayed.

  1. Enter the details in the mandatory (*) fields. <Related Field Description>

  2. Click [Refresh] to query for the invoice details matching the search criteria.

  3. Click [Clear] to clear the details entered.

  4. Click [Save] to save the support document.

  5. Click [Print] to print the support document.
