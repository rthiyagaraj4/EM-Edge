Record/Process Invoice Payments

From this function you can Record or Process the payments received from the Customer against the invoice dispatched.

This function enables you to settle the amount received from the Customer against each invoice or for the consolidated invoice generated. The details recorded can be viewed at the later stages for verification. You can un-finalize the invoice payment records to update the invoice associated to the receipt number. You can unfinalize and adjust the payment details only till receipt generation for the invoice payment. If the receipt is generated, you have to cancel the receipt to unfinalize and adjust the payment details. Special Clinic Encounters are highlighted in red.

Record/Process Invoice Payments

From the Invoice Management > Transaction, double-click on Record/Process Invoice Payments  function.

The Record/Process Invoice Payments screen gets displayed.

  1. Enter the details in the mandatory (*) fields. <Related Field Description>

  2. Click [Commit Form] to save the records or [Clear Record] to delete the data entered.

  3. Click [Execute Query] to query for the existing records.

 

The document amount must be equal to the difference of Total Debit Amount and Credit Amount.

You can unfinalize the records, only if the document is not posted.
