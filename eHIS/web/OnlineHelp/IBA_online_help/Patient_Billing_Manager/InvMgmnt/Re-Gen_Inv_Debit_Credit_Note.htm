Re-generate Invoice/Debit/Credit Note

From this function you can Re-generate an invoice, debit or credit note generated in the facility.

The Re-generate Invoice/Debit/Credit Note function enables you to re-generate an invoice, debit or credit note that is generated. The invoices, credit or debit notes that are generated through the Generate Direct Invoice/Debit/Credit Note function can be re-generated. Separate invoices are generated for bills that are associated to different policy groups.

Re-generating Invoice, Debit or Credit Notes

From Invoice Management > Transaction, double-click on Re-generate Invoice/Debit/Credit Note function.

The Re-generate Invoice/Debit/Credit Note screen gets displayed.

  1. Select the Transaction Type from the list. The system-defined values are 'Regenerate Invoice', 'Regenerate Debit Note' and 'Regenerate Credit Note'. 'Regenerate Invoice' is selected, by default. <Related Field Description>

  2. Click [List of Values] to select the Invoice, Debit or the Credit Note document type and number range. You can generate the report for the selected range of values.

  3. Click [Refresh] to view the Invoice and Bill details associated to the selected invoice, debit or credit note. The check boxes 'Cancelled', 'Printed' and 'Direct Invoice/Debit/Credit Note' are selected, if the invoice is either cancelled, printed or generated. The signatory details that are recorded for the respective Payer through the Invoice Process Parameter function are displayed. Enter additional details in the Remarks field prior to invoice/debit note/credit note re-generation.

    The bills that are associated to the respective Invoice that is highlighted in 'Invoice Details' are listed.

    The invoices that are generated for a Payer are listed based on the search criteria.

  4. Click [Charges] to view the charge details. A pop-up screen is displayed with the Payer Service Group, Drug Details, Payer Service Group/Line, Initial Amount, Actual Amount, and Difference Amount. You can also view the total amount. You can view the charge details associated to each invoice.

  5. Click [Generate] to generate the document number. A warning message is displayed with the document type and number.
