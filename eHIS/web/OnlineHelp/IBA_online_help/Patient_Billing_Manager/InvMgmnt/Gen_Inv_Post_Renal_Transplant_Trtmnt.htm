Generate Invoice-Post-Renal Transplant Treatment

From this function you can Generate an invoice for the post-renal treatment availed by an inpatient.

The Generate Invoice-Post-Renal Transplant Treatment enables you to generate invoice for the renal treatment availed by an inpatient from the facility. You can generate the invoice for the treatments availed during the past months. The invoices for the preceding months has to be generated to generate an invoice for a selected month. You can also view the service details associated to the inpatient in the facility during the renal transplant treatment. The special items associated to the patient are indicated using color. The treatments for which the diagnosis is mandatory, but not recorded are also indicated using color. Verification of pending invoices for preceding months are checked while trying to generate an invoice for current month. If the invoice is pending for preceding month, an error message is displayed. Several color codes are used to indicate various processes. Color indicators on the left hand side of each record are used to identify the status of a particular invoice. Color codes that represent a task are listed below:

 

  - Diagnosis to be recorded

  - Earlier Month Invoice has not been generated

  - Patient Discharge Request

  - No Invoice Generation Rule Setup

  - Bill Printing Required

  - Bill Posting Required

Generating invoice for post-renal transplant treatment:

From the Invoice Management > Transaction, double-click on Generate Invoice Post-Renal Transplant Treatment function.

The Generate Invoice-Post-Renal Transplant Treatment screen gets displayed.

  1. Enter the details in the mandatory (*) fields in the mandatory fields.

    Enter the following details to search for a specific patient record:

    1. Facility - The name of the logged-in facility is displayed, by default.

    2. Treatment Month/Year - Click Calendar function to select the treatment month/year. You can generate invoice for the preceding months. This is a mandatory field.

    3. Patient ID - Click [List of Values] to select the Patient ID. The name, gender and the age of the patient is displayed.

    4. [Search] - Click [Search] to search for the patient records matching the query criteria.

  2. The patient details, encounter details and the outstanding bill details are displayed on clicking [Search].

  3. Click [C] to view the additional details captured for the patient.

  4. Click [Service Details] to view the additional services and the items associated to the patient.

  1. Click [Generate Invoice] to generate the invoice for the patient for the selected month. An invoice number is generated.
