Generate Dispatch Document

From this function you can Generate dispatch document to dispatch the invoice to the Customers in the facility.

The Generate Dispatch Document function enables you to generate a document to mail the invoice for the outstanding bills of the patients who are associated to the selected Customer. You have an option to choose the Episode Type associated to the Customers to whom the invoices with the selected document have to be dispatched. You can also choose the Covering Letter format breakdown the documents based on the Policy Type. You can select to include the signature of the authorised personnel in the document.

Generating Dispatch Document

From the Invoice Management > Transaction, double-click on Generate Dispatch Document function.

The Generate Dispatch Document screen gets displayed.

  1. Enter the details in the mandatory (*) fields. <Related Field Description>

  2. Click [Commit Form] to save the records or [Clear Record] to delete the data entered.

  3. Click [Execute Query] to query for the existing records.
