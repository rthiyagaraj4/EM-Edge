Recal Approved Inpatients Enc

From this function you can Modify the financial details of the patient, which is reclassified and finalized. You can Reclassify the details for an Inpatient who is admitted to the facility or discharged from the facility.

Re-calculate Approved Inpatients Encounter function is used to modify the financial details of an Inpatient or a discharged patient based on financial reclassification. A patient can be currently admitted to the facility and availing services or discharged from the facility. On recalculating, the service charges are computed based on the new Billing Group assigned to the patient. The service prices are recalculated to charge the patient accordingly during bill generation. The patient records for which the reclassification is finalized are listed. Only an authorized user can recalculate the prices for a specific patient encounter. The bill amount is updated accordingly based on the reclassification. You can approve the reclassification details for the current patient admission.

Re-Calculating Approved Inpatients Encounter

From the Financial Reclassification > Transaction, double-click ReCal Approved Inpatients Enc Function.

The ReCal Approved Inpatients Enc screen gets displayed.

  1. Select either 'Current Inpatient' or 'Discharged Patient' to search for the respective patient records where the financial details are recalculated.

  2. Click the Calendar function to select the Encounter date range. The patient records, where the financial details are reclassified during the selected date range gets listed.

  3. Click [Search] to search for the respective patient records.

  4. The reclassification details get displayed.  <Related Field Description>

  5. Select the check box against each patient record to recalculate the financial details based on the reclassification. [Authorize for Recalculation] is enabled only on selecting the check box.

  6. Click [Authorize for Recalculation] to login and recalculate the details. Authorization to a User in a User Group to reclassify the financial details is given in the function 'User for Services' function under the 'Administration' menu by selecting the check box 'Reclassification Allowed'.

    1. User ID – Enter the User ID. The Users with the ‘Reclassification Allowed’ check box as selected in the ‘Users for Services’ function under the Administration function are authorized to finalize the details. This is a mandatory field.

    2. PIN No – Enter the PIN number of the respective User. This is a mandatory field.

    3. Remarks – Enter the Remarks in alphanumeric format. This is a mandatory field.

    4. [Accept] - Click [Accept] to login and recalculate the details.

    5. [Cancel] - Click [Cancel] to cancel the transaction.

     

  7. The Financial Details - Admission screen gets invoked. You can view the new classification details.

  8. Click [Commit Form] or press [F10] to approve the financial reclassification details for an inpatient or a discharged patient.
