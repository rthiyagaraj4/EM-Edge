Enter the following details to define the invoice generation rule:

 

  1. Op Rule - Select a value from the list to select the invoice generation rule for an Outpatient. The system-defined values are - 'One Invoice Per Day', 'One Invoice Across Days' and 'One Invoice Per Encounter'.

  2. One Invoice Per Day - Select' One Invoice Per Day' to generate an invoice for the bill against the services utilised. An invoice is generated each day until the patient is discharged from the facility.

  3. One Invoice Across Days - Select 'One Invoice Across Days' to generate an invoice for the services utilized across the days during patient’s stay in the facility. A consolidated invoice is generated for the services utilized by the patient.

  4. One Invoice Per Encounter - Select 'One Invoice Per Encounter' to generate an invoice for each bill raised for the services utilised. An invoice is generated for the patient encounter in the facility.

  1. AE & OP Together - Select the check box to generate a single invoice for the services utilised by the patient from the Accident and Emergency department and the Outpatient department. Clear the check box, if separate invoices are required for the patient encounters in the Emergency Care Management department and the Outpatient department.

  2. Discharged Encounter Only - Select the check box to generate an invoice for a specific patient class. You can generate an invoice through the ‘Generate Invoice – IP/DC’ or ‘Generate Invoice – OP/EM’ functions for the respective patient class before the patient is discharged from the facility.

  3. Emergency - Select the check box to generate invoice during discharge for an Emergency patient. Clear the check box, if the invoice generation is not required during patient discharge from the Emergency department.

  4. Outpatient - Select the check box to generate invoice during check out for an Outpatient. Clear the check box, if the invoice generation is not required during patient check out from the Outpatient department.

  5. Inpatient - Select the check box to generate invoice during discharge for an Inpatient. Clear the check box, if the invoice generation is not required during an Inpatient discharge from the facility.

  1. Separate For Special Clinic Encounters - Select the check box to generate a separate invoice for the encounters in an Outpatient department. Clear the check box, if a separate invoice is not required for special clinic encounters.

  2. Multiple Invoice Allowed for IP – Select the check box to allow multiple invoices for outstanding payments of an inpatient. Clear the check box to generate a single invoice for outstanding payments of an inpatient.
