Enter the following details to modify the discount details before bill generation:

 

  1. Episode Type – Select an Episode Type from the list. The system-defined values are ‘Outpatient’, 'Emergency' and ‘External’.

  2. Admission/Tfr. No – Enter the Admission and the transfer number in numeric format in the respective text boxes. This is a mandatory field. The admission or the transfer number gets displayed automatically on entering the Patient ID.

  3. Patient No – Enter the Patient number in alphanumeric format of 10 characters maximum length. You can update the discount details for the services utilized by the selected patient. The transaction date, gender, age, ward, doctor and bed/room number to which the patient is admitted gets displayed. You cannot update any of the details.

  4. Discount Option – Select the Discount Option. The services utilized by the selected patient gets displayed based on the Discount Option selected. You can issue a discount on the individual Billing Services or on the Enterprise Service Group/Line.

 

The Billing Services or the Enterprise Service Group details get displayed. Right-click on the 'Discount Amount' column for the respective Billing Service or the Enterprise Service Group to record the discount amount. A log-in screen pops-up with the Service selected, item code, gross amount, net amount, discount amount and additional amount. The 'Actual Gross Amount' is displayed as the difference between the 'Gross Amount' and 'Additional Amt'.

 

The 'User ID' and 'Pin' gets defaulted to the logged in user and corresponding pin. If the user is not permitted to issue a discount on the selected service, an error message ‘This user does not have right to give discount for this service’ gets displayed. An authorization for the user to issue discount on each service is given using the function ‘Users for Authorization/Refund/Cash Counters’ under the ‘Service Discount Applicability by User’ tab. The discount amount issued by the respective user cannot exceed the maximum amount specified for the respective service/service group.

 

Enter either the 'Adhoc discount %age' or the 'Adhoc discount amount'. Based on the value entered in a field, the equivalent value gets displayed in the other field.  Click [List of Values] to select a reason. The reasons defined using the function ‘Reasons for Billing Actions’ in the Masters with the ‘Bill Level Discount’ check box as selected gets displayed.

 

The discount amount is computed based on the 'Actual Gross Amount'. For Example, if the 'Gross Amount' is 300 and the patient is being issued discount on a service for the first time, then the 'Gross Amount' is displayed as 300 with the 'Net Amount' also as 300. The 'Discount Amount' is displayed as 0. If the services have additional charges associated to it, then the 'Additional Amount' is displayed. Consider the 'Additional Amount' as 52.07. In such scenario, the 'Actual Gross Amount' is displayed as 247.93 which is the difference of the 'Gross Amount' and 'Additional Amount'. If an authorized user records the discount as 5%, then the 'Discount Amount' is computed based on the 'Actual Gross Amount'. The 'Discount Amount' now is displayed as 12.40 i.e, (247.93*5/100).

 

Click [OK] to authorize the discount on the service or [Cancel] to exit from the authorization screen. On clicking [OK], control shifts back to the main screen. The 'Discount Amount' is displayed based on the percentage amount or the amount specified. The 'Net Amount' is calculated as the difference between the 'Gross Amount' and 'Discount'.

 

In the above example, the 'Net Amount' gets displayed as 287.60 i.e, [300 - 12.40]. The check box  'Use Prev. Disc. Reason' in 'Record Adhoc Discount'  gets enabled only for repeated transactions on the same service. If user has already issued a discount on the service and wishes to use the same reason as the previous, then the check box has to be selected. The reason gets displayed in the 'Discount Reason' field on selecting the check box. Clear the check box to select any other reason. Also, the 'Net Amount' displayed during the previous transaction gets displayed in the 'Net Amount' field in the 'Record Adhoc Discount' screen. The 'Discount Amount' from the previous transactions is displayed in 'Discount Amt' field.
