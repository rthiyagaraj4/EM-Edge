Enter the following details to create monthly posting accounts:

 

Posting Account

  1. Chart of Accounts [From–To] - Click [List of Values] to select a range of Charting Account codes. The values defined using the Chart of Accounts function get listed.

  2. Department [From–To] - Click [List of Values] to select a range of Department codes.

  3. Product [From–To] - Click [List of Values] to select a range of Product codes.

  4. Subledger [From–To] - Click [List of Values] to select a range of Subledger codes.

  5. Year - Enter the Accounting Year in numeric format of four digits maximum length. Default Account Year defined in the GL Parameter gets displayed. You can update the year, but the value has to be greater than the processing Accounting Year.

A confirmation screen gets displayed. Click [Yes] to proceed, or [No] to cancel the process. A log file gets generated. Click [OK] to view the log file and [Cancel] to exit from the screen.
