Reference Price by Item

A new tab Reference Price by Item is introduced in Drug Item Price Setup screen to define the reference price based on item. This tab contains the following fields:

  • Ref Price OP – Enter Reference Price for Outpatient in alphanumeric format of 10 characters maximum length.

  • Ref Price EM – Enter Reference Price for Accident & Emergency in alphanumeric format of 10 characters maximum length.

  • Ref Price IP – Enter Reference Price for Inpatient in alphanumeric format of 10 characters maximum length.

  • Ref Price DC – Enter Reference Price for Day-care in alphanumeric format of 10 characters maximum length.
