Handover Cash Request

  1. Handover Request Amount - Enter the handover request amount in numeric format. You can enter the details only when the 'Denomination mandatory' check box is cleared in 'BL Parameters'.

  2. Denomination - Enter the amount in numeric format. This is mandatory when the 'Denomination mandatory' check box is selected in 'BL Parameters'.

  3. Nos. - Enter the number in numeric format. The Denomination is multiplied with the Number to calculate the total amount.

  4. Amount - Enter the amount in numeric format. The Denomination is multiplied with the Number to calculate the total amount.

  5. Total - The total amount is computed based on the denomination entered.
