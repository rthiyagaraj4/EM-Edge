Enter the following details to generate support document for high cost items:

 

  1. Patient ID - Click [List of Values] to select a Patient ID. A list of inpatients' with invoice generated for outstanding bills are displayed.

  2. Encounter ID - Click [List of Values] to select the encounter ID. A list of inpatients' with invoice generated for outstanding bills are displayed.

  3. Invoice Number - Click [List of Values] to select an invoice document number from the list. A list of invoice numbers generated for the patient are displayed. This is a mandatory field.

  4. Age - The age of the patient is displayed either on selecting the patient ID or the encounter ID.

  5. Sex - Gender of the patient is displayed either on selecting the patient ID or the encounter ID.

  6. Support Doc Type - Click [List of Values] to select a support document type. This is a mandatory field.

  7. [Refresh] - Click [Refresh] to record Invoice details. The details recorded are displayed in respective invoice of the patient.

  8. Select the check box against a service to record the payment details. The name and designation of the Accounts Personnel associated to the respective 'Support Document Type' are displayed.

  9. [Clear] - Click [Clear] to clear the details recorded.

  10. [Save] - Click [Save] to save the service details.

  11. [Print] - Click [Print] to print the service details. A report is generated with the services utilized and charge for each service along with the total amount to be paid by Social Security for the respective invoice.
