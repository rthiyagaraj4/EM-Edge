The following details are displayed:

 

  1. Cash Counter Code - The Cash Counter codes that are defined for the facility are listed.

  2. Cashier User ID - The name of the Cashiers associated to the Cash Counter code is displayed.

  3. Cash Collection - The cash amount collected during the respective Cash Counter shift is displayed. The amount displayed is the amount collected excluding the opening balance. The net amount is displayed after deducting the refund adjustments.

  4. Cheque Collection - The amount collected with respect to cheque transactions during the respective Cash Counter shift is displayed.

  5. Requested Cash - The handover request from the Cash Counter is displayed. The latest requested cash details are displayed on clicking [Refresh].

  6. Requested Cheque - The handover request from the Cash Counter is displayed. The latest requested cash details are displayed on clicking [Refresh].

  7. Approved Cash - The total cash amount approved in the Cash Counter from the login time is displayed.

  8. Approved Cheque - The total cheque amount approved in the Cash Counter from the login time is displayed.

  9. Cash Balance - The net balance of the Cash amount at the Cash Counter is displayed. The Net Amount is the balance of [Cash Collection - Approved Cash].

  10. Cheque Balance - The net balance amount of the Cheque amount at the Cash Counter is displayed. The Net Amount is the balance of [Cash Collection - Approved Cheque].
