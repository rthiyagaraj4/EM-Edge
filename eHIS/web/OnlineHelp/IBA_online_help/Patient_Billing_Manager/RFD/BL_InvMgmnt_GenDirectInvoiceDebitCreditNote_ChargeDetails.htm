Charge Details

You can associate the Payer Service Group and adjust the amount to generate a direct invoice, debit or credit note. You cannot enter duplicate values for a Payer Service Group.

 

Enter the following details:

  1. Payer Service Group – Click [List of Values] to select a Payer Service Group. The Payer Service Group associated to the selected Payer Group, Payer and Patient Class through the function ‘Payer Service Group’ is displayed. The description of the selected Payer Service Group is displayed. You cannot complete the transaction, if the Payer Service Group is not associated to the Customer, Customer Group and Patient Class.

  2. Payer Service Group Line - Click [List of Values] to select a Payer Service Group Line. The Payer Service Group Line associated to the selected Payer Service Group is listed. The description of the selected Payer Service Group Line is displayed.

  3. Initial Amount – The initial amount incurred for the Payer Service Group is displayed.

  4. Actual Amount – Enter the actual amount for the Payer Service Group in numeric format. The actual amount entered has to be greater than zero.

  5. Difference – The difference is calculated and displayed as [Actual Amount – Initial Amount].

  6. Total – The total initial amount, actual amount and the difference amount of the associated records are displayed. You can generate direct invoice, debit or credit notes only if the total difference amount is greater than zero.

  7. Signatory 1 – The name of the authorized signatory 1 recorded through ‘Invoice Process Parameter’ function is displayed.

  8. Title 1 - The designation of the authorized signatory 1 recorded through ‘Invoice Process Parameter’ function is displayed.

  9. Signatory 2 – The name of the authorized signatory 2 recorded through ‘Invoice Process Parameter’ function is displayed.

  10. Title 2 - The designation of the authorized signatory 2 recorded through ‘Invoice Process Parameter’ function is displayed.

  11. Remarks – Enter the remarks in alphanumeric format. You can record additional details while generating direct invoice/debit/credit note.
