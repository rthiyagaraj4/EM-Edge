The following details are displayed for the respective Cash Counter:

 

Settlement-wise Collection Details

  1. Settlement Type – The Settlement Types using which the transactions were summed up gets listed. You cannot change the Settlement Type.  

  2. Total – System displays the total number of transactions during the relevant Cash Counter shift.

  3. Cancelled – System displays the total number of cancelled transactions during the relevant Cash Counter shift.

  4. Bounced – System displays the total number of bounced transactions during the relevant Cash Counter shift.

  5. Cancel Amount – If any cancellations had taken place during the relevant Cash Counter shift, the amount is summed up by the Settlement Type and gets displayed.

  6. Receipt Amount – The amount collected against the receipts generated during the relevant Cash Counter shift is summed up by the Settlement Type and gets displayed.

  7. Actual Amount – Enter the actual amount collected by the Cashier. This has to be entered while logging-off from the Cash Counter and the amount has to match with the receipt amount.

  8. HO Amount - The Handover amount is displayed. The report generated displays the handover amount that is recorded for the respective Cash Counter and appropriate settlement type.

  9. Discrepancy Amount – When the Actual amount entered varies from the Receipt Amount, the net difference appears as the Discrepancy.

 

Amount of Bills Generated

  1. Self-Pay Bill Amount – System displays the total amount paid by the patients’ for the services. The total number of bills for self-pay are displayed.

  2. Customer Payable Bill Amount – System displays the amount settled by the Customers’ for the services.  The total number of Customer payable  bills are displayed.

  3. Cash Opening Balance – The opening balance entered while logging into the Cash Counter by the Cashier gets displayed.

  4. Cash Closing Balance - Closing balance is derived from the opening balance entered by the Cashier during log-in and the net of transactions taken place in the Cash counter during this period. The entry cannot be changed.
