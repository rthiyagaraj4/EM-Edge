Counseling Details  

From this function you can Define the counseling details to identify the reasons for which the bill amount can be exempted for a patient. You can Query to view the counseling details defined.   

Counseling Details function is used to record the reasons to exempt a patient’s bill amount. Counseling details are associated to Exemption Setup. An exemption code is associated to a patient during transaction when a request has been made by the patient to exempt the bill amount.

Defining Counseling Details

From the Masters > Other Masters, double-click Counseling Details function.

The Counseling Details screen gets displayed.

  1. Enter the details in the mandatory (*) fields. <Related Field Description>

  2. Click [Commit Form] or press [F10] to save or [Clear Record] to clear the entered data.

  3. Click [Execute Query] or press [F8] to search for the existing records within the facility.
