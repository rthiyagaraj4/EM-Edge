Cash Counters

From this function you can Define the Cash Counters to generate bills, receipts or process cash collection and refund for the billing transactions. You can Query to view the existing Cash Counters.

The Cash Counters in a Facility play a significant role in bill processing, receipt generation and refund process. The Cash Counters are defined either to collect cash against the bill/receipt from the patients/customers/sponsors or issue a refund to the associated patient category. A Cash Counter Code identifies uniquely a location where the Cash payments and receipts are processed within the Facility. A Facility consists of a number of Cash Counters to collect Cash from the patients or to clear any billing transactions with regards to the service availed from the Facility. The patient category applicable for each Cash Counter is defined. You can also define a special cash counter, which is applicable for special clinics in the facility. The transactions for the special clinics are carried out only in the special Cash Counters. A deposit amount is collected from the patient/customers on patient admission based on the deposit parameters defined for a Bed Class. Specific Cashiers authorized to login to a relevant Cash Counter are permitted to access the counter to generate bills/receipts and accept deposits or payments against a bill/receipt. You have an option to accept the credit cards in the selected Cash Counter. The Credit Card Machine ID defined in the Banks and Branches function get listed. Each Credit Card Machine ID can be associated to a single Cash Counter.

Defining Cash Counters

From the Masters > Facility Specific Setup, double-click on Cash Counters function.

The Cash Counters screen gets displayed.

  1. Enter the details in the mandatory (*) fields. <Related Field Description>

  2. Click [Commit Form] or press [F10] to save or [Clear Record] to clear the entered data.

  3. Click [Execute Query] or press [F8] to search for the existing records within the facility.
