Payer Service Group  

From this function you can Define the Payer Service Groups and the Payer Service Lines. You can Query to view the Payer Service Groups and the Payer Service Group Lines associated to a respective Payer Service Group. You can Associate the Billing Services to the Payer Service Group.  

A Payer Service Group identifies the Payer or a Customer who is paying for the relevant Service Group. The Billing Services are associated with a relevant Payer Service Group. The information logged using this function is printed on the bill. Payer Service Group is also used for income reporting. You can define a Service Group or a Billing Service as DRG related. This is applicable for inpatient transactions only.

Defining Payer Service Group

From the Masters > Billing Services, double-click Payer Service Group function.

The Payer Service Group screen gets displayed.

  1. Click [List of Values] to select a Payer. This is a mandatory field.

  2. Click [List of Values] to select a Patient Class. This is a mandatory field.

  3. Define a Payer Service Group and associate services to a Payer Group using the tabs below:

Payer Service Group Lines

Service by Payer Service Group/Lines

  1. Click [Commit Form] or press [F10] to save or [Clear Record] to clear the entered data.

  2. Click [Execute Query] or press [F8] to search for the existing records.
