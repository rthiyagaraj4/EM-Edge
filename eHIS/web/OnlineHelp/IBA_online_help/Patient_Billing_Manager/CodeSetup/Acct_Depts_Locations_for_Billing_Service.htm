Acct Depts/Locations for Billing Service

From this function you can Associate an Accounting Department to a Service Location in the Facility. The price for a relevant service in the Service Location is calculated in the respective Account Department. You can Query to view the existing records.

Each Service Location has an Accounting Department or a Service Location to manage the billing transactions. A Service Location and an Accounting Department are associated to the  facility. The charges for the Billing Services in the facility are computed at the respective Accounting Department.

Defining Acct Depts/Locations for Billing Service

From the Masters > Facility Specific Setup, double-click Acct Depts/Locations for Billing Service function.

The Acct Depts/Locations for Billing Service screen get displayed.

  1. Enter the details in the mandatory (*) fields. <Related Field Description>

  2. Click [Commit Form] or press [F10] to save or [Clear Record] to clear the entered data.

  3. Click [Execute Query] or press [F8] to search for the existing records within the facility.

 

The same Facility cannot have duplicate entries for an Accounting Department.
