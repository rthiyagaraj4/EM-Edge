Enterprise Material Group    

From this function you can Define a Material Group and associate individual Material Items to a Material Group. You can Query to view the Enterprise Material groups and the Material Items associated to a respective Material Group.

The Enterprise Material Group function is used to create Material Groups and associate individual Material Items to a Material Group. When a Material Group is associated to a patient, the individual Material Items associated to the Material Group becomes a part of the service. During bill generation, The patient is charged for all the material items associated to the Material Group.

Defining Enterprise Material Group

From the Masters > Interfaced Modules Financial Dtls, double-click Enterprise Material Group function.

The Enterprise Material Group screen gets displayed.

  1. Select the Facility from the list.

  2. Enter the details in the mandatory (*) fields found in the following tab pages:

Enterprise Material Group

Items by Enterprise Material Group

  1. Click [Commit Form] or press [F10] to save or [Clear Record] to clear the entered data.

  2. Click [Execute Query] or press [F8] to search for the existing records within the facility.
