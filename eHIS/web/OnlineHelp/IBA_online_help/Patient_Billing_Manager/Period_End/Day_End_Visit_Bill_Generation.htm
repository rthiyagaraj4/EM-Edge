Day End Visit Bill Generation

From this function you can Generate a Visit bill for the outpatients checked-out from the facility. You can get an overview of the bills generated for all the outpatient check-out in the facility at the end of the day.

Day End Visit Bill Generation function is used to view the number of outpatient bills generated in a relevant Cash Counter. It lists the checked-out outpatient bill details generated in the relevant Cash Counter.

Day End Visit Bill Generation

From the Period End, double-click Day End Visit Bill Generation.

The Day End Visit Bill Generation screen gets displayed.

  1. Enter the details in the mandatory (*) fields. <Related Field Description>

  2. Select Process to generate a report on the visit bills.

  3. Click [Clear Record] to delete the entered records.

 

You have to login to the Outpatient Cash Counter to generate the bills at the end of the day for checked-out patients.
