Generate Prepaid Letter

From this function you can Generate a prepaid letter to a Payer to settle the outstanding payment of a Payer.

The pending payments associated to each patient are listed. System verifies the status of a patient and displays an alert message to confirm if the process can be continued when the patient is dead. The actual charges not more than the first payment are charged if the patient died within seven days after the protocol started. You can generate letter for later payments only if the letter is generated for the previous payments. Following color codes indicate the status of payment.

- Overdue

- Undue

- Current Due

Generating Prepaid Letters

From the Prepaid Management > Transaction, double-click on Generate Prepaid Letter function.

The Generate Prepaid Letter screen gets displayed.

  1. The logged-in facility is displayed, by default. The facilities associated to the logged-in user are listed.

  2. Enter the details in the mandatory (*) fields. <Related Field Description>

  3. Click [Refresh] to query the patient records.

  4. The details are displayed under the following tabs:

Create

Cancel

  1. Click [Commit Form] or press [F10] to save the records or [Clear Record] to delete the data.
