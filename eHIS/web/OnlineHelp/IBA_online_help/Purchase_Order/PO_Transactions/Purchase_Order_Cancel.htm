Purchase Order Cancel/Close

From this function you can Cancel/Close the purchase orders (POs).

This function allows you to cancel/close the purchase orders which are not yet received or close the purchase orders that are partially received. This is to prevent acceptance of subsequent stocks based on the same PO.

Cancelling/Closing Purchase Order

From the PO Transactions, select the Purchase Order Cancel/Close function.

The Purchase Order Cancel/Close screen gets displayed.

  1. In Action, click PO Cancel or PO Close, as required.

  2. The purchase orders for the last one month are displayed by default.

  3. Enter the details in the mandatory (*) fields.<Related Field Description>

  4. Click [Search]. The purchase orders matching the search criteria are displayed.

  5. Click ‘Purchase No’ hyperlink to view the item details of the purchase order in the Item details dialog.

  6. Select the Cancel/Close check box against the Purchase Order to be deleted or select the Cancel/Close check box in the header to select all the purchase orders.

  7. Click ‘Remarks’ hyperlink to enter the remarks for cancelling/closing the purchase orders in the Close/Cancel Remarks dialog respectively. Click Ok.

  8. Click [Apply] to cancel/close the purchase orders or [Reset] to clear the entered data.
