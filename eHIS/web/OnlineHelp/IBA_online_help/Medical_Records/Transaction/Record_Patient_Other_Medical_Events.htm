Record Patient Other Medical Events

From this function you can Record the Medical Events that have occurred during the patient’s stay in the facility.

Process Flow Diagram

Recording Other Medical Events

From the MR Transactions, select the Record Patient Other Medical Events function.

The Search Patient Screen gets displayed.

  1. Search the required patient and click the Patient ID hyperlink to select the patient and record other Medical Events. Record Patient Other Medical Events screen is displayed.

  2. Any Medical Events already recorded for the selected patient are displayed in the top part of the screen.

    1. Reference No – The Reference Number is displayed.

    2. Date – The recorded event Date is displayed.

    3. Category – The recorded medical event Category is displayed.

    4. Remarks – The recorded Remarks of the medical event is displayed.

  3. Enter the details in the mandatory (*) fields. <Related Field Description>

  4. Click [Record] to record other Medical Events or [Clear] to clear the entered data. Click [Cancel] to cancel the recording of other Medical Events.

  5. On successfully recording the other Medical Events, select the Reference No hyperlink to update Date, Category and/or Remarks fields. The medical event details are displayed in the lower part of the screen. You cannot change the Reference Number recorded. Click Remarks hyperlink to view the remarks recorded for the Medical Event.
