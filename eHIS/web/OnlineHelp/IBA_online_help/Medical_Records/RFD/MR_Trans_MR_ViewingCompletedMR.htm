Viewing the completed delivered Medical Report

From the MR Transactions > Medical Reports, select the Status tab.

The Status tab of the Medical Report screen gets displayed.

  1. Select the Medical Report Status as ‘Report Delivered’ and click [Search] to display all Medical Reports delivered or enter the search criteria and click [Search] to select the required Medical Report delivered.

  2. Click the Request ID hyperlink to invoke ‘Medical Report Completed’ screen.

  3. You can view details of the completed delivered Medical Report, Encounter Details, Date Details, Charges Details, Applicant Address and Previous Delivered Medical Reports of the patient.

  4. Click [Close] to exit from the ‘Medical Report Completed’ screen.
