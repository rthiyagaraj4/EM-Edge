Revising Medical Report

From the MR Transactions > Medical Board, select the Status tab.

The Status tab of the Medical Board screen gets displayed.

  1. Select the Medical Board Status as ‘Overdue’ and click [Search] to display all overdue Medical Report Requests that have crossed the collect date or enter the search criteria and click [Search] to select the required Medical Report Request.

  2. Click the Request ID hyperlink to invoke ‘Revise Medical Report’ screen.

  3. Enter the details in the mandatory (*) fields. <Related Field Description>

  4. Click [Record] to record revising of the medical report by board or [Close] to close the ‘Revise Medical Report’ screen.
