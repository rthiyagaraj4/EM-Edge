Enter the following details:

 

  1. Patient Details – The patient details that are displayed are patient Name, Age, Gender, Patient ID, Attending Practitioner, Location and Encounter ID.

  2. Medico Legal Case(MLC) – Select the check box if the encounter has to be identified as a Medico Legal Case. When the check box is selected, the ‘Marked By’ and ‘Marking Date’ fields become mandatory or the fields are disabled.

  3. One Stop Crisis Center (OSCC) - Select the check box if OSCC has to be marked against the patient’s encounter. When the check box is selected, the ‘Marked By’ and ‘Marking Date’ fields become mandatory or the fields are disabled.

  4. Marked By – Enter the name of the person recording MLC or OSCC against the encounter in alphanumeric format of 30 character maximum length.

  5. Marking Date – Enter the date or select the date on which the patient is marked as MLC or OSCC from the calendar function.
