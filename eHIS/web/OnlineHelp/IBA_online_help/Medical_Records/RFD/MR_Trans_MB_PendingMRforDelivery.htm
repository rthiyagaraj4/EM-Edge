Pending Medical Reports for Delivery

From the MR Transactions > Medical Board, select the Status tab.

The Status tab of the Medical Board screen gets displayed.

  1. Select the Medical Board Status as ‘Not Delivered’ and click [Search] to display all Medical Board Requests pending for delivery or enter the search criteria and click [Search] to select the required Medical Report not delivered.

  2. The Request Status displays the status of the Medical Board Request.

  3. Click the Request ID hyperlink to display the current stage of the Medical Board Request.

  4. Enter the required details and continue the process till the report is delivered by Medical Board.
