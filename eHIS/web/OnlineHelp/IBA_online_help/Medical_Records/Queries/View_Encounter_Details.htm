View Encounter Details

From this function you can Query all the encounters that a patient had with a facility.

This function is used to get a view of all the encounters that a patient had with a facility. The details populated are the Name, Age, Gender and Patient ID of the patient in the header. The encounter details are populated in the detail block. The information displayed in the detail block include the Encounter Date/Time, Encounter Type (inpatient, outpatient, etc), Encounter ID, Location Type where the patient had the encounter, the actual location where the encounter took place, room number and bed number if the patient had an inpatient encounter, the name of the attending practitioner, disposition date/time and the disposition type, referral details if the patient had been referred to an external location after the encounter and appointment details (if any) for the patient/encounter combination.

Querying to View Encounter Details

From the MR Queries, select the View Encounter Details function.

The View Encounter Details screen gets displayed.

  1. Enter the Patient ID in the alphanumeric format of 10 characters maximum length or select the Patient ID by clicking [?] function. This field is mandatory.  <Related Field Description>

  2. Click [Search] to select a specific record or [Clear] to clear the records displayed in the result area.

  3. Click the Encounter ID hyperlink to view the Encounter Details.

  4. Click [Reset] to clear the entered data.
