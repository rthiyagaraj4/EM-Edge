Check In to OR

Check In to OR function is used to record checking in of the patient to the Operating Room.

The Order ID, Date and Time of Booking, Surgeon, Specialty, Theatre, Booking Number, Date and Time of the surgery, Accession Number and current date are displayed.

The following details are displayed:

  1. Anesthesia Type – The Type of Anesthesia is displayed as recorded.

  2. Anesthetist – The Anesthetist is displayed as recorded.

  3. Theatre – The Theatre is displayed.

  4. Click Pre-operative clinical comments to view Pre-Operative details recorded for the patient.

  5. Click Verify Checklist hyperlink to verify the checklist.

 

Operations/Procedures

  1. The Operation Code, Description, Side Applicable and Remarks are displayed. Click the Pre-operative comments hyperlink to view the remarks recorded for the Operation/Procedure.

 

Check In to Details

  1. Check In Date Time – Enter the date and time of patient check in to the Operating Room. This is a mandatory field. The current system date and time is displayed, by default.

  2. Click [Apply] to save checking into operating room. The status of the patient is changed to ‘In OR’ with the colour indicator.

 

If an order catalog is configured as ‘consent required’ and the order is placed from OT without recording consent in Clinical Access module, the error message ’Consent is not taken for patient’ is displayed in Check-In to OR to restrict the patient checking into OR. You can check in to OR within the OT module, only if the consent is either recorded or refused for the OT order within the CA module.
