View Checklist

View Checklist function is used to view the pre operative, intra operative and post operative checklist for performing operation.

The Order ID, Surgeon, Specialty, Theatre, Booking Number, Date and Time of the surgery are displayed.

 

The following Checklist details are displayed:

  1. Check list Item - The Pre-Operative Checklist associated to the Specialty in Specialties function is displayed.

  2. Roles – The Roles displayed are associated to the Specialty under Pre-operative Checklist in the Specialties function. The Role selected for the OT Slate is enabled. Select the required checklist items.

  3. Remarks – Click the Remarks hyperlink to view the Previous Remarks and new remarks.

  4. Click [OK] to after viewing or [Cancel] to exit from the screen.
