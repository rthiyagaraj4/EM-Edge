Enter the following details:

 

  1. View Details - Select the check box ‘View Details’ to view the details of the chart. Select the check box ‘Summary’ to view the day summary of the recordings.

  2. Date Time – The current system date and time is displayed, by default. You can change the Date and Time. Enter the time in the hh: mm format. This field is mandatory.

  3. Sort Order – Select either Ascending or Descending from the list to sort records in the Ascending or Descending order based on selection.

  4. Period From/To – Enter the date range in the dd/mm/yyyy format. You can choose the date by clicking the calendar function.

  5. Scope – Enter the period range to view the details or summary, you can also enter the scope as number of days to view the summary (by using the direction tabs). The results matching the search criteria are displayed in the right hand side of the screen.
