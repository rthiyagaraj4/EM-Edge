Outcome

  1. Click Outcome from the menu to record the outcome of the Ectopic Pregnancy event.

  2. The mothercare pending tasks to be recorded for the event Ectopic Pregnancy is displayed if any. Click [Record] to continue recording the outcome or click [Cancel] to perform the pending tasks for the event. On clicking [Record], the screen to record outcome gets displayed.

  3. The Outcome list box displays Close as the outcome for the event Ectopic Pregnancy. Select Close as Outcome and message is displayed indicating the current cycle of recording Ectopic Pregnancy events for the patient will be closed. Click [OK] to close recording events and the current cycle ends. Click [Cancel] to cancel closing the event and cycle.

  4. In order to record any other events for the patient, a new cycle has to be created by selecting New from the Cycle list box on the top left side of the screen.
