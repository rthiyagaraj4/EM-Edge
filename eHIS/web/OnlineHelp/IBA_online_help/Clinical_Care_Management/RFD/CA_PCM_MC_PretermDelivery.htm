Preterm Delivery

Preterm Delivery is delivery that occurs before the 37th week of pregnancy. A birth that occurs before the 37th week of pregnancy is considered premature. Most pregnancies last 38-42 weeks. The due date is 40 weeks after the first day of the last menstrual period.

 

  1. Select Preterm Delivery. Click [Go].

  2. The tasks of Preterm Delivery are displayed on the left side of the screen. The tasks that can be recorded are

Assessment,

Labour and Delivery,

Post Natal Check List,

Post Natal Assessment and

Outcome.

  1. Click New Event icon to record additional events for any of the current event being recorded. The events available for recording are Reproductive Cancers and STD Prevention.

  2. Click icon to view the Maternity Home Page. The screen displays two tabs, namely, Home and History. History tab displays details of all events recorded for the patient.
