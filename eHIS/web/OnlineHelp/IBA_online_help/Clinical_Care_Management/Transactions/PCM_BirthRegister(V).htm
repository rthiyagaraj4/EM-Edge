Birth Register (V)

From this function you can View the Parent details and Newborn details of the newborn patient.

This function displays the Parent details and Newborn details as recorded in the Inpatient and Day Care Management or Patient Identity Management module.

Viewing Birth Register

From the CA Transactions > Clinician Access > Desktop Menu > select newborn patient > Patient Chart Menu > Patient Details, select Birth Register (V).

The View Birth Register screen gets displayed.

  1. The screen displays the newborn details in four frames:

Mother Details

Father Details

Newborn Details

Vaccination Details

  1. The Mother Details, Father Details, Newborn Details and Vaccination Details frames are displayed in a sequence. Click   to maximize the frames or   to minimize the frames. The mother details, father details, newborn details and vaccination details as recorded in the Inpatient and Day Care Management module or Master Patient Index module are displayed.
