Wait List is modified on Patient’s request or due to other reasons. This function is used to view details and modify certain details of the wait list. This function allows for reallocating the location for which the wait listing has been assigned as well as reallocates the resource for the wait listing. Further, this function is used to postpone the preferred date and reassign a priority for the waitlist.

To modify wait list, from the already created wait list record(s) click on ‘Modify Wait List’ hyperlink for the selected patient.

Enter the Reason for Modification or select the reason using [?] function.

The following details are displayed:

  1. Referral ID - The Referral Identification Number generated for referral is displayed and allows viewing of referral details on click of the hyperlink.

  2. Referred On – The date of referral is displayed.

  3. Referred To – The facility to which the patient referred is displayed.

  4. Specialty – The Specialty to which referral has been recorded is displayed.

  5. Location - The Location to which referral has been recorded is displayed.

  6. Practitioner - The name of the Practitioner against whom referral has been recorded is displayed.

  7. Appt. No – The appointment number is displayed, if appointment has been confirmed using the referral identification number, or the Book Appointment hyperlink appears to book appointment.

Close Consultation

From this function you can Close the consultation of a patient or check out an outpatient.

Check out is a process of concluding an encounter against a clinic at the end of consultation. This function is used for outpatients when the patient’s consultation is complete and the patient is either being sent home after treatment or is being referred to another facility for further treatment. Close Consultation function is used to close active an encounter and allows concluding an active episode by issuing a medical certificate, recalling a patient back to the same clinic by Booking an Appointment, creating and modifying Waitlist and referring the patient to another provider for further treatment.

Closing Consultation

From the CA Transactions > Clinician Access > Desktop Menu > select required patient > Patient Chart Menu > Discharge Activities, select Close Consultation.

The Close Consultation screen gets displayed.

  1. Enter the details in the mandatory (*) fields. <Related Field Description>

  2. Click [Book Appointment], if the patient is discharged and is required to come after a specified date by selecting the ‘Comeback’ check box and entering the recall details.

  3. Click [Create Waitlist] and [Modify Waitlist] if schedule is not free to Create and Modify waitlist.

  4. Click [Medical Certificate] to issue medical certificate. Click [OK] to record the Medical Certificate, [Clear] to clear the entered data or [Cancel] to exit from the screen.

  5. Click [Conclude Episode] to conclude episode. Enter Notes in the Notes field. Click [OK] to conclude episode or [Cancel] to exit from the screen without concluding the episode.

  6. Click [Record] to record the check out details and system prompts if patient requires a referral.

  7. On clicking [Record], the system prompts a pop-up message if patient requires a referral registration. Click [OK] in the message dialog to register a referral and the Register Referral Details screen is displayed. Click [Cancel] to return to patient queue screen.

  8. Enter the details in the mandatory fields in Register Referral Details screen. Click [Clear] to clear the entered details if required.

  9. On entering the details, click [Record] to add the patient as referral and Referral Details are displayed. Click [Clear] to clear the displayed details. Click [Refresh] to refresh the screen and on completion of registration of referrals, click [Close] to exit from the screen.
