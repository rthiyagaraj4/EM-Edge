View Alerts (V)

From this function you can View the alerts recorded for a patient.

This function helps in viewing all Allergies and High Risk Conditions recorded for the patient. The high risk conditions of a patient can be viewed only by authorized practitioners on entering a password as recorded in the Medical Records module for the practitioner.

Viewing Alerts

From the CA Transactions > Clinician Access > Desktop Menu > select required patient > Patient Chart Menu > Event Details, select View Alerts (V).

The View Alerts screen gets displayed.

  1. The screen displays the details of all active allergies recorded for the selected patient, by default. Select ‘All’ radio button to view the details of all allergies recorded for the patient. The details as recorded in the Allergies function are displayed.

  2. Select the Allergen hyperlink to view the Onset Details of the allergy and Close Details, if recorded.

  3. Click [Authorize] to view the high risk conditions recorded for the patient. A screen is displayed to enter the password. Click [OK] after entering the correct password to view active high risk conditions recorded for the patient. Select ‘All’ radio button to view the details of all high risk conditions recorded for the patient. The details as recorded in the High Risk Conditions function are displayed.

  4. Select the Description hyperlink to view the Onset Details of the high risk condition and Close Details, if recorded.
