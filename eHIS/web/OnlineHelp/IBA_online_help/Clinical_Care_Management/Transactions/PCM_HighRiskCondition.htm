High Risk Conditions

From this function you can Record new high risk conditions for a patient and view high risk conditions already recorded for the patient.

Recording High Risk condition is restricted only to authorized users, who have appropriate functional access rights, which is defined in the MR setup of Medical Records module. On selection of this function, system prompts for the Password.

Recording High Risk Conditions

From the CA Transactions > Clinician Access > Desktop Menu > select required patient > Patient Chart Menu > Other Clinical Event Recording, select High Risk Conditions.

The Record High Risk Condition screen gets displayed.

  1. The main screen displays an ‘Enter/Edit High Risk’ screen to enter the password to record or view high risk conditions for the selected patient. Enter the Password and click [OK] to validate the Password. If the entered Password is valid, the screen to record the High Risk conditions is displayed. If the entered Password is invalid, system displays an error message.

  2. Any High Risk Conditions already recorded for the patient is displayed on the top part of the screen.

  3. Enter the details in the mandatory (*) fields. <Related Field Description>

  4. Click [Record] to save the High Risk Condition recorded or [Clear] to clear the entered data. Click [Cancel] to exit from the screen.

  5. The details recorded are displayed on the top part of the screen. Click High Risk Conditions hyperlink to modify recorded details of the High Risk Condition.

  6. The field ‘Close Date’ is displayed to enter the close date of the High Risk Condition, if the High Risk Condition no longer exists for the patient.

  7. Click [Record] to save the modified details.
