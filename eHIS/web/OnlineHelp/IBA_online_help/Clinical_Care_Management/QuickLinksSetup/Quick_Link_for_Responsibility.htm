Quick Link for Responsibility

From this function, you can Create an association of Quick Link to Responsibility.

Creating an association of Quick Link to Responsibility

From the CA Code Setup > Quick Links Setup, select Quick Link for Responsibility function.

The Quick Link for Responsibility screen gets displayed.

  1. Click [Create] to invoke the Create screen.

  2. Enter the details in the mandatory (*) fields. <Related Field Description>

  3. Click [Apply] to save the association or [Reset] to clear the entered data.
