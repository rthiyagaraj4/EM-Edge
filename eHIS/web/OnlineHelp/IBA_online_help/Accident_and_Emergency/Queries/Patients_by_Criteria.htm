Patients by Criteria

From this function you can Query the patients who have visited the emergency department and this listing is generated based on various criteria such as the Presenting Symptom, Patient Type or Accident Type.

This function is used to filter the listing of emergency visits against a patient. This can be used to view a listing of patients who have visited the emergency department with common criteria.

Querying Patients by Criteria

From the AE Queries, select the Patient by Criteria function.

The Patient by Criteria screen gets displayed.

  1. Enter the details in the mandatory (*) fields or enter the search criteria and click [Search] to select a specific record. Click [Clear] to reset the records displayed in the result area. <Related Field Description>

  2. Click [Reset] to clear the entered data.

  3. Click ‘+’ hyperlink to view patient details.
