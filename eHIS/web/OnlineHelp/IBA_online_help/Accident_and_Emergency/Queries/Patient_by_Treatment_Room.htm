Patient by Treatment Room

From this function you can Query the patients who are currently in the emergency department. The patient details are viewed based on the occupancy within a particular treatment area.

This function is used to query a listing of patients currently in the emergency department. The function provides various query criteria that is used to filter the listing of patients within treatment rooms. The result also includes the list of patients currently treated in various treatment rooms with the current queue status.

Querying Patients by Treatment Room

From the AE Queries, select the Patient by Treatment Room function.

The Patient by Treatment Room screen gets displayed.

  1. Enter the search criteria and click [Search] to select a specific record or [Clear] to clear the records displayed in the result area. <Related Field Description>

  2. Click [Reset] to clear the entered data.
