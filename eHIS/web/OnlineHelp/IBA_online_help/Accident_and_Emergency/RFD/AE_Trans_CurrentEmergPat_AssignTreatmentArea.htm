Assign/Reassign Treatment Area

  1. If a Treatment Area is not assigned to a patient while registering attendance, the Assign Treatment Area screen gets displayed (If ‘Invoke Vital Signs from Queue’ check box is not selected in AE Parameters) on selecting the Patient Details hyperlink from Current Emergency Patients screen.

  2. Enter the details in the mandatory fields. <Related Field Description>

  3. Click [OK] to assign a Treatment Area to the patient or [Cancel] to exit from the screen.

  4. Visit Charge Details screen gets displayed on assigning the Treatment Area. Click [Continue] to proceed or [Revise] to revise charge details. On clicking [OK], the charge gets displayed.

  5. If the patient is assigned to a Treatment Area of any other Priority Zone, the patient is available under respective Priority Zone tab. Click the respective color tab and select Patient Name hyperlink to Reassign Treatment Area.

  6. Click Patient Name hyperlink and Assign Relationship screen gets displayed to assign a relationship with the practitioner.

If ‘Invoke Vital Signs from Queue’ check box is selected in AE Parameters, Record Vital Signs option is available for selection on clicking the Patient Details hyperlink from Current Emergency Patients screen.
