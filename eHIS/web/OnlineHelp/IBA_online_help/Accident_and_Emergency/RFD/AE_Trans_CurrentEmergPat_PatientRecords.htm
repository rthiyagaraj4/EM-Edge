1. Practitioner - The name of the Practitioner associated to the patient gets displayed. Practitioner name field is left blank, if the Practitioner is not associated to the selected patient.

  2. Priority – The Priority Zone assigned to the patient gets displayed. The colors used to represent the zones are:

  3. Red – Critical Zone

  4. Yellow – Semi Critical Zone

  5. Green – Non Critical Zone

  6. Date/Time In – The Date and Time the patient checked in to the facility gets displayed.

  7. Bed/Bay No – The Bed/Bay number assigned to the patient gets displayed.

  8. Patient Name – The Patient Name gets displayed.

  9. Patient ID – The Patient ID gets displayed.

  10. Patient Age - The ages of the Current Emergency Patients captured while registering the attendance of the Emergency patients get displayed. You can sort the patient queue in the ascending or descending order based on the age of the patient in all locations. This is applicable for all the tabs namely, Critical, Semi-Critical, Non-Critical, Unassigned and All.

  11. Wait Time – The Wait Time for the patient in the facility gets displayed.

  12. Status – The Status of the patient gets displayed. The various statuses applicable to a patient queue are ‘Checked In’, ‘Assigned Treatment Area’, ‘Undergoing Treatment’, ‘Checked Out’ and ‘Consultation in Progress’.

  13. Complaint - The patients' complaints captured during Secondary Triage get displayed. You can prioritize patients based on the complaints. The last recorded or the latest complaint gets displayed. If no complaints are entered, this column is displayed blank.

  14. Current Location – The Location Type/Location against which the patient visit is registered gets displayed.

  15. Disaster –  If the patient is a victim of Disaster, ‘yes’ is displayed or ‘no’ is displayed in the field.

  16. Dis. Regn. Reference - The Disaster Registration Number captured during the registration of disaster patients in 'Disaster Patient Registration' function gets displayed.

  17. Remarks - Click 'View Details' hyperlink to view the remarks captured during the registration of disaster patients in 'Disaster Patient Registration' function.  The 'View Details' hyperlink does not get displayed, if remarks is not captured during the disaster registration.

  18. Case of Trauma - If case patient is registered under case of Trauma, ‘yes’ is displayed or ‘no’ is displayed in the field.
