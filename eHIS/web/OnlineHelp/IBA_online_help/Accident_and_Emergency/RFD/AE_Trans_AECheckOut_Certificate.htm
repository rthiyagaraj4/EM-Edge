Enter the following details:

 

  1. Certificate Type – Select the Certificate Type from the list. The list box displays ‘Medical’ and ‘Others’. On selecting Medical, enter the Employment Details in the text area displayed.

  2. Medical Certificate No – Enter the Medical Certificate Number of the certificate being issued in alphanumeric format of 20 characters maximum length. This field is mandatory.

  3. Leave From – The leave from date and time is displayed in dd/mm/yyyy hh:mi format.

  4. Leave To – The leave to date and time is displayed in dd/mm/yyyy hh:mm format. The system date and time is displayed by default. This field is mandatory.

  5. No. of Days – System calculates and displays the number of days based on Leave From and To dates.

  6. Resume Activity – Select the check box to indicate that the patient can resume activity.

  7. Date – This field is enabled on selecting the Resume for activity check box. Enter the date in dd/mm/yyyy format. The entered date should be greater than or equal to ‘Leave To’ date.

  8. Certificate Issued – Select the check box to indicate whether the certificate has been issued.

  9. Issued Date – This field is enabled on selecting the Resume for activity check box. Enter the date in dd/mm/yyyy format. The entered date should be greater than or equal to ‘Leave To’ date.

  10. Authorized By –The assigned practitioner is displayed by default. You can change the practitioner name using [?] function. This field is mandatory.

  11. Remarks – Enter the remarks in alphanumeric format of 500 characters maximum length.
