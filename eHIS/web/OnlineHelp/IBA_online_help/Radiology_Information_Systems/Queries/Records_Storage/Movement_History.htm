Movement History

From this function you can View the movement details of a folder.

The receiving facility, the received date and the reason for the movement of the folder are displayed.

Viewing the Movement History of a Folder

From the Queries > Records Storage, double-click Movement History function.

The Movement History screen gets displayed.

  1. Enter the Patient ID in the length as specified in the MP Parameter or click ‘Right Click for User Options’ and click [Standard Patient Lookup] to select the Patient. The Patient Name, Sex and Age is displayed on selecting the patient. This is a mandatory field.

  2. The logged in facility is displayed, by default.

  3. Select the required folder from the ‘Select Folder’ list. All the folders created for the selected patient are populated in the list.

  4. On selecting the folder, Current Location/Status, Current Holder details are displayed.

  5. Enter the ‘From’ and ‘To’  Date in the format of dd/mm/yyyy or select the required date using the Calendar function.

  6. On entering the criteria, click [Search] or [Execute Query] or press [F8] to display the movement details of the selected folder.
