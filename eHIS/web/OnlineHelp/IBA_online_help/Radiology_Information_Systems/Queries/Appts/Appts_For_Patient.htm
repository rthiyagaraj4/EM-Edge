Appointments for Patient

From this function you can View the appointments scheduled for patients.

You can view the details of the appointments scheduled for patients.

Viewing the Appointments of a Patient

From the Queries > Appointments, double-click Appointments for a Patient function.

The Appointments for a Patient screen gets displayed.

  1. Click [Search], [Execute Query] or press [F8] to display the appointment details for the patients or enter the search criteria to display the appointment details based on the entered criteria.

    Enter the following query criteria:

    1. Patient ID – Enter the Patient ID in the length as specified in the MP Parameter or right click ‘Right Click for User Options’ to select a patient using Standard Patient Lookup function. On entering the Patient ID, the Name, Gender and Age of the patient are displayed.

    2. Date [From-To] - Enter the date range in the format of dd/mm/yyyy or select the date range using the Calendar function.

  2. On searching, the appointment details for the patients are displayed.
