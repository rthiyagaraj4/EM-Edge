Enter the following details:

  1. Order Catalog - The Order Catalog associated gets displayed.

  2. Panel/Service – Select a Panel or a Service from the list. The services associated to the Panel are associated to the patient records automatically and the charges are computed for the services. The charge is computed for the single service on selecting a service from the list.

  3. Service Code – Click [List of Values] to associate a Billing Service or a Billing Panel to the respective Patient. A bill is generated for the service during discharge, based on the quantity of service used. The services or the panels get listed based on the value selected for ‘Panel/Service’. This is a mandatory field.

  4. Incl/Excl as per Policy – The action is either displayed as ‘Included’ or ‘Excluded’ based on the value selected for ‘Include/Exclude Action’.

  5. Include/Exclude Action – Select a value from the list. You can either include the services or exclude the services based on the policy definition associated to the patient.

  6. Rate/Charge – The value gets displayed as ‘R’ or ‘C’ indicating that the selected service is either Rate or Charge based. You cannot update the value in this field.

  7. Original Qty – Enter the quantity in numeric format of four digits maximum length. System displays the value by default, if the selected service is charge based. You cannot update the quantity, if the service is charge based. This is a mandatory field.

  8. Revised Qty per Panel  – Enter the quantity in numeric format of four digits maximum length. System displays the updated quantity for the services associated to a Panel. You cannot update the quantity, if the service is charge based. This is a mandatory field.

  9. UOM – The Unit of Measure associated to the Billing Service during definition gets displayed. You cannot update the UOM value.

  10. Amount – The Charges are computed automatically based on the prices defined for the respective Billing Service. If the service is rate based, the prices are computed based on the Multipricing factor. If the service is charge based, the cost amount defined gets displayed.

  11. Pract/Staff Indicator – You can associate either a Practitioner or a Staff member to indicate who has associated the service to the patient. The values for ‘Doctor’ are listed based on the indicator selected.

  12. Practitioner/Staff ID – Click [List of Values] to select a Doctor. The values in the list differ based on the ‘Pract/Staff Indicator’ selected. This is a mandatory field.

  13. Total Payable – The total amount to be paid by the patient or the payer for the service availed is computed and displayed based on the service charge and the quantity of services used.  

  14. Patient Payable – The amount to be paid by the patient for the service gets displayed. If the Billing Group for the selected patient encounter is Insurance or Sponsor, a part of the amount to be paid by the patient as defined for the selected Policy Type is calculated and displayed.

  15. Patient Paid – The amount the patient has paid initially as the deposit towards the service gets displayed.

  16. Pre-Approval Required – The check box is selected or cleared based on the definition for the Policy Type associated to the patient. If the Billing Group is selected as Insurance and the Policy Type associated to the patient requires pre-approval while including or excluding services from the patient records, the check box is selected, by default. You cannot clear or select the check box.

  17. Serv Desc – The description for the selected Billing Service or Billing Panel gets displayed.

  18. Practitioner/Staff Name – The name of the Doctor or the Staff associated gets displayed.

  19. Pract/Staff Type desc – The designation of the Practitioner or the selected Staff gets displayed.

  20. [Add Services] - Click [Add Services] to associate additional services.

  21. [Remove Service] - Click [Remove Service] to associate additional services.

  22. [Accept] - Click [Accept] to save the charges.

  23. [Cancel] - Click [Cancel] to cancel the transaction.
