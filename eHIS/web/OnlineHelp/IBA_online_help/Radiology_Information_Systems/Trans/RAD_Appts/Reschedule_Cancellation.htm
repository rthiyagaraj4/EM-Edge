Reschedule/Cancellation

From this function you can Reschedule or Cancel a scheduled appointment for an examination and Query for the existing Rescheduled or Cancelled Appointment.

You must specify a valid reason for transferring/cancelling the appointment scheduled for an examination. You can cancel the Rescheduled appointments or unregistered examinations only. The Rescheduled appointments are displayed with unique color code.

Rescheduling/Cancelling a scheduled examination

From the Transactions > Appointments, double-click Reschedule/Cancellation function.

The Reschedule/Cancellation screen gets displayed.

  1. The scheduled appointments are displayed or enter the search criteria and click [Search] to select a specific appointment.

    Enter the following search criteria:

    1. Ordering Facility – Select the Ordering Facility from the drop-down list to display the orders based on the selected Ordering Facility. The value ’All Facilities’ is selected by default..

    2. Wing – Enter the Wing where the examination is booked or click [List of Values]. The Operating Unit/Wings are defined in the ‘Operating Units/Wings’ function under the ‘Administration’ Menu.

    3. Section – Enter the Section code or click [List of Values] to select the Section. The Sections associated to the selected Operating Unit/Wing in the ‘Sections for Operating Unit/Wing’ function are displayed.

    4. Priority – Select the Priority of the examination from the list. The listed values are All, Stat, Urgent and Routine. The value ‘All’ is displayed, by default.

    5. Patient ID – Enter the Patient ID in the length as specified in the MP Parameter or right click on ‘Right Click for User Options’ hyperlink to select the Patient ID using the Standard Patient Lookup function.

    6. Sex – Select the Gender of the patient. The value ‘All’ is displayed, by default.

    7. Appt. Ref. Num – Enter the patient’s Appointment Reference Number in the length as specified in the RD Parameter.

    8. Appointment Start Date/Time [From – To] – Enter the Appointment Start Date and Time range in the format of dd/mm/yyyy or select the Appointment Start and End Date/Time range of the appointment using the Calendar function.

  2. On searching, the scheduled appointments  are displayed based on the entered criteria.

  3. The displayed details are Priority, Patient ID, Name, Allergy Indicator, Rescheduled Appointment Indicator, Ordering Facility, Appointment Reference Number, Exam, Preferred Date and Time, Section, Study Location, Wing, Ordered By, Attended By, Appointment Booked By, Source Type, Source, Order Number, Order Date/Time, Episode Type, Episode Number and Visit Number.

  4. Right click on the Patient ID to Reschedule/Cancel the appointment.

Patient Demographics

On clicking Patient Demographics option, the ‘View Patient Details’ screen opens in a new Internet Explorer browser window. You can view the patient demographic details in the View Patient Details tab.

Reschedule

On clicking ‘Reschedule’ option, the Transfer Reason screen is displayed.  The Appointment details of the selected patient is displayed and disabled. You can modify the Wing and the Study Locations.

  • Reason – Enter the Reason code in alphanumeric format of six characters maximum length or click [List of Values] to select the Reason for Transfer. The Reason codes with Action Type ‘Transfer Appointment (Individual)’ are displayed for selection and are defined in the ‘Reasons’ masters. Click [OK] to record the reason for transferring an appointment or [Cancel] to exit from the Transfer Reason screen.  

  1. First Available – Select the check box if the appointment is rescheduled on the first availability basis.

  2. Other Study Locations (Double click to select) – Click [F8] or [Schedule] to display the Study Locations where the booked appointment can be transferred or rescheduled.

Right click on the booked slot or click [Transfer]. An alert message ‘This Slot is booked – Try a different slot’ is displayed. The Print Outputs screen is displayed, if ‘Internal Reports’ are defined for the selected Section Type and Facility.

Cancel

On clicking ‘Cancel’ the appointment details of the rescheduled or unregistered examinations is displayed and disabled.

  • Reason – Enter the Reason code in alphanumeric format of six characters maximum length or click [List of Values] to select the Reason for Cancellation. The Reason codes with Action Type ‘Cancel Appointment (Individual)’ are displayed for selection and are defined in the ‘Reasons’ masters.

  • Cancel – Select the check box to cancel the rescheduled appointment or unregistered examinations. On selecting the check box, a message ‘Do you want to cancel this Appt.?’ is displayed. Click [Yes] to proceed with the cancellation process or [No] to abort the process.

  1. Click [Save] to Reschedule or Cancel a Rescheduled Appointment or Unregistered examination or click [Clear Record] to clear the entered data.

  2. Click [Execute Query] or press [F8] to search for a Rescheduled or Cancelled examination.

The color indicators for Priority [P], Allergy Indicator [A] and Rescheduled Appointment [R] are displayed in the bottom of the screen. The Patient Name is highlighted with the color indicator ‘Red’ for the high risk patient. Click Rescheduled Appointment indicator to view the Appointment History. The Appointment History screen displays the initial booking details of the examination and the rescheduled booking details.
