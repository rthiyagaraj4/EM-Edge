Administration Routes/Reaction Codes

From this function you can Define Administration Route or Reaction Codes for a particular Drug and Query for the existing Administration Routes or Reaction Codes.

Administration Route is the path by which a drug, fluid or other substance is brought into contact with the body. You can define the Administration Route for a particular Drug used in the Radiology examinations. For example, Intramuscular, Intravenous and so on.

A Reaction is an effect produced by administering a drug or by a substance (allergen) to which a person is allergic. You can also define the Reaction codes which indicate the reaction after the drug has been administered. The Reaction depends on the examination that is performed on the patient.

Creating Administration Routes/Reaction Codes

From the Masters > Examinations, double click Administration Routes/Reaction Codes function.

The Administration Routes/Reaction Codes screen gets displayed.

  1. Enter the details in the mandatory (*) fields.  <Related Field Description>

  2. Click [Save] or press [F10] to save a created Administration Route/Reaction Code or click [Clear Record] to clear the entered data.

  3. Click [Execute Query] or press [F8] to search for an existing Administration Route/Reaction Code record.
