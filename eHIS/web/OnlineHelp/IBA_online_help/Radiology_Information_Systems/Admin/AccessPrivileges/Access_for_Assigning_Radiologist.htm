Access Rights for Assigning Radiologist

From this function you can Define the radiologist with assigning rights and Query for existing Radiologist with assigning rights.  

This function is used to define the radiologist with assigning rights so that the radiologist can assign and reassign patients to other radiologists until the exam report is finalized. This is achieved by including a drop-down list ‘Change To’ and two new check boxes ‘Unassigned’ and ‘Select All’ in the Authorise/Review by Radiologist function.

Defining Access Rights for Assigning Radiologist

From the Administration > Access Privileges > double-click Access Rights for Assigning Radiologist function.

The Access Rights for Assigning Radiologist screen gets displayed.

  1. Enter the details in the fields. <Related Field Description>

  2. Click [Save], or press [F10] to define radiologist with assigning rights or click [Clear Record] to clear the entered details.

  3. Click [Execute Query] or press [F8] to search for the existing radiologist with assigning rights.
