Tanner Staging

From this function you can Record the pictorial chart showing the stages of puberty for both boys and girls based on assessment.

Tanner Staging is a Pictorial Charting in which Clinician select an image designed for boys and girls to identify in which stages of puberty of boy/girl belongs to based on assessment. The Tanner Staging tool is used to assess the stages of puberty for both boys and girls.  

This function is applicable only for age group of 2 to 18 years. Also, this function is not applicable for unknown gender patient.

Tanner staging icon is introduced in Tool bar to invoke Tanner Staging function. On mouse over, the system displays the tip .

Recording Tanner Staging

From the CA Transactions > Clinician Access > Desktop Menu > select required patient, Patient Chart > Tanner Staging.

The Tanner Staging screen gets displayed.

  1. The Tanner staging images are displayed based on the patient gender.

    • For boys, there are two sections 1) Genital (Penis) Development 2) Pubic Hair.

    • For girls, there are two sections 1) Breast Development 2) Pubic Hair.

  1. Click the image relevant to the patient stage based on assessment. For girls, you can independently select the Breast Development and Pubic Hair section.  

  2. Only after the selection is done, the Record button gets enabled. Click [Record] to save the changes or click [Reset] to clear the changes done.

  3. Once recording is done, the Print and Audit Trail buttons get enabled. Click [Print] to take print of the tanner staging assessment done.

  4. Click [Audit Trail] to view the audit trail details in View Tanner Staging History dialog.
