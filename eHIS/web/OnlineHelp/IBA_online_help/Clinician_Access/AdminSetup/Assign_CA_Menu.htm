Assign CA Menu

From this function you can Assign the constructed CA menu to a specific Patient Class, Practitioner Type and Practitioner. You can also Query for an assigned CA Menu record and Delete a queried record.

Using ‘Assign CA Menu’ function, CA Menu displayed in Clinician Desktop and Patient Chart is customized for a particular ‘Practitioner’ belonging to a particular ‘Practitioner Type’ and associated with a particular ‘Patient Class’.

Assigning CA Menu

From the CA Admin Setup, select Assign CA Menu function.

The Assign CA Menu screen gets displayed.

  1. Click [Create] to invoke the Create screen.

  2. Enter the details in the mandatory (*) fields. <Related Field Description>

  3. Click [Apply] to save assigned CA menu record or [Reset] to clear the entered data.

  4. Click [Query] to search for an existing record.

  5. Click [Delete] to delete a queried record.
