Relationship for Responsibility

From this function you can Create an association of a Relationship to a Responsibility and assign rights. You can Query for a Relationship associated to a Responsibility record and Delete a queried record.

All Direct Relationships have to be associated to a responsibility to make it applicable to practitioners logging in to Clinician Access with that responsibility. The practitioners are able to associate only those relationships that are linked to the responsibility they have used for logging into the system. This function further helps defining, if a particular responsibility can ‘Hold’ a relationship, ‘Assign’ the relationship to other practitioners or ‘Terminate’ the relationship assigned to other practitioners.

Creating an association of Relationship to Responsibility

From the CA Admin Setup, select Relationship for Responsibility function.

The Relationship for Responsibility screen gets displayed.

  1. Click [Create] to invoke the Create screen.

  2. Enter the details in the mandatory (*) fields. <Related Field Description>

  3. Click [Apply] to save the Relationship associated to Responsibility created or [Reset] to clear the entered data.

  4. Click [Query] to search for existing Relationship for Responsibility record.

  5. Click [Delete] to delete a queried record.
