Health Risk Factors for Disease

From this function you can Create a new Health Risk Factor for Disease by grouping it under a diagnosis set and Query for the existing records.

Health Risk Factors for Disease function helps the users to group all the risk factors to be considered for identifying a disease in patient. A Health Risk Factors for Disease contains a group of risk factors. Each risk factor is assigned a risk score internally, based on the risk indicator selected while defining a risk factor.

Creating a Health Risk Factor for Disease

From CA Code Setup > CA Health Risk Assessment Setup, select the Health Risk Factors for Disease function.

The Health Risk Factors for Disease screen gets displayed.

  1. Click [Create] to invoke the Create screen.

  2. Enter the details in the mandatory (*) fields. <Related Field Description>

  3. Click [Apply] to save the Health Risk Factors for Disease created or [Reset] to clear the entered data.

  4. Click [Query] to search for existing Risk Factor Group records.
