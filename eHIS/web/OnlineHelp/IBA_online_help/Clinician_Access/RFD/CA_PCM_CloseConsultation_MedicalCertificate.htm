Enter the following details:

 

  1. Certificate Type – Select the Certificate Type from the list. The values are ‘Medical’ and ‘Others’. The Employment Details are displayed, if recorded at the bottom of the screen on selecting ‘Medical’.

  2. Medical Certificate No – Enter the Medical Certificate Number of the certificate being issued in alphanumeric format of 20 characters maximum length. This field is mandatory.

  3. Leave From – The Leave From date and time in dd/mm/yyyy hh:mm format is displayed.

  4. Leave To – The Leave To date and time in dd/mm/yyyy hh:mm format is displayed. The system date and time is displayed by default. This field is mandatory.

  5. No. Of Days – The system calculates and displays the number of days based on Leave From and To dates.

  6. Resume Activity – Select the check box to indicate that the patient can resume activity.

  7. Date – This field is enabled on selecting the Resume for activity check box. Enter the date in dd/mm/yyyy format. The entered date has to be greater than or equal to ‘Leave To’ date.

  8. Certificate Issued – Select the check box to indicate the certificate has been issued.

  9. Issued Date – This field is enabled on checking the Certificate Issued check box. Enter the date in dd/mm/yyyy format. The entered date has to be greater than or equal to ‘Leave To’ date.

  10. Authorized By – The assigned practitioner is displayed, by default. Change the practitioner name by clicking [?] function if required. This field is mandatory.

  11. Remarks – Enter the Remarks in alphanumeric format of 500 characters maximum length. This field is mandatory.

  12. Employment Details – Enter the Employment Details of the patient if the same has not been updated during the patient registration.
