Search

This function helps in searching past Social History of the patient and the required contents of the Note Type can be viewed by clicking on the required Note Type hyperlink.

  1. By default the Notes recorded are displayed for the date range specified in the Search Tab of the Patient Social History screen. Enter the query criteria and click [Search] to display specific Notes. <Related Field Description>

  2. The following details of the Notes are displayed. Date and Time of recording the Note, Note Type which is hyperlinked, Reference Number of the document, Medical/Ancillary Service, Title, Practitioner who recorded the Note, Practitioner who authorized the Note and Status of the Note.

  3. Click the Note Type hyperlink to view the contents of the Note recorded. The Clinical Note Details screen is displayed with details of the Note.

 The Operations that can be performed on a Document is based on the 'User Role' and 'Status' of the Document.

The different User Roles are Transcriber, Author but without Authorization Facility, Author with Authorization Facility, Reviewer Privilege for Verifying and Authorization, and View Only Privilege.

The various Statuses are In Progress, Transcribed, Unauthorized, Authorized, Modified and In Error.
