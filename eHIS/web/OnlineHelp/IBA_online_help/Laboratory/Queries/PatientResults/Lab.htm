Lab

From this function you can Query the status of all the registered requests and their results for a given patient number.

The Lab query is used to view the results for a given specimen number and it is used by the laboratory personnel. The query shows all the results for the request irrespective of its processing stage. The reports for all the tests are shown in the print format on the screen.

Querying for the Lab details

From Query > Patient Results, double-click the Lab function.

The Lab query screen gets displayed.

  1. If the Order Number is available, enter the Order Number, for which the specimen has to be registered.

  2. Enter the Patient ID or right-click on 'Right Click for User Option' link to select it. When you right click 'Hospital Patient Lookup' and 'External Patients Lookup' links are popped up. Select any of the options to select the patient ID. <Related Field Description>

  3. Click ‘Tab’ to view the patient details.

  4. Click [Patient Details] to view the patient details of the patient.

  5. Click [Conflicting Referral Patient] to invoke the query to view all such patients.

  6. Click [Worklist Details] to view the details of the worklist.

  7. Click [Specimen Image] to view the image of the specimen.

  8. Click [Whodunit] to view the details for all the activities for a given specimen number.

  9. Click [Patient Marker] to view the patient marker of the patient.

  10. Click [Result] to view the results for the tests performed.

  11. Click [Specimen] to view the specimen details for the selected request.

  12. Click [Transfusion History] to view the transfusion history of the patient.

  13. Click [View Patient Clinical History] to view patient clinical history details.

  14. Click [Clinical Comments] to view the clinical comments of the patient.

  15. Click [More Tests] to view all the tests requested for the specimen

  16. Click [Process Sheet] to maintain the process sheet.

  17. Click [Clear] clear the entered details.
