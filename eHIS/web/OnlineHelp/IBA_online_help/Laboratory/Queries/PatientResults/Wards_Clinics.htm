Wards/Clinics

From this function you can Query the status of all the registered requests and their results for a given patient number.

The ward/clinic personnel (non-laboratory) use wards/Clinics query. On searching, only the completed results for a request are displayed. The reports for all the completed tests are displayed in the print format. The print status for each of the completed test is displayed.

Querying status of registered requests

From Queries > Patient Results, double-click the Wards/Clinics function.

The Wards/Clinics screen gets displayed.

  1. If the Order Number is available, enter the Order Number, for which the specimen has to be registered.

  2. Enter the Patient ID or right-click on the ‘Right Click for User Options’ function to search for a patient record using the ‘Hospital Patient Lookup’ or the ‘External Patient Lookup’ screen. Double-click the Patient ID in the displayed list to select the patient record.. <Related Field Description>

  3. Click ‘Tab’ to view the patient details.

  4. Click [Print/Fax/Email] to Print or Fax or Email the specimen details of the selected patient to the selected Printer, Fax or Email address.

  5. Click [Patient Details] to view the patient details of the patient.

  6. Click [Result] or double click on specimen number to view the patient results.

  7. Click [View Confidential Tests] to view the confidential tests for the selected patient.

  8. Click [Specimen] to register the selected specimen.

  9. Click [More Tests] to view all the tests requested for the specimen.

  10. Click [Transfusion History] to view the transfusion history.

  11. Click [View Clinical History] to view the clinical history of the selected patient.

  12. Click [Clinical Comments] to view the clinical comments for the selected patient.

  13. Click [Clear] to clear the entire details.
