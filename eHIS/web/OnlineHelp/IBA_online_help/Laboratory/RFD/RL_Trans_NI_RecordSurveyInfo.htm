Enter the following details to record survey information:

  1. Serial No. – The Serial number generated for the selected patient is displayed.

  2. Patient ID – Enter the Patient ID in alphanumeric format of length as defined in the MP Parameter function of ‘Master Patient Index’ module or right click on ‘Right Click for User Options’ and select the Patient ID using the ‘Standard Lookup Function’. This is a mandatory field. Press [Tab] on selecting the Patient ID. This is a mandatory field.

  3. Sex – The Gender of the Patient is displayed.

  4. Date of Birth – The patient’s Date of Birth is displayed. This is a mandatory field.

  5. Race – The Race of the patient is displayed.

  6. Date of Admission – The Admission Date of the patient to the hospital, is displayed.

  7. Date of Discharge – The patients discharge date is displayed.

  8. Ward – The Ward where the patient is admitted is displayed. This is a mandatory field.

  9. Specialty – The Specialty where the Patient is treated is displayed. This is a mandatory field.

  10. Onset Date – The Onset date of the patient is displayed. This is a mandatory field.

  11. Remarks - Enter the remarks related to the infection/patient. This is a mandatory field.
