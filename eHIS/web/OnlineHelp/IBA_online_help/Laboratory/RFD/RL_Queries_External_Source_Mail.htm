Enter the following details:

 

  1. Location Code [From-To] – Click [List of Values] to select the code. The codes defined in the masters are displayed.

  2. Registration Date [From-To] – Select the date using calendar function. By default, the current date and time is displayed in these fields. You can enter the past date in the From Date section. You cannot enter the future date. The time in From date field should be less than time in To date field.

  3. Released Date [From-To] – Select the date using calendar function. By default, the current date and time is displayed in these fields. You can enter the past date in the From Date section. You cannot enter the future date. The time in From date field should be less than time in To date field.

  4. Email Date [From-To]- Select the date using calendar function. By default, the current date is displayed in these fields. You can enter the past date in the From Date section. You cannot enter the future date. The time in From date field should be less than time in To date field.

  5. Department [From-To]- Click [List of Values] to select the Department. The departments defined in the masters are displayed.

  6. Specimen No [From-To]- Click [List of Values] to select the Specimen No. The departments defined in the masters are displayed.

  7. Patient ID [From-To]- Enter the Patient ID or right-click on the ‘Right Click for User Options’ function to search for a patient record using the ‘Hospital Patient Lookup’ or the ‘External Patient Lookup’ screen. Double-click the Patient ID in the displayed list to select the patient record.
