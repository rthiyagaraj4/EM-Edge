Enter the following details:

  1. Specimen Number - Enter the Specimen Number to be resulted or click [List of Values] to select it from the displayed list.

  2. Patient ID – On selecting the Specimen Number, the unique ID assigned to the patient is displayed.

  3. Age/Sex - The Patient’s age and gender are displayed.

 

Resulting

  1. No. of White Cells – Enter the number of white blood cells to calculate.

  2. No. of Parasites – Enter the number of Parasites to calculate.

  3. Parasites Load % values – The calculated result in percentage will be displayed.

  4. Film Comments – Select the file comments from the [List of Values] function. The listed values are created in Administration Setup.

  5. Total – The total malaria cells will be displayed.

  6. KB -  Enter the valid keystrokes or select it using the [List of Values] function. This Test box will get enabled on clicking the [Keyboard].
