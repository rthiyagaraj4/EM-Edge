Transmit Worklist to Analyzer

From this function you can Transmit a worklist to an Analyzer.

The Transmit Worklist to Analyzer function is used to transmit the worklists to associated Analyzers. This is valid if the associated analyzers do not have the ‘download’ option as selected. The Analyzers (linked to the system) are programmed to download worklist information to perform the investigations on the specimen. The specimens are sent to the analyzer, if the analyzer is associated to the worklist. The specimens are sent to all the analyzers, if analyzer is not associated to the worklist.

This function is not required for those analyzers for which auto download is selected. The Analyzers (linked to the system) are programmed to download worklist information to perform the investigations on the specimen.

Transmitting Worklist to Analyzer

From the Transactions > Worklist menu, select the Transmit Worklist to Analyzer function.

The Transmit Worklist to Analyzer screen gets displayed.

  1. The name of the department is associated to the logged in user and facility is displayed. Select the department from the list, if you have access to multiple departments.

  2. Click [List of Values] to select a worklist name. The information is downloaded from the selected Worklist to the analyzer. This is a mandatory field.

  3. Select the check box ‘Repeat Transmit’ to repeat transmission of worklists to the analyzer. The check box is cleared, by default.

  4. Click [Qlty Cup Details] to invoke Quality Cup Details screen or [Close] to return to the ‘Transmit Worklist to Analyzer’ screen.

  5. Press [Tab] to query and select the corresponding worklists. The worklist number, date and associated analyzer is displayed.

  6. Select the check box ‘Download’ to download the work list to the analyzer. The check box is cleared, by default.

  7. Click [Commit] to transmit the worklist to the analyzer.
