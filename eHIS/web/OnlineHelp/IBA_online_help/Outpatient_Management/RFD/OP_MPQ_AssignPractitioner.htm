Assign Practitioner

The Assign Practitioner option is used to assign a Practitioner to patients with queue status as 'Checked-In' or 'At Nursing Station' or 'Vital Signs Recorded', if the Practitioner is not assigned to the patient during Visit Registration.

  1. Click the Assign Practitioner option from the queue status to display the Assign Practitioner screen.

  2. Select the Room Number from the list. The Room Number specified during Visit Registration is displayed, by default. This field is mandatory if no rooms are associated to the Clinic.

  3. Select the required Sub-service from the list. All the Sub-services associated to the displayed service are listed.

  4. Select the Other Resource type from the list. Based on the Other Resource type selected, the corresponding values are populated in the [?] function.

  5. Click [Record] to assign a Practitioner or [Close] exit from the screen.

  6. On recording, Visit Charge Details screen gets displayed.

  7. Click Hospital Charge or Professional Charge hyperlink to view/edit the service charge details based on the billing panel. The Services and the charges associated to the billing panel is displayed.

  8. Click [Continue] to proceed or [Revise] to revise the details entered in the Assign Practitioner option.

After assigning a practitioner, the queue options automatically changes to Record Vital Signs, Reassign Practitioner, Start Consultation, Revert to Check In and Patient Chart.
