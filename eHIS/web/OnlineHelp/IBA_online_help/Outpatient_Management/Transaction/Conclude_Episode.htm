Conclude Episode

From this function you can list all patients with open episodes and Conclude an episode by specifying a closing date and conclusion remarks.

An episode comprises of one or more encounters registered for a common service. Encounters are assigned to an Episode at the time of visit registration and the episode can be concluded at an appropriate stage using Conclude Episode function.

 

Hospital and Professional Charges are displayed on recording the financial details of a patient. The patient payable amount towards Hospital charge as well as Doctor’s charge is displayed. Professional Charge is displayed along with the Hospital Charge, only if the billing details and visit registration rules are defined for the Clinic. The doctor’s charge has to be a part of the panel, if the Professional charge has to be set as zero. If the Professional Charge is set as a Service, you cannot set the doctor’s fee as zero. You can instead associate the Hospital Charges to make up the Professional fees, when the doctor does not charge the patient for the services.

Concluding an Episode

From the OP Transactions, select the Conclude Episode function.

The Conclude Episode screen gets displayed.

  1. Enter the details in the mandatory (*) fields. <Related Field Description>

  2. Click [Search] to display the patient records. Click [Clear] to reset the records displayed in the result area.

  3. Click ‘+’ hyperlink to view the Episode Visit Details.

  4. Click Episode ID hyperlink to display the Conclude Episode screen.

  5. Enter the required end date and time in dd/mm/yyyy hh:mm format. The entered end date and time should be less than or equal to current date and time. This field is mandatory.

  6. Enter the Notes related to the episode conclusion in alphanumeric format. This field is mandatory.

  7. Click [Record] to conclude the Episode, [Clear] to clear the entered data or [Cancel] to exit from the screen.

 

For a patient record to populate in conclude episode function, the patient needs to register a visit and checked out from a particular Location.
