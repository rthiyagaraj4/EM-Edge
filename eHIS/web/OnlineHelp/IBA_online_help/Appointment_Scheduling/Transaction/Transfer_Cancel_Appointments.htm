Reschedule/Cancel Appointment

From this function you can Reschedule or Cancel Appointments due to non-availability of resources or patient’s request.

Reschedule function allows you to reschedule appointments to a different Practitioner within the same location and transfer the date of booking. Transfer of date is made to an earlier date or future date depending on the availability of schedules. Appointments are cancelled due to non-availability of resources or based on the patient’s request; appointments on future date are only allowed to be cancelled. This function is used to perform reschedule and cancellation of appointments apart from the ‘Schedule Appointment’ function.

Rescheduling/Cancelling Appointments

From the OA Transactions, select the Reschedule/Cancel Function.

Reschedule/Cancel screen gets displayed.

  1. Enter the details in the mandatory (*) fields. <Related Field Description>

  2. Click [Select] to display patients with appointments. Click [Clear] to clear the displayed data if needed.

  3. Click ‘Appt No’ hyperlink to Transfer Appointments or Cancel Appointments. The screen displays three options.

Cancel Appointment

Reschedule Appointment

Modify Appointment

For Radiology Appointments

If Appointment Scheduling and Radiology modules are interfaced,  follow the below mentioned conditions for Transfer/Cancel/Modify Radiology Appointments:

  1. The Appointment cannot be transferred once the appointment time is elapsed.

  2. The Transfer appointment can be done only to other Procedure Unit of Type Radiology having the same Order Catalog linked to it.

  3. ‘Procedure Unit of Type’ Radiology is not displayed in the Location lookup in the Bulk Transfer function.

  4. Appointment cannot be cancelled if it is linked to an order.  

  5. Once the transfer is complete a popup is displayed, on clicking [OK] the Waitlist screen gets displayed with Location Type, Location and Resource fields defaulted and disabled.
