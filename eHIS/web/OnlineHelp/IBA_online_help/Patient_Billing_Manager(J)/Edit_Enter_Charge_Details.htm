Edit/Enter Charge Details

The charge for the Billing Services associated to the Order Catalog are displayed based on the quantity of service used. You can modify the quantity of service utilized using this screen.

 

Following details are displayed:

 

  1. Order Catalog - Order Catalog is displayed, if any orders are placed.

  2. Panel - The Billing Panel code is displayed, if it is associated.

  3. Select - Select the check box and click [Modify] to update the charges.

  4. Service - The Billing Service associated to the respective Clinic and the Visit Type is displayed.

  5. Include/Excluded Action - The value is displayed as either 'Include' or 'Exclude' based on the policy coverage definition.

  6. Reason - The Reason selected is displayed.

  7. Rate/Charge - The value is either displayed as either 'Rate' or 'Charge' based on the value selected during Billing Service definition.

  8. Original Quantity per Panel - The quantity of service in the associated Panel is displayed.

  9. UOM - The unit of measure of the selected service is displayed.

  10. Revised Quantity Per Panel - The revised quantity of service per panel is displayed.

  11. Total Quantity - Total Quantity of the selected service associated to the patient is displayed.

  12. Rate - The rate of the Billing Service is displayed based on the quantity of service used.

  13. Practitioner/Staff Type - The associated Staff Type is displayed.

  14. Practitioner/Staff - The name of the selected Practitioner or other Staff is displayed.

  15. Total Payable - The total amount payable for the service is displayed.

  16. Patient Payable - The patient payable amount is displayed.

  17. Patient Paid - The amount paid by the patient for the service is displayed.

  18. Preapproved Reqd - The check box is selected, if pre-approval is required for insurance coverage.

  19. [Add] - Click [Add] to record bill settlement details. You can record settlement details using multiple payment modes by clicking [Add].

  20. Click [Modify] to update charges or [Accept] to save the details. Click [Cancel] to exit from the screen.

  21. [Remove] - Click [Remove] to dissociate respective settlement record. A confirmation message 'Do you want to Delete the Record?' is displayed. Click [OK] to delete the record or [Cancel] to abort the transaction.
