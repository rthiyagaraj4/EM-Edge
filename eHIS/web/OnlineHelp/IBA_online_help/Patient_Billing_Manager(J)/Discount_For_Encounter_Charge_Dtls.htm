Discount for Encounter Charge Details

The patient details are displayed along with the encounter charge details. The Billing Services associated to a patient, item name, service date, gross amount, discount amount and the net amount are listed. Billing Service and Discount Amount is displayed as a hyperlink.

Click Billing Service hyperlink to view service price for the respective encounter. If multiple payers are associated to the patient, service price is computed based on the coverage limit amount. The Billing Services associated to the Order Catalog and to the respective encounter are listed.

The Discount Amount is displayed as a hyperlink. Click the hyperlink to issue discount. You can issue a discount on the Order Catalog or on the individual Billing Service associated to the Order Catalog.

Click [Close] to record the details. A visit bill number is generated on clicking [Close].
