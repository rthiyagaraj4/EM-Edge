OP/EM Auto Bill Generation

From this function you can Generate payer bills and cash bills if cash component is zero or unbilled entries amounting to zero is present due to cancellation or recalculation for OP and Emergency visit.

The OP / EM Auto Bill Generation function OP / EM Auto Bill Generation is used to search for outpatients and emergency patients where the Payer Outstanding is present and Patient payable is equal to 0. You can select one or more records and generate payer bill for multiple patients. If error is occurred while generating the bill for any one of the selected records, the system must exclude that record and generate bill for the remaining selected records.

Generate OP/EM Auto Bill Generation

From the Transactions > OP Billing, select Modify OP/EM Auto Bill Generation

The OP/EM Auto Bill Generation screen gets displayed.

  1. Click [Create] to invoke the Create screen.

  2. Enter the search criteria <Related Field Description>

  3. Click [Search] to search for outpatients and emergency patients where the Payer Outstanding is present and Patient payable is equal to 0

  4. Click [Apply] to generate bill automatically or [Reset] to clear the entered data.

  5. Click [Query] to search for existing Age Group records.
