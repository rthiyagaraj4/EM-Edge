Auto Bill Generation Job Status

From this function you can Query job status of auto bill generation.

The Auto Bill Generation Job Status function is used to query job status of auto bill generation. You can generate the job status list and export the details in Excel.

Querying Auto Bill Generation Job Status

From the Queries > select Auto Bill Generation Job Status

The Auto Bill Generation Job Status screen gets displayed.

  1. Enter the search criteria <Related Field Description>

  2. Click [Execute] to display Job list containing Serial No, Job Id, Job Status, Job Submitted User and Job Submitted Date/Time.

  3. Click a Select button against one of the jobs to display two tabs Processed Without Error and Processed With Error. The Processed Without Error tab displays Patient ID, Visit Date, Type, Episode No., Visit, Location, Description, Bill Document No. and Payer Amount. The Processed Without Error tab displays Patient ID, Visit Date, Type, Episode No., Visit, Location, Description, Payer Amount and Error Description.

  4. Click [Export] to export the job status in excel sheet.
