Bill Settlement Details

The Bill Settlement Details screen pops-up, if the check box 'Allow AE Checkout with Bill Settlement' is selected. A bill-cum-receipt is printed on recording the settlement details. User can record multiple settlement records, if the patient settles bill amount using multiple settlement modes. Bill Settlement Details screen pops-up, if the Cashier has logged into the Cash Counter.

 

You cannot checkout the patient, if outstanding patient bills exist for the encounter. An error message 'Outstanding Patient Bill(s) exists for this encounter. Cannot Proceed' is displayed. Click [OK].

An hyperlink 'Outstanding Bills Settlement' is displayed in the 'Check Out' function. Click the hyperlink to settle bill details. The Bill Number and outstanding amount are displayed with the Bill Number displayed as a hyperlink. Click the hyperlink to view and settle bill details. A Receipt Number is generated on clicking [OK].

An error message 'Cannot cancel the Cash Bill Settlement Process as per the AE Parameter Setup' is displayed, if you click [Cancel].

 

Bill Details

  1. Bill No - The Bill Document Type and Number is displayed.

  2. Payable Amount - The amount payable for the services availed are displayed. If a discount has been issued, net amount is displayed as the payable amount.

  3. Settlement Amount - The outstanding amount to be settled by the patient during checkout is displayed.

  4. Received through mail - Select the check box, if the settlement amount is received through mail.

 

Settlement by Services

  1. Date - The date and time indicates the date and time of transaction.

  2. Billing Service - The Billing Services associated to the Order Catalog are displayed.

  3. Description - The description of the associated Billing Services are displayed.

  4. Charge Amount - The price of the respective Billing Service is displayed.

  5. Amount Settled - The amount settled by the patient for the respective Billing Service is displayed.

  6. Pending Settlement - The outstanding amount to be settled for the respective Billing Service is displayed.

  7. Current Settlement - The settlement amount for the respective Billing Service is displayed.

  8. Round-Off - Any decimal amount that is rounded-off to the nearest whole amount, is displayed.

  9. Total - The total charge amount for the services associated to the Order Catalog is displayed.

  10. Settled Amount - Total amount settled by the patient is displayed.

 

Payment Details

  1. Select - Select the check box to modify or dissociate the selected settlement record.

  2. Amount Collected - The amount collected through a specific Settlement Type is displayed.

  3. Settlement Type - The Settlement Type through which the amount is settled is displayed.

  4. Cash Given - The cash amount given by the patient during bill settlement is displayed.

  5. Cash Returned - Cash amount returned to the patient after deducting the bill amount is displayed.

  6. Cheque/Card/Bank A/c No. - The cheque number, credit card number or bank account number are displayed based on the settlement type selected and details recorded during bill settlement.

  7. Date - This indicates the transaction date.

  8. Credit Card Co. - The name of the credit card company recorded during bill settlement is displayed.

  9. Bank Name - The name of the bank recorded during bill settlement is displayed for cheque settlement types.

  10. Branch Name - The name of the branch associated to the selected bank is displayed.

  11. Batch No - The batch number recorded during bill settlement for credit card settlement type is displayed.

  12. Approval Code - The approval code recorded during bill settlement for credit card settlement type is displayed.

  13. Sale Draft No - The sale draft number recorded during bill settlement is displayed.

  14. Payer's Name - The Payer's Name recorded during bill settlement is displayed.

  15. Payer Ref Id/Deposit Ref No. - The Payer's Reference ID and Deposit Reference number is displayed.

  16. Online Approval - This check box is selected for Credit Card transactions. You cannot select or clear the check box.

  17. [Add] - Click [Add] to record bill settlement details. You can record settlement details using multiple payment modes by clicking [Add].

  18. [Modify] - Click [Modify] to update bill settlement details.

  19. [Remove] - Click [Remove] to dissociate respective settlement record. A confirmation message 'Do you want to Delete the Record?' is displayed. Click [OK] to delete the record or [Cancel] to abort the transaction.  

  20. Click [OK] to record the settlement details or [Cancel] to cancel the process. A Receipt Number is generated on clicking [OK]. An error message 'Cannot cancel the Cash Bill Settlement Process as per the AE Parameter Setup' is displayed.
