Enter the following details:

  1. Card Type –The card type of the selected patient is displayed. You cannot modify the data.

  2. Price Class – The Price Class of the selected patient is displayed. You cannot modify the data.

  3. Date [From-To] – The Health Card validity date is displayed. You cannot modify the data.

  4. Membership Fees – The membership Fee of the selected patient is displayed. You cannot modify the data.

 

Privilege Card Member

  1. Patient ID – The selected patient ID is displayed. You cannot modify the data.

  2. Patient Name – The patient name of patient ID is displayed. You cannot modify the data.

  3. Age – The Age defined in the Health Card Definition for the selected patient is displayed. You cannot modify the data.

  4. Price Class – The Price Classes defined in the Health Card Subscription screen are displayed. You cannot modify the data.

  5. Delete – Select the check box against the Patient ID to delete the Health Card details of the Patient ID.

 

OP Visit Utilization Status

  1. No. Of Allotment – The number of OP visit allotted for the selected patient is displayed. You cannot modify the data.

  2. Till Date Availed – The number of OP visits utilized by the patient is displayed. You cannot modify the data.

  3. Clinic –The clinics utilized by the patient is displayed. You cannot modify the data.

  4. Visit Type – Click [?] to select the Visit type. The visit types defined in the Outpatient Management module are listed. You cannot modify the data.

  5. No. of Visit –The number of OP visits completed by patient is displayed. You cannot modify the data.

  6. Availed – The number of OP visits utilized by the patient is displayed.
