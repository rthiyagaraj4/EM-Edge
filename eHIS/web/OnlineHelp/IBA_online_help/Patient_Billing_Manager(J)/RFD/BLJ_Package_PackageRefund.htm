The following details are displayed:

 

  1. Indicator - The indicator associated to the respective Package is displayed.

  2. Service Code - The Service Code associated to the selected Indicator is displayed.

  3. Service Description - The description of the respective service is displayed.

  4. Order Catalog Code - The Order Catalog associated to the respective service is displayed.

  5. Refund - The values are displayed as 'Y' or 'N' to indicate whether the service is refundable or not based on the package definition.

  6. Refund Rate - The refund rate recorded for the respective service during package definition is displayed.

  7. Utilized Service Qty - The quantity of respective service that is ordered and registered is displayed.

  8. Utilized Service Amt - The amount incurred for the quantity of service that is utilized is displayed.

  9. Available Service Amt - The amount available for the utilization of additional quantity of the respective service within the package limit is displayed.

  10. Refund Amt - Enter the values in numeric format to indicate the amount that can be issued as a refund against the service. You cannot issue a refund if the 'Available Service Limit' is zero. The amount specified has to be equal to or lesser than the available service amount.

  11. Apply Refund - Select the check box to issue refund against the service.

  12. Excl. - Click Dtls hyperlink to view the services that are excluded from package.
