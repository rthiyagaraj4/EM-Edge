Enter the following details to create Drug Dosage Limit record:

 

Drug Details

  1. Drug Name – Enter the Drug Name in alphanumeric format of 60 characters maximum length or select the required Drug Name using [?] function. This is a mandatory field.

 

On entering the required Drug Name, the following details are displayed:

  • Generic Name - The Generic Name of the selected Drug is displayed.

  • Strength - The Strength of the selected Drug is displayed.

  • Form of Drug - The Form of Drug of the selected Drug is displayed.

  • Base Unit - The Base Unit of the selected Drug Code is displayed.

 

  1. Calculate Dosage By – Select the value from the list. The values listed are Absolute, BSA and Weight. The list value population depends on the following conditions.

Age Group

  1. Age Group – Select the required Age Group to be associated to the Drug from the list. The Age Groups defined in Application Masters module are displayed in the list. This is a mandatory field.

 

On selecting an Age Group, the following fields are populated.

  1. Gender – The Gender associated (if any) to the selected Age Group in Application Masters module is displayed.

  2. Age Unit - The Age Unit of the selected Age Group is displayed.

 

Associate Age Group

Age Range

  • Min – The minimum age specified for the selected Age Group is displayed.

  • Max - The maximum age specified for the selected Age Group is displayed.

 

Unit Dose

  • Minimum – Enter the minimum Unit Dose in numeric format of 13 digits maximum length. This is a mandatory field.

  • Ceiling Dose - Enter the minimum Ceiling Dose in numeric format of 13 digits maximum length. This field is applicable only when the ‘Calculate Dosage By’ is selected as 'Weight’ or ‘BSA’.

  • Maximum – Enter the maximum Unit Dose in numeric format of 13 digits maximum length. This is a mandatory field.

  • Ceiling Dose - Enter the maximum Ceiling Dose in numeric format of 13 digits maximum length. This field is applicable only when the ‘Calculate Dosage By’ is selected as 'Weight’ or ‘BSA’.

  • Standard - Enter the Standard Unit Dose in numeric format of 13 digits maximum length. The specified value is displayed during Medication Ordering. This is a mandatory field.

 

Daily Dose

  • Minimum - Enter the minimum Daily Dose in numeric format of 12 digits maximum length. This is a mandatory field.

  • Ceiling Dose - Enter the minimum Ceiling Dose in numeric format of 13 digits maximum length. This field is applicable only when the ‘Calculate Dosage By’ is selected as ‘Weight’ or ‘BSA’.

  • Maximum – Enter the maximum Daily Dose in numeric format of 12 digits maximum length. This is a mandatory field.

  • Ceiling Dose - Enter the maximum Ceiling Dose in numeric format of 13 digits maximum length. This field is applicable only when the ‘Calculate Dosage By’ is selected as ‘Weight’ or ‘BSA’.

Monograph

  • Monograph – Enter the Monograph of the Drug Dosage Limit in alphanumeric format.
