The following details are displayed in the left hand side of the screen:

  1. Total No. of Patients – The total number of patients listed are displayed.

  2. Patient ID - Number of unregistered orders and names of the patient.

  3. Move the cursor over the Patient ID, a tool tip gets displayed. The Age, Gender and Nationality of the patient are displayed in the tool tip.

 

Selection of patient can be performed either from the patient list or searching based on Patient ID.

The following details are displayed on selecting the Patient ID.

  • Order(s) of the selected patient

  • Prescription details of the first Order

 

Selection based on Patient ID search

To search for a particular patient, enter the Patient ID in alphanumeric format of length as defined in Master Patient Index module. On entering the required details, click [Locate]. The following details are displayed, if the Patient ID exists and orders needs to be registered.

  • Patient Details

  • Order Details and

  • Prescription Details of the first Order
