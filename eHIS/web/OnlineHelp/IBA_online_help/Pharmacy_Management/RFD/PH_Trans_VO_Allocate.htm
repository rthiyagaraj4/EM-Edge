Allocate

The Dispensing Stage is selected as Allocation. The dispensing medication screen gets displayed depending on the Category selected. Allocation is performed during the filling stage for Inpatient orders. Select the stage as ‘Allocation’ in the login screen, Outpatient as category and appropriate Dispense Location. The ‘Dispense Medication’ screen gets displayed. Enter the required query conditions applicable to Outpatient to get the result screen.

In the Outpatient and Inpatient Allocate and Fill stages:

  • If the orders are allocated in the Allocate stage, you can modify the allocated prescription in the Fill stage if the billing is not settled.

  • If the orders are filled in the Fill stage , you can modify the filled prescription in the Allocate stage if the billing is not settled.

Allocate an Order

Allocate for Outpatient

• Token Series applicable

  1. Select the Token Series, if the medication order has allotted a Token Number. The Token is generated while registering the Order.

  2. Click [Refresh] to display the prescribed Medication Orders based on the entered criteria.

• Token Series not applicable

  1. If Token Series is not applicable for the prescribed Medication Order, enter the required search criteria and click [Refresh] to display the orders.

 

  1. Click [Allocate] to allocate stock for dispensing the Drug.

  2. Enter the Allocated Quantity in numeric format of five digits maximum length. If the Allocate Quantity is ‘Null’, the ‘Include in BMS’ check box is displayed. This check box is cleared by default.

The Pharmacist can partially dispense orders (BMS) and complete the prescription without entering the BMS Qty. The Include BMS check box must be cleared.  

  1. Click [Stock Availability] to view the Batch Details.

  2. Click [Auto Allocate for Drug] to automatically allocate Drug Stock.

  3. Click [Allocate for Drug] on entering the required quantity or [Reset] to clear the entered data. If allocated quantity is more than the prescribed quantity,  

  4. Click [Preview] to view the entered Allocated Quantity.

  5. Click [Complete Allocation] on allocating the required batches and quantities.

  6. The Allocated details are displayed in the Prescription Details frame.

  7. Select the UOM from the 'Issue UOM' or 'Drug UOM' list. This legend is displayed based on the 'Dispense By' value selected in the 'Dispensing Rules' tab of the PH Parameter function.

  8. Click Edit Label hyperlink to enter the instructions.

    Enter the following details:

    1. Patient Details – Patient ID, Patient Name, Prescription Date and Prescribing Practitioner details get displayed.

    2. Drug/Dosage Details – Prescribed Drug, Prescribed Quantity (stock unit) and Expiry Date of the selected batch are displayed. The batch expired date gets displayed only for prescribed drug.

    3. Auxiliary Instructions (English) – Select the Cautionary instructions and special instructions from the list.

    4. Enter the patient instructions in alphanumeric format.

    5. The drug indications entered in the PH Drug master or the drug indications modified during prescription is displayed in the Drug Indications by default.

    6. Click [Copy] to copy the drug remarks to the Patient Instructions.

    1. Auxiliary Instructions (Local Language) – Enter the Cautionary instructions, special instructions, patient instructions and drug indications in the required format.

    2. Click [Copy] to copy the drug remarks to the Patient Instructions.

    On entering the required details, click [OK] to record the instructions, [Reset] to clear the entered value or [Cancel] to exit from the Edit Label screen. The ‘Edit Label’ hyperlink gets displayed with a unique color.

  9. Click Findings hyperlink to record the Deliver Stage finding details.

    Enter the Filling Stage Findings details:

    1. Findings – Select the Findings from the list. This is a mandatory field.

    2. Remarks – Enter the Remarks in alphanumeric format. This is a mandatory field.

    On entering the required details, click [OK] to record the findings. The ‘Findings’ hyperlink gets displayed with a unique color-code.

  10. Click Detail hyperlink to view the Allocate/Reallocate details.

  11. Click [Complete Allocate] to invoke the Report screen; displays the Print/Edit Label screen.

  12. The Dispensing Sheet, Dispense Label and Medication Plan Sheet can be generated. These reports get displayed based on the Parameter for Facility setup. Click [OK] for generation of reports.

  13. Select the ‘Delivery Bin’ from the list to allocate drugs to the delivery bin. All the newly created delivery bins associated to the Dispense Location are listed. This legend is available only if the Register before Patient Arrival check box is selected in the Dispense Location screen. Click [OK] to allocate drugs to the selected delivery bin.

The barcode is captured as a combination of Item code +Batch ID +Expiry Date”  during Allocation/Delivery stage and its batch is selected automatically and dispensed. The Hospital name is also printed along with this Barcode label and affixed in the drug box. The barcode scanning is done for the Inventory Stock transactions during dispensing such as Sales/Patient issues, Stock Item consumption and Urgent Issue.

 

  1. The Allocation process completes for the selected patient.

 

The Pharmacist can capture the batch details by scanning the barcode. On scanning the bar code of the batch, the bar code number is displayed in the text field. If the item barcode matches with the item for which the user is trying to allocate the drug, the system defaults the Allocated Quantity based on the Prescribed Quantity. The allocated quantity can be modified if required. The barcode scanning is applicable only when the ‘Bar Code Scanning’ check box is selected in the Dispense Location function.

If 'Allow More Than Prescribed Quantity' check box is selected in the Drug Setup> Dispensing Rules tab; and the allocated quantity is more than the prescribed quantity, an alert message 'Allocated Quantity is more than prescribed quantity. Do you want to proceed?' is displayed. Click [Yes] to proceed or [No] to reallocate the entered quantity.   

 

If 'Allow More Than Prescribed Quantity' check box is cleared in the Drug Setup> Dispensing Rules tab; and the allocated quantity is more than the prescribed quantity, an alert message 'Allocated Quantity cannot be more than BMS quantity' is displayed. Click [OK] to reallocate the quantity.

 

If another user is accessing the same order, an icon is displayed in the 'User Locked' column. On clicking the Patient ID, a alert message 'This Patient Record is opened by another user. Access Denied' is displayed. Click icon to unlock and proceed.
