Trade Name Tab

Trade names of the defined drug are recorded in the tab along with their manufacturers. The Trade Names and manufacturers are defined in ‘Application Masters’ module.

 

Enter the following details to add Trade Names to the defined Drug:

  1. Trade Name – Enter the Trade Name in alphanumeric format of 100 characters maximum length or Select the Trade Name using [?] function. The Trade Name associated to the selected Item is displayed for selection.

  2. Manufacturer Name – On selecting the Trade Name, the Manufacturer associated to the Trade Name is displayed. You cannot change the displayed value.

  3. Image Filename – Enter the Image Filename Path of the Trade Name.

 

Click [Add] to add Trade Names to the defined Drug or [Reset] to clear the entered data.

The Trade Name details are displayed in the top half of the screen. Select the check box against the desired Trade Name. The check box is selected, by default. To remove the details from the list, clear the check box.
