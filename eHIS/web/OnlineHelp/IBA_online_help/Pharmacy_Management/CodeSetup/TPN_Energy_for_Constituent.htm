TPN Energy for Constituent

From this function you can Query for the TPN Energy for Constituent and Modify the queried record.

TPN Energy for Constituent provides details on energy released by the generic constituents. These values are used during TPN Ordering. The displayed values are pre-defined. You can change or modify the constituent name using this function.

Querying the TPN Energy for Constituent

From the PH Code Setup > PH TPN Setup, select the TPN Energy for Constituent function.

The TPN Energy for Constituent screen gets displayed.

  1. Click [Query] to invoke the Create screen.

  2. Click Constituent Code hyperlink to update the queried records.

  3. You can modify the Constituent Name and the Energy Value.

  4. Click [Apply] to save the changes on TPN Energy for Constituent or [Reset] to clear the entered data.
