Discontinue Drug

From this function you can Query the Drugs and record reason for discontinuing usage of a particular Drug.

Discontinue Drug function disables the drug in the formulary and prevents the discontinued drug form being prescribed.

Querying the Drug for Discontinuing Usage

From the PH Code Setup > PH Drug Setup, select the Discontinue Drug function.

The Discontinue Drug screen gets displayed.

  1. Click [Query] to invoke the Query screen.

  2. The Drug Search screen gets displayed.

  3. On selecting the required Drug, the Drug details are displayed and disabled in the Drug Detail frame.

  4. Click ‘Patient Education’ hyperlink to view the Drug Information and Patient Education details.

  5. Click [Drug Detail] to view the Drug Details recorded while creating the Drug.

  6. Select the ‘Discontinue Drug’ check box to discontinue the selected Drug.

  7. Enter the Discontinue Details in the mandatory (*) fields.

    To discontinue the use of a Drug, enter the following details:

    1. Discontinue Drug – Select the check box to discontinue usage of the selected Drug.

    2. Reason To Discontinue – This field is enabled, if the ‘Discontinue Drug’ check box is selected. Enter the Reason in alphanumeric format of 40 characters maximum length. This is a mandatory field.

  8. Click [Apply] to save the Discontinue Drug details or [Reset] to clear the entered data.
