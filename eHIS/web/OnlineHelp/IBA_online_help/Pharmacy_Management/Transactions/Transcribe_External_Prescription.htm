Transcribe External Prescription

From this function you can Record prescription details of a patient directed to the logged in facility from other facility.  

The directed patient must be a registered patient of the logged in facility. Practitioner selects the registered patient of the logged in facility to records the prescription details.

The following rules are validated:

• Refill/taper of order is not allowed.

• All the Dispense Locations of type ‘Pharmacy’ and ‘Clinic’ are listed.

• The Order Routing rules are not applicable.

Transcribing External Prescription Details

From the PH Transactions, select the Transcribe External Prescription function.

The Transcribe External Prescription screen gets displayed.

  1. Enter the details in the mandatory (*) fields. <Related Field Description>

  2. Click [Clear] to clear the entered data or [Place Order] to place an order.
