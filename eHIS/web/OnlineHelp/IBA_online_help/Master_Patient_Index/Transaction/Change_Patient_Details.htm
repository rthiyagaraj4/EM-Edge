Change Patient Details

From this function you can Update/Amend certain details of a registered patient.

Demographics of patients are vital information maintained in the enterprise. Changes to these details are to be updated regularly by the facility. Information on the user performing changes to details of any patient is also maintained. Details not recorded during other modes of registration are captured in this function and the patient profile is updated.

 

Process Flow Diagram

Updating Patient Details

From the MP Transactions, select the Change Patient Details function.

The Patient Search screen gets displayed.

  1. Search and select the desired patient to change the details.

 

Modify the required patient details across the following tab pages.

Demographic Details

  1. By default, this tab is selected.

  2. Enter the details in the required fields. <Related Field Description>

 

Contact Addresses

  1. Click Contact Addresses tab to update the address of the patient.

  2. Enter details in the required fields. <Related Field Description>

Related Contacts

  1. Click Related Contacts tab to update the Next of Kin, First to Notify and Employment Details.

  2. By default, Next of Kin tab is displayed to update the details of the patient’s closest living relative.

  3. Enter the details in the required fields. <Related Field Description>

  4. Click, First to Notify tab to record the details of the first person to notify in case of emergency.

  5. Enter the details in the required fields. <Related Field Description>

  6. Click Employment Details tab to record employment details of the patient.

  7. Enter the details in the required fields. <Related Field Description>

Documents and Others

  1. Click Documents and Others tab to record or update the details of documents available with the patient during registration.

  2. Enter the details in the mandatory (*) fields. <Related Field Description>

Who Done It

  1. Click 'Who Done It' tab to view the details of the user performing patient encounter related changes. The changes that are performed through 'Change Patient Details', 'Register Visit' or 'Admit Patient' functions are displayed.

  2. Details are displayed and the information is read-only. <Related Field Description>

  3. On changing/modifying the patient encounter details, click [Apply] to save the changes. On recording the changes, internal reports print screen gets displayed to generate the required internal reports (as described under ‘Register Patient’). On successful generation of internal reports the control shifts back to the search screen.

Financial Details

  1. Click Financial Details tab to record or update the financial details of the patient.

  2. Click [Modify] to invoke the Authorization Screen.

    1. User ID - Enter the User ID in alphanumeric format of 20 characters maximum length.

    2. PIN - Enter the PIN of the User in alphanumeric format of 20 characters maximum length.

    3. Remarks - Enter the Remarks in the text box.

    4. Click [OK] to modify the financial details or [Cancel] to exit from the Authorization Screen.

  3. Enter the details in mandatory (*) fields. <Related Field Description>

You can set the Race, Country, Region, and Mobile number to be mandatory or non-mandatory as required, by selecting or clearing the respective check boxes in the Mandatory fields during Registration field of the MP Parameter screen.

 

  1. Click [Apply] to save the changes made or [Reset] to restore the original values.

  2. If internal reports are defined, the Internal Reports screen gets displayed, click [OK] to generate Internal Reports or [Cancel] to exit from the screen.

  3. Click [Query] to search for existing Patient Details.

 

When you try to update patient details, the ‘Enter Password’ dialog gets displayed. This is applicable when the ‘Accept Password in Change Patient Details’ check box is selected in MP Parameter screen.
