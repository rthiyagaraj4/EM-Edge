Mark Patient MLC/Non-MLC

From this function you can Record the patient as either Medico Legal Case (MLC) or Non-MLC.

This function allows to mark the patient as either Medico Legal Case (MLC) or Non-MLC. Based on the marking, the patient banner gets updated.

The reasons to be used while marking the patient as MLC or Non/MLC is defined in the Reason masters in Application Masters (AM) module.

Marking Patients as MLC or Non-MLC

From the MP Transactions, select the Mark Patient MLC/Non-MLC function.

The Mark Patient MLC/Non-MLC screen gets displayed with the Patient Search dialog invoked.

  1. Search for the required patient and click the required ‘Patient ID’ hyperlink from the search results.

  2. The Mark Patient MLC/Non-MLC screen gets refreshed with the patient banner at the top, followed by Action field, Reason field and Encounter Details.

  3. Action - Displays the status of the action user is going to perform. For example, if the current status of the patient is 'Non-MLC', then the Action will be displayed as 'MLC'. Similarly, if the current patient status is MLC, then the Action field will appear as 'Non-MLC'. This field is non-editable, and it will populate the value as per the patient's current MLC Status.

  4. Reason - Select the required reason from the drop-down list for marking the patient as MLC or Non-MLC. This field is mandatory. The reasons recorded in the Reason master in AM module will be displayed in the drop-down list.

  5. Below the Reason field, all the encounter details of the patient get listed. It is only for viewing and user cannot perform any action on the encounter details.

  6. After selecting the required Reason, click [Apply]. The respective MLC status with reason is saved against that patient and patient banner gets updated.

  7. The 'Operation Completed Successfully' message gets displayed. Click [OK] to close the message. The Search Patient dialog gets invoked to proceed with next patient.

  8. Click [Query] to invoke the Search Patient dialog.
