Capture Patient Photo

From this function you can Capture a patient’s photo or provide an option to search for patient photo in the local drive or network drive and upload the photo.

Photograph of the patient is captured during registration. If the photo capturing is not performed, then ‘Capture Patient Photo’ function is used to perform the same. On invoking the function, the search patient screen is displayed. You can enter the query criteria and search for the required patient. If there is no photo available, on clicking the Photo Icon, the system provides an option to capture the Photo or upload the photo. If the patient photo is available in the database, the system prompts that the photo already exists in the database.

Capturing Patient Photo

From the MP Transactions, select the Capture Patient Photo function.

The Search Patient screen gets displayed.

  1. Search and select the required Patient ID.

  2. On selecting the Patient ID, the patient details are displayed on all the tab pages.

  3. Click on each tab pages to view the patient details.

  4. Click 'Photo' tab to Capture the photo through the Web cam or Upload the photo from the local drive.

    On selecting the 'Photo' tab, the Capture Patient Photo screen is displayed.

    Select the option 'Web cam' to capture the patients photo using a web cam or 'Upload File' to upload the file from the local drive.

    Click [OK] to capture the patient photo using the Web cam or upload the patient photo from the local drive. Click [Cancel] to exit from the 'Capture Patient Photo' screen.

    The Capture Patient Photo screen is displayed to upload the patient photo.

    Click 'Browse' and select the patients photo from the local drive.

    Click [Upload File] to upload the patients photo.

    A message 'Failed to insert' is displayed if uploading the image is not successful.

    Click 'Change Settings' check box to enable [Size] and [Option].

    You can change the size of the picture on clicking [Size] and on selecting the required option.

    You can select the device driver for the camera on Clicking [Option].

    You are connected to the photograph capturing equipment on clicking the [Start].

    Click [Capture] to capture the patient’s photo and [Save] to save the captured photo.

    An alert message 'Check whether Web cam is installed properly or not' is displayed if the Web cam is not installed or if it is not installed properly.

 

The camera icon appears based on the User access rights set for the Responsibility in the function 'Access Rights for Photo Capture'.
