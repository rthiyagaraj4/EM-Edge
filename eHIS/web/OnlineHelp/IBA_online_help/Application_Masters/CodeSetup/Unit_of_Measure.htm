Unit of Measure

From this function you can Create various Units of Measurement and Query for a required Unit of Measure record.

The defined Unit of Measurement is made applicable to Stock and Pharmacy module depending on the check box selected in the function.

Creating Unit of Measurement

From the Materials Setup, select the Unit of Measure function.

The Unit of Measure screen gets displayed.

  1. Click [Create] to invoke the Create screen.

  2. Enter the details in the mandatory (*) fields. <Related Field Descriptions>

  3. Click [Apply] to save a Unit of Measurement defined or [Reset] to clear the entered data.

  4. Click [Query] to search for an existing Unit of Measurement record.

 

UOM defined with Dosage checked and Dosage with Strength/Quantity checked appears in Pharmacy Management module. On selection of Strength check box, quantity is disabled and on clearing the Strength check box, quantity is enabled.
