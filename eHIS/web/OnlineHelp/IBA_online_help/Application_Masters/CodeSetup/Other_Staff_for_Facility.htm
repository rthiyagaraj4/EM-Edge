Other Staff For Facility

From this function you can Create an association of Other Staffs to a facility for a specified date range and Query for a required Other Staff for Facility record.

This function helps in associating Other Staffs to the various facilities of the Enterprise.

Creating an association of Other Staffs to a Facility

From Other Staff Setup, select the Other Staff for Facility function.

The Other Staff for facility screen gets displayed.

  1. Click [Create] to invoke the Create screen.

  2. Select the required facility from the list. The logged in facility is displayed by default. The list box displays facility records as created in the ‘Facility’ function of the System Manager module and associated to the logged in user and responsibility.

  3. Click [Search] to display the Other Staff Names associated to the selected facility.

  4. Click the + hyperlink to view other staff details. Click [Close] to get back to the Other Staff for Facility screen.

  5. Enter the Effective ‘From’ date in dd/mm/yyyy format. ‘From date’ corresponds to the date from which the user is allowed to work in the facility or select the date from the calendar function.

  6. Enter the Effective ‘To’ date in dd/mm/yyyy format. ‘To date’ corresponds to the date until when the user is allowed to work in the Facility or select the date from the calendar function.

  7. Select the check box against the applicable Other Staff to indicate that the selected Other Staff is available to the selected facility for the specified date range. Clear the check box to delete the other staff association with the selected facility.

  8. Click [Apply] to save an associated Other Staff for Facility or [Reset] to clear the entered data.

  9. Click [Query] to search for an existing Other Staff for Facility record.
