Circumstance of Injury

From this function you can Create a Circumstance of Injury and associate it to a Reimbursement Type. You can Query for a required Circumstance of Injury record.

Circumstance of Injury function helps you to create circumstance of injury codes to indicate the circumstances of injury for a visit. It is associated with an Injury Reimbursement Type indicator, Motor Vehicle Accident Fund (MV), Workmen’s Compensation Fund (WC) and Not Applicable (Null).

Creating Circumstance of Injury

From the AM Other Setup > Other Master Setup, select the Circumstance of Injury function.

The Circumstance of Injury screen gets displayed.

  1. Click [Create] to invoke the Create screen.

  2. Enter the details in the mandatory (*) fields. <Related Field Descriptions>

  3. Click [Apply] to save a Circumstance of Injury created or [Reset] to clear the entered data.

  4. Click [Query] to search for an existing Circumstance of Injury record.
