Delivery Instructions

From this function you can Create a Delivery Instruction at the Kitchen level of a facility and Query for the existing Delivery Instructions.

The Delivery Instructions function helps in maintaining various sets of Delivery Instructions which are used during the Inpatient Meal Ordering. These are special instructions given to each kitchen for dishing up and delivering food to the inpatients based on the orders placed. This function is used to indicate the delivery instructions while placing an order. The delivery instructions are given either by the dietitian to the kitchen staff or by the head of the kitchen to the staff.

Creating Delivery Instructions

From the Setup, select the Delivery Instructions function.

The Delivery Instructions screen gets displayed.

  1. Click [Create] to invoke the Create screen.

  2. Enter the details in the mandatory (*) fields. <Related Field Description>

  3. Click [Apply] to save a created Delivery Instruction or [Reset] to clear the entered details.

  4. Click [Query] to search for the existing records.
