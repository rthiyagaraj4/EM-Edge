Spl. Food Items

The Special Food Items function is used to associate the Item Type and Item for a particular special food meal type. The Special Food Item is defined as ‘Frequency Based’ or ‘Non-Frequency Based’. For Frequency Based Special Food Items, diet profile of a patient is not required. You can serve liquid diets for patients. If the Special Food Item is a Non-Frequency Based, (Meal Type based) diet profile of a patient is mandatory.

Click to expand the Special Food Items option and click to add or edit the food items for the Special Food Items.

 

  1. Frequency Based – Select the check box to indicate that the Special Food Item has to be served on frequency basis. Clear the check box to indicate that the Special Food Items are not to be served on Frequency basis. When a frequency based food item is entered the ‘Frequency’ and ‘Period Between’ are mandatory. Select the meal type when the food item is non-frequency based.

  2. From Date - Click the Calendar function to select the date from when the Special Food Item is to be served along with the diet order placed. The special food items are served from the selected date until the number of days based on the Diet Cycle. This is a mandatory field.

  3. Meal Types – Click on Meal Types. Select the required check box against each Meal Type to associate it to the Special Food Item. Click [Close] to exit from the Meal Types screen. The control goes back to the Special Food Items screen. This field is mandatory.

This field is disabled if ‘Frequency Based’ check box is selected.

  1. Item Type – Select the Item Type from the list. The values defined using the ‘Item Type’ function get listed. This is a mandatory field.

  2. Item – Select the Item by clicking on [?] function. The items associated to the Item Type using the ‘Food Item’ function get listed.  This is a mandatory field.

  3. Qty – Enter the quantity in numeric format. This is a mandatory field.

  4. Frequency – This field gets enabled on selecting ‘Frequency based’ check box. Enter the frequency in numeric format of two digits maximum length. If the Special Food Item is frequency based, this is a mandatory field. It indicates the number of times the food item is served to the patient.

  5. Period Between - This field gets enabled on selecting ‘Frequency based’ check box. Enter the Period Between in hh:mm format. If the Special Food Item is frequency based, this is a mandatory field.

 

Click [Select] to display the selected food item in the table. The item name, serving date and the food item quantity gets displayed along with the preparation UOM selected for the respective Food item and the Energy UOM selected in ‘DS Parameter’ function. Select the Item Type hyperlink to display the details of the selected food item in the respective fields. Click [Cancel] to remove the selected food item from the list. Click [Close] to exit from the Special Food Items screen. The total calorie content of Special Food Items is calculated and displayed.

Click [Record] to record the Special Food Items or [Close] to exit from the Special Food Items screen. Click [Edit] to edit the entered record. Click [Apply] to save the record.

If the patient dislikes a food item, it is displayed in red color. Click  to collapse the Special Food Items option.
