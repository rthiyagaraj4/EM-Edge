Lodge Complaint

From this function you can Record Complaint against defective linen items received from the laundry after cleaning or from a Nursing Unit/Clinic on receiving the loaned linen items.

The Lodge Complaint function is used to record complaints against the defective linen items received after cleaning from the laundry. You can also lodge complaint against a Nursing Unit or Clinic, if defect is found in a linen item that is loaned. The reasons to lodge a complaint can be late deliveries, infected linen, quality failures and shortage of linen supply. Lodge Complaint helps function in recording the quality check of the linen. You have to select a Source Type and Source for where the Complaint has to be lodged. The values selected are defaulted and displayed in the transaction functions.

Lodging Complaints

From the Linen > Linen Transaction, select the Lodge Complaint function.

The Lodge Complaint screen gets displayed.

  1. Enter the details in the mandatory (*) fields. <Related Field Description>

  2. Click [Select] to display a lodged complaint or [Cancel] to clear the entered or displayed details.

  3. Click ‘Reference Number’ displayed as a hyperlink to ‘Receive/Write-off’ or ‘View’ the request placed for issuing the loaned linen.

  4. The Reference details, such as, Reference Number, Date/Time, Linen Item and Quantity are displayed in a tabular column.

  5. Click [Submit] to save the lodged Complaint for a linen item in the Source Type.

  6. The Stock Status and Work Status of the linen items in the laundry are displayed. You can choose to view the status of the linen items in laundry by selecting a source Type and a Source. The current system is displayed. Click [Go] to search for the details in the respective Source and Source Type.
