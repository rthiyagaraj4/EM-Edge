Review Note

From this function you can Review clinical documents that are forwarded for review by other practitioners within the facility.

Clinical notes can be sent for review using the Forward Notes Option. This function allows practitioners to view the forwarded notes and study the content and revert with comments.

Reviewing Notes

From the CA Transactions > Clinician Access > Desktop Menu > Pending Activities > Reviews, select Review Notes function.

The Review Notes screen gets displayed.

  1. Notes pending for Review are displayed for the default date range or enter the query criteria to search for specific notes.

    1. Reviewed - This field consists of check box for confirming the review. You can either confirm the review of the entire list of notes by enabling the check box next to the Reviewed heading or enable the individual notes by selecting the respective check boxes against each note.

    2. Subject - The subject of the Note Type is displayed. Subject is entered while forwarding the note for reviews.

    3. Requested At - The date and time of requesting for review of notes is displayed.

    4. Requested By - The name of the practitioner who has forwarded the note for review is displayed.

    5. Requested To - The name of the practitioner to which the note was forwarded for review is displayed.

    6. Patient details - The patient details are displayed.

  2. Click View hyperlink to view the contents of the notes.

  3. Select the check box against the required note. A message is displayed ‘Revert back with Comments’. Click [OK] to record remarks. Enter the Remarks and click [Send] to revert back with comments. Click [Cancel] to exit from the screen.

  4. Click [Reviewed] to confirm reviewing the notes.
