Intervention for Transcription

Transcription Notes requested to the transcriber are displayed while searching.

  1. Select the search criteria from the list and click [Go] to display the request details.

  2. The details displayed are Time, Request Type, Request, Patient Instructions, Requested By, Priority, Stage, Position and Status. The Request is hyperlink if the status of transcription is Pending Transcription. If any patient has Patient Instructions pending, an icon is displayed.

  3. Select the Request hyperlink to enter the transcription notes where the transcriber can enter the notes.  

  4. Click the icon to open the patient instructions dialog to display the patient instructions and to print the patient instruction material.

  5. After entering the notes, the status is changed from 'Pending Transcription' to 'Performed'.
