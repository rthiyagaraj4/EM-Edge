View Patient Details

From this function you can Query and view the Demographic Details of registered patients.

The Demographic details are displayed as read-only text.

Querying for Patient Details

From MP Queries, select the View Patient Details function.

The Patient Search screen gets displayed.

  1. Search and select the desired patient to view the Patient details.

  2. The patient details are displayed under six tabs similar to the register patient screen layout. They are 'Demographic Details', 'Contact Addresses', 'Related Contacts', 'Documents & Others', 'Who Done It' and 'Financial Details'. Click the respective tabs to view the details recorded for the registered patient.

  3. Click [Close] to close the screen after viewing the details under ‘Financial Details’ tab.

 

The image uploaded in the Register Visit and Change Patient Details screens is viewed on click of the View Image hyperlink in the financial tab
