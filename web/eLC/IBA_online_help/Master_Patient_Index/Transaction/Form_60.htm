Form 60

From this function you can Record Form 60 details and Print the Form 60 in PDF.

This function is used to record Form No 60 of patient or patient’s father / mother / guardian.

As per the Indian Government, it is mandatory for hospitals to collect either PAN card or Form 60 of patient or patient’s father / mother / guardian if the bill amount exceeds 2 lakhs. If patient has no PAN card details in master record, then this function allows to record the Form No 60 details for different Encounter type (OP, AE, IP and Daycare) and print the recorded Form No. 60 in PDF.  

 

You can print Form 60 even if the patient is discharged or checked out; print Form 60 for Deceased, Inactive and Suspended patients.

Recording Form 60 Details

From the MP Transactions, select the Form 60 function.

The Form 60 screen gets displayed.

  1. Enter the Patient ID or searching using the lookup. This field is mandatory

  2. Select the Encounter Type from the drop-down list. The list values include Daycare, Emergency, Inpatient and Outpatient. This field is optional.

  3. Enter the Encounter ID in the respective field. This field is optional.

  4. Click [Search]. The patient records matching the search criteria gets displayed.

  5. Click + hyperlink to view the patient encounter details in the Encounter Details dialog.

  6. To record the Form 60 details for the patient, click [Create Form 60] hyperlink. The Create Form 60 dialog gets displayed.

  7. Enter the Form 60 details in the mandatory (*) fields <Related Field Description>

  8. Click [Save] to save the Form 60 details. Once the Form 60 is saved, the status becomes 'Draft'. In addition, Print and Cancel hyperlink appears under Action column.

• Draft hyperlink - To make changes to the Form 60 click Draft hyperlink. The Form 60 dialog gets invoked where you can make the required modifications.

• Print hyperlink -  Click the Print hyperlink to print the Form 60 before finalizing.

• Cancel hyperlink - To cancel the recorded the Form 60, click Cancel hyperlink. The message ‘Are you sure to cancel the Form 60? appears. Click [OK] and the Form 60 gets cancelled successfully. the Status is changed to 'Create Form 60' from any of the status Draft or Finalized.

  1. You can directly Finalize the Form 60 without saving by clicking the [Finalize] button. The status becomes 'Finalized'

 You cannot edit the Form 60 once it is finalized.

  1. To print  the finalized Form 60, click Print hyperlink.

  2. Click Finalized hyperlink to open the Report dialog where you generate the Form 60 file as PDF or spreadsheet.

  3. To cancel the Finalized Form 60, click Cancel hyperlink. The recorded Form 60 gets cancelled and the Status is changed to 'Create Form 60'.

 If the bill is generated already for the patient, you cannot cancel the Form 60 irrespective of whether the status is "Draft" or "Finalized". The message "The bill is already generated with Form 60, need to cancel the bill to proceed Further" gets displayed.
