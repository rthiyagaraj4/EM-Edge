Enter the following details to record Form 60:

 

Declarant Details:

  1. First Name, Middle Name and Surname - The first name, middle name and sur name of the patient gets displayed by default. You can enter maximum of 30 characters in alphanumeric format. These fields are mandatory.

  2. Date of Birth / Incorporation of Declarant - The date of birth of declarant gets displayed by default. You change the date using Calendar function. This field is mandatory.

  3. Father's First Name, Father Middle Name and Father Surname - Enter the father name details in the respective text boxes. You can enter maximum of 30 characters in alphanumeric format. These fields are mandatory.

Declarant Address Details:

  1. Flat/ Room No - The flat/room number of the patient gets displayed based on MP Patient Address. You can enter maximum of 30 characters in alphanumeric format. This field is optional.

  2. Floor No - The floor number of the patient gets displayed based on MP Patient Address. You can enter maximum of 30 characters in alphanumeric format. This field is optional.

  3. Name of premises - The premises name is displayed based on MP Patient Address. You can enter maximum of 30 characters in alphanumeric format. This field is optional.

  4. Block Name / No - The block name or number gets displayed based on MP Patient Address. You can enter maximum of 30 characters in alphanumeric format. This field is optional.

  5. Road/ Street/Lane - Enter the road or street name in the text field. You can enter maximum of 30 characters in alphanumeric format. This field is optional.

  6. Area / Locality - Enter the area/locality name in the text field. You can enter maximum of 30 characters in alphanumeric format.

  7. Town / City - The town/city name gets displayed based on MP Patient Residence Address. You can enter maximum of 60 characters in alphanumeric format. This field is mandatory.

  8. District - The district name gets displayed by default based on Patient Address. You can enter maximum of 60 characters in alphanumeric format. This field is mandatory.

  9. State - The state name gets displayed by default from Patient Address details. You can enter maximum of 60 characters in alphanumeric format. This field is mandatory.

  10. Country - The country name of the patient gets displayed by default based on MP Patient Address. You can enter maximum of 60 characters in alphanumeric format. This field is mandatory.

  11. Pin Code - The postal code of the patient gets displayed by default. You can enter maximum of 15 characters. This field is mandatory.

  12. Telephone Number - The telephone number with STD code gets displayed based on Contact no of patient. You can enter maximum of 20 Characters. This field is mandatory.

  13. Mobile Number - The mobile number of the patient gets displayed by default. You can enter maximum of 20 Characters. This field is mandatory.

 

Declarant Transaction Details:

  1. Amount of transaction (Rs.) – This field appears blank and enabled even if there are bills during Create mode. While in Draft mode, displays the value as read-only and disabled if the bill is generated.

  2. Date of transaction – This field appears blank and disabled even if there are bills during Create mode. While in Draft mode, displays only Date and not time. If there are multiple valid bills, the Minimum Date of the bill is displayed.

  3. In case of transaction in joint names, number of persons involved in the transaction - This field is blank and enabled in the application. You can enter only two digits and the values gets displayed in the report output.

  4. Mode of transaction – The corresponding Mode of transaction check box i.e., Cash, Cheque, Card, Draft/Banker's Cheque, Online transfer gets selected based on the receipts settlement type. This field is disabled in the application.

  5. Aadhar Number issued by UIDAI (if available) - Enter the Aadhar number in the text field. You can enter maximum of 12 digits in numeric format.

  6. Applied for PAN - Select the required Yes or No option.

  7. If Yes option is selected, the fields PAN Acknowledgement, PAN Date of application become mandatory.

  8. If 'Applied for PAN' is selected as No, the remaining fields become mandatory.

  1. PAN Acknowledgement - Enter the acknowledgement of the PAN number. You can enter maximum of 16 digits in alpha numeric format. This field is mandatory only if Yes option is selected for 'Applied for PAN' field.

  2. PAN Date of application - Enter the date of PAN applied by clicking calendar function in DD/MM/YYYY format.

  3. Estimated agricultural income - Enter the estimated agricultural income detail in numeric format upto 15 characters. This field is mandatory.

  4. Estimated non-agricultural income - Enter the estimated non-agricultural income detail in numeric format up to 15 characters. This field is mandatory.

  5. Details of document being produced in support of identify proof - Select the document being produced in support of identification proof from the drop-down list.

  6. Document identification number - Enter the document identification number in the text field. You can enter maximum of 30 characters in numeric format.

  7. Name and address of the authority issuing the document - Enter the name and address of the authority issuing the document in text field. You can enter maximum of 100 characters in alphanumeric format.

  8. Details of document being produced in support of address - Select the required document being produced in support of address from the drop-down list.

  9. Document identification number - Enter the document identification number in the text field. You can enter maximum of 30 characters in numeric format.

  10. Name and address of the authority issuing the document - Enter the name and address of the authority issuing the document in text field. You can enter maximum of 100 characters in alphanumeric format.
