Merged Patient IDs

From this function you can Query and view the details of the merged Patient IDs.

The records that are Merged/Replaced with the main Patient Numbers are displayed using this query function. All Records successfully merged (till date) using the merge process are displayed including the current merge process.

Querying for Merged Patient IDs

From DR Queries, select the ‘Merged Patient IDs’ function.

The ‘Merged Patient ID’ screen gets displayed.

  1. The Duplicate Groups details get displayed. <Related Field Description>

  2. Click the 'Main Patient ID' hyperlink from the 'Duplicates Groups' column. The details of the Main and Duplicate patients of the selected group are displayed in the Group Details area.

  3. Click the '+' hyperlink to view the Patient details. The Patient details are displayed under the six tabs, namely, Demographic Details, Contact Addresses, Related Contacts, Documents & Others, Who Done It and Financial Details.

You can specify the Criteria to search for patients under the duplicates groups as an alternate method of searching. If the number of groups are more, you can narrow down the patient search by specifying the patient ID as search criteria.

  1. Enter the valid Patient ID in alphanumeric format of length as specified in Master Patient Index module, or click [?] to select the required Patient ID.

  2. Enter the Merged Date period to list the merged groups during that period.

  3. On entering a valid Patient ID, click [Search]. For the selected patient, if records exist in the search result, the record details are displayed as shown, or the system displays an appropriate error message. <Related Field Description>

  4. Click [Reset] to clear the data entered.
