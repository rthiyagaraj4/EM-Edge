Drugs

From this function you can Query on specific Drugs.

The required drug can be queried either by specifying the drug characteristics or by ATC Classification. The Drugs defined in the Drug Masters function are displayed on searching.

Querying the Drugs

From the PH Queries, select the Drugs function.

The Drug search screen gets displayed.

  1. The Drug search screen displays two tabs, namely, ‘By Drug Characteristics’ and ‘By ATC Classification’. The ‘By Drug Characteristics’ tab is enabled, by default.

Drug By Characteristics Tab

By ATC Classification Tab

  1. Click ‘+’ hyperlink to view the Drug Details.

  2. Click [Reset] to clear the specified criteria.
