Patient Details Tab

The Patient details tab consists of four sections, namely, ‘Patient Details’ and ‘Drug allergy’, ‘Food Allergy’ and ‘Others’.

 

  1. Patient Details - Select the Patient ID from the Patient ID [?] function. On selecting the ID, all the other fields like the ‘Patient Name’, ‘Age’ and ‘Gender’ is furnished with the respective information as recorded in the Master Patient Index and disables all the field.

  2. Patient Name – This field is displayed on selecting the Patient ID or you can enter any name in alphanumeric format of maximum 60 characters.

  3. Age - You can enter the age of the patient, if the Patient ID is not selected from the [?] function. Age is entered in the format of years, month or days. The field cannot be inserted with zero.

  4. Ethnic Group – Select from the list the ethnic group.

  5. Drug Allergy – The enquirer has to enter the Drug Allergy details in alphanumeric format of 1000 characters maximum length.

  6. Food Allergy - The enquirer has to enter the Food Allergy details in alphanumeric format of 1000 characters maximum length.

  7. Others - The enquirer has to enter any other details in alphanumeric format of 1000 characters maximum length.
