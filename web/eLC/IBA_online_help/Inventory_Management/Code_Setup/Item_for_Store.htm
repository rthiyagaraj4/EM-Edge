Item for Store

From this function you can Associate an Item to a Store and Query for the desired association of Item to Store.

The Items are associated as Stock Item or Non-stock Item for a selected Store. The stock levels are specified for internal stock replacements. The internal stock replacements are done using Issue Transactions Process. You can associate individual Items to a Store using the ‘Individual Item’ tab or associate a range of Items to a group of stores using the ‘Range of Items’ tab.

Creating an association of Item to a Store

From the Code Setup, select the Item for Store function.

The Item for Store screen gets displayed.

  1. Click [Create] to invoke the Create screen.

  2. Select ‘Individual Item’ tab to associate Individual Item to a particular Store or select ‘Range of Items’ tab to associate a range of Items to a group of stores.

  3. Enter the details to associate Individual Item to a Store or Range of Items to a group of Stores in the mandatory (*) fields.

  4. On entering the details in the ‘ Individual Item’ tab or ‘ Range of Items’ tab, click [Add] to add the Items to the Store. On adding, the Items are displayed on the top half of the screen. Click [Cancel] to clear the entered data.

  5. Click ‘Store’ hyperlink to modify the added Items. Click [Modify] to modify the record or [Cancel] to reset the changes. Select the check box against the Store to select the Item to associate with the Store.

  6. Click [Apply] to save the association of Item to Store or [Reset] to clear the entered data.

  7. Click [Query] to search for associated Item to Store records.
