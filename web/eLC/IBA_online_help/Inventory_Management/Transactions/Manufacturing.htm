Manufacturing

From this function you can Record Manufacturing details of an Item.

Based on the request, the manufacturing receipt function allows a user to finalize the quantity required for manufacture and allocate the relevant batches so that the required Ingredients can be withdrawn for manufacture or assembly at the Prepare Location. On preparation, the quantity of Ingredients is reduced in the Store and manufactured Item quantity is increased. In case of extemporaneous Item, the quantity of Ingredients is reduced. The quantities of Ingredients required to make the requested quantity of a manufactured Item is derived from the ‘Assembly Details’ function.

Recording Manufacturing Details

From the ST Transactions > Receipt, select the Manufacturing function.

The Manufacturing screen gets displayed.

  1. Click [Create] to invoke the Create screen.

  2. Modify Document Details screen is displayed by default or click ‘Document’ hyperlink to modify the document details. Refer <Related Field Descriptions>

  3. On entering the document Details, click [OK] to record the manufacturing details or [Reset] to clear the entered data.

  4. By default, the ‘Direct Manufacturing’ option or ‘By Request’ option is selected based on the setup done in Facility Parameter. You change the option as required.

    • Direct Manufacturing

    • By Request

  1. If you select Direct Manufacturing, enter the mandatory details and click Get Data.

  2. If you select By Request, enter the mandatory details and click Search.

  3. Click in Workstation column to print the manufacturing worksheet.

  4. Click the MFG Item hyperlink  to view the Manufacturing details in the respective search fields. Click Get Data to view the item details.

  5. If you want to delete the item, click Delete check box. Else click the Item details hyperlink. Displays the item details in the right hand side. You cannot modify the item as it will be disabled.

  6. Click Edit/View and modify the details as required.

  7. Click Batch Search; displays the Batch Search dialog. You can enter the batch details and then search and select the batch.

  8. Click Modify to modify the details.

  9. Click Cancel to cancel the changes.

  10. Click Apply; displays Confirm Finalize popup. Click Ok.

You can change data in the Req Qty field. The Composition for Qty field is disabled and the Process Loss% is hidden. This is applicable only for eye hospital.
