Enterprise Service Group/Line

From this function you can Create the Enterprise Service Groups or Lines to categorize the services during bill generation. You can Query to view the existing Enterprise Service Groups and the Service Lines defined.

Enterprise Service Group/Line function is used to define the title to be printed on a patient bill. In the bill generated, the amount is not specified against the service group, but the service group description is displayed. The Enterprise Service Group/Line identifies whether the revenue generated has to be considered towards the income reporting of the Facility.

Defining Service Group/Line

From the Masters> Billing Services, double-click on Enterprise Service Group/Line function.

The Enterprise Service Group/Line screen gets displayed.

  1. Enter details in the mandatory (*) fields found in the Tab pages. <Related Field Description>

  2. Click [Commit Form] or press [F10] to save or [Clear Record] to clear the entered data.

  3. Click [Execute Query] or press [F8] to search for the existing Service Groups/Lines within the facility.
