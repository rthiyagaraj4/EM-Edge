Billing Services

From this function you can Define different services rendered by the facility and set the price for the services defined. You can Query to view the existing Billing Services.

Billing Service identifies the level of treatment rendered by the Facility. The prices and the discounts for the individual Billing Services are defined. It is mandatory to define the service and set them as either rate based or charge based. You can define multiple prices for a Rate based service, but set a default and a minimum price for a Charge based service. The services are later associated to the patient category and the bill is generated based on the prices defined for the services. An additional setup called Intercompany Setup is available using which Interfacility Charging is defined for a selected period.

Defining Billing Services

From the Masters > Billing Services, double-click on Billing Services function.

The Billing Services screen gets displayed.

  1. Enter the details in the mandatory (*) fields. <Related Field Description>

  2. The General Tab screen is displayed, by default. Enter the details in the mandatory (*) fields in the following tab pages:

General Tab

Acct Dept/Location Tab

Intercompany Setup

Standard Prices Tab

Price Class Specific Price Tab

Billing Group Specific Price Tab

Customer Specific Price Tab

  1. Click [Commit Form] or press [F10] to save or [Clear Record] to clear the entered data.

  2. Click [Execute Query] or press [F8] to search for the existing records within the facility.

 

The Billing Service Code has to be a combination of the Service Group and the Service Classification Code. The Billing Service is categorized under the selected Service Group and Service Classification.
