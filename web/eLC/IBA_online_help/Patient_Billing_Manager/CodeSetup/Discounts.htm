Discounts

From this function you can Define or Update the discounts for a Price Class/Billing Group/Customer Group. You can Query to view the discounts defined for a Price Class/ Billing Group/Customer Group.

Discount refers to the reduction in price towards the service availed from the Facility. Discounts are defined separately for Inpatients, Outpatients and Referral Patients. Discount is further categorized into:

  1. Customer Specific Discounts

  2. Billing Group Specific Discounts

  3. Price Class Specific Discounts

Customer Specific Discounts is mainly dependant on the Customer group. Each Customer group can have a separate discount price. Billing Group Specific Discounts is specifically defined for each Billing Group. The discount rate varies depending on the Billing Group the patient belongs. The reduction in the price is also specified depending on the Price Class. The discounts vary for each Price Class defined.

Defining Discounts

From the Masters > select MultiPricing, double-click on Discounts function.

The Discounts screen gets displayed.

  1. Enter the details in the mandatory (*) fields found in the following tab pages:

Customer Specific Discounts

The Customer Specific Discounts tab enables you to define, maintain and view the discount percentage for the Billing Services applicable to a patient category and associate it to a Customer. Each patient category can have a separate discount rate depending on the customer selected.

You can create, update and view the prices defined using the tab pages:

Maintain Discounts

Create Discounts

View Past Discounts

Billing Group Specific Discounts

A Billing Group can have separate discount rates for the patient categories. You can create, maintain and view the past discount rates using the Billing Group Specific Discounts tab.

You can create, update and view the prices defined using the tab pages:

Maintain Discounts

Create Discounts

View Past Discounts

Price Class Specific Discounts

The Price Class Specific Discounts tab enables you to define the discounts for a specific Price Class.

You can create, update and view the prices defined using the tab pages:

Maintain Discounts

Create Discounts

View Past Discounts

  1. Click [Commit Form] or press [F10] to save or [Clear Record] to clear the entered data.

  2. Click [Execute Query] or press [F8] to search for the existing records within the facility.

 

You can also use the Price Class Specific Prices function to define the discounts.
