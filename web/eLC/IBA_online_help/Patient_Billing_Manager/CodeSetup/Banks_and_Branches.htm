Banks and Branches

From this function you can Create Banks to deposit the cash collected from the patients or the customers against a bill or a receipt. You can also Create and Associate Branches for the Banks, so the cash transactions can take place in the associated branch as well. You can Query to view the existing Bank and Branch details. You can also Delete an existing record.

The amount received from the transactions at the facility is deposited in a Bank. The cash amount received from the Credit Card Company on processing the card received from the patients or the customers are also deposited in the bank associated to the facility. A credit card batch is created and the details of the payment received against the credit card batch is transferred to the Credit Card Company, which in turn checks for the card validation and transfers the amount to the Bank. You can define and associate any number of branches to a Bank, so the transactions can take place at the associated branch as well for the selected facility. Additionally, you can query to view the existing Banks and Branches. You can also interface the Credit Card Machine to the selected Bank. The ID of the Credit Card Machine supplied by the Bank to the facility has to be recorded. The ID of the Credit Card Machine associated to the facility gets listed in the Cash Counters enabling you to associate a default Credit Card Machine to the Workstation.

Creating Banks and Branches

From the Masters > Banks/Companies, double-click on Banks and Branches function.

The Banks and Branches screen gets displayed.

  1. Enter the details in the mandatory (*) fields. <Related Field Description>

  2. Click [Commit Form] or press [F10] to save or [Clear Record] to clear the entered data.

  3. Click [Execute Query] or press [F8] to search for Banks and Branches within the facility.
