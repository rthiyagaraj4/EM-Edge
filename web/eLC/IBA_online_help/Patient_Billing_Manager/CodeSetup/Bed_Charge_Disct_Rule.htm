Bed Charge Discount Rule

From this function you can Define the discount rule for inpatient bed charges on a discharge day, for reserved beds in the facility. You can also Define the discounts on bed charges for a leave period in the facility. You can Query to view the existing records in the Facility.

The Bed Charge Discount Rule function is used to define the discount rule for the bed charges in a Nursing Unit within the facility. You can define the discount rule for the bed charges computed on the discharge day and for the beds which are reserved for patient occupancy. The discount on the bed charges when the patient goes on leave is also defined which includes the leave or the stay rule for a normal and the discharge day. You can also record the charging rule for a facility, if the patient is admitted and discharged on the same day.

Defining Bed Charge Discount Rule

From the Masters > Interfaced Modules Financial Dtls, double-click Bed Charge Discount Rule function.

The Bed Charge Discount Rule screen gets displayed.

  1. Enter the details in the mandatory fields found in the following tab pages.

Discharge Day Bed Charge Discount Rule

Reserve Bed Charge Discount Rule

On Leave Bed Charge Discount Rule

Admission Day Bed Charge Discount Rule

  1. Click [Commit Form] or press [F10] to save or [Clear Record] to clear the entered data.

  2. Click [Execute Query] or press [F8] to search for the existing records within the facility.
