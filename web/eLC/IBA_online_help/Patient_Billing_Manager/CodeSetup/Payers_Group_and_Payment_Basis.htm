Payers Group and Payment Basis

From this function you can Associate multiple Payers to a Payer Group and Define the payment basis for each patient class. You can Query to view the records defined.

The Payers Group and Payment Basis function is used to associate Payers to a Payer Group. The policy definition is based on the Payer and the Payer Group combination. A Payer and the associated Payer Groups are listed in the transaction functions in the Insurance Management module. You can also define the payment basis and the relative weight based on which the price has to be calculated.

Defining Payers Group and the Payment Basis

From the Masters > Billing Services, double-click Payers Group and Payment Basis function.

The Payers Group and Payment Basis screen gets displayed.

  1. Enter the details in the following tabs:

Payer by Payer Group  

Payer by Payer Group

You can associate multiple Payers to a Payer Group on clicking ‘Payer by Payer Group’ tab. The Payers associated to the Payer Group get listed in the ‘setup Service Coverage Detail by Policy’ function in the Insurance Management module.  

Enter the following details to associate Payer to a Payer Group:

  1. Payer Group – The first record defined for the Payer Group gets displayed, by default. Press [F7] to select a Payer Group from the [List of Values] or press [Clear Record] or use the icons on the screen to navigate to the next or the previous record.

Press [F8] or click [Execute Query] to view or to associate a Payer to the Payer Group.

  1. Payer Code – The Payers associated to the respective Payer Group gets displayed. Click [List of Values] to associate a new Payer Code to the Payer Group. Click [Create Record] to associate a new Payer Code to the Payer Group.

  2. Payer Name – The name of the respective Payer gets displayed.

  3. [Payment Basis] – Click [Payment Basis] to define the payment basis.

Payment Basis by Payer

Payment Basis by Payer

You can define the payment indicator for a selected patient class using the ‘Payment Basis by Payer’ tab. The details get displayed in the Insurance Management module.

Enter the following details to define the amount per relative weight:

  1. Facility - The logged in facility name is displayed, by default. The facilities associated to the logged in user are listed. You can define the payment basis for a payer or referral hospital associated to the logged in facility.

  2. Payer Group – The Payer Groups defined get listed, by default. Click [Execute Query] or press [F8] to navigate to the next record. You can also click the upward arrow to navigate to the previous record or the downward arrow to navigate to the next record.

  3. Payers – The Payers associated to the 'Payer Group' in the 'Payer by Payer Group' tab gets displayed. The description of the selected Payer Group gets displayed.

  4. Referral Hospital - Click [List of Values] to select and associate a Referral Hospital in order to define the payment basis. Click [Create Record] to associate additional Referral Hospitals to the Facility. The values defined using the 'External/Referral Source' in Application Masters module get listed.

  5. Patient Class – Click [List of Values] to select a patient class. The description for the selected patient class gets displayed.

  6. Payment Basis Indicator – Select the indicator from the list. The value in the list as ‘DRG-based Relative Weight/Fee for Service’.

  7. Amt Per RW – Enter the amount per relative weight in numeric format of 12 digits maximum length. The price is calculated  as the product of the amount per relative weight and the quantity of DRG used.

  8. Expected less Payment %age - Enter the percentage of amount that is expected less while settling the amount. This is the percentage of amount that the payer does not pay out of the total amount. Example, if the expected less percentage is 10% and the amount per relative weight is 250, then the expected less payment is calculated as [[(250*10)/100]-25] where 25 is the expected less payment. Payer pays 225 instead of 250 and the remaining amount is considered to be expected less payment.

  9. Specific Payer - Select the check box, if the payment basis is valid for a specific Payer Group associated to the Patient Class. Clear the check box, if the payment basis is valid for a any Payer Group associated to the Patient Class selected. If the payment basis is valid for a specific Payer Group, the adjacent is enabled for selecting the Payer Group. Click [List of Values] to select a Payer Group.

  1. Click [Commit Form] or press [F10] to save or click [Clear Record] to clear the entered data.

  2. Click [Execute Query] or press [F8] to search for the existing records within the facility.
