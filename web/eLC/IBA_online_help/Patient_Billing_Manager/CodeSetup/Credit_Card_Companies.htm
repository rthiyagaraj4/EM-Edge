Credit Card Companies  

From this function you can Create Credit Card Companies pertinent to the Facility. You can Query to view the existing records.

The Credit Card Companies function is used to create a new Credit Card Company. You can accept the credit cards from the companies defined when the patient/payer chooses to clear the payment using a Credit Card. You have an option to update the Credit Card Company Name and the Branch Name for an existing Credit Card Company. Additionally, a search can be done using the [Execute Query] to view the existing records.

Creating a Credit Card Company or an Insurance Company

From the Masters > Banks/Companies, double-click on Credit Card Companies function.

The Credit Card Companies screen gets displayed.

  1. Enter the details in the mandatory (*) fields. <Related Field Description>

  2. Click [Commit Form] or press [F10] to save or [Clear Record] to clear the entered data.

  3. Click [Execute Query] or press [F8] to search for existing records within the facility.
