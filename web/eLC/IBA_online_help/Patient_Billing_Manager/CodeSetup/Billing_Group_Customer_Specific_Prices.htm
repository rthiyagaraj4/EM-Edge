Billing Group/Customer Specific Prices

From this function you can Define the price and the discounts for a Billing Service, a Billing Class and associate it to a relevant Billing Group or a Customer in the Facility. You can also Update the existing prices for a Billing Class or Query to view any past prices for the Billing Class.

The Billing Group/Customer Specific Prices function is used to create and maintain prices for a specific Billing Group or a Customer Group. The price defined is applicable for the selected Billing Group or Customer for a specified period. The discounts applicable for the services associated to the Billing Group or Customer in the Facility are also defined.

Defining Billing Group or Customer Specific Prices

From the Masters > select Multi Pricing menu, double-click Billing Group/Customer Specific Prices function.

The Billing Group/Customer Specific Prices screen gets displayed.

Billing Group Specific Prices

Billing Group Specific Prices is used to define the price for a Billing Group in a Facility. Lists of services are associated to the Billing Group. A patient is assigned a Billing Group during patient registration. The services associated to the Billing Group are charged based on the prices defined for a patient class within the facility. The discount percentage for a patient class is also defined.

You can create, update and view the prices defined using the tab pages:

Maintain Prices

Create Prices

View Past Prices

Customer Specific Prices

Customer Specific Prices is used to define the price for the Billing Services associated to a Customer in a Facility. List of services are associated to the Customer defined. The services associated to the Customer are charged based on the prices defined for the patient class within the facility. The prices are valid, if a Customer is associated to a patient during patient registration indicating that the Customer settles a part of the patient bill.

You can create, update and view the prices defined using the tab pages:

Maintain Prices

Create Prices

View Past Prices

  1. Click [View Past Prices] to view the prices set previously for the Price Class.

  2. Click [Commit Form] or press [F10] to save or [Clear Record] to clear the entered data.

  3. Click [Execute Query] or press [F8] to search for the existing records within the facility.
