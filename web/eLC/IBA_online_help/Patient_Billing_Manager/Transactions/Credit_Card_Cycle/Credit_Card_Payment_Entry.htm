Credit Card Payment Entry  

From this function you can Enter the payment details processed for a batch of credit cards. You can Cancel the payment entry, if the details are not right.

Credit Card Payment entry function is used to record the payments collected from the Credit Card company against the documents forwarded to the company through the batch process and online process as well. The details for payment received by means of Cheque or Demand Draft are captured in the header part of the function. The pending records of Credit Card documents forwarded to the Credit Card Company are displayed in the detailed block under ‘Outstanding Credit Card Batches for Payment’.

You can select the records and clear the payment for the selected records. The system generates the receipt for the payment received from the Credit Card company and does not effect any change in patient’s ledger, since the amount is credited to the patient account. The Amount Settled entered at the Header block has to match with the list of detail records selected.

You can cancel the Credit Card payment entry at any time using the Cancel option. The set of records are reversed against the entered batch on selecting Cancelling the Payment Entry.

Generating Credit Card Payment Entry

From the Transactions > Credit Card Cycle, double-click Credit Card Payment Entry function.

The Credit Card Payment Entry screen gets displayed.

  1. 1. Enter the details in the following tab pages:

Create

Cancel

  1. Click [Execute Query] or press [F8] to view and to select the outstanding credit card batches for payments.

  2. Click [Commit Form] or press [F10] to save the records or [Clear Record] to clear the entered data.

 

The Amount Settled has to be equal to the total amount of the selected outstanding credit card batches.
