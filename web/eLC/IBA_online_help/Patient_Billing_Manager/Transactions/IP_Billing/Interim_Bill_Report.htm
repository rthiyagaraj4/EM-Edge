Interim Bill Report

From this function you can Print a copy of the bills for the services availed from the facility by the Inpatients or the Daycare patients.

Interim Bill Report function is used to print interim bills for an Inpatient or a Daycare patient. The printed report displays details on the outstanding amount, unadjusted amount, unbilled amount and the total amount. Cashier has an option to compute the bed charges until the current date for a selected ward. Authorized Users are only permitted to login and to re-print the bills. The check box ‘Reprint Bill’ has to be selected in the ‘Users for Authorization/Refund/Cash Counters’ function in the Administration menu. You can also view the Encounter ID, Bill Printed Date, Hospital Details, Patient's Name, Admission and Discharge Date (if the patient is discharged during report generation), Service Groups, Print Groups, total charge, Payer and Patient Payable amount, total amount, discount amount, outstanding amount, remaining deposit amount, policy number and name of the authorized person who has generated the bill is displayed. An additional column indicates of the discharge advice is generated for the corresponding inpatient along with date and time when the discharge advice is generated. The generated report displays the subtotal of each Print Service separately. You can generate an interim report for inpatients' even though the discharge bill is generated.

Interim Bill Report

From the Transactions > IP Billing, double-click Interim Bill Report Function.

The Interim Bill Report screen gets displayed.

  1. Select an Episode Type to print interim bills. The bills generated for the selected Episode Type gets displayed. <Related Field Description>

  2. Click [Recalculate] to recalculate the patient charges if the patient is moved from one bed class to another bed class.

  3. Click [Process Run] to print the bill or [Clear Record] to clear the entered data.

  4. A bill print authorization screen pops-up. Enter the User ID, PIN Number and the Remarks to login and to re-print the bill. Click [OK] to login and [Cancel] to exit from the screen.

  5. A confirmation screen pops-up on clicking [OK]. Click [Yes] to re-print the bills and [No] to cancel the printing process.
