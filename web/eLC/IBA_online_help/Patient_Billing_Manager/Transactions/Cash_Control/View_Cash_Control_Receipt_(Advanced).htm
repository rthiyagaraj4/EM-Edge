View Cash Control Receipt (Advanced)

From this function you can View the status of the cashier transactions at a relevant Cash Counter in the facility. You can Query to view all the cash control receipt status, the settlement details based on a relevant shift or based on the shift.

View Cash Control Receipt (Advanced) is an option provided to view the status of the Cash Control Receipts. You can  also view the Settlement details at a specific Cash Counter, which indicates the Settlement Type and the amount settled. The details in the tab View Transaction By Shift indicates the patient details, total amount collected at the specified Cash Counter on a particular date. Cashier can view the details concerning the receipts generated for the payment/refund issued to the patients/customers paying on behalf of the patients.

View Cash Control Receipt Status (Advanced)

From the Transactions > Cash Control, double-click View Cash Control Receipt (Advanced) Function.

The View Cash Control Receipt (Advanced) screen gets displayed.

  1. Click [List of Values] to select the Cash Counter range. You can view the Cash Control Receipts generated by the selected Cash Counter range.

  2. Click Calendar function to select the date range. The Cash Control receipts are listed for the selected date range.

  3. Select criteria from the list. The values are ‘With Cash Control’, ‘Without Cash Control’ and ‘Both’. Select ‘With Cash Control’ to view the details against which the Cash Control Receipts are generated. Select ‘Without Cash Control’ to view the details against which the Cash Control Receipts are not generated. Select Both to view the Cash details with and without Cash Control Receipts.

  4. The Cash Control details are displayed under the following tabs:

Cash Control Receipt

The Cash Control Receipts generated using the function Cash Control Receipt (Advanced) get displayed.

View Settlement Details

The Settlement Details for the selected Cash Counter shift gets displayed. The Cancelled amount and the receipt amount are also displayed.

View Transaction by Shift

The opening and closing time for the selected Cash Counter along with the total amount, document amount, the total amount and the status of the Cash Control Receipt gets displayed.

  1. Click [Commit Form] to record the details or [Clear Record] to clear the entered data.

 

• The check box ‘Cash Control Receipt in use’ in the BL Parameters has to be selected to enable this function.

• The receipts generated using the ‘Cash Control Receipt (Advanced)’ function are displayed.
