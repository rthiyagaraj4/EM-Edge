IP U/Charge Bill Cum Receipt

From this function you can Generate the bills or the receipts for the undercharge items associated to an inpatient. A bill is computed for the services associated to an inpatient after the discharge bill is raised.

IP U/Charge Bill Cum Receipt function is used to generate the bills/receipts for an Inpatient/Daycare patient availing services from the facility after the discharge bill is raised. The services associated using the functions in the ‘IP Under Charges’ menu are considered towards bill generation. The services, which are cancelled, are not considered towards the bill generation. A discount report is also printed along with the bill details, if the patient has availed a discount for the services utilized from the facility. The bed charge is computed until the undercharge bill is generated based on the Bed Charge Computation rule. A single bill is generated, if there are multiple payers associated to the patient, listing the coverage amount for each service, agreed to be covered by the payer. The Surgeon's name is printed against each individual service on the report that is generated .

This is valid only for a detailed bill and it is not printed, if a summarized bill is generated. The name of the Surgeon captured through the 'OT Slate' function in 'Operation Theatre' module for the respective patient encounter is displayed. The attending surgeon's name is displayed, if the Surgeon's name is not captured in OT Slate function.

Generate Bill Cum Receipt for Undercharge items

From the Transactions > IP Cashier, double-click IP U/Charge Bill Cum Receipt Function.

The IP U/Charge Bill Cum Receipt screen gets displayed.

  1. Select an Episode Type for transaction.

  2. Enter the details in the mandatory (*) fields. <Related Field Description>

  3. Click [Commit Form] to generate bills/receipts or [Clear Record] to clear the data.

 

You have to login to the Cash Counter to generate discharge bills and receipts.
