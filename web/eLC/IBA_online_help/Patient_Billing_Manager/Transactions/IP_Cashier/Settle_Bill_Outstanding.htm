Settle Bill Outstanding

From this function you can Settle the outstanding amount for a relevant Service. A Cashier can cancel the refund document only if it is printed.        

Settle Bill Outstanding function is used to settle the outstanding amount towards a service. Cashier has to settle the outstanding amount for each service individually using this function. You cannot settle the bill amount, if credit agreement is made for the installment payment of the respective bill. A receipt number is generated when the bill amount is settled.

If the bill amount is settled by the Payer and Patient, an original receipt is generated indicating the amount settled against the services. If the Payer and Patient amount is adjusted through the 'Adjust Payer Distribution' function, then two receipts are generated to indicate the amount settled by the payer and patient. The Original receipt displays the actual amount, amount settled by the Payer and Patient. The Adjustment receipt displays the service details and the amount adjusted through the 'Adjust Payer Distribution' function. You can choose to print the receipt in local language. A receipt is printed in English, by default.

Receipt is printed in Arabic, if you select the local language as Arabic and the report details are aligned from right to left.

 

The hierarchy of receipt generation during settlement is based on the value selected for 'Priority Adjustment Slmt' in 'Receipts/Refund Controls' tab in 'BL Parameters' function.

Settle Bill Outstanding

From the Transactions > IP Cashier, double-click Settle Bill Outstanding Function.

From the Transactions > OP Cashier, double-click Settle Bill Outstanding Function.

The Settle Bill Outstanding screen gets displayed.

  1. Enter the Patient Number for the patient details to get populated in the respective fields..

  2. Enter details in the mandatory (*) fields. <Related Field Description>

  3. Click [Commit Form] to settle the bill amount or [Clear Record] to clear the data.

Only one payer either the patient or a payer can settle the outstanding amount for the transaction.

Single payer can settle any number of bills available for the patient.

Payer cannot settle the outstanding amount with the patient’s deposit.
