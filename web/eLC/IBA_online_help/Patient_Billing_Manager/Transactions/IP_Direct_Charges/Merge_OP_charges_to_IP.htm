Merge OP charges to IP

From this function you can Merge the charges from the outpatient clinic with the inpatient admission and the service charges. The charges are merged without recalculating the discounts towards the services for the relevant visit. The charges are combined to generate a single inpatient bill with all the service details.  

Merge IP Charges to OP function is used to transfer the unbilled charges from the Outpatient visit folio to the Inpatient episode folio of a patient. You can use this function only when a patient is admitted in a Nursing Unit and has availed services from the outpatient or emergency clinic in the facility. This helps in generating a consolidated bill during patient discharge at the facility. The amount is transferred from the Outpatient folio to the Inpatient folio without calculating the discounts and the additional factors for the services utilized in the Outpatient Clinic. Several validations  are carried out before transferring the charges.

  • Patient ID has to match for the Inpatient and the Outpatient transactions.

  • Billing Group Code has to match for the Inpatient and the Outpatient transactions.

  • Settlement Indicator has to match for the Inpatient and the Outpatient transactions.

  • Customer Code has to match for the Inpatient and the Outpatient transactions.

  • The unbilled transactions are only transferred. The billed transactions are not considered.  

  • Selected transactions for the Outpatient visit are cancelled by the system.

  • The transactions cancelled from the Outpatient folio are created for the Inpatient episode number.

Merge Outpatient charges to Inpatient folio

From the Transactions > IP Direct Charges, double-click Merge OP charges to IP function.

The Merge OP charges to IP screen gets displayed.

  1. Enter the details in the mandatory (*) fields. <Related Field Description>

  2. Click [Process Run] to merge the charges. The charges are computed automatically till the current date.
