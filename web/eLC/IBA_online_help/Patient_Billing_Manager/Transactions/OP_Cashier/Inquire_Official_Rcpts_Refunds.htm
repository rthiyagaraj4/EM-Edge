Inquire Official Receipts / Refunds

From this function you can Query to view the receipts for the deposit amount accepted from a patient during admission or for a refund issued to the patient.

Inquire Official Receipts/Refunds function is used to view the transaction details of an outpatient. You have an option to view the deposits accepted and the refund issued to a relevant patient. You cannot update any details, but only view the details associated with an outpatient.

Inquire Official Receipts/Refunds

From the Transactions > OP Cashier, double-click Inquire Official Receipts/Refunds Function.

The Inquire Official Receipts/Refunds screen gets displayed.

  1. Click [Enter Query] and enter the Patient Number; press [F8] or [Execute Query] to view the details of relevant patients at the Facility. Else, press [F8] or [Execute Query] to view the details of all the outpatients.  

  2. The details recorded using the function Enter OP Receipts/Refunds get displayed.
