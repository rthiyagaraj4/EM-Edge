Query Reminder Letters by Patient

From this function you can Query the details on the number of reminder letters dispatched to the patients.

Query Reminder Letters by Patient function is used to query on the reminder letter details dispatched to a patient. You can also view the total bill amount, the amount paid, the amount adjusted, bill amount exempted, outstanding amount and the amount written-off, if any. The reminder letter details dispatched to the patient are displayed and the total for a relevant patient. The information includes the bill details, details on the reminder letter delivered last and the write-off details. You can only view the contents, but cannot update any details.

Query Reminder Letters by Patient

From the Transactions > Reminder Letter Process, double-click Query Reminder Letters by Patient function.

The Query Reminder Letters by Patient screen gets displayed.

  1. Enter a Patient Number or click [List of Values] to view and to select a Patient Number. The Gender and the Age get displayed on selecting the patient number.

  2. Click [Execute Query] to query the details. The reminder letter details relevant to the selected patient get displayed. Click [Clear Record] to clear the entered data.
