By Episode and Service Group  – Summary Info

From this function you can Query to view the folio transactions for the nursing services utilized by the patient in the facility.

By Episode and Service Group – Summary Info function is used to query on the folio transactions  for the nursing services utilized. The details for a specific episode are summarized. You can view the encounter details of a specific patient to the facility. The service date and time, quantity of the services utilized and the practitioner assigned for the selected episode is displayed. The net and gross amount, discounts, departments associated, print line details are not displayed.  

Querying to view the complete Episode details

From the Queries > Transactions, double-click By Episode – Complete Info function.

The By Episode – Complete Info screen gets displayed.

  1. Enter the details in the fields to query  for a specific patient record. The patient records matching the search criteria get displayed in the result area. Click [Click for Searching Patients] to view the financial and encounter details of a specific patient. Click [Cancel Search Criteria] to clear the entered data.

OR

  1. Click [Click for Searching Patients] to list all the patient records. You can view the financial and encounter details for a specific patient. Click [Cancel Search Criteria] to clear the entered data. The records matching the search criteria get displayed in the result area.

  2. The records get displayed in the result area. Double-click on the relevant patient record. The By Episode and Service Group – Summary Info screen gets displayed listing the service details, doctor for the relevant episode, the billing details for the specific encounter and the charge encountered for the associated service.

  3. Click [List of Values] to select a nursing service from the list. You can query to view the patient details based on the nursing units.

  4. Click [Click for Patient Encounters] to view all the patient encounters recorded in the facility. The encounter details of a patient get listed in the Encounter Details screen. Double-click on the relevant record to view the service details, doctor for the relevant episode, the billing details for the specific encounter and the charge encountered for the associated service. Click [Close] to exit from the Encounter Details screen.
