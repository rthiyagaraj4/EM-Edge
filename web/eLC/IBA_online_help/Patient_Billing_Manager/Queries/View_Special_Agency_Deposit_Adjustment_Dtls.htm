View Special Agency Deposit Adjustment Dtls

From this function you can Query to view the adjustments made against the deposit amount accepted from the patient.  

The View Agency Deposit Adjustment Dtls function is used to query on the adjustment made against the deposit details. The deposit details of the patient get displayed on entering the query criteria. You can view the document details for the transactions during the selected episode, the service details, coverage amount, unutilized amount for the services and the transaction date.

Querying to view the adjustments against the deposit details

From the Queries > Transactions, double-click View Agency Deposit Adjustment Dtls function.

The View Agency Deposit Adjustment Dtls screen gets displayed.

  1. Enter the Patient Number in alphanumeric format with a maximum of 10 characters. The patient gender, age and the name gets displayed in the respective fields.

  2. Select an Episode Type from the list. The values are system defined. Select an appropriate Episode Type to view the deposit details, which are adjusted. Enter the Episode Type and the Visit Number.

  3. Click [Execute Query] or press [F8]. The deposit adjustments for the selected episode get listed.
