Maintain Discounts

The Maintain Discounts tab enables you to update the discounts defined for a Billing Group using the Create Discounts tab.

 

Enter the following details to maintain discounts:

 

  1. Billing Group - To select an attribute from [List of Values], click [Enter Query]. The Billing Groups defined get listed. Click [List of Values] to select a Billing Group. Click [Execute Query] to view the discount details associated to the respective Billing Group.

 

Click [View Past Discounts] to view any discounts defined earlier for the respective Billing Group.

 

  1. Service/Package - The Billing service associated to the Billing Group gets listed. The Service details cannot be changed.

  2. Billing Service/Package – The relevant Billing Service or the Panel associated to the Billing Group gets displayed.

  3. Description - The description for the respective Billing Service or the Panel gets displayed. The description for the Service cannot be changed.

  4. Valid From - The date selected initially specifying from when the discount is applicable gets displayed. Click Calendar function to update the date. This is a mandatory field.

  5. Valid To - The date selected initially specifying until when the discount is applicable gets displayed. Click Calendar function to update the date. This is a mandatory field.

  6. IP - The discount percentage defined initially for Inpatients gets displayed. To modify the discount percentage for Inpatients, enter a new discount percentage in numeric value. This is a mandatory field.

  7. OP - The discount percentage defined initially for Outpatients gets displayed. To modify the discount percentage for Outpatients, enter a new discount percentage in numeric value. This is a mandatory field.

  8. Emergency - The discount percentage defined initially for Emergency patients gets displayed. To modify the discount percentage for Emergency patients, enter a new discount percentage in numeric value. This is a mandatory field.

  9. Ext. - The discount percentage defined initially for External/Referral patients gets displayed. To modify the discount percentage for External/Referral patients, enter a new discount percentage in numeric value. This is a mandatory field.
