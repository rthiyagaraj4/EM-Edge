Enter the following details:

 

  1. Patient ID – The IDs of the patients with pending amount get displayed based on the search criteria specified.

  2. Encounter Date – The date when the encounter is created in the facility for the respective patient record gets displayed.

  3. Episode Type – The Episode Type of the respective patient is displayed.

  4. Encounter ID – The encounter ID of the patient is displayed.

The fields Counseling Finalized, Installment Payment Allowed, Assigned To, Assigned Date, Exemption Allowed, Assigned To and Assigned Date gets displayed based on the Counseling details recorded for the patient.

  1. Outstanding Amt – The unsettled bill amount of the patient for the respective encounter is displayed.

  2. Discharge Date – The date of discharge recorded for the patient in the Inpatient Management module is displayed.

  3. Adm/Visit Status – The admission or the visit status of the patient in the facility is displayed.

  4. Location – The location where the patient’s admission or visit is registered is displayed.
