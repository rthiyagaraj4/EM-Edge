Right Click on User Options

 

Patients Lookup

Click Patients Lookup to view the Inpatient/Daycare Patient Details. The lookup screen gets displayed. Press [F8] or click [Execute Query] to view all the Patient details. You can search for a relevant patient record by entering one of the search criteria. Click [Enter Query] to enter search criteria. Click [Execute Query] or press [F8] to search for the relevant patient record. The Patient ID, previous HN, National ID, First Name, Second Name, Third Name, Family Name of the patient, Driving License Number, Title, Residence Telephone Number, Patient Series and the Gender of the patient get listed.

Double-click on the relevant record to select it. The Patient ID, Name, Admission and Transfer Serial Number, Transaction Date, Ward, Bed/Room Number, Age, Gender and Name of the Physician associated to the patient get populated in the respective fields.

All the above details are populated based on the details input in the MPI module during the patient registration.

 

Patient/Admission/Transfer lookup

  • Click Patient/Admission/Transfer Lookup to view and to select the Inpatient or the Daycare Patient Details. The lookup screen gets displayed. Press [F8] or click [Execute Query] to view all the Patient details. You can search for a relevant patient record by entering one of the search criteria. Click [Enter Query] to enter search criteria. Click [Execute Query] or press [F8] to search for the relevant patient record. The Patient ID, previous HN, National ID, First Name, Second Name, Third Name, Family Name of the patient, Driving License Number, Title, Residence Telephone Number, Patient Series and the Gender of the patient get listed. Double-click on the relevant record to select it. The Patient ID, Name, Admission and Transfer Serial Number, Transaction Date, Ward, Bed/Room Number, Age, Gender and Name of the Physician associated to the patient get populated in the respective fields. Click [Close] to exit from the lookup screen.

  • Click [Admission Details] to view the admission details of the patient. The Admission Details screen gets displayed with the details on the number of times the patient was admitted and discharged from the facility. The date when the patient was admitted, the discharge date, episode type and number, patient ID and name, Billing Group and the Settlement type for the respective admission get displayed. Double-click on the relevant record to select it. The specific admission details get populated.

  • Click [Transfers] to view the patient transfer details within the facility. The Episode Number, Serial Number, Patient ID, Ward Code, Bed Code, Doctor and the Room Number get displayed. Double-click on the relevant record to select the respective patient record for transactions.

All the above details are populated based on the details input in the MPI module during patient registration. The admission details get populated from the transactions in the Inpatient Management module.

 

Admission/Transfer lookup

  • Enter a Patient ID and click Admission/Transfer Lookup to view and to select the Inpatient or the Daycare patient admission or transfer details. The Admission Details lookup screen gets displayed. You can select a patient admission recorded and associate services to the selected admission. You can also search for a patient transfer in a facility and associate services to the relevant patient encounters.

  • Click [Transfers] to search for a patient transfer associated to the admission in the facility.

Double-click on the selected record to associate services to the Inpatient or the Daycare patient.

You have to enter a relevant patient ID in the Patient No field to use the Admission/Transfer lookup. The admission details and the transfer details of an Inpatient or a Daycare patient within the facility get displayed.
