Click [Execute Query] or press [F8] to view all the bill details. You can search for a specific Inpatient bill by entering the search criteria on clicking [Enter Query]. Enter the data in the respective field and click [Execute Query] or press [F8]. The bill details get listed based on the search criteria.

 

  1. Patient ID - The Patient ID gets displayed. Enter the Patient ID in alphanumeric format of 10 characters maximum length to search for a record.

  2. Episode ID - Enter the Episode ID of the Inpatient. The bills generated during the respective encounter gets displayed.

  3. Visit ID - Enter the Visit ID of the specific patient.

  4. Doc Type Code - Enter the Document Type code. The bill generated with the specific Document Type code gets listed.

  5. Doc Num - The Document Number gets displayed. To search for a specific patient record, enter the Document number.

  6. Doc Date - The Document Date gets displayed. To search for a specific patient record, enter the Document Date.

  7. Bill Tot Outst Amount - The outstanding amount for the specific outstanding document gets displayed. To search for a specific patient record, enter the outstanding amount.

  8. Bill Tot Amount - The total bill amount for the specific outstanding document gets displayed. To search for a specific patient record, enter the total bill amount.

  9. Bill Paid Amt -  The total amount settled for the specific bill gets displayed. To search for a specific patient record, enter the total amount settled.

  10. Bill Trx Type Code - The transaction type code gets displayed. To search for a specific patient record, enter the bill transaction type code.

  11. Bill Nature Code - The bill nature gets displayed indicating the type of the bill. To search for a specific patient record, enter the bill nature.

  12. Bill Status - The bill status gets displayed indicating if the bill is active or cancelled.
