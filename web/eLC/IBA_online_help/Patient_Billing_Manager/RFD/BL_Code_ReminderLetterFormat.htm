Enter the following details to set up the Reminder Letter Format:

 

  1. Applicable for Transaction – Select a value from the list. The values are system defined - 'Bill Payment Reminder' and 'Installment Payment Reminder'. The Reminder Letter format is valid for the transaction selected. This is a mandatory field.

  2. Bill Payment Reminder – Select Outstanding Payment Reminder to define a reminder letter format for bill payment reminders.

  3. Installment Payment Reminder – Select Installment Payment Reminder to define a reminder letter format for installment payments.

  1. Format Id - Enter the Format ID in alphanumeric form of 20 characters maximum length. A Format ID has to be unique and cannot be changed after creation. This is a mandatory field.

  2. Short Description - Enter the short description in alphanumeric format of 15 characters maximum length. This is a mandatory field.

  3. Long Description - Enter the long description in alphanumeric form with 30 characters maximum length. This is a mandatory field.

  4. Letter Title - Enter the Letter Title in alphanumeric format. This is a mandatory field.

 

Letter Header

Enter the header information to be printed on the reminder letters, which are posted to a patient or a Customer. To select and to insert the inbuilt variables, right click in the respective field and select the variable. The variable gets displayed in the selected text box.

 

  1. Header Line [1-5] - Enter the data in alphanumeric format with a maximum of 100 characters. The information mentioned for the header part appears in the reminder letter header.

  2. Header Line [6-10] -  Enter the data in alphanumeric format with a maximum of 100 characters. The information mentioned for the header part appears in the reminder letter header.

 

Letter Body

Enter the text to be printed in the body of the reminder letter, which are posted to a patient/customer. To select and to insert the inbuilt variables, right click in the respective field and select the variable. The variable gets displayed in the selected text box.

 

  1. Body Line [1-5] - Enter the data in alphanumeric format with a maximum of 100 characters. The information recorded appears as the the body of the reminder letter.

  2. Body Line [6-10] - Enter the data in alphanumeric format with a maximum of 100 characters. The information recorded appears as the the body of the reminder letter.

  3. Body Line [11-15] - Enter the data in alphanumeric format with a maximum of 100 characters. The information recorded appears as the the body of the reminder letter.

  4. Body Line [16-20] - Enter the data in alphanumeric format with a maximum of 100 characters. The information recorded appears as the the body of the reminder letter.

 

Letter Footer

The data recorded using this tab appears in the footer of the reminder letter dispatched to a patient or a Customer.

 

  1. Footer Line [1-5] -  Enter the data in alphanumeric format with a maximum of 100 characters. The information recorded appears as the the footer of the reminder letter.

  2. Footer Line [6-10] - Enter the data in alphanumeric format with a maximum of 100 characters. The information recorded appears as the the footer of the reminder letter.  

  3. [Preview] - Click [Preview] to view the recorded details before printing the Reminder Letter Format.
