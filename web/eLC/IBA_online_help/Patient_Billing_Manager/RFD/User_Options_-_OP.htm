Right Click on User Options

Patients Lookup

Click Patients Lookup to view the Outpatient, Emergency or External Patient Details. The lookup screen gets displayed. Press [F8] or click [Execute Query] to view all the Patient details. You can search for a relevant patient record by entering one of the search criteria. Click [Enter Query] to enter search criteria. Click [Execute Query] or press [F8] to search for the relevant patient record.

The Patient ID, previous HN, National ID, First Name, Second Name, Third Name, Family Name of the patient, Driving License Number, Title, Residence Telephone Number, Patient Series and the Gender of the patient get listed.

Double-click on the relevant record to select it. The Patient ID, name, admission and transfer serial number, transaction date, ward, bed/room number, age, gender and name of the Physician associated to the patient get populated in the respective fields.

All the above details are populated based on the details input in the MPI module during patient registration.

 

Patient/Episode/Visit lookup

  • Click Patient/Episode/Visit Lookup to view and to select the Outpatient, Emergency patient or an External patient Details. The lookup screen gets displayed. Press [F8] or click [Execute Query] to view all the Patient details. You can search for a relevant patient record by entering one of the search criteria. Click [Enter Query] to enter search criteria. Click [Execute Query] or press [F8] to search for the relevant patient record. The Patient ID, previous HN, National ID, First Name, Second Name, Third Name, Family Name of the patient, Driving License Number, Title, Residence Telephone Number, Patient Series and the Gender of the patient get listed. Double-click on the relevant record to select it. The Patient ID, name, admission and transfer serial number, transaction date, ward, bed/room number, age, gender and name of the Physician associated to the patient get populated in the respective fields. Click [Close] to exit from the lookup screen.

  • Click [OP Details] to view the episode details of the patient. The Episode lookup screen gets displayed with the details on the number of times the patient had visited the facility. The date when the patient had visited, the episode number, last visit ID and the episode start date get displayed. Double-click on the relevant record to select it. The specific episode details get populated.

  • Click [Visit Details] to view the visit details of the patient. The Visit registration date, respective visit number, visit type, clinic details and the name of the doctor get displayed. Double-click on the relevant record to select the respective patient record for transactions.

All the above details are populated based on the details input in the Master Patient Index module during patient registration.

 

Episode/Visit lookup

  • Enter a Patient ID and click Episode/Visit Lookup to view and to select the Outpatient or External patient or the Daycare patient episode or visit details. The Episode lookup screen gets displayed. You can select a patient admission recorded and associate services to the selected admission. You can also search for a patient visit to the facility and associate services to the relevant patient encounters.

  • Click [Visit Details] to search for a patient visit. The visit details associated to the selected episode get listed.

  • Double-click on the selected record to associate services to the Outpatient or Emergency patient or the External patient in the facility.

You have to enter a relevant patient ID in the Patient No field to use the Admission/Transfer lookup. The episode details and the visit details of an Outpatient, External patient or an Emergency patient within the facility get displayed.
