Enter the following to cancel the indirect services:

 

  1. Line - The serial number for the services associated during associating the service to the patient gets displayed on selecting the transaction reference ID.

  2. Source Doc. Ref. – Click [List of Values] to select a reference ID. The transaction reference ID generated while associating the services to the patient using the functions ‘Enter Request – IP’ and ‘Enter User Dept Request – IP’ gets listed. The transaction date, service code, UOM, service quantity, total payable amount, patient payable amount and the practitioner or staff details associated to the patient during transaction get displayed.

  3. [Edit] – Click [Edit] to update the service details. The Edit/Enter Charge Details  screen gets displayed with the details populated in the respective fields. You can update and commit the details.
