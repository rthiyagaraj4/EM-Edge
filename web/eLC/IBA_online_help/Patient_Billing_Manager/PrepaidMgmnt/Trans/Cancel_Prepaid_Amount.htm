Cancel Prepaid Amount

From this function you can Cancel the lumpsum prepaid payment amount that is recorded for a Payer.

The Cancel Prepaid Amount function is used to cancel the lumpsum prepaid amount that is received from the Payer. The receipt that is generated through the 'Record Prepaid Receipt' function is cancelled. You can cancel the amount if the remaining prepaid amount is still higher than the receipt amount.

Cancelling Prepaid Amount

From the Prepaid Management > Transaction, double-click on Cancel Prepaid Amount function.

The Cancel Prepaid Amount screen gets displayed.

  1. The Deposit Type is displayed as 'LumpSum Deposit'.  

  2. Click [Execute Query] or press [F8] to query the records.

  3. The details that are recorded through the 'Record Prepaid Receipt' function are displayed. You can navigate to the next or previous records using the Upward or Downward arrow in keyboard. You cannot update the record.

  4. Select the check box 'Cancel' to cancel the prepaid payment. The check box is cleared, by default.

  5. Enter the reason for cancellation in alphanumeric format.

  6. Click [Commit Form] or press [F10] to save the records or [Clear Record] to delete the data.
