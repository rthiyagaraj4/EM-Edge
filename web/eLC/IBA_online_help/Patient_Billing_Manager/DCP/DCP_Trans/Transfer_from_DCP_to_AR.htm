Transfer from DCP to AR

From this function you can Transfer bills from DCP to AR.

Transfer from DCP to AR function is used to move bills from DCP to AR. These Bills are to be sent for medical aids.

Transfer from DCP to AR

From the Deferred Cash Patients > click DCP Transaction, double-click on Transfer from DCP to AR function.

The Transfer from DCP to AR screen gets displayed.

1. Enter the details in the mandatory (*) fields. <Related Field Description>

2. Click [Commit Form] or press [F10] to save and transfer the bill records to AR or [Clear Record] to clear the data entered.

3. Click [Execute Query] or press [F8] to query for the existing records.
