Record Installment Payment

From this function you can Settle the installment amount accepted from a patient/guarantor against an outstanding document. You can also View the outstanding bill details and the reminders dispatched.

Record Installment Payment function is used to accept payments from a patient against an installment plan. An installment reminder has to be generated using the function ‘Generate Installment Reminders’ to record the agreement for installment payments. You can view the outstanding bill details and the reminder letter details. The total outstanding amount along with the credit agreement number and amount are displayed. You have to login to the Cash Counter to settle the installment payment for an individual record. Right click on the selected record to settle the amount against the record. You can record the installment payments for the outstanding documents in a specific month.

Record Installment Payment

From the Deferred Cash Patients > Agreement for Installment Payment, double-click Generate Record Installment Payment function.

The Record Installment Payment screen gets displayed.

  1. Click Calendar function to select a month. The outstanding documents for the selected month get displayed. The outstanding document details get displayed.

  2. Select a value for Installment from the list. Select Unpaid to view all the unpaid document details and to settle the amount. Select Paid to view the documents for which the amount is settled.

  3. You can also search to view the installment details based on a patient ID. Press [F9] to search for a patient record using the lookup screen. Select a patient ID from the list. The patient name gets displayed in the adjacent text box. Press [F8] to view the installment details for the selected patient.

The outstanding document detail against which the reminder letter is generated gets displayed. The Installment Plan, Installment Payment Details, Outstanding Bill Details, Reminder Letter details are displayed under the respective screen.

The outstanding documents against which the reminder letters were dispatched get listed. If the remainder letter is written-off, approved or rejected, then appropriate details are listed. The written-off/approved document type and number, processed date, requested amount and approved amount are displayed. The check boxes 'Approved' and 'Cancel' when selected indicate that the outstanding amount is either approved or cancelled.

The documents with the outstanding bill details get displayed. You cannot change the details; you can view the bill document type and number, date when the bill document was generated, the outstanding amount against which the document was generated. The amount settled and the outstanding balance after the installment payment get displayed.

The installment number, document type code and number, date when the installment amount was paid and the amount settled against each installment are displayed.

The installment plan recorded using the ‘Enter Credit Agreement’ function gets displayed. You can settle the outstanding payment using this function. The payments, which are due, undue and paid are indicated using three different colors. Red indicates that the amount is not settled. Green indicates that the amount is undue. Yellow indicates that the amount is settled. You can settle the amount against an overdue record. <Related field description>

  1. Click [Commit Form] or press [F10] to save the records.

 

You have to generate a reminder letter for a patient before using the function ‘Record Installment Payment’. The documents against which the reminder letters are generated can only be used to record the installment payment.
