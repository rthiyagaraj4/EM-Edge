Inpatient Bill Generation Process

From this function you can Generate a report on the bill generated for the inpatients discharged from the facility. You can get an overview of the bills generated for all the inpatient discharges on a relevant day at the specific Cash Counter.  

Inpatient Bill Generation Process function is used to view the number of inpatient bills generated in a relevant Cash Counter. It lists the discharged patient bill details generated in the relevant Cash Counter. The Inpatient Bill Generation Process is usually executed at the end of the day to know the inpatient discharges at a relevant Cash Counter in the facility.

Inpatient Bill Generation Process

From the Period End, double Inpatient Bill Generation Process function.

The Inpatient Bill Generation Process screen gets displayed.

  1. Enter the details in the mandatory (*) fields. <Related Field Description>

  2. Select Process to generate a report on the admission bills generated for the inpatients in the facility on a relevant day.

 

• The cashier has to post all the charges using the function ‘Post Billing Transactions’.

• You have to login to the Inpatient Cash Counter to process the bill generation for the inpatients discharged from the facility.
