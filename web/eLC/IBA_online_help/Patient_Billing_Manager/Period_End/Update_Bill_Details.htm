Update Bill Details

From this function you can Update the details of the bill, which are printed. You cannot update a bill, which is cancelled or nullified.

Update Bill Details function is used to update the bills, which are printed. A bill for a patient is categorized as a ‘Cash Memo’ or a ‘Credit Invoice’. You can update only the active bills. Bills, which are cancelled or nullified, cannot be updated. All posted documents less than or equal to the current processing period are deleted from Originating Transactions, Allocating Transactions, Receipts, Direct Billing Entries. General Ledger distributions upto the processing period accepted are deleted.

Update Bill Details

From the Period End, double click Update Bill Details function.

The Update Bill Details screen gets displayed.

  1. Click [Execute Query] or press [F8] to view the active bills. The bill number, date when the bill was generated, transaction type, episode number, patient number, name of the patient, bill nature, Billing Group and the Customer details get displayed. The bill nature, Billing Group and the Customer associated to the bill can be updated.

  2. Select the Bill Nature as either ‘A’ or ‘C’. ‘A’ stands for ‘Cash Memo’ and ‘C’ represents ‘Credit Invoice’.

  3. Click [List of Values] to select a Billing Group. The description for the relevant Billing Group gets displayed in the adjacent text box.

  4. Click [List of Values] to select a Customer. The description for the relevant Customer gets displayed.

  5. Click [Commit Form] or press [F10] to save the records.

  6. Click [Execute Query] or press [F8] to view the records.
