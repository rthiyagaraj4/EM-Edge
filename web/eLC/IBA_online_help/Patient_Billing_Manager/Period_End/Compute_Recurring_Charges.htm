Compute Recurring Charges

From this function you can Calculate the recurring charges for the services utilized by the inpatients in the facility. The service charges of the inpatients currently admitted in the facility are calculated.

Compute Recurring Charges function is used to calculate the charges for the inpatient services in the facility. The charges are computed until date. The inpatients admitted to the facility and currently availing services are only considered towards the recurring charge computation. Among the recurring charge transactions, the billing services for which the current process date is in between the start and end dates are selected. Recurring charges for inpatients are computed only when the inpatient billing is enabled. The charges, after computation, are transferred to the patients’ charges folio. Several validations are carried out while computing recurring charges.

  • Recurring charge transactions are considered. The last recurring charge generated date has to be lesser than the process date.

  • The system checks, whether the financial details are defined for every admission number. If the financial details are not found, the system encounters an error message. System continues with the next eligible admission number. Recurring charge transactions for the relevant admission are processed.

  • For every admission number, the system captures the Billing Group from the financial details recorded during admission.

  • The current transfer serial number forms the basis for the billing details like billing class, bill type code, originating department code, etc.

  • The billing services whose start and end dates cover the process date are selected for recurring charge processing.

  • The system computes the patient charge, if the charge amount for the recurring charge line is null or zero. The amount is considered as the patient charge, if the charge amount is recorded. The billing rule and service discounts are not applied in such a case. In either case, system rounds-off the charge to the next higher integer and posts the charges to the patient's folio.

  • Standard billing computation is carried out to determine the gross charge, discount amount and the net charge, if the charge amount is null. After computation, the charges are rounded and then posted to the patient's folio.

  • The process date is set as the last date when the recurring charges were processed.

  • For every admission selected, system updates the admission and inpatient financial details automatically. The updated values get displayed when the financial details of the selected patient are accessed for further transactions.

Compute Recurring Charges

From the Period End, double-click Compute Recurring Charges function.

The Compute Recurring Charges screen gets displayed.

  1. The system date gets displayed as the Process Date, by default. You cannot change the Process Date. This is a mandatory field.

  2. The previous date until when the recurring charges were computed gets displayed. You cannot update the Service Date. The recurring charges are computed for the Billing Services, where the date for the standard prices defined is in between the Process Date and the Service Date. The Service Date is incremented by one on computing the recurring charges for the selected date. This is a mandatory field.

  3. Enter the Service Time in numeric format as hh:mm:ss. Service Time is used to determine the Day Type and the Time Type codes to find out the additional rate/factor for patient charge computation.

  4. Click [Run Process] to compute the charges. A screen pops-up requesting confirmation. Click [Yes] to compute the recurring charges and [No] to cancel the transaction. A log file is generated on clicking [Yes]. Click [OK] to view the report and [Cancel] to exit from the screen.

 

You have to login to the Inpatient Cash Counter to compute the recurring charges.
