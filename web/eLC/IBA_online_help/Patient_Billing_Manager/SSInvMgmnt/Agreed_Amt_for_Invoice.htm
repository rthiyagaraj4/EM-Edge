Agreed Amount for Invoice

From this function you can Define the amount which the Social Security has agreed to pay for the services utilised by the patient. You can Query for the existing details.

The Agreed Amount for Invoice function enables you to record the invoice amount, which Social Security has agreed to pay for the patients with the selected Policy. Social Security pays the selected amount to the facility when the invoice is sent against the bills.

Recording Agreed Amount for Invoice

From the SS Invoice Management > Setup, double-click on Agreed Amount for Invoice function.

The Agreed Amount for Invoice screen gets displayed.

  1. Enter the details in the mandatory (*) fields. <Related Field Description>

  2. Click [Commit Form] to save the records or [Clear Record] to delete the data entered.

  3. Click [Execute Query] to query for the existing records.
