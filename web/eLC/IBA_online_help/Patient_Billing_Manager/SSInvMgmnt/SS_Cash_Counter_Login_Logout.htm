SS Cash Counter Login/Logout

From this function you can Login or Logout from the SS Cash Counter. You can also View the logged in Cash Counter details.

The SS Cash Counter Login/Logout function enables an authorized user to login to an SS Cash Counter. SS Invoices are processed in SS Cash Counter. If the user has already logged into the Cash Counter, an error message 'User is already assigned as Cashier for One Cash Counter' is displayed. In such scenario, you can only view the logged in Cashier details. User has to logout from the Cash Counter for any other user to login to the Cash Counter. A Cashier Report is generated during logout.

Login/Logout from SS Cash Counter

From the SS Invoice Management > Transaction, double-click on SS Cash Counter Login/Logout function.

The SS Cash Counter Login/Logout screen gets displayed.

  1. Click [List of Values] to select a Cash Counter in the 'Cash Counter Login' tab. The Cash Counter description gets displayed on selecting the Cash Counter code.

  2. The name of the user who has logged into the Cash Counter gets displayed in 'Shift Started By'.

  3. The current system date and time gets displayed as 'Current Time' indicating the Login date and time.

  4. Click [Commit Form] or press [F10] to save or click [Clear Record] to clear the details.

  5. Click 'Logged In User/Counter Details' tab to view Cashier details who have currently logged into the Cash Counter. The Cash Counter Code, description of the selected Cash Counter, logged-in User name and the login date and time get listed. .

  6. Click [Logout] to logout from the Cash Counter. A confirmation message 'Cash Counter is logged out successfully' gets displayed. A Delivery Form is generated. You can view the total number of active, cancelled and processed receipts in the Cash Counter during the relevant shift.
