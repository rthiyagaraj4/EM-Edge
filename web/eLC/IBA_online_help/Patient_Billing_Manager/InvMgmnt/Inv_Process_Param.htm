Invoice Process Parameters

From this function you can Define the parameters to dispatch the invoices to the Customers. You can Query for the existing details.

The Invoice Process Parameters function enables you to define the Customer Specific and Facility Specific parameters to dispatch the invoices to the Customers. While dispatching the invoice, you can choose either Facility specific or Customer specific letter format. If the Customer specific letter format is not defined, the facility specific letter format is selected, by default, to dispatch the invoices.

Defining Invoice Process Parameters

From the Invoice Management > Setup, double-click on Invoice Process Parameters function.

The Invoice Process Parameters screen gets displayed.

  1. Enter the details in the mandatory (*) fields. <Related Field Description>

  1. Click [Commit Form] to save the records or [Clear Record] to delete the data entered.

  2. Click [Execute Query] to query for the existing records.
