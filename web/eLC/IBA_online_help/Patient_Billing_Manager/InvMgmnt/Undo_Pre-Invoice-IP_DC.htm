Undo Pre-Invoice-IP/DC

From this function you can Rollback the Pre-invoice that is finalized. You can Query to view the pre-invoice records that are finalized.

Undo Pre-Invoice - IP/DC function is used to cancel the pre-invoice. The financial details that are modified during pre-invoice generation are cancelled and the financial details recorded for a patient during admission retained. The pre-invoice records that are cancelled are highlighted in color. Authorized users are only permitted to cancel the pre-invoice that is generated. Access rights are granted through the 'Users for Services' function by selecting the check box 'Undo Pre-Invoice'.

Cancelling Pre-Invoice generated for an Inpatient or a Daycare patient

From Invoice Management > Setup, double-click on undo Pre-Invoice-IP/DC function.

The Undo Pre-Invoice - IP/DC screen gets displayed.

  1. The name of the logged-in facility is displayed, by default. You cannot select any other facility.

  2. Click [List of Values] to select the Patient ID. The name of the patient, age and gender are displayed when the patient ID is selected. <Related Field Description>

  3. Click [Refresh] to query the patient records that has to be cancelled. You can query for a specific record by specifying any of the search criteria.

  4. The patient encounters with the pre-invoice generated and finalized are listed. The patient ID, name of the patient, encounter ID, Payer and description, Payer, Account Sequence Number, Priority and Policy Type and description that are associated to the respective patient record during pre-invoice generation are displayed. Click [V] to view the additional details that are captured.

  5. The document type and number generated for pre-invoice, date when the pre-invoice is generated, amount pending for settlement are displayed under 'Invoice/Debit/Credit Note Details' tab. The outstanding bill details against which the pre-invoice is generated are displayed under the 'Bill Details' tab.

  6. The transactions associated to the patient record are displayed under 'View All Transactions'. Right-click on the record under 'View All Transactions' to cancel the invoice or adjustment.

  7. The financial details are rolled back to the previous financial details, if the Payer approval is pending. If the payer has approved, financial details are rolled back to the details recorded during registration.
