Generate Pre-Invoice-IP/DC

From this function you can Generate a Pre-invoice for a Payer covering the bill amount of an inpatient or a daycare patient.

Generate Pre-Invoice - IP/DC function is used to generate pre-invoice for an inpatient or a daycare patient in the facility. This function is used to generate a pre-invoice when the patient admitted to the facility is a coverage patient, but does not have any documents for payer coverage. The patient is admitted as a Cash patient. During discharge patient pays for the services availed and the facility collects the payer details of the patient and sends a bill to the payer for approval. Patient payable amount is considered for pre-invoice generation for an insurance patient. You can generate a pre-invoice for the Billing Groups, Payer Group and Payer combination selected in 'Invoice Process Parameter' function. The pre-invoice can be generated only after discharging the patient from the facility. Several color indicators are used to indicate the status of patient records.

 

You can generate a Pre-invoice for the Payer selected in the Invoice Process Parameter function.

=> You have to generate the pending invoice before generating pre-invoice.

=> A warning message is displayed if the DRG code is not recorded.

Generating Pre-Invoice for an Inpatient or Daycare patient

From Invoice Management > Setup, double-click on Generate Pre-Invoice-IP/DC function.

The Generate Pre-Invoice-IP/DC screen gets displayed.

  1. The name of the logged-in facility is displayed, by default. You cannot select any other facility.

  2. Click [List of Values] to select the Patient ID. The name of the patient, age and gender are displayed when the patient ID is selected. <Related Field Description>

  3. Click [Search] to query the patient records. You can query for a specific record by specifying any of the search criteria.

  4. The encounters that are available for pre-invoice generation are listed. The patient ID, name of the patient, episode type, encounter ID, admission date, discharge date, main Billing Group, Non-insurance Billing Group (if any) and the DRG code associated to the respective patient record are displayed. The description of the main Billing Group and the non-insurance Billing Group are also displayed.

  5. Select the check box against a patient record to generate pre-invoice for the respective patient encounter in the facility.

  6. The Pre-Invoice Financial Details screen is displayed. The financial details recorded during patient registration are displayed.

  7. Click [Modify] to enter the financial details and record the payer details.

  8. Authorized users are only permitted to modify the financial details.

  9. Click [Save] or [Commit Form] to save the details. A confirmation message 'Do you want to Confirm?' is displayed. Click [Yes] to save the financial details and generate a pre-invoice for the payer or [No] to cancel the process.

  10. The charges are recalculated and bills are generated based on the policy coverage.

  11. Generate Pre-Invoice screen is displayed.

  12. Pre-invoice document number is generated based on the document setup.
