Re-Classify/Approve Past Encounters

From this function you can Re-Classify the financial details of a patient who is discharged from the facility. You can Query to view the patient records. You can Finalize the re-classified patient records.

Re-classify/Approve Past Encounters function is used to re-classify the Billing Group of the patient who is discharged from the facility. The new Billing Group selected for the patient has to be different from the old Billing Group. You have an option to exclude certain Clinics, such that the re-classification is not for visits to the excluded clinics.

 

[Clinic Exclusion] is enabled on selecting the check box ‘Future Visits’.

Re-Classifying Patient Details for Past Encounters

From the Financial Reclassification > Transaction, double-click Re-Classify/Approve Past Encounters Function.

The Re-classify/Approve Past Encounters screen gets displayed.

  1. Enter the patient ID or click [List of Values] to search for a specific patient record. The patient details get displayed. [Expl. Note] is enabled only if the explanatory details are associated to the patient.

  2. [Income/Asset Details] is enabled only if the current Classification Type for the patient is selected as Income/Asset.

  3. Enter the details in the mandatory (*) fields. <Related Field Description>

  4. Click [Commit Form] or press [F10] to save the details or [Clear Record] to clear the entered data.

  5. Click [Execute Query] or press [F8] to query the existing records.

  6. Click [Finalize] to finalize the reclassification for the respective patient. This button is enabled in query mode only after the details are recorded and saved.

  7. A confirmation message ‘Record Finalized and Saved’ is displayed on finalizing the re-classification details.
