Risk Factors Group

From this function you can Create a new Risk Factor Group by grouping it under a predefined values and Query for the desired factor records.

Risk Factor Group function helps the users to group all the risk factors to be considered for identifying a disease in patient. A Risk Factor Group contains a set of risk factors. Each risk factor is assigned a risk score internally, based on the risk indicator selected while defining a risk factor.

Creating a Risk Factor Group

From CA Code Setup > CA Health Risk Assessment Setup, select the Risk Factor Group function.

The Risk Factor Group screen gets displayed.

  1. Click [Create] to invoke the Create screen.

  2. Enter the details in the mandatory (*) fields. <Related Field Description>

  3. Click [Apply] to save the Risk Factor Group created or [Reset] to clear the entered data.

  4. Click [Query] to search for existing Risk Factor Group records.
