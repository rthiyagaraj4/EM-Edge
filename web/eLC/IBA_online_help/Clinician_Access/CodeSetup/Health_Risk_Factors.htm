Health Risk Factors

From this function you can Create a new Health Risk Factor by grouping it under a predefined values and Query for the desired factor records.

The Health Risk Factors function is used to define certain factors to arrive at a diagnosis. You can associate diseases applicable to a particular Risk Factor Type. It is mandatory to categorize the defined Health Risk Factor under any of the predefined values.

Creating a Health Risk Factor

From CA Code Setup > CA Health Risk Assessment Setup, select the Health Risk Factors function.

The Health Risk Factors screen gets displayed.

  1. Click [Create] to invoke the Create screen.

  2. Enter the details in the mandatory (*) fields. <Related Field Description>

  3. Click [Apply] to save the Health Risk Factors created or [Reset] to clear the entered data.

  4. Click [Query] to search for existing Factor records.
