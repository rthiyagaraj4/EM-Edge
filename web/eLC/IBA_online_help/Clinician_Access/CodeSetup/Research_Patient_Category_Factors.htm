Research Patient Category Factors

From this function you can Create a new factor and Query for an existing factor created.

Research Patient Category Factors function is used to define factors for research categories. You can define factors and associate it with the research categories. One factor can be associated with one or multiple research categories.

Creating Research Patient Category Factors

From the CA Code Setup > CA Others Setup, select Research Patient Category Factors function.

The Research Patient Category Factors screen gets displayed.

  1. Click [Create] to invoke the Create screen.

  2. Enter the details in the mandatory (*) fields. <Related Field Description>

  3. Click [Apply] to save the Research Patient Category Factors created or [Reset] to clear the entered data.

  4. Click [Query] to search for an existing record.
