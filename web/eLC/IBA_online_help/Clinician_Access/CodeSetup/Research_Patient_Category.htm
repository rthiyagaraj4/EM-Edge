Research Patient Category

From this function you can Create a new Research Patient Category and Query for an existing Research Patient Category created.

Research Patient Category function provides various research procedures being performed or studied in the facility, which are classified into categories. These categories are associated with a Patient History to inform other practitioners viewing the Patient Chart Summary that the Patient History is relevant for the displayed Research Category.

Creating Research Patient Category

From the CA Code Setup, select Research Patient Category function.

The Research Patient Category screen gets displayed.

  1. Click [Create] to invoke the Create screen.

  2. Enter the details in the mandatory (*) fields. <Related Field Description>

  3. Click [Apply] to save the Research Patient Category created or [Reset] to clear the entered data.

  4. Click [Query] to search for an existing record.
