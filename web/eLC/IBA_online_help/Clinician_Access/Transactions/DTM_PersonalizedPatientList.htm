Personalized Patient List

From this function, you can Add patient list to their personalized patient list to keep track of their patients and customize the list as required.

The function allows the practitioners to add patient list to their personalized patient list to keep track of their patients and customize the list as required. Same patients can be added to Personalized list by any practitioner. Personalized Patient List is customizable and it is practitioner specific.

Adding Patients to Personalized Patient List

To add patients to the Personalized Patient List, follow the below steps:

  1. First the Practitioner must select the patient to be part of personalized patient list from any patient list such as Recently Accessed list.

  2. Click the required patient name to open the Patient Chart.

  3. Click Add icon  from the patient banner. The patient record gets added in the Personalized Patient List. On mouse over on the Add icon, a tool tip Add to Personalized Patient List gets displayed.

The Add icon is viewable and enabled based on below criteria:

• If the patient is not added yet in Personalized Patient List

• If the Login practitioner shall be an Attending practitioner for the selected patient

• If the login practitioner has access rights to the selected patient location.

• The Add icon will not be displayed for registered Mortuary patients and  Suspended patients.

  1. Once the patient is added in the Personalized Patient List, the Icon will not be available in the menu bar and the selected patients shall be displayed in personalized patient list.

Viewing Patients in Personalized Patient List

From the CA Transactions > Clinician Access > Desktop Menu > Patient List, select Personalized Patient List.  

The Personalized Patient List screen gets displayed.

  1. By default, the active patients for the current week gets displayed.

  2. Select the Period [From and To Date} to view patients for that time period. By default, the current week gets displayed. This field is mandatory.

  3. The Patient details include Patient Name, Birth Date, Age, Gender, Patient ID and Added At date and time.  

  4. Click the Patient Name hyperlink to view the patient encounter details in a dialog.

  5. If you click the Encounter ID in the dialog, the Chart Summary of the patient gets displayed.

  6. To view the list of active or inactive patients click Active or Inactive option button as required.  By default, Active option is selected.

  7. You can remove already selected patient from the personalized list by selecting the check box against the patient record and click [Remove] button.

 

Viewing the encounter details for a selected patient in Personalized Patient List is based on the criteria given below:

• Login practitioner shall be an attending practitioner for selected patient.

• Selected patient location has the access rights to the login practitioner.

• Only active open encounters are displayed while clicking on selected patient in Personalized Patient List to view the encounter details.

• Discharged patient encounter details is not displayed and it gets displayed under Inactive status.

• Practitioner login facility is equal to patient added facility.
