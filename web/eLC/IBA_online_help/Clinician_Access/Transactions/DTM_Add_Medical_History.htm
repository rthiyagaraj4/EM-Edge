Add Medical History

This screen is used to add the medical history of a family member. The knowledge of diseases and conditions that occur within a family can give the health care team invaluable information that may aid in a swift, accurate diagnosis and treatment of health problems.

A medical genogram is helpful in determining patterns of disease or illness within a family. Standard medical symbols  are used in creating a genogram.

Medical  History related symbols used in Genograms:

 

Suspected Alcohol Abuse:   (Indicated for male & female)

 

 

Confirmed Alcohol Abuse:    (Indicated for male & female)

 

 

In Recovery Alcohol Abuse:   (Indicated for male & female)

 

Severe Psychological Problems: (Indicated for male & female)

 

 

Severe Physical Problems: (Indicated for male & female)

 

  1. Select the check box against the patient in the Family Members grid to add medical history details.

  2. Select the medical history type of the patient from the drop-down list. This is a mandatory field.

  3. Enter remarks if any.

  4. Click [Add]. The medical history gets added in the Medical History grid.

  5.  To remove the added medical history, select the check box against the medical history in the grid and click [Remove].

  6. The genogram gets refreshed once the medical history of the family members is added.
