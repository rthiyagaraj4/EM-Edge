Emergency Unit Status Board

From this function you can View patients based on beds or bays assigned to the patient.

This function is relevant for viewing emergency patients Assigned beds/bays in a treatment area. This function is also used to view unassigned patients who are then assigned to a bed/bay within a specific treatment area.

Viewing Patient List by Bed/Bay Chart

From the CA Transactions > Clinician Access > Desktop Menu > Location Status Board, select Patient List by Bed/Bay Chart.

The Emergency Unit Status Board screen gets displayed.

  1. The screen displays 2 tabs – Patient Management and Unassigned List. Patient Management Tab is displayed, by default. The Patient Management tab is used to view the status of the location.

  2. The search results display all Occupied Beds/Bays in the form of Bed/Bay Charts, by default.

  3. Enter the search criteria display patients based on the entered search criteria.

    1. Location – Select the Emergency Location from the list.

    2. Treatment Area – Select from Treatment Area from the list based on the selected Location.

    3. Patient ID – Enter the Patient ID in the alphanumeric format as defined in the MP Parameter or use [?] function to search and select the Patient ID.

    4. Practitioner – Enter the Practitioner name or click [?] function to search the practitioner name. Only those practitioners associated to the location and those having access rights to the location are listed in the search screen.

    5. Bed/Bay Status – Select the Bed/Bay status from the list. The values are Vacant, Occupied, Reserved and All. The system displays bays that are occupied, by default.

  4. The Bed/Bays are classified into 4 Priority Zones namely Critical, Semi-Critical, Non-Critical and Unassigned. These Zones are displayed as tabs. The Bed/Bays belonging to all the above zones can be viewed in the All tab.

  5. Click the required tabs to view patients occupying the Priority Zone.

  6. Click the Patient ID hyperlink to view the Patient Details screen.

  7. Click the required Chart to display the Assign Treatment Area screen if practitioner is not assigned. If [OK] is clicked without assigning Practitioner, Assign Relationship screen is displayed to assign relationship. If Practitioner is assigned in the Assign Treatment Area screen, the Patient Chart is displayed.

  8. Click Unassigned List tab to view the list of patients registered in the Emergency location but Bay or Practitioner is not assigned.

  9. The system displays the list of all the patients under the clinics of the location whose attendance is registered and assigned to the logged in practitioner, by default.

  10. Click Critical, Semi Critical, Non-Critical, Unassigned or All to view unassigned patients under the under them.

  11. Click the Patient Name hyperlink to display the Assign Treatment Area screen and record details. If [OK] is clicked without assigning Practitioner, Assign Relationship screen is displayed to assign relationship. If Practitioner is assigned in the Assign Treatment Area screen, the Patient Chart is displayed directly.

  12. Select the ‘Select’ check box against the patient record in the Unassigned tab to display the Current Location of the selected patient in the Location field. Click the Patient Management tab to display Vacant beds and assign the patient to the bed.

  13. Click [Refresh] to refresh the screen at any time.
