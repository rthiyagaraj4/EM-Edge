Assign Relationship

From this function you can Assign a Practitioner-Patient Relationship.

A Practitioner-Patient Relationship represents the nature of responsibility that lies with the practitioner against a patient. PP Relationships are assigned to allow various practitioners or care providers to access a patient chart either for viewing clinical details or for recording certain clinical events within the patient chart. A practitioner uses this function either to assign a relationship to self or to another practitioner. The type of relationship that is allowed for assigning and the person to whom relationship is being assigned is controlled based on access rights assigned to the logged in practitioner. The function not only allows establishing a relationship, but also allows terminating an existing relationship. This function is accessible if the logged in user has the privilege to perform these activities as defined in the ‘Application Tasks’ function or ‘Task for Responsibility/Relationship’ function.

Assigning Relationship

From the CA Transactions > Clinician Access > Desktop Menu > select required patient > Patient Chart Menu > Encounter Activities, select Assign Relationship.

The Assign Patient Provider Relationship screen gets displayed.

  1. Any Relationships recorded for the patient is displayed on the top part of the screen.

    The details displayed on the top half of the screen are grouped based on the kind of relationship, that is encounter or lifetime. The following details are displayed:

    1. Practitioner - The name of the practitioner to whom the relationship was assigned is displayed. The name of the practitioner assigning the relationship also is displayed, since the practitioner has to have an active relationship while assigning relationship to other practitioners. This is a hyperlink field.

    2. Relationship – The description of the relationship assigned is displayed. The relationships displayed may be manually assigned previously using the same function, that is, ‘Assign Relationship’ or those relationships that the system may have assigned for the attending/admitting practitioners.

    3. Start Date/Time - The start date and time of the relationship is displayed.

    4. End Date/Time – The end date and time of the relationship (if the relationship has expired) is displayed.

  2. Enter the details in the mandatory (*) fields. <Related Field Description>

  3. Click [Record] to save the Relationship assigned or [Clear] to clear the entered data. Click [Cancel] to exit from the screen.

  4. The details recorded are displayed on the top part of the screen. Click the Practitioner hyperlink to Terminate a Relation. The details of the Relationship are displayed in the respective fields. Select the Terminate Relation check box to terminate the selected relation based on the following condition.

    • ‘Can Terminate’ flag is set against the responsibility/relationship combination and the user is logged in with the same responsibility.

    • The practitioner trying to terminate the relationship has assigned the relationship to the other practitioner for whom the relationship is being terminated.

  5. Click [Record] to save termination.
