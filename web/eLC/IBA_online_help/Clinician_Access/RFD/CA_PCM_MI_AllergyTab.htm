Enter the following Allergy details:

 

  1. Vaccine Name – The code of the selected Vaccine is displayed and disabled.

  2. Reaction Date – The Reaction Date is displayed as entered while recording Allergy. Enter the Reaction Date and Time in the format of dd/mm/yyyy hh:mm or select the required date and time using the Calendar function. This is a mandatory field.

  3. Reaction – Select the Reaction from the list. The Reactions defined in the Reaction function of Application Masters module are displayed. This is a mandatory field.

  4. Remarks – Enter the remarks in alphanumeric format of 500 characters maximum length.

  5. Treatment – Enter the Treatment details in alphanumeric format of 500 characters maximum length.

  6. Click [Record] to record the Allergy details or [Clear] to reset the entered data.

  7. On recording the Allergy details, the Reaction and Reaction Date are displayed in the left hand side of the Allergy details tab.

  8. The icon is displayed against the Vaccine name on recording the Allergy details. Click the icon to view the Allergy details recorded.

  9. Click Reaction hyperlink to modify the Remarks and Treatment details recorded for the Vaccine.
