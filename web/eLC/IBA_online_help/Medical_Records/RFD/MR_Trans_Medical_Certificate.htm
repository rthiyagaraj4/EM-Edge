Enter the following details:

 

  1. Certificate Type - Select the value as either 'Medical' or 'Others' from the list. On selecting 'Medical', the Employment Details are displayed as recorded at the bottom of the screen. This field is mandatory.

  2. Medical Certificate No. - Enter the Medical Certificate number in alphanumeric format of 20 characters maximum length. This field is mandatory.

  3. Leave From - The Admitted Date and Time of the patient is displayed.

  4. To - Enter the date of discharge or select the Date and Time using the Calendar function. The current system Date and Time is displayed, by default. This field is mandatory.

  5. No. of Days - The total number of days of the leave period is displayed. This value is displayed by taking the difference of 'Leave To' and 'Leave from' date.

  6. Resume Activity - Select the check box to indicate that the patient is fit to resume the activity. This field is selected, by default.

  7. Date - Enter the date when the patient resumes the activity, or select it using the calendar function. This field is enabled only if the 'Resume Activity' field is selected and the Date entered has to be greater than the 'Leave To' date. The very next date of the date mentioned in the 'Leave To' field is displayed, by default.

  8. Certificate Issued - Select the check box to indicate that the Certificate is issued to the discharged patient.

  9. Issued Date - Enter the date when the certificate is issued, or select it using the Calendar function. This field is enabled on selecting the 'Certificate Issued' field.

  10. Authorized By - Select the name of the practitioner who authorized the Certificate from [?] function. The treating Practitioner is displayed, by default. This field is mandatory.

  11. Remarks - Enter the Remarks in alphanumeric format of 500 characters maximum length. This field is mandatory.
