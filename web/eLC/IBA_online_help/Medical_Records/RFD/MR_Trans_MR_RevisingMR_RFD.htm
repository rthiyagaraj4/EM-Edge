Enter the following details:

 

  1. Collect Date - The Collect date as recorded in the 'Status of Medical Report Request' screen is displayed and disabled.

  2. Revised Collect Date - Enter a revise date on which the Medical Report can be collected. This field is mandatory.

  3. Authorized by - Enter the name of the Practitioner who authorized the revision of the collection date for the Medical Report or select the name from the [?] function. This field is mandatory.

Click [Previous Delivered Medical Report] in the Medical Report Request screen to view the details of Medical Reports already delivered for the patient.

If the collect date is revised using this function, the revised collect date is displayed on all the screens at any stage in preparing the Medical Report.
