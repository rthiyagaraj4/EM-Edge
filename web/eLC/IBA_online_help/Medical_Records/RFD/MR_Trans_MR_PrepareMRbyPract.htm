Prepare Medical Report by Practitioner

From the MR Transactions > Medical Reports, select the Status tab.

The Status tab of the Medical Report screen gets displayed.

  1. Select the Medical Report Status as ‘Forwarded to Practitioner’ and click [Search] to display all Medical Report Requests forwarded or enter the search criteria and click [Search] to select the required forwarded Medical Report Request.

  2. Click the Request ID hyperlink to invoke ‘Prepare Medical Report’ screen.

  3. You can only view details recorded while Forwarding the Medical Report, Encounter Details, Date Details, Charges Details, Applicant Address and Previous Delivered Medical Reports of the patient.

    1. Report Type - The type of report as selected in the 'Forward Medical Report Request' screen is displayed and disabled.

    2. Department/Specialty - The Department or Specialty as selected in the 'Forward Medical Report Request' screen is displayed and disabled.

    3. Remarks - The Remarks are displayed as recorded which can be updated.

    4. Collect Date - The Collect Date as recorded in the 'Status of Medical Report Request' screen is displayed.

    5. Forwarded Practitioner - The name of the Practitioner to whom the request is forwarded as selected in the 'Forward Medical Report Request' screen is displayed. This field remains disabled when invoked from MR module but it is enabled when invoked from CA module so as to allow you to change the practitioner name.  

    6. Prepare Date - The current system date and time is displayed as the date when the medical report is prepared, by default. This field is mandatory.

    7. Prepared by Practitioner - The Forwarded Practitioner is displayed as the practitioner who will prepare the report, by default. This field is mandatory.

    8. Requested Practitioner - The name of the practitioner who requested for the medical report of the patient is displayed as read only text.

    9. Forwarded HOD/Co-ordinator - The name of the HOD/Coordinator to whom the request is forwarded is displayed.

    10. Required Documents – The list of required documents are displayed.

    11. Check List – Click [Check List] to view the check list details

  4. Click [Record] and an alert message is displayed if the report is not prepared in the Prepare Medical Report function of Clinician Access module or click [Close] to exit the ‘Prepare Medical Report’ screen.
