Medical Board

From this function you can Record a request for a medical report from the Medical Board, process the request and deliver the report to the requester.

This function is used to record the activities of the medical board. A Medical Board consists of a team of practitioners who discuss on patient’s Medical Report and take decisions whether the patient has to be shifted to another facility for further treatment etc. A medical report is generated based on a request from the Medical Board. The medical board can generate a medical report for a patient who is not registered to any of the facilities of the enterprise. The different activities of the Medical Board function helps you to record requests, prepare medical report and issue the medical report to various requesters.

 

The various steps to request, prepare and deliver a medical report by Medical Board are as follows:

• Medical Board Request

• Record status of Medical Board Request

• Record formation of Medical Board

• Forward Medical Board Request to Practitioner

• Book Appointment

• Confirm Appointment

• Prepare Medical Report by Board

• Receive prepared Medical Report

• Deliver completed Medical Report by Medical Board

• View the completed delivered Medical Report

• Revise Medical Report

• Pending Medical Reports for Delivery by Medical Board

• Display All Medical Board Requests
