Holding Area

From this function, you can Create a Holding Area and Query for existing Holding Area.

A Holding Area is location where patients are temporarily held before moving to the operating room. Pre-operation activities like verifying the pre-operative checklists, recording the Pre-Anesthesia details, etc. are performed in the holding areas.

Creating Holding Areas

From the OT Admin Setup > Operating Room, select the Holding Area function.

The Holding Area screen gets displayed.

  1. Click [Create] to invoke the Create screen.

  2. Enter the details in the mandatory (*) fields. <Related Field Description>

  3. Click [Apply] to save the created Holding Area or click [Reset] to clear the entered data.

  4. Click [Query] to search for an existing Holding Area records.
