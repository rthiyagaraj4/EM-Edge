Queue Series

From this function you can Define a Queue Series applicable to Walk-in Patients, Referral Patients and patients with Appointments. You can Query for a desired Queue Series record.

Clinics adopt a queue system to manage patients based on a queue number with a prefix added to each department. This queue number is available to the Patient (either in the generated outputs or in the form of a token). This is used by the facility staff to call out patients by the number which helps in streamlining patient flow in the departments. A Queue Prefix and reset number is defined for each series. The queue number is generated for a Clinic/Practitioner or a Clinic. The queue number is initialized from one at the start of a day, by default. Queue Series is an optional setup used under situations like re-initializing the number more frequently within a day and starting with an initial number other than 1.

Defining Queue Series

From the OP Admin Setup, select the Queue Series function.

The Queue Series Screen gets displayed.

  1. Click [Create] to invoke the Create screen.

  2. Enter the details in the mandatory (*) fields. <Related Field Description>

  3. Click [Apply] to save the Queue Series created or [Reset] to clear the entered data.

  4. Click [Query] to search for existing Queue Series.
