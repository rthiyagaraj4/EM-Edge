Record Vital Signs

The Record Vital Signs function is used to record the Vital Signs of a Patient. Vital signs are set in the OP Parameter, Clinic or Visit Type for Clinic functions. The Vital Signs can be recorded any number of times.

  1. Click Record Vital Signs option from the queue status to display the Record Vital Signs screen.

  2. Enter the value appropriate to the Vital Signs variable.

  3. Click [Record] to record the Vital Signs, [Clear] to clear the entered data or [Cancel] to exit from the screen.
