Appointment List Tab

Appointment List tab is applicable only when the Appointment Scheduling module is installed. This tab lists out patients with Appointment for which visit is not yet registered.

  1. Click [Book Appointment] button to book an appointment for a patient by generating an Appointment Number.

  2. The Appointment Number is displayed while searching the patient in the Appointment List Tab.

  3. Click [Search] to display patient records with Appointment number. Alternately, enter the query criteria to search for a specific patient with an appointment. <Related Field Description>  

  4. Click + hyperlink to view the appointment details.

    Appointment Details

    1. Appt Ref No – Displays the appointment reference number generated while booking an appointment.

    2. Appt Date – Displays the appointment date recorded at the time of scheduling the appointment.

    3. Appt Time – Displays the Appointment Time of the patient.

    4. Location – Displays the Location Name.

    5. Visit Type – Displays the type of visit whether it’s a first time, follow up visit or routine visit.

    6. Resource – Displays the Primary Resource Name against whom the patient has been booked for appointment.

    Patient Details

    7. Patient ID – Displays the Patient ID of the patient if it has been recorded at the time of scheduling the appointment.

    8. Name – Displays the Patient Name.

    9. Gender – Displays the gender of the patient.

    10. Age – Displays the age of the patient recorded at the time of scheduling the appointment.

    11. Date of Birth – Displays the date of birth of the patient.

    12. PMI – Displays the PMI if the patient is a registered patient else this will be blank.

    13. Nationality – Displays the nationality of the patient if it has been recorded at the time of scheduling the appointment.

    14. Birth Place – Displays the birth place of the patient if it has been recorded at the time of scheduling the appointment.

    15. Race – Displays the race of the patient has recorded at the time of scheduling the appointment.

    16. Ethnicity – Displays the ethnicity corresponding to the particular race recorded at the time of scheduling the appointment

    17. Residency – Displays the residency of the patient.

    18. Status – Displays the status of the patient.

    Other Details

    19. Contact Reason – Displays the contact reason recorded at the time of scheduling the appointment.

    20. Remarks – Displays the remarks if it has been recorded at the time of appointment.

    21. Contact Mode – Displays the contact mode.

    22. Recorded By – Displays the name of the person who recorded the details.

    23. Recorded On – The date/time of the details recorded will be displayed.

  5. Click ‘Appointment Number’ hyperlink to populate patient details in Add Visit Screen.

  6. If a patient is not registered within the facility while booking an appointment then on selecting the patient record for registering a visit, ‘Enter Patient ID’ dialog is displayed to register the patient.

  7. Click [Register Patient] and register the patient.

  8. The details of the patient are populated in Add Visit Screen.
