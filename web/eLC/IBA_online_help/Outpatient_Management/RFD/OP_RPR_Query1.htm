Query

  1. Click [Query] to display the Referral Search screen.

  2. Enter the details in the mandatory (*) fields. <Related Field Description>  

  3. Click [Search] to view the referral patient details or [Clear] to clear the displayed data. Click [More Criteria] to display more patient details tabs. Click [Close] to close the screen.

  4. Click Referral ID hyperlink of the selected record, the Register Patient Referral screen gets displayed with the selected patient details.

    The Patient Details recorded while registering the referral are displayed. The Referred From and Referred To details as recorded while registering the referral are displayed. The Follow-up/Rejected section is displayed for the 'Old Referrals'.

     

    Follow-up / Rejected

    1. Action – Select the Action from the list. The system-defined values are ‘Follow-up’ and ‘Rejected’. If the Status is ‘Closed’ only ‘Follow-up’ is listed. If the Status is ‘Open’ both ‘Follow-up’ and ‘Rejected’ are listed.

    2. Reason – The field is enabled when an Action is selected. Enter the Reason in alphanumeric format of 40 characters maximum length or select the Reason using [?] function. The Reasons are defined in ‘Reason’ function of System Configuration module.

    3. Reference – Enter the Reference Number in alphanumeric format of 20 characters maximum length.

    4. Remarks – Enter the Remarks in alphanumeric format of 30 characters maximum length.

    5. Date/Time – The Date and Time recorded for the Action is displayed.

    6. Action – The recorded Action is displayed.

    7. Reason – The recorded Reason is displayed.

    8. Reference – The recorded Reference is displayed.

    9. Remarks – The recorded Remarks is displayed.

    10. Action By – The name of the logged-in-user is displayed.

  5. Update the details and click [Apply] to register the patient as referral.

  6. List of internal reports defined as in print routing setup is displayed. Click [OK] to print the internal reports or [Cancel] to cancel the printing. The Referral Registration number is generated.
