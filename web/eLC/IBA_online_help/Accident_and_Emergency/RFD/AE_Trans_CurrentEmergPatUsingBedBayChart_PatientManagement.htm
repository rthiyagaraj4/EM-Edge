Patient Management

By default, Patient Management tab displays all bed statuses that are ‘Occupied’ and patients occupying them.

  1. The beds or bays within a Treatment Area are displayed in cells and are uniquely colored to indicate the status. Enter the query criteria to view the required patient in bed or bay. The beds are sorted based on the priority zone under the following sub tabs, namely, Critical, Semi-Critical, Non-Critical, Unassigned and All. <Related Field Description>

    The status of each bed/bay is represented using a color indicator as background against the bed/bay. The beds/bays within a treatment area are displayed in cells and are uniquely colored to indicate the status. The color codes represent the following status:

    • - Vacant Normal Bed/Bay

    • - Occupied Normal Bed/Bay

    • - Reserved Normal Bed/Bay

    • - Vacant Spare Bed/Bay

    • - Occupied Spare Bed/Bay

  2. Click [Refresh] to refresh the records or re-query the screen manually. By default, this screen is refreshed automatically based on the Refresh Interval value set in AE Parameter function.

  3. Click the occupied bed or bay cell to perform queue management functions and click Patient ID hyperlink to view patient details as recorded while registering attendance. The following functions can be performed on clicking the occupied bed or bay cell.

Assign Treatment Area

  1. If a Treatment Area is not assigned to a patient while registering the attendance, the bed within a Treatment Area is assigned in the Unassigned List Tab. The Assign Treatment Area screen gets displayed on selecting the Patient Details cell from Current Emergency Patients using Bed/Bay Chart screen.

  2. Enter the details in the mandatory (*) fields. <Related Field Description>

  3. Click [OK] to assign a Treatment Area to the patient or [Cancel] to exit from the screen.

  4. Visit Charge Details screen gets displayed on successfully assigning the Treatment Area. Click [Continue] to proceed or [Revise] to revise charge details.

  5. If the patient is assigned to a Treatment Area of any other Priority Zone, the patient is available under that Priority Zone tab. Click the respective tab and select Patient Details hyperlink to Reassign Treatment Area.

  6. Click the Occupied bed or bay cell of the patient and Assign Relationship screen gets displayed to assign a relationship with the practitioner.

 

Notes

When you click Assign Treatment area or Reassign Treatment area options, and the patient has any unsettled or unbilled due, the following actions are expected based on based on selection of the value in Action when unsettled/unbilled amount for the encounter drop-down list in AE Parameter setup..

• No action – The system will not display any warning or error, and you can proceed with the Assign or Reassign Treatment area activities for the patient without interruption.

• Warning – The system will display a warning message “Unsettled/Unbilled amount exists for the encounter. Do you want to continue?” when assigning or reassigning treatment area for the patient. You can click OK and still proceed with the activities.

• Error – The system will display an error message, “Unsettled/Unbilled amount exists for the encounter. Cannot proceed.” You cannot assign or reassign treatment area for the patient.

 

Assign Relationship

  1. Once a Treatment Area is assigned to a patient, the Assign Relationship screen gets displayed on selecting Occupied bed or bay cell of the required patient from Current Emergency Patients using Bed/Bay Chart screen.

  2. Enter the details in mandatory (*) fields. <Related Field Description>

  3. Patient Chart in Clinician Access module gets displayed. You can navigate into Clinician Access module from the Accident and Emergency module. All functions like ED Assessments, Encounter Activities and Discharge Activities are performed from Clinician Access module.

After assigning a practitioner for the patient, the Clinician Access chart menu is displayed. You are able to perform the following functions from the Clinician Access Chart menu:

Primary Assessment

Trauma Assessment

Secondary Triage

Check Out/Close Consultation
