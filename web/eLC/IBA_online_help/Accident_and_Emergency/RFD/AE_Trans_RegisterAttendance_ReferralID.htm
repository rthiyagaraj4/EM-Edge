Referral ID

  1. Enter the Referral ID or click the Referral ID [?] function to search for the referral patient. The Referral Search screen gets displayed.

  2. If required patient is not available while searching, click [Register Patient Referral] and the Register Patient Referral screen gets displayed to register the patient.

  3. Click [Apply] and control shifts to the Register Attendance screen.

  4. The Referral ID generated for the patient is displayed in the Referral ID field, which is disabled.

  5. If the referral patient with a Referral ID is not registered in the facility, the ‘Enter Patient ID’ screen gets displayed.

  6. Click [Register Patient] and the Register Patient screen gets displayed to register the patient in the facility.

  7. The Patient ID generated for the referral patient is displayed in the Patient ID field and is disabled.

  8. The patient details are displayed as entered while registering.

  9. Enter the mandatory details in the Tab pages. The Register Attendance screen comprises of four tabs, namely, 'Visit Detail’, ‘Arrival’, ‘Related Contacts’ and ‘Financial Details’. <Related Field Description>
