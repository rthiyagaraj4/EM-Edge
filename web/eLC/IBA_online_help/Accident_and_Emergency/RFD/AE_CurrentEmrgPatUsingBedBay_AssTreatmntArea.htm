Enter the following details:

 

  1. Priority Zone – The Priority Zone assigned to the patient is displayed.

  2. Treatment Area – The Treatment Area assigned to the patient is displayed.

  3. Service - The Service against which the patient is registered to the Emergency Department is displayed.

  4. Bed/Bay No – The bed/bay assigned to the patient is displayed. Enter a valid Bed/Bay number that belongs to the Treatment Area. Use [?] function to view the bed/bay availability chart and to select the desired bed/bay. This is a mandatory field.

  5. Practitioner - Select the required Practitioner using [?] function. Practitioner lookup screen gets displayed and on entering appropriate search criteria you can select the required practitioner. The practitioners associated to the selected Treatment Area are displayed. This field is mandatory.

  6. Date/Time – The Date and Time details are displayed and the field is disabled.
