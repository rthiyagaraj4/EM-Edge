Enter the following details:

 

  1. Priority Zone – Select a Priority Zone from the list of valid values. The Priority Zone is displayed if it was assigned to the patient during registration.

  2. Treatment Area – Select a Treatment Area from the list of valid values. The Treatment Area assigned to the patient during registration is displayed.  

  3. Service - The Service against which the patient is registered to the Emergency Department is displayed.

  4. Bed/Bay No – Enter a valid Bed/Bay Number that belongs to the treatment area selected. Use the [?] function to view the bed/bay availability chart and to select the desired Bed/Bay. This is a mandatory field.

  5. Practitioner - Select the required Practitioner using [?] function. Practitioner lookup screen is displayed. Enter appropriate search criteria and select a relevant practitioner. The practitioner associated to the selected Treatment Area gets listed. This field is mandatory.

  6. Date/Time – The Date and Time is defaulted to system date and time.
