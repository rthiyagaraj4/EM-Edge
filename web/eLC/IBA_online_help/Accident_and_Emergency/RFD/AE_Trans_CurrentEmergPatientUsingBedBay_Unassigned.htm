Unassigned List

This tab displays all the patients not assigned to any Bed/Bay. It is possible to select a patient from the unassigned list and assign a bed or bay within a treatment area.

  1. Click Unassigned tab to view a list of patients who are not assigned to the treatment area or enter the query criteria to select a required patient not assigned to a bed/bay within a treatment area.

    1. Select – Select the check box against the required patient to assign a bed/bay in the Treatment Area. Click Patient Management Tab to display all beds and assign a vacant bed to the selected patient. Select a vacant bed or bay within a Treatment Area by clicking the required cell. A message is displayed prompting whether the selected bed needs to be assigned to the patient. Click [OK] to assign the bed to the patient or click [Cancel] to cancel assigning the bed. The patient is now displayed in the cell under the assigned Treatment Area in the Patient Management Tab.

    2. Date/Time In – The date and time of arrival of the patient at the Emergency Department is displayed.

    3. Patient Name – The Patient Name is displayed as a hyperlink.

    4. Patient ID – The Patient ID is displayed.

    5. Wait Time – The Waiting Time of the patient from time of registration to the time of assigning a Treatment Area is displayed.

    6. Current Location – The Location Type and the Care Location where the patient currently belongs is displayed.

  2. Select a patient from the queue by selecting the Select check box against the required patient. Only one patient can be selected at a time.

  3. On selecting the required patient, click Patient Management Tab. System lists out the beds/bays that are vacant in that Treatment Area. Click desired vacant bed or bay cell. The system prompts you to confirm assigning of bed or bay to the patient.

  4. Click [OK] to confirm assigning bed or bay or click [Cancel] to abort the process.

  5. Click Patient Name hyperlink in the Unassigned List Tab to perform following function.

Assign Treatment Area

  1. If a Treatment Area is not assigned to a patient while registering the attendance, the bed within a Treatment Area is assigned in the Unassigned List Tab. The Assign Treatment Area screen gets displayed on selecting the Patient Details cell from Current Emergency Patients using Bed/Bay Chart screen.

  2. Enter the details in the mandatory (*) fields. <Related Field Description>

  3. Click [OK] to assign a Treatment Area to the patient or [Cancel] to exit from the screen.

  4. Visit Charge Details screen gets displayed on successfully assigning the Treatment Area. Click [Continue] to proceed or [Revise] to revise charge details.

  5. If the patient is assigned to a Treatment Area of any other Priority Zone, the patient is available under that Priority Zone tab. Click the respective tab and select Patient Details hyperlink to Reassign Treatment Area.

  6. Click the Occupied bed or bay cell of the patient and Assign Relationship screen gets displayed to assign a relationship with the practitioner.

 

Assign Relationship

  1. Once a Treatment Area is assigned to a patient, the Assign Relationship screen gets displayed on selecting Occupied bed or bay cell of the required patient from Current Emergency Patients using Bed/Bay Chart screen.

  2. Enter the details in mandatory (*) fields. <Related Field Description>

  3. Patient Chart in Clinician Access module gets displayed. You can navigate into Clinician Access module from the Accident and Emergency module. All functions like ED Assessments, Encounter Activities and Discharge Activities are performed from Clinician Access module.

 

After assigning a practitioner for the patient, click the Patient ID hyperlink to display the Clinician Access chart menu. You are able to perform the following functions from the Clinician Access Chart menu:

Primary Assessment

Trauma Assessment

Secondary Triage

Check Out/Close Consultation
