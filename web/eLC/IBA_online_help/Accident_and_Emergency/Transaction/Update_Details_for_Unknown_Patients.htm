Update Details for Unknown Patients

From this function you can Update the details of an Unknown Patient.

During an emergency patient registration or disaster registration, the patient is considered as unknown with minimum details captured. The details recorded during patient registration are updated and retained for further use. These patients may have visited the facility and has got a Patient ID, but the patient is unable to recall the Patient ID. Therefore, patient is registered with a new patient ID. Patient now has two different Patient IDs. Using this function duplicate records are merged into a single record and maintained.

 

Process Flow Diagram

Updating details for Unknown Patients

From the AE Transactions, select the Update Details for Unknown Patients Function.

The Update Details for Unknown Patients screen gets displayed.

  1. Enter the ‘Patient ID’ if known under Existing Details or click [?] function to search for the required Unknown Patient ID. Patient Search screen gets displayed. On selecting the Unknown Patient ID, the patient details are displayed. This field is mandatory.

  2. Enter the ‘Patient ID’ if known under New Details or click [?] function to search for the required Patient. Patient Search screen gets displayed. On selecting the Patient ID, the patient details are displayed. This field is mandatory.

  3. Click [Start Now] to start the merger process immediately and the process gets submitted. A confirmation message appears and the merge process begins automatically.

  4. Click [Start Later] to begin merge process during the specified date and time.

  5. Enter the Date and Time using the Calendar function.  Click [Start Later] and the merge process begins during the specified date and time.

  6. Click [Apply] to merge the patient details or [Reset] to clear the entered data.

 

On invoking the Update Details for Unknown Patients function from the menu, if the DR Cycle is not closed, a message ‘The DR Process Cycle is already in progress’ is displayed. Set the user rights to close the administer search process in Duplicate Registration module and abort the administer search process to invoke the Update Details for Unknown Patients function.
