Anesthesia History

Anaesthesia History is used to view history of anaesthesia prescribed to the selected patient.

Enter the following details:

  1. Anaesthetist – Select the anaesthetist using [?] function. The listed values are created in ‘Application Masters’ module.

  2. Specialty – Select the specialty using [?] function. The listed values are created in ‘Application Masters’ module.

  3. Date [From-To] – Select [From-To] date from the calendar function. To display the Anesthesia History for the selected date range.

  4. Click [Search] to search the details for the entered criteria. Click [Clear] to clear the entered criteria.

  5. Patient ID – Patient identification number is displayed.

  6. Patient Name – The name of the patient is displayed.

  7. Gender – The gender of the patient is displayed.

  8. Procedure – The name of the procedure is displayed.

  9. Surgery Date – The date of the surgery is displayed.

  10. Surgeon – The name of the surgeon is displayed.

  11. Anaesthetist – The name of the Anaesthetist is displayed.

  12. Details – Click ‘Details’ hyperlink to view the details of the Anaesthesia.

  13. Notes – Click ‘Notes’ hyperlink to view the notes entered by the Anaesthetist.

The Statues of the Anaesthesia is displayed against the patient details. The following statues are Outstanding, Pre Operative Details Record, Intra Operative Details Record, Post Operative Details Record and Finalized.
