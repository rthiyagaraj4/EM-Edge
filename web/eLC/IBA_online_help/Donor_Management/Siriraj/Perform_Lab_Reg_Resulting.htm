Perform Lab Reg/Resulting

From this function you can Register the specimen and Record result of the test performed.

The Perform Lab Reg/Resulting function is used to register the specimens sent for screening. You can also record the result of the tests completed the screening process. The register specimen option in the Specimen List section invokes the Blood Donor Outstanding Orders screen. The Enter Result option invokes the Result Entry by Specimen Screen, where you can enter the result. The Second Level Review option invokes the Second Level Review Screen to perform the Second Level Review.

The color code associated to the unit number denotes the status of the unit.

– Specimen not Registered

- Result Outstanding

– First Level Review

– Second Level Review [Abnormal Results]

– Second Level Review

Performing Lab Reg/Resulting

From the Transactions > Blood Unit Management, double-click Perform Lab Reg/Resulting function.

The Perform Lab Reg/Resulting screen gets displayed.

  1. Click the Calendar function to select the Donation Date range. System displays the Donation Dates for the range of a month, by default.

  2. Click [List of Values] to select a range of registered Unit Numbers to register the specimens sent for screening.

  3. Click [Display] to view the blood unit details or [Clear] to clear the entered details.

  4. Click the required blood unit to view the specimen list details.

  5. Click [Register Specimen] to register Specimen. The Blood Donor Outstanding Orders screen is displayed. The screen displays all the specimens waiting for registration. On registering the specimen, the specimen number is displayed against the specimen and the label of [Register Specimen] is changed to [Enter Results].

  6. Click [Enter Results] to record the screening results. The Result Entry by Specimen screen is displayed.

  7. Click [Second Level Review] to send the blood unit for second level review. The Second Level Review screen is displayed.
