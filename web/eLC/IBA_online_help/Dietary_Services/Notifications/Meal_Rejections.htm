Meal Rejections

From this function you can View list of Meals Rejected in ’Document/Acknowledge Meal' screen.

The Meal Rejections function is used to display notifications every time a rejection is registered in the ’Document/Acknowledge Meal’ screen. You can acknowledge the status of the rejected meal record.

Viewing Meal Rejection

From the Notifications, select the Meal Rejection function.

The Meal Rejection screen gets displayed.

  1. Enter the details in the respective fields. <Related Field Description>

  2. Click [Search] to display list of meals rejected in ’Document/Acknowledge Meal’.  

  3. The search details include Serving Date, Patient ID, Patient Name, Patient Class, Location, Meal Type, Meal Status, Complaints, Rejection Date & Time, Food Replacement, Acknowledged By, Acknowledged Date and Time.

  4. To acknowledge a meal rejection, select  ‘Yes’ value  from the Acknowledge from drop-down list against the required record. By default, the value ‘No’ is selected.

  5. Click [Apply] to save the Acknowledgement status of Meal Rejection or [Reset] to clear the entered data.

  6. On saving, the Acknowledge column with ‘Yes’ value gets disabled. The Acknowledged By and Acknowledged Date and time gets displayed in the respective columns. Also, the status of the Food Replacement column is changed from No to Yes.
