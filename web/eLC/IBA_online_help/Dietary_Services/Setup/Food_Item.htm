Food Item

From this function you can Define a Food Item that is prepared in the Kitchen and served to the inpatients or staffs in the facility.  You can Query for the existing Food Items.

The Food Item function helps in recording the various food items that are prepared and delivered from the kitchen as part of the Routine Meal or as a Special Food Item. A Food Item can be associated to a Nutrient to identify it as a nutritious diet. The dietitian can further examine the Food Items while placing a diet order for the patient. This function maintains the information on nutritious food items used for menu definition.

Defining a Food Item

From the Setup, select the Food Item function.

The screen gets displayed with the Food Item tab enabled.

  1. Click [Create] to invoke the Create screen.

  2. Enter the details in the mandatory (*) fields under the following tabs:

Food Item

Nutrient

  1. Click [Apply] to save a created Food Item or [Reset] to clear the entered details.

  2. Click [Query] to search for the existing records.
