  1. Existing Item – Click [?] to select the existing Items defined in the masters. This field is mandatory.

  2. New Item - Click [?] to select the new items defined in the masters. This field is mandatory.

  5. Click ‘Clear Filter’ hyperlink to clear the selection made.
