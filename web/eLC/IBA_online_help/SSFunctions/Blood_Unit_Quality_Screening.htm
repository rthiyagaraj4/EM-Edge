Blood Unit Quality Screening

From this function you can Send the blood products and reagents for quality screening and View pre-defined quality tests. You can also Print the list of reagents sent for quality tests.

The Blood Unit Quality Screening function is used to send blood products and reagents that are already added to the stock for quality tests. You can view pre-defined quality screening tests of selected products through this function. The Quality screening is done based on the tests defined in the Quality Screening Test Setup function. When you query, system lists the blood units that are outstanding and not expired. You can also print the list of reagents sent for quality tests after the screening is performed. The quality screening is a random check performed on the blood units added to the stock for verifying quality of blood units.

Screening Blood Products or Reagents Quality

From the Transactions > Blood Unit Management, double-click Blood Unit Quality Screening function.

The Blood Unit Quality Screening screen gets displayed.

  1. Enter the query criteria to search for the blood units that are already added to stock. <Related Field Description>

  2. Click [Display] to view a list of Blood Products or Reagents satisfying the query criteria.

  3. The query results are displayed under the following tabs:

Products for Screening

Reagents for Screening

  1. Click [Commit Form] or press [F10] to save the records or click [Clear Record] to clear the records.
