Staff Profile

From this function you can Query the profile of staffs.

The function helps in querying staff details like identity, qualification, training and contact details that were recorded while creating the staff in the Application Masters module. It also queries the skill, experience, training and licensure details of the staffs that were recorded while creating the staff profile in the Staff Profile function.

Querying for Staff Profile

From RS Queries, select Staff Profile function.

The Staff Profile screen gets displayed.

  1. Enter the details in the mandatory (*) fields. <Related Field Description>

  2. Click [Show Details]. The details of the staff including Identity, Contact no./addr, qualification and training details are displayed.

  3. Click [Show Profile]. The details of Experience, Skill, Licensure and Training can be viewed. Remarks of Experience, Training and Licensure are hyperlinked.
