Manage Work Schedule

From this function, you can Manage a generated Work Schedule.

You can do the following as activities on the queried work schedule:

- Allocate a shift to a staff on an adhoc basis. This feature is also used where requirement is identified on a daily basis and allocation has to be done just a day in advance or right before the start of the shift

- Changing a shift based on staff member's request

- Swapping two staff members within/across workplaces

- Transferring a staff member from one workplace to another workplace based on need

- Assigning Overtime to a staff member

- Based on a staff member's request marking the shifts on a particular day as Leave

- Cancel a shift already assigned to a staff member

- Cancel Leave recorded for a staff

- View all the work schedules of a staff

You can perform the above listed activities by clicking the cells in the staff related rows from the generated work schedule. Depending on the status of the cell, an appropriate popup menu is displayed. Using this function, you can perform activities in a sequence as activity by activity, and not for a date range.

Managing Work Schedule

From RS Transactions, select Manage Work Schedule function.

The Manage Work Schedule screen gets displayed.

  1. Enter the details in the mandatory (*) fields. <Related Field Description>

  2. Click [Show Details].

  3. The screen displays the work schedule generated for 14 days. Use the button to view the work schedule for the next 14 days till the specified end date. Use the button to return to the work schedule generated for the first 14 days. The month and the year are also displayed.

  4. Based on the schedule generated for the workplace in "Generate Work Schedule" function, the screen displays the work schedule for Working shifts, On-call shifts and Unproductive shifts. The number of staffs scheduled and the total staff requirement against each shift and days of the week are displayed.

  5. By default the system displays only the positions selected for the schedule. Click the + hyperlink to view the work schedule of the staffs under the displayed position for each day of the week.

  6. The total staff requirement and the number of staffs scheduled against each shift and days of the week are displayed for the position.

  7. The shift details generated for the staff in "Generate Work Schedule" function is displayed against the staff name for each day of the week. The shift details of the staff are displayed in the mnemonic codes of the shift. A particular staff can work in more than one shift and workplace in a day.

  8. Cells with a red border show that the staff is also assigned to an external workplace on the same day other than the current workplace.

  9. Empty cells with an icon show that the staff is not working for the day or the staff is on Leave for the day. The Leave record for a staff can be recorded in "Record Staff Unavailability" function of transactions.

  10. Click the cell against the staff name and day of the week. Depending on the status of the cell, a popup menu is displayed with relevant options. The clicked cell turns blue in color indicating that the activities displayed in the menu can be performed for that particular staff for the day.

Allocate - Allocate a shift to the staff.

Change - Change the shift of a staff within the workplace.

Swap - Swap two staffs of same position within or across workplaces.

Transfer - Transfer a staff from one workplace to another workplace for the same position and shift.

Over Time - Assign over time to the staff.

Leave - Record leave for the staff on a particular day.

Cancel - Cancel a shift already scheduled for a staff.

Cancel Leave - Cancel Leave recorded for a Staff.

View Schedule - To view the work scheduled to the staff on a particular day.

  1. All the activities are active to current shifts and future shifts. If any activity displayed in the menu is in bold, it indicates that the activity can be performed and if the activity displayed in the menu is not bold, it indicates that the activity is not applicable to the selected cell. For example, for an empty cell, the only activity that can be performed is allocation of a new shift and therefore the "Allocate" function in the menu will be bold. Once allocation is done, all the other functions in the menu can be performed and hence it will be displayed in bold.

  2. If the staff is on leave for a particular day, the menu displays with Cancel Leave option in bold.
