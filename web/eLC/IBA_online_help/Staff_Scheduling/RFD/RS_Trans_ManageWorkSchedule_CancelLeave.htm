Cancel Leave

Select [Cancel Leave] from the menu to cancel leave for a staff on a particular day. You can  cancel leave applied for the current date and future dates before the start of the shift.

 

The following details are displayed:

  1. Staff - The Staff name is displayed and disabled.

  2. Role - The role type of the staff is displayed and disabled.

  3. Position - The position of the staff is displayed and disabled.

  4. Staff type - The staff type is displayed and disabled.

  5. Leave Period [From - To] - The selected date is displayed as the Leave period.

 

Enter the following:

  1. Cancel Leave - Select the check box to cancel leave.

  2. Reason - Select a valid reason from the list. The reasons are defined for Cancel Unavailability in the 'Reason' function. This is a mandatory field.

  3. Click [Apply] to save the record. The system prompts you before finalizing the action 'Do you want to allocate the shift?'. Click [OK] to allocate shift or click [Cancel] to exit from the screen.
