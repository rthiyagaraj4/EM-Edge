Operational Status

From this function you can Set the operational status of Enterprise Management modules at the facility level. Financial Modules are made operational at the Accounting Entity level.

Setting a module as operational implies that the particular module is going to be used in the facility selected. The various modules of Enterprise Management are grouped under different Application Groups, namely:

• Core Applications

• Clinical Support

• Patient Care

• Materials Management

• Human Resource Management

• Specialty Services

• External Interfaces

Setting Operational Status

From the SM Admin Setup > Enterprise Admin, select the Operational Status function.

The Operational Status screen gets displayed.

  1. Click [Create] to invoke the Create screen.

  2. The Operational Status is set under two Tabs, namely:

By Facility

By Accounting Entity

  1. Click [Apply] to save the record or [Reset] to clear the entered data.
