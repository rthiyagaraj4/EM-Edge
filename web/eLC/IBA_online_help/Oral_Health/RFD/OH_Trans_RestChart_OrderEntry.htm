You can view the following details and perform transactions as mentioned:

 

  1. Click [Drug Profile] to view the patient drug profile.

  2. Click [Allergies] to view the details recorded on allergies for the patient.

  3. Click [Active Problems] to record any active problems the patient is suffering from due to medication or any other cause.

  4. Click [Demographics] to view the demographic details of the patient. The details recorded during the patient registration are displayed on clicking [Demographics].

  5. Click View/Edit hyperlink to view the charges. The Edit/Enter Charge Details screen gets displayed.

    Enter the following details to modify the charges:

    1. Select the check box to modify the charges for the selected service. You cannot modify the charges, if the check box is cleared.

    2. Click [Modify] to update the charges. The Edit/Enter Charge Details screen gets displayed.

      Enter the following details:

      1. Rate – Enter the rate for the service in numeric format. You can update only the Rate field. This is a mandatory field.

      2. [Accept] – Click [Accept] to save the changes. On clicking [Accept], the charges are updated in the Edit/Enter Charge Details.

      3. [Cancel] – Click [Cancel] to exit from the screen.

    3. Click [Accept] to accept the changes or click [Cancel] to exit from the screen.

If the service charge is not defined for the selected treatment, the details are highlighted in  color. You cannot associate the service to the patient and the price has to be defined using the Patient Billing Manager to proceed further.

  1. Click [Drug Profile] to view the drugs details associated to the patient. You can associate External Drug Profile using the ‘External Drug Profile’ tab. Click [Add External Drug] to assign external drug to the patient. Several colors are used to indicate the drug status.

  2. Click [Clinical Comments] to record any comments for the patient.

  3. Click the hyperlink Addl. Info. (optional) to record the order format. The Comments entered are displayed in the Order Preview.

  4. Click [Preview] to view the treatment details.

  5. Click [Record] to record the treatment details.

  6. If the patient does not have an appointment booked with the facility, an alert message gets displayed. Click [OK] to book an appointment or [Cancel] to record the treatment without booking an appointment.

  7. The Book Appointment screen is displayed on clicking [OK]. You can book an appointment on a selected date and time. You can select to book an appointment by clicking [OK]. On selecting you can schedule an appointment for the patient in a Clinic or a Nursing Unit.

  8. Click [Cancel], if the appointment booking is not required.

  9. A confirmation message ‘Operation Completed Successfully’ gets displayed.
