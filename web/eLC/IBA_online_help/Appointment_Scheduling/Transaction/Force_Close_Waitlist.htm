Force Close Wait List

From this function you can Force Close Wait list for a particular patient or multiple patients.

Force Close Wait list function is used to close the waitlist for a particular patient or multiple patients. The reason for forcibly closing the waiting list has to be specified. The cutoff period for closing elapsed wait list is specified in the OA Parameter. Elapsed Wait lists are those records with the preferred date of appointment for waitlist less than the current system date.

Force Closing Waitlist

From the OA Transactions, select the Force Close Wait list Function.

Force Close Wait list screen gets displayed.

  1. Enter the details in the mandatory (*) fields. <Related Field Descriptions>

  2. Click [Apply] to Force Close Wait List.
