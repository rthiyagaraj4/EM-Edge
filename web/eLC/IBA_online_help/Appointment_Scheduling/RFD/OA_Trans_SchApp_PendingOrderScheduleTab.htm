Pending Order Schedule Tab

This tab displays orders that require appointments. The specific order is viewed and a booking is done by entering appropriate criteria.

  1. Click [Search] to display orders that require appointments or enter the required query criteria to search for a required order.

  2. Click Start Date Time hyperlink to book an appointment for the order and control shifts back to the Schedule Status Tab. Schedule Status Tab displays schedules for the locations associated to the particular Order Catalog.

  3. Click Orderable hyperlink to view the Order details. Click [View Audit Trial] to view the Audit Trail of the orderable or [Close] to close the screen.
