Tray Discrepancy

From this function you can Manage the discrepancies of non-consumables in a Tray.

The Tray Discrepancy function aids the owner store in managing discrepancy(s) of non-consumables in a Tray. The variation in quantity is specified and discrepancy reason can be specified. Tray discrepancies specified at Unit Level (requestor) and/or Service Location (sterilization location) are monitored using this function.

Managing Tray Discrepancies

From SS Transactions > Tray Maintenance, select the Tray Discrepancy function.

The Tray Discrepancy screen gets displayed.

  1. Click [Create] to invoke the Create screen.

  2. Enter the details in the mandatory (*) fields. <Related Field Description>

  3. Click [Add] to add the discrepancy details or [Cancel] to clear the entered details.

  4. On adding the discrepancy details, the Item details are displayed in the right hand side of the screen.

  5. Click ‘Item’ hyperlink to view or modify the discrepancy details.

  6. Click [Modify] to modify the changes or [Cancel] to clear the entered data.

  7. Click [Apply] to record the discrepancy details or [Reset] to clear the entered data.
