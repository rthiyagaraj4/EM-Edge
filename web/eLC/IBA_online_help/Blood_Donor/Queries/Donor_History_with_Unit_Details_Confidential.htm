Donor History With Unit Details – Confidential

From this function you can Query the donor’s previous encounter details and respective transactions in the facility.

The Donor History with Unit Details – Confidential function allows you to query donor’s previous encounter details along with the donated unit details. You can view the transaction details of the donor using this function. The components, which are to be prepared, added to stock or cancelled, are displayed using color. The units, which are cancelled, are displayed using color. Blood Groups and Rhesus Factors with initial Blood Typing are indicated using color indicator.

Querying the Donor History with Unit Details – Confidential

From Query, double-click the Donor History with Unit Details – Confidential function.

The Donor History with Unit Details – Confidential screen gets displayed.

  1. Click [Donor Lookup] to select the Donor. The donor details are displayed in the respective fields on selecting the donor.

  2. Click [Execute Query] or press [F8] to query the donor details.

  3. The donation date, donation type, unit number and the product details recorded during blood donation by the respective donor are displayed under the 'Donation Details' tab.

  4. The details recorded through the 'Deferred Donor (Rescreen) function is displayed under the 'Rescreen Details' tab.  

  5. Click [Unit Details] to view the unit details associated to the donor records. The Unit details screen is invoked with the details.  

  6. Click [Print Donor History] to either print, view or save the output of the report generation in a file. The donor’s previous encounter details in the facility are displayed.

  7. Click [Transfusion Details] to view the transfusion details of the selected confidential patient. The transfusion details are recorded in the ‘Transfuse Blood Units’ function.

  8. Click [Antibodies] to view Antibodies associated to the selected donor.

  9. Click [Antigens] to view Antigens associated to the selected donor.

  10. Click [Donor Notes] to view the donor notes recorded during previous encounters.

  11. Click [Donor Details] to view the donor’s demographic details recorded through the ‘Register Donor’ function. On clicking [Donor Details], the Donor Details screen is displayed. The Donor Details recorded during donor registration is displayed.

 

[Antigens], [Antibodies] and [Donor Notes] get enabled if details of antigens and antibodies and donor notes are recorded.
