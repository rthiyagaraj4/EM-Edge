Record Donation

From this function you can Perform the blood donation or Discontinue the blood donation process. You can Query to view the existing records.

The Record Bleeding function enables you to perform the blood donation and capture the donation information such as bleeding start time, end time, performer’s name, storage location, etc. The donation is discontinued, if the donor has serious reactions, during blood donation. You can record donation details of a deferred donor through this function. The deferred donor donation details are highlighted using color. An authorized user with appropriate access rights can record donation details. Separate warning messages are displayed to indicate that the donor is temporarily or permanently deferred.

Recording the Blood Donation

From Transactions > Donation Management, double-click the Record Donation function.

The Record Donation screen gets displayed.

  1. Click [Donor Lookup] to select the donor. Click [Execute Query] to view the unit details of the selected donor.

  2. Enter the details in the mandatory (*) fields. <Related Field Description>

  1. Click [Dispose Unit] to cancel the unit and its components that are already prepared or to dispose and proceed with re-screening or to dispose and proceed with the normal flow for donors' who are deferred temporarily or permanently. The Dispose Cancelled Units functional screen is displayed to record and dispose the selected units.

    An alert message 'This donor is permanently deferred. Select the option to proceed?' is displayed.

    An alert message 'This Donor is deferred till hh:mm:ss. Select the option to proceed?' is displayed.

  2. Click [Dispose and Proceed With Re-screening Flow] to dispose the blood unit and proceed with re-screening. The donor with the unit number appears in the list of 'Re-screening Donors' in the Deferred Blood Donor function. This is valid for both temporary and permanently deferred donors'.

  3. Click [Proceed with Normal Flow] to proceed further to Blood Unit Screening Result Entry function for a donor who is deferred temporarily. This is valid for donors' who are deferred temporarily.

  4. Click [Materials] to view the Materials  screen.

    1. Code – Enter the material code  or click [List of Values] to select the material code.

    2. Description - On selecting the code, the short description is displayed.

  5. Click [Save] or press [F10] to save the record or click [Clear Record] to clear the entered data.  

  6. Click [Execute Query] or press [F8] to view the existing records.
