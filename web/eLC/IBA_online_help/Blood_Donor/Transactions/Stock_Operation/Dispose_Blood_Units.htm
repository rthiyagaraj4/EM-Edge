Dispose Blood Units

From this function you can Dispose a blood unit permanently from the blood donor stock by associating an appropriate Fate code. You can Query to view the existing details.

The Dispose Blood Units function enables you to dispose a blood unit permanently from the existing blood stock. You can view the Antigen and Antibody details of the blood unit, if they are recorded for the selected blood unit. The disposed blood units are disposed from the blood bank and are not available for further transactions.

Disposing Blood Units

From the Transactions > Stock Operation, double-click the Dispose Blood Units function.

The Dispose Blood Units screen gets displayed.

  1. Enter the Product Code or click [List of Values] to view the unit details and dispose the blood unit.

  2. Click [Execute Query] or press [F8] to view the blood unit details of the selected Product Code. <Related Field Description>

  3. Click [Antibodies] to view the details. This button is enabled, if antibody details are recorded for the donor during the screening.

  4. Click [Antigens] to view the details. This button is enabled, if antigen details are recorded for the donor in the Record Donor/Unit Antigens function.

  5. Click [List of Values] to associate a fate code to the blood unit.

  6. Click [Save] or press [F10] to dispose the blood unit from the stock or click [Clear Record] to clear the entered data.
