Record Approval

From this function you can Record approvals for the requests placed for the Billing Services.                   

The function Record Approvals for Billing Services enables you to approve the request placed for a Billing Service by a patient. The Billing Services associated to the patient record. You have to login to the Cash Counter in order to approve or reject the service request placed for a patient.

Record Approvals for Billing Services

From the Transactions, double-click on Record Approvals for Billing Services function.

The Record Approvals for Billing Services screen gets displayed.

  1. Enter the details in the mandatory (*) fields. <Related Field Description>

  2. Click [Search] to display Srl No, patient ID, Patient Name, Episode ID, Age, Mobile Number, Payer Group, Payer, Policy, Policy Effective To, Effective To, Approved Amount.

  3. The requests placed for the Billing Services using the function Encounter Specific Policy Definition – Billing Services gets listed. You can place additional requests for Billing Services, by clicking on [Create Record].

  4. Click [Apply] to save the records or [Reset] to clear the entered details.

  5. Click [Query] to search for existing records.
