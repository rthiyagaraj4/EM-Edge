Maintain Health Card

From this function you can Modify the patient details in the Health Card.

The Maintain Health Card function is used to modify the patient details in the Health Card. You can view and verify the patient details defined during Health Card Subscription.

Maintaining Health Card

From the Transactions > Health Card, select Maintain Health Card.

The Maintain Health Card screen gets displayed.

  1. Click [?] to select the patient ID. The Patient Name, Gender, Age, Patient ID are displayed. The details of Privilege Card are displayed in disabled state. The No. Of OP Allotment and Till Date Availed details are displayed under the section ‘OP Visit Utilization Status’ in disabled state. The Clinic name, Visit Type, No of Visit and Availed are displayed in the disabled state.

  2. Click [Modify] to invoke the Authorization Screen.

    Enter the following details:

    1. User ID – Enter the User ID in alphanumeric format of character maximum length.

    2. PIN – Enter the PIN in alphanumeric format of character maximum length.

    3. Remarks – Enter the comments for modifying the Heath Card details.

  1. Enter the details in the mandatory (*) fields. <Related Field Description>

  2. Click [Print] to print the Health Card with patient details.

  3. Click [Add] to add Card Type, Price Class, Date range, and Membership fees. You can edit the Card Type, Price Class, Date range, and Membership fees.

  4. Click [Apply] to save the changes or [Reset] to clear the details.
