Visit Charge Details

The Visit Charge Details are displayed based on the setup defined for the respective Clinic through 'Outpatient Clinic Details' function in Patient Billing Manager module. A confirmation message 'Visit charge exempted upto dd/mm/yyyy hh:mm' is displayed, if 'Exempt Hospital Fee' check box is selected for the respective Clinic through 'Outpatient Billing Details' function in Patient Billing Manager module.

 

Following details are displayed:

  1. Hospital Charge - The Hospital Charge is displayed as a hyperlink. Click the hyperlink to edit charges. Edit/Enter Charge Details screen is displayed on clicking the hyperlink. Hospital Charges are computed based on the Billing Service associated to the respective Clinic through the function 'Outpatient Billing Details' in the Patient Billing Manager module.

  2. Patient Registration Charge - The Patient Registration Charge screen is displayed only if the ‘Charge for Patient Registration’ check box is selected in ‘BL Parameters’ function. The registration charges are computed based on the Billing Service associated to the Billing Group, Payer Group, Payer and the Patient Series through the ‘Patient Registration Billing Dtls’ function. Click Patient Registration Charge hyperlink to modify the charge for patient registration ID. The Bill Settlement Details screen is displayed on modifying the charges. You can add, modify or remove the payer details. A receipt number is generated on visit registration. Patient registration ID charges are computed during registration only if the check box 'Chargeable at Encounter' is selected in the function Patient Registration Billing Dtls. If the check box is cleared, the registration ID charge is computed during patient registration in Patient Identity Management module.

  3. Patient Payable - The patient payable amount is displayed. If the patient is an Insurance patient, amount to be paid by the patient after deducting the insurance coverage is displayed.

  4. Professional Charge - The Professional Charge is displayed as a hyperlink. Click the hyperlink to edit charges. Edit/Enter Charge Details screen is displayed on clicking the hyperlink.

  5. Patient Payable - The patient payable amount is displayed. If the patient is an Insurance patient, the amount to be paid by the patient after deducting insurance coverage is displayed.

  6. AE Bed Charge – The bed charge is computed and displayed in 'AE check Out' function, if the parameters are selected in ‘BL Parameters’ function and the ‘Bed Computation Rule’ is defined. The bed charges are computed from the time when the bed is alloted to the patient.

The bed charges are not posted when patient’s check out is cancelled.

=> If the bill is already generated, you cannot cancel check out.

  1. Click [Continue] to register the attendance or visit or [Revise] to halt the transaction.
