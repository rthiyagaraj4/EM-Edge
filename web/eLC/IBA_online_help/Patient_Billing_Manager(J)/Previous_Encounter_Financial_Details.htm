Previous Encounter Financial Details

The financial details of a patient associated with the past encounters in the facility are displayed. You can copy the financial details from previous encounters, if the financial details remain the same.  

 

Following details are listed on clicking [Select from Previous Encounters] hyperlink:

  1. Episode Type - Episode Types of patient for various encounters in the facility are displayed.

  2. Encounter Date - The encounter registration date and time is displayed as a hyperlink. Click the hyperlink to copy financial details of the respective encounter for current encounter.

  3. Encounter ID - Encounter ID generated during encounter registration is displayed.

  4. Billing Group - The Billing Group code selected for respective encounter is displayed along with the description.

  5. Payer - Payer description is displayed, if the respective encounter has any payers associated to it.

  6. Priority - The Priority set for respective Payer in case of Multiple Payers is displayed.

  7. Policy Type - Policy Type associated to the Payer for respective encounter is displayed.

  8. Policy No - The Policy Number is displayed.

  9. Effective [From - To] - The policy effective date is displayed.

  10. [Close] - Click [Close] to exit the screen.
