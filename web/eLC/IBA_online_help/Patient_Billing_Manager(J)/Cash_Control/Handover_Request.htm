Handover Request

From this function a Cashier can Handover the cash or cheque amount to Accounts officer or a Bank Personnel. You can Query to view the Handover details of a specific Cash Counter. You can Cancel the handover request that is placed from a Cash Counter.

Handover Request function is used to hand-over the Cash or Cheque amount to the Accounts officer or the Bank Personnel without exiting the Cash Counter. Only one cash or cheque hand-over request can exist at a time. You can view the Cash and Cheque collection details and denomination details. The cash amount that is being handed over has to be recorded as denominations or the handover amount. You cannot specify the same denomination value that is already recorded. The cash and cheque amount recorded as denomination cannot exceed the respective collection amount.

Cash/Cheque Handover Request

From the Transactions > Cash Control, select Handover Request Function.

The Handover Request screen gets displayed.

  1. Click [Create] to record cash and cheque handover details.

  2. Click [?] function to select a Cash Counter. The description of the respective Cash Counter is displayed.

  3. If the handover request is already placed, click [Query] and select the respective Cash Counter to associate additional details.

  4. The name of the Cashier, Login date and Time, Requested Date and Time, Cash Collection, Cheque Collection, collection details and transaction details are displayed.

    The transactions that are cancelled, acknowledged or rejected through this function and 'Acknowledge Handover Request' function are displayed.

    The cheque collection details are displayed. Cheque number, date, bank, payer, patient ID and amount is displayed.

    Total amount collected in the Cash Counter from the cheque transactions processed in the respective shift is displayed. The cheque details that are not processed during handover are listed, which can be associated to the cheque handover request using the [List of Values] option.

    Total amount collected in the Cash Counter from the cash transactions processed in the respective shift is displayed.

    The current date and time is displayed as the default Requested Date and Time.

    The date and time when the Cashier has logged into the Cash Counter is displayed.

  5. Select the 'Select All' check box to select the cheque records that has to be handed over. You can select and handover amount collected from an individual cheque transaction. On selecting the check box, the total amount that can be handed over is displayed.

  6. Enter the Handover Request Amount in numeric format for cash or cheque transactions. You can enter the amount, if denominations are not mandatory.

  7. Click [Add] associated below cheque collection to associate additional cheque transactions that has to be handed over.

  8. Enter cash handover details. <Related Field Description>

  9. Click [Add] associated below to associate additional cash transactions that has to be handed over.

  10. Click [Cancel] to cancel the handover request.

  11. A confirmation message 'Do you want to cancel the Request?' is displayed. Click [OK] to cancel or [Cancel] to abort the transaction. The cancelled records are displayed under transaction details. The date and time when the handover request is placed and cancelled, cash and cheque amount, status and User ID is displayed.

  12. Click [Apply] to record the details or [Reset] to clear the entered data.

 

The check box ‘Handover Request Without CC Logout' is mandatory to enable this function.
