Transfer Patient Out

From this function you can Transfer patients out from a source Nursing Unit to the destination Nursing Unit.

Requested transfers confirmed by the destination Nursing Units and regular transfers (transfers without request) are listed and color codes are used to indicate the transfer priorities.

The details recorded while transferring out an Inpatient are Occupancy Details, Other Details and Porterage Details.

Transferring Patient Out

From the IP Transactions > Transfer, select the Transfer Patient Out function.

The Transfer Patient Out screen gets displayed.

  1. Click [Search] to display the list of all Inpatients with confirmed transfer request or enter the search criteria and click [Search] to select a specific Inpatient. Click [Clear] to reset the records displayed in the result area.

    Enter the following query criteria to select the required patient record for transferring out of the Nursing Unit:

    From Details

    1. Nursing Unit – Enter the Nursing Unit from where the patient has to be transferred in alphanumeric format of 15 characters maximum length or select the Nursing Unit using the [?] function.

    2. Specialty – Enter the Specialty from where the patient has to be transferred in alphanumeric format of 15 characters maximum length or select the Specialty using the [?] function.

    3. Practitioner – Enter the Practitioner name in alphanumeric format of 30 characters maximum length or select the Practitioner using the  [?] function.

    To Details

    1. Nursing Unit - Enter the Nursing Unit to where the patient has to be transferred in alphanumeric format of 15 characters maximum length or select the Nursing Unit using the [?] function.

    2. Specialty - Enter the Specialty to where the patient has to be transferred in alphanumeric format of 15 characters maximum length or select the Specialty using the [?] function.

    3. Practitioner - Enter the Practitioner name in alphanumeric format of 30 characters maximum length or select the Practitioner using the  [?] function.

    4. Admission Date From-To - Enter the Admission Date Range in date format of dd/mm/yyyy or select the date using the calendar function.

    5. Encounter ID - Enter the Encounter ID in numeric format of 12 digits maximum length.

    6. Request/Inpatient – Select the required value from the list. The listed options are ‘All’ and ‘Confirmed’. If ‘Confirmed’ is selected, all those ‘Request for Transfers’ that are confirmed are listed. If ‘All’ is selected, all those ‘Request for Transfers’ that are confirmed and records without transfer request are displayed.

    7. Patient ID - Enter the Patient ID in alphanumeric format of maximum length as specified in MP Parameter or select the Patient ID using the [?] function.

  2. On searching, Inpatient Details and Transfer Request Details are displayed based on the entered criteria.

  3. Click ‘+’ hyperlink to view the Current Encounter Details.

  4. Click the ‘Encounter ID’ hyperlink to display the Transfer Patient Out screen. The screen displays the Admission Details, Occupancy Details, Other Details and Porterage Details.

  5. Select the Reason for Transfer from the list. The reasons listed are defined in the Transfer Type function of ‘IP Code Setup’. This field is mandatory.

  6. Enter the details in the mandatory (*) fields of the Occupancy Details, Other Details, and Porterage Details tabs.

    The Porterage Details tab consists of two sub-tabs, namely ‘Patient Details’ and ‘Medical Details’.

    Patient Details Tab

    1. Mode of Transport – Enter the Mode of Transport in alphanumeric format of 30 characters maximum length (in the single field provided) used to physically transfer the patient from the bed occupied in one Nursing Unit to a vacant bed in another.

    2. Accompanying Persons – Enter the names of the Accompanying Person(s) in alphanumeric format of 30 characters maximum length in each of the seven text boxes while transferring the patient from bed occupied in one Nursing Unit to another.

    3. Patient Belongings – Patient Belongings are belongings of the patient carried along with the patient while transferring from the bed occupied in one Nursing Unit to a vacant bed in another. Enter the patient belongings in alphanumeric format of 40 characters maximum length in each of the seven text boxes.

    Medical Details Tab

    1. Medical Equipment – Enter the Medical Equipments used in alphanumeric format of 40 characters maximum length in each of the seven the text boxes while transferring the patient from one Nursing Unit to another.

    2. Blood and Medication - Enter the Blood Bag/Bottles and medications carried along with the patient while transferring the patient from one Nursing Unit to another in alphanumeric format of 40 characters maximum length in each of the ten text boxes.

    3. Records and Documents - Enter the records and the documents of the patient carried along while transferring the patient from one Nursing Unit to another in alphanumeric format of 40 characters maximum length in each of the five text boxes.

    1. Transferring Practitioner – The name of the practitioner currently attending the patient is displayed, by default. To change the transferring practitioner, enter the name of the Transferring Practitioner in alphanumeric format of 30 characters maximum length or select the Practitioner’s Name using the [?] function. The Transferring Practitioner is responsible till the patient is transferred. This is a mandatory field. The Practitioners associated to the Specialty selected for the destination Nursing Unit are to be entered.

    2. Authorization Date/Time – Enter the Authorization Date and Time or select the date using the calendar function. The entered date should not be greater than the Preferred Transfer Date/Time. The Authorization Date/Time is displayed as the Preferred Date/Time.

    Bed Block Details

    1. Block Type – The Block Type is displayed on selecting the ‘Reason for Transfer’.

    2. Block Period – Enter the period to block the bed (in hours) in numeric format of three characters maximum length. The specified period must not exceed the bed-blocking period set for ‘Transfer Out’ in IP Parameter. The Blocking Type for this is always non-overridable in nature. The Block Period is displayed as recorded on selecting the ‘Reason for Transfer’.

    3. Bed Blocked Until – The date and time until when the bed is blocked is displayed on selecting the ‘Block Period’.

    4. Remarks – Enter the Remarks if any.

    Informed Details

    1. Informed – Select the check box if the Next of Kin or the patients relatives are informed about the patient’s transfer. Once the informed check box is selected, the other fields under Informed Details can be entered.

    2. To – Select to whom the transfer of the patient is informed from the list. The listed values are Next to Kin, First to Notify and Others.

    3. Name – Enter the Name in alphanumeric format of 30 characters maximum length.

    4. Date/time – Enter the Date and Time in the format dd/mm/yyyy hh:mm or select the date and time from the calendar function. This field is mandatory. The entered period has to be greater than or equal to admission date/time and within the preferred transfer date/time.

    If the person informed is ‘Next of Kin’ or ‘First to Notify’, [Next of Kin/First to Notify Details] is enabled.

    The source Nursing Unit details of the patient are displayed in the ‘Current’ heading. The values can be entered in some fields under ‘Transfer To’ heading for patient records with Transfer Status as ‘Regular’. All the fields in this tab under ‘Transfer To’ heading are disabled for patient records with Transfer Status as ‘Confirmed’.

     

    Nursing Unit Details

    Transfer To

    1. Nursing Unit – Enter the Nursing Unit in alphanumeric format of 15 characters maximum length or select the Nursing Unit using the [?] function. All the Nursing Units of the facility are listed. This field is mandatory.

    2. Service – Select the Service of the Nursing Unit from the list. The listed services correspond to the selected Nursing Unit. The primary Service associated to the selected Nursing Unit is displayed, by default. This field is mandatory.

    3. Sub-Service – Select the Sub-service from the list. The listed Sub-services correspond to the selected Service.

    4. Request Bed Class Change – Select the check box to request for a change in the current Bed Class to which the patient is associated. This field is enabled on selecting the Nursing Unit.

    5. Bed Class – This field is enabled on selecting the ‘Request for Bed Class Change’ checkbox. Select the bed class from the list. This field is mandatory.

    6. Bed Type - This field is enabled on selecting the ‘Request for Bed Class Change’ check box. Select the Bed Type from the list. This field is mandatory.

    7. Bed No – The source Nursing Unit is not allowed to select bed numbers for ‘Regular’ transfers.

    8. Room No – This field is blank and disabled for patient records with ‘Regular’ transfer status not allowing bed assignment for the same cases at this stage.

    9. Specialty – Select the Specialty using the [?] function. The Specialties displayed on selecting the lookup function correspond to the selected Nursing Unit. This field is mandatory.

    10. Practitioner - Enter the Practitioner or select the Practitioner using the [?] function. The Practitioners displayed in the lookup function correspond to the selected Specialty. This field is mandatory.

    11. Practitioner Designation – The designation of the Practitioner is displayed on selecting the Practitioner.

    12. Medical Team – Select the Medical Team from the list. The Medical Teams to which the selected attending Practitioner belongs to are displayed.

     

    Reserve Bed Details

    1. Reserve Bed - Select the check box to Reserve the Bed

    2. Reason for Reserve Bed - Select the Reason for Reservation from the list.

  7. Click [Record] to transfer the patient out or [Close] to exit from the screen.

  8. If Bed is reserved while Requesting for Transfer, an alert message 'This bed will be reserved on Transfer In of this Patient. Do you want to proceed??' is displayed. Click [OK] to proceed.

 

The Transfer To details are displayed and disabled for the Inpatients with Transfer Request confirmed.
