Enter the following details:

 

  1. Role – The role of the practitioner is selected by clicking either ‘Consultant’ or ‘Assisting’. On selecting the option as ‘Consultant’, the practitioner to be selected mantles the role of a consulting practitioner. If the option selected is ‘Assisting’, the practitioner to be selected assists other practitioner/s during the stay of the selected inpatient. More than one practitioner may be assigned for each role, based on the requirement, during the period of inpatient stay.

  2. Practitioner – Enter the Practitioner Name in alphanumeric format of 30 characters maximum length or select the Practitioner from [?] function. The list contains Practitioners other than the attending practitioner. These practitioners belong only to Practitioner Type ‘Physician/Surgeon/Psychiatrist/Dentist’. This field is mandatory.

  3. Specialty – Select the Specialty of the Practitioner from the list. The Primary Specialty of the selected practitioner is defaulted along with other Specialties (if available) in the list. This field is mandatory.

  4. Role Start Date - The current system date and time is displayed, by default. Enter the date and time in date format of dd/mm/yyyy hh:mm. The entered date and time must not be less than admission date and time, or choose the date using the Calendar function.

  5. Role End Date - Enter the Role End Date and time in date format of dd/mm/yyyy hh:mm, or choose the date using the Calendar function. The entered date cannot be greater than system date. This may be done only for selected Consulting or Assisting Practitioners. The Role End Date of Attending Practitioners can be changed only using the IP Transfer function ‘Practitioner Transfer’ or if the Attending Practitioner is changed during, one/more of the activities like ‘Assign Bed’/‘Transfer Patient’/‘Request for Transfer’/‘Transfer Patient in’. The role end date/time cannot be specified while associating a practitioner to the patient. This field is applicable only when the details of the role associated needs to be modified.

  6. Remarks – Enter Remarks in alphanumeric format of 200 characters maximum length.
