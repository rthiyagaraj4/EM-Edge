1. Discharge Medication – Select the check box if the Discharge Medication has been given to the patient.

  2. Existing Medication - A hyperlink with the legend ‘View Details’ is displayed if the patient is on any medication during discharge. On clicking the hyperlink, the details regarding the medication that the patient is currently undergoing is displayed.

  3. Disc Summary Prepared – This field indicates if the discharge summary has been prepared for the patient. The values selected in Prepare Discharge Advice function are displayed and modification is not allowed. The check box is selected and disabled if the discharge summary has been prepared in the Clinician Access module. It is cleared if the discharge summary is not prepared. If the check box is not selected, the system does not allow the patient discharge even if other details in the checklist are marked.

  4. Prepared By – The Name of the Practitioner who prepared the discharge summary is displayed and disabled.

 

Informed Details

This section is used to record the details of the person to whom the information regarding the discharge of the patient has been conveyed. These details are populated automatically if the same has been recorded during the preparation of the ‘Discharge Advice’ of the patient. If the informed details are not recorded, the ‘Discharge Patient’ transaction cannot be recorded.

  1. Informed To – Select a valid value from the list. The values listed in the list are ‘Next of kin’, ‘First to Notify’ and ‘Others’. This field is used to indicate if the relatives of the patient have been informed regarding the discharge of the patient.

  2. Name – Enter the Name of the person to whom the information regarding the discharge of the patient is conveyed. If the value selected in ‘Informed To’ field is either ‘Next of Kin’ or ‘First to Notify’, the name of the person identified as either ‘Next of Kin’ or ‘First to Notify’ during the registration of the patient in Master Patient Index module is displayed. The name can be changed. If the value selected in the ‘Informed To’ field is ‘Others’, this field is blank and you can enter the name of the person to whom the information has been given. If none of the options are selected in ‘Informed To’ field, this field is disabled.

  3. Date/Time – Enter the Date/Time in dd/mm/yyyy hh:mm format or select the Date/Time using the Calendar function. This field is disabled if none of the values listed in ‘Informed To’ field are chosen.

 

Certificate

  1. Certificate Number – Enter the Certificate Number in alphanumeric format of 30 characters maximum length.

  2. Date From – Enter the From Date using the calendar function.

  3. Date To – Enter the To Date using the calendar function. ‘To Date’ must not be less than ‘From Date’.

  4. Duration – Select the duration type from the drop-down list. The listed values are Day, Month and Year. Enter the duration in numeric format of two characters maximum length.

 

Follow up Appointments

This section contains a list of all the referrals that has been made for the patient at the time of preparing the Discharge Advice. The Referral can be to a location within the facility or to a location within the enterprise or to an external location. The details of the referrals that have been made for the patient are as explained.

  1. Referral ID – The Referral ID that was generated for the patient when a referral was recorded is displayed. This field is hyperlinked. On clicking the hyperlink, the details that were captured while recording the referral are displayed.

  2. Preferred on – The Preferred Date for the patient to go to the referral location as recorded in the Referral Registration function while preparing the discharge advice for the patient is displayed.

  3. Referred To – The name of the facility is displayed, to which the patient is referred.

  4. Specialty – The Specialty is displayed, to which the patient is referred is displayed.

  5. Location – The Location to which the patient is referred is displayed.

  6. Practitioner – The Name of the Practitioner to whom the patient is referral is displayed.

  7. Appt No – The Appointment Reference Number of the referral is displayed. This is applicable when the patient is referred to a Clinic within the facility or within the enterprise and an appointment is scheduled to this Clinic. The Appointment Reference Number is a system-generated number. The appointment is mandatory if the patient is referred to the logged in facility. If the appointment is not scheduled, the discharge of the patient cannot be recorded.

 

Planned Admission

  1. Location -  The short description of the nursing unit recorded in Booking Details section in 'Create Booking' screen gets displayed.

  2. Date of Planned Admission - The Preferred Admission Date/Time recorded in ‘Create Booking’ screen gets displayed.
