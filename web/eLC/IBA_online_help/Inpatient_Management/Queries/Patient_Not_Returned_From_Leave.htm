Patients Not Returned From Leave

From this function you can Query the patients who are due for return from their leave during an Inpatient admission.

This function is used to query on the patients who are due for return from their leave during an Inpatient admission. Further, this can be used to view whether beds were released for such patients. If it is released, the beds could be prepared for patients due to returning within a short time frame.

Querying for Patients not Returned from Leave

From IP Queries, select the Patients Not Returned From Leave function.

The Patients Not Returned From Leave screen gets displayed.

  1. Enter the details in the mandatory fields to query the details of Patients not Returned from Leave. <Related Field Description>

  2. Click [Search] to view the Patients not Returned from Leave details or [Reset] to clear the entered search criteria.

  3. On searching, the details of patients who are not returned from leave are displayed.
