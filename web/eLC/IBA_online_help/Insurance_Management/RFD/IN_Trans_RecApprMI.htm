Enter the following details to record the approvals:

 

  1. Patient No – Enter a valid patient ID or click [List of Values] to search for a valid patient ID. A Lookup window gets displayed on clicking the [List of Values]. Double-click on a relevant patient record to select it. The patient name, age and gender get displayed along with the Episode Type. This is a mandatory field.

  2. Episode ID – Click [List of Values] to select a valid Episode ID. You have to select an Insurance related encounter to record the approval for the associated material items or the Billing Services.

  3. Visit ID – On selecting the Episode ID, relevant Visit ID is displayed. Click [List of Values] to select a valid Visit ID. You have to select an Insurance related encounter to record the approval for the associated material items or the Billing Services. This is a mandatory field.

  4. Cur Acct Seq – On selecting the Episode ID, relevant Current Account Sequence is displayed.

 

On entering the required details, the financial details of the patient recorded during admission get displayed. If the financial details were updated, the updated financial details get displayed.
