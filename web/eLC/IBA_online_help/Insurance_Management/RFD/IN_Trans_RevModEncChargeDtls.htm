Enter the following details to review or modify the charge details:

 

  1. Admission/Tfr No. – Enter the Admission Number of the patient. The Admission Number has to be valid.

  2. Charge Date/Time – The date and time when the charge was calculated for the relevant service gets displayed.

  3. Doc Reference – The document reference type and number generated automatically for the service associated to the patient gets displayed. The document reference type and number is generated while associating the services to the patient in the Patient Billing Manager and other external modules.

  4. Line – The priority for the selected service is displayed.

  5. Service/Panel – Service or Panel is displayed based on the association to the patient record.

  6. Panel Code – The Service or the Panel associated to the patient gets displayed.

  7. UOM – The Unit of Measure of the selected Service or Panel is displayed.

  8. Total Payable – The total amount payable towards the service is displayed.

  9. Patient Payable - The Amount to be settled by the patient towards the service is displayed.

  10. Patient Paid – The amount paid by the patient towards the service is displayed.

  11. Completed – The status indicates whether the order placed from the external modules towards the service is completed or not.

  12. Description – The Description for the selected services are displayed.

  13. Total Charges – The total charges for the services associated to the patient are computed and displayed.

 

Right click on each record to perform the following actions:

  • Adjust

    You can only select unconfirmed services to adjust the service details. A screen pops-up on clicking Adjust. Click [Edit] to update the service details. An additional screen pops-up where you can enter the details and click [Accept] to save the changes made. You can also refer the write-up for the function Adjust Direct Billing Items or Adjust OP Direct Billing Items to know more on updating the charges for Inpatients and Outpatients respectively.

  • Cancel

    You can cancel only the unbilled transactions for an Inpatient. The transactions, which are already billed cannot be cancelled. A screen pops-up on clicking Cancel to record the modified  details. Click [Commit Form] to record the details. You can also refer the write-up for the function Cancel IP Direct Billing Items to know more on cancelling the unbilled charges for an Inpatient in the facility.

  • Change Billing Service Coverage

    You can update the Coverage Details for a Billing Service using this function. The details recorded using the function Encounter Specific Policy Definition – Services gets displayed for a relevant encounter. A screen pops-up on clicking Change Billing Service Coverage. You can update the required details and click [Commit Form] to save the changes. To recalculate charges, select the check box ‘Recalculate Charges’. The charges are re-computed and posted to the Patient Billing Manager module.

  • Change Billing Material Item

    You can update the Coverage Details for a material item using this function. The details recorded using the function Encounter Specific Policy Definition – Material Items gets displayed for a relevant encounter. A screen pops-up on clicking Change Billing Material Item. You can update the required details and click [Commit Form] to save the changes. To recalculate charges, select the check box ‘Recalculate Charges’. The charges are re-computed and posted to the Patient Billing Manager module.
