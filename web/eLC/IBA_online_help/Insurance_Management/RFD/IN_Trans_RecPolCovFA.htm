Enter the following details to record the policy coverage details for future admission:

 

  1. Patient No – Enter a valid patient ID or click [List of Values] to search for a valid patient ID. A Lookup window  is populated. The patient name, age and gender get displayed along with the Episode Type. This is a mandatory field.

  2. Telephone – Enter the Telephone Number of the patient in numeric format of 18 digits maximum length.

  3. Sex – On selecting the Patient Number, the gender of the patient recorded in the Master Patient Index module is displayed. ‘Male’ is displayed, by default.

  4. Age – On selecting the Patient Number, the Age of the patient recorded in the Master Patient Index module is displayed in years, months and days format.

 

Admission & Policy Details

  1. Admission Status – Select a value from the list. The system defined value is ‘Future Admission’. On selecting the status, ‘Expected’ is displayed on the screen indicating that the patient is expected to arrive at the facility on the admission date.

  2. Episode Type – The Episode Type is displayed, by default, based on the patient ID selected. ‘Inpatient’ is displayed, by default.

  3. Adm Date – Click Calendar function to select a date. This indicates the next admission date for when the patient is supposed to arrive at the facility. Expected Admission must be a future date.

  4. Date Initiated - Click Calendar function to select a date when the request was placed for future admission. The date selected has to be the same as the system date or lesser than the current date. This is a mandatory field.

  5. Date Faxed - Click Calendar function to select a date when the required documents were faxed by the patient. The date selected has to be the same as the system date or lesser than the current date. Faxed date must not be less than the Initiated Date.

  6. Medical Record Recd – Select the check box, if the medical record has been received for the selected patient.

  7. MR Recd Date - Click Calendar function to select a date when the medical records for the selected patient were received. The date selected has to be the same as the system date or lesser than the current date.

  8. Consent Form Recd – Select the check box, if the Consent Form has been received for the selected patient.

  9. Consent Form Recd Date - On selecting the Consent Form Received check box, the system date is displayed. You can click Calendar function to select a date when the Consent Form for the selected patient was received. The date selected has to be the same as the system date or lesser than the current date.

  10. Consent Form Signed – Select the check box, if the Consent form has been signed by the patient.

  11. Cost Estimated - Select the check box to record the estimated cost for the future admission.

  12. Estimated Cost Amt – Enter the estimated cost in numeric format. The text box is enabled on selecting the check box Cost Estimated.

  13. Urgent/Routine – Select either ‘Urgent’ or ‘Routine’ from the list. ‘Routine’ is displayed, by default.

  14. Admission Type – Select a value from the list. The listed values are ‘Elective, ‘Emergency’ and ‘Delivery’. ‘Elective’ is displayed, by default.

  15. Admission Doctor – Click [List of Values] to select and to associate a Doctor for future admission of the patient .

  16. Availed Amt – Enter the amount availed by the patient in numeric format.

  17. Active - Select the check box and the details recorded for a patient are accessible for future transaction. Clear the check box, if the details recorded for a patient must not be available for future transaction. The following message pops-up requesting confirmation, whether to suspend the record.

“This Record will be suspended and will not be accessible for future transaction. Do you want to continue?”

 Click [Yes] to suspend and [No] to cancel.

  1. [Remarks] – Click [Remarks] to record the remarks for future admission.

  2. [Admission Remarks] – Click [Admission Remarks] to record the remarks for future admission .

  3. [Discharge Remarks] – Click [Discharge Remarks] to record the remarks. The remarks are valid while the patient is discharged.

 

Billing Details

  1. Additional Billing Group – Click [List of Values] to associate an Additional Billing Group to the patient records along with the Policy Details.

  2. Billing Group - Click [List of Values] to select a Billing Group. This is a mandatory field.

  3. Insurance Company - Click [List of Values] to associate an Insurance Company to the patient record.

  4. Priority  – Enter the value in numeric format with a maximum of two digits. This indicates the priority of the Billing Group in the Insurance Company. Priority has to be entered in a sequence.

  5. Policy Type Code - Click [List of Values] to select a Policy Type. This is a mandatory field.

  6. Policy Number – Enter the Policy Number in numeric format. This is a mandatory field.

  7. Policy Start Date - Click Calendar function to set a beginning date from when the Policy is applicable to the selected patient.

  8. Policy Expiry Date – Click Calendar function to set an end date on when the selected Policy expires. Policy Expiry Date has to be greater than Expected Admission Date. This is a mandatory field.

  9. Credit Authorization Ref – Enter the Credit Authorization Reference with regards to the selected Policy .

  10. Credit Auth Date – Enter the date to authorize the Credit with regards to the patient. The credit authorization date cannot be greater than the encounter date.

  11. Effective From – Click Calendar function to set the beginning date from when the policy is valid for future admission of the patient. The date selected has to be the same as the Admission Date.

  12. Effective To – Click Calendar function to set the end date until when the policy is valid for the selected patient.

  13. Approved Amt – Enter the amount approved by the Insurance Company in numeric format.

  14. Approved Days – Enter the number of days is numeric format to indicate the number of days until when the approved amount is valid.

  15. Billing Group – The Description for the selected Billing Group is displayed.

  16. Customer – The name of the selected Insurance Company is displayed.

  17. Policy Type – The Description for the selected Policy Type is displayed.

  18. Status – Select a value from the list. The values are system defined- ‘Approved’, ‘Rejected’ and ‘Pending’.

  19. Select Rejected and the insurance authorization for future admission of the patient is rejected. The following message gets displayed.

‘Insurance credit authorization is rejected. Record will not be available for any vital modifications. Do you want to continue?’

 Click [Yes] to reject or [No] to cancel rejection.

  2. Select Approved and the records are saved.
