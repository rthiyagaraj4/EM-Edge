Policy Credit Authorization Details

From this function you can Query on the Insurance Credit Authorization Details for a patient.             

The Policy Credit Authorization Details function is used to inquire on the Insurance Credit Authorization Details for a patient. The credit details recorded during transactions for a relevant patient encounter gets displayed. Click [Execute Query] to view the credit authorization details for all patients. Click [Enter Query] and enter the patient ID to view and modify the credit authorization details for a relevant patient. Any unbilled transactions associated with the patient has to be settled.

Querying to view Policy Credit Authorization Details

From the Queries, double-click on Policy Credit Authorization Details function.

The Policy Credit Authorization Details screen gets displayed.

  1. Click [Execute Query] or press [F8] to query for all the records.

OR

  1. Press [F9] to select a valid Admission Number from the list. Click [Execute Query] or press [F8] to query for the record associated to the admission number selected.

  2. The financial details recorded for the admission gets displayed.

  3. Click [Modify] to login and to update the details. If the billing remarks were recorded for the respective admission, the details get displayed. Click [Close] to close the screen. A confirmation screen pops-up requesting the login details. Click [OK] to login and to view the details recorded or [Cancel] to exit.

    Enter the following details to login:

    1. User ID - Enter the User ID in alphanumeric format. This is a mandatory field.

    2. Pin No - Enter the Pin number for the respective User. This is a mandatory field.

    3. Remarks - Enter the Remarks in alphanumeric format. This is a mandatory field.
