Adjust Payer Distribution

From this function you can Adjust the Payer’s payable amount for the services availed by a patient.

The Adjust Payer Distribution function enables you to adjust the payer’s payable amount. You can transfer the gross amount from a Payer to Patient. You can also issue discount on a specific service. Based on the adjustment and the discount issued, the new amount to be paid by the payer is calculated. The total gross adjustment has to be zero for the services associated to the patient. You cannot adjust the amount for Cash patients using this function. Latest adjustments are highlighted in color and initial adjustments are highlighted in color. The amount transferred from Payer to Patient gets displayed in invoice generated to indicate the changes. Two separate receipts are generated to indicate the adjusted amount and the original patient payable amount for the services based on the coverage criteria. The service details and the adjusted amount are printed in the adjustment report. Report generation hierarchy is based on the priority set in the 'BL Parameters' function. The Adjustment Receipt is a detailed receipt with the service details. You can reallocate the amount before bill generation. You can reallocate the amount for the services only if the bill is already generated.

You can reallocate the amount, if the financial details are modified after bill generation.

Adjusting Payer Distribution

From the Transactions, double-click Adjust Payer Distribution Function.

The Adjust Payer Distribution screen gets displayed.

  1. Select an Episode Type from the list. The system-defined values are Inpatient, Outpatient, Emergency and Daycare.

  2. Enter the Patient ID. The values are populated in Ward/Clinic, Transaction date, Sex, Age and Doctor on selecting the Patient ID. This is a mandatory field.

  3. The admission/transfer number is displayed, if there is a single encounter. If there are multiple encounters, the values get listed. Click [List of Values] to select an admission/transfer number. <Related Field Description>

  4. Click [Search] to query the details based on the search criteria. The legend changes to [As On: dd/mm/yyyy hh:mm:ss] on clicking [Search].

  5. The service details associated to the patient are displayed. <Related Field Description>

  6. Enter the adjustment amount. <Related Field Description>

  7. The adjustment details are displayed indicating the old value and the new value after adjustment. <Related Field Description>

  8. Click [Process] to adjust the payer distribution details.
