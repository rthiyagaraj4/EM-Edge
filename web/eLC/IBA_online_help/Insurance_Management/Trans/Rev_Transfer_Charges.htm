Review and Transfer Charges

From this function you can Review and Transfer the charges of a patient in the facility to another patient’s record. You can generate a single bill for the services utilised by the patients.

The Review and Transfer Charges function is used to view and to transfer the charges of a patient in the facility to another patient’s record. You can associate the charges of a baby to the mother’s record. So, only a single bill is generated for the services utilized by the mother and the baby in the facility.

Review and Transfer Charges

From the Transactions, double-click on Review and Transfer Charges function.

The Review and Transfer Charges screen gets displayed.

  1. Select an Episode Type from the list. The values are system defined - Inpatient, Outpatient, Daycare, Emergency and External.

  2. Enter a patient ID or click [List of Values] to select a patient ID. A lookup screen appears.

  3. Click [Enter Query] and enter a valid Patient Number. The patient name, age, gender, patient registration date and time get displayed on entering the patient number. Click [Execute Query] or press [F8] to query the required patient record. Double-click on the record to select it.

OR

  1. Click [Execute Query] or press [F8] to view and to select a patient record from the list. Double-click on the record to select it.

  2. The services associated to the selected patient gets displayed. <Related Field Description>

  3. Select the check box ‘ Select All ’ to transfer all the service and the charge details to another patient's folio in the facility.

  4. Enter the patient ID under ‘ Transfer Charges To ’ to transfer the charges to selected patient's folio.

  5. Click [Clear] to clear the entered data.  

  6. Click [Commit Form] or press [F10] to save or click [Clear Record] to clear the entered data.
