The following details are displayed:

 

  1. Priority – The Priority of the specimen is displayed. The valid values are ‘Stat’, ‘Urgent’ and ‘Routine’.  The priority is recorded for the specimen through ‘Registration’ function in Laboratory module.

  2. Patient ID – On selecting the specimen number, the Patient ID and Name of the patient is displayed.

  3. Comments – The crossmatch comments recorded while requesting the blood unit is displayed.

  4. Blood Group – The Blood Group of the patient recorded in the ‘Record Patient Blood Group’ function in Medical Records module is displayed.

  5. Rhesus – The Rhesus factor of the Blood Group recorded in the ‘Record Patient Blood Group’ function in Medical Records module is displayed.

  6. Sex – The Gender of the patient recorded during patient registration is displayed.

  7. Age – The Age of the patient recorded during the patient registration is displayed. If an asterisk (*) is displayed next to the age, it is a calculated age of the patient and not the actual age.

  8. Regd Date/Time – The Registered Date and Time of the Specimen is displayed.

 

Product/Category

  1. Product – The Product code for the requested blood unit recorded in ‘Request Blood Unit’ function is displayed.

  2. Description – The Description of the product code defined in ‘Product Codes’ function is displayed.

  3. Category – The Category associated with the requested product defined in ‘Product Codes’ function is displayed.

  4. Remarks – The Remarks recorded while requesting the blood unit is displayed.

  5. Volume – The volume of the product defined in the ‘Product Codes’ function is displayed.

  6. Volume Unit – The Unit of the volume defined in the ‘Product Codes’ function is displayed.

  7. Units – The number of units requested is displayed.

  8. Date – The Date upto which the units are required to be reserved for the selected patient, is displayed. The required date is recorded in the ‘Request for Blood Units’ function.  

  9. Expected Transfusion Date – The Expected Date for Transfusion recorded in ‘Request Blood Unit’ function is displayed.

 

Unit Details

  1. Unit Number – The Unit Number of the blood unit is displayed.

  2. Product – The Product Code of the unit is displayed.

  3. Volume – The Volume of unit specified in the ‘Product Codes’ function for the selected product is displayed.

  4. Blood Group – The Blood Group of the unit is displayed.

  5. Rhesus – The Rhesus factor of the unit is displayed.

  6. Expiry Date – The Expiry Date of the product defined in the ‘Product Codes’ function is displayed.

  7. Status – The status of the unit is displayed. The displayed values are Allocated, Crossmatched, Issued, Transfused, Returned, Reverted, Outstanding, Compatible/Suitable, Incompatible, Suitable, Compatible, Least Incompatible, Reserved, Fated/disposed and Unit On Hold. The status of the blood units is based on the transactions ‘Allocate/Xmatch/Issue - Blood Units’ function.

  8. Reserve Date – The reserve date is displayed. After the allocation of blood units in ‘Allocate/Xmatch/Issue - Blood Units’ function, the blood unit is reserved for the patient. Reserve date is the date until which the blood unit can be reserved for the patient based on the expiry date of the blood unit.

  9. Reaction Cd – The Reaction Code entered during transfusion, if any is displayed.
