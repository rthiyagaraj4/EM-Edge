Crossmatch Comments

From this function you can Create Crossmatch Comments that are used in Crossmatch process. You can Query to view the existing records.

The Crossmatch Comments function is used to record Crossmatch Comments that are used during the crossmatching process. Cross-matching refers to the testing that is performed to determine the compatibility of a donated unit of blood with its intended recipient. It is used in determining good candidates for organ transplants. Cross-matching is done by a qualified laboratory technologist in a blood bank, and can be done electronically, with a computer database, or serologically.

Creating Crossmatch Comments

From the Masters, double-click Crossmatch Comments function.

The Crossmatch Comments screen gets displayed.

  1. Enter the details in the mandatory fields. <Related Field Description>

  2. Click [Commit] or press [F10] to save the created Crossmatch Comments or click [Clear Record] to clear the entered details.

  3. Click [Execute Query] or press [F8] to search for the existing records.
