Xmatch Method Details

From this function you can Query the Xmatch Method details and Print the details.

The Xmatch Method Details function is used to query the details of the Xmatch method performed before blood transfusion. You can print the Xmatch method details.

Querying Xmatch Method Details

From the Queries, double-click Xmatch Method Details function.

The Xmatch Method Details screen gets displayed.

  1. Enter the details in the respective fields. <Related Field Description>

  2. Click [Display] to display the Patient Id, patient Name, Specimen No, Unit No, Unit Status, Xmatch method, Description, Xmatch test, Description, Result Indicator, Xmatch Date/Time.

  3. Click [Execute Query] or press [F8] to search for the existing Xmatch Method Details records. You can navigate through the records using the upward and the downward arrows in the keyboard.

  4. To search a specific record, press [F7] or click [Enter Query], enter the value in a field and press [F8] or click [Execute Query]. The selected record is displayed.

  5. Click [Print] to print the Xmatch Method Details.
