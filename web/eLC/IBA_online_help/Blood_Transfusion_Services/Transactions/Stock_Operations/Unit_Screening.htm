Unit Screening

From this function you can Send blood units to the Laboratory for screening and View the units that are waiting results after undergoing a test in the Laboratory. You can also Add the units to stock or Reject from adding to stock.

The Unit Screening function is used to send the blood units to the Laboratory for screening. The blood units received from the external collection centre are sent for screening using this function. Only the products whose ‘Screening Required’ check boxes are selected in the ‘Product Codes’ and ‘Screening Setup for Collection Center’ functions are displayed in this function. If both the check boxes are cleared, the products are added to the Blood Transfusion stock on entering the stock in the ‘Blood Units Entry’ function. This function allows multiple blood units to be sent to lab, view all the blood units that are waiting results, accept all the resulted units from the lab and dispose units that are mismatching.

Blood Unit Screening

From the Transactions > Stock Operations, double-click Unit Screening function.

The Unit Screening screen gets displayed.

  1. Enter the search criteria and press [Tab] or [F8] or click [Execute Query] to view the unit details. <Related Field Description>

  2. The unit details are displayed in the following tabs:

Units for Screening

Awaiting Results

Results from Lab

  1. An asterisk adjacent to the record indicates the active record.

  2. Click [Commit] to send the blood units for screening.

  3. After the blood units are sent to laboratory, you have to do the Registration for the unit, Acknowledge/Verify Specimen in Acknowledge/Verify Specimen function, perform Result Entry by Specimen and do the Review. After the process is completed, the results are displayed in Results from Lab tab.

  4. Click [Commit] or press [F10] to save or click [Clear Record] to clear the entered details.

  5. Click [Execute Query] or press [F8] to search for the existing records.
