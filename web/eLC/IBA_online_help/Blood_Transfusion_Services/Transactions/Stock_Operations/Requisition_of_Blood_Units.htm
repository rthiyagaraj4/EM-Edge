Requisition of Blood Units

From this function you can Request for the blood products that are reduced below the reorder level.

The Requisition of Blood Units function is used to request the blood products that have reduced below the stock amount specified for reorder level. Reorder level is the level of stock in a Blood Bank based on which the decision is taken to reorder the blood product and fill the stock. This function enables you to print requisition letters and request the quantity of blood units from the Collection Centre/Suppliers. Different color codes are used to indicate if a blood product is below the reorder level or if requisition is generated for a product.

- Requisition Generated

- Below Re-order Level

Requisition of Blood Units

From the Transactions > Stock Operations, double-click Requisition of Blood Units function.

The Requisition of Blood Units screen gets displayed.

  1. Click [List of Values] to select the Collection or Supplier code. The list displays the values defined in the Collection Supplier function. This is a mandatory field. The orders placed to the selected Collection /Supplier is listed. <Related Field Description>

  2. Press [Tab], or press [F8] or click [Execute Query] on entering the search criteria to view the details. <Related Field Description>

  3. Enter the quantity of blood units required in numeric format of five digits maximum length. System displays the quantity as ‘1’, if you select the check box without specifying the quantity.

  4. Select the check box against the required product to request the number of units specified.

  5. Click [Save/Print Requisition] to record the request or print requisition letter.

  6. A report is generated listing the requisition details.
