Accept Transferred Blood Units

From this function you can Acknowledge the receipt of the blood units that are transferred from blood donor stock to the blood transfusion stock. You can View the requisitions and corresponding details that are pending for Acknowledgement.

The Accept Transferred Blood Units function is used to acknowledge receipt of the blood units that are transferred from blood donor stock to the blood transfusion stock. You can view the requisitions and corresponding details that are pending for acknowledgement. You can also query for all the requisitions and corresponding details that are pending for Acknowledgement. The criteria to display the information include the date range, the requisition number, or the combination of the Product Code, Blood Group and Rhesus Code.

Acknowledging Transferred Blood Units

From the Transactions > Stock Operations, double-click the Accept Transferred Blood Units function.

The Accept Transferred Blood Units screen gets displayed.

  1. Enter the query criteria to search for the details of transferred blood units. <Related Field Description>

  2. Press [Tab] to view blood unit details. <Related Field Description>

  3. Select the check box next to the unit number to select the blood unit to be accepted.

  4. Select the Select All check box to select all the blood units to be accepted.

  5. Click [Acknowledge] to accept the blood units from the donor stock to the blood transfusion stock, or [Cancel] to abort the transaction.
