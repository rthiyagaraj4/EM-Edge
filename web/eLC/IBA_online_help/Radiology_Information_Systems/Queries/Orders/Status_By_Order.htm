Status By Order

From this function you can View the status of a Radiology order.

You can view the Radiology Orders that are placed using the Order Entry and Result Reporting module.

Querying the Status of a Radiology Order

From the Queries > Order, double-click Status By Order function.

The Status By Order screen gets displayed.

  1. Enter the criteria to view the status of a Radiology Order or click [Execute Query] or press [F8] to view the status of a Radiology Order.

    Enter the following query criteria:

    1. Order No – Enter the Order Number in alphanumeric format of four characters maximum length or click [List of Values] to select the Order Number. The Order Number is generated while placing an order in Order Entry and Result Reporting module.

    2. Order Date – Enter the Order Date in the format of dd/mm/yyyy.

    3. Order Status – Enter the Order Status in alphanumeric format of two characters maximum length.

    4. Episode No – Enter the Episode Number of the encounter.

    5. Visit/Tfr No. – Enter the Visit/Transfer Number in alphanumeric format of six characters maximum length.

    6. Patient ID - Enter the Patient ID in the length as specified in the MP Parameter or select the Patient ID using the Standard Patient Lookup option. Right click ‘Right Click for User Options’ hyperlink to invoke Patient Lookup screen.

    7. Name – On selecting the Patient ID, the name of the patient is displayed by the system.

    8. Sex – On selecting the Patient ID, the Gender of the patient is displayed.

    9. Age – On selecting the Patient ID, the Age of the patient is displayed in Year/Month/Days format.

    10. Referral Source – Select the Referral Source from the list. The description of the referral source is displayed in the adjacent field.

    11. Referral Doctor – Enter the Referral Doctor’s code or click [List of Values] to select the referral doctor.

  2. On clicking [Search] or [Execute Query] or [F8], the order details are displayed.
