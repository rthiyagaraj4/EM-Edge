Enter the following details:

  1. Registration No – The Registration Number is auto generated on registering the examination.

  2. Registration Date – The current system date and time is displayed.

  3. Section/Wing – Enter the Section or Wing code or click [List of Values] to select the Section/Wing from which the film has come for reporting.

  4. Priority – Select the priority from the list. The options are Stat, Urgent and Routine.

  5. Patient ID – Enter the Patient ID in the length as specified in the MP Parameter or click ‘Right Click for User Options’ hyperlink to select the patient using Standard Patient Lookup or External Patient Lookup function. On selecting the Patient ID, the Patient Name, Gender and Age of the patient is displayed.

  6. Referral Source – Enter the Source code or click [List of Values] to select the Referral Source from which the film has come for reporting

  7. Digitize – Select the ‘Digitize’ check box to store the film and report on electronic media type. On selecting the ‘Digitize’ check box, Study Location column is displayed in the Examinations tab.

  8. No. of Films – Enter the number of films received for reporting in numeric format of two digits maximum length.

  9. Film ID – Enter the Film ID in alphanumeric format of 30 characters maximum length.

  10. Sender – Enter the name of the sender who has sent the film from the referral source.

  11. Receiver – Enter the name of the person who is receives the film at the Study Location for reporting.

  12. Received Date Time – Enter the date and time of receiving the film for reporting in the format of dd/mm/yyyy or select the received date and time using the Calendar function.

 

Click [New External Patient] to enter the details of the patient whose film has been sent in for reporting. Enter the details like the patient demographics, the address and the contact details of the patient.
