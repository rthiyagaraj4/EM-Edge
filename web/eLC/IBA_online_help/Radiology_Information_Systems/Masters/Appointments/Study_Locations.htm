Study Locations

From this function you can Define a Study Location and Query for an existing Study Location.

The Study Location is a Room in an Operating Unit attending to a particular Section that is associated to the Operating Unit. A Study Location consists of modalities (equipments) for conducting the radiology examination. From this function, you can associate Exams and modalities (equipments) to the Study Location. You can define whether the Study Location operates on all days, gender specific, the modality (equipment) is mobile and it is shared by other study locations associated with this modality.

Creating a Study Location

From the Masters > Appointments, double click Study Locations function.

The Study Locations screen gets displayed.

  1. Enter the details in the mandatory (*) fields. <Related Field Description>

  2. Click [Associated Locations for Modality] to view all the locations that are sharing the modality (equipment) if the modality (equipment) is shared by two or more study locations. This is an important criterion to book an appointment in Study Locations that are sharing modalities.

  3. Click [Save] or press [F10] to save a created Study Location or click [Clear Record] to clear the entered data.

  4. Click [Execute Query] or press [F8] to search for an existing Study Location.
