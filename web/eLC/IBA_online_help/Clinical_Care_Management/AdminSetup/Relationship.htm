Relationship

From this function you can Create a new Relationship that care providers can have with the patient by specifying it as Encounter Specific or Lifetime. You can also Query for an existing Relationship created.

A relationship is a method of identifying that a Practitioner is taking care of a patient and is responsible for the care of the patient. Relationship is broadly classified into Lifetime and Encounter Specific. Encounter Relationships are classified into Direct and Indirect Relationships. An Indirect Relationship is one that the system assigns implicitly based on certain transactions in the other modules like Visit Registration, Admit Patient, etc. On the other hand Direct Relationships are available to explicitly assign a Relationship. Additionally this function provides an expiry rule to be assigned for an Encounter Relationship to specify when a relationship ends. This feature is optional for the Lifetime Relationship.

Creating a Relationship

From the CA Admin Setup, select Relationship function.

The Relationship screen gets displayed.

  1. Click [Create] to invoke the Create screen.

  2. Enter the details in the mandatory (*) fields. <Related Field Description>

  3. Click [Apply] to save the Relationship created or [Reset] to clear the entered data.

  4. Click [Query] to search for existing Relationship record.
