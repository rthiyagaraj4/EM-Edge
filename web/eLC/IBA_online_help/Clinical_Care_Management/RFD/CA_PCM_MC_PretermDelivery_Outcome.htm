Outcome

  1. Click Outcome from the menu to record the outcome of the Preterm Delivery event.

  2. The mothercare pending tasks to be recorded for the event Preterm Delivery is displayed if any. Click [Record] to continue recording the outcome or click [Cancel] to perform the pending tasks for the event. On clicking [Record], the screen to record outcome gets displayed.

  3. The Outcome list box displays Delivered and Still Birth as the outcome for the event Preterm Delivery.
