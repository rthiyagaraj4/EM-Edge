Outcome

  1. Click Outcome from the menu to record the outcome of the Menopause event.

  2. The mothercare pending tasks to be recorded for the event Menopause is displayed if any. Click [Record] to continue recording the outcome or click [Cancel] to perform the pending tasks for the event. On clicking [Record], the screen to record outcome gets displayed.

  3. The Outcome list box displays Close as the outcome for the event Menopause. Select Close as Outcome and message is displayed indicating the current cycle of recording Menopause event for the patient will be closed and new cycle has to be created for recording details of any other events. Click [OK] to close recording Menopause event and the current cycle ends. Click [Cancel] to cancel closing the event and cycle.
