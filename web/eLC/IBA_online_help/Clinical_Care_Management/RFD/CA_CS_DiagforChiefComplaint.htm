Select the following to associate Diagnosis Codes to a Chief Complaint:

 

  1. Chief Complaint - Select the required Chief Complaint from the list. All the enabled chief complaints created in the function ‘Define Chief Complaint’ is listed in the list. This field is mandatory.

On selecting the required Chief Complaint, click [Search].

  1. Search Criteria – Click [Search] to display the entire list of diagnosis codes defined in Medical Records module, or enter the required search criteria in alphanumeric format of 20 characters maximum length. Click [Search] to display codes based on the entered search criteria.

The Diagnosis Code and Diagnosis Description are displayed.

  1. Select - Select the check box to select the required diagnosis to be associated with the selected chief complaint. The selected diagnosis is displayed under the ‘Selected Diagnosis’ header on the top part of the screen. The diagnosis selected for inclusion under the ‘Selected Diagnosis’ is excluded by clicking on the ‘Select’ check box.
