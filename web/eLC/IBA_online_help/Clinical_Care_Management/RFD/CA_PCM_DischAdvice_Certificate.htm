Certificate

Medical certificate is issued to the inpatients on discharge. The certificate provides the period of inpatient stay in the hospital and fitness of the patient to resume duty. The person authorizing the certificate is defaulted to the treating practitioner, but you can select other practitioners.

 

  1. Enter the details in the mandatory (*) fields. <Related Field Description>

  2. Click [OK] to save the medical certificate, [Clear] to clear the entered data or [Cancel] to exit from the Certificate screen.
