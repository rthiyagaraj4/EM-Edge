Related Patient Treatments

From this function you can View patients with similar treatments.

Related Patient Treatment function allows you to list patients with similar diagnosis. Based on the listed details, the practitioner can check the treatment that has been given to each of the patients with similar diagnosis.

Viewing Related Patient Treatments

From the CA Transactions > Clinician Access > Desktop Menu > History, select Related Patient Treatments.

The Related Patient Treatments screen gets displayed.

  1. Enter the details in mandatory (*) fields. <Related Field Description>

  2. Click [Search] to view related patient treatments or [Clear] to clear the entered data.

  3. On searching, patients with related treatments are displayed.

  4. Click Details icon to view the encounter summary of the patient.

  5. Select the Patient ID hyperlink of the desired record to view patient chart of the selected record. If Patient is not assigned a relationship between the patient and practitioner, the Assign Relationship screen is displayed to assign relationship. The Patient Chart is displayed after assigning relationship.
