Enter the following details:

 

  1. Document Type – Enter the valid Document Type in alphanumeric format of 6 characters maximum length or click [List of Values] to select the required Document Type. This is a field mandatory.

  2. Document No – Enter the Document Number in numeric format of eight digits maximum length. This is a mandatory field.

  3. Doc Date – Enter the Document Date in the format of dd/mm/yyyy or click the Calendar function to select the required Document Date. This is a mandatory field.

  4. Blng Grp – Enter the valid Billing Group or click [List of Values] to select the required Billing Group. This is a mandatory field.  

  5. Patient No – Enter the Patient ID in the length as defined in the MP Parameter or click [List of Values] to select the required Patient ID. This is a mandatory field.  

  6. Amount – Enter the Amount in decimal format of eight digits maximum length. This is a mandatory field.

  7. Bill Type – Enter the valid Bill Type or click [List of Values] to select the required Bill Type. This is a mandatory field.

 

Patient Related Bill Details (Single Invoice)

If the Invoice is consolidated and not a patient related Invoice, enter the following bill details:

 

  1. Document Type/No – Enter the valid Document Type or click [List of Values] to select the required Document Type. On selecting the Document Type, enter the Document Number in numeric format of eight digits maximum length. This is a mandatory field.

  2. Document Date - Enter the Document Date in the format of dd/mm/yyyy or click the Calendar function to select the required Document Date. This is a mandatory field.

  3. Billing Group – Enter the valid   Billing Group or click [List of Values] to select the required Billing Group. This is a mandatory field.  

  4. Patient No - Enter the Patient ID in the length as defined in the MP Parameter or click [List of Values] to select the required Patient ID. The Patient Name is displayed adjacent to the Patient ID. This is a mandatory field.  

  5. Bill Type Code - Enter the valid Bill Type or click [List of Values] to select the required Bill Type. This is a mandatory field.
