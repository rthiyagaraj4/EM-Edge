Enter the following details to record miscellaneous Customer details:

 

Name

  1. Long Name – Enter the name of the customer in alphanumeric format of 40 characters maximum length.  

  2. Short Name - Enter the short name in alphanumeric format of 15 characters maximum length.

 

Address & Tel No

  1. Address - It consist of 3 lines. Enter all the three lines address in alphanumeric format of 30 characters maximum length for each line.

  2. Tel No - Enter the Telephone Number  in alphanumeric format of 30 characters each.  

 

Contact Person

  1. Contact - Enter the Contact Name in alphanumeric format of 40 characters maximum length.

  2. Title - Enter the Title in alphanumeric format of 40 characters maximum length.

 

Local Language Name and Address

  1. Long Name - Enter the name of the customer in local language in alphanumeric format of 40 characters maximum length.  

  2. Short Name - Enter the name of the customer in local language in alphanumeric format of 15 characters maximum length.  

  3. Address - It consist of 3 lines. Enter all the three lines address in alphanumeric format of 30 characters maximum length for each line.

  4. [Close] - Click [Close] to record the entered details and exit from the Miscellaneous Customer’s Details screen.
