Enter Adjustments

From this function you can Generate an Adjustment Document for Customers to record the correction or adjust the amount against the deposit collected. You can Query to view the adjustments recorded for specific Customers.

The Enter Adjustments function is used to record the adjustment details for the Customers. One of the criteria for raising Adjustment entries is to rectify the mismatching balances of the transactions or erroneous entries. The sum of adjustment amount has to match the document amount.

Adjusting the amount

From the Transactions > Adjustments, double-click Enter Adjustments function.

The Enter Adjustments screen gets displayed.

  1. Enter the details in the mandatory (*) fields. <Related Field Description>

  2. Click [Commit Form] or press [F10] to save a generated Adjustment or click [Clear Record] to clear the entered or queried data.

  3. Click [Execute Query] or press [F8] to search for an existing Adjustment record.
