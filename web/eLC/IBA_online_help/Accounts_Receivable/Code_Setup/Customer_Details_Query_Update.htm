Customer Details Query/Update

From this function you can Query and Update a Customer record within the facility.

The function Customer Details Query/Update  helps to query the Customer details. You can also update the details defined using the function Customer. The function also allows you to update the data other than the Customer Code. You can sort the details in the screen either in ascending or descending order by clicking on the selected column header.

Querying or Updating a Customer

From the Masters, double-click Customer Details Query/Update function.

The Customer Details Query/Update screen gets displayed.

  1. Click [Execute Query] or press [F8] to list all the Customer details.

OR

  1. Click [List of Values] and click [Execute Query] or press [F8] to list relevant Customers’ details. You can search for a Customer’s record based on the associated Customer Type, Customer Group or Payment Terms.

  2. Click [Customer Details] to view all the particulars of a relevant Customer. The details are populated in the Customer  screen. You can update the values in any field other than the Code Setup.

  3. Click [Commit Form] or press [F10] to save the records or [Clear Record] to clear the entered data.
