Payment Terms

From this function you can Create a payment term to classify the Customers according to the credit facility and Query for a desired payment term record.

The function Payment Terms is used to classify Customers according to the credit facility offered by the organization. It is advisable to have a unique code for each credit facility offered. Multiple payment terms are used to specify the credit days, settlement days and the settlement percentage offered to the Customer by the facility. The payment terms created gets listed in the Customer Group and Customer functions. You can associate the selected Payment Term to the Customer Group and the Customer using the respective functions.  

Creating a Payment Term

From the Masters, double-click Payment Terms function.

The Payment Term screen gets displayed.

  1. Enter the details in the mandatory (*) fields. <Related Field Description>

  2. Click [Commit Form] or press [F10] to save a created Payment Term or [Clear Record] to clear the entered or queried data.

  3. Click [Execute Query] or press [F8] to search for an existing Payment Term.
