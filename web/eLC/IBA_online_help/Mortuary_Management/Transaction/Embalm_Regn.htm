Embalm Registration

From this function, you can Create the embalm registration as requested for the deceased.

An embalm service is requested for the deceased for various reasons. Embalm is a process of preserving the deceased body for a longer period of time without decomposition. It can be requested in any of the following scenarios:

 

  • Embalm service requested at hospital

  • Embalm service requested at home

 

Also, embalm service can be requested irrespective of where the patient has deceased, either at home or hospital. You can Place Order for Embalm service even without performing post-mortem.

Registering Embalm Service

From MO Transactions, select the Embalm Registration function.

The Embalm Registration screen gets displayed.

  1. Click [Create] to invoke the Create screen. The Patient ID field is displayed.

  2. Click [?] and select a patient ID from the lookup.

  3. Click [Search] to display the patient details and the embalm registration fields are displayed on the screen.

  4. Enter the details in the mandatory (*) fields. <Related Field Description>

  5. Click [Apply] to save the embalm registration or [Reset] to clear the entered data.

  6. Click [Query] to search for existing embalm registration records.

Viewing Embalm Statuses in Query Mode

You can view the Embalm Registration statuses in the Query Results:

 

  • Registered – Embalm registered

  • Completed – Embalm completed

  • Cancelled – Embalm Cancelled

 

1. You can modify the registration details for records with  "Registered" and "Completed" Status by Clicking on the Sl.No hyperlink.

For records with "Cancelled" Status, the Sl.No hyperlink will not be enabled.

 

2. Also, you can click [Contact Details] and enter the contact details of the applicant and the address for embalm. If both are the same, you can click the Copy Applicant Address hyperlink. The same will get copied in the Embalm address field.
