Reprint Specimens Sent to Referral Labs

From this function you can Reprint the details of the specimen to the Referral Laboratory for investigation.

Reprint Specimens Send to Referral Labs function is used to Reprint the specimens sent to Referral Labs or In-house for investigation.

Reprint Specimens Sent to Referral Labs

From Laboratory > Transactions > Specimen menu, select the Reprint Specimens Sent to Referral Labs function.

The Specimens Sent to Referral Labs screen gets displayed.

  1. Enter the data in the mandatory (*) fields. <Related Field Description>

  2. Click [Patient Details] to view the specimen details satisfying the specified query range.

    The following details are displayed:

    1. Specimen No - Specimen numbers that are to be sent to Referred labs are displayed in this field.

    2. Patient No - The patient Number to which the specimen belongs is displayed.

    3. Test - The name of the Test referred out is displayed.

  3. Click [Print List] to print the specimen list.
