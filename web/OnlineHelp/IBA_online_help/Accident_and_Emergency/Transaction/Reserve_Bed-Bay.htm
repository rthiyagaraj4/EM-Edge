Reserve Bed/Bay

From this function you can Reserve a vacant bed or bay in a Treatment Area for a specific period with a valid reason.

Reserve Bed/Bay function is used to block a normal vacant bed which cannot be used for patient occupancy. On completion of the blocking period recorded, the system releases the blocked bed or bay automatically.

 

Process Flow Diagram

Reserving Bed or Bay

From the AE Transactions, select the Reserve Bed/Bay Function.

Reserve Bed/Bay screen gets displayed.

  1. Enter the details in the mandatory (*) fields. <Related Field Description>

  2. Click [Apply] to block a Bed or Bay for a particular date and time or [Reset] to clear the entered data.
