Revise Attendance Details

From this function you can Revise the Attendance Details of an emergency patient within the number of days specified in the AE Parameter.

Attendance details are revised after the attendance registration is complete. Revision of attendance details includes revision of employment details, arrival details, and related contact details. You are allowed to enter details to the fields while revising attendance details. Some details captured while registering the visit are displayed and you cannot modify any of the displayed details.

 

Hospital and Professional Charges are displayed on recording the financial details of a patient. The patient payable amount towards Hospital charge as well as Doctor’s charge is displayed. Professional Charge is displayed along with the Hospital Charge, only if the billing details and visit registration rules are defined for the Clinic. The doctor’s charge has to be a part of the panel, if the Professional charge has to be set as zero. If the Professional Charge is set as a Service, you cannot set the doctor’s fee as zero. You can instead associate the Hospital Charges to make up the Professional fees, when the doctor does not charge the patient for the services.

 

Process Flow Diagram

Revising Attendance Details

From the AE Transactions, select the Revise Attendance Details Function.

The Revise Attendance Details screen gets displayed.

  1. Enter the details in the mandatory (*) fields. <Related Field Description>

  2. Click [Search] to display the list of patient visit records or click [Clear] to clear the displayed data.

    The following Patient Details are displayed:

    1. Encounter ID – The Encounter Identification Number for the selected Visit gets displayed. This field is a hyperlink and allows the selection of the patient to revise the visit details.

    2. Registration Date/Time – The Date/Time of visit registration is displayed.

    3. Patient ID – The Patient Identification Number generated during Visit Registration is displayed.

    4. Patient Name – The Name of the patient gets displayed.

    5. Location – The Location, where the patient has visited gets displayed.

    6. Practitioner Name – The Practitioner Name assigned to the particular patient gets displayed.

    7. Queue Status – The Queue Status of the Patient gets displayed.

  3. Click ‘Encounter ID’ hyperlink of a particular patient whose attendance details need to be revised. Revise Attendance Details screen displays with three tabs, namely, Visit Detail, Arrival and Related Contacts. <Related Field Description>

  4. Click [Apply] to save the revision of attendance details of a particular patient or [Reset] to clear the entered data.
