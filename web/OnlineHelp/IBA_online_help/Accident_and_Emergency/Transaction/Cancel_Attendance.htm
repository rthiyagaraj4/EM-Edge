Cancel Attendance

From this function you can Cancel attendance registered against the Emergency Location.

Cancel Attendance function is used to rectify any erroneous entry or cancel attendance for patients’ refusing to proceed with treatment after being registered. Cancellation of an attendance is not allowed if any clinical details are recorded against a patient encounter.

 

Process Flow Diagram

Cancelling Attendance

From the AE Transactions, select the Cancel Attendance Function.

Cancel Attendance screen gets displayed.

  1. Enter details in mandatory fields. <Related Field Description>

  2. Enter the date by clicking the Calendar function. The current date is displayed, by default. This field is mandatory.

  3. Click [Search] to display patient visit records or click [Clear] to clear the displayed data.

    The following Patient Details are displayed:

    1. Encounter ID – The Encounter Identification Number is displayed. This field is a hyperlink, which allows selection of the patient to cancel the visit.

    2. Registration Date/Time – The Date and Time of the registration is displayed.

    3. Patient Details – The Patient Identification Number, Name, Age and Gender is displayed.

    4. Location – The Location of the patient visit is displayed.

    5. Treatment Area – The Treatment Area assigned to a patient during attendance registration is displayed.

    6. Queue Status – The Queue Status of the Patient is displayed.

  4. Click ‘Encounter ID’ hyperlink of a particular visit to be cancelled and Cancel Attendance screen gets displayed with visit details.

  5. Enter Reason for Cancellation details.

    Queue Status

    1. Status - Current Queue Status of a Patient for a selected visit is displayed along with the date/time when the visit was registered.

    2. Appointment – This field is disabled for Emergency patients.

    3. Sub-Service – The Sub-Service associated to a visit is displayed.

    4. Visit Type – The Visit Type associated to the visit and the visit type indicator is displayed.

    Treatment Area Detail

    1. Priority Zone – The Priority Zone assigned to the patient during attendance registration is displayed.

    2. Treatment Area – The Treatment Area assigned to the patient during attendance registration is displayed.

    3. Bed/Bay No. – The Bed or Bay Number assigned to the patient during attendance registration is displayed.

    Reason for Cancellation

    1. Cancel - Select the check box to cancel the selected visit. The check box is selected, by default.

    2. Reason – The Reason field is enabled on selecting the ‘Cancel’ check box. Select a Reason to cancel the visit in the Emergency Location. The Reasons defined in ‘Other Master Setup’ of Application Masters module are displayed. This field is mandatory.

  6. Click [Apply] to save the record or [Reset] to clear the entered data.
