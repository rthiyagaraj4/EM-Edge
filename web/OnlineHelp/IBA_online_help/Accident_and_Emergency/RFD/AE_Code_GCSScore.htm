The following details are displayed:

  1. Eye Response – The score for various details listed in Eye Response namely, Opens Eye Spontaneously, Opens Eye in response to Speech, Opens Eye in response to Pain and No Response are set to a score on the scale of 1 to 4. The scoring for any two details cannot be the same.

  2. Verbal Response - The score for various details listed in Verbal Response, namely, Orientated, Confused Conversation, Inappropriate words, Incomprehensible Sounds and No Response are set to a score on the scale of 1 to 5. The scoring for any two details cannot be the same.

  3. Motor Response - The score for various details listed in motor response namely, Obeys Commands, localizes to Pain, Withdrawals to Pain, Flex on to Pain, Extension to Pain and No Response are set to a score on the scale of 1 to 6. The scoring should be different for all the details.
