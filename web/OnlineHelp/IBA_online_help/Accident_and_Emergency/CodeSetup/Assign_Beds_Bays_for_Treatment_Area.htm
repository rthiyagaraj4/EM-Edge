Assign Bed/Bays for Treatment Area

From this function you can Associate a Bed/Bay to a Treatment Area in an Emergency Location and Query for a desired Bed/Bay associated to a Treatment Area.

Assign Bed/Bays for a Treatment Area is an option provided to assign bed/bay to a Treatment Area in a Location. You have an option to assign either all, normal or pseudo bed/bay to a Treatment Area. The associated bed/bays are applicable to the selected Treatment Area in a Location.

Creating an association of Bed/Bays to a Treatment Area

From the AE Code Setup, select the Assign Bed/Bays for Treatment Area function.

The Assign Bed/Bays for Treatment Area screen gets displayed.

  1. Click [Create] to invoke the Create screen.

  2. Enter the details in the mandatory (*) fields. <Related Field Description>

  3. Click [Search] to display the Bed/Bay details. Select the check box against the Bed/Bay to associate the Bed/Bay to a Treatment Area or [Clear] to clear the entered data.

  4. Click [Apply] to assign Bed/Bays to a Treatment Area or [Reset] to clear the entered data.

  5. Click [Query] to search for existing Bed/Bays for a Treatment Area.
