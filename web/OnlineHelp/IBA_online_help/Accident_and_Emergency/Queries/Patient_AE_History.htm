Patient AE History

From this function you can Query the patient's history of visits to the emergency department.

Patient AE History function is used to query on a patient's history of visits to the emergency department. This function provides various query criteria that is used to filter the list of emergency visits recorded against a patient. This function is used to view the various emergency visits of a patient and the recorded outcomes of each visit.

Querying Patient History

From the AE Queries, select the Patient AE History function.

The Patient AE History screen gets displayed.

  1. Enter the Patient ID or Encounter ID to find the patient history details or enter the search criteria. <Related Field Description>

  2. Click [Search] to select a specific record or [Clear] to clear the records displayed in the result area.

  3. Click ‘+’ hyperlink to view patient details.

  4. Click ‘Secondary Triage’ hyperlink to view triage details pertaining to an episode.
