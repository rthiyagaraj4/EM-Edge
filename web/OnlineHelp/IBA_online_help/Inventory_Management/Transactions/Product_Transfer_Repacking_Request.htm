Product Transfer/Repacking Request

From this function you can raise Request for a Product Transfer and Re-pack the product.

You can specify the Repack Store, the Item required to be repacked, required quantity of the Item, the Item to be used, repack factor and the quantity.

Raising Request for Product Transfer

From the ST Transactions > Transfer, select the Product Transfer/Repacking Request function.

The Product Transfer/Repacking Request screen gets displayed.

  1. Enter the details in the mandatory (*) fields. <Related Field Description>

  2. Click [Apply] to generate the request or [Reset] to clear the entered data.
