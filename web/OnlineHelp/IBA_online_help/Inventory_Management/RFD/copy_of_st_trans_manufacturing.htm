Enter the following details to add the Items required to manufacture the entered Item:

  1. Click ‘Document’ hyperlink to modify the document details.

  2. On entering the Document Details, click [OK] to record the Manufacturing details or [Reset] to clear the entered data.

  3. Enter the details in the mandatory (*) fields. <Related Field Description>

  4. Click [Get Data] to display the Ingredient(s) associated to the requested Manufacturing Item on the left hand side of the screen.

  5. Click Item Details hyperlink to display the details of the Ingredients. Based on the composition quantity and the Ingredients quantity, the Value is calculated and displayed.

  6. On clicking the Item Details hyperlink, the details of the Items are displayed on the right hand side of the screen.

  7. The quantity of Item selected for manufacturing is displayed. You can update the displayed quantity by entering a numeric value of six digits maximum length. This is a mandatory field.

  8. Click [Edit/View] to include the first expiring batch in the slot for manufacturing. On selecting [Edit/View], a message ‘Batch Date will be refreshed’ is displayed’. Click [OK] to overwrite the Batch Details or [Cancel] to retain the previous batch details. The first expiring batch is displayed, by default.

  9. Click [Batch Search] to view the Batch details. The selected Item Batch details are displayed. Select the check box to select the Batch and click [Close] to exit from the batch search screen. If batch details are selected using [Edit/View], the selected Batch details are refreshed on selecting the new batch.

  10. On selecting the batch, the batch details are displayed in the Batch Entry area.

  11. Enter the Quantity in numeric format of five digits maximum length.

  12. Click [Modify] to update the changes or [Cancel] to reset the changes.

  13. Click [Apply] to finalize the Manufacturing details or [Reset] to clear the entered data.

  14. The ‘Confirm Finalize’ dialog is displayed. Click [Yes] to finalize the Manufacturing details or [No] to finalize the Manufacturing details in the query mode.

  15. The ‘Confirm Printing’ dialog is displayed based on the attribute set for the Document Type. Click [Yes] to print the Manufacturing details or [No] to cancel the action.

  16. Click [Query] to invoke the Query screen.

  17. The unauthorized Manufacturing details are displayed based on the default criteria or enter the search criteria and click [Search] to select a specific record.

    Enter the following query criteria:

    1. Store – Select the ‘Store’ from the list. Only those stores are displayed for which you have access and ‘Manufacturing’ transaction function applicable.

    2. Doc Type – Select the Document Type from the list. The values listed are defined in the function ‘ST Document Type’ and the default value displayed is specified in the function ‘ST Facility Parameter’.

    3. Doc No. – Enter the Document Number in numeric format of eight digits maximum length.

  18. On searching, the unauthorized Manufacturing details are displayed.

  19. Click ‘Doc Type’ hyperlink to authorize the Manufacturing details.

  20. Click [Apply] to finalize the Manufacturing details or [Delete] to delete the selected record.

  21. On clicking [Apply], the ‘Confirm Finalize’ dialog is displayed. Click [Yes] to finalize the Manufacturing details or [No] to finalize the Manufacturing details in the query mode.

  22. On selecting the ‘Delete’ check box, ‘Confirm Delete’ dialog is displayed. Click [Yes] to delete the record or [No] to cancel the action.
