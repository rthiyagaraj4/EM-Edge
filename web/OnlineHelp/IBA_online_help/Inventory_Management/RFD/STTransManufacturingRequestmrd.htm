Enter the following details to add the Items required to manufacture the entered Item:

  1. Ingredients - Enter the required Ingredients name in alphanumeric format of 60 characters maximum length or select the Item using [?] function. The Items defined as ‘Manufacturing’ are displayed in the search result screen. This is a mandatory field.

  2. Stock UOM – The Unit of Measure of the selected Ingredients is displayed.

  3. Quantity – Enter the quantity of Ingredients required in numeric format of eight digits maximum length. This is a mandatory field.

  4. MFG UOM – Select the required manufacturing UOM from the list. This is a mandatory field.

  5. Click [Add] to associate the Ingredients to main manufacturing Item, [Cancel] to clear the entered data or [Close] to exit from the Manufacturing Request Details screen.

  6. Select the check box to delete the Ingredients from association.

  7. Click Item hyperlink to [Modify] the associated Ingredients, [Cancel] to reset the changes or [Close] to exit from the Manufacturing Request Details screen.

Last entered/selected Item record is highlighted by a unique color.
