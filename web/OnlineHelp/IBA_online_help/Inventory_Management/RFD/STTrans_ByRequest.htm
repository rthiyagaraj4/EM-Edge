1. Enter the search criteria and click [Search] to select specific manufacturing requests.

  2. On searching, the manufacturing requests are displayed based on the entered criteria.

  3. Click Worksheet icon to generate the Manufacturing Item worksheet.

    1. The Report Options screen gets displayed.

    2. Select ‘Report Option’ to generate the Manufacturing Item worksheet.

    3. Click [OK] to generate the Manufacturing Item worksheet and spool it in the file on the print server or [Cancel] to cancel the action.

  4. Click Manufacturing Item hyperlink to record the Manufacturing details.
