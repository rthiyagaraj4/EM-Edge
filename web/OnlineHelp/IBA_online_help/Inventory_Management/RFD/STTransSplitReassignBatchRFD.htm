Enter the following details:

  1. Quantity - Enter the quantity of the Item in numeric format of six digits maximum length. The entered quantity must not be greater than the available quantity of the Item or an error message ‘Transaction quantity cannot be greater than available quantity’ is displayed. This is a mandatory field.

  2. Cost – The cost is displayed based on the Item cost entered.

  3. Value – The Value is displayed and computed based on the quantity and cost entered. This is a mandatory field.

  4. Remarks – Enter the Remarks in alphanumeric format or select the required Remarks using [?] function.
