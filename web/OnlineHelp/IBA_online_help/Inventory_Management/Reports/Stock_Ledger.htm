Stock Ledger

From this function you can List all types of finalized stock transactions for a specified period range.

The stock ledger is a detailed report, which gives information against an Item, Store or a combination of both. This report contains details of the transactions performed on an Item or from a Store including the Opening and Closing Stock Balances.

Listing Finalized Stock Transactions

From the ST Reports, select the Stock Ledger function.

The Stock Ledger screen gets displayed.

  1. Enter the details in the mandatory fields. <Related Field Description>

  2. Click [Run] to generate the report based on the entered report criteria or [Reset] to clear the entered data.

  3. Select ‘Report Option’ to generate the report.

  4. Click [OK] to generate the report and spool it in the file on the print server or [Cancel] to cancel the action.
