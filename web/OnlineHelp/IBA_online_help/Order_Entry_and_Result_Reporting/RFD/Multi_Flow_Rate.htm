Multi Flow Rate

Click Multi Flow Rate hyperlink to allow the prescribing practitioner to record multi flow rate for a particular IV Medication with different infusion over time. The Multi Flow Rate is applicable for IV Fluids with additives, IV continuous and not applicable for IV intermittent. The Multiple Flow Rate is not applicable for IV Admixture.

Click ‘Multi Flow Rate’ to invoke the Administration Details screen.

Administration Details

  1. Total Infusion Volume – The total number of infusion volume rate of the selected additive is displayed, by default. The volume of the infusion recorded is the Total infusion volume.

  2. Total Flow Duration – The total flow Duration of the selected additive is displayed. The value entered in the Infuse Over field is displayed, by default.

The total flow duration for the infusion volume should not exceed more than 24:00 hours for the IV additives.

  1. Total Duration - The total flow duration for the infusion volume including the gap time is displayed, by default.

The Administration details recorded for the Additive is displayed.

  1. Flow Rate # – The number of flow rates recorded for the additives are displayed.

  2. Infuse Vol – The total number of infusion volume is displayed, by default.

  3. Infusion Rate – Enter the infusion rate in numerical format of 7 digits maximum length. The infusion rates are defined for the various flow rates. This is mandatory field. The calculation is done as follows

Total Volume = Order Quantity X Volume/Unit

  1. Infuse Over – The infuse over values are auto calculated and displayed based on the following calculation:

Infuse Over = Total Volume / Infusion Rate

  1. Start Time – The start Time is automatically calculated and displayed based on the Infuse Over. This is a mandatory field.

  2. End Time – The start Time is automatically calculated and displayed based on the Infuse Over. You cannot edit the date.

  3. Order Quantity – The value ‘1’ is displayed by default. You cannot enter the decimal value. This is mandatory field. This field is displayed only . If the value ‘Quantity is selected in the Infuse Over Calculate by drop-down list in PH Parameter screen.

  4. Total Volume - The Total Volume field is defaulted with one unit of the selected fluids. The Total Volume is calculated once the Order Quantity is entered. Only Doctor can modify the Total Volume. If the value ‘Quantity is selected in the Infuse Over Calculate by drop-down list in PH Parameter screen.

  1. Infusion Gap Time – Enter the infusion gap time in hh:mm format to record and display the time gap between two consecutive infusions with different infusion rate. The time gap at the end of each flow against a particular flow rate is computed for the gap of time between the previous flow rate and the current flow rate. The infusion gap time is not applicable for the first flow rate.

  2. Admin Rate – Click ‘Admin Rate’ hyperlink to view the Drug Administration details. The admin rate is calculated based on the flow rate, weight and BMI.

  3. – Click to validate the changes made to the Multi Flow Rate. The system displays an error message ‘Changes made to any of the Flow Rates needs to be validated’ if you record the infusion rate without validating. On validating the infusion rate you can enter the multi flow rate remarks in ‘MFR Remarks’ hyperlink.

  4. + - Click  icon to add additional flow rates.

  5. [X] – Click [X] to cancel the additional flow rate.

 

Click [OK] to record the Multi flow rate administration details or [Cancel] to clear the entered details.
