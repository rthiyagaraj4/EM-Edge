Stain Indicator

From this function you can Create stain indicator codes that are used in anatomic pathology and Query for the existing records.

Stain Indicator codes are used to identify the stains types in the sample specimens submitted for investigation.

Defining Stain Indicator Codes

From Masters > Anatomic Pathology, double-click Stain Indicator function.

The Stain Indicator screen gets displayed.

  1. Enter the data in the mandatory (*) fields. <Related Field Description>

  2. Click [Commit] or press [F10] to save the created records or click [Clear Record] to clear the entered data.

  3. Click [Execute Query] or press [F8] to search for the existing records.
