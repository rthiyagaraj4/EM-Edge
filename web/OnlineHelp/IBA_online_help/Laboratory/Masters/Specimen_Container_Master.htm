Specimen Container Master

From this function you can Create various types of Specimen Containers. You can Query for the existing records.

The Specimen Container Master function is used to maintain various types of specimen containers. Specimen Containers are categorized into different types based on the volume and other dimensions.

Creating Specimen Container Master

From the Masters, double-click the Specimen Container Master function.

The Specimen Container Master screen gets displayed.

  1. Enter the data in the mandatory fields. <Related Field Description>

  2. Click [Save] or press [F10] to save the created records or click [Clear Record] to clear the entered data.

  3. Click [Execute Query] or press [F8] to search for existing records.
