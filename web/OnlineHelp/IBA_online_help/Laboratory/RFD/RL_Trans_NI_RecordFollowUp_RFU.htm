Enter the following details:

  1. Serial No. – The Serial number of the selected patient is displayed.

  2. Status – Select the status from the list. The listed values are ‘In-process’, ‘Outstanding’ and ‘Completed’. On selecting the Status, click [Tab] to display the selected patients details.

  3. Patient ID – The Patient ID of the patient is displayed.

  4. Sex – The Gender of the patient is displayed.

  5. Date of Birth – The Date of Birth of the patient is displayed.

  6. Race – The Race of the patient is displayed.

  7. Date of Admission – The Admission date of the patient is displayed.

  8. Date of Discharge – If the patient is discharged, discharge date is displayed.

  9. Onset Date – The Onset date of the patient is displayed.

  10. Ward – The Ward at which the patient is admitted is displayed.

  11. Specialty – The Specialty associated to the patient is displayed.

  12. Diagnosis – The Diagnosis details of the patient is displayed.

  13. Remarks – The Remarks record for the patient is displayed.

  14. Completed By – The name of the physician who has completed the follow up task is displayed.

  15. Completed Date/Time – The Completion date and time of the follow up task is displayed.
