Print Setup Tab

Enter the following details in the Print Setup tab:

  1. Laboratory Title – Enter the Title for Laboratory in alphanumeric format of 80 characters maximum length. You can enter laboratory title in five lines, but the first line is mandatory. The title recorded gets displayed in reports. This is a mandatory field.

 

Print Results for

  1. Inpatients – Select the check box to print Inpatients results on completion of the investigation. Clear the check box, if Inpatient results are not required on completion of investigation.

  2. Outpatients – Select the check box to print Outpatients results on completion of the investigation. Clear the check box, if Outpatient results are not required on completion of investigation.

  3. Referral - Select the check box to print Referral patient results on completion of the investigation. Clear the check box, if Referral patient results are not required on completion of investigation.

  4. External - Select the check box to print External patient results on completion of the investigation. Clear the check box, if external patient results are not required on completion of investigation.

  5. Emergency – Select the check box to print Emergency patient results on completion of the investigation. Clear the check box, if emergency patient results are not required on completion of investigation.

  6. Discharged/Deceased - Select the check box to print Discharged/Deceased Patients results on completion of the investigation. Clear the check box, if discharged/deceased patient results are not required on completion of investigation.

 

Fax Results for

  1. Inpatients – Select the check box to fax Inpatient results on completion of the investigation.

  2. Outpatients – Select the check box to fax Outpatient results on completion of the investigation.

  3. Referral - Select the check box to fax Referral patient results on completion of the investigation.

  4. External - Select the check box to fax External patient results on completion of the investigation.

  5. Discharged/Deceased – Select the check box to fax Discharged/Deceased patient results on completion of the investigation.

  6. Emergency – Select the check box to fax Emergency patient results on completion of the investigation.
