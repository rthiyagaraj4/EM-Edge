Ext. Patient Info

The external patient details are displayed in the General and Additional Tab.

 

General

  1. Ext/Ref Source – Enter the External or Referral Source code or click [List of Values] to select the External or Referral Source.

  2. Source Prefix & Patient ID – Enter the source prefix and patient identification number in alphanumeric format of 18 characters maximum length.

  3. ID No – Enter the identification number is alphanumeric format of 20 characters maximum length.

  4. Ext. Patient ID – Enter the external patient identification in alphanumeric format of 20 characters maximum length.

  5. Insurance ID – Enter the insurance identification number in alphanumeric format of 30 characters maximum length.

  6. First Name – Enter the first name of the patient in alphanumeric format of 80 characters maximum length.

  7. Second Name – Enter the second name of the patient in alphanumeric format of 80 characters maximum length.

  8. Previous HN – Enter the Previous Hospital Number in alphanumeric format of maximum length as specified in the MP Parameter and enter the expiry date of the Previous HN.

  9. Family Name – Enter the Family Name in alphanumeric of 80 characters of maximum length has specified in MP Parameter.

  10. Government No. – Enter the Government Number in alphanumeric format of 30 characters maximum length as specified in the MP Parameter.

  11. Expatriate No. – Enter the Expatriate Number in alphanumeric format of 30 characters maximum length as specified in the MP Parameter.

  12. Contact No. – Enter the contact number of the patient in alphanumeric format of 30 characters maximum length.

  13. Contact Mode – Select the contact mode of the patient from the list. The listed values are ‘E-mail’, ‘Fax’, ‘Mobile No.’, ‘Primary Resi No’, ‘Tel (Home)’.

  14. Birth Date – Enter the date of birth of the patient in alphanumeric format of 30 characters maximum length or select the Birth Date using the Calendar function.

  15. Alt Type/No – Select the Alternate ID Type from the list and enter the alternate number.

  16. Gender – Select the gender of the patient. The listed values are ‘Male’, ‘Female’ and ‘Unknown’.

  17. Nationality – Enter the Nationality code or click [List of Values] to select the Nationality. The listed values are defined in ‘Country’ function in Patient Identity Management module.

  18. Race – Enter the race code or click [List of Values] to select the Race. The listed values are defined in ‘Race’ function of Master Patient Index module.

  19. Place of Birth – Enter the place of birth code or click [List of Values] to select the place of birth. The listed values are defined in Patient Identity Management module.

  20. Order by – Select the ‘Order By’ value from the list. The listed values are ‘Patient Name’, ‘Sex’, ‘Date of Birth’ and ‘NRIC’.

The search criteria is displayed based on the MP Parameter setup set in the Master Patient Index module.

Additional

  1. Deceased Only – Select the check box to display the deceased patients.

  2. Suspended Only – Select the check box to display the suspended patients.

  3. Inactive Only – Select the check box to display the inactive patients.

  4. New – Click [New] to register the an external patient.

  5. Search – Click [Search] to search the patient based on the entered criteria.

 

On searching, the Donor details of external patient is displayed.
