Registration

From this function you can Register the specimen ordered through the Order Entry and Result Reporting module or the Laboratory.

This function is used to capture the details of the request and to specify a unique specimen number for the request. On registering a specimen if verification function is not applicable for the department, the specimen is available for further processing. If the analyzers are linked to the system, the data to program the analyzers can be downloaded automatically or as a batch. It is used to print the referral charges slip for external patients.

The system alerts about drug-lab interaction while placing/adding tests based on the setup done in Drug Interaction screen in PH. You can override the alert by entering remarks. In case, if the drug is restricted, you cannot proceed with order placement.

When you try to register services for an inpatient after the patient’s Discharge Bill is generated, an error message is displayed if the ‘Allow Specimen Registration even if Patient Discharge Bill Generated’ check box is cleared in the Parameter for Facility screen. Select this check box to register services for an inpatient even if the patient’s Discharge Bill is generated.

Registration of the Specimens

From Laboratory Transactions > Specimen menu, select the Registration function.

The Registration screen gets displayed.

  1. Scan the Specimen number using Barcode Scanner or click [F8] to display the first specimen number details, by default or enter the specimen number and [Close/Confirm].

  2. Enter the details in the mandatory (*) fields <Related Field Description>

  3. Click [Select Encounter] to select the encounter and register the specimen.

  4. Click [Ext.Patient Info] to select the outstanding orders of external patients.

  5. Click [Hospital Orders] to retrieve the outstanding hospital orders.

  6. Click [Screen Units] to select the outstanding orders from the blood donor or blood transfusion department.

  7. Click [Order Comments] to view order comments of the practitioner placing order.

  8. Click [Recall Patient Info] to recall the patient information.

  9. Click [New Patient] to register the patient.

  10. Click [Add Clinical History] to add the clinical history details.

  11. Click [Request Units] to request the blood units.

  12. Click [Print Labels] and the Reprint Specimen Label screen is displayed.

  13. Click [Reg Specimen] to register the specimen.
