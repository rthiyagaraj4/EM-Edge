Acknowledge Results from Referral Labs

From this function you can Accept the results sent from the referral labs.

Acknowledge Result from Referral Labs function used to acknowledge the receipt of results for specimens from Referred Labs. The referral lab must be linked to the hospital in the Referral Labs setup, if the hospital is within organization’s network.

Accepting Acknowledge Result from Referral Labs

From Laboratory > Transactions > Specimen menu, select the Acknowledge Result from Referral Labs function.

The Acknowledge Result from Referral Labs screen gets displayed.

  1. Enter the details in the respective fields. <Related Field Description>

  2. Click [Patient Details] to view the patient details.

  3. Click [Print List] to print the specimen details.

  4. Enter the received date or select it from calendar function and click [Result] to invoke Result Entry by Specimen screen. You can alter, accept or view the results based on the privileges.
