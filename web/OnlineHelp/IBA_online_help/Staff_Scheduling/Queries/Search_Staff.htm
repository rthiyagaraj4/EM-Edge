Search Staff

From this function you can Search for the Staffs in the enterprise.

The function also helps in querying staff details like identity, qualification, training and contact details that were recorded while creating the staff in the Application Masters module. It also queries the skill, experience, training and licensure details of the staffs recorded while creating the staff profile in the Staff Profile function.

Querying for Staff

From RS Queries, select Search Staff function.

The Search Staff screen gets displayed.

  1. Enter the details in the mandatory (*) fields. <Related Field Description>

  2. Click [Search] to view the staff details or [Reset] to clear the entered search criteria.
