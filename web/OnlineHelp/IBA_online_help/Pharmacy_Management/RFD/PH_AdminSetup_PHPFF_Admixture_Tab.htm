Admixture Tab

You can define rules for Intravenous Admixture in this tab.

 

Enter the following details:

  1. Working hours for Preparation room [From - To] – Specify the working hours of the admixture preparation room in time format of hh:mm. The ‘From’ time must be less than or equal to the ‘To’ time. The practitioner is alerted when ordering for Admixture after the working hours of the preparation room.

  2. Alert Ordering Practitioner beyond working hours - If a Practitioner places an order for admixture after the working hours of the preparation room, an alert is displayed. Select the check box to display the alert during ordering.

  3. IV Admixture Applicable – If IV Admixture is applicable to the logged in facility, select the check box.

  4. Oncology Admixture Applicable - If Oncology Admixture is applicable to the logged in facility, select the check box.

  5. Preparation of TPN Non-Standard regimen Applicable - If preparation of TPN non-standard regimen is applicable to the logged in facility, select the check box.

  6. Admixture Preparation Charges Applicable – If Admixture preparation charges are applicable to the logged in facility, select the check box.
