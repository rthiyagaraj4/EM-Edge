Constituents Tab

The TPN constituents details are specified in this tab. On selecting this tab, the header details are disabled.

The TPN constituent group names are displayed. The generics of each group are displayed below them. The generics are displayed based on two conditions.

  • In the function ‘TPN Constituent Generic’, if the group contains generics with UOM Flag as ‘G’ (Group), the generics are displayed.

  • If the group contains both the generics, that is, generics with UOM flag as ‘G’ (group) and generics with UOM flag as ‘I’ (Individual), individual generics are listed.

 

  • TPN Generic Constituent – Enter the quantity in numeric format in the area. The Unit of Measurement displayed is defined for the Constituent group in the function ‘UOM for TPN Constituents’ (under Order Setup).

  • Fluid - Enter the required quantity of fluid in numeric format of five digits maximum length. This is a mandatory field.

  • Macro Nutrient - Before specifying the Macro Nutrient details, ‘Fluid’ quantity has to be specified. Enter the volume of macronutrient required to be part of the TPN/PPN in term of the volume percentage and the system computes the quantity of macronutrient required. The system validates the volume specified for all macronutrients, the total of which cannot exceed the total volume of fluid specified.

 

• Total Energy – The system computes the total energy based on attributes of the macronutrients defined in TPN Energy for Constituent function.

• Lipid-Emulsion Type – Select type from the list. The listed values are pre-defined. The types listed are LCT, MCT and Mix of LCT & MCT.

• NCP: N Ration - The system displays the energy ratio by computing the non protein component against a constant value of one.

 

  • Electrolytes - Enter the required quantity of Electrolytes in five digits maximum length.

  • Trace Elements - Enter the required quantities of Trace Elements in five digits maximum length.

  • Vitamins - Enter the required quantities of Vitamins in numeric format of five digits maximum length.

  • Other Components - Enter the required quantities of Other Components in numeric format of five digits maximum length.
