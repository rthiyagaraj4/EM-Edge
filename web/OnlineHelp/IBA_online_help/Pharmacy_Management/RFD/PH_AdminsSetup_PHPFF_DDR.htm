Direct Dispensing Rules Tab

Rules that influence recording of Adverse Drug Reaction and Therapeutic Drug Monitoring are specified in this tab.

 

Enter the following details:

 

  1. Direct Dispensing Allowed – Select the check box to indicate Direct Dispensing allowed.

  2. Applicable Patient Class – Select the Patient Class to which direct dispensing is applicable. The patient class is ‘Outpatient’, ‘Emergency’, ‘External’ and ‘All’.

  3. Applicable Encounters – Select direct dispensing applicability for Open or Closed or Both encounters.

  4. Consider Last – Enter number of encounter to be considered for direct dispensing.

  5. Encounter Details Required During Direct Dispensing – Select the check box, if the encounter details are required at the time of direct dispensing.
