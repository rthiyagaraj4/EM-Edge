Reissue Medication

From this function you can Query and Record the medication re-issue details.  

Dispensed medications are re-issued to patients due to various reasons like, breakage, refusal by patient, etc.

System validates the following rules before invoking the transaction function.

• Logged in user must be a ‘Pharmacist’.

• User rights for dispensing must be assigned to the logged in user.

Recording Medication Re-issue Details

From the PH Transactions, select the Reissue Medication function.

The Reissue Medication login screen gets displayed.

  1. Re-issue Medication Login screen gets displayed.

    Enter the following details to login to re-issue medication(s) screen:

    1. User Name - The logged in User Name gets displayed.

    2. Password – Enter the valid password for logging in. This is a mandatory field.

    3. Dispense Location – The Locations assigned to the logged in user are listed. Select the required Dispense Location. This is a mandatory field.

  2. Click [OK] to login to the Reissue Medication function or [Cancel] to cancel the login process.

  3. Reissue Medication screen gets displayed.

  4. Enter the details in the mandatory (*) fields. <Related Field Description>

  5. Click [Search] to display the issued drug details and re-issue drug details.

    You can re-issue the medication only if you are a pharmacist and has the dispensing rights for reissue of medications to the patient.

     

    The following details are displayed:

    1. Dispensed Drug – The dispensed drug is displayed and hyperlinked.

    1. Reissue Facility – The name of the Facility from where the drug is reissued is displayed.

    2. Reissue Location – The location name where the drug is reissued is displayed.

    3. Reissue Time - The time when the drug was reissued.

    4. Dispense No - The number of the dispensed drug details form.

    5. Re-issued Quantity/UOM – The quantity re-issued along with its unit of measurement are displayed.

    6. Reissue Reason – Select the reason for reissue. The listed values are defined in ‘Reason codes’ master function.

    The dispensed drugs listed are those for which ‘Dispense against Damage’ is selected in Drug master function.

    The following details are displayed:

    1. Patient details – Name of the Patient, Gender and Age.

    2. Reissue Location – The Reissue Location selected during logging gets displayed.

    The result screen is divided into three sections. The first section displays the ‘Issue Details’, second section displays ‘Reissued Details’ and ‘Stock Details’ in the third section.

    Issued Details

    The issue details displayed are explained below.

    1. Dispense Drug – The dispensed drug is displayed and hyperlinked.

    2. Dispensed Facility – The name of the Facility from where the drug is dispensed is displayed.

    3. Dispensed Location – The location name where the drug is dispensed is displayed.

    4. Issued Quantity/UOM – The quantity issued along with its unit of measurement are displayed.

    5. Reissue Quantity – Select the check box to indicate the stock is allocated for reissue. On specifying the stock details under ‘Stock Details’, the re-issue quantity gets displayed.

    6. Charge Amount – The amount for the issued medication is displayed.

    7. Patient Payable Amount – The amount payable by the patient for the issued medication is displayed.

    8. Reissue Reason – Select the reason for reissue. The listed values are defined in ‘Reason codes’ master function. This is a mandatory field.

    The first drug details are displayed, by default, under the ‘Stock Details’ section.

  6. Click [Cancel] to cancel the entered search criteria.

  7. Click ‘Dispensed Drug’ hyperlink to view the Dispensed Drug details.

    The following stock details are displayed:

    1. Expiry Date – The batch expiry date of the drug gets displayed.

    2. Batch ID – The Batch ID of the drug currently available in the Pharmacy gets displayed.

    3. Trade Name – Trade name of the dispensed drug gets displayed.

    4. Manufacturer – The drug manufacturer name gets displayed.

    5. Bin Location – The storage location of the drug gets displayed.

    6. Available Quantity – The stock quantity available of the dispensed drug gets displayed.

    7. Allocated Quantity – The dispensed quantity gets displayed, by default. Enter the required quantity in numeric format. The allocated quantity cannot be greater than dispensed quantity.

    8. Select – Select the check box to indicate the selected batch is considered for allocation.

  8. Click [Stock Availability] to display the batch details screen. Select the required batch for re-issuing. Click [Close] to exit from the batch details screen.

  9. Enter the required ‘Allocated Quantity’ in numeric format. The allocated quantity cannot be greater than dispensed quantity.

  10. Click [Allocate] to re-issue the allocated quantity.

  11. Select ‘Reissue?’ check box for the allocated drug and ‘Reissue Reason’.

  12. Click [Apply] to reissue the dispensed drug or [Reset] to clear the entered data.

The Pharmacist can select the required batches under ‘Stock Details’ section and re-issue the balance medication. On re-issuing the dispensed drug fully, the message ‘Dispensed Quantity has been re-issued fully’ gets displayed.
