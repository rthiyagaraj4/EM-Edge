By Facility

  1. Select a Facility from the list. This is a mandatory field.

  2. Click [Search] to display the list of all Responsibilities or enter the Responsibility name to search for a selected responsibility. You can also specify an alphabet and search for the desired responsibility by selecting a criteria from the list. Click [Clear] to clear the data.

  3. The responsibilities are listed along with two other columns, 'Remarks' and 'Select'.

  4. Click 'Remarks' hyperlink to enter remarks for a Responsibility. Click [OK] to record remarks for the selected facility.

  5. Select the check box under ‘Select’ to associate the Responsibility to the Facility.
