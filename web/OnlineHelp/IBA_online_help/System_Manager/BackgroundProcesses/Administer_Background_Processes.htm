Administer Background Processes

From this function you can Manage the Background Processes at the enterprise level.

Administer Background Processes function helps to manage the background processes like Duplicate merge process, Duplicate search process, etc. You can submit a new process for execution, update the status of the process in execution and remove a process from the execution queue. The Background Processes are scheduled on date and time basis. You can update the date and time of the process currently being executed. Background process provides maximum computing resources for the process.

Managing Background Processes

From the Background Processes, select the Administer Background Processes function.

The Administer Background Processes screen gets displayed.

The screen gets displayed with upper half of the screen under Job Queue (Process) showing the background process job that has been submitted with the details like the last execution time, next execution time and the status. The Last Execution Time is not displayed, if the submitted job is under execution.

The lower half of the screen under Job queue helps you to submit a new job for execution by entering the Process Details.

  1. Enter the details in the mandatory (*) fields. <Related Field Description>

  2. Click [Submit] to submit the process. The details are displayed on the top half of the screen under Job Queue Process.

  3. Select the Process hyperlink to modify the process.

  4. Click [Change] to effect the changes made for ‘Next Execution Date/Time’ and ‘Interval’.

  5. Click [Run] to start the background process.

  6. Click [Remove] to clear the background Process from job queue.

  7. Click [Refresh] to display the changed status of the background Process.

 

The Background process scheduled for execution appears under the Job Queue. The status of the job is displayed. A status can be Idle, Running or Failing.

• Idle – The status is Idle, if the process is submitted.

• Running – This status is displayed when an idle process is selected for execution by clicking [Run].

• Failing – This status is displayed when the process submitted initially remains idle and is not executed after the submission of the next process.
