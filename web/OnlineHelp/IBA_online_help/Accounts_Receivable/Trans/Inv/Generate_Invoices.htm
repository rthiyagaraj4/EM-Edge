Generate Invoices

From this function you can Generate an Invoice against a Customer for a range of Bills.

The Generate Invoices functionality is used to generate Invoices for a particular Customer for a range of Bills or separate Invoices for each Bill raised based on Bill Type, Bill Number and Bill Date for a specified period. The Invoice is generated for a Customer of the selected facility. In the Patient Billing Manager module, a patient is charged for the services availed from the hospital in the form of Bill. If an external party settles the amount on behalf of the patient, all the Bills raised in the Patient Billing Manager module is generated into Invoices in the Accounts Receivable module.

Generating an Invoice

From the Transactions > Invoices > Billing Invoices, select the Generate Invoices function.

The Generate Invoices screen gets displayed.

  1. Enter the details in the mandatory (*) fields. <Related Field Description>

  2. Click [Run Process] to generate an Invoice or [Clear Record] to clear the entered data. Click [Yes] to confirm or [No] to cancel the invoice generation process.

  3. A log file gets generated. Click [OK] to view the report or [Cancel] to exit from the screen.
