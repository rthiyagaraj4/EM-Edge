Patient Transactions

From this function you can Query the transactions of the patients in the Accounts Receivable module.

The Patient Transactions function enables you to view the document details, total debits, open credit and the net balance. You can also query to view the ageing analysis, patient details like Name, Address and Contact details of the patient. Ageing analysis gets displayed based on the ageing slots defined in the AR parameters and the ageing is calculated from the current system date using the base date of each document. The transaction details of the original documents and the associated documents are also displayed.

Querying Patient Transactions

From the Queries, double-click Patient Transactions function.

The Patient Transactions screen gets displayed.

  1. Select a valid entry for the Selection Criteria from the list. This field contains the following pre defined values are ‘All’ and ‘Outstanding’.

  2. Press [F8] or click [Execute Query] to view all the patient details. Press upward arrow key or downward arrow key to navigate to the previous or the next patient record.

OR

  1. Click [List of Values] to select a valid Patient ID. On selection of Patient ID, the total debits, open credits, credit limit and net balance amount for the relevant patient is displayed. On double-click of the Patient Id field in the Patient Transactions screen, the Query All Bills  screen is displayed with the patient details related to the Patient Id entered.

  2. Click Calendar function to select the date range. The respective Customer transactions between the selected date gets displayed.

  3. Click [Refresh] to clear the date entered.

  4. Click [Commit Form] or press [F8] to view the output. The original and the associated documents, customer address, ageing analysis details, miscellaneous customer address and the consolidated invoice details, if any, gets displayed. The related records associated with the patient are populated for view.
