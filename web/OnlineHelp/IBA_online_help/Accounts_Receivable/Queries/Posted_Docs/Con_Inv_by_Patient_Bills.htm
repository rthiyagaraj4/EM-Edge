Consolidated Invoices by Patient Bills

From this function you can Query the consolidated Invoices by Patient Bills.

This query is used to view the Original consolidated invoices as well as associated bills document breakup. In addition to the document details, Customer address, contact details are also listed.

Querying Consolidated Invoices by Patient Bills

From the Queries > Posted Documents, double-click Consolidated Invoices by Patient Bills function.

The Consolidated Invoices by Patient Bills screen gets displayed.

  1. Click [Execute Query] or press [F8] to view the consolidated invoices.

OR

  1. Click Calendar function to select the date range. Click [Execute Query] or press [F8] to view the consolidated invoices for the specified date range.

  2. Click [Refresh] to view all the Consolidated Invoices by Patient Bills Details associated with the facility for the specified Date range. When the Date range is not specified, clicking on [Refresh] displays all the Consolidated Invoices by Patient Bills details from the database.

  3. After entering date field values, click [Execute Query] or press [F8]  by placing the cursor on document type, and related records are populated for view. If all the records are to be displayed, just click [Execute Query] or press [F8] by placing the cursor on document type, system displays all the Consolidated Invoices by Patient Bills record.

  4. Click [Consolidated Invoices] to view the information regarding the Consolidated Invoices, Patient Bills, User access and Remarks pertaining to a relevant Document Type.
