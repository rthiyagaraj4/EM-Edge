Pharmacy Orders – On  Hold

From this function you can View or Discontinue medicines on hold.

Holding medicines is a method of intimating the practitioners that medicines placed by them is to be clarified, is not available in stock or only a substitute is available. Once the notification is done, the practitioner can view or discontinue the medicines kept on hold.

On Holding Orders

From the Clinician Access > Summary Menu, select Pharmacy Orders – On Hold function.

The Pharmacy Orders – On Hold screen gets displayed.

  1. The medicines which are kept on hold for a period of one week from the current system date are displayed.

  2. Enter the details in the mandatory fields (*). <Related Field Description>

  3. Click [Search] to display all the medicines that are on hold or click [Clear] to clear the entered criteria.

  4. Click Order Catalogs hyperlink, a context menu View or Discontinue is displayed.

  5. Click View to the view the details of a particular order placed. Status is displayed as a hyperlink.

    • Click Status hyperlink to view the reason for medicine to be on hold.

    • Click View Audit Trail to view the history of the medicine kept on hold.

  1. Click Discontinue, the Discontinue Reason dialog gets displayed.

    1. Discontinue Reason – Select the reason for the medicine to be discontinued. The listed values are ‘Wrong Entry’, ‘Duplicate Order’, ‘Wrong Patient’, ‘Patient Refused’ and ‘Medication Reaction’.  The selected value gets displayed in the text box provided. You can also enter additional information in the space provided.

    2. Click [Record] the medicine ordered gets discontinued.
