Insurance Authorization Form

From this function you can Print Insurance Authorization Form for Inpatient/Outpatient services.

The function allows you to print the Insurance Authorization Form for Inpatient/Outpatient services to Daman and Thiqa. You can launch the authorization request form for closed encounters from practitioner Desktop or by clicking the icon from View Chart Summary.

Printing Insurance Authorization Form

From the CA Desktop menu > Navigation menu, select Insurance Authorization Form.

The Insurance Authorization Form gets displayed.

  1. Select the required Patient ID from the lookup. This field is mandatory.

  2. On selecting the patient ID, the encounter IDs related to the patient appear in the Encounter ID drop-down list. Select the required encounter ID.

  3. Click [Search] to view the encounter authorization form for the selected Patient ID.

  4. The Results section appear with Primary Insurance label with Insurance Provider name, Approval Number field , and Print button

  5. Enter or scan the approval number of the insurance document.

  6. Click [Save] to accept the changes.

  7. Click [Print] to print the authorization form with the entered approval number.
