Clinical Studies Patient List

From this function you can View the list of patients for whom the clinical studies is recorded.

Clinical Studies Patient List function is used to view the list of patients for whom the clinical studies are recorded. You can get the details according to the criteria selected. Access to this function is given to the practitioner by the admin user in the Access Rights for Research Studies function.

Viewing Clinical Studies Patient List

From the CA Desktop menu > Navigation menu, select Clinical Studies Patient List function.

The Clinical Studies Patient List screen gets displayed.

  1. Enter the details in the mandatory (*) fields. <Related Field Description>

  2. Click [Search] to view the patient for whom the clinical studies are recorded or [Clear] to clear the entered data.

  3. Based on the entered search criteria, the Patient ID, Patient Name, Recorded Practitioner, Research Category, Active Diagnosis and Details gets displayed.

  4. Click Patient ID hyperlink to view the patient records.

  5. Click Details  icon to view the remarks entered during clinical studies.
