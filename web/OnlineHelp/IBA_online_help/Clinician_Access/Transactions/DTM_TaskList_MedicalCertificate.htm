Medical Certificate

From this function you can Record and View Medical Certificate details for the patient.

Medical Certificate is a statement about the patient issued to the inpatients on discharge. The certificate provides the period of inpatient stay in the hospital and fitness of the patient to resume duty. The user can set up privileges for the practitioners that govern the access rights of practitioners to record or view a Medical Certificate. The system facilitates maintaining a draft copy of the certificate until it is signed or authorized by the practitioner recording the certificate or by another practitioner based on their access rights. A certificate once recorded can be modified based on the setting for the note type. A certificate can be forwarded to a specialty or practitioner for review.
