Pregnancy

Pregnancy is the carrying of one or more offspring, known as a fetus or embryo, inside the body of female humans. In a pregnancy, there can be multiple gestations, as in the case of twins or triplets. Human pregnancy is the most studied of all mammalian pregnancies and obstetrics is the medical field that studies and treats pregnant patients.
