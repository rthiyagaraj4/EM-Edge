Enter the following details:

 

  1. High Risk Condition - Select the High Risk condition from the list. This field is mandatory. The values listed are as set in the 'High Risk' master function in Medical Records module.

  2. Remarks – Enter the remarks in alphanumeric format of 200 characters maximum length.

  3. Onset Date - This field denotes the date from which the Patient is having the High Risk Condition. The current system date is displayed by default, which can be changed to an earlier date. To change the date, choose the date form the Calendar function. The date cannot be greater than the current system date/time. This field is mandatory.

  4. From Last - Select the check box to enter the Days, Months and Years. Clock icon is displayed next to the calendar icon of the 'Onset Date' field if the 'From Last' check box is selected. The clock icon indicates that the date entered is an estimated date. This field provides an alternate method of entering the onset date. The three fields represent the days, months and years respectively. Enter the value in numeric format of two characters maximum length in all the fields or any one of them as the case may be to indicate that the patient has been experiencing the condition from the last number of days, months or years.
