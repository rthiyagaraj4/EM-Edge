Assessment

Mother

  1. Click Mother Document from the menu to record clinical notes that reflect the clinical condition of the mother patient for the event Pregnancy. Note Types created with Note Group of Applicable Task as ‘Mothercare Consultation’ is used for recording.

  2. Click Mother Chart from the menu to record clinical charts for the mother patient for the event Pregnancy.

Fetus

  1. Click Fetus Document from the menu to record clinical notes that reflect the clinical condition of the fetus for the event Pregnancy. Note Types created with Note Group of Applicable Task as ‘Mothercare Consultation’ is used for recording.

  2. Click Fetus Chart from the menu to record clinical charts for the Fetus for the event Pregnancy.
