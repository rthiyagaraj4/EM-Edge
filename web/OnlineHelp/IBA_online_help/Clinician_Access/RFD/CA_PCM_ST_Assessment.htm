Assessment

  1. Click the Assessment Tab to record Assessment details.

  2. The Components of the Chart are displayed as defined in the Chart Component function for the Variable Chart created in the Chart Setup function. The Chart displayed on clicking the Assessment Tab is assigned to the Patient Class, Specialty, Practitioner Type, Location Type, Location and Age Group in the Chart Applicability function.

  3. Enter the required details.

  4. Click [Print] to print chart details.

  5. Click [Record] to save the Assessment details or click [Cancel] to exit from the Assessment screen without saving details.
