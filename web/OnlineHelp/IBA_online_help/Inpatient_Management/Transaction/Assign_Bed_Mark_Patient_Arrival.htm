Assign Bed/Mark Patient Arrival

From this function you can Assign bed and attending practitioner to a patient if not assigned while admitting the patient and Mark the arrival of a patient in the Nursing Unit.

Marking the arrival of a patient becomes compulsory, if the ‘Allow Patient Check in’ flag is selected in the ‘IP Parameter’. Only vacant, unblocked or overrideable beds are allocated. If vacant beds are not available in the Nursing Unit and Pseudo Bed creation is allowed, create Pseudo Beds and assign them to the patient.

The logged in user is able to access this function, if the Operator Station associated has the privilege of assigning beds, which are identified by the flag ‘Assign Bed’ in the Operator Station master setup in Application Masters module.

 

The patients who has arrived after the number of hours specified for 'Grace period for Assign Bed/Mark arrival' in 'IP Parameters' is highlighted using color indicator.

Assigning a Bed and Marking Patient’s Arrival

From the IP Transactions > Admission, select the Assign Bed/Mark Patient Arrival function.

The Assign Bed/Mark Patient Arrival screen gets displayed.

  1. Click [Search] to display all the admitted patients without bed assigned or enter the search criteria and click [Search] to select specific admitted patients for whom beds are not assigned. Click [Clear] to reset the records displayed in the result area.

    Enter the following search criteria:

    1. Nursing Unit – Enter the Nursing Unit in alphanumeric format of 15 characters maximum length or select the Nursing Unit using the [?] function.

    2. Admission Date (From-To) – Enter the Admission Date range in date format of dd/mm/yyyy or select the date using the calendar function.

    3. Specialty – Enter the Specialty in alphanumeric format of 15 characters maximum length or select the specialty using the [?] function.

    4. Practitioner – Enter the Practitioner Name in alphanumeric format of 30 characters maximum length or select the Practitioner using the [?] function.

    5. Encounter ID – Enter the Encounter ID in numeric format of 12 digits maximum length.

    6. Patient ID - Enter the Patient ID in alphanumeric format of maximum length as specified in MP Parameter or select the Patient ID using the [?] function.

  2. On searching, the Admission Details are displayed based on the entered criteria.

  3. Click ‘+’ hyperlink to view the Current Encounter Details.

  4. Click the ‘Encounter ID’ hyperlink to display the Assign Bed/Mark Patient Arrival screen. If Bed is assigned while admitting a Patient and ‘Allow Patient Check in’ flag is cleared in the ‘IP Parameter’, the Encounter ID is disabled.

  5. On clicking ‘Encounter ID’ hyperlink, the Assign Bed/Mark Patient Arrival screen displays the Admission Details and Nursing Unit details as recorded during patient admission.

  6. Enter the details in the mandatory (*) fields. <Related Field Description>

  7. Click [Referral Details] to view the referral details if selected patient is referred from another location for admission to the current facility. On clicking [Referral Details], the referral details are displayed.

  8. Click [View Future Bookings/Appointments ] to view the future bookings and appointments of the patient if any.

  9. Click [Record] to assign the bed and mark patient’s arrival or [Close] to exit from the Assign Bed/Mark Patient Arrival screen.

  10. On clicking [Record] the Accept File Type and Volume Number screen gets displayed. Select the File Type, Volume Number and Reason for Borrowing from the list. Click [OK] to accept the File Type and Volume Number or [Cancel] to exit from the screen.

  11. If internal reports are defined, the Internal Reports screen gets displayed. Click [OK] to generate Internal Reports or [Cancel] to exit from the screen.
