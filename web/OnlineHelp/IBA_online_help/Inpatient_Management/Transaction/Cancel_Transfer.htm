Cancel Transfer

From this function you can Cancel transfer once the patient is transferred from one ward to another.

This function is used to cancel patient transfer once the patient is transferred from one ward to another. The access rights for this function can be provided in User Access Rights screen in Medical Records module.

The Return Due Date (RDD) of patient file will revert to previous RDD value in ‘File Current FS Location’ screen in FM queries while Cancel Transfer.

You cannot perform cancel transfer if Discharge Advice is recorded. You need to cancel the discharge advice and then cancel the Transfer.

You cannot perform cancel transfer if Discharge Bill is generated. You need to cancel the discharge bill and then cancel the Transfer.

Cancelling Patient Transfer

From the IP Transactions > Transfer, select the Cancel Transfer function.

The Cancel Transfer screen gets displayed.

  1. Enter the Patient ID or searching using the lookup. This field is mandatory

  2. Click [Search]. The patient records matching the search criteria gets displayed.

  3. Click + sign before the Encounter ID to invoke the Encounter Details dialog that shows the current Encounter Details and Stay Details of the patient.

  4. Click the 'Encounter ID' hyperlink of the patient record, the Cancel Transfer dialog gets displayed.

  5. Enter the reason for cancelling the transfer in the text box. You can enter 100 characters in alphanumeric format. Special characters are allowed. This is a mandatory field.

  6. Click [OK]. The patient transfer gets cancelled.
