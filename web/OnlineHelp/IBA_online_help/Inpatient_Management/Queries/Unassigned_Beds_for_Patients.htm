Unassigned Bed for Patients

From this function you can Query on the patients admitted to various Nursing Units without beds being allocated.

The result screen provides details of the Admission date and time, Encounter ID, Patient ID, Name of the patient and Nursing unit associated with the patient. The option to view the current encounter details of the patient is provided to the user.

Querying for Unassigned Bed for Patients

From IP Queries> Admissions/Transfers, select the Unassigned Bed for Patients function.

The Unassigned Bed for Patients screen gets displayed.

  1. Enter the details in the fields to query the ‘Unassigned Bed for Patients’ details. <Related Field Description>

  2. Click [Search] to select the records that satisfy the query criteria or [Reset] to clear the entered search criteria.

  3. On searching, the Unassigned Bed for Patients details are displayed.

  4. Click the '+' hyperlink, the system displays the Current Encounter Details. The information displayed is categorized as Patient details, Encounter details and Stay details.

  5. Click [Clear] to clear the result screen.
