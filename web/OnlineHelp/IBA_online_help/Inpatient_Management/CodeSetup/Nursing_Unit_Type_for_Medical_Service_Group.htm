Nursing Unit Type for Medical Service Group

From this function you can Create an association of Nursing Unit Types to a particular Medical Service Group. You can Query for Nursing Unit Types associated to a Medical Service Group and Delete Nursing Unit Type for Medical Service Group records.

Bed bookings against a Nursing Unit are controlled based on the rules defined against a Medical Service Group. This association influences the bookings made against all the Nursing Units classified under the Nursing Unit Type.

Creating an association of Nursing Unit Types to Medical Service Groups

From the IP Setup > IP Code Setup, select the Nursing Unit Type for Medical Service Group function.

The Nursing Unit Type for Medical Service Group screen gets displayed.

  1. Click [Create] to invoke the Create screen.

  2. Enter the details in the mandatory (*) fields. <Related Field Description>

  3. Click [Apply] to save the association of Nursing Unit Types to Medical Service Group or [Reset] to clear the entered data.

  4. Click [Query] to search for Nursing Unit Type for Medical Service Group records.

  5. Click [Delete] to delete a queried record.
