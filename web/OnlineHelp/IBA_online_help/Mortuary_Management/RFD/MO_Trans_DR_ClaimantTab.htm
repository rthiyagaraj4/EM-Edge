Claimant Tab

This tab is used to record the claimant details of the body part.

  1. Claimant Type - Select the claimant type from the list. The listed values are 'Next of Kin', 'Employer', 'Embassy of a Country', 'Welfare Associations', 'Religious Organizations', 'Under Taker', ‘Released to Other Facility’, and  'Others'. If the 'Unclaimed Body' check box is selected in the 'Registration' Tab, the list does not list, 'Next of Kin' and 'Employer'. If ‘Released to Other Facility’ is selected, the drop-down list Facility will be enabled in this screen.

  2. Claimant - Select the name of the claimant from the list. The list is enabled and becomes mandatory based on the Claimant Type selected. The values are populated from the Claimant Type function. The list is disabled on selecting the Claimant Type as 'Next of Kin' or 'Employer'.

  3. Click [Copy Details] on selecting the Claimant type as 'Next of Kin' or 'Employer' to copy the details from Master Patient Index module to their respective fields. [Copy Details] is disabled on selecting any other Claimant type. On selecting any other Claimant Type; select the required claimant from the 'Claimant' field to display the details in their respective fields.

  4. Name - The name of the claimant is displayed if recorded.

  5. Relationship - The relationship of the claimant with the deceased is displayed if it was recorded or select the relationship from the list. The list is enabled only if 'Next of Kin' or 'Employer' is selected as 'Claimant Type'.

  6. NRIC - The National ID of the patient is displayed if it was recorded while registering the patient in the facility.

  7. Employee ID - The ID of the employee is displayed.

  8. Occupation - The occupation of the patient is displayed.

  9. Organization name - The name of the organization is displayed.

  10. Facility – If you have selected the Claimant Type as ‘Released to Other Facility’, you can select a facility from this drop-down list.

  11. Reason – You can enter the reason for releasing the deceased to another facility. You can enter or paste free text up to a maximum of 100 characters.

Note: You can modify the facility in the Release Deceased/ Body Parts screen and view the modified facility in the Revert Released Deceased / Body Parts screen .

  1. Address Line (1-4) - The Address of the claimant is displayed.

  2. Town/City - The town/city of the claimant is displayed as recorded.

  3. Area - The area of the claimant is displayed as recorded.

  4. State - The state of the claimant is displayed as recorded.

  5. Post Code - The postal code of the claimant is displayed.

  6. Country - The country of the claimant is displayed.

  7. Home Phone No. - The claimant's residence telephone number is displayed.

  8. Mobile Phone No. - The mobile phone number of the claimant is displayed.

  9. Office Phone No. - The claimant's office telephone number is displayed.

  10. Email ID - The email Id of the claimant is displayed.

  11. Default Previous Claimant Details – If you are transferring the deceased from another facility, click this hyperlink to copy the claimant details recorded earlier and display them here. This hyperlink will be enabled only when the deceased body transferred from another facility has got previously recorded claimant details.

  12. View Claimant History – This hyperlink will be enabled only when the deceased body has got previously recorded “Claimant details”, if it was transferred from another facility. When you click this hyperlink, a View Claimant History dialog will be launched displaying the deceased registration details recorded in earlier facilities in multiple rows. You can click the facility name, which will be a hyperlink, to view all the details of deceased registration recorded in that particular facility.

The displayed details can be updated.
