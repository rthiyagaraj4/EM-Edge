Undo Transfer Units

From this function you can Reverse the status of the blood units from Transfer to Issued.

The Undo Transfused Units function is used to reverse the status of the blood units from ‘Transferred’ to ‘Issued’. You can reverse the status of blood units that are issued and not transferred to a patient for which the transfusion status is updated as ‘transferred’ erroneously. The color codes displayed at the bottom of the screen denotes the following status.

– Units issued in emergency

– Autologous Unit

– Stat (to be issued immediately)

– Urgent

– Directed Unit

– Routine

– Multi allocation Unit

Undoing Transfused Units

From the Transactions > BT Operations, double-click Undo Transfer Units function.

The Undo Transfused Units screen gets displayed.

  1. Click [List of Values] to select the specimen and revert the status of the blood unit from transferred to Issued.

  2. Click [Execute Query] to view the patient details, product category and unit details. <Related Field Description>

  3. Click [Details] to view the patient details.

  4. Click [Antibodies] to view the antibodies recorded for the patient. The [Antibodies] gets enabled if antibodies are associated to the selected patient.

  5. Click [Transfusion History] to view the previous transfer details of the patient, if any.

  6. Click [Requirements] to view the transfer requirements recorded for the patient, if any.

  7. Click [View Multi-Allocation] to view the units allocated for multiple specimens. Current specimen is displayed using color. Click [Close] to close the screen.

  8. Select a record and click [Undo Transfuse] to revert the unit status of a record from 'Transferred’ to ‘Issued’. The status gets changed to ‘Issued’. You can view the selected specimen with the Issued status in the ‘Allocate/Xmatch/Issue - Blood Units’ function.

  9. Click [Save] or press [F10] to save the records or click [Clear Record] to clear the details.
