Dispose Blood Units

From this function you can Dispose the blood units from the existing stock.

The Dispose Blood Units function is used to dispose the blood units permanently from the existing stock. The disposed blood unit is  removed from the stock and the details are made available in the Disposed/Transfused/Issued Units function.

Disposing Blood Units

From the Transactions > Stock Operations, double-click Dispose function.

The Dispose Blood Units screen gets displayed.

  1. Enter the search criteria and click [Execute Query] to view the details. You can use the upward and downward arrows to navigate to the previous and next records. <Related Field Description>

  2. To query for a specific record, click [Enter Query] or press [F7], enter the details in the fields and click [Execute Query] or press [F8]. The records matching the query criteria are displayed.

You can specify the values in the fields, which are not in grey to query records.

  1. Click [List of Values] to associate a Fate Code to the blood unit. The fate codes provide the reason for disposing the blood unit.

  2. Click [Unit Attributes] to view Attributes associated to the unit.

  3. Click [Antigens] to view Antigens associated to the unit.

  4. Click [Commit] to dispose a selected blood unit or click [Clear Record] to clear the details.
