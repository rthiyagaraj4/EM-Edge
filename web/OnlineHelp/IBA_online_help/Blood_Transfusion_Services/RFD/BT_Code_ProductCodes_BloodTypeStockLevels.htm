Enter the following details:

 

  1. Blood Group – Click [List of Values] to select the Blood Group. The Blood Group of the patient recorded in the ‘Record Patient Blood Group’ function in Medical Records module is displayed.

  2. Rhesus Code - Click [List of Values] to select the Rhesus Code. The Rhesus Code of the Blood Group of the patient recorded in the ‘Record Patient Blood Group’ function in Terminology and Coding Management module is displayed.

  3. Minimum Level – Enter the value for minimum stock level of the Product in numerical format of five digits maximum length. This indicates the minimum level of stock for the particular Product and the stock level cannot be lesser than the number specified.

  4. Maximum Level - Enter the value for Maximum Stock Level of the Product in numerical format of five digits maximum length. This indicates the maximum level of stock for the particular Product and the stock level cannot exceed the number specified.

  5. Reorder Level - Enter the value for Reorder Stock Level of the Product in numerical format of five digits maximum length. When the stock reaches the entered value, an alert message pops-up, alerting you to reorder the particular product. You have to recall the donors or select new donors to donate the blood again.

  6. Current Level – The Current Stock Level of the product is displayed and you can update the value.
