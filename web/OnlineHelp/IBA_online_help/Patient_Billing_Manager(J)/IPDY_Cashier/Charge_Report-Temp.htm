Charge Report Temporary

From this function you can Generate a temporary report on the patient transactions in the facility.

Charge Report Temporary function is used to query on the financial details of the patient in the facility. You can view the Pharmacy Bill details, various patient encounters in the facility, bill details for the specific patient encounter and interim bill details, if any.

Generate Temporary Charge Report

From the Transactions > IP/DY Cashier, select Charge Report Temporary Function.

The Charge Report Temporary screen gets displayed.

  1. Enter the details in the mandatory (*) fields.

  2. Click [Pharmacy Bill] to view various bills generated for the pharmacy items.

  3. Click [ViewEncounterBillDet] to view the bill details for respective encounter in the patient.

  4. Click [ViewPatEncounterDtls] to view the encounter details of the respective patient.

  5. Click [Query] to view the Order Catalog details associated to the respective patient.

  6. Click [Reset] to clear the details.

  7. You can view the financial details recorded during visit registration by clicking [Click].

  8. Click [BillStmt] to view the statement of the patient.

  9. Click [BLBillInterim] to view the interim bills generated for the patient transactions in the facility.

  10. Click [BLPrevEnctr] to view the previous encounters of the patient in the facility.

    An Alert message ‘User is already logged into Cash Counters valid for OP, IP and EXT gets displayed, if you have already logged into the Cash Counter.
