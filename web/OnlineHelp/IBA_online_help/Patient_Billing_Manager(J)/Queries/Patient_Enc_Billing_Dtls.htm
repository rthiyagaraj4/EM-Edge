Patient Encounters/ Billing Details

From this function you can Query to view the patient encounter and the respective billing details in the facility.

Patient Encounters/Billing Details function is used to query the encounter details of the patient in the respective facility. You can view the encounter type, date of admission, location, bed and the room number associated to the patient. The date when the patient was discharged from the facility and the current status of the patient in the facility gets displayed.

Querying Patient Encounters and Billing Details

From the Queries, select Patient Encounters/Billing Details Function.

The Patient Encounters/Billing Details screen gets displayed.

  1. Click [?] to select the Patient ID. This is a mandatory field. <Related Field Description>

  2. Click [Search] to search for the selected patient encounter and bill details. Click [Clear] to reset the entered data.

  3. The respective patient encounter details get listed.

  4. Click the Encounter ID to view the billing details for the respective patient encounter in the facility.

  5. The Document Number is displayed as a hyperlink in the bill details screen. Click Document Number to view the bill charge details. You can also view the discount amount, exemption amount and the adjusted amount on clicking >>.
