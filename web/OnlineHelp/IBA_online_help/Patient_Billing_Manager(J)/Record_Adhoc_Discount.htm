Record Adhoc Discount

You can issue a Service level discount by clicking the Discount Amount hyperlink. You can either record the amount or percentage to issue discount on a Billing Service associated to the Order Catalog.

 

The following details are displayed:

  1. Service - The Billing Service selected to issue discount is displayed. You cannot update the Billing Service.

  2. Gross Amount - The Gross Amount for the respective Billing Service is displayed. You cannot update the Gross Amount.

  3. Discount Amount -  The Discount Amount issued for the respective Billing Service previously is displayed. You cannot update the Discount Amount.

  4. Net Amount - Net Amount for the  respective Billing Service is displayed. You cannot update the Net Amount. The Net Amount is displayed as the difference of [Gross Amount - Discount Amount], if a discount has been offered for the respective Billing Service.

  5. Additional Charge - Additional Charge indicates additional service tax defined for the respective Billing Service.

  6. User ID - Enter the User ID in alphanumeric format. Authorized Users with valid access rights to issue adhoc discount are permitted to record discount. This is a mandatory field.

An error message 'This User Does Not Have Right to Give Discount For This Service' is displayed, if the user is not authorised to issue discounts. Authorization to issue discounts for a particular service is given through the function 'User Privileges and Cash Counter Access' in the Administration menu of Patient Billing Manager module.

  1. PIN - Enter the PIN Number of the respective user. This is a mandatory field.

  2. Actual Gross Amount - Actual Gross Amount indicates the charge incurred for respective Billing Service. Discount is issued on the actual gross amount.

  3. Adhoc Discount %age - Enter the adhoc discount percentage in numeric format. Adhoc Discount Amount is calculated based on the percentage specified.

  4. Adhoc Discount Amount - Enter the adhoc discount amount in numeric format. Adhoc Discount Amount is calculated based on the percentage specified.

The Adhoc Discount %age field value gets updated when you update the Adhoc Discount Amount and vice versa.

  1. Use Previous Discount Reason - Select the check box to select the Adhoc Discount Reason used in the previous transaction. This check box is enabled only if the patient has received a discount on the same Billing Service previously.

  2. Adhoc Discount Reason - Click [?] function to select Adhoc Discount Reason. The values defined through 'Reasons for Billing Actions' function in Patient Billing Manager module with the 'Service Level Discount' check box as selected are listed. The 'Adhoc Discount Reason' is displayed automatically, if you select the check box 'Use Previous Discount Reason'.
