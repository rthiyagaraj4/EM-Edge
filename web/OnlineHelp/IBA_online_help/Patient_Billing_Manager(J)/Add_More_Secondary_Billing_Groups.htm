Enter the following details to associate additional Billing Groups:

 

  1. Billing Group – Click [?] function to select a Billing Group. The Billing Groups defined in the Patient Billing Manager module gets listed. This is a mandatory field.

  2. Primary Referral Source - Click [?] function to select a Primary Referral Source. This field is mandatory, if the Primary Referral Source is selected as mandatory during Billing Group definition.  An icon adjacent to the primary referral source is displayed, if the long description of the referral source exceeds 100 characters. The additional details are displayed on mouse over.

  3. Secondary Referral Source - Click [?] function to select a Secondary Referral Source. This field is mandatory, if the Secondary Referral Source is selected as mandatory during Billing Group definition. An icon adjacent to the secondary referral source is displayed, if the long description of the referral source exceeds 100 characters. The additional details are displayed on mouse over.

 

The Payer Group, Payer and Policy Types combination associated to the selected Billing Group are listed. Payer Group, Payer, Priority, Policy and Credit Authorization date recorded are displayed.

  1. Payers/Policy Details - Select the Payers/Policy Details check box against a record to select and associate it to the selected patient encounter. On selecting the check box, click [Modify Payer] to enter the Policy details.

  2. [Add More Payers] - Click [Add More Payers] to associate additional Payers to the patient encounter.

  3. [Modify Payer] - Click [Modify Payer] to update Policy and Payer details.

  4. [Remove Payer] - Select the check box against a record and click [Remove Payer] to dissociate it. A confirmation message 'Please confirm to exclude the Selected Payer' is displayed. Click [OK] to confirm or [Cancel] to cancel.

  5. Click [Accept] to save the record or [Cancel] to cancel the process.
