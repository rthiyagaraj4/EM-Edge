Patient Registration Billing Dtls

From this function you can Define billing rules based on which a patient is charged during registration. You can also Query to view the billing rules defined.

Patient Registration Billing Details function is used to define rules based on which a patient is charged during registration. A Patient Series and Billing Group is associated to a rate based Billing Service or Billing Panel, based on which the patient is charged during registration in Master Patient Index module. A Payer Group and Payer combination is chargeable during patient registration, if the Billing Group selected is Sponsor/Insurance/Customer.

Define Patient Registration Billing Details

From the Setup > Interfaced Modules Financial Dtls, select Patient Registration Billing Dtls Function.

The Patient Registration Billing Details screen gets displayed.

  1. The name of the logged in Facility gets displayed, by default.

  2. Enter the details in the mandatory (*) fields. <Related Field Description>

  3. Click [Query] to view the details recorded.

  4. Click [Apply] to save the details or [Reset] to clear the details.
