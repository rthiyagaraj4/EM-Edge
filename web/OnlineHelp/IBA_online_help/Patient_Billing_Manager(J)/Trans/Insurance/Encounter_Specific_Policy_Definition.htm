Encounter Specific Policy Definition

From this function you can Include or Exclude the services for a particular patient encounter associated to a Policy Type and Query for a desired Encounter Specific Policy Type record.

The function Encounter Specific Policy Definition is used to include or exclude the services for a particular patient encounter in the facility. The coverage charge can be recalculated for specific patient encounter. You can associate additional services to the patient encounter. The services associated to the selected Policy Type, Customer Group and the Customer are defined. You can associate additional services to the patient encounter other than the services, which are already associated to the Policy Type, Customer Group and the Customer. You can also delete the services from the patient encounter.

Encounter Specific Policy Definition

From the Setup > Insurance, Select the Encounter Specific Policy Definition function.

The Encounter Specific Policy Definition screen gets displayed.

  1. Click [?] to select Patient ID.

  2. Select an Episode Type from the list. The values are system defined - Inpatient, Daycare, Outpatient and Emergency. The policy definition for services is applicable for the selected Episode Type.

  3. Click [?] to select an Episode ID. The Episode ID applicable for the selected Episode Type gets listed. The patient details get displayed based on the Episode ID selected. This is a mandatory field.

  4. Click [Search] to view the patient date or [Modify] the patient data.

  5. Click [Modify] to invoke Authorization dialog. Enter User ID, PIN and Remarks for authorization. This button is enabled only if you do not have modification rights.

  6. The Policy Type details associated to the patient during the specific encounter registration gets displayed. <Related Field Description>

  7. Click [Apply] to save the entered data.

  8. Click [Query] to search for the existing records within the facility
