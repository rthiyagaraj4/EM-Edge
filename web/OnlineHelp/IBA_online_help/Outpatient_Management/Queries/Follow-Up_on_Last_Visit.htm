Follow-Up on Last Visit

From this function you can Query all the patients who require follow-up visit to an Outpatient Clinic.

Patients who require a re-visit to a Clinic for re-evaluation can be scheduled by recording a follow-up visit. This function is used to query for patients who require follow up visit to an Outpatient Clinic. The list is filtered using various criteria such as specialty, practitioner and clinic combination and is based on a time frame defined. This function is used by both practitioners and healthcare managers to organize resources required to manage planned future visits.

Querying for Follow-Up details on Last Visit

From the OP Queries, select the Follow-Up on Last Visit function.

The Follow-Up on Last Visit screen gets displayed.

  1. Enter the search criteria and click [Search] to select a specific record or [Clear] to clear the records displayed in the result area. <Related Field Description>

  2. Click [Reset] to clear the entered data.
