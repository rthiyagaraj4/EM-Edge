Patient Visits

From this function you can Query all the list of visits registered against a patient.

This query function generates a list of visits registered against a patient. The query is filtered based on Location, Practitioner or Service and is based on a period range. If the episode maintenance is set, in addition to the visit details, the episode details is also viewed. The function allows further filtering based on a medical record number and is used to query on all visits with open episodes. These list of values are used to study frequency of visits made by patients against a location or practitioner and is also used to study visit trends to various services within the facility.

Querying Patient Visit details

From the OP Queries, select the Patient Visits function.

The Patient Visits screen gets displayed.

  1. Enter the Patient ID in alphanumeric format or enter the MRN criteria.

  2. Click [Search] to select a specific record or [Clear] to clear the records displayed in the result area. <Related Field Description>

  3. Visits and Episode tabs gets displayed.

    This tab is applicable only when the episode maintenance is set. On click of ‘Episodes’ tab, the details of the outpatient episodes other than the ones only with cancelled visits are displayed.

    On click of ‘Visits’ tab, the details of outpatient visits other than the cancelled visits for the specified criteria are displayed.

  4. Click [Reset] to clear the entered data.
