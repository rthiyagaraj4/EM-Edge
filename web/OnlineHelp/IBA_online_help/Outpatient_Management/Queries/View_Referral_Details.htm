View Referral Details

From this function you can Query all the patients who have been referred either within the facility or from external facilities.

Patients who are referred for Outpatient services in the facility are identified using referral registration. This function is used to query patients who have been referred either within the facility or from external facilities. The list of values are filtered based on priority or patient identifiers. The function allows further filtering based on the details of the source location or the recipient location and is used to study referral volumes within the facility and to study referral volumes from or to an external facility.

Querying for Referral Details

From the OP Queries, select the Outpatient Statistics function.

The Outpatient Statistics screen gets displayed.

  1. Enter the search criteria and click [Search] to select a specific record or [Clear] to clear the records displayed in the result area. <Related Field Description>

  2. Click [Reset] to clear the entered data.

  3. On searching the referral details are displayed.

 

The Referral Notes entered for the patient during referral is displayed on clicking the ‘Notes’ hyperlink. You cannot edit the details.

Place the mouse on the    icon to display the long name of the External or Referral Source recorded in ‘External/Referral Source’ function of Application Masters module. The  icon appears on the screen if the long name for the External Referral Source is recorded in ‘External/Referral Source’ function of Application Masters module.
