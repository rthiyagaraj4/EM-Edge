Arrived

The Arrived option is used to record the first time arrival and subsequent arrival of patients to the location.  

  1. Click the ‘Arrived’ option from the queue status to display the First Time Arrival screen.

  2. Select the Room from the list. The Room specified during Visit Registration is displayed, by default. This field is not mandatory if no rooms are associated to the Clinic.

  3. Select the Other Resource Type from the list and click [?] function and select the Other Resource based on the type selected. The Other Resource specified during Visit Registration is displayed, by default.

  4. On entering the required details, click [OK] to record Arrival date and time or [Close] to exit from the screen.

  5. On recording, Internal Reports screen is displayed if defined, click [OK] to generate Internal Reports or [Cancel] to exit from the screen.

On selecting Patient Details hyperlink from the patient queue, Arrived, Assign Practitioner and Start Consultation options are available for selection. On recording the First Time Arrival, the queue options automatically change to Revert to Check In, Record Vital Signs and Assign Practitioner. The queue status is displayed as ‘Arrived at NS’. For Subsequent Arrival recording, the queue status changes to ‘Returned from Investigations’, if presently undergoing Treatment or Investigations or the status is left unchanged.
