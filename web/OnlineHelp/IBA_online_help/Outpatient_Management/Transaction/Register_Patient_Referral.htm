Register Patient Referral

From this function you can Register a Patient as a referral patient to other facilities within an enterprise or other external facilities.

The referred patient is either a registered or an unregistered patient. If the patient is registered the ‘Patient Search’ function can be invoked to retrieve the patient record and continue by entering the remaining details. If the patient is not registered, the mandatory fields have to be entered. On completion of the referral registration a unique referral identification number is generated to accept the patients within the enterprise.

Registering a Patient as Referral

From the OP Transactions, select the Register Patient Referral function.

The Register Patient Referral screen gets displayed.

  1. Click [Create] to invoke the Create screen.

  2. Enter the details in the mandatory (*) fields. <Related Field Description>

  3. Click [Contact Details] to enter/modify the contact details or [Close] to exit from the screen.

  4. Click [Clinical Details] to enter the details or [Close] to exit from the screen.

  5. Click [Notes] to enter the Referral Notes or [Close] to close the screen.

  6. Click [Apply] to register the patient as referral or [Reset] to clear the entered data.

  7. Internal Reports screen gets displayed, click [OK] to generate Internal Reports or click [Cancel] to exit from the screen.

  8. The system prompts with a message, if you want to continue with Referral Registration for the same patient.

  9. Click [OK] to continue the Referral Registration and the Referral Registration number gets generated or [Cancel] to abort the process.

  10. Click [Query] to search for the existing referral patients and update the referral details.
