Enter the following additional information in the Document section:

  1. Supplier – Select the Supplier to whom the goods are to be returned from the list. This field is mandatory.

  2. Replacement RTV – Select the check box, if the goods are returned for replacement.

  3. Entry Completed – Select the check box to indicate that entry is completed. The check box is cleared, by default. This field is provided to avoid the document from being finalized before a thorough check by the concerned in charge before the goods are returned.

 

Item Entry Area

 

Enter the following details to record the Item Return Details:

 

  1. Item - Enter the required Item to be returned in alphanumeric format of 60 characters maximum length or select the Item using [?] function. The Items displayed are limited to the selected Store. On selection of an Item, the Supplier is disabled and the Maximum available quantity and the corresponding UOM is displayed and disabled. This is a mandatory field.

  2. Normal – Enter the Normal Quantity of the Item to be returned in numeric format of 12 digits maximum length. This is a mandatory field.

  3. Max Available – This value corresponds to the total available quantity against goods received from the Supplier excluding the Bonus quantity entered using the GRN function.

  4. Bonus – Enter the Bonus Quantity of the Item to be returned in numeric format of 12 digits maximum length.

  5. Max Available – This value corresponds to the total Item quantity received as bonus in the selected Store.

  6. Remarks – Enter the remarks for the Items to be returned in alphanumeric format of 200 characters maximum length.
