Stock Status

From this function you can Query to view the stock status of a product in the facility. You can Print the stock status of the selected product.

The Stock Status query function enables you to query on the current stock status of the products. You can view and print the details of the stock details either in summarized format or in detail. The expired units are highlighted with color.  

Querying the Stock Status

From Query, double-click the Stock Status function.

The Stock Status screen gets displayed.

  1. Enter a valid Product Code or click [List of Values] to view and select the Product Code.

  2. Click [Execute Query] or press [F8] to query the stock status.

  3. The product code and description, associated blood group and Rhesus factor, number of stock items available for use and the total stock are displayed.

  4. Click [Print] to print the stock status details for the selected product. Select ‘Summary’ to print a summarized report. To print a detailed report on the stock status, select ‘Detail’.

  5. Click [Details] to view the stock details. The Stock Details screen is invoked with the product details. The expired products are displayed in color.
