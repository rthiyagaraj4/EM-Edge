Storage Instructions

From this function you can Define various storage instructions to store the blood products in the blood bank. You can Query for the existing records.

The Storage Instructions function enables you to define storage instructions applicable for various blood products in a blood bank. The storage instructions defined are used in the ‘Products’ function.

Defining Storage Instructions

From the Masters, double-click the Storage Instructions function.

The Storage Instructions screen gets displayed.

  1. Enter the details in the mandatory fields. <Related Field Description>

  2. Click [Save] or press [F10] to save the created records or click [Clear Record] to clear the entered data.

  3. Click [Execute Query] or press [F8] to search for the existing records.

 

The Whole Blood, Packed cells and Washed RBCs are to be stored between 2-6 degree centigrade for a maximum of 31-35 days. The Fresh Frozen Plasmas are to be stored in minus 70 degree centigrade for a maximum of one year. The Platelets are to be stored between 20-24 degree centigrade for a maximum of five days. Cryoprecipitate has to be stored in minus 80 degree centigrade for a maximum of five years. The Buffy coat has to be stored between 2-4 degree centigrade for a maximum of seven days.
