The Donors Section displays the donor details recorded during registration:

 

  1. Donor ID – The Donor ID is displayed.

  2. S – The status of the Patient is displayed.

  3. Donor Name - The Patient Name is displayed.

  4. Gender - The Gender of the Patient is displayed.

  5. Date of Birth - The Date of Birth of the Patient is displayed.

  6. Source - The Source of the Patient is displayed.

  7. Description - The Description of the source is displayed.

  8. Prefix - The Prefix attached to the source is displayed.

  9. Patient ID - The Patient ID is displayed.

  10. ID No - The ID Number entered in the Change National ID No function in Master Patient Index Module is displayed.

 

The following colors indicate the status of the donor:

– Deceased

– Inactive

- Suspended
