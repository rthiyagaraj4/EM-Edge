Appointment History

From this function you can View the appointments of a Study Location.

You can view the history of all the appointments scheduled for a patient in a Study Location.

Viewing the Appointment History of a Study Location

From the Queries > Appointments, double-click Appointment History function.

The Appointment History screen gets displayed.

  1. Click [Execute Query] or press [F8] to search for the Appointment history of a Study Location or enter the search criteria and click [Search] to display the Appointment details based on the entered criteria.

    Enter the following query criteria:

    1. Study Location – Enter the Study Location in alphanumeric format of four characters maximum length or click [List of Values] to select the Study Location. This is a mandatory field.

    2. Patient ID – Enter the Patient ID in the length as specified in the MP Parameter. On selecting the Patient ID, the name of the patient is displayed.

    3. Type – Select the Appointment Type from the list. The listed values are ‘Waitlist for Appointments’, ‘Book Appointments’, ‘Transfer Appointment (Individual)’, ‘Transfer Appointments(Bulk)’, ‘Transfer Waitlisted Appointment’, ‘Cancel Appointment (Individual)’ and ‘Cancel Appointments (Bulk)’.

    4. Date [From-To] – Enter the date range for the appointments in the format of dd/mm/yyyy or select the date range using the Calendar function.

    5. Appt Ref Num – Enter the Appointment Reference Number generated while booking the appointment.

  2. Click [+] to view the Appointment History of patient. The name of the logged in user and the date is displayed in ‘Done By’ and ‘Done On’ fields.
