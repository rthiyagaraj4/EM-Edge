Appointments for Study Location

From this function you can View the appointments scheduled for a Study Location.

You can view the details of the appointments scheduled for the specified date, Section and Study Location.

Viewing the Appointments for Study Location

From the Queries > Appointments, double-click Appointments for Study Locations function.

The Appointments for Study Locations screen gets displayed.

  1. Enter the details in the mandatory fields. <Related Field Description>

  2. Click [Search] to display the details of the appointments based on the entered criteria.
