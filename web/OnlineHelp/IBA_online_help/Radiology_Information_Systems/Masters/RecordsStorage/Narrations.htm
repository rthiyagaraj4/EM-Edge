Narrations

From this function you can Record the reasons mentioned when the records are moved from one location to another and Query for the required Narration records.

Narration is a message that tells the particulars of an act. Narrations are recorded while issuing the records, transferring the record from one storage location to another or returning the record to the original location. You can define a default Narration for returning the record in the ‘RD Parameters for Facility’ function.

Creating a Narration

From the Masters > Records Storage, double-click Narrations function.

The Narrations screen gets displayed.

  1. Enter the details in the mandatory (*) fields. <Related Field Description>

  2. Click [Save] or press [F10] to save a recorded narration or click [Clear Record] to clear the entered data.

  3. Click [Execute Query] or press [F8] to search for existing records.

If the mandatory values are not entered, the system alerts you to enter them. The mandatory field is highlighted with a unique color code. Only 10 records can be added at a time. You cannot duplicate the code. The system alerts you on saving the record by highlighting the duplicate codes with a unique color code.
