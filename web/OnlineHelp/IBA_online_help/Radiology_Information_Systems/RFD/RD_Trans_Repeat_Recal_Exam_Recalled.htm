The recalled patient details are displayed.

  1. Enter the search criteria to select a specific recalled patient for examination.

  2. Right click on the Patient ID to 'Book Appointment' and 'Record Called Details'.

    Enter the following details:

    1. Recalled By - The Recalled By Radiologist/Technicians ID and Name is displayed.

    2. Reason - The recalled reason is displayed.

    3. Receiver Type - The recalled receiver type value is displayed. You can modify this value. This is a mandatory field.

    4. Received By - The Received By persons name is displayed. You can modify this field. This is a mandatory field.

    Click [OK] to record the recalled details or [Cancel] to exit from the screen.

  3. Right click on 'Attended By' and 'Ordered By' to view the physicians contact details.
