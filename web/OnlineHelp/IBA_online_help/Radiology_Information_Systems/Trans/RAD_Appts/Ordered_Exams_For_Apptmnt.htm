Ordered Exams for Appointment

From this function you can Book an appointment for a patient to a Radiology Section/Study Location and Query for the required ordered exams for appointment.

The booking can be done for pending orders, referral orders or for orders in waitlist. All the Radiology orders placed which do not require departmental authorization are displayed under ‘Pending Orders’ tab. If the examination requires departmental authorization, the orders (examination) are authorized in the ‘Departmental Authorization’ function. On authorizing the orders (examination), the exam gets displayed under ‘Pending Orders’ tab. The referral orders for Radiology examination are displayed under ‘Referral Orders’ tab. The Pending orders, which have been waitlisted, is displayed under the ‘Waitlist’ tab.

Booking an appointment for a patient

From the Transactions > Appointments, double-click Book function.

The Ordered Exams for Appointment screen gets displayed.

  1. The exams pending for booking are displayed or enter the search criteria and click [Search] to select a specific examination.

  2. On searching, examinations pending for booking are displayed based on the entered criteria. The tabs 'Pending Orders', 'Referral Orders' and 'Waiting List' are displayed. The Pending Orders Tab screen is displayed, by default.

Pending Orders

The radiology orders (orders are placed in the Clinician Access module) directly reflect under the ‘Pending Orders’ tab. Double click to book an appointment. If the duration for the exam is defined in the Exam master, the system takes directly to the ‘Appointment Detail’ Screen, otherwise the system pops up the ‘Book’ screen to select the number of slots to be booked. The patient details such as Patient ID, Name, Age, Sex; and order details such as Wing, Section, Exam, Study Location, Month, Week, Preferred Date, Preferred Date and Time get populated in the Book screen. If it is a direct appointment, these fields  are blank. Select View Dept Auth Audit Trail option in the menu invoked on right click of the Patient ID. On click of this option, the Audit Trail screen gets invoked with Authorized by and Authorised Date details.

 

Based on the duration of the examination, the number of time slots that are required are consecutively available. In case all the slots are booked on a particular day and if any overbooking is available on that day, double click to overbook.

If you want to book the first available slot in that day select the 'First Available’ check box.  Or, if you wish to force book an appointment to a slot select the ‘Force Book’ check box.

 

  • [Schedule] - Click [Schedule] to display the appointment status for the entered room and section combination.

  • [Select] - Click [Select] after double clicking on a slot, which is booked to transfer the appointment to any subsequent period. Select the transfer reason from the list of values. Click [OK] to record the reason for transfer or [Cancel] to cancel the selection.

  • [Transfer] – Use the left click button of the mouse to double click on a slot, which is booked. Click [Transfer] to transfer the appointment. The system transfers the appointment booked to the given slot and date.

  • [Cancel] – Use the left click button of the mouse to double click on a slot, which is booked. Click [Cancel] to cancel the appointment. The system displays the ‘Cancel Appointment’ screen where the Booking can be cancelled.

Referral Orders

Appointment Booking Screen gets displayed. If there is no Order / Wait List reference; enter the Patient's Episode Type and Episode Number. Valid values for Episode Type are ‘Outpatient’, ‘Inpatient’ and ‘Referral’. If it is a Referral appointment, the Episode/Visit Number is not required. The Referral Source Type is defaulted to ‘Clinic’ for Outpatients, ‘Ward’ for Inpatients and ‘Referral’ for Referral cases. The Referral Source code has to be a Clinic, Ward or an External Source. Lookups are available on each of these according to the Source Type. The current Doctor for the Outpatient/inpatient episodes is defaulted, while it must be entered for Referral cases. The patient's number, name and other details are defaulted by the system.

Waiting List

The Orders which are in waiting list are displayed.

  1. Right click on the Patient ID to select the Patient Demographics option.

    On clicking Patient Demographics option, the ‘View Patient Details’ screen opens in a new Internet Explorer browser window. You can view the patient demographic details in the View Patient Details tab.

  2. Click [Create Wait list] to Create a wait list for the pending orders.

  3. Click [Direct Registration] for register the Exam directly.

  4. Click [Save] to book the appointment for the patient or click [Clear Record] to clear the entered data.

  5. Click [Execute Query] or press [F8] to search for a booked appointment for a patient.

A pop-up showing the ‘Number of slots booked’ and ‘Number of vacant slots’ detail is displayed when you right click on the Preferred Date or any calendar field in the ‘Book’ screen.
