Repeat Exam/Recall Patient

From this function you can Recall a patient and Repeat the examination and Query for the exams repeated or patients recalled for the same examination.

The Repeat Exam/Recall Patient function is used to recall a patient and repeat the conducted examination. If the examination has to be repeated while the patient is still in the study location, it is referred to as ‘Repeating the examination’; if at the time of reporting, it is realized that the exam needs to be repeated, the patient is ‘recalled’ for conducting the same examination again.

Recalling Exam and repeating the exams

From the Transactions > Registration, double-click Repeat Exam/Recall Patient function.

The Repeat Exam/Recall Patient screen gets displayed with two tabs. Completed tab is enabled, by default.

  1. Enter the details in the mandatory (*) fields of the following tab pages.

Completed

Recalled

  1. Click [Save] to repeat the exams or click [Clear Record] to clear the entered data.

  2. Click [Execute Query] or press [F8] to search for a registered patient for whom exam has to be repeated or recall a patient.
