Lodge Complaint

From this function you can Lodge Complaint against a Linen Item received from the sources, on delivery of linen items. You can Cancel the complaints recorded against a linen item. Lodge Complaint helps in recording the quality check of the linen items received for cleaning or mending.

The Lodge Complaint function is used to record complaints against a Source, if the linen items sent for mending or cleaning fail the quality check. You can record the Linen Delivery details, reason for Late Delivery, details regarding Infected Linen, reason for quality failure and details on shortage of linen supply. You have the option to cancel the records while placing the request for new or cleaned linen under the Source function.

Recording complaint against a Source

From the Linen > Laundry, select the Lodge Complaint function.

The Lodge Complaint screen gets displayed.

  1. Enter the details in the mandatory (*) fields. <Related Field Description>

  2. Click [Select] to display a lodged complaint or [Cancel] to clear the entered or displayed details.

  3. The selected Linen Item is displayed along with the reference number, date/time, linen item and quantity. The Linen Item is displayed as a hyperlink. On clicking the Linen Item, the selected Linen Item, its reference quantity, date/time, linen item and quantity are displayed in the respective fields for editing.

  4. Click [Submit] to save the lodged Complaint for a Source Type.

  5. The Stock Status and Work Status of the linen items in the laundry are displayed. You can choose to view the status of the linen items in laundry by selecting a source Type and a Source. The current system date is displayed. Click [Go] to search for the details in the respective Source and Source Type.
