Enter Cleaned Linen

From this function you can Record details about the linen items cleaned based on the request placed. You can View the requests placed.

The Enter Cleaned Linen function is used by the laundry department to record details about the cleaned linen details. The details on the linen cleaned on a particular date have to be entered by the Laundry personnel. Laundry personnel can verify if a linen item has passed the quality check. In case of quality failure, the nature of defect and the reason for defect are identified and recorded.

Recording cleaned linen item details

From the Linen, select the Enter Cleaned Linen function.

The Enter Cleaned Linen screen gets displayed.

  1. Enter the details in the mandatory (*) fields. <Related Field Description>

  2. Click [Search] to view cleaning request placed by a Nursing Unit/Clinic/Department. <Related Field Description>

  3. Click [Submit] to save the details recorded.

  4. The Stock Status and Work Status of the linen items in the laundry are displayed. You can choose to view the status of the linen items in laundry by selecting a source Type and a Source. The current system is displayed. Click [Go] to search for the details in the respective Source and Source Type.
