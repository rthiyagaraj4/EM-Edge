View Prepaid Payment Details

From this function you can Query to view the prepaid payment details.

The View Prepaid Payment Details function is used to view the prepaid payment details. The details that are recorded through the 'Record Prepaid Receipt' function are listed. You can reprint the receipts that are printed during transaction.

Querying Prepaid Payment Details

From the Prepaid Management > Queries, double-click on View Prepaid Payment Details function.

The View Prepaid Payment Details screen gets displayed.

  1. Click [Execute Query] or press [F8] to enter the details and query the records. You can use the upward and downward arrow in the keyboard to navigate to the previous and next records respectively.

  2. Click [Enter Query] or press [F7] to enter the details and click [Execute Query] or press [F8] to query specific record.

  3. The details as recorded through the 'Record Prepaid Receipt' function are listed. You can also see if the record is active or cancelled.

  4. Click [Reprint Receipt] to reprint a receipt that is printed during while recording the details through 'Record Prepaid Receipt' function.
