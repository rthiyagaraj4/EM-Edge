Maintain Prepaid Patient Records

From this function you can Update the patient records that are registered for Prepayment. You can Query to view the existing patient records.

The Maintain Prepaid Patient Records function is used to update the patient records that are registered through the 'Patient Registration for Prepaid Program' function. You can update the status of the patient, effective to date, prepaid group/protocol, total prepayment amount, amount received and the remaining balance. You can associate multiple protocols to a patient record. The payment hierarchy is based on the priority of the associated protocol. The subsequent payment begins only when the first prioritized payment is complete.

Maintaining Prepaid Patient Records

From the Prepaid Management > Transaction, double-click on Maintain Prepaid Patient Records function.

The Maintain Prepaid Patient Records screen gets displayed.

  1. The logged-in facility is displayed, by default. The facilities associated to the logged-in user are listed.

  2. Click [List of Values] to select a Prepaid code. This is a mandatory field. <Related Field Description>

  3. Click [Refresh] to query the patient records.

  4. The patient records associated to the selected Prepaid code are listed. The details recorded during registration for the respective patient through 'Patient Registration for Prepaid Program' are listed. <Related Field Description>

  5. Click [Commit Form] or press [F10] to save the records or [Clear Record] to delete the data.
