Print Under Charges Advice

From this function you can Print an advice for all the services associated to the Inpatient or the Daycare patient after the discharge bill is generated.

Print IP Under Charges Advice function is used to print the bills for the services associated to the patient after raising the discharge bill. You can choose to print from a range of bills based on the Bill Date, Bill Number, Customer or print all the bills where the discharge bill is raised. An option is also provided to list and print all the bills, where the under charge advice is generated or only the bills where the entire amount was not settled during patient discharge. Authorized Users are only permitted to login and to re-print the bills. The check box ‘Reprint Bill’ has to be selected in the ‘Users for Authorization/Refund/Cash Counters’ function in the Administration menu.

Print Under Charges Advice for an Inpatient/Daycare patient

From the Transactions > IP Billing, double-click the Print IP Under Charges Advice Function.

The Print IP Under Charges Advice screen gets displayed.

  1. Select an Episode Type to print the undercharge advice for the Inpatients or the Daycare patients in the facility. The bills generated for the selected Episode Type gets displayed. <Related Field Description>

  2. Click [Process Run] to print the undercharge advice or [Clear Record] to clear the entered data.

  3. A bill print authorization screen pops-up. Enter the User ID, PIN Number and the Remarks to login and to re-print the bill. Click [OK] to login and [Cancel] to exit from the screen.

  4. A confirmation screen pops-up on clicking [OK]. Click [Yes] to re-print the bills and [No] to cancel the printing process.
