Reprint Pharmacy Bill

From this function you can Reprint a copy of the pharmacy bills based on the patient request.   

Reprint Pharmacy Bill function is used to re-print the bills for Pharmacy items. You can choose to print from a range of bill numbers generated. A report is printed based on the parameters selected. An authorized User is permitted to login and re-print the visit bills. The check box ‘Reprint Bill’ has to be selected in the ‘Users for Authorization/Refund/Cash Counters’ function in the Administration menu for the selected User. The bill/receipt format that is printed during patient discharge is based on the format defined for the respective Episode Type through the Bill Print Format function.

Re-print Pharmacy Bill

From the Transactions > OP Billing, double-click the Reprint Pharmacy Bill Function.

The Reprint Pharmacy Bill screen gets displayed.

  1. Select a Report Type for transaction.

• Select Single Bill to re-print only one bill. You cannot choose the date range.

• Select Range of Bills by Bill Number to select a range of bills. You cannot choose the date range.

• Select Range of Bills by Bill Date to select a date range. The bills printed during the selected date range for the pharmacy items are printed. You have to enter the bill date.

• Select All Range to print the bills based on the range of bill numbers and the date range selected.  

  1. Click [List of Values] to select a range of Bill Numbers. A bill lookup screen appears. Click [Execute Query] or press [F8] to view and to select the bill numbers. Double-click on a record to select it. The Bill Number and the Bill Document Type gets displayed. This is a mandatory field.

  2. Click Calendar function to select the date range. The visit bills printed for the pharmacy items during the specific date range gets displayed.

  3. Click [Process Run] to reprint the bills or [Clear Record] to clear the entered data.
