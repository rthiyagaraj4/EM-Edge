Credit Card Company Payment Dtls

From this function you can Generate a report on the payments accepted from the credit card companies. The batch date, payment document number and payment amount are displayed.   

Credit Card Company Payment Details function is used to generate a report on the payments accepted from the Credit Card Companies. A detailed report is generated with the document type and number and the receipt details. The amount collected against each receipt type is also displayed. The date of payment along with the receipt number and the bank in which the amount deposited is displayed.

Credit Card Company Payment Dtls

From the Transactions > Credit Card Cycle, double-click the Credit Card Company Payment Dtls function.

The Credit Card Company Payment Dtls screen gets displayed.

  1. Click [List of Values] to select a Settlement Type.

  2. Click Calendar function to select the date range. The payment received from the Credit Card Companies for the selected Settlement Type during the selected period is listed. This is a mandatory field.

  3. Click [Process Run] to generate a report or [Clear Record] to clear the entered data. A confirmation screen pops-up. Click view, print or file to view the report, print the report or to save the output in a file.

  4. A log file is generated with the report details. Click [OK] to view the report and [No] to exit from the screen.
