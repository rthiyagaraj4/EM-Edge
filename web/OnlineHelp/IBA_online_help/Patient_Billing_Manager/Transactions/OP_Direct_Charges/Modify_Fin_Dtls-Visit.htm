Modify Financial Details – Visit

From this function you can Update the financial details of an outpatient/external patient. The financial details are updated and considered only for the visit registration.  

Modify Financial Details – Visit function is used to update the financial details of an outpatient/external patient. The financial details recorded during visit registration are updated. The visit registration charges are calculated based on the updated financial details recorded. Authorized User can update the financial details recorded during visit registration in the facility. The check box ‘Fin. Dtls Change’ has to be selected in the function ‘User Privileges and Cash Counter Access’ under the Administration menu for the User to login and to modify the details.

Modify Financial Details – Visit

From the Transactions > OP Direct Charges, double-click Modify Financial Details – Visit Function.

The Modify Financial Details – Visit screen gets displayed.

  1. Click [Enter Query] and enter a valid Patient Number. The patient name, age, gender, patient registration date and time gets displayed on entering the patient number.

OR

  1. Click [Execute Query] or press [F8] to view all the patient details. Press downward arrow key to navigate to the next record and upward arrow key to navigate to the previous record.

OR

  1. Press [F9] to search and to select a patient ID from the lookup  screen. Click [Execute Query] to view all the patient details or click [Enter Query] and enter search criteria. The records matching the search criteria get displayed. Double-click on the record to select it.

  2. Click [Modify] to login and to update the details. The financial details associated to the patient get displayed. <Related Field Description>

  3. Update the details and click [Commit Form] to save the changes or [Clear Record] to clear the details.
