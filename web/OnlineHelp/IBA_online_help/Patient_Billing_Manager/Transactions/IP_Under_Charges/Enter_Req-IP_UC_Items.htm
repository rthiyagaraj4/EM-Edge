Enter Request – IP Under Charge Items

From this function you can Associate the billing services, which were either not included in the bill or served to the patient after the discharge bill was generated to the Inpatient.

Enter Request – IP Under Charge Items function is used to associate the Billing Services to the Inpatients or the Daycare patients who are discharged from the facility. The services, which were not a part of the bills during bill generation, are associated to the patient records using this function. A transaction reference ID is generated for each service associated to the patient. The authorized Users only can associate the services to the patient after discharge bill is raised. The check box ‘Enter Service Request’ has to be selected for the logged in User in the function ‘Users for Services’ under the Administration menu to associate the services to the patients in the facility. The services defined with the ‘Direct Entry Allowed’ check box selected are listed. Authorized User has to choose a service from the list. During discharge, a bill is generated for the services utilized based on the quantity of the service used. The service price is based on the Multipricing factor, if the service is rate based. If the service is charge based, the charge defined is calculated. The charges for the services associated is calculated automatically and displayed. You cannot update the charges for the rate based services. The total charge is computed and displayed. The Services with the ‘Autoconfirm’ check box selected during definition are confirmed automatically. If the check box is cleared during definition, the services have to be confirmed manually using the function ‘Confirm Inpatient Service Requests’.

Associating a Service

From the Transactions > IP Under Charges, select the Enter Request - IP Under Charge Items Function.

The Enter Requests – IP Under Charge Items screen gets displayed.

  1. Select an Episode Type from the list. The values are system defined - Inpatient and Daycare. Select Inpatient to associate services to an Inpatient admitted to the facility and Daycare patient to associate services to a patient admitted to the facility.

  2. Enter the details in the mandatory (*) fields. <Related Field Description>

OR

  1. Right click on Right Click for User Options to search for the relevant patient record. You can search for a patient record based on the Patients Lookup, Patient/Admission/Transfer lookup or Admission/Transfer lookup.

  2. Select the check box View Billing to view the billing details.

    The financial details of the patient for the selected episode get displayed. You can also view the unconfirmed services associated to the patient by clicking [Unconfirmed Services]. The Settlement Details, Billing Group details, service charges, gross amount for the services utilized, adjustments or refunds issued, unbilled and outstanding amount gets displayed. The name of the user, who has recorded or modified the financial details gets displayed.

  3. Click [Future Order] to view any orders placed for future visits. If any orders are placed for future visits using the external modules, you can view the orders and the details by clicking [Future Order].

  4. Associate the Billing Services or a Billing Panel to the patient. The services or the panels are charged based on the price defined for the Billing Service. <Related Field Description>

  5. Click [Commit Form] to associate the service or [Clear Record] to clear the entered data.

  6. A Transaction Reference ID is generated on successfully associating a service or a panel.
