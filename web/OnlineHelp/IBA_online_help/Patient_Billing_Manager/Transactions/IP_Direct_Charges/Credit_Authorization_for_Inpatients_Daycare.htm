Credit Authorization for Inpatients/Daycare

From this function you can Authorize the credit details of the Inpatient and Day Care patients

The Credit Authorization function is used to authorize the inpatient and day care patient details.

Authorizing Credit details of Inpatient/Day Care

From the Patient Billing Manager > Transactions > IP Direct Charges, double click the Credit Authorization for Inpatient function.

The Credit Authorization for Inpatient screen gets displayed.

  1. Select Episode Type from the drop-down list. The listed values are ‘Inpatient’ and 'Daycare’. The Credit Authorization for Inpatients screen gets displayed.

    Enter the following details:

    1. Patient ID – Enter the inpatient ID to display the description of the patient.

    2. Episode ID – Enter Episode ID of the selected Episode type.

    3. Customer - The customer name is displayed based on customer associated with patient.

    4. Credit Doc Reference – The Credit Doc Reference is displayed based on customer associated with patient.

    5. Credit Auth Ref No – Enter the credit Authorization reference number that is defined in the billing masters.

    6. Approved Amt - Enter the amount approved by the accountant.

    7. Approval Dt - Enter the amount approved date.

    8. No. of Days – The number of days for authorization is displayed.

    9. Ward – Enter the ward name or click [List of Values] to select ward name.

    10. Bed - Enter the bed name or click [List of Values] to select bed name.

    11. Proc Code - Enter the process code for credit authorization.

    12. Authorized By - Enter the name who have authorized for credit authorization.

  2. Click [Commit Form] or press [F10] to save or [Clear Record] to clear the entered data.

  3. Click [Execute Query] or press [F8] to search for the existing records within the facility.
