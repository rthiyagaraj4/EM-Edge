Service Analysis – Mth/Yr

From this function you can Query on the services utilized by the facility for a specific month and year. You can view the services utilized by the accounting department, originating department and by a Doctor.

The Service Income – Mth/Yr function is used to query upon the services utilized by the facility for the specified month and year. You can view the total quantity of the service utilized during each month for the year specified. The name of the department gets listed along with the Doctor name who has utilized the service. The total quantity of the service utilized by a specific department gets listed based on the criteria selected. You have an option to view the services utilized by an originating department, a doctor or an accounting department associated to the facility.

Querying Service Analysis for a month and year

From the Queries > Analysis, double-click Service Analysis – Mth/Yr function.

The Service Analysis – Mth/Yr screen gets displayed.

  1. Click Calendar function to select the month and year to query the service analysis. The accounting month and period specified in the BL Parameters function gets displayed. You can update the value by entering the month and year in mm/yyyy format. This is a mandatory field.

  2. Select Criteria from the list. The values are system defined – Billing Service, Orig Dept, A/C Dept and Doctor. The details get displayed based on the Criteria selected.

  3. The fields Billing Service, A/C Dept, Orig Dept and Doctor get enabled based on the Criteria selected. Click [List of Values] to select a value for the criteria selected. The description for the selected value gets displayed.

  4. Enter the search criteria and click [Execute Query] or press [F8]. The records matching the entered search criteria get displayed under the respective tab. Click [Clear] to clear the records displayed in the result area.
