Statement of Accounts

From this function you can Query to view the billing details and the payment details of a patient in the facility.

Statement of Accounts function is used to inquire on the billing and the payment details of a patient. You can inquire on all the transactions for a patient irrespective of the episode details. Using this function you can also view the status of selected documents.  

Querying to view the Statement of Accounts

From the Queries > Transactions, double-click Statement of Accounts function.

The Statement of Accounts screen gets displayed.

  1. Enter the details in the fields to query  for a specific patient record. The patient records matching the search criteria get displayed in the result area. Click [Click for Searching Patients] to view the financial and encounter details of a specific patient. Click [Cancel Search Criteria] to clear the entered data.

OR

  1. Click [Click for Searching Patients] to list all the patient records. You can view the statement of a specific patient. Click [Cancel Search Criteria] to clear the entered data. The records matching the search criteria get displayed in the result area.

  2. The records get displayed in the result area. Double-click on the relevant patient record. The Statement of Accounts screen gets displayed listing the billing details and the payment history.

  3. Click [Click for Patient Encounters] to view all the patient encounters recorded in the facility. The encounter details of a patient get listed in the Encounter Details screen. Double-click on the relevant record to view the billing details and the payment history of the selected patient.  Click [Close] to exit from the Encounter Details screen.
