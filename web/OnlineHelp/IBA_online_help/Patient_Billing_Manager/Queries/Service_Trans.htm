Service Trans

From this function you can Query to view the service transactions associated to a patient in the facility.

Service Trans function is used to inquire on the service transactions of a patient in the facility. You can query the admission details in case of Inpatients, visit details in case of Outpatients and the details of referral patients based on the services used during a specified date range. The episode type differentiates a patient category. This function also accepts a billing service code or a treatment package code for which the transactions are to be viewed.

Querying to view the service transactions

From the Queries > Transactions, double-click Service Trans function.

The Service Trans screen gets displayed.

  1. Enter the details in the fields to query for a specific patient record. The patient records matching the search criteria get displayed in the result area. Click [Click for Searching Patients] to view the financial and encounter details of a specific patient. Click [Cancel Search Criteria] to clear the entered data.

    Enter the following details to query for a patient record:

    1. Patient No – Enter the Patient Number in alphanumeric format with a maximum of 10 characters. The records matching the patient number gets displayed.

    2. National ID No – Enter the National ID in numeric format with a maximum of 15 digits. The records matching the National ID number gets displayed.

    3. Title – Enter the title in alphanumeric format of 15 characters. The records matching the Title get displayed.

    4. Nationality – Select a Nationality from the list. The values defined using the function Country in the Master Patient Index module get listed.

    5. First Name – Enter the first name of the patient to search for a specific patient record. The records matching the patient’s first name gets displayed.

    6. Second Name – Enter the second name of the patient to search for a specific patient record. The records matching the patient’s second name gets displayed.

    7. Third Name – Enter the third name of the patient to search for a specific patient record. The records matching the patient’s third name gets displayed.

    8. Family Name – Enter the family name of the patient to search for a specific patient record. The records matching the patient’s family name gets displayed.

    9. Area - Enter the area to search for a specific patient record. The records matching the area get displayed.

    10. Sex – Select a value from the list. The values are system defined – All, Male, Female and Unknown.

    11. DOB – Click Calendar function to select a date of birth of the patient. The records matching the date of birth of the patient get displayed.

    12. By – Select a value from the lsit. The values are – Date and Year. Select Date to search for the records matching the birth date specified in the field DOB. Select Year to search for the records matching the birth year specified in the field DOB.

    13. Res Telephone No – Enter the residence telephone number. The patient records matching the residence telephone number gets displayed.

    14. Other Contact No – Enter alternative contact number. The patient records matching the alternative telephone number gets displayed.

OR

  1. Click [Click for Searching Patients] to list all the patient records. You can view the statement of a specific patient. Click [Cancel Search Criteria] to clear the entered data. The records matching the search criteria get displayed in the result area.

  2. The records get displayed in the result area. Double-click on the relevant patient record. The Service Trans screen gets displayed.

  3. Click [Click for Patient Encounters] to view all the patient encounters recorded in the facility. The encounter details of a patient get listed in the screen. Double-click on the relevant record to view the service transactions in the Service Trans screen for the specified period. The patient details are displayed in the Service Trans screen. Click [Close] to exit from the Encounter Details screen.

  4. Click [List of Values] to select a Service Code. The transaction document reference number generated while associating the service to the patient gets displayed. This is a mandatory field.

  5. Click Calendar function to select the date range. The transaction document details for the selected service during the period selected gets displayed.
