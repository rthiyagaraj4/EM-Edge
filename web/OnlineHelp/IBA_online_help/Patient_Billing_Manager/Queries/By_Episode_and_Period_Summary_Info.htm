By Episode and Period – Complete Info

From this function you can Query to view the folio transactions for a specific period for a patient admitted in the facility.

The function By Episode and Period – Complete Info is used to query on the episode details of a specific patient. This function is used by the accounts section to view the financial services related to services for a range of transaction dates.

Querying to view the complete billing details based on episode and period

From the Queries > Transactions, double-click By Episode and Period – Complete Info function.

The By Episode and Period – Complete Info screen gets displayed.

  1. Enter the details in the fields to query  for a specific patient record. The patient records matching the search criteria get displayed in the result area. Click [Click for Searching Patients] to view the financial and encounter details of a specific patient. Click [Cancel Search Criteria] to clear the entered data.

OR

  1. Click [Click for Searching Patients] to list all the patient records. You can view the financial and encounter details for a specific patient. Click [Cancel Search Criteria] to clear the entered data. The records matching the search criteria get displayed in the result area.

  2. The records get displayed in the result area. Double-click on the relevant patient record to view the complete charge details recorded during a specific episode. The billing details, discounts and the charges for the specific encounter only gets displayed.

  3. Click Calendar function to select the date range. The billing details for the selected patient episode gets displayed based on the period selected.

  4. Click [Click for Patient Encounters] to view all the patient encounters recorded in the facility. The encounter details of a patient get listed in the Encounter Details screen. Double-click on the relevant record to view the billing details. Click [Close] to exit from the Encounter Details screen.
