Audit Trail – Financial Details

From this function you can Query on the financial details of a patient admitted in the facility.

Audit Trail – Financial Details function is used to query the changes done to the financial details of the patients. The system assigns a unique serial number for the change each modification to the patient financial details. The modifications are queried using the serial number assigned. The old financial details, new financial details, the name of the user, who has updated the financial details get displayed.  

Querying to view the financial details

From the Queries > Audit Trail, double-click Audit Trail – Patient Financial Details function.

The Audit Trail – Patient Financial Details screen gets displayed.

  1. Enter the details in the fields to query for a specific patient record. The patient records matching the search criteria get displayed in the result area. Click [Click for Searching Patients] to view the financial and encounter details of a specific patient. Click [Cancel Search Criteria] to clear the entered data.

    Enter the following details to query for a patient record:

    1. Patient No – Enter the Patient Number in alphanumeric format of 10 characters maximum length. The records matching the patient number get displayed.

    2. National ID No – Enter the National ID in numeric format of 15 digits maximum length. The records matching the National ID number get displayed.

    3. Title – Enter the title in alphanumeric format of 15 characters. The records matching the Title get displayed.

    4. Nationality – Select a Nationality from the list. The values defined using the function Country in the Master Patient Index module get listed.

    5. First Name – Enter the first name of the patient to search for a specific patient record. The records matching the patient’s first name get displayed.

    6. Second Name – Enter the second name of the patient to search for a specific patient record. The records matching the patient’s second name get displayed.

    7. Third Name – Enter the third name of the patient to search for a specific patient record. The records matching the patient’s third name get displayed.

    8. Family Name – Enter the family name of the patient to search for a specific patient record. The records matching the patient’s family name get displayed.

    9. Area - Enter the area to search for a specific patient record. The records matching the area get displayed.

    10. Sex – Select a value from the list. The values are system defined – All, Male, Female and Unknown.

    11. DOB – Click Calendar function to select a date of birth of the patient. The records matching the date of birth of the patient get displayed.

    12. By – Select a value from the list. The values are – Date and Year. Select Date to search for the records matching the birth date specified in the field DOB. Select Year to search for the records matching the birth year specified in the field DOB.

    13. Res Telephone No – Enter the residence telephone number. The patient records matching the residence telephone number get displayed.

    14. Other Contact No – Enter alternative contact number. The patient records matching the alternative telephone number get displayed.

  2. Click [Click for Searching Patients] to list all the patient records. You can view the financial and encounter details for a specific patient. Click [Cancel Search Criteria] to clear the entered data.

  3. The records matching the search criteria get displayed in the result area. Double-click on the relevant patient record to view the encounter financial details recorded during a specific encounter. Click [Click for Patient Encounters] to view all the patient encounters in the facility. The encounter details get listed in the Encounter Details screen. Double-click on the relevant encounter to view the financial details and the folio details for the specific encounter. Click [Close] to exit from the Encounter Details screen.
