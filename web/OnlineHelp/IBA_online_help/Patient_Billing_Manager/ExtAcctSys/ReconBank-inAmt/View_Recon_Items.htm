View – Reconciled Items

From this function you can Query to view the reconciled and unreconciled records associated to an account code.

View - Reconciled Items function is used to view the receiver’s statement record status for reconciled or unreconciled statements. Click [Execute Query] or press [F8] to view the details. System populates the reconciled or unreconciled records as per Hospital’s Account or Receiver's Account.

View Reconciled and Unreconciled items

From the External Accounting System > Reconcile Bank-in Amount, double-click View – Reconciled Items Function.

The View – Reconciled Items screen gets displayed.

  1. Click [Execute Query] or press [F8] to view reconciled or unreconciled items.

  2. Select a value from the list. The system-defined values are ‘Receiver A/c’ and ‘Hospital A/c’. Receiver’s statement is considered as the base statement and matching records are listed in the subsequent block. Hospital statement is considered as the base statement and matching record will populate in the subsequent block.

  3. Click the Calendar function to select the month and year. The records reconciled in the selected month are displayed.

  4. Select 'Reconciled' or 'Unreconciled' to view either the reconciled records or unreconciled records respectively.

  5. The Hospital and Receiver's Account details are displayed under the respective blocks.
