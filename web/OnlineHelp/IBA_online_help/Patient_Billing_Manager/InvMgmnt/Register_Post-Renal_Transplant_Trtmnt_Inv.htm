Register Post-renal Transplant Treatment Invoice

From this function you can Register an inpatient for renal treatment after discharged from the facility.

The Register Post-renal Transplant Treatment Invoice function enables you to register an inpatient for renal treatment after being discharged from the facility. The details about the post-renal treatment is recorded while discharging the patient from the facility. You have to associate the renal treatment code selected in the 'Invoice Process Parameters' function to the patient. Bill has to be raised for the selected treatment. Once the bill is generated, you can raise an invoice using the 'Generate Invoice - IP/DC' function. The generated invoice is used to register the patient for the post-renal treatment. You have to finalize the treatment once it is registered. You can generate invoice for the treatment availed by the patient from the facility using the 'Generate Invoice Post-Renal Transplant Treatment' function. This is valid for inpatients only.

Registering Post-Renal Transplant Treatment for the patient:

From the Invoice Management > Transaction, double-click on Register Post-Renal Transplant Treatment Invoice function.

The Register Post-Renal Transplant Treatment Invoice screen gets displayed.

  1. Enter the details in the mandatory (*) fields in the following tabs:

View/Edit

Register

  1. Click [Commit Form] to save the records or [Clear Record] to delete the data entered.

  2. Click [Execute Query] to query for the existing records.
