Generate Haemodialysis Invoice/Debit/Credit Note

From this function you can Generate an Invoice, Debit or Credit Note for Haemodialysis services utilized by a patient in the facility.  

Generate Haemodialysis Invoice/Debit/Credit Note function is used to generate an Invoice, Debit or Credit Note for Haemodialysis services. A bill is printed during invoice generation listing the outstanding payment, if the check box ‘Bill Printing Required’ is selected in ‘Invoice Process Parameters’ function. A single invoice is generated for multiple bills that are grouped under the same Policy Group and associated to a Payer. Different color indicators are used to highlight the status of a specific patient record.

- No Invoice Generation Rule Setup

- Diagnosis to be recorded/recoded

- Previous Payers Invoice to be Generated

- Patient Discharge Required

- Bill Printing Required

- Bill Posting Required

- Special Service Item

- Not Printed

- Not Posted

Generating Invoice, Debit or Credit Note for Haemodialysis

From the Invoice Management > Transaction, double-click on Generate Haemodialysis Invoice/Debit/Credit Note function.

The Generate Haemodialysis Invoice/Debit/Credit Note screen gets displayed.

  1. Enter the details in the mandatory fields. <Related Field Description>

  2. Click [Generate] to generate invoice, debit note or credit note. Click [Clear Record] to delete the data entered. A document reference number is generated on clicking [Generate].
