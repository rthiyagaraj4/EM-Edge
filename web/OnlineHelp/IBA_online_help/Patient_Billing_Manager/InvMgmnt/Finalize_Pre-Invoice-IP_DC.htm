Finalize Pre-Invoice-IP/DC

From this function you can Finalize the Pre-invoice generated for a Payer covering the bill amount of an inpatient or a daycare patient.

Finalize Pre-Invoice - IP/DC function is used to finalize the pre-invoice generated for an inpatient or a daycare patient in the facility.

You can finalize the pre-invoice that is generated through the 'Generate Pre-Invoice - IP/DC' function after the Payer has approved coverage for pre-invoice.  The pre-invoice is cancelled on finalizing the records and a normal invoice is generated.

Finalizing Pre-Invoice for an Inpatient or Daycare patient

From Invoice Management > Setup, double-click on Finalize Pre-Invoice-IP/DC function.

The Finalize Pre-Invoice - IP/DC screen gets displayed.

  1. The name of the logged-in facility is displayed, by default. You cannot select any other facility.

  2. Click [List of Values] to select the Patient ID. The name of the patient, age and gender are displayed when the patient ID is selected. <Related Field Description>

  3. Click [Search] to query the patient records that have to be finalized. You can query for a specific record by specifying any of the search criteria.

  4. The encounters that are available for pre-invoice generation are listed. The details recorded during pre-invoice generation are listed. The Document Type and Number, date when the pre-invoice is generated, document amount, patient ID, name of the patient, encounter ID, Payer Group and description, Payer, Account Sequence Number, Priority and Policy Type and description that are associated to the respective patient record during pre-invoice generation are displayed.

  5. Select the check box against a patient record to finalize pre-invoice generated for a patient encounter in the facility.

  6. Click [Finalize] to finalize the pre-invoice records.

  7. Generated Finalized Pre-Invoice Dtls  screen is displayed.
