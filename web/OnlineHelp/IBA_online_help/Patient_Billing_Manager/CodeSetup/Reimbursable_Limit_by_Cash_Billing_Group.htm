Reimbursable Limit by Cash Billing Group    

From this function you can Define a reimbursable amount for a particular Billing Group. You can specify the reimbursable amount for a Billing Service, an Enterprise Service Group, an Enterprise Service Group/Line, a Material Item associated to the Billing Group. You can Query to view the Reimbursable Amount defined.

Reimbursable Limit by Cash Billing Group is used to define the maximum reimbursable amount for an Enterprise Service Group, Enterprise Service Group/Line, or a Billing Service associated to a Billing Group. You have to specify the reimbursable amount for the material items associated to the Billing Group as well. The reimbursable amount recorded is specific for a patient encounter in the facility. You cannot reimburse an amount greater than the amount defined for a Billing Service linked to a Billing Group.

Defining Reimbursable Limit by Cash Billing Group

From the Masters > Interfaced Modules Financial Dtls, double-click on Reimbursable Limit by Cash Billing Group function.

The Reimbursable Limit by Cash Billing Group screen gets displayed.

  1. Select a Facility from the list. The facilities associated to the logged in user get listed. The limit set for reimbursable amount is applicable for the selected services and the associated Billing Group in the facility. This is a mandatory field.

  2. Click [List of Values] to select a Billing Group. The limit set on reimbursable amount is applicable for the selected Billing Group in the facility. This is a mandatory field.

  3. Enter the details in the mandatory (*) fields found in the following tab pages.

Billing Service Reimbursable Limit

Material Item Reimbursable Limit

  1. Click [Commit Form] or press [F10] to save or [Clear Record] to clear the entered data.

  2. Click [Execute Query] or press [F8] to search for the existing records within the facility.
