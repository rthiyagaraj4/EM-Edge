Enter the following details to record the bounced cheque entries on clicking [Authorize]:

 

  1. Reason – The reason selected while logging in to authorize the entries get displayed.

  2. Bounced Date – Click Calendar function to select the date. The date indicates when the cheque was rejected by the bank.

  3. Reference No – Enter the bank reference number for Cheque in alphanumeric format of 30 characters maximum length. This number is used to identify the respective bounced cheque.

  4. Reference Date - Enter the reference date recorded by the bank on the bounced Cheque.

  5. Remarks – Enter the Narration in alphanumeric format of 50 characters maximum length. You can specify the reason why the cheque was considered invalid.  

  6. Black Listed – Select the check box to black list the record.

  7. Remarks – The text box is enabled, if the check box ‘Black Listed’ is selected. You cannot enter the remarks, if the check box is cleared.

  8. Patient Remarks – Select the check box to record the patient remarks against the bounced cheque. The patient remarks recorded for the receipt during transaction gets displayed. You can enter additional remarks in alphanumeric format of 50 characters maximum length.

  9. Print Replacement Letter – If the check box is selected, letter for intimation to the patient or customer is printed. If the check box is cleared, the letter for intimation is not printed.
