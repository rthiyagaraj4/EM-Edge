Enter the following details to record prepaid receipts:

 

  1. Payer Group - Click [List of Values] to select the Payer Group. The values that are defined through the 'Payer Group' function are listed.

  2. Payer - Click [List of Values] to select the Payer. The values that are defined through the 'Payer' function are listed.

  3. Doc Date - System date and time is displayed as the document date and time, by default. You cannot change the value.

  4. Receipt Option - Select a receipt option from the list. The system-defined values are 'Patient wise' and 'Consolidated by Payer'. Select 'Patient wise' to generate a receipt for each payer transaction. Select 'Consolidated by Payer' to generate a consolidated receipt for a Payer. The list box is enabled for selection only if the 'Deposit Type' is selected as 'Deposit Against Patient'.

  5. Prepaid Program - Click [List of Values] to select a Prepaid Program. The values that are defined through the 'Prepaid Payment Setup Rule' function are listed.

  6. Amount Settled - Enter the amount settled by the Payer in numeric format. The amount settled has to be same as the total settlement amount.

  7. Cash Counter - The Cash Counter code and description to which the user has logged-in is displayed, by default.

  8. Narration - Enter the narration in alphanumeric format. You can record any additional description.

  9. Srl No - The serial number is displayed automatically. You cannot update the value.

  10. Doc Amt - The amount settled is displayed as the document amount, by default. You can update the document amount, but it should not be greater than the total settlement amount.

  11. Settlement Type - Click [List of Values] to select a  Settlement Type. The values defined through the 'Settlement Types' function are listed.

  12. Cash Given - Enter the amount collected from the Payer in numeric format.

  13. Returned - The difference of [Cash Given - Doc Amt] is displayed automatically. You cannot update the value.

  14. Cheque/Bank A/C No. - Enter the cheque or the bank account numeric in alphanumeric format. You can enter the value only if the Settlement Type is selected as 'Cheque' or 'Bank Transfer'.

  15. Date - Click the Calendar function to record the date of cheque settlement.

  16. Bank - Enter the name of the Bank where the transaction has to take place.

  17. Bank/Card Co. Name - Enter the name of the Bank or the Credit Card Company name in alphanumeric format.

  18. Online Approval - Select the check box if the transaction has to be approved online.

  19. Batch No -  Enter the Batch number in alphanumeric format. This is valid for Credit Card transactions.

  20. Approval Code - Enter the Approval Code in alphanumeric format. This is valid for Credit Card transactions.

  21. Sale Draft No - Enter the sale draft number in alphanumeric format.

  22. TID No. - Enter the transaction ID in alphanumeric format.

  23. CC Issued Bank - Enter the name of the Bank that has issued the Credit Card.

  24. Total - The total settlement amount is displayed.

  25. Settlement Type Desc - The description of the selected settlement type is displayed.

  26. Payer's Name - The name of the selected Payer is displayed.

  27. Payer's Ref ID - Enter the Payer's Reference ID in alphanumeric format.

  28. Nominee - Enter the name of the nominee.

 

Deposit Against Patient

The following tabs are displayed when the 'Deposit Type' is selected as 'Deposit Against Patient'. The patient details are recorded through the 'Patient Details for Settlement' tab and the settlement mode is recorded through the 'Settlement Details' tab.

Patient Details for Settlement

  1. Patient ID - Click [List of Values] to select a Patient ID. The prepaid amount is settled for the respective patient. The name of the patient is displayed.

  2. Register No. - The register number recorded during patient registration through 'Patient Registration for Prepaid Program' function is displayed. You cannot  update the register number.

  3. Register Date - The registration date recorded during patient registration through 'Patient Registration for Prepaid Program' function is displayed. You cannot  update the register date.

  4. Protocol - The protocol associated to the patient through 'Patient Registration for Prepaid Program' function is displayed. You cannot  update the Protocol. The description of the associated Protocol is displayed.

  5. Payment No. - The Payment Number associated to the patient during registration is displayed. You cannot  update the Payment Number.

  6. Due Date - The due date associated to the patient record during registration through 'Patient Registration for Prepaid Program' function is displayed.  You cannot update the due date.

  7. Payable Amt - The payable amount recorded for the respective patient is displayed.

  8. Settled Amount - The amount settled for the respective patient record by the payer is displayed. The total amount for all the patient records are displayed.

  9. Payer Group - The Payer Group associated to the patient is displayed. The description of the corresponding Payer Group is displayed.

  10. Payer - The Payer associated to the patient is displayed. The description of the corresponding Payer is displayed.

  11. Policy - The Policy associated to the patient is displayed. The description of the corresponding Policy is displayed.

 

Settlement Details

  4. Doc Amt - The amount settled is displayed as the document amount. You can update the value, but it has to be equal to the settlement amount.

  6. Cash Given - Enter the amount collected from the Payer in numeric format.

  7. Returned - The difference of [Cash Given - Doc Amt] is displayed automatically. You cannot update the value.

  10. Bank - Enter the name of the Bank where the transaction has to take place.

  11. Bank/Card Co. Name - Enter the name of the Bank or the Credit Card Company name in alphanumeric format.

  12. Online Approval - Select the check box if the transaction has to be approved online.

  13. Batch No -  Enter the Batch number in alphanumeric format. This is valid for Credit Card transactions.

  14. Approval Code - Enter the Approval Code in alphanumeric format. This is valid for Credit Card transactions.

  15. Sale Draft No - Enter the sale draft number in alphanumeric format.

  16. TID No. - Enter the transaction ID in alphanumeric format.

  17. CC Issued Bank - Enter the name of the Bank that has issued the Credit Card.

  18. Total - The total settlement amount is displayed.

  19. Settlement Type Desc - The description of the selected settlement type is displayed.

  22. Nominee - Enter the name of the nominee.

 

Lookup for Search and Patient Selection

Search Criteria

Either of Due Date, Register Date, Patient ID or the Register Number is mandatory to search for a specific patient record.

Enter the following details to specify the search criteria:

  1. Due Date [From - To] - Click the Calendar function to select the due date range. The patient records with the due date matching the search criteria are listed.

  2. Patient ID - Click [List of Values] to select a Patient ID. The records of the patient matching the search criteria are listed.

  3. Register No. - Enter the register number of the patient in alphanumeric format to search for a specific patient record.

  4. Register Date [From - To] - Click the Calendar function to select the register date range. The patient records with the register date matching the search criteria are listed.

  5. Protocol - Click [List of Values] to select a Protocol. The patient records with the protocol specified are listed.

  6. Payment No [From - To] - Click [List of Values] to select a Payment Number. The patient records with the payment number matching the search criteria are listed.

  7. Option - Select a value from the list. The system-defined values are 'All' and 'Pending Payments'. Select 'All' to query all the patient records. Select 'Pending Payments' to query the patient records with outstanding amount.

  8. [Search] - Click [Search] to query the patient records matching the search criteria. The patient records matching the search criteria are displayed.

  9. Select - Select the check box to allocate the prepaid amount and settle the outstanding amount.

  10. [Allocate] - Click [Allocate] to allocate the amount against the patient record.
