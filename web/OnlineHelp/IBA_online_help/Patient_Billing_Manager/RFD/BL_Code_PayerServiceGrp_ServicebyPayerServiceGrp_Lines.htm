Service by Payer Service Group/Lines

You can choose and associate multiple billing services to a Payer service Group. This tab is enabled only on committing the records in the Payer Service Group Lines tab.

 

Enter the following details to associate services to the Service Group:

 

  1. Payer Service Group - Click [List of Values] to select a Payer Service Group. The description for the selected Payer Service Group gets displayed. The Payer Service Group selected under the ‘Payer Service Group Lines’ tab gets displayed, by default.

  2. Payer Service Group Line – Click [List of Values] to select a Payer Service Group Line. The description for the selected Payer Service Group Line gets displayed. The Payer Service Group Line selected under the ‘Payer Service Group Lines’ tab gets displayed, by default.

  3. Non-DRG Related - The check box is selected, if the Payer Service Group is selected as Non-DRG Related under ‘Payer Service Group Lines’ tab. The check box is cleared, if the Payer Service Group is DRG related.

  4. Billing Service – Click [List of Values] to select a Billing Service. The selected Billing Service is associated to the Payer Service Group and the Payer Service Group Line. This is a mandatory field.

  5. Description - The description for the selected Billing Service is displayed.

  6. Effective [From-To] – Click the Calendar function to select the date range during which the selected Billing Service is under selected payer coverage. The coverage amount for the specific Billing Service is calculated based on the policy definition for the respective patient class, Payer and Payer Group combination. The respective Billing Service is under policy coverage for the date range selected.

  7. Non-DRG Related - Select the check box, if the Billing Service is Non-DRG Related. The DRG Related services are valid for inpatient transactions only.  Clear the check box, if the Billing Service is DRG related.
