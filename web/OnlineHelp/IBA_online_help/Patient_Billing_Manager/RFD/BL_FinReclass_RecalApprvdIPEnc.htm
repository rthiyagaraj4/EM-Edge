The following details are displayed based on the patient details entered during reclassification:

 

  1. Patient ID - The patient IDs are listed based on the search criteria.

  2. Admission Date - The date when the patient is admitted to the facility is displayed.

  3. Episode ID - The Episode ID of the patient for which the financial details are reclassified is displayed.

  4. Billing Group - The Billing Group associated to the patient during admission is displayed.

  5. Payer Group/Payer - The Customer Group/Customer selected for the respective patient admission is displayed.

  6. Policy Type - The Policy Type selected when the Billing Group is selected as Insurance, gets displayed.

  7. Finalized By - The User ID of the user who has finalized the financial reclassification is displayed.

  8. Unbilled Amt - The unbilled amount for the services availed is displayed.

  9. Outstanding Amt - The outstanding amount for the services availed is displayed.

  10. Patient Name - The name of the respective patient is displayed based on the selection of individual patient records.

  11. Billing Group Description - The description of the respective Billing Group is displayed.

  12. Customer Name - The description for the selected Customer Group/Customer is displayed.

  13. Policy Description - The description for the selected Policy Type is displayed.

 

Classification Details

Previous

The classification details associated to the patient during specific encounter is displayed.

 

Requested

The requested classification details recorded and finalized for the patient are displayed.
