Create

The patient records that match the search criteria specified are displayed. The details that are recorded through the 'Patient Registration for Prepaid Program' and 'Maintain Prepaid Patient Records' function are displayed.

 

Enter the following details:

  1. Select All - Select the check box to generate prepaid letters for all patient records that are listed. Color indicators adjacent to the patient record indicate if the amount is overdue, undue or due currently.

  2. Select - Select the check box to generate prepaid letter for the respective patient record. Clear the check box if prepaid letter generation is not required for the respective patient record.

  3. Payment No. - The Payment Number is displayed automatically based on the protocols associated to the patient through the 'Maintain Prepaid Patient Records' function. You cannot update the value that is displayed.

  4. Due Date - The due date is displayed based on the patient registration date.

  5. Payment Amt - The payment amount recorded for the respective Protocol is displayed.

  6. Patient Id - The ID of the patient is displayed.

  7. Patient Name - The name of the patient as recorded during registration is displayed.

  8. Register No. - The registration number recorded through the 'Patient Registration for Prepaid Program' function is displayed.

  9. Register Date - The registration date is displayed.

  10. Protocol Code - The Protocol code that is associated to the patient during registration through 'Patient Registration for Prepaid Program' function is displayed.

  11. Policy Type - The Policy Type that is associated to the patient during registration through 'Patient Registration for Prepaid Program' function is displayed.

  12. Additional Infor. - Click [C] to record additional information about the patient.

    Additional Information

    1. Letter Number - Enter the Letter Number in alphanumeric format of 100 characters maximum length.

    2. Policy Number - Enter the Policy Number alphanumeric format of 30 characters maximum length.

    3. Letter For - Enter the value in alphanumeric format of 49 characters maximum length.

    4. Donor Id - Click [List of Values] to select a Donor ID. Select the donor ID if the letter is being generated for the donor.

    5. Visit/Admsn Date and Time [From - To] - Click the Calendar function to select visit or admission date range.

    Diagnosis and Procedure

    1. Diagnosis [1-4] - Click [List of Values] to select and associate a diagnosis code, if the patient has undergone a diagnosis in the facility.

    2. Procedure [1-4] - Click [List of Values] to select and associate a procedure, if the patient has undergone procedures in the facility.

    3. Discharge Status - Enter the discharge status of the patient.

    4. Discharge Type - Enter the discharge type of the patient.

    5. Remarks - Enter the remarks, if any.

    6. [<<Back] - Click [<<Back] to switch to 'Generate Prepaid Letter' screen.

  13. Protocol Desc - The description of the respective protocol is displayed.

  14. Policy - The description of the respective policy is displayed.

  15. A document number is generated with the Document Type associated to the protocol in 'Prepaid Payment Setup Rule' function.
