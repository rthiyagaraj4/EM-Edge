Enter the following details to register post-renal transplant treatment for the patient:

 

  1. Patient ID - Click [List of Values] to select the Patient ID. The details of patients' discharged from the facility are displayed. The name, gender and the age of the patient is displayed on selecting the patient ID are displayed.

  2. Episode Type - Select the Episode Type from the list and enter the encounter ID.

  3. Discharge Invoice Doc - Click [List of Values] to select the discharge invoice document type and number generated for the patient.

  4. Donor Patient ID - Click [List of Values] to select the Donor Patient ID. This is applicable only if the patient is a registered blood donor in the facility.

  5. Discharge Invoice Date - The date when the discharge invoice is generated for the renal treatment gets displayed along with the time.

  6. Payer Group - The Payer Group associated to the patient while generating the invoice for renal treatment is displayed.

  7. Payer - The Payer associated to the patient while generating the invoice for renal treatment is displayed.

  8. Policy Type Code -  The Policy Type associated to the patient while generating the invoice for renal treatment is displayed.

  9. Treatment Policy Type Code - Click [List of Values] to select and associate the Policy Type for the treatment. The description for the selected Policy Type gets displayed.

  10. Transplant Date - The transplant date recorded for the patient while registering the renal treatment during discharge is displayed.

  11. Discharge Date - The date when the patient is discharged from the facility gets displayed.

  12. Treatment Regn Date - Click Calendar function to select the treatment date. The respective patient is treated on the selected date. This is a mandatory field.

  13. Effective To - Click Calendar function to select the date until when the treatment is valid for the selected inpatient.

  14. Remarks - Enter the remarks, if any, in alphanumeric format.

  15. Recurring Invoice - Select the check box, if the invoice is a recurring invoice for the selected inpatient for post-renal treatment.

  16. Agreed Invoice Amount - The agreed invoice amount for the selected Payer, Policy and Payer Group combination defined using the function 'Agreed Amt by Payer/Policy' function is displayed, by default.

 

Recurring Invoice Amount By Month

  1. Month [From-To] - The agreed invoice amount defined using the function 'Agreed Amt by Payer/Policy' function for subsequent months for the selected Payer, Policy and Payer Group combination is displayed, by default.
