Enter the following details:

 

  1. Patient No – Click [List of Values] to select the patient ID and exempt the patient from bill payment. You can authorize the patient to settle the bill amount in installment after counselling the patient.

  2. Episode – Select an Episode from the list to search for the patient records. On selecting the Episode, enter the encounter ID to search for the respective patient encounter.

  3. National Id – The National ID of the patient gets displayed based on the details recorded during patient registration.

  4. Encounter Date [From-To] – Click the Calendar function to select the encounter date. This is a mandatory field.

  5. Outstanding Amt Only – Select the check box to search for patient records with outstanding amount. Clear the check box to search for all the patient records.

  6. Today’s Encounters Only – Select the check box to search for present days encounters. To search for the encounters during the selected date range, clear the check box.
