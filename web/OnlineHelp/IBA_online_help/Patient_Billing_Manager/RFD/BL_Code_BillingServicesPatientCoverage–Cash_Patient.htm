Enter the following details to define the coverage details:

 

  1. Patient No - Click [List of Values] to select a patient number. A search screen gets displayed. A list of Patient ID's generated during patient registration gets displayed in the search screen. Select the required Patient ID. The name of the patient recorded during patient registration gets displayed.

  2. Sex - The Gender gets displayed automatically when the Patient number is selected.

  3. Age - The age of the respective patient is calculated and displayed in the form of Year, month and days based on the details recorded during patient registration.

  4. Eff. Date From – Click Calendar function to select the date. The patient coverage details are valid from the date selected. This is a mandatory field.

  5. Eff. Date To - Click Calendar function to select the date. The patient coverage details are valid up to the date selected. This is a mandatory field.

  6. Deposit Amount - The amount paid by the Patient in Cash as Deposit during the registration gets displayed.

  7. Utilized Amount - The amount utilized from the patient deposit to clear the service charges gets displayed.

 

Create Range of Services for Coverages

  1. Covered Services [From - To] – Click [List of Values] to select a range of Billing Services.

  2. Effective Date [From - To] – Click Calendar function to select the effective date during which the Billing Services are applicable for coverage.

  3. Search option - Select a value from the list. The listed values are 'Starts with', 'Contains' and 'Ends with'.

  4. Search for Service Description - Enter the data to search for the Billing Services matching the criteria.

  5. [Display Services] - Click [Display Services] to view the selected Services.

  6. Select All - Select the check box to select and associate all the services displayed.

  7. Select - Select the check box against each service to associate the service to the patient.

  8. [Process] - Click [Process] to associate the services. A confirmation screen pops-up. Click [Yes] to confirm or [No] to cancel. The associated service gets displayed under the Maintain Covered Services tab.
