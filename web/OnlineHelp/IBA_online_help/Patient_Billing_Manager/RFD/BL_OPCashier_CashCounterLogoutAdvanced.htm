Enter the following details to logout of the Cash Counter:

 

  1. Cash Counter – Enter the Cash counter code for the facility to be logged out. This can be selected from the list of valid values attached.  On entering Cash counter, the details of ‘In Use By’, ‘Login Date & Time’, ‘Closing Date & Time’ will be displayed by the system, which cannot be modified by the user. Settlement wise summary of transactions taken during the log-in and log-off period will be listed in the “Settlement wise Collection Details”.

 

Settlement-wise Collection Details  

  1. Settlement Type – This cannot be changed.  Transactions are summed up by the Settlement type and listed out in this column.

  2. Cancel Amount – If any cancellations taken place during the Cash Counter logged-in and logged-off period is summed up by settlement type and displayed.

  3. Receipt Amount – This cannot be changed. Actual receipt amount collected during the logged-in and logged-out period is summed up by the Settlement type and displayed here.

  4. Actual Amount - Actual receipts collected by the Cashier at the time of closing the Cash counter to be entered here. The actual amount should be match with the receipt amount displayed.

  5. HO Amount - The Handover amount is displayed. The report generated displays the handover amount that is recorded for the respective Cash Counter and appropriate settlement type.

  6. Discrepancy Amount - This can not be changed. When the Actual amount entered varies from the Receipt Amount, the net difference appears as Discrepancy.

  7. Amount of Bills Generated - The breakup of ‘Self-pay Bill amount’ and ‘Customer payable Bill amount’ is displayed by the system.

  8. Cash Opening Balance - At the time of Cash Counter Log-in, opening balance entered gets displayed.

  9. Cash Closing Balance - Closing balance is derived from the opening balance entered by the user at the log-in and net of transactions taken place in the Cash counter during this period.

 

Collection Details Report

Click [Collection Details Report] the Cash Collection by operator during the period of log-in and log-out will be run by the system. The deposit details report, exemption and discount summary report and outstanding bills report is generated, if there are respective transactions. These reports are printed only for Cashier transactions. You can view, save the output in a file in the local machine or print the report.

 

View Cashier Journal Report

Click [View Cashier Journal Report] to view the Cashier journal report for the logged-in and logged-out period, this can be clicked.

 

Cancel

Click [Cancel] to cancel the log-out operation.

 

Close Cash Counter

Click [Close Cash Counter] to logout from the Cash Counter. A report is be printed with the details of transactions taken place during the log-in and log-out interval.

 

Denomination

Click [Denomination] to enter the denomination values .

 

Currency counts should be a positive value. Denomination value and Cash Closing Balance should be equal.

When you click Close Counter without entering Denomination value, an error message is displayed.
