View Past Prices

The View Past Prices tab enables you to view the rates defined for the patient class and the Billing Services associated to the Customer. The Billing Services associated to the Customer get displayed along with the date and the rates for the patient class.

 

The following details are displayed:

 

  1. Facility – The Facility associated to the Customer gets displayed.

  2. Customer Code - Click [Execute Query] to view the details. The prices defined for the Customers’ in the facility get listed. The description for the Billing Group gets displayed.

 

Billing Service Details

  1. Billing Service - The Billing Services associated to the respective Customer get displayed.

  2. Description – The Description for the Billing Service gets displayed.

  3. Valid From – The date selected when associating the Billing Service to the Customer gets displayed. The Billing Service is associated to the Customer from the date selected.

  4. Valid To - The date selected when associating the Billing Service to the Customer gets displayed. The Billing Service is associated to the Customer until the date selected.

  5. Allowed - The check box is selected or cleared based on whether the check box was selected or cleared during the transactions. It indicates if the selected Billing Service is reimbursable or not for the respective patient class.

  6. Type – The reimbursable type gets listed for the respective patient class.

  7. Reim Amt – The reimbursable amount recorded for the respective patient class gets displayed.

  8. Override – The check box is selected or cleared based on whether the check box was selected or cleared during the transactions. It indicates if reimbursable amount can be overridden or not for the selected Billing Service.

 

Pricing by Billing Class

  1. Class – The Billing Class associated to the Billing Service and the Customer gets displayed.

  2. Description – The Description for the Billing Class gets displayed.

  3. R/F – The value is displayed as 'Rate' or 'Factor' based on the value selected while defining the prices.

  4. IP Rate/Factor - The rate/factor defined for the Inpatients get displayed.

  5. DC Rate/Factor - The rate/factor defined for the Daycare patients gets displayed.

  6. OP Rate/Factor - The rate/factor defined for the Outpatients gets displayed.

  7. Emg. Rate/Factor - The rate/factor defined for the Emergency patients gets displayed.

  8. Ext. Rate/Factor - The rate/factor defined for the External/Referral patients gets displayed.

  9. Min. Charge – The Minimum Charge defined for a range of services gets displayed.

  10. Max Charge – The Maximum Charge defined for a range of services gets displayed.

  11. Base Qty – The Base Quantity defined for a range of services gets displayed.

  12. Gross/Net Applicability – Either Gross or Net is displayed based on the value selected when creating the price for the Billing Services associated to the Customer. The service charge and the reimbursable amount towards the service are calculated accordingly.

  13. Disc. – The check box is selected, if discount was selected as applicable for a range of services.

  14. Check for Cost – The check box is selected, if the Cost Amount is selected as applicable while defining the price for a range of services. If cleared, the Cost Amount is not valid for a range of services.

 

Applicable Discounts for Service and Billing Class

The discount details for the patient class get displayed. The discount value is either displayed as the percentage or amount.
