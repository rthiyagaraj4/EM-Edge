Cash Control Document Details

The Cash Control Document Details are displayed listing the document date, type and number, total amount, credit card amount, balance amount, Cash Counter and the name of the personnel who has approved the Cash Control receipts.

Detail by Bank-in Batch No

The 'Detail by Bank-in Batch No' displays a breakup of the settlement amount along with the document date, type, number and amount, receipt nature, account code, department code, instrument number and date.
