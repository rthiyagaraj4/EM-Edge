SS Query Policy Utilization by Patient for a Period

From this function you can Query policy details of a patient during a selected period in the facility.

The SS Query Policy Utilization by Patient for a Period function is used to query the payer, policy and visit or admission details of a patient. The invoice amount for the services utilized by the patient are also displayed. You can select to query the summary of details based on the service group or on the individual services.

Querying SS Policy Utilization by Patient for a Period

From the Invoice Management > Queries, double-click on SS Query Policy Utilization by Patient for a Period function.

The SS Query Policy Utilization by Patient for a Period screen gets displayed.

  1. Click [List of Values] to select a Patient ID. The name, age, gender and Payer Group of the patient gets displayed on selecting the patient ID. This is a mandatory field.

  2. Click [List of Values] to select a Payer and Policy. You can leave the fields blank, if you wish to search policy details irrespective of the Policy and Payer.

  3. Click the Calendar function to select a Encounter Date Range. The invoice details recorded for the patient during the selected period is displayed. This is a mandatory field.

  4. Select an option from the list. The system-defined values are 'Summary By Payer Service Group' and 'By Individual Services'.

  5. The values are displayed in the following three tabs based on the search criteria specified.

By Payer

The following details are displayed:

  1. Payer - The Payer associated to the patient is displayed.

  2. Description - The description of the selected Payer is displayed.

  3. Payer Service Group - The Payer Service Group associated to the patient is displayed.

  4. Description - The description of the selected Payer Service Group is displayed.

  5. Invoice Amount - The invoice amount generated for the transaction is displayed.

  6. Paid Amount - The amount paid against the invoice generated is displayed.

By Policy

The following details are displayed:

  1. Payer - The Payer selected in 'By Payer' tab is displayed. The description of the selected Payer is displayed.

  2. Policy - The Policy associated to the selected Payer and Patient is displayed. The description of the selected is displayed.

  3. Payer Service Group - The Payer Service Group associated to the Patient and Payer is displayed.

  4. Description - The description of the selected Payer Service Group is displayed.

  5. Invoice Amount - The invoice amount for the services utilized by the patient is displayed.

  6. Paid Amount - The amount settled by the patient against the invoice is displayed.

Visit/Admission

The following details are displayed:

  1. Payer - The Payers associated to the patient are listed.

  2. Policy - The Policies associated to the patient are listed.

  3. Patient ID - The Patient IDs are listed.

  4. Episode Type - The Episode Type of the respective Patient ID is displayed.

  5. Encounter ID - The encounter ID of the patient is displayed.

  6. Adm Date - The admission date of the patient is displayed.

  7. Discharge Date - The discharge date of the patient from the facility is displayed.

  8. Invoice Number - The invoice type and number generated for outstanding amount is displayed.

  9. Invoice Date - The date when the invoice is  generated is displayed.

  10. Invoice Amount - The amount against which the invoice is generated gets displayed.

  11. Paid Amt - The amount paid against the invoice is displayed.

  1. Select the check box to select an Episode Type. All check boxes are selected, by default. Clear the check box against an Episode Type to exclude the selected episode from search criteria. Select the check 'All' to search bill or invoices of all Episode Types.

  2. Click [Refresh] to query the records matching search criteria.

  3. The SS Invoice Details and Service Charges associated to the selected Patient ID is displayed.

  4. Select the check box 'Print/Cancel' to print or cancel the respective record. Click [Print] to print or [Cancel] to cancel the invoice details. On clicking  [Print] a dialog box is displayed. Select 'View' to view the details, 'Print' to print the details or 'File' to save the details in a file.

    Authorized Users are only permitted to cancel the invoice details. An authorization screen pops-up on clicking [Cancel].

    Enter the following details to authorize:

    1. User ID - Enter the User ID in alphanumeric format. This is a mandatory field.

    An error message 'This User is not Authorized for Cancel Invoice' is displayed, if the user is not authorized to cancel the invoice. The check box 'Cancel Invoice' has to be selected for the respective user in the 'Users for Services' function in the Administration menu to cancel the invoice. If the check box is cleared, user is not authorized to cancel the invoice.

    1. Pin No - Enter the PIN Number of the respective User.

    2. Reason - Click [List of Values] to select a Reason. The values defined through the 'Reasons for Billing Actions' function with the 'Cancel Invoice' check box as selected are listed.

  5. Click [D] to view the payment details.

  6. The details associated to the selected patient ID during transactions are displayed.
