Create Agreement for Installment Payment

From this function you can Create a credit agreement for installment payment. You can Query to view the existing patient records.

Create Agreement for Installment Payment function is used to create an agreement for installment payment of a patient. A Cash patient is given credit and the credit agreement is recorded to settle outstanding bill amount in installments. Pending Tasks for the logged in user are displayed with the number of pending requests. The counseling details recorded for the patient are displayed. The outstanding bill details of a patient are listed. Preview option enables you to generate a report.

Creating Agreement for Installment Payment

From the Deferred Cash Patients > Agreement for Installment Payment, double-click Create Agreement for Installment Payment Function.

The Create Agreement for Installment Payment screen gets displayed.

  1. Enter the Patient ID in alphanumeric format of 10 characters maximum length. The patient records with outstanding bill details are listed. <Related Field Description>

  2. Click [Search] to query the patient records matching the search criteria. The legend changes to current date and time on clicking [Search].

  3. The cash patient records with outstanding payment are listed. Patient and Encounter ID, Encounter date, counseling details, total outstanding amount are listed. The counseling details are also displayed.

  4. Click [Commit Form] to record the details or [Clear Record] to clear the entered data. A document number is generated.

  5. Click [Preview] to view the details, print a report or save the output in a file.
