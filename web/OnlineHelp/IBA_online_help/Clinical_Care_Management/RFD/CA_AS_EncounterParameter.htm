Enter the following details to create Encounter Parameter:

 

  1. Backward Visits – Specify the number of days in numerical format of six digits maximum length. Based on the number of days specified, the search for Patient Visits is backdated accordingly in ‘Clinician Desktop’ Patient Search. This field is mandatory

  2. Backward Discharge – Specify the number of days in numerical format of six digits maximum length. Based on the number of days specified, the search for Discharged Patients is backdated accordingly in ‘Clinician Desktop’ Patient Search.

  3. Establish Relationship – Select ‘Establish Relationship’ to enable the relationship functionality in the Clinician Access module. Leaving the field cleared allows the practitioners to select a patient without having to establish a relationship.
