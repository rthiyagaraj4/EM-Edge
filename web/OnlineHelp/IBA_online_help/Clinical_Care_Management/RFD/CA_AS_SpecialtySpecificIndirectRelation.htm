Enter the following details to create Specialty Specific Indirect Relationship record:

 

  1. Specialty – Click [?] function to select the required Specialty. This field is mandatory. On selecting the required ‘Specialty’, click [Search].

  2. Attending Relationship – The Attending Relationships displayed are IP Encounter, IP Admit, OP Visit, Accident and Emergency, Day care, Referral Relation and Nurse Relation.

  3. Relationship – Select the required relationship from the list. All Indirect Relationships defined in the Relationship function are displayed.
