Registered Email Recipient

From this function you can Create a Registered Email Recipient and Query for a created Registered Email Recipient record.

Authorized recipients will have system generated password and unauthorized recipients will have Patient ID as the password. If the recipient is unauthorized and the report is not patient specific then the report is not password protected.

Creating Registered Email Recipient

From the CA Code Setup > Emailing Setup, select Registered Email Recipient function.

The Registered Email Recipient screen gets displayed.

  1. Click [Create] to invoke the Create screen.

  2. Enter the details in the mandatory (*) fields. <Related Field Description>

  3. Click [Apply] to save the created Registered Email Recipient or [Reset] to clear the entered data.

  4. Click [Query] to search for existing ‘Registered Email Recipient’ records.
