Recently Accessed Patient List

From this function you can View patients that were recently accessed by the logged in practitioner.

This function helps practitioners in reducing and saving time taken to search a patient from the general patient list. On selection of this function from the menu, system displays the list of patients recently accessed in descending order of accessed date and time. The system maintains and displays the details of 20 recently accessed patients.

Viewing Recently Accessed Patient List

From the CA Transactions > Clinician Access > Desktop Menu > Patient List, select Recently Accessed.

The Recently Accessed Patient List screen gets displayed.

  1. Click the Patient Name hyperlink to display the Patient Chart and perform transactions.
