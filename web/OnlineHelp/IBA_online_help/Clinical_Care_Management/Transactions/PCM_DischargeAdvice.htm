1. Patient Details – The Patient Details displayed are the Name of the Patient, Age, Gender, Patient ID, Attending Practitioner, Nursing Unit, Bed No., Room No. and Encounter ID.

  2. Encounter Details – The Encounter Details displayed are Admission Date and Time, Encounter ID, Admission Type, Specialty, Practitioner and Patient Type.

  3. Nursing Unit Details – The Nursing Unit Details displayed are the Name of Nursing Unit attached to the patient, Service, Bed Number allocated to the patient, Room Number, Bed Class and Bed Type.

Discharge Advice

From this function you can Prepare a Discharge Advice for a patient.

This function is used to notify the discharge desk that a patient is ready for discharge. The discharge details are entered and discharge notification slip is printed using the function. This function is applicable for all inpatients of the facility. Inpatients include patients whose care in the facility is completed, patients absconded, patients sent on leave and have not yet returned after the expiry of their leave period or patients granted leave and do not want to come back after the leave. This function generates a discharge advice for the patient. Only those Inpatients with discharge advice prepared can be discharged.

Preparing Discharge Advice

From the CA Transactions > Clinician Access > Desktop Menu > select required patient > Patient Chart Menu > Discharge Activities, select Discharge Advice.

The Prepare Discharge Advice screen gets displayed.

  1. The screen displays patient details and two tabs to enter discharge advice details: Discharge tab and Other Details tab. The Discharge is tab gets opened to enter the details by default.

  2. Enter the details in the mandatory (*) fields in both the tabs. <Related Field Description>

  3. Click [Discharge Diagnosis] to view active problems of the patient and record the discharge diagnosis.

  4. Click [Certificate] to record medical certificate details.

  5. Click [Record] to save the Discharge Advice prepared or [Clear] to clear the entered data. Click [Close] to exit from the screen.
