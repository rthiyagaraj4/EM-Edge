Record Clinical Notes

From this function you can Record and View Clinical Notes that reflect the clinical condition of a patient.  

Clinical note is a statement about a patient with clinical relevance and forms a part of the patient record. Notes are also referred to as point of care documentation usually recorded on a regular basis during the stay of the patient, and all such recording form an important and integral part of the patient history. The user can set up privileges for the practitioners that govern the access rights of practitioners to record or view a note. The system facilitates maintaining a draft copy of the note until it is signed or authorized by the practitioner recording the notes or by another practitioner based on their access rights. A note once recorded can be modified based on the setting for the note type. A note can be sent for review before making it a part of the patient history. Analysis can be performed based on some components of the note.

Recording Clinical Notes
