Rebuild Section Template

From this function you can Rebuild or refresh Sections and Notes created. You can also rebuild notes and sections of the Oral Health and Maternity Specialty modules.

This function enables you to refresh changes made to the existing section templates incase of any addition or deletion of components.

Rebuilding Section Templates

From the CA Code Setup, select Rebuild Section Template function.

The Rebuild Section Template screen gets displayed.

  1. Click [Create] to invoke the Create screen.

  2. Enter the details in the mandatory (*) fields. <Related Field Description>

  3. Click [Apply] to Rebuild Section Templates or [Reset] to clear the entered data.
