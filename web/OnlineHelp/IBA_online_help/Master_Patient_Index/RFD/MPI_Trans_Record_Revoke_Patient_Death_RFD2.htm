Enter the following details to record Death of a patient:

 

The Name of the Patient, Gender, Age and the Patient ID are displayed.

 

  1. Action - This is a display field. It displays the status of the selected Patient as ‘Record Patient Death’.

  2. Deceased Date - Enter the Deceased Date in dd/mm/yyyy format or select the date using Calendar function. The deceased date must be less than or equal to current date. This is a mandatory field.

  3. Deceased Remarks - Enter Deceased Remarks in alphanumeric format of 200 characters maximum length. This is a mandatory field.
