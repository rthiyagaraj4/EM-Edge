Change Home Phone No/Mobile No

From this function you can Update Phone No and Mobile No of the patient. You can also Query about the phone/mobile no of the desired Patient.

This function is accessible to the Nurses only. User can update the Phone or Mobile No of the patient.

Updating Home Phone No/Mobile No

From the MP Transactions, select the Home Phone No/ Mobile No function.

The Home Phone No/Mobile No screen gets displayed

  1. The ‘Search Patient’ dialog gets displayed.

  2. Search for the required patient and click the required ‘Patient ID’ hyperlink from the search results.

  3. The ‘Change Home Phone No/Mobile No’ window gets invoked with the selected patient demographic details in read only mode.

  4. Under the ‘Contact Mode’ section, ‘Phone No’ and ‘Mobile No’ fields appear with patient details if recorded during patient registration or updated in Change Patient Details function.

  5. User can update the ‘Phone No’ and ‘Mobile No’ fields if required.

  6. Click [Apply] to save the Patient phone number or [Reset] to clear the entered data.

  7. Click [Query] to invoke the ‘Patient Search’ window to search for existing Patient details.
