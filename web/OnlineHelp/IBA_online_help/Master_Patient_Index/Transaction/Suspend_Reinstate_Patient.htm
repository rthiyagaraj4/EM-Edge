Suspend/Reinstate Patient

From this function you can Suspend services to a patient or Re-instate suspended services for a patient for various reasons.

The reason for suspension is specified by the facility while suspending. System prohibits any transactions on suspended patients. A unique color indicator displays the 'Suspended' status. Once the suspension is revoked, transactions are performed.

Suspending/Reinstating a Patient

From the MP Transactions, select the Suspend/Reinstate Patient function.

The Patient Search screen gets displayed.

  1. Enter at least one search criteria if not all and click [Search] to display patient records according to the entered criteria.

  2. Select the desired patient by clicking the Patient ID hyperlink to Suspend the Patient from the Suspend/Reinstate Patient screen that displays.

  3. Enter the details in the mandatory (*) fields to suspend the patient. <Related Field Description>

  4. Click [Apply] to save the suspension.

  5. To reinstate a suspended patient, select the patient using the search patient function. A unique color code indicates the suspended patient. Select the desired patient by clicking the Patient ID hyperlink to Reinstate Patient.

  6. The 'Suspended' status appears as a blinking gif on the screen. Enter Reinstatement Remarks.

  7. Click [Apply] to save reinstating the suspended record or [Reset] to clear the entered data.

 

In MP Parameter, if ‘Suspending Service to Patient’ check box is selected, this function is operational.
