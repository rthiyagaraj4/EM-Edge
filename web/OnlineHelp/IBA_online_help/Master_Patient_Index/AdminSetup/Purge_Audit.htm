Purge Audit

From this function you can Purge or Clear the log records on changes performed to Demographics/Status of patients.

The system maintains log records on changes performed to Demographics/Status of patients based on the audit trail parameter. The log record contains both old and new values. Information not required by the enterprise needs to be cleared on a regular basis.

Clearing Log Records

From the MP Admin Setup > MP Admin Setup, select the Purge Audit function.

The Purge Audit screen gets displayed.

  1. Select the date until when the log records need to be purged.

  2. Click [Apply] to save the Purge Audit details or [Reset] to clear the entered data.
