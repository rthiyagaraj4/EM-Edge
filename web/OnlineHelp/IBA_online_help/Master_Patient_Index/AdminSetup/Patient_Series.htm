Patient Series

From this function you can Create a new Patient Series, set attributes and  group the Patient Series  under a Patient Series Type. You can also Query for the desired Patient Series.

Patients are classified under specific groups for the purpose of Identification number generation. The Patient Series types are Alternate ID Series, National ID Series, General Series, Emergency Series, Newborn Series, Unrestricted, External Source Series and External Patient/Donor Series.

The generation of patient identifier is influenced by the numbering attribute of the Patient Series, which could be common across facilities or specific to individual facility. The identifier generation is controlled by system or manually generated.

Creating a Patient Series

From the MP Admin Setup > MP Admin Setup, select the Patient Series function.

The Patient Series screen gets displayed.

  1. Click [Create] to invoke the Create screen.

  2. Enter the details in the mandatory (*) fields. <Related Field Description>

  3. Click [Apply] to save the Patient Series created or [Reset] to clear the entered data.

  4. Click [Query] to search for existing Patient Series records.

 

The values ‘By Site’ and ‘By Facility’ are available in this function based on the setting in ‘MP Parameter’ (under ‘Patient Administration’ tab > Numbering Control). If Numbering control is defined ‘By Site’ in the parameter, ‘By Facility’ is not available in this function. Similarly, if ‘By Facility’ is selected in the parameter setup, ‘By Site’ is not available. If ‘By Combination’ is selected, both ‘By Site’ and ‘By Facility’ are available.
