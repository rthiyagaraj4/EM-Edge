Policy Type Details

 

The policy details associated to the patient are displayed on clicking [Execute Query].

  1. Billing Group – Billing Group for specific Policy Type selected while recording or updating the financial details for the patient gets displayed.

  2. Payer – Payer Code for specific Policy Type selected while  recording or updating the financial details for the patient gets displayed.

  3. Priority – The priority for the selected Policy Type recorded while updating the patient financial details gets displayed. The Priority associated to the patient during registration or admission gets displayed.

  4. Policy Type Code – The Policy Type Code selected while  recording or updating the financial details for the patient gets displayed.

  5. Policy Number – The Policy Number entered while  recording or updating the patient financial details gets displayed.

  6. Start Date - The date from when the policy is valid is displayed. The Start Date selected while recording or updating the patient financial details gets displayed.

  7. Expiry Date - The Policy Expiry Date selected while recording or updating the patient financial details gets displayed.

  8. Credit Auth Ref – The Credit Authorization Reference details entered while recording or updating the patient financial details gets displayed.

  9. Date – The date when the policy coverage was associated for the selected material item is displayed.

  10. Amount – The coverage amount specified while  recording or updating the financial details gets displayed.

  11. Days – The number of days until when the policy coverage is valid gets displayed.

  12. Effective [From–To] - The beginning and the end date until when the policy is effective get displayed.  

  13. Limit Amount OP - The coverage limit on the amount for the Outpatients is displayed.

  14. Limit Amount IP - The coverage limit on the amount for the Inpatients is displayed.
