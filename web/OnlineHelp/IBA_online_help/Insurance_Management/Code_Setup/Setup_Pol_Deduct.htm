Setup Policy Deductibles

From this function you can Record the Insurance deductible details for a Policy Type and Customer associated to a facility. You can Query for a desired record.

The Setup Policy Deductibles function is used to record the Insurance deductible details for a Policy Type and Customer in the facility. You can update the details of the existing Policy Type and Customer details associated to the facility. The Policy deductibles define the amount the patient has to pay to the hospital before the Insurance Company settles the benefits.

Setup Policy Deductibles

From the Masters, double-click on Setup Policy Deductibles function.

The Setup Policy Deductibles screen gets displayed.

  1. Enter the details in the mandatory (*) fields. <Related Field Description>

  2. Click [Commit Form] or press [F10] to save or click [Clear Record] to clear the entered data.

  3. Click [Execute Query] or press [F8] to search for the existing records within the facility.
