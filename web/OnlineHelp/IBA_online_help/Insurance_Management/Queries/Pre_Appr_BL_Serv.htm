Pre-Approval for Billing Services

From this function you can Query the pre-approval status of the Billing Services.    

The function Pre-Approval for Billing Services is used to inquire on the pre-approval status of the Billing Services. You cannot update the details, but only view the recorded details.

Pre-Approval for Billing Services

From the Queries, double-click on Pre-Approval for Billing Services function.

The Pre-Approval for Billing Services screen gets displayed.

  1. Click [List of Values] to select a Patient Number. The patient lookup screen gets displayed.

  2. Click [Execute Query] or press [F8] to view all the patient details.

OR

  1. Click [Enter Query] and enter the detail in the respective text box. Click [Execute Query] or press [F8] to view the selected patient record.

  2. Double-click on the record to select it. The patient details get displayed in the respective text boxes.  

  3. Click [Authorize] to login and to view the details. An authorization screen pops-up requesting you to enter the login details. Authorized Users only are permitted to login. Click [OK] to proceed with the changes or [Cancel] to discard the entries.

    Enter the following details to login:

    1. User ID - Enter the User ID in alphanumeric format. This is a mandatory field.

    2. Pin No - Enter the Pin number for the respective User. This is a mandatory field.

    3. Remarks - Enter the Remarks in alphanumeric format. This is a mandatory field.

  4. The financial details for the specific patient encounter and the policy coverage details recorded using the function Encounter Specific Policy Definition – Services gets displayed.

  5. Click [Close] to exit from the screen.
