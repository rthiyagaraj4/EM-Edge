Document/Acknowledge Outpatient/Emergency Patient Meal Order

From this function you can Acknowledge the meal order placed for outpatients

The Document/Acknowledge Outpatient/Emergency Patient Meal Orders function helps to acknowledge completion of food preparation, confirmation on meals served and to document the meal served. You can acknowledge or document the outpatient meal order placed within the facility and cannot acknowledge orders placed for another facility.

Documenting meals prepared and served for outpatients/emergency patients

From the Transactions, select the Document/Acknowledge Outpatient and Emergency Patient Meal Order function.

The Document/Acknowledge Outpatient/Emergency Patient Meal Order screen gets displayed.

  1. Click [Create] to invoke the Create screen.

  2. Enter the details in the mandatory (*) fields. <Related Field Description>

  3. Click [Search] to display the Patient Name, Patient ID, Appt. Date/Time, Visit Date/Time, Appt. No, Encounter ID , Status and Date/Time and Food Replacement.

  4. Select the Status corresponding to the patient record from the drop-down list. The listed values are Meal Prepared, Meal Received, Meal Served and Meal Rejected. If you select the Status as 'Reject' for a meal order, the Others hyperlink (mandatory) appears under Complaints column. Click Others hyperlink to enter comments in alphanumeric format up to 50 characters (special characters allowed).

  5. Select the Date/Time using the calendar function. By default, the system date/time is displayed.

If the meal status is “Reject/Meal Rejected” then Food Replacement drop-down is enabled’ else it is disabled.

  1. Click [Apply] to document the status change of meal order or [Reset] to clear the entered details.

 

If you click [Apply] without entering any value, an error message ‘Atleast One Record Should Be Updated’ is displayed.
