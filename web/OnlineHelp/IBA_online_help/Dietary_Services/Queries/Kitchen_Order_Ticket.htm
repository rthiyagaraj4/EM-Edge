Kitchen Order Ticket

From this function you can Query and View a report/summary of the kitchen order ticket for all the therapeutic diets and an individual item required as per the diet types.

The Kitchen Order Ticket function is used to generate a report/summary of the kitchen order ticket for all the therapeutic diets and an individual item required as per the diet types.

Querying Kitchen Order Tickets

From Queries, select the Kitchen Order Tickets

The Kitchen Order Tickets screen gets displayed.

  1. Enter the details in the mandatory (*) fields. <Related Field Description>

  2. Click [Select] to display the Food Items and Quantity details or [Reset] to clear the entered details.

  3. Click [Print] to print a report/summary of the kitchen order ticket.
