Kitchen Area Items

From this function you can Associate a food Item to a kitchen area of a main kitchen in the hospital. You can Query for the food items associated to a Kitchen and a Kitchen Area.

The Kitchen Area Items function is used to maintain the different food items in each Kitchen area associated to a Kitchen. If certain food items are prepared in particular kitchen areas, the definition of food items for kitchen areas helps in routing it to the respective areas and printing food labels.

Creating Kitchen Area Items

From the Setup, select the Kitchen Area Item function.

The Kitchen Area Item screen gets displayed.

  1. Click [Create] to invoke the Create screen.

  2. Enter the details in the mandatory (*) fields. <Related Field Description>

  3. Click [Apply] to save a created Kitchen Area Item or [Reset] to clear the entered details.

  4. Click [Query] to search for the existing records.
