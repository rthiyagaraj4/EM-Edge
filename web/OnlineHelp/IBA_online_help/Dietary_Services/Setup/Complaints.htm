Complaints

From this function you can Create a Complaint record and Query for the existing Complaints recorded.

The Complaints function helps in recording various types of complaints received regarding the food items prepared and served in the facility. The complaints are related to wrong supply of food, insufficient quantity, poor quality in food served, items which are recorded as disliked by the patients while serving, , unhygienic or uncovered food, delay in the food supply and so on. The records created using this function get populated in the 'Document/Acknowledge Meal Prepared/Served/Complaints' function. These complaints are further documented or acknowledged by the Head of the Department.

Creating Complaint records

From the Setup, select the Complaints function.

The Complaints screen gets displayed.

  1. Click [Create] to invoke the Create screen.

  2. Enter the details in the mandatory (*) fields. <Related Field Description>

  3. Click [Apply] to save a created Complaint or [Reset] to clear the entered details.

  4. Click [Query] to search for the existing records.
