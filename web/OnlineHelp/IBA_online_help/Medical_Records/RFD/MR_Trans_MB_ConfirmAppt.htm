Confirming Appointment

From the MR Transactions > Medical Board, select the Status tab.

The Status tab of the Medical Board screen gets displayed.

  1. Select the Medical Board Status as ‘Appointment Given’ and click [Search] to display all Medical Boards Request with appointment given or enter the search criteria and click [Search] to select the required Medical Board Request.

  2. Click the Request ID hyperlink to invoke ‘Intimate Appointment for Medical Board Request’ screen intimate appointment for the Medical Board Request.

  3. Enter the details in the mandatory (*) fields. <Related Field Description>

  4. Click [Record] to save intimating appointment for Medical Board Request or [Close] to exit the ‘Intimate Appointment for Medical Board Request’ screen.

  5. On recording, if internal reports are defined, the Internal Reports screen gets displayed. Click [OK] to generate Internal Reports or [Cancel] to exit from the screen.

  6. A booking confirmation alert specifying the reference number of the appointment is displayed. Click [OK] to close the screen.
