Deliver BHT

From this function you can Deliver the Bed Head Ticket (BHT) in pending status to Medical Record Department (MRD).

This function allows to search for pending BHT for a discharge patient with closed encounter and deliver it to MRD.

Whenever patients are discharged from the respective department and encounter is closed, Nurses will generate and deliver the BHT to Medical Record Department (MRD) for validation. The status of the BHT will be changed from Pending to ‘Delivered’.

 

  • When the number of days defined in ‘Max No of days for BHT Submission’ field in MR Parameter has crossed for a discharge patient, the pending BHT status gets automatically changed to ‘Overdue’.

  • Only ‘Pending’, ‘Overdue’, and ‘Returned’ BHT records can be searched and delivered to MRD.

 

Delivering BHT

From the MR Transactions, select the Deliver BHT function.

The Deliver BHT screen gets displayed.

  1. Click [Create] to invoke the Create screen.

  2. Enter the details in mandatory (*) fields. <Related Field Description>

  3. Click [Search] to search the records matching the search criteria or [Clear] to clear the entered data.

  4. In the search results, click + symbol to view the Encounter Details.

·       Click Patient ID hyperlink to view the patient demographics details.

·       Click Diagnosis Details hyperlink to invoke the Diagnosis Details dialog. The diagnosis details of the patient gets displayed.

·       Remarks - Click Remarks to invoke the Remarks dialog. Enter the remarks in the text box up to 100 maximum characters in alphanumeric format and click [OK].

  1. To deliver the patient record, select the check box against the required patient record and click [Deliver BHT]. The status of the patient record gets changed from Pending to 'Delivered'. The patient record automatically disappears from the search results.

Receive BHT

From this function you can Receive/Return the Bed Head Ticket (BHT) in ‘Delivered’ status.

This function allows MRD users to validate the delivered BHT and updaBed Head Ticket (BHT) te the status as Received. If the BHT information is not correct, it is Returned to Nursing unit for correction. The status is updated as ‘Returned’. User can search for patient records for all types of BHT status: Pending, Overdue, Delivered, Received and Returned.

User cannot action on BHT with status of Pending, Overdue and Returned, it will be view only.

Receiving/Returning BHT

From the MR Transactions, select the Receive BHT function.

The Receive BHT screen gets displayed.

  1. Click [Create] to invoke the Create screen.

  2. Enter the details in mandatory (*) fields. <Related Field Description>

  3. Click [Search] to search the records matching the search criteria or [Clear] to clear the entered data.

  4. From the search results, user can receive or return the patient records with BHT status as ‘Delivered’.

• Click + symbol to view the Encounter Details.

• Click Patient ID hyperlink to view the patient demographics details.

• Click Diagnosis Details hyperlink to invoke the Diagnosis Details dialog. The diagnosis details of the patient is displayed if available.

• Remarks - Click Remarks to invoke the Remarks dialog. Enter the remarks in the text box up to 100 maximum characters in alphanumeric format and click [OK].

  1. To Receive the BHR, first validate the BHR record and if it is correct, select the check box against the patient record and click [Receive BHT]. The BHT status for the patient record gets changed to ‘Received’. The record disappears from the search results.

  2. If the patient’s BHT is incorrect, user must select the Delivered BHT status record and click [Return BHT], The BHT will be returned to the nursing unit for correction. The BHT status is updated as ‘Returned’ and disappears from the search results. The record will appear in the ‘Delivered BHT’ function.

• Pending, and Overdue records cannot be returned.

• Only if user selects delivered BHT records, the ‘Return BHT’ button will be enabled.

• If user selects multiple records a combination of Pending, Overdue, Delivered, Returned, then the ‘Receive BHT’ button will only be enabled as there is a return record also in the select combination which cannot be returned.
