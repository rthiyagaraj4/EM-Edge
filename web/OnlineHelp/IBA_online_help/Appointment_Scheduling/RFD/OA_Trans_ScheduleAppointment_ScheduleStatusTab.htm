Schedule Status Tab

This tab displays the schedule for the next 30 days from the current date for the Location/Resource (primary resource identified for the location in Outpatient Management module) combination, if generated.

  1. Select required Specialty or Location and click [Search] to display the schedule status for the next 30 days from the current date or enter the query criteria to search for a specific schedule. <Related Field Description>

    1. Location – This field displays the identity of the location for which the schedule exists.

    2. Resource – This field displays the name of the resource against the location for which the schedule is generated.

    3. S – Select the check box to select the day schedule for more than one Location/Resource combination. A maximum of three Location/Resource(s) schedules are selected at any point of time.

    The schedule status for each day has a distinct color to indicate the schedule status. The color represents:

    • Blue – This color indicates that the bookings are full and no further bookings can be made.

    • Red - This color indicates that the schedule has been blocked.

    • Yellow - This color indicates that only overbooking can be made.

    • Pink – This color indicates the holiday against the displayed date.

    • Blank - This indicates that schedule is not generated or defined for the Location/Resource combination.

    • Light Green - This color indicates that only ‘First Visit’ slots are available in the Schedule.

    • Dark Green - This color indicates that only ‘Other Visit’ slots are available in the Schedule.

    • Green - This color indicates that both ‘First Visit’ and ‘Other Visit’ slots are available in the schedule.

  2. Click to view the schedule status for the next period. Click to view the schedule status for the previous period.

  3. The schedule for each day shows a distinct color to indicate the schedule status.

  4. Select the corresponding day for which appointment is required by clicking the hyperlinked box under a particular date and the control shifts back to the Day Schedule Tab.

  5. Select the check boxes under ‘S’ up to a maximum of three Location/Resource(s) schedules to view all the selected schedules at a time. The control shifts back to the Day Schedule Tab.

  6. Click [Clear] to clear the details displayed.

  7. Click [Create Waitlist] to assign patients on a Wait List.
