Cancel Wait List

  1. Select Cancel Wait list option from the list. Cancel Wait list screen gets displayed with ‘Wait List Appointment Details’, ‘Patient Details’, and ‘Contact Details’.

  2. Click [Contact Details] to view the contact details of the patient.

  3. Select the reason for cancellation from [?] function.

  4. Click [Cancel] to cancel the wait list or [Close] to exit from the screen.

Elapsed waitlist records cannot be cancelled.
