Ext. Treatments

The external treatments recorded using the tab are displayed on the right hand side of the screen. You can cancel the external treatments by marking the condition as complete, incomplete or error. Click the treatment, which is set as a hyperlink with date and the Condition name. On clicking the hyperlink, the External Treatment tab is enabled and the recorded data is populated in the respective fields with the field entry being disabled. The Status, Outcome and Remarks can only be updated.   

External treatments that are recorded for a tooth are highlighted using color. Treatments that are recorded as failure are indicated using a cross mark. A treatment that is marked as ‘Failure’ cannot be changed to ‘Success’. External treatments that are marked as failure is highlighted in color with a cross mark. Successful or external treatments cannot be marked as an error. The external treatments that are recorded through the ‘External Treatment’ tab are displayed in the Treatment chart. Successful external treatments are highlighted in color.
