Periodontal Charting

From this function you can Create a Periodontal Chart to record the gingival status of a patient.

A Practitioner can record the gingival details by entering it in the data entry area or on clicking the related icons in the screen. Periodontal charting provides a graphic description of the conditions in a patient's mouth. Charting has to be updated for each visit to follow the patient's progress in recording and completing the dental procedures. The images are displayed based on the values selected.

Periodontal charting functionality includes various cycles of data collection by going around the dentition. They are:

• Missing tooth

• Pocket depth

You can perform the create the following charts using the Periodontal Charting screen:

• Baseline Chart

• Evaluation chart

• Compare

Baseline Chart is created before the treatment, Evaluation Chart is created after the treatment and once the treatment is performed, Compare option facilitates comparing the Baseline Chart with the Evaluation Chart.

Creating a Periodontal Chart

From the CA Transactions > Clinician Access > Desktop Menu > Charting, select Periodontal Charting Function.

The Periodontal Charting screen gets displayed with the Baseline .

  1. Enter details in the mandatory (*) fields under the following tabs:

Baseline Chart

Evaluation Chart

Compare

  1. Click [Refresh] to create a chart or compare the chart details based on the selected values.

  2. Click [Reset] to clear the details.

  3. Click Remarks hyperlink to record remarks. The hyperlink is displayed both in Baseline Chart and Evaluation Chart tab. Click [OK] to record or [Cancel] to abort the process. Click Remarks History to view the name of the Practitioner and the date when the remark is modified.

  4. Click Periodontal Summary hyperlink to view the summary. On clicking the Periodontal Summary hyperlink, system displays all the indexes calculated and the components along with the tooth numbers, which are indicated in red.

  5. Click View Graph to view the graphical representation of the recorded details. On clicking the View Graph Hyperlink from the Baseline Chart View, system displays a graphical representation on the conditions recorded for a patient. The patient details and the chart name get displayed on the top of the screen. You can only view the graphical representation, but cannot make any changes.

    The View Graph enables you to view a graphical representation of the selected Charts. Click 'View Graph' hyperlink adjacent to  to view the graph. The graph displays the Patient ID, Chart Name and the Chart Type selected. You can only view the graphical representation and cannot make any changes to the chart. Select the component by clicking on the appropriate radio button to view the graphical representation. Click to view the graphical representation of CAL, CEJ-GM or PD components. The values recorded through the Baseline Chart and the Evaluation charts are displayed using, and images respectively over the tooth range.

A missing tooth is indicated through a ‘X’ mark in the graph.

=> The values on the crown are minus and the values recorded on the root are considered as plus.

=> The centre line in the graph moves through the cervical margin of the tooth.

    The active Baseline charts are listed under the menu Baseline Charts (Active). The screen displays the active Baseline Charts, which are displayed for the patient. The date when the chart was created gets displayed along with the Chart name, which is made as a hyperlink. Click on the hyperlink to view the chart details.

    The Charts, which are completed, get listed under the Baseline Charts (Active) with the status as ‘(CM)’, which indicate that the charts are complete. The names of the Charts, which are Active, get displayed with the status as ‘(PE)’. Click on the Chart name, which is displayed as a hyperlink to view the details.

  2. Click adjacent to ‘Baseline Charts (Closed)’ to view the Baseline Charts, which are closed.

  3. Click  to view other links. Diagnosis, Clinical Notes and Restorative Chart are displayed as hyperlinks. This option is available under ‘Baseline Chart’ and ‘Evaluation Chart’ tabs.

    Click Restorative Chart hyperlink to view active Restorative Chart of the patient in facility. If the active Restorative Chart has crossed the validity period, a warning message ‘Restorative Chart validity days are crossed. Do you want to create a new chart?’ is displayed. Click [OK] to proceed or [Cancel] to cancel to display the existing chart. A message ‘Do you want to copy the current chart conditions and treatments?’ is displayed when you click [OK]. Click [OK] to create a new chart by copying the conditions and treatments in the existing chart or click [Cancel] to abort the process.

    Click Diagnosis hyperlink to record diagnosis for the selected patient or view recorded diagnosis details.
