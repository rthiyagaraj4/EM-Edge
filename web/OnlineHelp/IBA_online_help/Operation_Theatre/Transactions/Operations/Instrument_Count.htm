Instrument Count Set

This function is used to add an Instrument Count Set.

  1. Bio Hazard? - Select the 'Bio Hazard?' check box to transfer 'Y' values to the interface or 'N' values will be transferred to the interface. This flag is accepted at the patient level and not at the Individual Item level.

  2. Click [Add a Set] to display the Add a Set screen.

  3. Operation – Select the required Operation from the list.

  4. Set – Select the Instruments from the list.

  5. Tray – Enter the number of Trays for the Instrument Set.

  6. Required Quantity – Enter the number of quantity of  items required for surgery. This is a mandatory field.

  7. Quantity On Hand – The Item/Set associated with the surgery within the OT store is displayed.

  8. Click [OK] to save the record. Click [Cancel] to exit the screen.

  9. On saving, the details are displayed on the screen. The details displayed are Operation, Set, Tray and Allocated Quantity.

  10. Select the Tray hyperlink to display the Items of the selected Instrument Set.

The ‘Required Quantity’ and ‘Quantity on Hand’ fields are displayed only if the Operation Theatre and Sterile Stock modules are interfaced. Messages are prompted if the ’Required Quantity’ is greater than ’Quantity on Hand’ or if the Quantity on Hand is zero or not available.

Enter the following details:

  1. Item – The Items of the Instrument Set is displayed.

  2. Std Qty – The Standard Quantity of the Items are displayed.

  3. CSSD Qty – The CSSD Quantity of the Items are displayed.

  4. Open Qty – Enter the Open Quantity of the Items.

  5. Initial Status - Select the Initial Status of the Instrument items as Incomplete or Complete

  6. Remarks – Click the Remarks hyperlink and enter remarks. Click [OK] to save the recorded details. Click [Cancel] to exit from the remarks screen.

  7. Return Qty – Select the Status as Incomplete or Complete.

  8. Final Status – Select the Final Status as Incomplete or Complete.

  9. Final Remarks – Click the Remarks hyperlink and enter final remarks. Click [OK] to save the recorded details. Click [Cancel] to exit from the remarks screen.

  10. General Remarks – Click the Remarks hyperlink and enter general remarks. Click [OK] to save the recorded details. Click [Cancel] to exit from the remarks screen.

  11. Initial Status Completed – Select the check box to indicate that recording initial status of the instrument set is completed.

  12. Final Status Completed – Select the check box to indicate that recording final status of the instrument set is completed.

  13. Click [Apply] to save the Instrument Count Details.
