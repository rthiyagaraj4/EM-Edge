Record Pre-Anesthesia Details

Pre-Anesthesia function is used to record the Pre-Anesthesia details.

The Order ID, Date and Time of Booking, Surgeon, Specialty, Theatre, Booking Number, Date and Time of the surgery are displayed.

The following details are displayed:

  1. Anesthesia Serial No – The Anesthesia Serial Number is displayed.

  2. Referral Source – The referral source of the patient is displayed.

  3. Weight – Enter the Weight of the Patient in Kgs.

  4. Height – Enter the Height of the Patient in Cms.

  5. BSA – The BSA of the Patient is displayed if recorded.

  6. BMI Details – The BMI details of the Patient is displayed if recorded.

  7. Click the Remarks hyperlink to record remarks for pre-anesthesia details.

  8. Click Pre-Anesthesia Details hyperlink to record Pre-Anesthesia Details.

Operations/Procedures Tab

The Operation Code, Description and Side Applicable are displayed.

Assessment Tab

  1. Pre-Morbid Status – Select the Pre-Morbid Status using the [?] function. The Pre-Morbid Status is created in the Pre-Morbid Status function. This is mandatory field.

  2. Remarks – Enter the Remarks if required.

  3. Click [Select] to add the details to the screen. Select the Pre-Morbid Status hyperlink and click [Cancel] to cancel recording Assessment details.

Status

  1. Pre-Anesthesia Completed - Select the check box to indicate completion of recording Pre-anesthesia details.

  2. Click [Apply] to save Pre-Anesthesia Details.
